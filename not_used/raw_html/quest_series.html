Quests/Series

This is a list of quest series which includes all quests that are recognised to share a common and continuing plotline. Quests in a series have a clear sequence and usually share the main protagonist(s) and/or antagonist(s) as well as the general storyline. The list is based on the ingame "Storyline" sorting in the Quest List interface.

See the quest series section of the quests article for further information. The lists below are in chronological order; quests not listed here are considered as standalone quests.

List[edit | edit source]

Camelot[edit | edit source]

Main article: Camelot quest series
No Quest Difficulty Release date Synopsis
1 Merlin's Crystal Intermediate 27 February 2002 The player helps free Merlin from a magical crystal prison.
2 Holy Grail Intermediate 23 July 2002 The player helps the Knights of the Round Table in their search for the Holy Grail.
3 King's Ransom Experienced 24 July 2007 The player investigates the Sinclair family's departure from their mansion and the disappearance of the Knights of the Round Table.

Main antagonist - Morgan Le Faye

Demon Slayer[edit | edit source]

No Quest Difficulty Release date Synopsis
1 Demon Slayer Novice 4 January 2001 The player discovers they are destined to slay Delrith, a demon who tried to destroy Varrock 150 years ago.
2 Shadow of the Storm Intermediate 14 November 2005 The player assists two Saradominist priests in stopping a group of dark wizards from summoning the demon Agrith Naar.

Related story quests - The Golem
Main antagonist - Denath

Dorgeshuun[edit | edit source]

Developers: Paul G, John A

No Quest Difficulty Release date Synopsis
1 The Lost Tribe Intermediate 31 May 2005 The player investigates a broken wall in the Lumbridge Castle cellar, only to discover a lost tribe of peaceful goblins known as the Dorgeshuun.
2 Death to the Dorgeshuun Intermediate 21 June 2006 The player accompanies Zanik to explore the surface world on behalf of the Dorgeshuun. However, a plot by the extremist group H.A.M. is discovered by the two.
3 Another Slice of H.A.M. Intermediate 24 April 2007 In the process of building a railway system between Dorgesh-Kaan and Keldagrim, an archaeological discovery leads to the revelation of Zanik's destiny.
4 Land of the Goblins Experienced 9 February 2022 The player and Zanik investigate Grubfoot's dreams about Yu'biusk, the land of the goblins.

Antagonists - Sigmund, Humans Against Monsters, Bandos

Dragonkin[edit | edit source]

No Quest Difficulty Release date Synopsis
1 Dragon Slayer I Experienced 23 September 2001 The player is tasked to slay Elvarg, the dragon who destroyed Crandor thirty years ago, to gain entry into the prestigious Champions' Guild.
2 A Tail of Two Cats Intermediate 26 September 2005 The player helps Unferth find their lost cat, Bob. In the process, the player discovers Bob's heritage and help set him up with a lover.
3 Dragon Slayer II Grandmaster 4 January 2018 The player, seeking to gain entry into the mythical Myths' Guild, helps Dallas Jones with the simple task of investigating Elvarg's lair. This chain of events results in the emergence of a world-ending threat sealed long ago during the Fourth Age.

Related story quests - Bone Voyage
Antagonists - Elvarg, Dragonkin

Elemental Workshop[edit | edit source]

Main article: Elemental Workshop quest series
No Quest Difficulty Release date Synopsis
1 Elemental Workshop I Novice 2 June 2004 The player stumbles upon a battered book, leading them to rediscover an old workshop beneath Seers' Village.
2 Elemental Workshop II Intermediate 2 October 2006 The player stumbles upon a beaten book, leading them to discover additional levels of the old workshop.

Elf[edit | edit source]

No Quest Difficulty Release date Synopsis
1 Plague City Novice 27 August 2002 The player helps Edmond with finding his daughter Elena, who has not returned from the plague-ridden city of West Ardougne.
2 Biohazard Novice 23 October 2002 The player helps Elena to recover her medical equipment and smuggle a sample of the plague to her mentor Guidor.
3 Underground Pass Experienced 3 March 2003 The player helps King Lathas find a way through the Underground Pass to stop his brother, King Tyras.
4 Regicide Experienced 20 September 2004 The player helps King Lathas in killing his brother, who he claims has been corrupted by a Dark Lord. In the process, the player stumbles upon a thousands-year long civil war between the Elven Clans.
5 Roving Elves Experienced 7 February 2005 The player is tasked to consecrate the tomb of Glarial by her grandson Islwyn.
6 Mourning's End Part I Master 19 July 2005 The player is tasked by Arianwyn, leader of the rebel elves, to infiltrate the mourners of West Ardougne.
7 Mourning's End Part II Master 17 October 2005 The player discovers the Temple of Light and is tasked to simply restore its safeguards.
8 Song of the Elves Grandmaster 25 July 2019 The player joins a resistance in West Ardougne to overthrow King Lathas for his lies about the plague and collusion with the Iorwerth Clan, and discovers their true goal.

Related story quests - Waterfall Quest, Sheep Herder, Making History
Antagonists - King Tyras, King Lathas, Lord Iorwerth

Fairytale[edit | edit source]

Main article: Fairy Tale quest series
No Quest Difficulty Release date Synopsis
1 Fairytale I - Growing Pains Intermediate 27 February 2006 The player helps Martin the Master Gardener investigate why crops are producing less yields around Gielinor.
2 Fairytale II - Cure a Queen Experienced 11 July 2006 The player helps cure the Fairy Queen and discovers a conspiracy to take her throne.

Related story quests - Lost City
Main antagonist - Fairy Godfather

Fremennik[edit | edit source]

Main article: Fremennik series
No Quest Difficulty Release date Synopsis
1 The Fremennik Trials Intermediate 2 November 2004 The player undergoes several trials to become an honorary member of the Fremennik tribe.
2 The Fremennik Isles Experienced 6 February 2007 The player must help two islands set aside their differences to face the looming threat of the ice trolls of the Fremennik Isles.
3 Lunar Diplomacy Experienced 24 July 2006 The player attempts to bring peace between the Fremennik tribe and the mystical Moon Clan.
4 The Fremennik Exiles Master 26 September 2019 What starts as a simple investigation results in the player's exile from Rellekka. With the help of the chieftain, the player must regain the trust of the Council and save the Fremennik Province from an ancient threat.

Gnome[edit | edit source]

Main article: Gnome quest series
No Quest Difficulty Release date Synopsis
1 The Grand Tree Intermediate 12 December 2002 The player helps King Narnode Shareen to investigate the cause behind the deterioration of the Grand Tree's health.
2 The Eyes of Glouphrie Intermediate 15 August 2006 The player helps Brimstail with an ancient machine from the Gnomes' Golden Age, and discover a new threat from an exiled gnome.
3 The Path of Glouphrie Experienced 13 September 2023 The player helps King Bolren to investigate if Glouphrie is spying on him through his new pet.
4 Monkey Madness I Master 6 December 2004 The player helps King Narnode Shareen (again) to investigate the disappearance of the elite 10th Squad.
5 Monkey Madness II Grandmaster 6 May 2016 The player helps King Narnode Shareen investigate Glough's disappearance, where the player discovers plans of a large scale assault on the human kingdoms.

Related story quests - Tree Gnome Village, Waterfall Quest, While Guthix Sleeps
Antagonists - Glough, Glouphrie, Awowogei

Kharidian[edit | edit source]

No Quest Difficulty Release date Synopsis
1 Prince Ali Rescue Novice 28 February 2001 The player assists Hassan in the rescue of Prince Ali, the son of Al Kharid's Emir.
2 Icthlarin's Little Helper Intermediate 26 April 2005 The player stumbles upon a wanderer in the Kharidian Desert and suddenly find themselves in Sophanem with no memory of how they got there.
3 Contact! Experienced 10 January 2007 The player attempts to make contact with the Sophanites that are unable to leave Menaphos due to the lockdown of the city.
4 Beneath Cursed Sands Master 27 April 2022 The player helps Maisa investigate unusual happenings at the old necropolis and discover the Devourer's hand behind recent events in the Kharidian Desert.
5 Into the Tombs (MQ) Master 24 August 2022 With the blockage cleared at the Jaltevas Pyramid, the player must brave the dangers within to put a stop to Amascut's plans.

Main antagonist - Amascut

Kourend[edit | edit source]

Main article: Great Kourend quest series
No Quest Difficulty Release date Synopsis
1 X Marks the Spot Novice 7 February 2019 The player helps Veos, a treasure hunter from the distant Kingdom of Great Kourend, in finding a treasure somewhere around Lumbridge.
2 Client of Kourend Novice 20 April 2017 The player helps Veos with a client of his in gathering knowledge in Kourend's hierarchy and general social organisation.
3A The Queen of Thieves Intermediate 9 November 2017 The player helps Tomas Lawry investigate the Saviours of Kourend, a gang in Port Piscarilius that has been absorbing many of the other gangs in the city.
3B The Depths of Despair Intermediate 9 November 2017 The player helps Lord Kandur Hosidius in the search for his eldest son Artur.
3C The Ascent of Arceuus Intermediate 10 January 2019 The player investigates the death of a Citizen of Arceuus, who gained the secrets to immortality nearly a thousand years ago.
3D The Forsaken Tower Intermediate 10 January 2019 The player assists Undor in recovering the hammer of Dinh, a legendary smith, in order to repair the Doors of Dinh.
3E Tale of the Righteous Intermediate 19 April 2018 The player and Phileas Rimor investigate the claims of an old journal, delving into a conspiracy a thousand years in the making.
4 A Kingdom Divided Experienced 16 June 2021 As protests against the Kourend Council continue with increasing fervor, the player helps uncover the circumstances behind the death of Kourend's last king, the fate of his daughter, and the mastermind behind it all.

Main antagonists - Kourend Council, Xeric
Secondary antagonists - Xamphur, Disciples of Yama

Mahjarrat[edit | edit source]

No Quest Difficulty Release date Synopsis
1 The Dig Site Intermediate 9 July 2003 The player takes an archaeological exam in order to help excavate the digsite where the city of Saranthium once stood.
2 Hazeel Cult Novice 15 August 2002 The player is tasked by Sir Ceril Carnillean in recovering a family heirloom and discovers a plot by the cult to resurrect the Mahjarrat Hazeel.
3 Fight Arena Intermediate 23 July 2002 The player helps Lady Servil in rescuing her husband and son from the Fight Arena, who were kidnapped by General Khazard, a Mahjarrat.
4 Enakhra's Lament Experienced 23 January 2006 The player helps Lazim the sculptor with his artistic endeavours, only to discover a centuries-long rivalry between two Mahjarrat.
5 Temple of Ikov Intermediate 17 June 2002 The player helps Lucien recover the Staff of Armadyl, a legendary artefact closely guarded by the Guardians of Armadyl in the Temple of Ikov.
6 Defender of Varrock Experienced 21 February 2024 Ramblings of a drunkard prompt an investigation that results in the discovery of an impending invasion of Varrock.
7 Desert Treasure I Master 18 April 2005 The player helps the legendary archaeologist Asgarnia Smith in translating a stone tablet, resulting in a journey across the world to unseal the Ancient Pyramid.
8 Curse of the Empty Lord (MQ) Experienced 26 July 2005 The player stumbles upon Valdez the explorer and discovers the events leading up to the Betrayal of Zaros at the end of the Second Age.
9 The General's Shadow (MQ) Experienced 12 February 2007 The player is tasked by General Khazard to deliver a message to his scouts hidden in the Shadow Realm.
10 The Curse of Arrav Master 6 November 2024 The player teams up with Elias White and learns about the dark magic used to enslave Arrav. Together they attempt to undo it.
11 Devious Minds Experienced 19 December 2005 The player helps a monk craft a sword-bow, which is stolen by an assassin.
12 His Faithful Servants (MQ) Experienced 14 June 2023 The player recovers an artefact for the Strange Old Man at the Barrows.
13 Secrets of the North Master 11 January 2023 The player investigates a murder and inadvertently cross paths with the Mahjarrat.
14 Desert Treasure II - The Fallen Empire Grandmaster 26 July 2023 The player stumbles upon an Ancient Vault, uncovering many long-forgotten secrets of the Zarosian Empire as they endeavor to unlock it.
15 While Guthix Sleeps Grandmaster 10 July 2024 The player works with Crux Eqal, the Temple Knights, and the Guardians of Armadyl to try and stop Lucien from obtaining the Stone of Jas.

Miscellania[edit | edit source]

No Quest Difficulty Release date Synopsis
1 Throne of Miscellania Experienced 29 November 2004 The player must prove themselves worthy to succeed King Vargas as regent of Miscellania.
2 Royal Trouble Experienced 22 May 2006 The player investigates some robberies that occurred in Miscellania and Etceteria in order to stop both kingdoms from declaring war.

Myreque[edit | edit source]

No Quest Difficulty Release date Synopsis
1 In Search of the Myreque Intermediate 10 January 2005 The player helps a mysterious man named Vanstrom Klause deliver weapons to the Myreque, a resistance group seeking to liberate Morytania from the rule of the vampyres.
2 In Aid of the Myreque Intermediate 22 March 2006 The player helps the Myreque find a new secret base and establish friendly relations with the locals of Burgh de Rott.
3 Darkness of Hallowvale Experienced 4 September 2006 The player is ordered to make contact with the Myreque base in Meiyerditch and assist them with their reconnaissance.
4 A Taste of Hope Experienced 24 May 2018 The player investigates Vanstrom Klause's visit to the Theatre of Blood and sets out to craft a weapon capable of harming the trueborn vampyres.
5 Sins of the Father Master 4 June 2020 The player and the Myreque hatch a plan to kill Lowerniel Drakan and liberate Morytania.
6 The Blood Moon Rises [Upcoming] Grandmaster Early 2026 The player takes on Lowerniel Drakan in a battle which will decide the fate of Morytania.

Related story quests - Priest in Peril, Nature Spirit, A Night at the Theatre
Main antagonist - Lowerniel Drakan
Secondary antagonists - Vanstrom Klause, Ranis Drakan

Order of Wizards[edit | edit source]

No Quest Difficulty Release date Synopsis
1 Rune Mysteries Novice 1 December 2003 The player helps Duke Horacio deliver a talisman to Archmage Sedridor of the Wizards' Tower, resulting in the rediscovery of the rune essence mine.
2 Enter the Abyss (MQ) Intermediate 13 June 2005 The player helps a Mage of Zamorak gain access to the rune essence mine in exchange for knowledge on Abyssal Space.
3 Temple of the Eye Intermediate 23 March 2022 The player helps famed wizard Mia Persten in search of an ancient teleportation matrix to the runic altars.

Related story quests - Desert Treasure II

Penguin[edit | edit source]

No Quest Difficulty Release date Synopsis
1 Cold War Intermediate 27 January 2007 The player is tasked by Larry to investigate the dealings of penguins on an iceberg in the Lunar Sea.

Antagonist - Pescaling Pax

Pirate[edit | edit source]

No Quest Difficulty Release date Synopsis
1 Rum Deal Experienced 31 October 2005 The player helps Captain Braindeath in producing Braindeath 'rum' in order to quell a protest of zombie pirates.
2 Cabin Fever Experienced 7 February 2006 The player helps Bill Teach reach the pirate enclave of Mos Le'Harmless in one piece.
3 The Great Brain Robbery Experienced 6 March 2007 The player helps the monks of Harmony Island recover their brains and repel the invasion of zombie pirates.

Main antagonist - Rabid Jack
Secondary antagonist - Mi-Gor

Rag and Bone Man[edit | edit source]

No Quest Difficulty Release date Synopsis
1 Rag and Bone Man I Novice 10 April 2006 The player helps an Odd Old Man in Silvarea to collect some special bones.
2 Rag and Bone Man II Experienced 10 April 2006 The player helps the Odd Old Man collect even more bones from his wish-list.

Red Axe[edit | edit source]

No Quest Difficulty Release date Synopsis
1 The Giant Dwarf Intermediate 31 May 2005 The player must make amends with the dwarves of Keldagrim after crashing a boat into the statue of King Alvis.
2 Forgettable Tale... Intermediate 26 July 2005 The player helps Commander Veldaban investigate a lead by a drunken, kebab-loving dwarf about The Red Axe's activities.

Main antagonists - The Red Axe
Secondary antagonists - Hreidmar, Colonel Grimsson

Temple Knight[edit | edit source]

No Quest Difficulty Release date Synopsis
1 Recruitment Drive Novice 27 June 2005 The player is put forward as a candidate to join the Temple Knights and must pass the organisation's test to be initiated.
2 Wanted! Intermediate 17 October 2005 The player discovers that they did not join the Temple Knights properly, but is given knowledge of a loophole that requires them to help apprehend a crazed criminal with access to powerful magicks.
3 Sea Slug Intermediate 9 September 2002 The player investigates a strange occurrence on the Fishing Platform.
4 The Slug Menace Intermediate 20 September 2006 The player investigates the ongoings of Witchaven on behalf of the Temple Knights.

Main antagonist - Mother Mallum
Secondary antagonists - Solus Dellagar, Slug Prince, Eustace Hobb

Troll[edit | edit source]

No Quest Difficulty Release date Synopsis
1 Death Plateau Novice 9 August 2004 The player helps the Burthorpe Imperial Guard find a safe way to reach Death Plateau in order to raid the troll camp located there.
2 Troll Stronghold Intermediate 24 August 2004 The player helps Dunstan rescue his son Godric from the Troll Stronghold prison after a failed raid on Death Plateau.
3 Eadgar's Ruse Intermediate 5 October 2004 The player assists Sanfew in acquiring a rare herb used in troll cooking in order to progress with the stone circle's purification ritual.
4 My Arm's Big Adventure Experienced 23 October 2006 The player assists My Arm on behalf of Burntmeat in finding a new source of goutweed for the trolls.
5 Making Friends With My Arm Master 6 September 2018 The player helps My Arm establish relations with the trolls of Weiss, and help him woo the daughter of Mother, the tribe's leader.

Twilight Emissaries[edit | edit source]

No Quest Difficulty Release date Synopsis
1 Children of the Sun Novice 10 January 2024 The player disrupts a plot to attack a Varlamore delegation visiting Varrock.
2 Twilight's Promise Intermediate 20 March 2024 The player and Prince Itzla Arkan delve deeper into the assassination attempt against Servius, Teokan of Ralos.
3 The Heart of Darkness Experienced 25 September 2024 The player digs deeper into the Twilight Emissaries and sect of Ranul to figure out who's behind the attempts on Servius' life.
4 The Final Dawn [Upcoming] Master July 2025 The player explores the history of Varlamore and the fate of the mysterious Old Ones.

Main antagonists - Twilight Emissaries

Twisted Tales[edit | edit source]

Main article: Twisted Tales

Though officially recognised as a series of quests, they are considered as standalone quests when sorting the Quest List interface by "Storyline".

No Quest Difficulty Release date Synopsis
1 Getting Ahead Intermediate 25 November 2020 The player is tasked to slay a beast for a farm owner and return with its head as proof for their reward.
2 The Garden of Death Intermediate 30 November 2022 The player happens upon an abandoned campsite and uncovers ruins of an extinct race.
