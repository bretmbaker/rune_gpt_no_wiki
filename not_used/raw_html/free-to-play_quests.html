There are a total of 22 free-to-play quests in Old School RuneScape, offering a total of 44 Quest points.

Below is a list of all free-to-play quests. While the depicted difficulties and lengths are the averages of each quest, these can still vary for every player. Some recommendations presented in quest guides may only represent a single point of view out of many possible strategies. Balance these walkthroughs with your own abilities and your knowledge of RuneScape.
