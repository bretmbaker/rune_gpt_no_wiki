The Colossal Wyrm Agility Course is an Agility course found in the Avium Savannah which involves running around the scaffolding that surrounds the Colossal Wyrm Remains. Before attempting the course, players must first speak to Worm Tongue, an anteater, about helping him gather termites around the scaffolding in order to preserve the wyrm's remains.

There are two courses available to players beginning at 50 Agility for the basic course, and 62 Agility for the advanced course. It is a lower intensity course, with fewer obstacles each with longer animations, averaging about 1 minute per lap.

Players can speak to Worm Tongue to see statistics about the course, including the total number of laps the player has completed as well as their fastest lap completion time, along with global statistics.

Getting there[edit | edit source]

  • Use a Colossal wyrm teleport scroll, bought from Worm Tongue or the Grand Exchange. This is the quickest method to get there.
  • Using the Quetzal Transport System after building the nearby Landing Site.
  • Using the fairy ring code AJP to Avium Savannah and then running south.

The course[edit | edit source]

The course can be started at the north-east ladder, located near the Wyrm's head after talking to Worm Tongue. Both courses share a common entrance ladder and first tightrope obstacle. After the first obstacle, players with 62 Agility can climb a ladder to complete the Advanced course for higher experience rates and 3 obstacles unique from the basic course. The courses merge for a final zipline, granting a total of 504.1 experience for basic lap completion, and 749.6 experience for advanced lap completion.

Players never fail obstacles on either course. With perfect attentiveness, the basic course can be completed in 0:54.6 for a total of 62 completions per hour (including 3.6 seconds of downtime between laps) for a maximum of 31,181 xp/hr. The advanced course can be completed in 0:59.4 for a total of 57 completions per hour (including 3.6 seconds of downtime between laps) for a maximum of 42,834 xp/hr.

Termites[edit | edit source]

While completing either course, termites will occasionally spawn, which the player will automatically scoop up given that they complete the obstacle that precedes the termites. The shared and basic portions of the course yield 8-10 termites per scoop, while the advanced portion of the course yields 12-15 per scoop; players may also scoop up 22–28 blessed bone shards in the process. Should players take roughly one minute to complete each lap of the advanced course, they can accumulate roughly 340 shards per hour with maximum efficiency and focus.

Rewards[edit | edit source]

Main article: Worm Tongue's Wares

Termites collected from running the Colossal Wyrm Agility Course can be exchanged at Worm Tongue's Wares. The following rewards are available:

Reward Cost in Description
Amylase pack 100 A pack containing 100 crystals of amylase.
Colossal wyrm teleport scroll 40 A teleport to the Colossal Wyrm Remains in the Avium Savannah.
Graceful crafting kit 650 Gives your graceful kit a Varlamore-themed makeover.[1]
Calcified acorn 900 A brittle looking acorn. Perhaps a squirrel would be interested in this?

Players will need a total of 1,590 termites to complete the Colossal Wyrm Agility tab of the collection log (amylase pack is not included on the collection log).

  1. ^ Requires full graceful set, no individual pieces

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
9 October 2024
(update)

The following changes have been applied to the advanced course:

  • Obstacles which gave 62 Agility experience now give 70.
  • Agility experience rewarded on completion has been increased from 325 to 358.
  • The quantity of termites being scooped up when completing Advanced Course obstacles has been increased from 8-10 to 12-15.
2 October 2024
(update)
  • The cost of amylase packs has been reduced from 200 termites to 100 termites.
  • The Colossal Wyrm Agility section of the collection log now turns green upon completion.
