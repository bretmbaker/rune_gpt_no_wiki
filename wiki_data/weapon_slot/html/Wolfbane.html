Wolfbane is a dagger given as a quest item reward in Priest in Peril. It is obtained from Drezel after completion of the quest.

It is used to keep the citizens of Canifis from transforming into werewolves when the player attacks them. It has the same bonuses as a mithril dagger except it also gives a prayer bonus. Wolfbane cannot be poisoned. It can be reclaimed by talking to Drezel in the dungeon below Paterdomus.

Killing a werewolf using wolfbane is an Easy Morytania Diary task.
