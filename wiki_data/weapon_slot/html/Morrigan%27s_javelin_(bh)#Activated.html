Morrigan's javelin (bh) is a ranged throwing weapon requiring 75 Ranged to wield, and is purchased from the Bounty Hunter Store for 550 points. Unlike other javelins, it is not used as ammunition for ballistae, nor is it stackable, effectively providing unlimited "charges".

As with all Ancient Warriors' equipment from Bounty Hunter, it is initially purchased in inactive form, and must be charged with 10,000,000 coins before it can be used by the player. Upon dying in PvP, the coins used to activate the item are given to the killer, and players are left with the inactive item.

In addition, it can only be used within Daimon's Crater, Castle Wars, Clan Wars, and Soul Wars; attempting to attack anything outside the listed areas will result in the player being interrupted from doing so.

Special attack[edit | edit source]

Morrigan's javelin has a special attack, Phantom Strike, that consumes 50% of the player's special attack energy and applies a damage over time effect to the opponent after the special attack is performed. This special attack has no effect on NPCs. The special attack has 150% accuracy.

Every tick (0.6 seconds) after the special attack is performed, the opponent will take an additional 10 hitpoints of damage until 75% of the damage dealt by the special attack has been delivered. When this occurs, the target will receive a message in their chatbox stating "You start to bleed as a result of the javelin strike." Subsequent damage will result in the chatbox stating "You continue to bleed as a result of the javelin strike."
