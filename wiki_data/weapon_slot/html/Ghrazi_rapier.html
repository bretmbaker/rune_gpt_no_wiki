The Ghrazi rapier is a one-handed stab weapon obtainable as a rare drop from the Theatre of Blood. It requires level 80 Attack to wield. It provides the highest strength and stab bonus out of any weapon for its attack speed. This makes the rapier tied with the Blade of saeldor and the Inquisitor's mace for the melee weapon with the highest damage output for general use, although it is outperformed by the Osmumten's fang against high defence monsters and Scythe of vitur against large monsters.

The rapier can be combined with a holy ornament kit to make a Saradomin-themed holy Ghrazi rapier. This cosmetic upgrade won't change the stats in any way, but it will make the rapier count as a Saradomin item in the God Wars Dungeon.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
11 May 2022
(update)

The Ghrazi rapier now requires level 80 Attack to wield instead of level 75 Attack.

21 June 2018
(update)

The stabbing animation changed from the standard sword variant to a unique one after player feedback.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The name of this weapon refers to House Ghrazi, a vampyre house known for their aggression and ferocity in battle.
  • Despite its examine text stating the rapier has been smeared in blood, there is no visible blood on the rapier when equipped or dropped.
  • Three Ghrazi rapiers, along with an uncharged scythe of vitur, can be seen leaning against a wall in Death's Office.
