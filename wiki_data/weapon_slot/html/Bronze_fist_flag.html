The bronze fist flag can be purchased from Honest Jimmy, who oversees the Trouble Brewing minigame in Mos Le'Harmless, for 3,000 pieces of eight. Once purchased, it cannot be resold to the shop. It can be stored in a magic wardrobe in a player-owned house.
