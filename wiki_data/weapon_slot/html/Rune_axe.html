The rune axe is the fifth best axe in the game, behind the crystal axe, infernal axe, 3rd age axe and dragon axe. The infernal, 3rd age and dragon variants do not differ from each other in their Woodcutting capabilities. Therefore, when it comes to obtaining logs by members, this runite version - together with the gilded axe - constitutes the third best tier of axes. For free-to-play players, those two are the best axes available.

Like any axe, its primary use is as a tool to cut trees in order to obtain logs and gain experience in the Woodcutting skill. A level of 41 in that skill is required to use this axe to chop down trees. As with all axes, the rune axe can be used for that purpose even when it is not wielded but merely carried in the player's inventory. The success rate for getting a set of logs is approximately 350% that of a bronze axe, or 17% better than an adamant axe. It can also be used as a weapon for melee combat; the player must have an Attack level of 40 to wield this axe.

Players can make a rune axe with the Smithing skill at level 86 using one runite bar, granting 75 Smithing experience. It can be purchased from Perry's Chop-chop Shop for 40,960.
