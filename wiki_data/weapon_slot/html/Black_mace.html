A black mace is a melee weapon stronger than the steel mace, requiring 10 Attack to wield. It is only obtainable in free-to-play by killing dark warriors, from beginner clue scrolls or from Camdozaal Vault lockboxes. Members can also loot it from bronze chests in the Shade Catacombs in the Shades of Mort'ton minigame or from level 106 Cyclops in the Warriors' Guild basement.

As with most maces, it has a Prayer bonus and like all black equipment it cannot be made from raw materials with Smithing.
