A holy handegg is an item obtained during the 2018 Easter event. It is a reward for defeating the Colossal Chocco Chicken.

With the item in your inventory, you can right-click Use on it, and click another player. If the player has both of their hands empty, they will catch the egg, and it will end up in their weapon slot. Ironmen cannot throw the egg to other players, nor can they have the egg be thrown to them.

After the easter event was removed, they became available from Diango's Toy Store.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The name is likely a reference to the Holy Hand Grenade from Monty Python and the Holy Grail.
  • The term "handegg" is likely a reference to how people from England refer to American Football.
