A Cursed goblin staff is one of the three weapons released during Old School RuneScape's fourth event: the Goblin Invasion. It was a drop from the goblin lieutenants found in the cave that the Varrock scout led to.

It can now be bought from Diango for 45 coins.

The Cursed goblin staff is useful for players who want to train their Magic skill through splashing. A staff for autocast and a magic bonus of at most -64 is required for splashing. Normally a player can use green d'hide vambraces to achieve -64 magic bonus while wielding an elemental staff. Players who cannot equip the vambraces can still splash with this staff, at the cost of using more runes.
