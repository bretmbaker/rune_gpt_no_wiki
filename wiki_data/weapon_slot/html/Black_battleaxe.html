The black battleaxe is a battleaxe stronger than the steel battleaxe, but weaker than the Mithril battleaxe. It requires 10 Attack to wield. Players can purchase this battleaxe from Brian's Battleaxe Bazaar in Port Sarim. It can also be received as a drop from a Dark beast, and as a reward from an easy clue scroll.

As with all black and white equipment, the black battleaxe cannot be made from raw materials using the Smithing skill.
