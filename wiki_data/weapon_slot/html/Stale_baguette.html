The stale baguette is a rare reward from random events. It shares the same stat bonuses with all the other fun weapons. This item can be stored in the fancy dress box of a costume room.

There is a 1/64 chance of receiving the stale baguette in place of a regular baguette when offered one by the sandwich lady (making the effective drop rate 1/448, as there are seven possible foods that can be offered). When this happens, a message in the chatbox will state: The baguette looks tougher than expected! There is also a 1/256 chance of receiving one when opening a mystery box, obtained by participating in the Quiz Master's game. This instead prompts the message: Inside the box you find a baguette! Make of it what you will.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The examine text is a pun on the French word for bread (pain).
  • The look and animation of the stale baguette bear a resemblance to the baguette used by the sandwich lady NPC before the September 2014 random events update.
