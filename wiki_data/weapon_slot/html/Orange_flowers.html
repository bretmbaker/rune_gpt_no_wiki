Orange flowers are a type of flower that can be harvested after planting mithril or adamant seeds.

When planting the seeds, players have an option to either pick the flowers (and obtaining a random coloured flower) or leave them planted on the ground. A player only has one chance to pick their flowers; if they refuse to do so, they will not get the option again unless they plant another seed. Flowers that are left on the ground will stay there temporarily for around 5 minutes.

Flowers can be wielded in the weapon slot, and are classified as a fun weapon in legacy duels within the Emir's Arena because it gives the wielding player negative bonuses.
