Rune darts are Ranged throwing weapons that are only available to members. Rune darts require level 40 Ranged to wield. They can be obtained by either using the Smithing and Fletching skills or trading with another player. Rune darts can be poisoned and can be wielded with a shield or god book for protection.

To create rune darts the player must have completed the Tourist Trap quest and have at least level 89 Smithing and level 81 Fletching. Players can smith rune bars into rune dart tips. Each rune bar creates 10 rune dart tips and gives the player 75 Smithing experience. Feathers then must be added to create the rune darts. 18.8 Fletching experience is rewarded per rune dart.

Smithing and fletching 10 rune darts while in Varrock is required to complete the Elite Varrock Diary.

The rune dart's ranged bonuses has been changed:

  • Ranged Attack bonus decreased from +15 to 0.
  • Ranged Strength bonus increased from +14 to +26.

(update)
  • The extra strong poisoned variant was renamed from "Rune dart(+)" to "Rune dart(p+)".
  • The super strong poisoned variant was renamed from "Rune dart(s)" to "Rune dart(p++)".
