The mithril pickaxe is a pickaxe that is weaker than the adamant pickaxe. However, since both it and the black pickaxe give the player an opportunity to mine every 5 ticks, there is no benefit to it over the black pickaxe, unless combat is also required. It requires an Attack level of 20 to wield, and a Mining level of 21 to mine with. It can be used in combat or in mining to mine rocks. It is also one of the lightest pickaxes in the game (although again, heavier than the black pickaxe), making it ideal for activities such as training Runecraft via the Abyss.
