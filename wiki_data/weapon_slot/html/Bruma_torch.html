The bruma torch is a possible reward from the Reward Cart upon subduing the Wintertodt. This item can be stored in the magic wardrobe of a costume room.

When worn during the Wintertodt boss fight, it acts as a warm clothing item, and can also be used as a replacement for a tinderbox for the purpose of lighting braziers, regardless of whether the torch is equipped or in the player's inventory.

Around the game world, the torch acts as a light source that can be equipped. It has no possibility of triggering the explosions that hit the player for 12 damage in the Lumbridge Swamp Caves. It can also be used in place of a lit torch when smoking traps.

Players can trade in bruma torches for an extra supply crate or 50 burnt pages via Ignisia.

If a player has three bruma torches in their bank and/or inventory and/or Magic wardrobe in a POH, searching the Reward Cart will give 2-3 torstol seeds when it would have given another torch.[1] Currently the Grand Exchange price of 50 burnt pages is 80,950 and the price of a torstol seed is 7,948.

It is generally more beneficial to exchange spare torches for 50 burnt pages, unless you are an ironman who wishes to receive torstol seeds. Note Wintertodt's unique rewards cannot be received from the extra supply crate.

The torch can be swapped via the "Swap" option, converting it to its off-hand variant so that it can be equipped in the shield slot.
