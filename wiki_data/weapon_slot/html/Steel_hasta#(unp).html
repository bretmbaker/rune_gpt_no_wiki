The steel hasta is a one-handed melee members weapon, similar to the spear. To wield this weapon the player needs at least 5 Attack and the Smithing section of the Barbarian Training miniquest. To obtain a steel hasta the player can either create one using the Smithing skill or trade with another player. It can be poisoned.
