The dragon cane is a reward from elite Treasure Trails that requires level 60 Attack to wield. This weapon has similar stats to the dragon mace (including the +5 Prayer bonus), although no quest completion is required to wield the dragon cane (while completion of Heroes' Quest is needed to wield the dragon mace). Unlike the mace however, the Dragon Cane has an attack speed of 5 ticks (3.0s).

Unlike other canes, which use the Combat Options of maces, the dragon cane uses the combat options of polestaves.

As with most dragon equipment and all Treasure Trails rewards, the dragon cane cannot be made from raw materials using the Smithing skill.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room.
