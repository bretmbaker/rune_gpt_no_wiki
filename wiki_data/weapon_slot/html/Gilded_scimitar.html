The Gilded scimitar is a possible reward from elite and master Treasure Trails.

It has the same stats as a rune scimitar, requiring 40 Attack to wield.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
11 April 2019
(update)

The gilded scimitar can now be stored in a player's treasure chest.

19 May 2016
(update)

The item was made available to free-to-play players.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Prior to an update on 19 May 2016, gilded scimitars were not available to free-to-play players.
