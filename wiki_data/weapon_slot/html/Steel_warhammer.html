The steel warhammer is a warhammer stronger than the iron warhammer. Unlike other steel weapons, it requires 5 Strength to wield. Players can make a steel warhammer with the Smithing skill at level 39 using three steel bars, giving 112.5 Smithing experience.

Player can purchase this warhammer from Vigr's Warhammers in Keldagrim and Skulgrimen's Battle Gear in Rellekka (completion of The Fremennik Trials is required). The base price of a steel warhammer at NPC stores is 832 coins.

A steel warhammer is one of the weapons requested during the In Search of the Myreque quest.

The steel warhammer can be used as a substitute for a regular hammer when banging the big door at the entrance of Bandos' Stronghold in the God Wars Dungeon.
