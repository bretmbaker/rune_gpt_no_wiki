The dragon axe (or) is a dragon axe with a Trailblazer tool ornament kit added onto it. The kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the axe. Players can additionally select the "Change-Style" option to alternate between the Trailblazer Reloaded variant and the original Trailblazer variant.

While the axe is untradeable in its ornamented state, it can be reverted anytime, returning the dragon axe and kit.

This version of the dragon axe can be used for the master clue step in the Enchanted Valley and stored in its respective STASH unit.
