The Bronze mace is a mace made of bronze. It is the weakest mace in the game, and can be equipped at level 1 Attack. A bronze mace can be created at level 2 Smithing using 1 bronze bar. This item spawns in Sorceress' house in southern Al Kharid, and can be bought at Flynn's Mace Market in Falador.
