The rune crossbow (or) is a rune crossbow with a shattered relics variety ornament kit added onto it. The kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the crossbow.

Whilst in its ornamented state, the item can be reverted anytime, returning the rune crossbow and kit.
