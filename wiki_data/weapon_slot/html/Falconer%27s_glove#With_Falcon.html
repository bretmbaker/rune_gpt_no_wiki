The Falconer's glove is used in the Hunter skill in the falconry hunting technique in the Piscatoris falconry area, which is located east of the Piscatoris Hunter Area. While wearing the glove, a player has a gyr falcon on their hand which they can send to catch spotted kebbits, dashing kebbits and dark kebbits. While wearing the glove, players cannot wear any other gloves, bracelets, weapons or shields.

A player can temporarily borrow the glove and falcon from Matthias for a fee of 500 coins, and it will be taken away from the player when they leave the fenced area. The fee must be paid every time the player returns to the area later to hunt more kebbits. Alternatively, players may pay a one-time fee of 500,000 coins for unlimited free glove rentals in the future. Players need not worry about logging out with the glove on, it will remain with them when they log back in.

When attempting to leave the area with the glove equipped, the player will state "I should return the glove I borrowed from the falconer before leaving.". Attempting to remove the glove yourself, will give the message You should speak to Matthias to get this removed safely., the Basic quetzal whistle and its upgrades bypass this restriction with the text As you leave, Matthias' falcon flies back to him.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The glove provides a minimal +1 boost to both crush and slash defence, which is odd considering the player cannot participate in combat inside the falconry area, and cannot leave the area with the glove.
  • The weight of the falcon is equal to the weight of the actual glove, as, when the falcon has hunted a kebbit, the empty glove drops to a weight of 0.907 kg.
