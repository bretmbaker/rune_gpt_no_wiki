The '24-carat' sword is a fun weapon first obtained as a reward from the 2020 Easter event by using the carrot sword on the paint buckets at the top of Duke Rabbacio's castle. Upon doing so, a message in the chatbox states You dunk your sword into the paint turning it into a 24 Carot Gold Sword!

It is classified as a fun weapon in legacy duels within the Emir's Arena because it gives the player negative bonuses.

If destroyed, a replacement can be obtained from Diango in Draynor Village or from the toy box of a costume room, as part of the 2020: Easter Carrot Swords set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The carat is a measurement of the purity of gold divisible into 24 parts. 24-carat gold is pure gold, or at least 99.95% pure.
