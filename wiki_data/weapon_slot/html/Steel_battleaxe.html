The steel battleaxe is a battleaxe stronger than the iron battleaxe. The steel battleaxe requires 5 Attack to wield. Players can make a steel battleaxe with the Smithing skill at level 40 using 3 steel bars, giving the player 112.5 experience. Players can purchase this battleaxe from Brian's Battleaxe Bazaar in Port Sarim or Bob's Brilliant Axes in Lumbridge.
