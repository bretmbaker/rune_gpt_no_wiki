Dragon darts are a type of Ranged throwing weapons. Dragon darts require level 60 Ranged to wield. They can be obtained by using the Fletching skill, trading with another player, or via dragon implings. Dragon darts can be poisoned and can be wielded with a shield or god book for protection, or added into a toxic blowpipe.

To create dragon darts the player must have at least level 95 Fletching. Players must obtain dragon dart tips (these cannot be smithed) and then attach feathers to the dart tips. 250 Fletching experience is given per set of dragon darts created.

Fletching some dragon darts while in the Bedabin Camp is required to complete the Elite Desert Diary.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
30 June 2021
(update)

The dragon dart's ranged bonuses has been changed:

  • Ranged Attack bonus decreased from +18 to 0.
  • Ranged Strength bonus increased from +20 to +35.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • As they do not have the colour of Orikalkum, they are most likely made from parts of a dragon instead, similar to Dragon arrowtips.
  • Unlike other darts, Dragon darts have a unique projectile animation when thrown or shot out of a toxic blowpipe.
  • Dragon darts are the only dragon weapon aside from the dragon cane that does not posses a special attack.
