The abyssal dagger (bh) is a weapon that requires level 70 in Attack to wield. It is created by using an abyssal dagger imbue scroll on an abyssal dagger, which is purchased as a reward from Bounty Hunter. As with most daggers, it can be poisoned.

In comparison to the regular dagger, the imbued dagger has a +10 increased stab bonus, and the special attack's 15% damage reduction is reduced to 5%, though the cost is raised to 35% of the special bar from the original 25%.

In addition, it can only be used within Daimon's Crater, Castle Wars, Clan Wars, and Soul Wars; attempting to attack anything outside the listed areas will result in the player being interrupted from doing so. If player dies in Bounty Hunter they lose abyssal dagger (bh) but keep the scroll.

Special attack[edit | edit source]

The abyssal dagger (bh) has a special attack, Abyssal Puncture, which hits twice in quick succession with a 25% increase in accuracy and 5% reduced damage, consuming 35% of the player's special attack energy. A single attack roll is performed, meaning that either both hits are successful, or both hits miss. The damage calculation rolls against the opponent's slash defence.
