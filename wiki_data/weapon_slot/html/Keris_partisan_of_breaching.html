The keris partisan of breaching is a one-handed spear requiring 80 Attack to equip. It is made by attaching the breach of the scarab on the keris partisan. As with the keris partisan, weapon poisons cannot be applied to the partisan.

The keris partisan of breaching retains the 33% bonus damage effects of the keris. The Breach of the scarab attached to the keris partisan grants an additional 33% accuracy[1] against all Kalphites and Scabarites, alongside the inherited 1/51 chance of puncturing a hole in their exoskeleton that inflicts triple damage. When this special effect occurs, the chatbox will display the message: You slip your weapon through a gap in the creature's chitin, landing a vicious blow.

Unlike the other two jewelled keris partisans, this partisan can be used outside the Tombs of Amascut raid. However, it does not possess a special attack.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
20 March 2024
(update)

Players may no longer attach jewels obtained from the Tombs of Amascut to the partisan until they have level 80 Attack.

21 September 2022
(update)

When players deal a critical hit to kalphites and scabarites with the keris partisan, the chat message was updated from "You slip your weapon through a chink in the creature's chitin, landing a vicious blow." to "You slip your weapon through a gap in the creature's chitin, landing a vicious blow."
