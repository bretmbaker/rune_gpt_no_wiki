The gilded axe is a cosmetic variant of the rune axe that can be obtained as a rare reward from elite and master Treasure Trails.

Players must have an Attack level of 40 to wield the gilded axe as a weapon and a Woodcutting level of 41 to use it to chop down trees.

Unlike other axes, the gilded axe cannot be converted into a felling axe.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room.
