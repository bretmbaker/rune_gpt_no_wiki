The 3rd age longsword is a part of the 3rd age melee set and was released as part of the Treasure Trail Expansion on 12 June 2014. It is a possible reward from elite and master Treasure Trails and cannot be made through the Smithing skill. The 3rd age longsword is currently the strongest longsword in-game, surpassing the dragon longsword. It requires 65 Attack to wield.

Despite being a longsword, the 3rd age longsword uses two-handed sword combat styles, giving it a crush attack instead of a stab attack style.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room.
