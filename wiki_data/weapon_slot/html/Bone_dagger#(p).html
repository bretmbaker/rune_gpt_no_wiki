Bone daggers can be bought from Nardok's Bone Weapons in the Dorgeshuun Mines outside Dorgesh-Kaan for 2,000 coins. They can also be poisoned.

Its stats are the same as those of the iron dagger.

Special attack[edit | edit source]

The bone dagger has a special attack, Backstab, (75%) which will never miss as long as the target has not been HIT previously or you weren't the last one to damage the target. It lowers the target's Defence by the amount of damage dealt, similar to the Bandos godsword's and Dorgeshuun crossbow's special attacks. The defence lowering is only applied when the target doesn't currently have their defence levels lowered. [1] Backstab's accuracy is not guaranteed on Kephri upon entering her room, nor against Duke Sucellus after he is woken up.

While the weapon can be used prior to the Death to the Dorgeshuun quest, the special attack can only be used after completing it.
