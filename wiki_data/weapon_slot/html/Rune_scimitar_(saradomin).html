The rune scimitar (saradomin) is a rune scimitar with a rune scimitar ornament kit (saradomin) attached to it. The attached ornament kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the scimitar. It costs 28,795 for both the items required to make the ornamented scimitar. This item will provide protection from Saradominist forces in the God Wars Dungeon when equipped.

While the scimitar is untradeable in its ornamented state, the weapon can be dismantled anytime, returning the tradeable ornament kit and scimitar.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room.

  • Ultimate Ironman will retain this in their inventory after death.
