The crystal grail is obtained as a drop from killing the powerful rabbit located in the cave in the Gwenith Hunter area north of Prifddinas, requiring completion of Song of the Elves to access.

This item is purely cosmetic. When equipped, it causes the player to sway around when standing or walking to mimic drunkenness, similar to the ale of the gods.
