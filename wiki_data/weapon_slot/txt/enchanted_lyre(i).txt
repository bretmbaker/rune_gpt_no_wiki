[Weapon: Enchanted Lyre(I)]


Anenchanted lyre(i)is anenchanted lyrethat contains infiniteteleportcharges toRellekka. When thehard Fremennik Diaryis completed, the lyre can also teleport toWaterbirth Island, and gains the ability to teleport toJatizsoandNeitiznotafter completion of theelite Fremennik Diary.
Players can imbue their un-enchantedlyreafter completion ofThe Fremennik Trialsby offeringFossegrimena one-off offering of 1,000raw sharks,raw bass,raw manta rays, andraw sea turtles, costing2,670,000 coins. Fossegrimen will accept both noted and un-noted offerings, and they can be given in increments; she will inform players of the remaining amount required to imbue the lyre. Should players lose the imbued lyre, they can obtain another un-enchanted lyre and summon Fossegrimen, where she will imbue the lyre free of charge.
