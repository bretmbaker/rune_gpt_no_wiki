[Weapon: Opal Machete]


Theopal macheteis a machete which cuts at a faster rate than a normalmachete, but is still outclassed byjadeandred topaz machetes.
It can be obtained either fromGabooty'sstore with 600trading sticks(500 if you are wearing any tier of theKaramja gloves), looting aninja impling, or bringing 300 trading sticks, 3 cut or uncut opals, and 1gout tubertoSafta DocinTai Bwo Wannai. The opal machete can be used as a weapon but is primarily used to cut jungle in theTai Bwo Wannai Cleanupminigame. It has no requirements to wield though cuttinglight,medium, anddense junglerequires 10, 20, and 35Woodcuttingto harvest respectively.
