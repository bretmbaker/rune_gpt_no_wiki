[Weapon: Black Longsword]


Theblack longswordis alongswordstronger than thesteel longsword, but weaker than themithril longsword. Like all otherblack weapons, it requires 10Attackto wield, and cannot be made using theSmithingskill. Players can purchase this longsword from theVarrock SwordshopinVarrock.
The black longsword and the steel longsword have the same strength bonus. Black longswords have the same bonuses as awhite longsword, other than the +1prayer bonusthe latter offers.
