[Weapon: Master Wand]


Themaster wandrequires 60Magicto wield. It can be obtained by either exchangingpizazz pointsat theMage Training Arenaor buying one from another player. It is one of ten weapons able toautocast Ancient Magicks, and one of only four weapons able to autocast both Ancient Magicks andArceuus combat spells. This means the wand is able to autocast the Ancient, Arceuus and Standard spellbooks.
A master wand can be upgraded to aKodai wandif the player has aKodai insignia, obtained from theChambers of Xeric.
