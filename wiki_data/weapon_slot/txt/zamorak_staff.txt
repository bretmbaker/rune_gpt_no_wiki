[Weapon: Zamorak Staff]


TheZamorak staffis agod staffaligned withZamorak, obtainable after completingMage Arena Iminiquestby defeatingKolodion. Like the other God staves, this staff requires 60Magicto acquire and wield.
After the miniquest, players pick a god to worship to receive agod capeand the god staff corresponding to that god. If a different god is chosen, the staff can be purchased from theChamber guardianfor80,000coins. This staff and corresponding runes are required to castFlames of Zamorakfrom thestandard spellbook.
