Achaotic handeggis an item obtained during the2018 Easter event. It is a reward for defeating theColossal Chocco Chicken.
With the item in your inventory, you can right-clickUseon it, and click another player. If the player has their right hand free, they will catch the egg, and it will end up in their weapon slot.Ironmencannot throw the egg to other players, nor can they have the egg be thrown to them.
