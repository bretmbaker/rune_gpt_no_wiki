[Weapon: Maple Blackjack]


Themaple blackjackis amembers onlymelee weaponmainly used to lure/knockoutNPCs. Level 30Thievingis required to wield this weapon. To obtain one, the player must have startedThe Feudquest by talking toAli Morrisane.
There are three NPCs that can be knocked out:Villagers,BanditsandMenaphite Thugs, all located in Pollnivneach. While there is no level requirement to knock out a villager, the Bandits require level 45Thieving(or 55 for scimitar wielding ones),  and the Menaphite Thugs level 65Thieving. When fighting with a blackjack, there is a slight chance tostunthe opponent.
