[Weapon: Pearl Barbarian Rod]


Apearl barbarian rodis an item that can be purchased for 150Molch pearlsfromAlry the AngleronMolch island. It is an equipable version of theBarbarian rod, and can only be purchased if the relevant part of theBarbarian Trainingminiquestis completed withOtto Godblessed. It can only be used in thefishing spotsatOtto's Grotto, andMount Quidamortemin theKebos Lowlands. Since barbarian fishing gives marginalStrengthandAgilityexperiencewith each catch, it is more efficient than other training methods such as fly fishing.
This rod can be stored in theTackle boxand anyMagic Wardrobein a player-owned house.
