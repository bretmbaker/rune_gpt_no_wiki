[Weapon: Warped Sceptre#Uncharged]


Thewarped sceptreis apowered staffrequiring 62Magicto wield, available as a drop fromwarped terrorbirdsorwarped tortoiseswith level 56Slayer. Like most powered staves, it is only tradeable when it is fully uncharged.
Its cast speed gives it an improved damage output compared to theIban's staff(at 81 or higher Magic), but less than thetrident of the seas. This is particularly useful for midgameironmen playerswho are unable to killCave krakens.
