[Weapon: Noose Wand]


Thenoose wandis used in theHunterskill to catchkebbitswhile tracking. The kebbits caught through tracking arepolar kebbits,common kebbits,feldip weasels,desert devils, andrazor-backed kebbits.
Once a kebbit has been tracked down, the player must equip the wand to catch it; without the noose wand, a Kebbit will simply be scared off. The noose wand serves no purpose when huntingHerbiboar. The noose wand can also be used to slice bananas.
