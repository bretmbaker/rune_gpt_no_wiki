[Weapon: Steel Knife#(P+)]


Thesteel knifeis athrowing knifethat requires aRangedlevel of 5 to wield. It is stronger than aniron knifebut weaker than ablack knife. To create steel knives, the player must have at least 37Smithing. Eachsteel barwill create 5 steel knives and give the player 37.5 Smithing experience. They can bepoisonedand can be wielded with ashieldorgod bookfor protection.
Throwing knives are among the fastest weapons in the game (along withdarts), and are commonly used forPKingor training Ranged. Steel knives can be purchased fromMartin Thwait's Lost and Foundshop in theRogues' Denwith 50Thievingand 50Agility. A single steel knife also spawns in the cave of the Kendal located north of theMountain Camp.
