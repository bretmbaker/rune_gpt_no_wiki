[Weapon: Oak Blackjack(O)]


Anoak blackjack(o)is amembers-onlymelee weaponmainly used to lure/knockoutNPCs. You must have anAttackandThievinglevel of 10 to wield this blackjack. To obtain one, the player must have completed the blackjack section of theRogue Traderminigame.
There are three NPCs that can be lured/knocked out with this blackjack:villagers,banditsandMenaphite Thugs, all located inPollnivneach. However for luring/knocking out these NPCs, the player must have the rightThievinglevel: level 30, 45, and 65, respectively. When fighting with a blackjack, there is a slight chance that it willstunthe opponent.
