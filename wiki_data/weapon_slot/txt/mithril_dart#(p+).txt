[Weapon: Mithril Dart#(P+)]


Mithril dartsareRangedthrowing weaponsmade frommithrilthat are only available tomembers. Mithrildartsrequire level 20 Ranged to wield. They can be obtained by either using theSmithingandFletchingskills or trading with another player. Mithril darts can bepoisonedand can be wielded with ashieldorgod bookfor protection.
Mithril darts are one of the most cost effective Ranged training weapons for lower level players, as they have an extremely quick rate of fire and cost very little compared to similar training methods likethrowing knivesorarrows. They can be bought in large numbers from theGrand Exchange, costing less than half of what similar tier ranged ammunition costs.
