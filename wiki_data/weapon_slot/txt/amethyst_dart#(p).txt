[Weapon: Amethyst Dart#(P)]


Amethyst dartsareRangedthrowing weapons. To wield them, the player must have at least 50 Ranged. They can be obtained by using theMining,Crafting, andFletchingskills or trading with other players. Amethyst darts can bepoisonedand are one-handed, so they can be wielded with ashieldorgod bookfor protection. To fletch amethyst darts, the player must have at least 90 Fletching.Amethyst dart tipsare combined withfeathers, awarding 21 Fletching experience per dart.
Amethyst dart tips can becraftedfromamethyst, creating 8amethyst dart tipsand earning 60 Crafting experience for each mineral.
