[Weapon: Adamant Knife#(P)]


Theadamant knifeis athrowing knifethat requires aRangedlevel of 30 to wield. It is stronger than amithril knifebut weaker than arune knife. To create adamant knives, the player must have at least 77Smithing. Eachadamant barwill create 5 adamant knives and give the player 62.5 Smithing experience. They can bepoisonedand can be wielded with ashieldorgod bookfor protection.
Throwing knives are among the fastest weapons in the game (along withdarts), and are commonly used forPKingor training Ranged.
