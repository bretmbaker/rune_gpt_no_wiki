[Weapon: Falconer%27S Glove#No Falcon]


TheFalconer's gloveis used in theHunterskillin thefalconryhunting technique in thePiscatoris falconry area, which is located east of thePiscatoris Hunter Area. While wearing the glove, a player has agyr falconon their hand which they can send to catchspotted kebbits,dashing kebbitsanddark kebbits. While wearing the glove, players cannot wear any othergloves,bracelets,weaponsorshields.
A player can temporarily borrow the glove and falcon fromMatthiasfor a fee of 500 coins, and it will be taken away from the player when they leave the fenced area. The fee must be paid every time the player returns to the area later to hunt more kebbits. Alternatively, players may pay a one-time fee of 500,000 coins for unlimited free glove rentals in the future. Players need not worry about logging out with the glove on, it will remain with them when they log back in.
