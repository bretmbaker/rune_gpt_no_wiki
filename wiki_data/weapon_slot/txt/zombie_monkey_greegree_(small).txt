[Weapon: Zombie Monkey Greegree (Small)]


Thesmall zombie monkey greegreeis used for transforming into asmall zombie monkeyonApe Atoll. To make one, a player must take amonkey talismanandsmall zombie monkey bonestoZooknock, located at the very end of theApe Atoll Dungeon. AfterMonkey Madness II, it can be reobtained fromZooknockin theTree Gnome Strongholdif lost (listed as small zombie greegree). No materials are required.
There are two different zombie greegrees. This type is made using bones from the level 82monkey zombiesin theTemple of Marimbo Dungeon. While transformed, a player can use thewall of flameto create anuncut zenyteand may walk safely across the bed of hot rocks in the dungeon.
