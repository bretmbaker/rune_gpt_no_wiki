[Weapon: Smoke Battlestaff]


Asmoke battlestaffis a smokeelemental staffwhich provides unlimited amounts ofairandfirerunes as well as theautocastoption when equipped. It requires 30Attackand 30Magicto wield.
Themystic smoke staffis an even stronger alternative, providing better melee and magic stat bonuses. It is obtained by taking the smoke battlestaff and40,000toThormac. (Reduced to30,000or20,000with completion of thehardandelite Kandarin diariesrespectively.)
