Thepharaoh's sceptreis apolestafffound during thePyramid PlunderminigameinSophanem, from thegolden chestsandsarcophagi. It requires 30Attackand 30Magicto wield.
The sceptre provides teleports to each of the great pyramids of theKharidian Desert, using a charge to do so. Initially, the sceptre can have up to three charges, but can hold up to 100 charges as a reward from theDesert Diary. The sceptre is only tradeable while uncharged.
