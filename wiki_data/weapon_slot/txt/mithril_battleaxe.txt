[Weapon: Mithril Battleaxe]


Amithril battleaxeis abattleaxemade from 3mithril bars, requiring 60Smithing, yielding 150 Smithingexperience. 20Attackis required to wield it. It is stronger than theblack battleaxe, but weaker than theadamant battleaxe.
Players can purchase it fromBrian's Battleaxe BazaarinPort Sarim,Bob's Brilliant AxesinLumbridge, orGulluck and Sonsin theTree Gnome Stronghold.
