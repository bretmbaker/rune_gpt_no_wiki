[Weapon: Dragon Hunter Wand]


TheDragon hunter wandis a magicdragonbane weaponrequiring level 65Magicto wield. It is obtained as a rare drop from thethe Hueycoatl.
The wand has apassive effectthat increases accuracy by 50% and damage by 20% when fightingdragons(excluding Elvarg and revenant dragons) and otherdraconiccreatures, such aswyrms,wyverns, andhydras. This stacks multiplicatively with theSlayer helm (i)and Elite Void but not salve (ei). The wand can also autocast spells from theStandard spellbook.
