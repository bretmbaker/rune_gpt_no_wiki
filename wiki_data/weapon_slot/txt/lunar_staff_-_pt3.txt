[Weapon: Lunar Staff - Pt3]


Lunar staff - pt3is made by using adramen staffon theAir Altar,Fire AltarandWater Altar(in that order) during theLunar Diplomacyquest. After using the staff on theEarth Altar, it becomes alunar staff. An unfinished lunar staff at any stage of completion will still function as a regular dramen staff when usingfairy ringsand entering the shed toZanarisin theLumbridge Swamp.
After the quest is complete, lunar staves can no longer be made through this process.
