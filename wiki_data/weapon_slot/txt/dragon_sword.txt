[Weapon: Dragon Sword]


Thedragon swordis a one-handed stab weapon obtained as a rare drop fromwyrms, and requires 60Attackto wield. It has the higheststab attackbonus of thestandard shortswords, and the third highest stab attack bonus of all shortswords. The dragon sword provides 2 less stab attack than theleaf-bladed sword, but provides 13 morestrength bonus.
As with mostdragon equipment, the dragon swordcannotbe made from raw materials using theSmithingskill.
