[Weapon: Infernal Harpoon#Uncharged]


Theinfernal harpoonis a special non-tradableharpooncreated by using asmouldering stoneon adragon harpoon, requiring 75Fishingand 85Cookingto craft. This cannot be boosted. It is non-reversible and grants 350Cookingand 200Fishingexperience. It can be stored in atackle box.
Fishcaught with a charged harpoon have a 1/3(33%)chance to be cooked and destroyed instantly, granting half of theCookingexperience that would normally be gained from cooking the fish otherwise.Harpoonfishcaught within theTemporossminigame arenotdestroyed when cooked, making it a popular upgrade that saves 3 ticks (1.8 seconds) per fish. The harpoon willnotconsume big fish, such as thebig shark.[1]Fishing training methods usingtick manipulationalso can use the infernal harpoon to avoid dropping 1/3rd of the fish caught.
