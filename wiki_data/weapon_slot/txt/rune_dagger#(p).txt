[Weapon: Rune Dagger#(P)]


Therune daggeris adaggermade fromrune. Rune daggers can be made from a singlerune barat level 85Smithing. Each rune dagger created grants 75 experience. Rune daggers are the third-strongest daggers inRuneScape, behind thedragon daggerand theabyssal dagger. Like most rune weapons they require 40Attackto wield. As with most daggers, this weapon can bepoisoned.
