[Weapon: Rune Crossbow]


Therune crossbowis acrossbowthat is stronger than theadamant crossbowbut weaker than thedragon crossbow. It requires aRangedlevel of 61 to wield, and can fire up to and includingrunite bolts. It is one-handed so it can be equipped alongside ashield, such as ananti-dragon shieldorgod book. It has an attack range of 7 increased to 9 with longrange.
Rune crossbows can be made through theFletchingskill at level 69; a player must first cut ayew stockfor the crossbow fromyew logs, granting 50 Fletchingexperience. Then, a player must addrunite limbsto the stock with a hammer in their inventory, granting 100 experience and making anunstrung rune crossbow. Finally, a player must string the crossbow with acrossbow string, granting 50 experience. Creating a rune crossbow from raw materials grants 200 Fletching experience in total, and doing so within Witchaven and/or Yanille is a task for theelite Ardougne Diary.
