[Weapon: Mithril Knife#(P++)]


Themithril knifeis athrowing knifethat requires aRangedlevel of 20 to wield. It is stronger than ablack knifebut weaker than anadamant knife. To create mithril knives, the player must have at least 57Smithing. Eachmithril barwill create 5 mithril knives and give the player 50 Smithing experience. They can bepoisonedand can be wielded with ashieldorgod bookfor protection.
Throwing knives are among the fastest weapons in the game (along withdarts), and are commonly used forPKingor training Ranged.
