[Weapon: Trailblazer Cane]


TheTrailblazer caneis a cosmetic reward that is obtained alongside purchasing thetier 3 Trailblazer Relic Hunter, which costs 15,000 League points from theLeagues Reward Shop.
If players have the cane equipped while wearing the tier 3 Trailblazer Relic Hunter, they can right-click the equipped cane to perform the Trailblazer Relic emote, which is the animation used when unlocking arelic.
