[Weapon: Trollweiss]


Atrollweissis a rare flower obtained and used during theTroll Romancequest. They are light blue and white in colour, and are endangered (as revealed during the quest).
Players can obtain more than one, and only use one as part of the quest. They can also be equipped, and have the same stats as regularflowers.
