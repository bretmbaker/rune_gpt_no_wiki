[Weapon: Iron Battleaxe]


Theiron battleaxeis the second-weakestbattleaxeinOld School RuneScape, being more powerful than thebronze battleaxe. Like alliron weapons, it can be wielded at level 1Attack.
Players can make an iron battleaxe with level 25Smithingusing threeiron bars, giving 75 experience. It is aslashing weapon, which makes it good for monsters with a slash weakness. Players can purchase this battleaxe fromBrian's Battleaxe BazaarinPort SarimandBob's Brilliant Axesshop inLumbridge.
