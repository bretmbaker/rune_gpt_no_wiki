[Weapon: Mystic Earth Staff]


Themystic earth staffis anelemental staffrequiring 40Attackand 40Magicto wield. In addition to increased Melee and Magic Attack bonuses, the staff provides unlimited amounts ofearth runesas well as theautocastoption when equipped.
This staff can be created after the completion of the questScorpion Catcherby taking anearth battlestaffand40,000toThormac. After completion of theHard Kandarin DiaryandElite Kandarin Diary, Thormac will only require30,000or20,000respectively.
