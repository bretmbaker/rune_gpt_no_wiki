[Weapon: Mithril Longsword]


Themithril longswordis alongswordthat is stronger than theblack longsword/White longsword, but weaker than theadamant longsword. Like all othermithril weapons, it requires anAttacklevel of 20 to wield. Players can make a mithril longsword with aSmithinglevel of 56 using twomithril bars, granting 100 experience. Players can purchase this longsword from theVarrock Swordshop.
