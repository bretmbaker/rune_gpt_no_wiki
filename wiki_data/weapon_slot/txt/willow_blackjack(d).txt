[Weapon: Willow Blackjack(D)]


TheWillow blackjack(d)is amembers onlymelee weaponmainly used to lure/knockoutNPCs. Players require level 20 Defence and level 20 Thieving to equip and use one of these. To obtain one, the player must have completed the blackjack section of theRogue Traderminiquest.
There are three NPCs that can be lured/knocked out with this blackjack:Villagers,banditsandMenaphite Thugs, all located inPollnivneach. However for luring/knocking out these NPCs, the player must have the rightThievinglevel: level 30, 45, and 65, respectively. When fighting with a blackjack, there is a slight chance tostunthe opponent.
