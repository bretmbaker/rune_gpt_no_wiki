[Weapon: Dragon Mace (Bh)]


Thedragon mace (bh)is a weapon that requires level 60 inAttackto wield. It is created by using adragon mace imbue scrollon thedragon mace, which is purchased as a reward fromBounty Hunter.
In comparison to the regular mace, the imbued mace has a +4 increased stab and crush bonus. In addition, the special attack will roll against 60% of the target's crush defence rather than 100%.
