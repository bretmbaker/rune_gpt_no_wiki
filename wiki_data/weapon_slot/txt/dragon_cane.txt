[Weapon: Dragon Cane]


Thedragon caneis a reward fromeliteTreasure Trailsthat requires level 60Attackto wield. This weapon has similar stats to thedragon mace(including the +5Prayer bonus), although no quest completion is required to wield the dragon cane (while completion ofHeroes' Questis needed to wield the dragon mace). Unlike the mace however, the Dragon Cane has an attack speed of 5 ticks (3.0s).
Unlike other canes, which use theCombat Optionsofmaces, the dragon cane uses the combat options ofpolestaves.
