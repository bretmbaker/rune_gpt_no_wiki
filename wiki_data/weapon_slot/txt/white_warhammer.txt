[Weapon: White Warhammer]


Thewhite warhammeris aWhite Knightweaponthat requires 10Strengthand the completion of theWanted!questto wield. It has the same bonuses as ablack warhammer, with an added +1prayer bonus.
Players can purchase it fromSir Vyvinafter achieving therankof Master (1300black knightkills). It can also be bought on the Grand Exchange or obtained through trading other players.
