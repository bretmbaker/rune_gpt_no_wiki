[Weapon: Fremennik Blade]


TheFremennik bladeis aslash weaponequivalent to anadamant scimitar. This weapon is dropped by variousFremennikpeople inRellekka,Fremennik warriors, and some monsters onWaterbirth Island. Completion ofThe Fremennik Trialsand 30Attackis required to equip it. The sword is not tradeable.
