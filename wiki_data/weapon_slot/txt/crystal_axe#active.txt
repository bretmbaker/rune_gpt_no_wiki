[Weapon: Crystal Axe#Active]


Thecrystal axeis an item used inWoodcutting. It requires level 71 Woodcutting andSong of the Elvesto create and use, with 50Agilityand 70Attackrequired to wield it. It possesses the samespecial attackas thedragon axe, and gives the player a 15% increase in efficiency chopping logs over therune axe, surpassing the 10% increase of the dragon axe. This makes the crystal axe approximately 4.5% faster than the dragon axe.
Players can create a crystal axe via asinging bowlby combining a dragon axe, 120crystal shardsand acrystal tool seed. Doing so requires level 76Smithing(boostable)and 76Crafting(boostable)tosingthe crystal, and grants 6,000 experience in both skills. Players can configure the charges while the axe is in their bank by right-clicking on Configure-Charges, as long as players have the required materials. If the player does not meet the Smithing and Crafting requirements, they can haveConwennaorReesesing the crystal for them for an extra 60 crystal shards, for a total of 180.

[Special Attack]
A player performs the crystal axe's special attack,Lumber Up.
The crystal axe has aspecial attack,Lumber Up, that it shares with thedragon,infernal, and3rd age axesand their variants. Ittemporarily booststhe player'sWoodcuttinglevel by 3, consuming 100% of the player's special attack energy. The boost degrades every 60 seconds, with the player's Woodcutting level returning to normal after 3 minutes. UsingPreservecan increase the duration by 50%.
Note that any levels boosted above 99 with the special attack will be treated as 99. Therefore, you will start to receive diminishing returns with the special attack at level 97 and 98[1], with visible boosts past level 99 only influencing thepetandclue nestchances.[2]
