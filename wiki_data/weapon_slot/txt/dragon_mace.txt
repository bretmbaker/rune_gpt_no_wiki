[Weapon: Dragon Mace]


Thedragon maceis the fifth-strongestmacestat-wise after theTzhaar-ket-em,Sarachnis cudgel,Viggora's chainmaceandInquisitor's mace. It can only be wielded by players who have anAttacklevel of at least 60 and who have completed theHeroes' Quest.
The mace can be purchased for 50,000 coins at theHappy Heroes' H'emporiumin theHeroes' Guild, or from other players. It can also be obtained as a reward fromelite cluesandmaster cluesor a possible reward fromSoul Warsspoils of war.
