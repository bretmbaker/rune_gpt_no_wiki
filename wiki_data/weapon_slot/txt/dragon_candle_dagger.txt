[Weapon: Dragon Candle Dagger]


Thedragon candle daggeris an item that can be obtained from participating in anybirthday event, and was first obtained from the2023 Birthday event.
The weapon has a special attack,Celebrate, that performs an "attack" which leaves the area "feeling more festive", consuming 25% of the user's special attack energy. This will always deal 0 damage. This can also be done by selecting the "Celebrate" option on all other players. The special attack cannot be used on free to play worlds.
