[Weapon: Black Knife#(P+)]


Theblack knifeis athrowing knifethat requires aRangedlevel of 10 to wield. It is stronger than asteel knifebut weaker than amithril knife. As with allblack equipment, this item cannot be made via theSmithingskill. A black knife must either be obtained as a monster drop, or bought from another player. They can bepoisonedand can be wielded with ashieldorgod bookfor protection.
Throwing knives are among the fastest weapons in the game (along withdarts), and are commonly used forPKingor training Ranged.
