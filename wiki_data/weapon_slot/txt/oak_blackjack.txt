[Weapon: Oak Blackjack]


Theoak blackjackis amelee weaponthat has no level requirement to wield and is mainly used to knockout certainNPCs. An oak blackjack can be obtained fromAli the OperatorduringThe Feudquest. When fighting with a blackjack, there is a slight chance tostunthe opponent.
There are three NPCs that can be knocked out:Villagers,banditsandMenaphite Thugs, all located in Pollnivneach. While there is no level requirement to knock out a villager, the Bandits require level 45Thieving(or 55 for scimitar wielding ones),  and the Menaphite Thugs level 65Thieving.
