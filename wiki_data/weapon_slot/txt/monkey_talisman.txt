[Weapon: Monkey Talisman]


Amonkey talismanis used for makinggreegreesuntil the completion ofMonkey Madness II. When taken toZooknockalong with the corresponding materials, he will enchant it into a greegree.
It is first acquired by talking to theMonkey ChildonApe Atollwhile wearing aM'speak amulet. The talismans sold atTutab's Magical Marketmay only be bought after Zooknock enchants the original, or the player can get another one for free from the monkey child.
