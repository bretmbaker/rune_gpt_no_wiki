[Weapon: Iron Mace]


TheIron maceis amacemade ofiron. It is the second weakest mace available in the game. It requires level 1Attackto wield, and can be created by using aniron baron an anvil (as long as the player has level 17Smithing). It can be bought atFlynn's Mace MarketinFalador.
It can be found in theEdgevillejail and in a house to the east of theArdougne market.
