[Weapon: Bronze Battleaxe]


Thebronze battleaxeis abattleaxemade ofbronze. The weakest battleaxe in the game, it requires 1Attackto wield. Players can make a bronze battleaxe with theSmithingskill at level 10 using 3bronze bars, giving 37.5 experience. It is aslashingweapon, which makes it good for monsters with a slash weakness. Players can purchase this battleaxe fromBrian's Battleaxe BazaarinPort Sarim.
