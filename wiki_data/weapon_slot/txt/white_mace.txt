[Weapon: White Mace]


Thewhite maceis aWhite Knightweaponthat requires 10Attackand the completion of theWanted!questto wield. It has the same bonuses as ablack mace, with an added +1prayer bonus.
Players can purchase it fromSir Vyvinafter achieving therankof Noble (500black knightkills). It can also be bought on the Grand Exchange or obtained through trading other players.
