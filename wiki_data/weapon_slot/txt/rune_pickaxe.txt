[Weapon: Rune Pickaxe]


Therune pickaxeis the third bestpickaxe, after thecrystal pickaxeand thedragon pickaxevariants. It mines on a 3-tickcycle and is the best pickaxe infree-to-play. The rune pickaxe requires 41Miningto use, and 40Attackto equip. Players lacking 40 Attack can still use it for mining. Playerscannotsmith the rune pickaxe, so it must be obtained from other players or from a shop, as a monster drop, or as a quest reward from the quest "Between a Rock...".
