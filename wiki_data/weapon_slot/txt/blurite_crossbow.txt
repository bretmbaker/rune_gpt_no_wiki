Blurite crossbows require 24Fletchingto be made. Anoak stockmust be fletched fromoak logs, yielding 16 Fletchingexperience, thenblurite limbsmust be attached to the stock with ahammer, yielding 32 Fletching experience and anunstrung blurite crossbow. Finally, the crossbow must be strung with acrossbow string, yielding 16 experience. In total, making a blurite crossbow yields 64 Fletching experience.
