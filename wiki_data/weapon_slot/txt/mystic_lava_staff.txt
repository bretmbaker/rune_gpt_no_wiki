[Weapon: Mystic Lava Staff]


Themystic lava staffis anelemental staffrequiring 40Attackand 40Magicto wield. In addition to increased Melee and Magic Attack bonuses, the staff provides unlimited amounts ofearthandfire runesas well as theautocastoption when equipped.
This type of elemental staff is the best (once you've reached or surpassed level 87Magic) for getting enchantment points when training Magic at theMage Training Arena. It is also useful forEnfeeble-alching.
