[Weapon: Adamant Battleaxe]


Theadamant battleaxeis abattleaxethat is stronger than amithril battleaxe, but weaker than arune battleaxe. As with alladamant weapons, anAttacklevel of at least 30 is required in order to wield it.
Players are able to obtain adamant battleaxes via trading with players, monster drops, or purchasing it atBrian's Battleaxe Bazaar(located inPort Sarim). It may also be a reward from aclue scroll. At level 80Smithing, players can use 3adamant barsto smith an adamant battleaxe, granting 187.5 experience.
