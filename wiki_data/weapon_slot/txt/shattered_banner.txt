[Weapon: Shattered Banner]


TheShattered banneris a cosmetic reward representing theShattered Relics League, which costs 500 League points from theLeagues Reward Shop.
The banner can be displayed by using it on furniture built in theLeague hall'sbanner stand space.Ultimate Ironmencan use this as a storage mechanism, but since a player may only build one league hall, only one league banner can be stored in aPOH.
