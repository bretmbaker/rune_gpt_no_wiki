[Weapon: Kruk Monkey Greegree]


Kruk monkey greegree(also known as alarge monkey greegree) is used for transforming intoKrukonApe AtollduringMonkey Madness II. To make one, a player must take amonkey talismanandKruk's pawtoZooknock, located at the very end of theApe Atoll Dungeon.
A glitch exists where if the player gets caught and they try to transform back, there is a small chance they will lose their greegree; however, this glitch is not common. If the player has a full inventory, and teleports from Ape Atoll without removing their greegree, they will lose it.
