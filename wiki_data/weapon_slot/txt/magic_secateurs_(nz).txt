[Weapon: Magic Secateurs (Nz)]


Magic secateurs (nz)are aNightmare Zoneexclusive version of themagic secateurs, and share its combat stats. They spawn on the ground ifTanglefootis present in a Nightmare Zone session, as they are the only possible means of damaging the boss.
As with all items with the (nz) suffix, they cannot be taken outside of the Nightmare Zone, and if discarded, can be retrieved from the same place it spawned.
