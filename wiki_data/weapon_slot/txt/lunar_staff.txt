[Weapon: Lunar Staff]


Thelunar staffis amagic weaponand piece oflunar equipment, requiring 65Magicand 40Defenceto wield. It is created duringLunar Diplomacyby using adramen staffon theair,fire,water, andearthaltars(in that order). This cannot be done after Lunar Diplomacy is completed, however.
As an enhanced dramen staff, the lunar staff can similarly be used to accessZanarisand thefairy ringnetwork. This can be done even before completing Lunar Diplomacy if the player retrieved their staff back from theOneiromancer, or if the player made copies and gave one of them to her.
