[Weapon: Mystic Mud Staff]


Themystic mud staffis anelemental staffrequiring 40Attackand 40Magicto wield. In addition to increased Melee and Magic Attack bonuses, the staff provides unlimited amounts ofearthandwater runesas well as theautocastoption when equipped.
Wielding a mystic mud staff is ideal forcurse spells, as almost all of them use both water and earth runes.
