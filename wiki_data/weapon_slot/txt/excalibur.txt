[Weapon: Excalibur]


Excaliburis a one-handedswordand aquest itemobtained from theMerlin's Crystalquestand used in theHoly Grailquest. It requires 20Attackto wield and has similarstatsto theadamant longsword, except for 6 lessstrength bonusand 1 more magic defence.
In the Merlin's Crystal quest, the player must use Excalibur on the crystal to smash and freeMerlin. It is obtained fromLady of the Lakeafter she tests the purity of the players spirit when giving abeggarbreadatGrum's Gold ExchangeinPort Sarim.
