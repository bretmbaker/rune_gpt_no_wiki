[Weapon: Twisted Cane]


Thetwisted caneis a cosmetic reward that is obtained alongside purchasing thetier 3 Twisted Relic Hunter, which costs 10,000 League points from theLeagues Reward Shop.
If players have the cane equipped while wearing the tier 3 Twisted Relic Hunter, they can right-click the equipped cane to perform the Twisted Relic emote, which is the animation used when unlocking arelic. This emote cannot be performed while in combat.
