[Weapon: Easter Basket]


TheEaster basketwas given out as a reward by theEaster Bunnyduring the2014 Easter event. It can be operated when worn to perform anemote, flinging two easter eggs randomly in a 5x5 radius.
While worn, the player's walking and running animations will be replaced with a skipping animation.
