[Weapon: Iron Dagger#(P++)]


Theiron daggeris adaggerstronger than thebronze dagger. Players can make an iron dagger with theSmithingskill at level 15 using 1iron bar, giving the player 25 Smithingexperience.Memberscanpoisonthis weapon, giving them an advantage incombat. Players can purchase this dagger from theVarrock Swordshop. There are also several spawn points throughout the map. As with mostdaggers, it can bepoisoned.
Players with at least level 32Thievingcan get an iron dagger (p) by pickpocketingRogues. Rogues are located south-west of the deepWildernessMage Arena.
