[Weapon: Battlestaff]


Abattlestaffis amembers-onlymagic weaponwhich provides standard magical bonuses, heightened melee bonuses, and theautocastoption when equipped. It requires 30Attackand 30Magicto wield. Due to not having anyelementalfeatures, this staff has limited long-term use. Its advantage overbasic tier elemental stavesand other lesser staves are its melee and magic bonuses.
The staff can be purchased fromZaff's Superior Staffs!inVarrockafter the completion of the questWhat Lies Below, and he'll stock 15 to 120 extra staves daily for players who have fully completed task tiers of theVarrock Diary(they can be obtained by interacting with the barrel in the north-west corner of the shop). The magic store owner in theWizards' GuildandBaba Yagaon theLunar Islealso stock them. It can also be bought on theGrand Exchange, obtained through other players, dropped by various enemies, or fletched fromCelastrus barkwith level 40Fletching.
