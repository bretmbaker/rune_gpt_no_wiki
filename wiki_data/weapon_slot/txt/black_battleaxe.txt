[Weapon: Black Battleaxe]


Theblack battleaxeis abattleaxestronger than thesteel battleaxe, but weaker than theMithril battleaxe. It requires 10Attackto wield. Players can purchase this battleaxe fromBrian's Battleaxe BazaarinPort Sarim. It can also be received as a drop from aDark beast, and as a reward from an easyclue scroll.
As with allblackandwhite equipment, the black battleaxe cannot be made from raw materials using theSmithingskill.
