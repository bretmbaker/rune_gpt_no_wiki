[Weapon: Volatile Nightmare Staff (Deadman)]


TheDeadman volatile nightmare staffis an ornamented variant of thevolatile nightmare staff, made by giving it toNigelafter having used anArmageddon weapon scrollon him. The attached ornament kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the volatile nightmare staff.
The staff can be reverted to its original form by speaking to Nigel.
