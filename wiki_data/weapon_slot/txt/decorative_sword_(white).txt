[Weapon: Decorative Sword (White)]


Thedecorative sword (white)can be bought in theCastle Warsarea south of theObservatoryfor 50Castle wars ticketsfromLanthus. The sword offers the same stats as amithril longsword, and requires level 20Attackto wield. Also, it has similar shape and colour palette to theblurite sword. However, the blurite sword is considerably weaker.
This item can be stored in thearmour caseof acostume room.
