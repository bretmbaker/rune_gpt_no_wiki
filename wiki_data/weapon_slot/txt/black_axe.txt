[Weapon: Black Axe]


Ablack axeis an item mostly used in theWoodcuttingskill. Using thisaxerequires level 11 Woodcutting. Level 10Attackis required to equip it. As with all axes, carrying it in yourinventoryalong with the necessary Woodcutting level will work.
The success rate for chopping a log is approximately 225% that of thebronze axe, or 12.5% better than asteel axe.
