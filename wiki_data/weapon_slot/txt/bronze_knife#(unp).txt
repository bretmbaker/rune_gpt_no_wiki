[Weapon: Bronze Knife#(Unp)]


Bronze knivesare the lowest levelthrowing knivesavailable, and do not require aRangedlevel to wield. They can bepoisoned, and due to being one-handed, can be wielded alongside ashield.
Bronze knives can be created from abronze barat level7Smithing. Each bar makes 5 knives, and grants the player 12.5 Smithing experience. The knives can also be purchased fromMartin Thwait's Lost and Foundshop in theRogues' Den, requiring50Thievingand50Agility.
