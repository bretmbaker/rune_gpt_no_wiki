[Weapon: Mystic Lava Staff (Or)]


Themystic lava staff (or)is amystic lava staffwith alava staff upgrade kitattached to it. The kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the staff. It costs415,624for both the parts to make the lava staff (or).
This staff can be also be created after the completion of the questScorpion Catcherby taking alava battlestaff (or)and40,000toThormac. After completion of theHard Kandarin DiaryandElite Kandarin Diary, Thormac will only require30,000or20,000respectively.
