[Weapon: Mithril Axe]


Amithril axeis anaxestronger than theblack axe, and weaker than theadamant axe. Like all mithril weapons, it requires level 20Attackto wield. It can be used in combat or inWoodcuttingto cuttrees. To cut a tree with this axe, a player must have 21 Woodcutting.
The success rate for chopping a log is approximately 250% that of theBronze axe, or 11% better than aBlack axe.
