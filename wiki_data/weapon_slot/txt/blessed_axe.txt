[Weapon: Blessed Axe]


Theblessed axeis an axe made byTuraelby giving him amithril axeand aholy symbol, which requires completing theAnimal Magnetismquest up until the pointAvasuggests talking to him. Talking to Turael after this step will then make it. It is used to chopundead twigsfrom the undead trees within the vicinity ofDraynor Manor. Turael will make additional blessed axes if the required materials are brought to him again inBurthorpe.
As the axe possesses a silver blade, it can damagevampyres.
