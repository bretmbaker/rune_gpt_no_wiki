[Weapon: Lava Battlestaff]


Alava battlestaffis a lavaelemental staffwhich provides unlimited amounts ofearthandfirerunes as well as theautocastoption when equipped. It requires 30Attackand 30Magicto wield.
Themystic lava staffis an even stronger alternative, providing better melee and magic stat bonuses. It is obtained by taking the lava battlestaff and40,000toThormac. (Reduced to30,000or20,000with completion of thehardandelite Kandarin diariesrespectively.)
