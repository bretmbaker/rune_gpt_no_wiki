[Weapon: White Dagger#(P++)]


Thewhite daggeris aWhite Knightweaponthat requires 10Attackand the completion of theWanted!questto wield. It has the same bonuses as ablack dagger, with an added +1prayer bonus.
Players can purchase it fromSir Vyvinafter achieving therankof Peon (200black knightkills). It can also be bought on the Grand Exchange or obtained through trading other players.
