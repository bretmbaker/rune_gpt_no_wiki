[Weapon: Keris Partisan Of Breaching]


Thekeris partisan of breachingis a one-handed spear requiring 80Attackto equip. It is made by attaching thebreach of the scarabon thekeris partisan. As with the keris partisan, weapon poisons cannot be applied to the partisan.
The keris partisan of breaching retains the 33% bonus damage effects of thekeris. The Breach of the scarab attached to the keris partisan grants an additional 33% accuracy[1]against allKalphitesandScabarites, alongside the inherited 1/51 chance of puncturing a hole in their exoskeleton that inflicts triple damage. When this special effect occurs, the chatbox will display the message:You slip your weapon through a gap in the creature's chitin, landing a vicious blow.
