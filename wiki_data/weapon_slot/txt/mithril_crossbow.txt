[Weapon: Mithril Crossbow]


Amithril crossbowis acrossbowthat is stronger than thesteel crossbowbut weaker than theadamant crossbow. It requires aRangedlevel of 36 to wield, and can fireboltsup to and includingmithril bolts. It has an attack range of 7 increased to 9 with longrange.
Mithril crossbows can be made through theFletchingskill at level 54; a player must first cut amaple stockfor the crossbow frommaple logs, granting 32 Fletchingexperience. Then, a player must addmithril limbsto the stock with a hammer in their inventory, granting 64 experience and making anunstrung mithril crossbow. Finally, a player must string the crossbow with acrossbow string, granting 32 experience. Creating a mithril crossbow from raw materials grants 128 Fletching experience in total.
