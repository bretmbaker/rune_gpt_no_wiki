[Weapon: Cormorant%27S Glove#Bird]


Cormorant's gloveis an item obtained for free by speaking toAlry the AngleronMolch island. Requiring both theHunterandFishingskill to obtain, it can be used inAerial fishingto catchBluegills,Common tenches,Mottled eel, andGreater siren. Alongside the fish, players can also catchMolch pearlswhich can then be traded to Alry for a number of items which can assist in standard fishing.
In order to use the cormorant's glove, players must have the appropriate bait in their inventory.King wormscan be found scattered around the island which can be used, however should the player bring aknifeto the island or pick up the one that spawns there, they can use it to obtainFish chunksfrom the many varieties of fish caught there. The player received 1 Fish chunk for each fish cut, regardless of the type.
