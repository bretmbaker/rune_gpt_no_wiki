[Weapon: Earth Battlestaff]


Theearth battlestaffis anelemental staffrequiring 30Attackand 30Magicto wield. In addition to increased Melee and Magic Attack bonuses, the staff provides unlimited amounts ofearth runesas well as theautocastoption when equipped.
This staff can be created at level 58Craftingby combining anearth orbandbattlestaff. It can also be upgraded to amystic earth staffby payingThormac40,000coinsto upgrade it (less with the completion of certainKandarin Diaries). The mystic earth staff is an even stronger alternative, providing better melee and magic stat bonuses.
