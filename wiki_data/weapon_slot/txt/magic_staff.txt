[Weapon: Magic Staff]


Amagic staffis amagic weaponproviding standard magical bonuses and theautocastoption when equipped. It is an improvement over the basicstaff, with over double the magical bonuses and higher melee bonuses. Due to not possessing anyelementalfeatures, it is a very unpopular weapon of choice.
The staff can most easily be purchased fromZaff's Superior Staffs!inVarrock, dropped by various enemies, or obtained as a reward from theShades of Mort'tonminigame.
