[Weapon: Skull Sceptre (I)]


Theskull sceptre (i)is an imbued version of theskull sceptre. Like the regular skull sceptre, the imbued sceptre can be used to teleport toBarbarian Village, and can autocastCrumble Undeadas well ascombat spellsfrom theArceuus spellbook.
Unlike the regular skull sceptre, this sceptre does not break after depleting the teleport charges, and it can be recharged withbone fragmentsorsceptre piecesas explainedbelow. As it is one of the few teleport items available tofree-to-play, it is highly desired among those players.
