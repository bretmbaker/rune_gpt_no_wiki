[Weapon: Iban%27S Staff (U)]


Iban's staff (u)(referred to in-game as anUpgraded Iban's  staff) is an upgradedIban's staffthat holds 2,500 charges instead of 120. It is obtained by bringing anIban's staffand200,000 coinsto theDark Mage, in the tower east of the entrance to theUnderground PassinWest Ardougne. Upgrading the staff will also fully charge it. It requires 50Magicand 50Attackto wield. CastingIban Blaston amonsterconsumes one charge from the staff and casting it on another player consumes two charges. Casting otherspells, likeFire Blast, does not consume any charges.
If Iban's staff is lost prior to completing the Underground Pass quest,Disciples of Ibanmust be killed for a broken Iban's staff. After the quest is completed,Koftikwill give a broken staff to the player at the entrance to theUnderground Pass. The broken Iban's staff must be brought to theDark Magewith200,000 coinsto repair it, then to reobtain the upgraded staff, the player must pay another200,000 coinsto the Dark Mage to upgrade the new staff. The broken staff can be repaired and upgraded directly for400,000 coins.
