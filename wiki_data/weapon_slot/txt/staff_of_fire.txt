[Weapon: Staff Of Fire]


Astaff of fireis the basic fireelemental staff. It provides unlimited amounts offire runesas well as theautocastoption when equipped. All elemental staves offer the sameMagicbonuses and only differ by the type ofrunethey supply and theirMeleestats.
The staff can be purchased from various shops acrossGielinor, includingZaff's Superior Staffs!inVarrock. It can also be bought on theGrand Exchange, obtained through other players, or dropped by various enemies.
