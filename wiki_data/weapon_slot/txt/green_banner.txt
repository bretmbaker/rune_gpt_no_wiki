[Weapon: Green Banner]


Thegreen banneris an item that was made available during the MacMillan Cancer Support Charity Event that was hosted during the 14th and 15th of November 2015 via usage of therotten onion, which was distributed byJagex Moderatorsto certain players of theOld School RuneScapecommunity. It was made available fromDiango's Toy Storethe week after.
