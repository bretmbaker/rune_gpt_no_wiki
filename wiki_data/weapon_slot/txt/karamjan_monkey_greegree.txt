[Weapon: Karamjan Monkey Greegree]


TheKaramjan monkey greegreeis used for transforming into aKaramjatypemonkeyonApe Atoll. To make one, a player must take amonkey talismanandmonkey bonesorcorpsetoZooknock, located at the very end of theApe Atoll Dungeon.
While transformed, allmonsterson Ape Atoll become non-aggressive. This transformation effect is only in place on Ape Atoll as Zooknock cannot use his magic over long distances. However, for reasons unknown, it can also be used at theArdougne Zoo. Be aware that a greegree cannot be held while in combat.
