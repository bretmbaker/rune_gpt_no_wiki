[Weapon: Inquisitor%27S Mace]


TheInquisitor's maceis a one-handedcrush weaponrequiring 80Attackto wield. Received as a drop fromThe Nightmare, it has bonuses nearly identical to theGhrazi rapierandblade of Saeldor, acting as the crush variant of these weapons; unlike those weapons, it also has +2 Prayer bonus.
Once belonging toPhosani, a formerSaradoministJusticiarand one of the founders ofSlepe, the Inquisitor's mace had remained sealed in theSisterhood Sanctuarydeep beneath Slepe until The Nightmare's arrival unearthed it.
