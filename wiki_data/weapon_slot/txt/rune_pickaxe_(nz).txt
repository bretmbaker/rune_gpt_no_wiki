[Weapon: Rune Pickaxe (Nz)]


Arune pickaxe (nz)is aNightmare Zoneexclusive version of therune pickaxe, and shares its combat stats. It spawns on the ground ifSlagilithis present in a Nightmare Zone session, as using a pickaxe is the only possible means of damaging the boss.
As with all items with the (nz) suffix, it cannot be taken outside of the Nightmare Zone, and if discarded, can be retrieved from the same place it spawned.
