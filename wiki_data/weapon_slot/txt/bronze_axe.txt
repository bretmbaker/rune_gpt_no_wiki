[Weapon: Bronze Axe]


TheBronze axeis the weakestaxeinOld School RuneScape. It can be wielded for combat, or used to cut trees inWoodcutting. It requires anAttacklevel of 1 to be equipped, and a Woodcutting level of 1 to be used.
Players can make a bronze axe with theSmithingskill at level 1 using oneBronze bar, giving the player 12.5 Smithingexperience. Players can take one for free fromlogsfound at various locations, such as at the chicken farm by theLumbridge cow field, orFred the Farmer's house. One can also be obtained by talking to theWoodsman tutor.
