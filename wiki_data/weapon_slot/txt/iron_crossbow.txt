[Weapon: Iron Crossbow]


Aniron crossbowis acrossbowstronger than theblurite crossbowbut weaker than thesteel crossbow. It requires aRangedlevel of 26 to wield, and can useboltsup to and includingiron bolts. It has an attack range of 7 increased to 9 with longrange.
Iron crossbows can be made through theFletchingskill at level 39; a player must first cut awillow stockfor the crossbow fromwillow logs, granting 22 Fletchingexperience. Then, a player must addiron limbsto the stock with a hammer in their inventory, granting 44 experience and making anunstrung iron crossbow. Finally, a player must string the crossbow with acrossbow string, granting 22 experience. Creating an iron crossbow from raw materials grants 88 Fletching experience in total.
