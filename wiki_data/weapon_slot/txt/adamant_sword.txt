[Weapon: Adamant Sword]


Theadamant swordis the second-strongestswordavailable tofree players, after therune sword. As with mostadamant weapons, level 30Attackis required to wield it.
Adamant swords can be created at level 74Smithing. Oneadamant baris required to make the sword, and 62.5 Smithing experience is rewarded per sword created.
