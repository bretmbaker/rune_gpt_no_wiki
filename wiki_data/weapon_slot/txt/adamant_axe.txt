[Weapon: Adamant Axe]


Anadamant axeis a one-handedaxerequiring 30Attackto wield and 31Woodcuttingto use to cuttrees. Making one requires 1adamant barand 71Smithing, yielding 62.5 Smithingexperience. As with all axes, it can be used to cut trees even when in the player's inventory.
The success rate for getting a set of logs is approximately 300% that of abronze axeand 20% better than amithril axe.
