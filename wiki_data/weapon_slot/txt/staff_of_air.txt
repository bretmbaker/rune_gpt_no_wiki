[Weapon: Staff Of Air]


Astaff of airis the basic airelemental staff. It provides unlimited amounts ofair runesas well as theautocastoption when equipped. All elemental staves offer the sameMagicbonuses and only differ by the type ofrunethey supply and theirMeleestats.
The staff can be purchased from various shops acrossGielinor, includingZaff's Superior Staffs!inVarrock. It can also be bought on theGrand Exchange, obtained through other players, dropped by various enemies, or obtained as a reward frombeginnerandeasyclue caskets.
