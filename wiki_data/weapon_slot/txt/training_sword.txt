[Weapon: Training Sword]


ATraining swordis a beginnerweapon. It has identical stats to aBronze sword.
It is obtained for free along with atraining shieldfrom theMelee combat tutor, just south of theLumbridge General Store. It can be replaced if lost, but only one can be obtained at a time. Due to the accessibility, this sword is ideal for players who are new to the game and have just finishedTutorial Island.
