[Weapon: Lucky Cutlass]


Thelucky cutlasscan be bought fromSmithonMos Le'Harmlessfor 2,560coinsafter completing the questCabin Fever, and requires 30Attackto wield. ABook o' piracyis required to purchase from Smith.
It was previously owned by a pirate named Unlucky Jenkins, who died after a long series of improbable and very unlucky calamities. After his gruesome demise, his cutlass was discovered, untouched. The other pirates concluded that, having survived so much terrible bad luck already, it would be impossible for it to go through any more bad luck.
