[Weapon: Rune Battleaxe]


Therune battleaxeis the fourth strongest battleaxe inRuneScape, withzombie axebeing the strongest, followed by theleaf-bladed battleaxeand thedragon battleaxe. Like mostrune weapons, it requires 40Attackto wield.
Players may make a rune battleaxe with 95Smithingby using 3rune barsat ananvilwith a hammer, giving the player 225 Smithing experience.
