[Weapon: Rune Knife#(P+)]


Therune knifeis athrowing knifethat requires aRangedlevel of 40 to wield. It is the second strongest throwing knife in the game. To create rune knives, the player must have at least 92Smithing. Eachrunite barwill create 5 rune knives and give the player 75 Smithing experience. They can bepoisonedand can be wielded with ashieldorgod bookfor protection.
Throwing knives are among the fastest weapons in the game (along withdarts), and are commonly used forPKingor training Ranged.
