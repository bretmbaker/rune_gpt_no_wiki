[Weapon: Dragon Longsword (Bh)]


Thedragon longsword (bh)is a weapon that requires level 60 inAttackto wield. It is created by using adragon longsword imbue scrollon thedragon longsword, which is purchased as a reward fromBounty Hunter.
In comparison to the regular longsword, the imbued longsword has a +4 increased stab and +6 slash bonus, and a +25% accuracy to the special attack. In addition, the special attack consumes only 15% special attack energy rather than 25% and is onetickfaster (2.4 seconds instead of 3.0 seconds).

[Special Attack]
A player performing the dragon longsword (bh)'s special attack,Cleave.
The dragon longsword (bh) has aspecial attackcalledCleave, which raises both the player's maximum hit and accuracy by 25%, as well as temporarily increasing theattack speedof the weapon by onetick(from 3.0 seconds to 2.4 seconds) for one hit after performing the special attack; potentially allowing the player to use multiple special attacks in quicker succession than the regulardragon longsword. This consumes 15% of the player's special attack energy.
The accuracy roll forCleaveis based on the attack bonus of the player's currently select style, which is rolled against an opponent's slash defence.[1]
