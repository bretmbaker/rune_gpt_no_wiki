[Weapon: Imcando Hammer#Repaired]


TheImcando hammeris a wieldable variant of ahammermostly used forSmithingorConstruction. It can be obtained through smashingbarronite depositson thebarronite crusherin theRuins of Camdozaalafter completing theBelow Ice Mountainquest.
When first obtained, the hammer will be in a broken state. The broken hammer can be fixed by using it onRamarno, who will repair it for 1,250 barronite shards. Alternatively, it can be exchanged for 350 barronite shards, or 500 shards for a fixed hammer. Once repaired, it can be used as a hammer, both while equipped and unequipped, in any way that a normal hammer can be used.
