[Weapon: Frozen Abyssal Whip]


Thefrozen abyssal whipis anabyssal whipwith afrozen whip mixadded onto it. The whip mix gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the whip. It costs2,991,596for both the parts to make the frozen abyssal whip.
While the whip is untradeable in its frozen state, it can be reverted to the tradeable abyssal whip by using acleaning clothon it. However, the frozen whip mix willnotbe returned.
