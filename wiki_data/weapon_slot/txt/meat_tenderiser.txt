[Weapon: Meat Tenderiser]


Themeat tenderiseris ameleekitchen weaponthat can be bought from theCulinaromancer's Chest. To wield it, the player must have completed all 8Recipe for Disastersubquests,andthefinal battle(it may be purchased, but not wielded, before completing the final battle).
The weapon has almost the same stats as arune warhammer, but it requires level 40Attackinstead ofStrength, being left out in thefirst 2021 equipment rebalance. Although it is held over the player's shoulder similarly to atwo-handed sword, it is a one-handed weapon and can be used with ashield.
