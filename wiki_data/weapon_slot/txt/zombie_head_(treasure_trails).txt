[Weapon: Zombie Head (Treasure Trails)]


Azombie headis a weapon obtained as a reward fromhardTreasure Trails. It is wielded in the main weapon-slot and has the same bonuses as abronze warhammer.
It features sounds of azombie's defensive sounds when the player attacks an opponent, and sounds of a zombie's death when wielded, unwielded and stored into amahogany treasure chest.
