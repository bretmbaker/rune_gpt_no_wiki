[Weapon: Staff]


Astaffis a cheapmagic weaponwhich provides modest magical bonuses and theautocastoption when equipped. Due to not possessing anyelementalfeatures and offering poor combat stats, it is only useful as very cheap option for autocasting.
The staff can most easily be purchased fromZaff's staff shopinVarrock. It can also be bought on theGrand Exchange, obtained through other players or dropped by various enemies.
