[Weapon: Red Topaz Machete]


Thered topaz macheteis a tribal weapon that can be used to chopJungleonKaramja. It is the highest tier of machete, and offers the best chance to successfully cut through the jungles.
It can also be used as a weak low level weapon. The machete can be bought fromGabooty's shop after the completion of the questJungle Potion. This can be found in theTai Bwo Wannai VillageonKaramja.Gabootytrades the machete for 2,400trading sticks(or 2000 if you are wearingkaramja gloves 1). Additionally,Safta Doctrades 1,200 trading sticks, agout tuber, and threered topazfor the machete. The red topaz can be either cut or uncut.
