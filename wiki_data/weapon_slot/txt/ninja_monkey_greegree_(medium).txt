[Weapon: Ninja Monkey Greegree (Medium)]


Themedium ninja monkey greegreeis used for transforming into amedium ninja monkeyonApe Atoll. To make one, a player must take amonkey talismanandmedium ninja monkey bonestoZooknock, located at the very end of theApe Atoll Dungeon.
There are two different ninja greegrees. This type is made using bones fromPadulahor thenamedlevel 149monkey guardssummoned by Ape Atoll residents, although players don't have to kill the summoned guards (Karamkills them). It is recommended to let Karam kill a guard instead, as fighting Padulah may be more difficult.
