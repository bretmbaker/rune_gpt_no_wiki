[Weapon: Keris Partisan Of Corruption]


Thekeris partisan of corruptionis a one-handed spear requiring 80Attackto equip. It is made by attaching theeye of the corruptoron thekeris partisan, granting it a special attack which can only be used within theTombs of Amascutraid. As with the keris partisan, weapon poisons cannot be applied to the partisan.
The keris partisan of corruption retains the effects of the keris partisan, dealing 33% bonus damage against allkalphitesandscabarites, alongside a 1/51 chance of finding a gap in their exoskeleton, dealing triple damage. When this special effect occurs, the chatbox will display the message:You slip your weapon through a gap in the creature's chitin, landing a vicious blow.

[Special Attack]
A player performing the Keris partisan of corruption 's special attack,Wrath of Amascut.
The keris partisan of corruption has aspecial attack,Wrath of Amascut, which consumes 75% of the player's special attack energy. The special attack, for the next hit, provides a 100% increase to accuracy and a 25% increase to the player's damage.
The target hit by the special attack will also receive 25% increased damage for 10ticks(6 seconds); the special attack must be asuccessful hitin order for it to take effect. However, players performing the special attack will be unable to attack for 8 ticks (4.8 seconds). As such, it is recommended to have teammates focus on the target hit by the special attack afterwards if possible.
