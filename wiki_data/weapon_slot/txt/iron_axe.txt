[Weapon: Iron Axe]


Theiron axeis the second weakestaxeinOld School RuneScape, being slightly better than thebronze axe. It can be used incombator inWoodcuttingto cuttrees. An iron axe has the same level requirements as a bronze axe, making it the better choice for new players.
The success rate for chopping a log is approximately 150% that of theBronze axe.
