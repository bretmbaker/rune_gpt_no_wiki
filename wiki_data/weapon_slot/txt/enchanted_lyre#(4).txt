[Weapon: Enchanted Lyre#(4)]


Theenchanted lyreis acquired inThe Fremennik Trialsquest. It is used in the quest to perform in the longhall to getOlaf the Bard's vote for the player to become aFremennik. Thelyrecan be made by using anaxeto cut a branch from theswaying treeeast ofRellekka, and then using aknifeon thebranchto obtain anunstrung lyre, then attaching spungolden fleece. This part needs 25Fletching. Alternatively, players may receive one as a drop fromLanzig,BorrokarorFreidirin their huts in the south-eastern area of the village.
After the quest, the player can enchant their normal lyre into an enchanted lyre by charging it withFossegrimento be used as ateleporttoRellekka. When thehard Fremennik Diaryis completed, the enchanted lyre can also teleport toWaterbirth Island, and gains the ability to teleport toJatizsoandNeitiznotafter completion of theelite Fremennik Diary.
