[Weapon: Machete]


Themacheteis an item used in theWoodcuttingskill. It can be obtained from the mainlandKaramja general storeandShilo Village general storeor by trading with another player. It can also be found by looking inRadimus Erkle's cupboard in theLegends' Guild; however, after the quest is complete you may no longer search the cupboard for a machete and are given the message "You don't think Radimus Erkle would be very pleased if he caught you rifling through his possessions".
The machete can be used as a weapon but is primarily used to cut Jungle during theTai Bwo Wannai Cleanupminigame. It has no requirements to wield though cuttingLight,Medium, andDense junglerequires 10, 20, and 35 Woodcutting to harvest respectively. A machete is also required to gain access toKharazi Jungleif the player doesn't have access to the level 79agility shortcutto the south-east ofShilo Village.
