[Weapon: Bronze Longsword]


Thebronze longswordis alongswordmade ofbronze. It is the weakest longsword available, and any player can wield it. It requires 2bronze barsand level 6Smithingto make, granting 25 experience. The bronze longsword is available for purchase atVarrock SwordshopinVarrock. It is rarely used due to the existence of theiron longsword, which is stronger and has the same requirements. It can be found as a drop from various monsters.
