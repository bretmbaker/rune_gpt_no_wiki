[Weapon: Saradomin Staff]


TheSaradomin staffis agod staffthat can only be obtained by finishing theMage Arenaminigame. Like the other God staves, this staff requires level 60Magicto acquire and therefore wield. To get it, the player must defeatKolodionthe mage in all of his forms. Afterwards, they will have to pick a god to worship to receive a cape and the staff corresponding to that god. If the player has already chosen another god, the staff can be purchased from theChamber Guardianfor 80,000coins.
The staff allows wielders to cast theSaradomin Strikespell, which must be cast 100 times inside the Mage Arena minigame to gain the ability to use it outside of the arena. The spell can deal a maximum of 20 damage. Saradomin Strike lowers the target'sPrayerlevel by one point on each successful cast.
