[Weapon: Purple Flowers]


Purple flowersare a type offlowerthat can be harvested after plantingmithriloradamant seeds.
When planting the seeds, players have an option to either pick the flowers (and obtaining a random coloured flower) or leave them planted on the ground. A player only has one chance to pick their flowers; if they refuse to do so, theywill notget the option again unless they plant another seed. Flowers that are left on the ground will stay there temporarily for around 5 minutes.
