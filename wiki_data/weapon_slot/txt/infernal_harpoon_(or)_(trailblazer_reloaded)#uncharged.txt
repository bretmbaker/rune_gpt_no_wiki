[Weapon: Infernal Harpoon (Or) (Trailblazer Reloaded)#Uncharged]


Theinfernal harpoon (or)is aninfernal harpoonwith aTrailblazer tool ornament kitadded onto it, and then changed to theTrailblazer Reloaded Leaguestyle via the "Change-Style" option. The kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the harpoon. It can be stored in atackle box.
Players can select the "Change-Style" option to alternate between the Trailblazer Reloaded variant and the originalTrailblazervariant.
