[Weapon: Trident Of The Swamp (E)#Charged]


Thetrident of the swamp (e)is a one-handedpowered staffthat requires 78Magicto wield. It is an enhanced version of the regulartrident of the swampobtained by givingLieve McCracken10kraken tentacles, costing6,202,410 coins. It holds 20,000 charges, rather than the 2,500 charges of the unenhanced version.
To fully charge an enhanced trident, it would cost10,860,000 coins, requiring one death rune, one chaos rune, 5 fire runes, and a Zulrah scale per charge. Players can configure the charges while the trident is in their bank by right-clicking on Configure-Charges, as long as players have the required materials.
