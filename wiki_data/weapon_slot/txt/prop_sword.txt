[Weapon: Prop Sword]


Theprop swordis a weapon first obtained as a reward from the2018 Birthday event. It is the iconic sword seen in the logo ofRuneScapefrom 2001 to 2007.
While equipped, players can right-click the sword in theEquipment Statsinterface to perform an emote, in which players assume a fencing position and slash the air to create the letters "RS", the acronym for "RuneScape".
