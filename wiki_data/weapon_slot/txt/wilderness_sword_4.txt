[Weapon: Wilderness Sword 4]


TheWilderness sword 4is a reward from completing the eliteWilderness Diarytasks given to you by theLesser FanaticinEdgeville, and can be retrieved from him for free if lost.
Its stats are identical to theadamant swordbesides a lower weight, which makes it a rather inefficient weapon when compared to the skills and costs required to acquire it. The sword does not need to be equipped or held in the inventory to benefit from its Diary bonuses.
