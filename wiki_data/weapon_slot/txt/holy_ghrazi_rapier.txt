[Weapon: Holy Ghrazi Rapier]


Theholy Ghrazi rapieris aGhrazi rapierwith aholy ornament kitattached to it. The ornament kit gives no additional stat bonuses, but it does make the rapier count as aSaradominitem in theGod Wars Dungeon.
While the rapier is untradeable in its ornamented state, it can be dismantled anytime, returning the ornament kit and tradeable rapier.
