[Weapon: Steam Battlestaff]


Asteam battlestaffis a steamelemental staffwhich provides unlimited amounts ofwaterandfirerunes as well as theautocastoption when equipped. It requires 30Attackand 30Magicto wield.
Themystic steam staffis an even stronger alternative, providing better melee and magic stat bonuses. It is obtained by taking the steam battlestaff and40,000toThormac. (Reduced to30,000or20,000with completion of thehardandelite Kandarin diariesrespectively.)
