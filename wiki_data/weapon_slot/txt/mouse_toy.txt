[Weapon: Mouse Toy]


Themouse toyis afun weaponobtained fromBob the Catafter completion of the questA Tail of Two Cats. Occasionally, if the player wields the toy and has a petcatfollowing them, the cat will say "Meow" and perform a pouncing animation at it.[1]
The attack animation is similar to theabyssal whip. When it is used to attack, it makes the same noise as arubber chicken"whacking" someone. This item is not commonly seen, as its damage and accuracy bonuses are noticeably poor.
