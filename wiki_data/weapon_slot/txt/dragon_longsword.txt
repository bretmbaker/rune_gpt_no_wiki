[Weapon: Dragon Longsword]


Thedragon longswordis the second-strongestlongswordavailable inOld School RuneScape, and can only be wielded by players who have at least 60Attackand have completed theLost Cityquest.
As with alldragon weapons, players cannot make this item using theSmithingskill. However, it can be purchased in theZanaris marketplacefromJukatfor 100,000 coins (requires aDiamondfor entry). The weapon can also be received as a reward from eliteTreasure Trails.

[Special Attack]
A player performing the dragon longsword's special attack,Cleave.
The dragon longsword has aspecial attackcalledCleave, which raises the player's maximum hit by 25% for one hit, consuming 25% of the player's special attack energy. The accuracy roll forCleaveis based on the attack bonus of the player's currently selected style, which is rolled against an opponent's slash defence.[1]
