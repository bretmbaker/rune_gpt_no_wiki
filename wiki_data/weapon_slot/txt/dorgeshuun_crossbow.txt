[Weapon: Dorgeshuun Crossbow]


TheDorgeshuun crossbowis a crossbow requiring level 28Rangedto equip. It is often used to trainRangedat low to medium levels, due to it having several advantages over similarcrossbowswith the same level requirements. It has the same attack bonus as aniron crossbow.
It has an attack speed that is onetickfaster than most other crossbows, and it is able to firebone bolts, which are significantly cheaper than most other ammunition of similar strength. The fact it is a crossbow also gives it an advantage overshortbows, allowing the use of theshield slotfor defensive or offensive purposes.

[Special Attack]
A player performs the Dorgeshuun crossbow's special attack,Snipe.
The Dorgeshuun crossbow has aspecial attack,Snipe(75%), which will never miss as long as the target has not been damaged previously or you weren't the last one to damage the target.[1]It lowers the target'sDefenceby the amount of damage dealt, similar to theBandos godsword's andbone dagger's special attacks. The defence lowering is only applied when the target doesn't currently have their defence levels lowered.[2]
The special attack requires completion of thequestDeath to the Dorgeshuun.
