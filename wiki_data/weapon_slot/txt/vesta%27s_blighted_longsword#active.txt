[Weapon: Vesta%27S Blighted Longsword#Active]


Vesta's blighted longswordis an untradeable sword that can be purchased atJustine's stuff for the Last Shopper Standingfor 300Last Man Standingpoints. It requires 75Attackboth to wield it and to purchase it from the reward shop.[1]
The sword is inactive upon purchase, and must be activated by using50,000,000coins on it. Once activated, it becomes equipable, sharing the same stats andspecial attackas itsother variants. However, the blighted longsword can only be used on aBounty Hunterworld while in theWildernessor insideDaimon's Crater.[2]This restriction applies to bothPvMandPvPcombat activities.
