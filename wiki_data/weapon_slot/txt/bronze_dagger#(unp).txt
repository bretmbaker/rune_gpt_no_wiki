[Weapon: Bronze Dagger#(Unp)]


Thebronze daggeris the most basicdaggerinOld School RuneScape, and can be made at ananvilusing theSmithingskill. This requires onebronze barand ahammer, and yields 12.5 Smithingexperience. There are no level requirements to create or wield a bronze dagger; like many melee weapons, it can bepoisoned.
