[Weapon: Keris Partisan Of The Sun]


Thekeris partisan of the sunis a one-handed spear requiring 80Attackto equip. It is made by attaching thejewel of the sunto thekeris partisan, granting it a special attack that can only be used within theTombs of Amascutraid. As with the keris partisan, weapon poisons cannot be applied to the partisan.
The keris partisan of the sun retains the effects of the keris partisan, dealing 33% bonus damage against allkalphitesandscabarites, alongside a 1/51 chance of puncturing a hole in their exoskeleton, dealing triple damage. When this special effect occurs, the chatbox will display the message:You slip your weapon through a gap in the creature's chitin, landing a vicious blow.
