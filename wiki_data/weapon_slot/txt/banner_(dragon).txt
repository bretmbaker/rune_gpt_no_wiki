[Weapon: Banner (Dragon)]


ABanneris a decorative flag made on abanner easelin aplayer-owned housewith the symbol of adragon. They can be created at 48Craftingwith 1plank, 1bolt of cloth, and the appropriate crest assigned bySir Renitee.
Players must have completed theDragon Slayer Iquestin order to make the dragon banner. Sir Renitee will not assign a crest unless the player has over level 16Construction. Players can use a banner easel in another player's house if they do not have one.
