[Weapon: Imbued Tephra]


Imbued tephrais obtained by imbuingrefined tephraat theSeren altarin the western end ofZalcano's prison. It can also be obtained fromgolemsthat Zalcano occasionally summons.
The imbued tephra is the only item that can deal damage to Zalcano while she is still standing; the damage dealt is scaled based on the player'sSmithingandRunecraftlevels. In addition, if she has summoneddemonic symbolsaround the prison, the blue symbols will increase the damage dealt. Players can either attack Zalcano directly while having the imbued tephra in their inventory to use them, or equip and throw them at her.
