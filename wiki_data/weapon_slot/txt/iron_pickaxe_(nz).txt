[Weapon: Iron Pickaxe (Nz)]


Aniron pickaxe (nz)is aNightmare Zoneexclusive version of theiron pickaxe, and shares its combat stats. It spawns on the ground during a Nightmare Zone session only ifSlagilithis present and the player'sAttackis lower than 20. This is because a pickaxe is the only way to damage Slagilith.
As with all items with the (nz) suffix, it cannot be taken outside of the Nightmare Zone, and if discarded, can be retrieved from the same place it spawned.
