[Weapon: Jade Machete]


Ajade machetecan be used to repair theTai Bwo Wannaigates. It is the second-highest tier ofmachete, outclassed only by thered topaz machete.
It may be obtained by purchasing it fromSafta Docin northern Tai Bwo Wannai Village with items, or fromGabooty'sCooperative storein the village gates for 1200trading sticks(1000 if you are wearing any tier of theKaramja gloves). You will need to get one from Safta Doc for theKaramja Achievement Diary, by exchanging aGout Tuber, 3 cut/uncut jade and 600 trading sticks.
