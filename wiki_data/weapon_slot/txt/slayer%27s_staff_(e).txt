[Weapon: Slayer%27S Staff (E)]


Theslayer's staff (e)is an upgraded version of theslayer's staff, obtained by using aslayer's enchantmenton it. It requires 75Magicand 55Slayerto wield. It can be used to castMagic Dartlike the normal version of the staff.
Upon enchanting the staff, it becomes untradeable and gains 2,500 charges. Once the charges run out, it will revert to a regular Slayer's staff, and an additional Slayer's enchantment must be used on it.
