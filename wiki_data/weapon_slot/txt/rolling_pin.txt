[Weapon: Rolling Pin]


Therolling pinis ameleekitchen weaponthat can be bought from theCulinaromancer's chest. To purchase, the player must have completed 6 of theRecipe for Disastersubquests. To wield, in addition to having 40Attack, 7 subquests must be completed. This weapon has the same stats as therune mace, including theprayer bonus. However, unlike the mace, it did not get updated to an attack speed of 4 along with thefirst stage of the 2021 equipment rebalance.
