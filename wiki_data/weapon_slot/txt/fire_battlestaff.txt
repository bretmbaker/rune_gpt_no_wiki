[Weapon: Fire Battlestaff]


Thefire battlestaffis anelemental staffrequiring 30Attackand 30Magicto wield. In addition to increased Melee and Magic Attack bonuses, the staff provides unlimited amounts offire runesas well as theautocastoption when equipped.
This staff can be created at level 62Craftingby combining afire orbandbattlestaff. It can also be upgraded to amystic fire staffby payingThormac40,000coinsto upgrade it (less with the completion of certainKandarin Diaries). The mystic fire staff is an even stronger alternative, providing better melee and magic stat bonuses.
