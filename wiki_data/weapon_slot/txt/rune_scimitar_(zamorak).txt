[Weapon: Rune Scimitar (Zamorak)]


Therune scimitar (zamorak)is arune scimitarwith arune scimitar ornament kit (zamorak)attached to it. The attached ornament kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the scimitar. It costs32,171for both the items required to make the ornamented scimitar. This item will provide protection from Zamorakian forces in theGod Wars Dungeonwhen equipped.
While the scimitar is untradeable in its ornamented state, the weapon can be dismantled anytime, returning the tradeable ornament kit and scimitar.
