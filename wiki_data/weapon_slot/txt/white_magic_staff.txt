[Weapon: White Magic Staff]


Thewhite magic staffis aWhite Knightmagic weaponthat requires 10Attackand the completion of theWanted!questto wield. It has the same bonuses as amagic staff, with an added +1prayer bonus. It also provides theautocastoption when equipped.
Players can purchase it fromSir Vyvinafter achieving therankof Noble (500black knightkills). It can also be bought on the Grand Exchange or obtained through trading other players.
