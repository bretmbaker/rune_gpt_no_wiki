[Weapon: Ghrazi Rapier]


TheGhrazi rapieris a one-handedstab weaponobtainable as a rare drop from theTheatre of Blood. It requires level 80Attackto wield. It provides the highest strength and stab bonus out of any weapon for itsattack speed. This makes the rapier tied with theBlade of saeldorand theInquisitor's macefor themeleeweapon with the highest damage output for general use, although it is outperformed by theOsmumten's fangagainst high defence monsters andScythe of vituragainst large monsters.
The rapier can be combined with aholy ornament kitto make aSaradomin-themedholy Ghrazi rapier. This cosmetic upgrade won't change the stats in any way, but it will make the rapier count as a Saradomin item in theGod Wars Dungeon.
