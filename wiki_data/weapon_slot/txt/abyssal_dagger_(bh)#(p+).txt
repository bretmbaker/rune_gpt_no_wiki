[Weapon: Abyssal Dagger (Bh)#(P+)]


Theabyssal dagger (bh)is a weapon that requires level 70 inAttackto wield. It is created by using anabyssal dagger imbue scrollon anabyssal dagger, which is purchased as a reward fromBounty Hunter. As with mostdaggers, it can bepoisoned.
In comparison to the regular dagger, the imbued dagger has a +10 increased stab bonus, and the special attack's 15% damage reduction is reduced to 5%, though the cost is raised to 35% of the special bar from the original 25%.

[Special Attack]
A player performs the abyssal dagger's special attack,Abyssal Puncture.
The abyssal dagger (bh) has aspecial attack,Abyssal Puncture, which hits twice in quick succession with a 25% increase in accuracy and 5% reduced damage, consuming 35% of the player's special attack energy. A single attack roll is performed, meaning that either both hits are successful, or both hits miss. The damage calculation rolls against the opponent's slash defence.
