[Weapon: Adamant Longsword]


Theadamant longswordis alongswordthat is stronger than theMithril longsword, but weaker than theRune longsword. Like all otheradamant weapons, it requires 30Attackto wield. Players can make an adamant longsword with 76Smithingusing 2adamant bars, granting 125 experience. It can be bought from theVarrock Swordshopfor 3,200 coins.
