[Weapon: Dragon Dart#(P)]


Dragon dartsare a type ofRangedthrowing weapons. Dragon darts require level 60 Ranged to wield. They can be obtained by using theFletchingskill,tradingwith another player, or viadragon implings. Dragon darts can bepoisonedand can be wielded with ashieldorgod bookfor protection, or added into atoxic blowpipe.
To create dragon darts the player must have at least level 95 Fletching. Players must obtaindragon dart tips(these cannot besmithed) and then attachfeathersto the dart tips. 250 Fletching experience is given per set of dragon darts created.
