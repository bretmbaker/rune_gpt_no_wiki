Frost naguas are Slayer monsters that require level 48 Slayer to kill. They are found in the Ruins of Tapoyauik, accessed from the Twilight Temple.

As with their sulphurous counterparts, Frost Nagua have -4 flat armour, so all successful hits will do an extra 4 damage to them.

Protect from Magic will negate all damage from their basic attacks. Periodically, the Frost Nagua will mark the ground under the player's feet with icicles, which will erupt after a short period, draining some of the player's prayer. This can be dodged by stepping onto another tile.
