Zombie pirates can be found at the Chaos Temple in the Wilderness. Zombie pirates have an uncommon chance to drop adamant seeds, teleport anchoring scrolls and zombie pirate keys.

Medium Wilderness Diary drops[edit | edit source]

Drop rates estimated based on 1,648,437 samples from the Drop Rate Project, unless otherwise cited.

Players who have completed the Medium Wilderness Diary will have a 50% chance to receive drops, in addition to bones, from a zombie pirate kill. This is greatly boosted from the base chance of 15% for players without the diary completion, as shown in the drop table below.[1]

The average zombie pirate kill with the Medium Wilderness Diary completed is worth 5,888.53. Before completion of the diary, this is only 1,817.66.

If a player uses the Zombie pirate key drops to open the Zombie Pirate's Locker, the effective drop rate of the Teleport anchoring scroll increases approximately 4x to ~1/4904.
