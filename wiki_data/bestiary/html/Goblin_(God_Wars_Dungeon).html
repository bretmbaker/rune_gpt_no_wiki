Goblins in the God Wars Dungeon are troops for the Bandosian army from the goblin tribe, Rekeshuun, fighting against the other monsters inside the dungeon. While stronger than most goblins found elsewhere, they are still relatively weak followers of Bandos and often killed for kill count.

The level 12 goblins carrying banners are always passive and are not aggressive even without Bandosian protection. They will run away from the player when approached.
