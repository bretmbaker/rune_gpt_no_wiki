Mounted terrorbird gnomes are gnomes riding terrorbirds. They are stronger than regular unmounted terrorbirds. They are found spread around and to the south of the Tree Gnome Stronghold and the Battlefield. They may be killed for the bird Slayer task. They attack with melee and have mediocre combat stats for their level, and consequently pose little threat.
