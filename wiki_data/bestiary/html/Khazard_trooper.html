Khazard troopers are the foot soldiers of the Khazard Army. They can be found fighting gnomes in the Khazard Battlefield near West Ardougne and spectating at the Fight Arena.

While fighting gnomes, the troopers will say phrases such as "Get off of me, ya little pest!" and "Arrgh! Get the gnome!".
