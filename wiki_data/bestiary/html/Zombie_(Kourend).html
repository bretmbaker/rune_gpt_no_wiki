Zombies are monsters found in Kourend to the south of Shayzien and in the woods between Lovakengj and Arceuus. They have combat levels of 56, 70, 72, and 76 and are aggressive to players. The level 72 variant is only found near Shayzien.

All the zombies share the same drop table as the level 24 standard zombies found across Misthalin and Kandarin.
