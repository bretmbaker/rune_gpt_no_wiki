Odd-coloured cyclopes can be found in the God Wars Dungeon, as part of the Bandosian army. They are aggressive unless the player has a Bandosian item equipped, and killing them will contribute to the required kill count to enter General Graardor's arena.

Cyclopes may be killed as a substitute for hill giants on a Slayer task.
