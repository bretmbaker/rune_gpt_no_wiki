The Spiritual mage is a potent Magic Slayer monster found in the God Wars Dungeon that can easily kill low-level players. Like all monsters in the God Wars Dungeon, they are aggressive to any players that do not wear items showing loyalty to their god. Spiritual mages require 83 Slayer to kill. Completion of Death Plateau is required before Spiritual creatures can be assigned as a Slayer task.[1] Just like Spiritual rangers and Spiritual warriors, which require 63 and 68 Slayer respectively, there are spiritual mages for each god, and each one has unique stats.

Slaying a Spiritual mage in the God Wars Dungeon is a requirement for the Elite Fremennik Diary and slaying a Spiritual mage in the Wilderness God Wars Dungeon is a requirement for the Elite Wilderness Diary.

The normal spiritual mages are mostly known for dropping various runes and dragon boots, while the Zarosian mages have a vastly improved drop table, most notably that they can drop the boots as an additional drop alongside the main drop.

Mechanics[edit | edit source]

The Zamorakian variant casts Flames of Zamorak, which will drain a Magic level when it successfully hits and deals damage to the player. The Armadylean variant, being a former aviansie, cannot be directly attacked with melee and will attack back when in range and clicked on. The Zarosian variant, being much stronger than the other four, casts spells from the Ancient Magicks; smoke inflicts poison starting at 4 damage, shadow lowers the player's Attack level by up to 15 levels (subsequent activations will not reapply until the player's Attack level is nearly restored), blood heals the mage for 20% of the damage dealt and ice freezes the player for 5 seconds. While damage from successful hits are blocked with Protect from Magic, it does not stop secondary effects from occurring.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Spiritual mages will use Magic against players, but will use a weak Ranged attack with a max hit of 1 when fighting other monsters. As a result, they often remain in combat until killed.
  • The Bandosian spiritual mages do not move when idle unless attacking a player, and are one of the few monsters to have this trait.
  • Spiritual creatures are blocked by 113,241 players and was the most blocked slayer monster in 2017.[confirmation needed]
