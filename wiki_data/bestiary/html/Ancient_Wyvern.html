Ancient Wyverns are Slayer monsters that require a Slayer level of 82 in order to be harmed. They reside in the Wyvern Cave on Fossil Island and are the strongest species of Wyvern in Old School RuneScape. Like with other Wyverns, an elemental shield, mind shield, dragonfire shield, dragonfire ward or ancient wyvern shield provides significant protection against their icy breath. These monsters require completion of the Bone Voyage quest to be assigned as a Slayer task.

In addition to being harder to fight, Ancient Wyverns share the ability with Taloned Wyverns and Long-tailed Wyverns to use a Typeless Magic attack that resembles a lighter coloured Water Blast. This attack ignores Protection prayers and hits for up to 10 damage, though it can also miss. They also use a Slash melee attack. When fighting them, the highest defensive armour the player can access is best suited to use against them. Should one choose to use ranged to fight them, it is recommended to fight in melee range and use Protect from Melee after de-aggroing the Wyverns.

Aggression can be removed by setting up a Tolerance zone and staying within it.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • They appear to use all attack styles when using melee, depending on the animation they use: slashing when they use their talons, stabbing when they jab with their tail and crushing when they bite the target.
  • Ancient wyverns had a festive override wearing a yellow partyhat during the 2017, 2018, and 2019 Christmas events.
