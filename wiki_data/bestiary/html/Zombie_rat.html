A zombie rat is a monster only found in Melzar's Maze. It comes in two sizes, but both have the same stats. It is quite similar to the giant rat except with a few cuts, gashes, and a washed-out colour. The small zombie rat with a long tail is the only variant that will drop the red key. Players may choose to equip a salve amulet for a combat bonus against this creature.
