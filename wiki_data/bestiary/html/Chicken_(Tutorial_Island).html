Chickens are found on Tutorial Island as part of the Magic training tutorial. The Magic Instructor provides air and mind runes to cast Wind Strike on a chicken to progress in the tutorial.

The chickens are located behind a fence, so they can't be attacked using melee, and any dropped bones can't be picked up.
