Borrokar is one of Rellekka's many citizens. Borrokar lives in a hut next to the Rellekka mine. He also drops lyres which can be used in The Fremennik Trials, rather than having to make them with 25 Fletching. At level 45 Thieving and after The Fremennik Trials, he can be pickpocketed for 40 coins and 65 Thieving experience.
