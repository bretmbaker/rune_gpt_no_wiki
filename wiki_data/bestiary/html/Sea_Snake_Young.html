Sea Snake Young are found in the Miscellania and Etceteria Dungeon, which can be entered during and after the Royal Trouble quest. The snakes are poisonous; their poison damage starts at 8 Hitpoints during the quest and 7 Hitpoints after the quest. Players fighting them should bring poison protection of some sort, such as Antipoison, Antipoison+, or Antipoison++. They attack with Melee and are vulnerable to Magic.

Players can use the blue eggs halfway through the tunnel as a safespot.
