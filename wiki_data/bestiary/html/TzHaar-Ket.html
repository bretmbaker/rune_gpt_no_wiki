The TzHaar-Ket are guards of Mor Ul Rek.

While fighting a TzHaar-Ket, players can go into the Fight Caves and die to fully heal themselves and recharge Prayer. A TzHaar-Ket can become aggressive when any nearby TzHaar is attacked up to six tiles away from TzHaar-Ket. For example, if a TzHaar-Hur is attacked within a six tile radius, TzHaar-Ket will yell, Kot e TzHaar-Hur!.

All TzHaar have an uncommon chance to drop uncut sapphires, uncut emeralds, and uncut rubies as well as a rare chance to drop uncut diamonds. The drop rates listed below for materials (gems and Onyx bolt tips) increase by 33% when wearing a charged Ring of wealth.

It is possible to safespot the TzHaar-Ket by standing behind sulphur vents found within Mor Ul Rek and using ranged or magic.

There are stronger TzHaar-Ket located in the inner area of the city which drop pieces of the obsidian armour set. In order to access these, a fire cape must be shown at the barrier to the inner city.
