Alomone is the leader of the Cult of Hazeel, a group dedicated to the revival of the Zamorakian mahjarrat Hazeel.

The cult stole a set of armour from Ceril Carnillean then broke in four more times in search of the scroll required to revive Hazeel, but failed each time.[1][2] In Hazeel Cult, Ceril commissions the player to retrieve the armour, pointing them to the cult's lair in the sewers.

Players may choose to side with Hazeel during the Hazeel Cult quest, in which Alomone will accept Clivet's recommendation to let the player join the cult, welcoming them as the newest member of the Cult of Hazeel. He tasks them with finding the scroll needed to revive Hazeel, which they manage to do, allowing the cult to revive Hazeel. If the player chooses not to side with him, Alomone must be killed later on in the quest in order to retrieve the stolen armour from a nearby chest. He can be recoiled. The drop trick can be used to obtain more than one by looting the chest multiple times.

He also plays a small role in Secrets of the North if the player sided with Hazeel, summoning both Hazeel when the player requests him to, and General Khazard when requested to by Hazeel.
