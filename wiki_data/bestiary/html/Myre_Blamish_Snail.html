The Myre Blamish Snail is a common snail which lives in and around the Mort Myre Swamp. Their attacks deal Ranged damage despite having a similar animation to Curse. Like other snails found within Mort Myre, wearing a snelm will reduce the damage taken by their attacks.

Two variants of the snail exist in close proximity with each other - one with a round shell and another with a pointed shell, both of which are 100% drops from their respective variants. A chisel can be used on the dropped shell to make a myre snelm.
