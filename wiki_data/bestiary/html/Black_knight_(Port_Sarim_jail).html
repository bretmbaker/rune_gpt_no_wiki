A unique black knight can be found locked up in the jail at Port Sarim, which only drops bones when killed rather than the usual variety of items. The knight's cell cannot be entered, though the player can use Ranged, Magic, or long-distance melee weapons, such as halberds, to attack him through the bars.

Unlike most black knights, killing this one does not contribute the player's White Knight rank.
