Ranging Ro is a breach monster in Deadman: Armageddon.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • She appears to be wearing karil's leathertop, karil's leatherskirt, black chinchompas, a crystal shield, pegasian boots, an archer's helm, 3rd age vambraces, and a necklace of anguish.
