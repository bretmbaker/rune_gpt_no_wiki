The Knights of Varlamore are found in Civitas illa Fortis, around the Kualti Headquarters. Two are also found in the Sunrise Palace.

Like the Knight of Ardougne, they can be pickpocketed at level 55 Thieving, giving the player a coin pouch worth 50 coins and 84.3 Thieving experience.
