Ice warriors are mid-level elemental warriors. Ice warriors can be formidable foes to those with low levels. Ice warriors can be assigned by Krystilia for a Wilderness Slayer task, or by Vannaka or Mazchna for normal tasks.

Kamil fought during Desert Treasure I is considered an ice warrior for a Slayer assignment.

Strategy[edit | edit source]

Ice warriors have good accuracy and can cut through Ranged and Magic armour with ease. They also seem to have higher levels of aggression than many other mobs as they'll often engage the player from a rather long distance. Food such as Swordfish or better is advised. Wearing adamant or Rune armour will be a good option, as the warrior's melee attacks will be less effective. Ice warriors are weak against magic spells and crush weapons.

In the Asgarnian Ice Dungeon, they can be safespotted by trapping an ice warrior on one side of a plus shaped formation of rocks (not to be confused with the floor ice spikes) in the north-western area of their room.

At the White Wolf Mountain they can be safespotted directly south of the dungeon marker, or in the ladder area (1 step west of middle ladder or east of tree. Switch to another warrior if they pull you from your spot). This method requires a pickaxe and level 50 Mining to clear the landslide.
