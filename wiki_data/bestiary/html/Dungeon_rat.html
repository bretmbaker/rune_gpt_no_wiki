Dungeon rats are rare monsters found only in select members' dungeons. They play a key role in the Clock Tower quest in which players poison their food trough so that they attempt to escape by chewing away a railing, which allows the player to grab the white cog.

Drops (short tail)[edit | edit source]

All dungeon rats besides one drop only bones and the rat's tail.

The following drop rates are provided by Jagex, unless otherwise specified.

100%[edit | edit source]

Item Quantity Rarity Price High Alch
Bones1Always730
Rat's tail1Always[d 1]Not sold1
  1. ^ Rat's tails are only dropped during Witch's Potion.

Drops (full tail)[edit | edit source]

The variant of the large dungeon rat that has a full tail, which is found once in the Goblin Cave and once in the western part of the Clock Tower Dungeon, has a similar drop table to giant rats. The small rats with a full tail do not fall under this drop table.

100%[edit | edit source]
