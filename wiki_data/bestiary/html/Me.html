Me is a foe encountered during the quest Lunar Diplomacy in the Dream World. It is fought during the quest, and is an exact copy of the player wearing full Lunar equipment, only inversed in colour. Despite its high Combat level, it is very weak.

"Me" only attacks by hitting the player with the Lunar staff.

It can be fought in the Nightmare Zone after the quest is complete.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
14 September 2022
(update)

The line in which Me says "You do know those clothes make you look fat!" has been replaced by "You've got an outrageous accent, you silly thing!"

28 May 2020
(update)

The model for the female version of Me was updated to better hold her Lunar staff.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The model for Me always appears as male in the Nightmare Zone, even if the player's character is female.
  • The line "Do I look bothered?" is a reference to Catherine Tate's character Lauren Cooper.
  • The line "You've got an outrageous accent, you silly thing!" is a reference to the taunting Frenchman in Monty Python and the Holy Grail.
