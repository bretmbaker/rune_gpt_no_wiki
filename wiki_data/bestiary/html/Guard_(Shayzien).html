Kourend guards are Shayzien soldiers located around the kingdom of Kourend. They have identical stats and drops to regular guards. Players with 40 Thieving can pickpocket them.

They appear to be wearing a black scimitar and a black kiteshield. They have a tougher version, the Head Guard.
