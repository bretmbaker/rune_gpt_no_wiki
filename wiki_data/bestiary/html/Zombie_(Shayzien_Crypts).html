Zombies are level 132 undead monsters found in the Shayzien Crypts. The crypts were constructed long before the founding of Great Kourend 1,510 years ago, but within the last few hundred years were overrun by zombies and skeletons.

There are three types of zombies found in the crypts, with each variant attacking with a different combat style. Because of their high maximum hit and the fact that the crypts are a multicombat area, fighting these zombies can be quite difficult. Strong armour coupled with prayer (pray against magic is strongly recommended) can help to mitigate their damage, and the Salve Amulet and its variants are helpful in dealing damage to quickly kill them.

Killing a zombie within the Shayzien Crypts is a task in the Hard Kourend & Kebos Diary.
