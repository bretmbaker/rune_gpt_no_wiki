The screaming twisted banshee is a superior variant of the twisted banshee. It has a chance of spawning after purchasing the unlock Bigger and Badder for 150 Slayer reward points from any Slayer master.

Drops[edit | edit source]

In addition to 100% drops and tertiary drops, superior slayer monsters will always roll 3 additional items, of which only 1 roll may be from the superior unique table. The other rolls are performed on the regular counterpart's table; their displayed drop chances represent a single roll.

Drop rates estimated based on 35,191 samples from the Drop Rate Project, unless otherwise cited.

The average Screaming twisted banshee kill is worth 97,369.82.

The average Screaming twisted banshee kill while on a Konar task is worth 97,369.82.
