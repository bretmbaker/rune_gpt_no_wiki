A cyclops (plural cyclopes) is a large one-eyed giant. They are known for dropping many alchable drops, so bringing nature and fire runes is helpful as it allows players to make longer trips and more money without banking or taking up inventory space. They can only be attacked with melee.

Cyclopes may be killed as a substitute for hill giants on a Slayer task.

Defenders[edit | edit source]

Main article: Defender

Cyclopes in the Warriors' Guild have a rare chance of dropping a defender. These defensive weapons range from bronze to dragon and must be obtained in order, starting with bronze, by killing cyclopes on the top floor of the Warriors' Guild and then showing the defender to Kamfreena outside the chamber. Once a player earns the rune defender, they can enter the basement of the Warriors' Guild and kill the stronger cyclopes found there for a chance at the dragon defender. As this is located in the Warriors' Guild, cyclopes found in these areas are immune to Ranged and Magic attacks and must be fought with melee combat.

[Note]: You do not need to have the defender in your inventory to be able to get the next one. The only requirement is that you use the defender on Kamfreena when you get the drop.
