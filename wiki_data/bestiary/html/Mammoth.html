Mammoths are a species of elephant-like creatures that inhabit the area south-east of the Ferox Enclave.

Mammoths can be easily safespotted due to their large size (3x3). Players can use the many trees or tree stumps in the area to safespot them from behind. It is an efficient place to train with a Dwarf multicannon due to the high HP of the Mammoths, a nearby altar providing unlimited Prayer for boosts and the short walk to the Enclave for resupplying. However, players are vulnerable to Player killers while training and are therefore advised not to carry too many supplies on them at once.

Killing a Mammoth is a task in the Easy Wilderness Diary.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Mammoths were originally a cut race in RuneScape 2. A Behind the Scenes article from March 2004 promised that one of the upcoming updates would be "A large 'frozen lands' area including penguins, mammoths, polar bears and more.". The original version of Mammoths remained in the game files long after the Wilderness Mammoths were added, only being removed in 2017 when Mammoths became a Slayer task.
  • Mammoths first made their appearance at an exhibit in Prifddinas during Old School RuneScape's first anniversary. They were added to the Wilderness with the Wilderness Rejuvenation update of 13 March 2014.
  • Mammoths are referred to as 'elephantine' in their examine texts despite no elephants existing in Gielinor for them to resemble.
  • Mammoths have had a festive override wearing the wise old man's santa hat during every Christmas event since 2018.
