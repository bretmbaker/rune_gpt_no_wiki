Undead Zealot are zombies that have a 5% chance to spawn[1] when a player opens any chest in the Shade Catacombs. Only the player who opens the chest to spawn them will be able to attack them.

They will always drop bleached bones, which are used on the Altar of the Damned to recharge a player's prayer points to full like a regular altar.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Despite being in a single-way combat area, if multiple of these enemies are spawned by one player at once, they can all attack at the same time.
