Tstanon Karlak is one of K'ril Tsutsaroth's bodyguards and can be found in the God Wars Dungeon, along with Zakl'n Gritch and Balfrug Kreeyath. Tstanon attacks with Melee and has a maximum hit of 15. Tstanon is usually not considered a threat compared to K'ril, since most players already have Protect from Melee active while fighting the Zamorak bosses.

Tstanon Karlak is considered a greater demon for the purpose of a Slayer task. As with most demons, he is susceptible to the effects of demonbane weapons.
