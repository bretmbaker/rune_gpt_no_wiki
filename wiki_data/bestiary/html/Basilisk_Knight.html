Basilisk Knights are a Slayer creature that can be found in Jormungand's Prison beneath the Island of Stone, requiring level 60 Slayer and completion of The Fremennik Exiles to kill. Like cockatrice and basilisks, players must equip a mirror shield or V's shield when fighting these monsters or they will take heavy damage and their stats will be drained.

The basilisk knight has an improved drop table from regular basilisks, and can drop the basilisk jaw, which can be used to upgrade the Helm of Neitiznot to the Neitiznot faceguard. It should be noted that the basilisk jaw drops at an extremely low rate (1/5000) when not on a Basilisk Slayer task (purchased and unlocked via Slayer Rewards). Killing Basilisk Knights while on task grants a drop rate five times higher (1/1000) than killing regularly.

Players cannot use a dwarf multicannon on Basilisk Knights, as the only places players can place a cannon in Jormungand's Prison are those which contain dagannoth. Additionally, players cannot resurrect thralls in Jormungand's Prison.

While on a basilisk Slayer task, there is a 1/200 chance that a Basilisk Sentinel will spawn after you kill a Basilisk Knight (if you have Bigger and Badder unlocked).
