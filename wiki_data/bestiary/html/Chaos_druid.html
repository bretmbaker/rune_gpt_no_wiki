Chaos druids are low-level humans commonly killed for their frequent drops of herbs. Unlike other druids, they worship Zamorak. They attack with their fists and make use of a spell that appears to be a combination of the Confuse and Bind spells, binding their target very briefly if successful.

Players who decide to kill chaos druids in the Edgeville Dungeon should beware of player killers who disguise themselves in Zamorak robes to take on the appearance of a chaos druid. If you attack another player, you will become skulled and potentially lose all of your items.
