A soldier (tier 5) is a Shayzien soldier from Great Kourend. Players can fight her in the Combat Ring to obtain Shayzien armour (tier 5), provided that they have a set of tier 4 or 5 Shayzien armour on them or in their bank or Armour case, as well as at least level 20 Defence.

Players who do not wish to fight soldiers for armour can alternatively smith a tier 5 Shayzien armour set with lovakite bars, requiring a Smithing level of 93 for a full set.
