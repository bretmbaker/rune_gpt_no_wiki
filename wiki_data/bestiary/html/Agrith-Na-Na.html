Agrith Na-Na is a banana-based Agrith-Naar, from the Shadow of the Storm quest, fought in the final battle of the Recipe for Disaster quest. He attacks with Magic and melee and has good defensive stats. He can be fought in the Nightmare Zone after the quest is complete.

Strategy[edit | edit source]

Using Ranged with black dragonhide armour, an archer helm, and a magic shortbow using rune arrows or a rune crossbow with diamond bolts (e) are recommended.

Using melee with crush defence armour and a dragon scimitar or abyssal whip with a dragon dagger for special attacks.

Low-level players can use Rings of recoil, and with low Defence can opt to attack and run repeatedly as he will attempt to get into melee range while his teleport wastes one of his attack cycles. Spam clicking away from him will allow him to land only one hit.

Note: The player cannot use Prayer in this boss fight or any other Recipe for Disaster boss fight.
