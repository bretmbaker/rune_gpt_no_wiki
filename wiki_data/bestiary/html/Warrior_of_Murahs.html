Warriors of Murahs were NPCs that wandered around the 2018 Summer Seasonal Deadman Mode finals arena. They were later removed and readded to the game's cache with the release of Ferox Enclave, but cannot be found anywhere in game.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The shield equipped by the spear and scimitar wielding guards appears to be the defensive shield from the Warriors' Guild.
