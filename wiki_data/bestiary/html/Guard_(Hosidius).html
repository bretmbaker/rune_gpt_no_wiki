Hosidius guards can be found around Hosidius, in Great Kourend. In most ways, they act like common guards, sharing the same drops and being pickpocketable at level 40 Thieving for the same 30 coins and 46.8 Thieving experience. However, unlike regular guards, some Hosidius guards are equipped with bows, and thus attack with range.
