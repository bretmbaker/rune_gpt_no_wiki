Colonel Radick is located in Yanille near the west gate and has extremely high hitpoints for his combat level. He is probably the Colonel of the Yanille Army. He does not play any role in any quest despite being a unique character.

When talking to him, he asks if the player is a friend or a foe. Of course, if the friend option is chosen, he will let you go but if the foe one is chosen, he will attack the player. When asked why Yanille is so heavily defended, he explains that as the southernmost city of Kandarin it must be protected from the Ogres of the Feldip Hills.
