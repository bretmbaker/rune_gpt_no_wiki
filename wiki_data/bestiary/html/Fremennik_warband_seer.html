Fremennik warband seers are enemies encountered in the Fortis Colosseum. They appear at the start of each wave (except wave 12 without Quartet), alongside a Fremennik warband archer and Fremennik warband berserker. Attacking them with ranged will always result in a max hit. If attacking with Ice Barrage, the player needs at least a +104 magic attack bonus to guarantee a freeze, although this will not guarantee a max hit.

They have smart pathing and will path around pillars to reach you. They attack on a fixed 6 tick cycle, and can only attack when they are not moving. They only attack in melee range.
