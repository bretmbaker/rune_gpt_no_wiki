The Dramen Tree Spirit only appears in Entrana Dungeon when doing the Lost City quest. Players have to fight it with limited armour and weapons, as it only appears on Entrana.

During the quest, it is also considered a ghost for the purposes of a Slayer task. It can be fought in the Nightmare Zone after the quest is complete, but will not count towards a ghost task. It also loses its undead attribute when fought in the Nightmare Zone.

The Dramen Tree Spirit drops nothing, but allows the player to cut dramen branches from the Dramen tree when defeated, which players can use to finish the Lost City, or make cinnamon for the Sir Amik Varze subquest in Recipe for Disaster.

Strategy[edit | edit source]

It can be ranged or maged safely in a safespot using materials allowed on Entrana. It is not advised to use the bronze axe dropped from the zombies to kill it as the axe is weak and the tree spirit has high defence. It's easy to range the tree spirit, or use magic, with it being weak to Crumble Undead. Arrows, unstrung bows, and bow strings are allowed on the island. Simply string the bow when you reach the dungeon. For those who prefer not to use ranged or magic attacks, the ring of recoil is an option. However if you take too long, after about 10 minutes, the spirit will despawn and you will have to refight it. The spirit will respawn after attempting to chop the tree again.
