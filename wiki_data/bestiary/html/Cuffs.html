This article is about the NPC Cuffs. For light cuffs, see light tuxedo cuffs. For dark cuffs, see dark tuxedo cuffs.

Cuffs is a thug who can be found loitering north of Falador, along with his associates Narf, Jeff, and Rusty. Since he is level 3 (compared to his friends, who are level 2) it is implied he is the leader/strongest member of the gang.

He gives the same amount of coins as well as similar drops and Thieving experience as a man.
