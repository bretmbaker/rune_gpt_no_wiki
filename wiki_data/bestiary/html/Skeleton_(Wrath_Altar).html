Skeletons are found in the Wrath Altar across the chasm. Despite having a combat level of 35, they cannot be attacked by the player. Monster Examine cannot be cast on them, but they attack with melee, a max hit of 3 and around 50 hitpoints.

They are often engaged in fighting each other.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • If the player tries to attack a skeleton while they are in combat with another skeleton, the message "Someone else is fighting that." appears in the players chatbox. However if the player tries to attack when the skeleton is not currently being attacked, the message "You can't attack this npc." appears instead.
