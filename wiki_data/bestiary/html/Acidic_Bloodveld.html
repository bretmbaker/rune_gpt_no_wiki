The Acidic Bloodveld is a monster encountered while fighting Vanstrom Klause during Sins of the Father. During the fight, Vanstrom will occasionally say: My pets will feast on your corpse! which summons the bloodveld. The bloodveld should be killed from a distance using a ranged or magic attack, or else it will explode for 35-50 damage[1]. When the bloodveld is killed, a pool of acid appears on the ground. Stepping on the pool of acid will deal additional damage.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Acidic Bloodveld has a festive override wearing a santa hat during the 2023 and 2024 Christmas event.
