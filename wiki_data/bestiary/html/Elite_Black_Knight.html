Elite Black Knights are elite units among the Kinshra who reside in the Black Knight Catacombs. They are much more powerful than regular black knights and utilise protection prayers; as such, using two combat styles is required. Additionally they have 7 Flat armour, which means they take -7 damage from attacks made against them.

Elite Black Knights count as 12 black knight kills for the purpose of accessing the White Knight Armoury.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • At combat level 138, Elite Black Knights match the maximum possible player combat level at the time of the original release of While Guthix Sleeps in RuneScape. This differs from the maximum in Old School (126) due to Summoning.
