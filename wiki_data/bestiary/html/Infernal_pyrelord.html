The infernal pyrelord is a superior version of the pyrelord. It has a chance of spawning after purchasing the unlock Bigger and Badder for 150 Slayer reward points from any Slayer master.

Drops[edit | edit source]

In addition to 100% drops and tertiary drops, superior slayer monsters will always roll 3 additional items, of which only 1 roll may be from the superior unique table. The other rolls are performed on the regular counterpart's table; their displayed drop chances represent a single roll.

The following drop rates are provided by Jagex, unless otherwise specified.

The average Infernal pyrelord kill is worth 102,929.59.
