Scorpia's guardians appear while fighting Scorpia. Two appear on the next attack after Scorpia reaches 99 health or lower, even if you splash. The guardians will each heal Scorpia for 8 health every 1.8 seconds, shown with a curse projectile animation, and can do so from several tiles away. If they do not heal Scorpia in 15 seconds, they will despawn. If killed they will not respawn, even if they manage to heal Scorpia back to full health.

Players can either freeze them with ice burst/barrage or snare and lure Scorpia away to cause them to despawn, or kill them.
