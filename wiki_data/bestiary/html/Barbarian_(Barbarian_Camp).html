Barbarians are attackable NPCs found at the Barbarian Camp in the Kourend Woodland. If the player chooses to talk to them, they will taunt the player with quotes such as "Wanna fight?" or "You look funny!" and immediately attack; players can avoid this by immediately exiting the dialogue instead of clicking to continue.
