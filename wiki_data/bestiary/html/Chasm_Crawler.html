The chasm crawler is a superior variant of the cave crawler. It has a chance of spawning after purchasing the unlock Bigger and Badder for 150 Slayer reward points from any Slayer master.

The chasm crawler has a strong poison, starting at 12 damage.

Drops[edit | edit source]

In addition to 100% drops and tertiary drops, superior slayer monsters will always roll 3 additional items, of which only 1 roll may be from the superior unique table. The other rolls are performed on the regular counterpart's table.

Drop rates estimated based on 80,500 samples from the Drop Rate Project, unless otherwise cited.

The average Chasm Crawler kill is worth 88,690.18.
