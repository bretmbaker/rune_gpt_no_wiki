Double agents appear when doing hard or master clue scroll emote clues. They call "I expect you to die!" upon appearing. When doing hard clues, double agents have a combat level of 65 in the Wilderness and 108 outside the Wilderness. When doing master clues, they have a combat level of 141.

If the double agent is killed while the player is wearing the equipment instructed to wear for the clue, Uri will immediately appear. Otherwise, the player must re-equip the items and perform the emote again.

For double agents fought in the Wilderness, only bring items you are prepared to lose.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
6 July 2016
(update)

Level 141 Double agents now appear ingame when doing Master clue scrolls.

10 September 2015
(update)
  • All treasure trail combat NPCs now cannot prevent a player from being attacked by a player inside a single combat zone of the wilderness.
  • All treasure trail combat NPCs now also do not allow a player to be attacked by several players at once in a single combat zone.
12 June 2014
(update)

Level 141 Double agents were added to the cache, presumably to appear when doing Elite clue scrolls, but were unused.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Their appearance is identical to Uri.
  • The phrase "I expect you to die!" is a reference to the 1964 film Goldfinger which was based on an Ian Fleming novel.
