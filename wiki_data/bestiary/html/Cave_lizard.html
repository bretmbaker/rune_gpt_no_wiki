Cave lizards are creatures native to the Ourania Cave. They can be seen fighting the Zamorakian guards on the path to the Ourania Altar.

Lower levelled players can take the long route to go around them. Good armour that is not too weak against any of the three combat types is advised as the area the lizards reside in is multicombat. The lizards are aggressive to players, but they prioritise Zamorakian guards over other players.

Players gain no experience for damage inflicted on these lizards, and they do not count towards a slayer assignment.
