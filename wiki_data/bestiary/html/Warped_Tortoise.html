Warped Tortoises are creatures found in the Poison Waste Dungeon beneath the Galarpos Mountains, requiring partial completion of The Path of Glouphrie to kill. Players must have the crystal chime in their inventory to fight them, otherwise they will be invulnerable.

Strategy[edit | edit source]

Warped Tortoises only attack with melee, meaning players should equip armour with melee defence. Alternatively, players can nullify all damage by praying Protect from Melee.

They can be safespotted in the Poison Waste Dungeon by traversing to one of the rooms further in the dungeon, where there are two tortoises surrounded by four pillars in the centre of the room, as well as pillars along the walls. By standing on the east or west of these four pillars in the centre, the player can attack the tortoises using ranged or mage without being attacked.

  • A safespot available south of the northern tar chamber.
