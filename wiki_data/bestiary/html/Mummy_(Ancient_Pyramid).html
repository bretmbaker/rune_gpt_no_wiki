Mummies are level 103 undead monsters found in the Ancient Pyramid during Desert Treasure I. They can drop ancient staves and can only be accessed once the four Diamonds of Azzanadra have been recovered during Desert Treasure I.

Mummies attacked with fire spells or fire arrows will be set alight. It is possible to douse a burning mummy by casting a water spell on it, which will turn the mummy back to its original state.

Despite being undead, mummies are not vulnerable to the effects of the Salve amulet.[1]

When a flaming mummy is defeated, it will temporarily become mummy ashes before dropping loot.
