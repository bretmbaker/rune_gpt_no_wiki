Bandosian guards are orks that appear and attack the player when doing coordinate steps of elite clue scrolls. Bandosian guards use only melee attacks, so Protect from Melee will prevent all damage taken from them. Upon killing the guard, players can dig a second time in the correct location in order to receive the next clue.

On clue scroll steps where the Bandosian guard can appear, alternatively an Armadylean guard may appear in its place. The lone exception is this clue on the Isle of Souls, where a Bandosian guard will always appear.
