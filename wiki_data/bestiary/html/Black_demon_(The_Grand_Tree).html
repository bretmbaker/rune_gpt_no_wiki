A unique Black demon is summoned by Glough during The Grand Tree. This demon can be re-fought in the Nightmare Zone after the quest has been completed. Killing Black demons in Nightmare Zone will count as a kill for a Slayer task.

Players can either fight the demon using melee, or hide in a safespot and use ranged, magic, or a halberd. If the demon is not killed within 8-9 minutes, it will disappear and players will have to start the battle all over again.

  • The safespot directly north of the ladder.

  • The safespot where Glough stands.
