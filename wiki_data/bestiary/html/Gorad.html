Gorad is an ogre who lives on an island north of Feldip Hills. He is killed during the Watchtower quest. He always drops two things: Big bones and the Ogre tooth required to get the second part of the Ogre relic. The Ogre tooth appears in your inventory and not on the ground so you must have an empty inventory space to receive it.

Should you speak with him and insult him at any point outside the quest section for the ogre tooth, he will deal 12 damage upon the dialogue's completion (Note: if lacking sufficient hitpoints, the damage will kill you!)
