The icelords are icy beings found inside the iceberg, and are encountered during the Cold War quest. They were found when Pescaling Pax moved much of the penguin population to the iceberg to prepare for the invasion of Gielinor. Some icelords enjoy hurling large icicles at their enemies, while others prefer to use their four arms to pummel them into submission.

After completing Cold War, icelords can be accessed by entering a crack in the ice on the iceberg, then entering the icelords' cage. Three of the icelords use melee, and the one wielding icicles uses ranged, with all of them having low magic defence.

Icelords can be slain in place of ice warriors for the purposes of completing a Slayer task. They have slightly more hitpoints than ice warriors as well as notably weaker offensive and defensive stats, however unlike ice warriors they have no elemental weakness and drop no notable loot.

Killing an icelord will award the player with a bonus 40 Attack experience during the Cold War quest.
