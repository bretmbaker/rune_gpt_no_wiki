Reanimated demon spawns are monsters that appear during the fight with Skotizo. They appear whenever Skotizo chants: Gar mulno ful taglo!, which summons three reanimated demon spawns to attack the player. Even though Skotizo can continue chanting the summoning phrase, there can only be three reanimated demon spawns present in the arena at any given time.

They only use melee to attack, although they can be ignored as the player will be using Protect from Melee, unless they are ranging or maging Skotizo. After Skotizo dies, all present spawns in the arena will disappear after approximately 3 seconds.
