A room full of ghosts can be found on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] of Melzar's Maze. They must be killed in order to obtain the orange key, which can unlock the orange doors and progress through the maze.

The orange key is always dropped by the hooded ghost with no cape, and will never be dropped by the other ghosts.

These ghosts can be spoken to with a Ghostspeak amulet; their speech hints that they were victims of Elvarg the dragon.
