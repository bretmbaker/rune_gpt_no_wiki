Guard Bandits can be found at the entrance to the Wilderness Bandit Camp. If the player tries to enter the camp, the guards will exclaim You shall not pass! and attack the player. Like most Wilderness monsters, Guard Bandits are aggressive to players of all levels. Guard Bandits usually only drop bones, as opposed to the other bandits that reside in the camp.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The shield these bandits wear has a shape resembling a wooden shield, but with the colour of a steel shield.
  • "You shall not pass!" is a reference to the Lord of the Rings.
  • In the Official RuneScape Handbook from 2006, it is stated that Guard Bandits weigh "up to 70lbs." This is likely a mistranslation from kilograms to pounds.
