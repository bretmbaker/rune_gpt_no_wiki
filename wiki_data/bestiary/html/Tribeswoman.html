Tribeswomen are warriors that inhabit the island of Karamja. They can be found in a few areas around Karamja, specifically congregated around a cauldron west of Brimhaven, and also on the eastern shore of the island near the ship yard. In both cases, tribeswomen are encountered in concert with their male counterpart, the tribesmen. Though their attacks have a fairly low max hit, any successful hit from them will inflict a potent poison, dealing 11 poison damage per hit. As a result, players are advised to bring a form of antipoison when travelling through areas where tribeswomen are known to reside.

They are not aggressive towards players with a Combat level of 65 or higher, eliminating the need to counteract their poison.
