Aberrant spectres are Slayer monsters that require level 60 Slayer to kill, and completion of Priest in Peril to receive as a slayer task. They frequently drop large numbers of grimy herbs and herb seeds. Because of this, bringing a herb sack and seed box when killing them is recommended.

Deviant spectres, found in the Catacombs of Kourend, can be killed in their place if you are assigned aberrant spectres.

Strengths and weaknesses[edit | edit source]

Wearing a nose peg or slayer helmet is required when fighting aberrant spectres; otherwise they will use a special attack which will always hit, even through Protection Prayers, and drain your combat stats extremely quickly. Boosts cannot be used to equip a nose peg, so either level 60 Slayer or a slayer helmet is required.

Aberrant spectres attack using Magic, so using the Protect from Magic prayer or wearing magic resistant armour such as dragonhide is helpful. As they are undead, the Salve amulet or one of its variants can be worn for a damage and accuracy bonus when fighting them. The Salve amulet's effects do not stack with those of a slayer helmet when both are worn, in which case only the Salve amulet effect will apply.[1] Regardless, players should still wear their slayer helmet or nose peg to protect from the spectres' unforgiving damage and stat drains.

Note: It is possible to use a dwarf multicannon inside the Stronghold Slayer Cave. This is recommended for fastest slayer experience.

It is possible to safespot in the Slayer Tower by standing on the brick pile just west of the Agility chain shortcut and pulling spectres just in front of the suit of armour from the room south of the shortcut. A crossbow's rapid attack style will be able to target aberrant spectres from this distance, making it a very useful spot. It is also possible to wait out the 10 minute hostility period with minimal risk by hiding beside the table in the north-west corner. You may be attacked occasionally, but it won't be often. This works well in connection with the safespotting/trapping process.

It is also possible to safespot one of the aberrant spectres by using the south-eastern corner of the wall of the staircase-room.

In the Stronghold Slayer Cave, they can be safespotted around a section of the cave wall in the middle of the eastern part of the dungeon.

All safespots only work with Ranged or Magic, since there must be at least six spaces between the player and the aberrant spectre to avoid their attack range. Halberds or thrown weapons such as throwing knives and throwing axes do not have a long enough attack range to safespot. In addition, these safespots are only usable when the spectres are non-aggressive, since nearby spectres will attack the player and prevent them from killing the trapped spectre.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Regardless if you have a shield, without a nosepeg, when you are attacked by an Aberrant Spectre, you will use the NPC style of blocking unarmed, which is using your forearms rather than covering your face. This is the only time you use this animation.
