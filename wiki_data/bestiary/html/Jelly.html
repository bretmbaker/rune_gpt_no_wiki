Jellies are Slayer monsters found in the Fremennik Slayer Dungeon and Wilderness Slayer Cave that require a Slayer level of 52 to kill. They give 75 Slayer experience per kill and drop hard clue scrolls and steel weaponry. Jellies and their variants are the only creatures to drop Mithril boots.

These monsters attack with a magic-based melee attack; therefore high magic defence armour, such as dragonhide is effective against their attacks. However, Protect from Melee will prevent all damage from them. Players may Pray Flick if the cost of prayer potions seems too high.

In the Wilderness cave, they can be safespotted by using either side of the rockfall in the northern part of the cave, which the Jellies will not wander away from. It is also possible to safespot them by standing on the corner of the rocks in the western section of the chamber or from behind the stones in the hallway south of the Jelly chamber.

Stronger variants of jelly called warped jellies may be found in the Catacombs of Kourend. The similarly strong chilled jelly can be found in the Ruins of Tapoyauik.

Strategy[edit | edit source]

  • Killing jellies is simple, and no particular strategy is required.
  • Metallic armours and other armours with low magic defence are not advised, as jellies use a magic-based melee attack. Dragonhide armour should be considered instead.
  • The best safespot to use is on either side of the rockfall in the northern part of the cave, although the jellies may need to be lured into position. There is also a safespot on the north wall near the hallway you enter the room from. The safespot may be difficult to use due to the jelly's unusual route mechanic.
  • It is a good idea to bring high-level alchemy spells for the drops.
