Zombies are found in the labyrinth before Tarn's personal chamber. They have a massive span of combat levels and highly varied drops. Some of the drops are valuable such as rune and adamant armours and weapons. These monsters can be dangerous as they are in a multicombat area. It is very easy to get lost in the tunnels, and without a teleport low level players may quickly die.
