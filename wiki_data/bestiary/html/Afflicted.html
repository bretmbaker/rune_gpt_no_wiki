The afflicted are villagers of Mort'ton who were cursed by a strange mist sent from the Sanguinesti region by Lord Lowerniel Vergidiyad Drakan. They speak nonsense to the player if talked to.

Afflicted can be cured temporarily by using either Serum 207 or Serum 208. Even though Serum 208 is supposed to be a "permanent" cure, ordinary villagers will always return to their afflicted state.
