Animated steel armour is a set of armour enchanted by Tarn Razorlor when players battle with him in his lair. He will enchant scrap armour in the area to come alive and attack players, but these are more of an inconvenience rather than a threat. They do not need to be killed to complete the fight.

Strangely, they drop an iron platebody on defeat instead of steel armour.
