Jungle spiders are monsters that can be found on Karamja and the archipelago east of Yanille. They are not displayed on the minimap so they can be a threat to low level players who don't notice them. Jungle spiders appear in the Tai Bwo Wannai Cleanup minigame.

Unlike other jungle monsters and their Ape Atoll counterpart, they are not poisonous.
