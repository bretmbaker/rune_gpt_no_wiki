Grimy Lizards are slayer monsters that require level 44 Slayer to kill, who are found in the Streambound Cavern within Neypotzli. They can be killed as part of a lizard Slayer task.

Unlike desert lizards, grimy lizards do not require ice coolers to finish off, nor are special footwear required like for sulphur lizards; however, they are poisonous, and partial completion of the Perilous Moons quest is required. While quest completion isn't necessary, the room remains instanced until progressing further in the quest. If the lizards do not re-spawn, continue progressing until they do.

Grimy lizards have -2 flat armour, so all successful hits will do an extra 2 damage to them. This incentivises using faster weapons, or weapons with multiple hitsplats, such as the sulphur blades or the dual macuahuitl.
