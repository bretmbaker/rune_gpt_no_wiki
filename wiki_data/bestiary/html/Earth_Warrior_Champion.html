The Earth Warrior Champion can only be fought after receiving the earth warrior champion scroll as part of the Champions' Challenge minigame.

The special rule in this fight is that the player cannot use Prayer.

The Earth Warrior Champion does not count as an earth warrior for the purpose of a Slayer task.

Rewards[edit | edit source]

  • 432 experience
  • 432 experience
  • A banner hung under the Champions' Guild to proclaim victory over the Earth Warrior Champion.
  • A Champion's lamp which awards 4,320 Slayer and Hitpoints experience when rubbed. This is obtained from the Chest of Champions by the north side of the basement.
