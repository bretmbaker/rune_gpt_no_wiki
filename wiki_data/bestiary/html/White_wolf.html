A white wolf is a wolf found atop the White Wolf Mountain. Its bones can be used with a chisel to create wolfbone arrowtips used to make ogre arrows for use in the Big Chompy Bird Hunting quest and Freeing Skrach Uglogwee during Recipe for Disaster. These wolves can also be killed for Slayer tasks.
