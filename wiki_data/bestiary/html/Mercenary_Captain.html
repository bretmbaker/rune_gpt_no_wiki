The Mercenary Captain is the leader of all of the Mercenaries in the Desert Mining Camp, and was hired to guard the entrance of it to prevent people from going in and discovering the slave-trade that was going on there. The Mercenary Captain plays a role in the quest The Tourist Trap, in which he is portrayed as a hot-headed and angry individual, and must be provoked to the point of outrage so that he initiates combat with the player himself, rather than having his mercenaries do his dirty work for him. Once killed the captain 'drops' the Metal key, which is automatically added to the player's inventory with the message "The mercenary captain drops a metal key on the floor. You quickly grab the key and add it to your inventory." appearing in the chatbox.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The Captain claims he is hunting someone called Al Zaba Bhasim and may task the player to kill him. Al Bhasim is an anagram of the name Al Shabim, the leader of the Bedabin Nomads. The task cannot be done as Al Shabim cannot be fought.
  • He appears to be wielding the Defensive shield from the Warriors' Guild in one hand.
  • Every time you kill him the message "You kill the captain!" appears in the chatbox
  • If a player is attacked by a Mercenary before getting dumped in the desert, they will attempt to run/walk back into the Mining Camp and attack the mercenary that previously attacked them.
  • Because the Metal Key is directly added to the player's inventory, killing the captain with only recoil still counts.
