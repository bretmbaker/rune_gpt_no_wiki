Dark wizards are mages who worship Zamorak and cast Strike spells.

They play a vital role in the Demon Slayer quest, performing a ritual to summon the demon Delrith.

Strategy[edit | edit source]

Dark wizards will attack with a variety of magic attacks, varying depending on the level of the Dark Wizard being fought.

The Level 7 variant will attack with Water Strike, and Confuse spells, working effectively against warriors in metal armour.

The Level 20 variant will attack with Earth Strike and Weaken spells, again working well against warriors in metal armour.

A good way to deal with them is to attack using Ranged, wearing leather or dragonhide armour, since Ranged armours generally excel in Magic defence.
