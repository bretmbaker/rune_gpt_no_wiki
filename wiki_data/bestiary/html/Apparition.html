Apparitions are the manifestations of some of the Menaphite Pantheon; specifically Het, Apmeken, Scabaras, and Crondis. They are fought during Icthlarin's Little Helper, depending on which canopic jar is taken during the quest.

Note: If not killed within 10 minutes, the apparition will despawn.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
27 April 2022
(update)
  • The manifestations of the Menaphite Pantheon that are encountered during the quest have been renamed "Apparition", rather than the god they take the form of.
  • Their examine texts have been changed as well; previously, they were:
    • Het: "Guardian of the liver. Guess he doesn't drink."
    • Apmeken: "Body of a woman, face of a monkey."
    • Scabaras: "A scarab headed demi-god."
    • Crondis: "Body of a woman, head of a crocodile."
