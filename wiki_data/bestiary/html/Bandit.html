Bandits are enemies that inhabit the Bandit Camp (Wilderness). The bandits are guarded by Guard Bandits. These bandits are aggressive, so players should take caution when approaching the camp. The camp's champions, however, such as Donny the lad, are not aggressive.

Strategy[edit | edit source]

Players who receive Bandits as a Slayer task may trap 5 of the level 130 Bandits in the Pizza shop house and the General Store house. One of the level 130 Bandits in the northern area of the Bandit Camp cannot be trapped, as his aggro range does not extend to the houses - he will de-aggro players who try to trap him in the huts. But players may freely kill the Level 22 and 34 Bandits without expensive protective gear or Prayer-boosting armour, if they trap the five level 130s that they can, and as long as they keep an eye out for the single level 130.

If going to the single bandit in the wilderness slayer cave, it will respawn almost instantly, as opposed to the 60s it may normally take.
