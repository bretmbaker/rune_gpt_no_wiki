Delrith is a demon of unknown origin resembling a Lesser demon in appearance. He is summoned by dark wizards from a stone table during the Demon Slayer quest. Delrith was once summoned in ancient times by a cult of monks. Immediately after being summoned he was confronted by Wally, an ancient knight of sorts, and quickly defeated with the powerful weapon known as Silverlight and an incantation. For unknown reasons the cult of Zamorakian Dark wizards at the south entrance of Varrock are performing the ritual to summon Delrith into this world again.

Delrith is relatively easy to kill and players can evade the wizards if they take their time. Low-levelled players should exercise caution however as the wizards will deal significant damage against them.

All damage dealt to Delrith is doubled when Silverlight is equipped. [1] Ring of recoil damage will therefore always inflict 2 on Delrith when he lands a successful hit on the player. When using a ring of recoil to lower Delrith's hitpoints, it's not necessary to deal any damage to him in order to finish the quest; you must simply have Silverlight equipped.

Additionally, by wielding Silverlight, mages can manually cast spells to quickly finish him off.
