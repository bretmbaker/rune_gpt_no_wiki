Otherworldly beings are unusual Zanarian monsters. They are commonly assigned as Slayer tasks by Vannaka. They are found south of the bank in Zanaris and can be safespotted by standing next to the flower pond and attacking the ones on the other side, however they move around quite a lot and players may find them difficult to safespot.

More specifically, five of the six otherworldly beings have a wander radius which permits safespotting without having them retreat as long as the player stands right next to the pond. One of these five will retreat if attacked horizontally across the pond, and another will retreat when attacked vertically. The other three will not retreat as long as you stay next to the pond.

Otherworldly beings may be a decent source of Magic experience, herbs, runes, and the materials for rings of forging for Ironmen.

As Otherworldly beings are considered spectral creatures, the Ectoplasmator will provide Prayer experience when killing them.
