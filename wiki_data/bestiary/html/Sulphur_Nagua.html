Sulphur nagua are slayer monsters that require level 48 Slayer to kill. They are found in the Ancient Prison within Neypotzli, accessed from the south-west entrance of the antechamber.

Their attacks are unique in that they hit twice per attack, rolling damage normally and splitting it in two hitsplats. After five attacks they will use a special attack in which they raise their weapon, and if the player remains within their melee range (including diagonal spaces) after three ticks, they will have 5 prayer points drained.

They are spectral creatures but not undead, meaning the Ectoplasmator will provide prayer experience for defeating them, but the Salve amulet and its variants will not affect them.

Sulphur nagua have -4 flat armour, so all successful hits will do an extra 4 damage to them. This incentivizes using faster weapons, or weapons with multiple hitsplats, such as the sulphur blades they drop or the dual macuahuitl.

A common strategy for replenishing prayer points while killing sulphur nagua is to create moonlight potions, which also boost melee combat stats. Alternatively, players with level 75 Hunter may choose to catch moonlight moths with a butterfly net (or barehanded with 85) to replenish prayer points, however due to their lack of additional boosting effects - along with catching one interfering with the player's attacks - relying solely on them is not recommended.

It is possible to safespot sulphur nagua by hiding behind the cooking stove, although the presence of free supplies within Neypotzli will likely render this unnecessary.
