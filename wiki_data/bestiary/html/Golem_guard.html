The golem guard is an NPC found guarding the entrance to the lower level of the Uzer Mastaba, whom players must defeat during The Curse of Arrav. It attacks with melee, cannot be safespotted, and has a prayer-piercing special attack. When the game screen shakes, it will stand in place and attack three times every two ticks, dealing up to 30 damage per hit; simply move one tile away from the golem guard to avoid this.

Players can either bring ranged equipment (as it has no ranged defence), or a crush-based weapon; a barronite mace is useful for lower levelled players as it is vulnerable to golembane weapons.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The examine text is likely a reference to the 1975 film The Rocky Horror Picture Show.
