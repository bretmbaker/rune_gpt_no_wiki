Chaos druid warriors are a more powerful version of chaos druids which can be found in the Yanille Agility Dungeon (40 Agility required) or on the roof of the Slepe church. They drop more ingredients than their lower-level counterparts, and their frequent herb drops make them useful for players seeking to train Herblore.

Both locations feature an altar, but players training should be aware that praying at the chaos altar in the Yanille dungeon drops the player into a pit of level 64 poison spiders, dealing up to 15 damage.
