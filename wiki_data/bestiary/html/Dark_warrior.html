Dark warriors are mainly found in the Dark Warriors' Fortress, a castle in level 12 to 16 Wilderness north-west of Edgeville.

There are a mix of combat level 8 and 145 on the ground floor[UK]1st floor[US] and only combat level 8 dark warriors on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US]. They are usually more annoying than an actual threat to players, and have higher defence than their combat level suggests.
