Spiders are found all over Gielinor, but most notably in the Stronghold of Security, where a stronger variant can be found in large numbers. Spiders are usually considered a nuisance and rarely drop items, and are generally only killed to quickly complete a spider slayer task.

Similar to rats, spiders do not appear on the minimap.
