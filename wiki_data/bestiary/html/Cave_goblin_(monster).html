Cave goblins are goblins of the Dorgeshuun who can be found in the Lumbridge Swamp Caves. They are friendly creatures, and are willing to give many adventurers a lit torch (if the player has no light source) or a tinderbox (if the player does not have one) when spoken to after the player assures that they are not there to hurt the cave goblins. Upon being attacked, they will scream "Nooo!", "Don't hurt us!" or "Eeek!" and nearby Cave goblins will try and flee.
