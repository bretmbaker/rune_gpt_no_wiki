Porazdir is a Tsutsaroth demon[1] who was once one of Zamorak's most trusted generals during the God Wars. Said to kill any in his path without mercy, even if they are demons, Porazdir is described as "completely mad", and regularly clashes with Justiciar Zachariah.[2]

During the Mage Arena II miniquest, he has to be defeated in order to obtain a demon's heart for Kolodion's spell. In order to summon him, players must use an enchanted symbol to locate him, similar to using an enchanted key or strange device.

When fighting Porazdir, he can use a special attack that will fire a ball of energy at the player that can hit through protection prayers for upwards of 30 damage. When he uses this attack a message in the chatbox will state Porazdir fires a ball of energy directly linked to his power! This damage cannot be negated through prayer, however if the player is at least 12 tiles away when the ball lands, no damage will be dealt.

Porazdir can only be harmed by the Flames of Zamorak spell. As such, a Zamorak staff, staff of the dead, its toxic counterpart, or an accursed sceptre is required to defeat him. Porazdir is considered a black demon for the purpose of a Slayer assignment.
