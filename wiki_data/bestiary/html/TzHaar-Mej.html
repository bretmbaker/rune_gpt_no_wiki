The TzHaar-Mej are both the leaders of the TzHaar people and powerful mages. They fight with both melee and magic using their staves. When attacked within 6 squares of other higher level TzHaar (Ket and Xil), the mage will cry out "Kot kl, zek e JalYt!" which roughly translates to "Save me, attack the human!" This will cause the nearby TzHaar to aid the TzHaar-Mej in combat.

The drop rates for gems increase by 33% when wearing a charged Ring of wealth.
