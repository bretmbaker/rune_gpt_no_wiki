Hobgoblins are an inaccessible variant of the standard hobgoblins seen in the Witchaven Dungeon during The Slug Menace. The hobgoblins can be seen after entering the wall opening and arriving in the second section of the dungeon. A copy of the entrance can be seen over the wall and the hobgoblins can be observed wandering the area, but cannot be accessed.

It is not known if the variant hobgoblins have any difference from the standard hobgoblins found near the real entrance of the dungeon.
