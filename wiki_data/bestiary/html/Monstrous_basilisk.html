The monstrous basilisk is a superior variant of the basilisk. It has a chance of spawning after purchasing the unlock Bigger and Badder for 150 Slayer reward points from any Slayer master. Like their standard variant, a mirror shield or V's shield is required to safely engage them, as otherwise the player will take rapid damage and have their stats drained.

Drops[edit | edit source]

In addition to 100% drops and tertiary drops, superior slayer monsters will always roll 3 additional items, of which only 1 roll may be from the superior unique table. The other rolls are performed on the regular counterpart's table; their displayed drop chances represent a single roll.

Drop rates estimated based on 48,470 samples from the Drop Rate Project, unless otherwise cited.

The average Monstrous basilisk kill is worth 116,365.58.

The average Monstrous basilisk kill while on a Konar task is worth 222,303.77.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The monster's examine text is a reference to the Harry Potter series, in which phoenix tears are one of the only cures for a basilisk's powerful venom. However, basilisks in RuneScape aren't poisonous.
