The H.A.M. Mage is only fought during the Another Slice of H.A.M..

After he and a H.A.M. Archer attack the goblins of Goblin Village, the player is sent to kill them. They can only be fought with magic or ranging.

Habitat(s)[edit | edit source]

Goblin Village, only during Another Slice of H.A.M.
