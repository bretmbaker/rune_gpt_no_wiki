Penance Fighters are only encountered during the Barbarian Assault minigame. Like Penance Rangers, they can only be killed by the Attacker. There are four ways to damage this monster:

  1. Combat-style/arrow-type/magic-spell announced by Collector
    • Controlled/Bullet/Wind
    • Accurate/Field/Water
    • Aggressive/Blunt/Earth
    • Defensive/Barbed/Fire
  2. Poisons (from both weapons and green eggs)
  3. Egg fired from the Egg Launcher
  4. Recoil damage from any player

No experience is gained from damaging this monster.
