Scorpia's offspring are tiny scorpions that inhabit Scorpia's cave. Their Ranged attacks are incredibly accurate and capable of inflicting poison, starting at 6 damage.

Interestingly, their aggression seems to only apply to unpoisoned players and those currently under attack by Scorpia.
