Chaos dwarves are a type of corrupted and dangerous dwarves following Zamorak found in various locations, most notably the Wilderness. It is revealed during the Forgettable Tale of a Drunken Dwarf quest that they are the result of experiments by The Red Axe in their attempt to conquer Keldagrim.

On occasion, they may drop a muddy key, which can be used to unlock the muddy chest deep in the Wilderness, which contains an assortment of mid-level items. Both the dwarves in the Wilderness and those in Taverley Dungeon drop the muddy key.
