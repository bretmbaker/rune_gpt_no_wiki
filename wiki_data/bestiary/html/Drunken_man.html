The drunken man can be found in the upstairs of the Rising Sun Inn in Falador square. He has a distinct walking animation, resembling the "drunk" stance held by players when equipping Ale of the gods or Crystal chalice. When spoken to, he asks why there are two of the player before describing "giant hairy cabbages" and moving away across the room.

His drops and pickpocket loot are the same as a normal man.
