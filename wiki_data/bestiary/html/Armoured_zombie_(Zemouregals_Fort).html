Armoured zombies can be found in the tunnels west of the basement in Zemouregal's Fort, who appear after the completion of The Curse of Arrav. They have slightly higher stats than the regular armoured zombies found in Zemouregal's Base, with their drop table being slightly more generous; notably, they exclusively drop the broken zombie helmet while also dropping the broken zombie axe at a higher rate than the zombies in Zemouregal's Base.

As undead, they are weak to the effects of both the salve amulet and the Crumble Undead spell. They similarly also reside in a multicombat area and spawn in close proximity to each other, and can thus be killed particularly effectively with multi-hitting weapons such as burst spells and the venator bow. However, players cannot set up a dwarf multicannon.
