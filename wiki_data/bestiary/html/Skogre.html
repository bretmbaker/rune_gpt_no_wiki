Skogres are skeletal ogres encountered during the Zogre Flesh Eaters quest, who live south of Castle Wars. An aggressive skogre may also appear when you bury zogre bones inside Jiggig.

Strengths and weaknesses[edit | edit source]

Skogres are almost immune to normal weapons (you will hit just as often, but damage is reduced by 75%).[1] Brutal arrows are effective against them, as is the Crumble Undead spell (however, damage is halved, for a maximum of 7).

Skogres are also capable of infecting the player with disease. Disease can be cured with Sanfew serum or Relicym's balm, the latter of which may be bought from Uglug Nar outside Jiggig for 200. An inoculation bracelet will prevent damage taken from disease, but will not grant immunity to becoming diseased.

When you attempt to bury their bones after killing them, a new one may spawn and attack you.
