Hazeel Cultists are followers of the Zamorakian Mahjarrat, Hazeel, located in the southern Ardougne Sewers. Accessing their location requires starting the Hazeel Cult quest and correctly turning the five sewer valves outside. The cultists are eager to resurrect their lord, which the player helps with or refuses.
