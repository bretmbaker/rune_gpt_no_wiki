Grizzly bears are a monster found in the Escape Caves, and are the strongest among grizzly bear variants. Along with poison spiders, they are intended to deter player killers from world switching whilst inside the Escape Caves, as they will never stop attacking and will pursue players until they leave the caves. These bears attack using typeless melee/Slash which cannot be prayed against.
