Tree spirits are monsters which can be found in the Enchanted Valley using the fairy ring code BKQ when attempting to cut down a tree (an axe is required). Because of the common nature rune drops supplemented by drops of rune and adamant axes and various seeds, tree spirits can be good money, although not much experience will be gained. The tree spirit's combat level varies depending on that of the player's, a minimum of 14, and up to a maximum of level 159.

Player combat level Tree spirit level
3–10 14
11–20 29
21–40 49
41–60 79
61–90 120
91+ 159

Unlike the tree spirit fought in the Lost City quest, these tree spirits are not undead; therefore they are not affected by Crumble Undead or the effects of the salve amulet and its variants. Magic is effective against Tree spirits due to their low Magic level that does not increase as their combat level and other stats do. They can also be easily safespotted by using plants close to the trees cut to spawn them.

Tree spirits are commonly killed by low-leveled ironmen for their rune axe drop; this drops on all levels, and at a much higher rate compared to other sources, as well as being an efficient method of obtaining Nature Runes for low-to-mid level players as the spirits drop 5.578 nature runes on average per kill.

History[edit | edit source]

Before the random event update in 2014, the tree spirit used to appear randomly when attempting to cut a tree. On appearance, it would say Leave these woods and never return!. As with similar guardian random events, the tree spirit's combat level and stats scaled with the player's combat level, as well as only being attackable by the targeted player.

As with all other aggressive random events, it was removed in an update.
