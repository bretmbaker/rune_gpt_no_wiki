The insatiable bloodveld is a superior variant of the bloodveld. It has a chance of spawning after purchasing the unlock Bigger and Badder for 150 Slayer reward points from any Slayer master. Bloodveld slain in the God Wars Dungeon and Wilderness God Wars Dungeon do not have a chance of spawning their superior variant.[1][2]

Drops[edit | edit source]

In addition to 100% drops and tertiary drops, superior slayer monsters will always roll 3 additional items, of which only 1 roll may be from the superior unique table. The other rolls are performed on the regular counterpart's table; their displayed drop chances represent a single roll.

Drop rates estimated based on 803,317 samples from the Drop Rate Project, unless otherwise cited.

The average Insatiable Bloodveld kill is worth 134,899.92.

The average Insatiable Bloodveld kill while on a Konar task is worth 240,838.11.

Insatiable Bloodvelds now drop vile ashes in place of regular bones.

(update)

Several monsters, including Insatiable Bloodveld, were tagged as demonic, allowing demonbane weapons to work on them.

23 February 2017
(update | poll)

The insatiable bloodveld was given a graphical update to be more distinguishable from their regular variant.
