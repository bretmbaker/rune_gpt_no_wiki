Infernal Mages are Slayer monsters that require 45 Slayer to kill. They are sometimes hunted for their frequent drops of death runes and their rare dark mystic boots and hat drops. Players who plan on fighting them should be wary of their accurate magic attack, although Protect from Magic negates this completely.

To reach the Infernal Mage area in the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] of the Slayer Tower, players can take the long way up the south-east stairs. Players with 60 Slayer and 61 Agility can use the shortcut, but should wear a Nose peg or Slayer helmet to avoid the draining effect of the aggressive Aberrant spectres. Players below 60 Slayer could run past, but should bring a restore potion.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Infernal Mages appear to be wielding a version of the Slayer's staff, along with a set of green, decaying mystic robes.
  • They appear to be casting Fire Blast, however their max hit suggests they are actually casting Fire Strike.
