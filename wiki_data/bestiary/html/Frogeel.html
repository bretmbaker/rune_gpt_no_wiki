A Frogeel is a hybrid of a Giant frog and a Raw cave eel. Players can create frogeels in Creature Creation, in the basement dungeon of the Tower of Life building once they complete the Tower of Life quest.

A frogeel is created by a player using one pair of Giant frog legs and a Raw cave eel on the altar located on the western wall in the Tower of Life's dungeon. The Homunculus in the dungeon then creates the monster, which appears near the altar and will attack the player.
