Sulphur lizards are creatures found in the middle level of the Karuulm Slayer Dungeon in Mount Karuulm, requiring level 44 Slayer to kill. As they are only found within the volcano, players must wear the boots of stone, boots of brimstone, or granite boots to protect themselves from the extreme heat of the dungeon floor, unless they've completed the elite Kourend & Kebos Diary.

Players can kill these as an alternative to desert lizards, provided they have the higher Slayer level. Additionally, sulphur lizards do not require ice coolers to finish off.

They can be safespotted by standing by the corner of the entrance to their room. A cannon placed nearby can easily draw the lizards in.
