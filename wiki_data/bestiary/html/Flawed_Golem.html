The Flawed Golem is one of the golems found in the Ruins of Camdozaal. It can be found by awakening the Rubble lying in the section south of the bank chest.

When you walk far away from an awakened golem without killing it, it will disintegrate as if it died, without dropping any loot.

Flawed golems are notable for dropping the barronite guard, needed for construction of the barronite mace.
