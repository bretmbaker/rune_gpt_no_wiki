Several H.A.M. guards are found in the lower level storeroom of the H.A.M. Hideout through a hidden trapdoor during Death to the Dorgeshuun. The guards can be pickpocketed at level 20 Thieving to obtain various rewards, including metallic keys that open chests in the storeroom.

Zanik will knock out the guards using a crossbow as the room is traversed. After listening in on the secret meeting a guard will appear, and the player is knocked out and transported to the H.A.M. jail cell.

Three H.A.M. guards appear during the final boss fight of Death to the Dorgeshuun. They appear in the water mill cellar. They need to be killed before you can attack Sigmund.

Players intending to gather easy clue scrolls should prefer to pickpocket H.A.M. Members in the main floor of the hideout, which have a higher rate of giving clues.
