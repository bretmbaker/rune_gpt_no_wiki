Scorpia is a large female scorpion that resides in a cave beneath the Scorpion Pit. She is the only monster to drop the odium shard 3 and malediction shard 3, which are used to forge the odium ward and malediction ward, respectively.

Fight overview[edit | edit source]

Main article: Scorpia/Strategies

The Scorpion Pit is filled with many Scorpia's offspring, which use weak but accurate ranged attacks. Scorpia herself attacks with melee which, whether successful or not, drain the player's prayer points by 2 if they have the Protect from Melee prayer active. Both Scorpia and her offspring are capable of inflicting poison, starting at 20 and 6 damage, respectively.

Once Scorpia falls below 50% health, the next attack against her, whether successful or not, will cause her to summon two Scorpia's guardians, which heal her rapidly.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
5 June 2024
(update)

The collection Log entry for Scorpia now correctly lists the dragon 2h sword.

10 April 2024
(update | poll)

Scorpia's respawn time was decreased from 17 ticks (10.2 seconds) to 14 ticks (8.4 seconds).

7 February 2024
(update)

Scorpia has had consumables and other low priced items removed from her drop table, in favour of more profitable items such as the dragon 2h sword, wilderness crab teleports, and onyx bolt tips.

19 March 2015
(update | poll)

Scorpia, Venenatis, Vet'ion and Callisto now have a very slim chance of dropping a pet version of themselves when killed.
