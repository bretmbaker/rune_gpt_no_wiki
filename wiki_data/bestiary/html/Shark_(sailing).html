Sharks are predatory fish found in the Sailing alpha around Karamja, such as near the Mudskipper Sound, and south of the Ship Yard.

They are intended to be killed via naval combat as part of Bounty tasks (unavailable in the alpha) through which they will drop a specific item, similar to Hunter rumours.
