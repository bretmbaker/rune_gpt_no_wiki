Chilled jellies are stronger variants of jellies that require level 52 Slayer to kill. They are found exclusively within the Ruins of Tapoyauik.

They are very similar to warped jellies, except for having a 50% fire weakness and dropping frozen tears and tooth halves of moon keys instead of ancient shards and dark totem pieces. Like warped jellies, frozen jellies have a chance to spawn a vitreous warped jelly on death if the Bigger and Badder Slayer unlock is enabled.

Jellies attack with magical melee, so armour with high magic defence is recommended, such as Karil's armour or black dragonhide armour. Like their lower levelled counterparts, using Protect from Melee will prevent all damage from them.
