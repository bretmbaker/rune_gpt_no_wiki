Rogues are human monsters that can also be pickpocketed, with level 32 Thieving. Pickpocketing a rogue grants 36.5 Thieving experience. It is the only pickpocketing target found exclusively in the Wilderness. The level 135 variant cannot be pickpocketed.

Unlike many other NPCs found in the Wilderness, rogues are not hostile and will only attack players if provoked or if they try to loot the chests within their castle.

Pickpocketing Info[edit | edit source]

Rogue
Level required 32
Thieving XP36.5 xp
TypePickpocket
Stun Timer5s
Damage2

BEWARE: Lower-levelled accounts and/or Hardcore Ironmen, whilst thieving at Rogue's is generally safe due to them being unaggressive, the risk of both Player Killers and the Chaos Elemental are very big possibilities. Take care while inside the Rogue's Castle courtyard and within the Castle itself as the Chaos Elemental may attack you through the walls.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
10 April 2024
(update)

Improved the quantity of rogues at the Rogue's Castle for Wilderness Slayer Tasks, and added 1,000-3,000 GP per kill for high-level rogues.

21 August 2014
(update)

Reduced the wander range of high level Rogues to prevent trapping.

20 March 2014
(update)

The level 129 rogues outside the Rogues' Castle can no longer be lured into the building or out of the keep.

13 March 2014
(update | poll)

High level rogues have been added to the Rogues' Castle.
