The Dire Wolf Alpha is a monster fought during the Meat and Greet quest, after Alba sends the player to deal with the wolves that have been attacking the buffalo of Ortus Farm.

The wolf can be safespotted by standing north to the entrance in the corner.

The wolf will summon Dire wolves which can attack with ranged. These wolves have 10 hitpoints and can deal up to 3 damage. Defeating the Dire Wolf Alpha will cause any remaining dire wolves to flee.
