Desert snakes are monsters found in the Kharidian Desert. Unlike bush snakes, desert snakes are not poisonous. Due to most of these snakes being in the desert, protection from desert heat is highly recommended for extended periods of combat. They only drop bones, and a snake spine during the Rag and Bone Man II quest.
