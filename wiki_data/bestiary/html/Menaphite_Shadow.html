The Menaphite Shadow is summoned by the Menaphite Akh during the Beneath Cursed Sands quest. It will use ranged or magic attacks.

The Menaphite Shadow uses ranged attacks until Menaphite Akh reaches 240 Hitpoints, 50% HP. The Menaphite Shadow then swaps to magic attacks for the remainder of the fight.

The Menaphite Shadow is immune to Vengeance and the Ring of recoil/Ring of suffering.
