Zogres are encountered during the Zogre Flesh Eaters quest and live south of Castle Wars. When burying their bones a skogre may appear and attack. Their name is a portmanteau of 'zombie ogre'. According to Juna, zombie ogres fought for Zamorak during the God Wars and 'could repel the forces of Saradomin with their stench alone.'

Strengths and weaknesses[edit | edit source]

Zogres are almost immune to normal weapons (you will hit just as often, but damage is reduced by 75%[1], a halberd of any kind is recommended to strike from distance), brutal arrows are effective against them, as is the Crumble Undead spell (damage is halved, for a maximum of 7). Also, poison works full power, so p++ weapons might be good against them.

Zogres are also capable of infecting the player with disease. Disease can be cured with Relicym's balm or Sanfew serum, which may be bought from Uglug Nar outside Jiggig for 200 coins (Relicym's balm only), or made using Herblore. Note that you can only trade with Uglug Nar once you have completed at least some of the Zogre Flesh Eaters quest. An inoculation bracelet will prevent damage taken from disease, but will not grant immunity to becoming diseased.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The zogres playing the drums can be attacked but only with Ranged or Magic. Players will also need to telegrab their items, should they wish to pick them up.
  • The colour of the drums that the zogres play is slightly lighter than that of the other drums.
