Baby tanglefootii[a] are located in the shady grove, which is located close to the Cosmic Altar in Zanaris. They are a weaker version of the Tanglefoot monster, which is fought during the Fairytale I - Growing Pains quest.

Baby tanglefootii have the trait of being invulnerable to all damage except magic secateurs, which is shared with the stronger Tanglefoot. A player's max hit against a baby tanglefoot will also partially depend on their Farming level.
