Wallasalkis can be found throughout the Waterbirth Island Dungeon. They attack with powerful Magic and their projectile resembles Water Wave.

Unlike most other monsters in the dungeon, wallasalkis are much stronger than their combat level suggests; their attack, while somewhat inaccurate, can deal up to 30 damage, and they are highly resistant to both melee and magic attacks. Players killing them should use ranged.

When their health falls low, wallasalkis may attempt to retreat, much like Giant Rock Crabs.

Wallasalkis are primarily known for dropping components to create skeletal armour, along with the skeletal boots and skeletal gloves.
