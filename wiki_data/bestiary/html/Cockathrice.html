The cockathrice is a superior variant of the cockatrice. It has a chance of spawning after purchasing the unlock Bigger and Badder for 150 Slayer reward points from any Slayer master. Like their standard variant, a mirror shield or V's shield is required to safely engage them, as otherwise the player will take rapid damage and have their stats drained.

Drops[edit | edit source]

In addition to 100% drops and tertiary drops, superior slayer monsters will always roll 3 additional items, of which only 1 roll may be from the superior unique table. The other rolls are performed on the regular counterpart's table; their displayed drop chances represent a single roll.

Drop rates estimated based on 63,641 samples from the Drop Rate Project, unless otherwise cited.

The average Cockathrice kill is worth 98,671.18.
