Kolodion is the shape-shifting master of the Mage Arena. He is fought to gain entrance to the arena, where players fight for their god of choice.

When a player fights him during the Mage Arena I miniquest, he first starts out as a man, then transforms into an Ogre, a giant ice blue spider, a large ghost, and finally a black demon.

Strategy[edit | edit source]

Kolodion attacks players with the three god spells, which all have a maximum hit of 20, regardless of his form. For this reason, it is recommended that players achieve level 37 Prayer to use Protect from Magic.

Players should bring the required runes for their most powerful combat spell, since melee and ranged attacks cannot be used against him. In addition, it is recommended to either bring good food to heal with, or a few prayer potions.

Since the fight takes place in the Wilderness, it's not recommended to bring expensive gear; monk, wizard or Xerician robes are a good budget option.

God Spells[edit | edit source]

After Kolodion is defeated, players may enter the sparkling pool, leading them to a chamber where they can then worship one of the three god statues. Players will then receive a god cape based on their choice of worshiping the statue of Guthix, Saradomin and Zamorak; they can then claim that god's staff by speaking to the chamber guardian for free. After obtaining their free staff, players can buy additional staves of any god for 80,000 each.
