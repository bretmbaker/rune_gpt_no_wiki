Disciples of Iban are cultists who worship Iban, and are located in the Underground Pass. They drop Zamorak monk robes, which must be worn to enter Iban's temple during the Underground Pass quest. After Iban is killed, they disappear permanently.

Formerly, Disciples of Iban would drop a broken Iban's staff if the player lost theirs. With the release of Song of the Elves, this is no longer the case. To retrieve a new staff, you must now talk to Koftik at the entrance of the Underground Pass.

Trivia[edit | edit source]
