Muggers are weak human non-player characters that have a combat level of 6. Because of their low combat level, they are generally more of a nuisance, but they can still be a threat for brand-new players.

Muggers are aggressive, which means that they attack players who are two times the mugger's combat level or less. Thus, muggers are aggressive against players who have a combat level between 3 and 12.
