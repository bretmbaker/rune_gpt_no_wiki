Flockleader Geerin is one of the Aviansie bodyguards of Kree'arra, along with Flight Kilisa and Wingman Skree. She uses Ranged attacks with a max hit of 25 hitpoints. When fighting Kree'arra, it is very unlikely Flockleader Geerin will do any damage at all, as most players use the Protect from Missiles prayer to defend from Kree'arra.

The ability to be assigned aviansie by Konar quo Maten, Chaeldar, Nieve, and Duradel can be purchased for 80 slayer reward points, which allows the use for the bonuses of the slayer helmet (i).
