Women are generic NPC civilians of RuneScape, particularly common in human-dwelling towns such as Lumbridge, Falador, among other settlements. They are also less commonly found outside of towns, and in remote places. Oddly enough, guards do not react to players killing these civilians.

Women can be pickpocketed by members at level 1 Thieving, yielding 8 experience points.
