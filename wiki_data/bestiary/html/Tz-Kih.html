The Tz-Kih is a monster found in the TzHaar Fight Pits and Fight Caves.

Fight Caves[edit | edit source]

In the Fight Caves, it is the first enemy you will have to kill. Each hit it inflicts on a player will drain the player's Prayer points, whether it inflicts damage or not. Though these monsters are not dangerous on their own, the prayer-draining effect of their hits make it increasingly difficult to conserve prayer points throughout later waves.

If you wish to proceed to TzTok-Jad, killing Tz-Kihs should be a first priority each wave in order to preserve enough prayer to make it to the end of the Fight Caves. It is preferred that you use range or magic against them so that they do not drain your prayer. A good strategy is trapping them behind the larger monsters.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Their name may be a reference to Keese, bat-like enemies from the Legend of Zelda series.
