The Guardians of Armadyl are found in the Temple of Ikov. They are members of an ancient order, tasked with protecting the legendary Staff of Armadyl. If players wish to claim the Staff for Lucien, they must defeat all of the guardians in the room. Players can also talk to or kill a guardian to obtain an Armadyl pendant if they lose theirs.

The Guardians of Armadyl are among the very few known human followers of Armadyl.
