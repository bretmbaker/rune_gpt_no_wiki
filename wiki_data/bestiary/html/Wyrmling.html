Wyrmlings are slayer monsters that require level 62 Slayer to kill, who are found in the Earthbound Cavern within Neypotzli, though partial completion (after talisman enchantments) of Perilous Moons is required for them to spawn. They can be killed as an alternative to wyrms for a Slayer task. However, they cannot spawn the superior Shadow Wyrm.
