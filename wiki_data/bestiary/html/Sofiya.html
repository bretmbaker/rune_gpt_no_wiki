Sofiya is one of the citizens of Canifis. If you are not wielding a wolfbane dagger when you attack, she will turn into a werewolf. She is more friendly if you are wearing a Ring of charos.

She does not play a role in any quest or minigame. She cannot be killed as part of a werewolf Slayer task in her human form.
