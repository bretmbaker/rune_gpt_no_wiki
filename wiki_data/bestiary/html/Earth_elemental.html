Earth elementals are found beneath Seers' Village during the Elemental Workshop I and Elemental Workshop II quests. There are several roaming around in the quest area, but the player can also cause one to appear by mining a rock in the earth room.

There are two types of Earth elementals: the ones roaming around and the ones that can be mined. Only the latter will drop elemental ores.

Killing a roaming variant is a requirement for the Easy Kandarin Diary.
