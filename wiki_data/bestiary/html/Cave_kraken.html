Cave kraken are slayer monsters that are found in the Kraken Cove. They can only be attacked if players have them as a Slayer task. To be assigned cave krakens, players require level 87 Slayer and 50 Magic. Along with their boss variant, the Kraken, they are the only monsters to drop the Kraken tentacle and the Trident of the seas.

In order to fight a cave kraken, a player must first "disturb" one of the many whirlpools found within the cove. Fishing explosives cannot be used to surface the regular cave kraken like one might their boss counterpart.

Magic is the only reliable way of dealing damage to the cave kraken, as Ranged attacks are heavily reduced and Melee cannot be used. Their attacks are magic rather than typeless (meaning they can be prayed against).

Compared to the Kraken they have poor drops, so are generally only killed by ironmen seeking a Trident of the seas, which they drop at a more frequent rate than the boss, albeit uncharged.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Cave kraken use the same model as the Sea Troll Queen but it is recoloured and resized.
  • Cave kraken and their boss variant are similar to RuneScape's Strykewyrms, which can only be attacked while on a Slayer task and must be disturbed to fight.
  • The cave kraken had a festive override wearing a santa hat during the 2016, 2017, 2018, and 2019 Christmas events. Both the NPC and skill guide icon had the override.
  • If a player attempts to use Fishing explosives to disturb a cave kraken's whirlpool, they will path accordingly but ultimately be unsuccessful in doing so. They will simply be met with the game message, "I can't reach that!"
