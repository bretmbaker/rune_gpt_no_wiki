These basilisks are only encountered during the The Fremennik Exiles during their attempt to take over Rellekka. Defeating one adds 3.33% towards the completion of defending the region. Like other basilisks, they have a gaze ability that drains all combat stats, so defeating them requires a mirror shield or V's shield, which is collected shortly before the invasion.
