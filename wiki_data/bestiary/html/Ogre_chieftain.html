Ogre chieftains are ogres that can be found in the Feldip Hills and inside the Ogre Enclave. Players can kill ogre chieftains towards an ogre Slayer task.

Due to their lacklustre drops and being more difficult to kill compared to standard ogres, players usually avoid killing them.
