The Imp Champion can only be fought after receiving the imp champion scroll as part of the Champion's Challenge minigame. Like regular imps, it can teleport small distances, although it will not teleport outside of the arena.

Players cannot use special attacks during this fight.

Rewards[edit | edit source]

  • 160 experience
  • 160 experience
  • A banner hung under the Champions' Guild to proclaim victory over the Imp Champion.
  • A Champion's lamp which awards 1,600 Slayer and Hitpoints experience when rubbed. This is obtained from the Chest of Champions by the north side of the basement.
