Icefiends are found on top of the Ice Mountain, in various locations around Varlamore, the southern part of the God Wars Dungeon, and summoned during fights with the Ice demon in the Chambers of Xeric. Killing an icefiend is one of the three options to cool down the Dwarven rock cake in Recipe for Disaster.

(update)

Several monsters, including Icefiend, were tagged as demonic, allowing demonbane weapons to work on them.

8 August 2019
(update)

The drop rate for ecumenical keys have been changed to be 1/60 regardless of keys owned.
