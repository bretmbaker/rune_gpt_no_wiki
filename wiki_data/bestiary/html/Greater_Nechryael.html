Greater nechryael are stronger variants of nechryael that require level 80 Slayer to kill. They are found within the Catacombs of Kourend, Iorwerth Dungeon and the Wilderness Slayer Cave.

Like normal nechryael, greater nechryael can summon up to two Death spawns when fighting a player. These death spawns are the same as the ones summoned by normal nechryael. Using Protect from Melee is recommended, as the greater nechryael is more accurate and deals even more damage. Arclight can be very effective against greater nechryael, as these are considered to be demonic creatures.

Unlike the twisted banshee, mutated bloodveld, deviant spectre and warped jellies, who have unique superior creatures compared to their standard counterparts, greater nechryael do not. They simply get the normal nechryarch instead, although its drop table corresponds to the unique superior creatures.

Strategy[edit | edit source]

Greater nechryael in the Catacombs of Kourend are popularly killed using burst or barrage spells. To do this, turn on Protect from Melee drink a Goading potion dose to automatically draw their aggression. Run through the nechryael to stack them and cast the best burst or barrage spell available. Those without Goading potions can alternatively draw aggressiveness with a fast ranged weapon (such as cheap darts or knives) or the Dinh's bulwark special attack. This is slower than using Goading potions, however.

Prioritise equipment that gives high Magic damage bonuses, such as virtus robes. If Magic damage-boosting equipment is not available, use Prayer-boosting equipment that does not penalise Magic accuracy, such as vestment robes. Because of the low Magic defence of Greater Nechryael, having a high Magic accuracy is not important as this does not significantly increase damage output. Negative Magic accuracy will increase splashing however, so using equipment that penalises Magic accuracy (such as Proselyte armour) is not advised.

Pay attention when a Nechryarch spawns, as its chaotic death spawns deal high damage. To deal with them, autocast Blood Barrage to outheal the damage taken (provided that there are more regular nechryael next to it), use ice spells to safespot the Nechryarch, or disengage them by running out of their range of aggression and then returning to the Nechryarch.

Greater nechryael can also be found in a multi-combat setting in the Wilderness Slayer Cave where they may also be cannoned. However, this is part of the Wilderness and there is the risk of running into player killers. In this location, take less risk and bank more frequently. Fortunately, they are found below level 30 Wilderness so a royal seed pod can be used for a fast escape option. These nechryael can only be killed when on a Slayer task.

Finally, greater nechryael also reside in the Iorwerth Dungeon. Unlike the other two locations, this location is solely single-combat, although a cannon can be permitted in this area. This location requires completion of Song of the Elves and is ideal for players who are not intending to use multi-target combat methods to kill nechryael.
