Scarab Mages are encountered during the Contact! and Beneath Cursed Sands quests. They attack using Magic. Their magic attack looks like a swarm of beetles and hits up to 17 hitpoints.

The mages have an unusual attack speed of 15, attacking every 9 seconds, making prayer flicking very easy against them. This somewhat compensates for their high accuracy and max hit.

The Keris dagger special effect works against them.

Though not directly assigned, Scarab mages may be slain as part of a Scabarite assignment. These monsters respawn at a slower rate compared to the Locust Riders.

An easy way to reach the scabarites is to enter the Dungeon and immediately head west and down the ladder, though make sure to bring a light source with you.

Warning: Scarab mages are able to extinguish a player's light source, including bullseye lanterns. Bring a tinderbox! A Firemaking cape, bruma torch or Kandarin headgear with any level of the Kandarin Diary completed, can be used to eliminate the need for a tinderbox.
