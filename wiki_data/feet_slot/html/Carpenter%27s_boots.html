The Carpenter's boots are acquired from Amy, leader of Mahogany Homes, in exchange for 200 Carpenter points. The boots grants 0.2% bonus Construction experience. This is increased to 2.5% when wearing the full Carpenter's outfit. It can be stored in a magic wardrobe in a player-owned house.
