Holy sandals are a pair of footwear obtainable by completing medium Treasure Trails. They require 31 Prayer to wear. They have the third-best prayer bonus for the footwear slot, and can be upgraded into devout boots by using a drake's tooth on them. They also count as a Saradomin item for the God Wars Dungeon and the Bandit Camp.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room.
