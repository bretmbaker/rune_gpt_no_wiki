Mystic boots (light) are a piece of magic armour worn in the feet slot. Like all parts of the mystic robes set, they require 40 Magic and 20 Defence to wear, and can be stored in a magic wardrobe in a player-owned house.

Mystic boots are primarily used in offensive setups due to their magic attack bonus. Despite not being a magic item, silly jester boots give a greater magic defence bonus.
