Clue hunter boots are obtained from completing the first clue during the Crack the Clue! event. It is part of the clue hunter outfit. Obtainable only once if you already have it in your inventory or bank. If you happen to destroy it, you will need to discover it again.

These items are obtained alongside the clue hunter gloves.

In order to obtain/reobtain the boots and gloves, the player must dig at an oak tree south-west of the Fishing Guild and just north of East Ardougne.

This item can be stored in the armour case of a costume room, as part of the Clue hunter outfit set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
