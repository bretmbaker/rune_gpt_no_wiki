Boots of lightness reduce weight by 4.535 kg when worn, and offer slight defensive bonuses. As they are easy to obtain in bulk, they may be safely used in the wilderness. While they grant a higher weight-reduction than the graceful boots, they do not give an increase to run energy restoration rate.

Obtaining[edit | edit source]

The boots are found in the cellar of the Temple of Ikov dungeon, but does not require starting the Temple of Ikov quest.

The cellar is down a set of stairs directly west of the dungeon's entrance ladder. A light source and a slash weapon or knife are required to get to the spawn. Their respawn time is 60 seconds. Players can hop between worlds to quickly obtain multiple pairs.

If lost they can be reobtained by:

  • Picking up the boots in the Temple of Ikov dungeon.
  • Purchasing them from Perdu for 9,800 coins.
