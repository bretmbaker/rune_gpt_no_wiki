Fremennik boots are dark green boots in a unique colour only available to Fremennik. They are sold by Yrsa at Yrsa's Accoutrements. In order to purchase these boots from Yrsa, completion of The Fremennik Trials is required. They can also be given as a reward from Managing Miscellania.
