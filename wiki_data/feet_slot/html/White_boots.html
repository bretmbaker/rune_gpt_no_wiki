The white boots are a pair of White Knight metal boots that requires 10 Defence and the completion of the Wanted! quest to wield. They have the same bonuses as the black boots, with an added +1 prayer bonus. White boots are the cheapest boots in game to offer a Prayer bonus.

Players can purchase them from Sir Vyvin after achieving the rank of Adept (800 black knight kills). They can also be bought on the Grand Exchange or obtained through trading other players.

As with all white armour and metal boots, they cannot be made using the Smithing skill.
