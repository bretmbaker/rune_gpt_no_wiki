Shoes are a quest item in the Spirits of the Elid quest. They are in Nardah's mayor's house. When a knife is used on them, two soles are obtained. One of these must be given to the Genie, who lives in the crevice west of Nardah, in exchange for the Statuette of Elidinis. They can be worn but have no bonuses.
