Builder's boots are obtained during the Tower of Life quest. This item is part of the builder's costume.

If you have destroyed or lost your builder's boots, pickpocket 'No fingers' for another pair.

This item can be stored in the armour case of a costume room, as part of the Builder's costume set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
