[Weapon: Ghostly Boots]


Ghostly bootsare pieces ofmembers onlyarmourworn in thefootwear slot. Players can obtain a pair after speaking toKharrimduring theCurse of the Empty Lordminiquest. The boots, according toRennard, were messenger boots that he used when he was alive.
There are no requirements to wear and, when worn with the other pieces, the player appears 'ghostlike' (i.e.transparent). After initially obtaining the piece, the player can talk to any of the ghosts to get more pieces. A lost pair of boots can be purchased fromPerdufor 15,000 coins.
