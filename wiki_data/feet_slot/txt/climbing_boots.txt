[Weapon: Climbing Boots]


Climbing bootsare a low-level piece of armour used in thetroll quest series, requiring the completion of theDeath Plateauquestto wear. After finishing Death Plateau, players may buy climbing boots fromTenzingfor12each.
Climbing boots are popular among players of all combat levels due to their low price, lack of negative bonuses, and the fact that they give +2Strength bonuswith noDefencerequirement. They offer the same strength bonus asrune bootsandmixed hide boots, which drop fromNechryaelor can be crafted with 69Crafting, respectively. By contrast, climbing boots can be obtained by most players within 30 minutes of making an account.
