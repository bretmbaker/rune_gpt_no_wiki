[Weapon: Silly Jester Boots]


Thesilly jester bootsare part of thesilly jester costume, which is obtained and used duringThe Fremennik Islesquest. They offer the second highestMagicdefence bonus of any pair of boots, tied withinfinity boots,pegasian boots,bloodbark boots, andboots of brimstone, and only beaten by theeternal boots.
If a player were to lose their silly jester boots, they can get them back by searching the king's chest onJatizso.
