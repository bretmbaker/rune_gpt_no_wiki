[Weapon: Pegasian Boots]


Pegasian bootsare boots that require level 75RangedandDefenceto wear. They currently have the highest ranged attack bonus of any pair of boots.
They can be created by using apegasian crystalwith a pair ofranger boots. This process cannot be reversed, and requires level 60 inRunecraftandMagic(boosts cannot be used), granting 200 experience in both skills.
