[Weapon: Decorative Boots (White)]


Decorative bootsare boots available tofree-to-playplayers from theCastle Wars Ticket Exchangeshop for 40 Castle Wars tickets. Players need at least 20Defenceto wear these armoured boots in thefootwear slot. Their bonuses are equivalent to those ofmithril boots.
When unprotected upon death, these boots can be retrieved from thegravestonewithout a fee. However, they will be lost if the death takes place beyond level 20 of theWilderness.
