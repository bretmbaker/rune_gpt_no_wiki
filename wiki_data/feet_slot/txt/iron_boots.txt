[Weapon: Iron Boots]


Iron bootsaremember's onlyarmour pieces inRuneScape, and are worn in thefootwear slotof the player'sInventory. There are no requirements for wearing these boots. To obtain Iron boots the player can either obtain them as a drop fromCockatricesandCave slimes, defeated using theSlayerskill, or from trading with another player.
As with allmetal boots, this piece of armourcannotbe made using theSmithingskill.
