[Weapon: Eternal Boots]


Eternal bootsare boots that require level 75MagicandDefenceto wear. They currently have the highest Magic attack bonus and highest Magic defence of any pair of boots. They have aMagic damagebonus of 1%.
They can be created by using aneternal crystalwith a pair ofinfinity boots. This process cannot be reversed, and requires level 60 inRunecraftandMagic(boosts cannot be used), granting 200 experience in both skills. Unlike the infinity boots, the eternal bootscannotbe stored in theplayer owned house.
