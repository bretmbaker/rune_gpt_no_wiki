[Weapon: Fancier Boots]


Fancier bootsare a fusion offancy bootsandfighting bootsthat are obtained from theCradle of Lifeon the fourth level of theStronghold of Securitydungeon, provided the player's account is aJagex Accountand has two-factor authentication enabled.
Upon death below the 20 level of theWilderness, the boots are sent to the gravestone. Otherwise they are deleted. When lost, they can be reclaimed for free from theCradle of Life. Multiple pairs can be claimed and owned at the same time.
