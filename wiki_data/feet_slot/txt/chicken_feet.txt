[Weapon: Chicken Feet]


Chicken feetare an untradeable cosmetic item. It is part of thechicken outfit, which can be obtained by completing one of theEasterholiday eventsthat occur once per year. This also rewards the player with all other holiday items associated with Easter.
If the "Flap"emotehas been unlocked from theStronghold of Security, when wearing all the pieces of the set, players can do an enhanced "Flap" emote where their character lifts off the ground slightly as if flying.
