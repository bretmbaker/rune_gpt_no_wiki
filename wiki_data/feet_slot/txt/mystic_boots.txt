[Weapon: Mystic Boots]


Mystic bootsare an item part of theMystic robes setworn in the feet slot. They are a type of foot armour for mages. All mystic items require 40Magicand 20Defenceto wear. The blue mystic boots can be bought from theWizards' GuildinYanille. It can be stored in amagic wardrobein a player-owned house.
Despite not being a typical magical item,silly jester bootsgives a greater magic defence bonus but don't have a magic attack bonus, so mystic boots are still usually a good choice overall.
