[Weapon: Rune Boots]


Rune bootsare a pair ofbootsmade ofrunite. They require aDefencelevel of 40 to equip. Players without the required Defence level can choose to wearclimbing bootsinstead, which have the same strength bonus but lower defence bonuses.
As with allmetal boots, this piece of armourcannotbe made using theSmithingskill.
