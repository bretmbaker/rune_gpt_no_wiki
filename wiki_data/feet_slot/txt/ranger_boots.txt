[Weapon: Ranger Boots]


Ranger bootsare a rare reward received from the completion ofmediumTreasure Trails. It is part of theranger kit, and requires 40Rangedto wear; it can also be stored in atreasure chest. Ranger boots are the second best Rangedfootwearin terms of ranged attack bonus.
The drop rate of ranger boots is 1/1,133 per slot.[1]With eachmedium cluereward, a player will receive 3-5 slots of items; this makes the effective drop rate ~1/283.6 per casket. The ranger boots are the most valuable drop from medium clues.
