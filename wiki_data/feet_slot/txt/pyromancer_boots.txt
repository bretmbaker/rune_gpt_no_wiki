[Weapon: Pyromancer Boots]


Thepyromancer bootsare a possible reward from theReward Cartupon subduing theWintertodt. The boots provide a 0.2%Firemakingexperience boost. When wearing the fullpyromancer outfit, the set bonus is 2.5%. Players can also trade in extra pieces for anextra supply crateor 50burnt pagesviaIgnisia.
According to the outfit drop priority of thereward cart, the boots are always the last piece dropped to complete the pyromancer outfit. If thepyromancer robehas been filled in its properSTASH unit, they will not receive the boots, but another robe instead (See thereward cart's unique drop tablefor more information on the mechanics).
