[Weapon: Flippers]


Flippersare an uncommon drop frommogres, which require 32Slayerand completion ofSkippy and the Mogresto obtain. They are worn in the footwear slot and allow players to 'run' while in theunderwaterarea beneathFossil Island(you only need to have the flippers equipped when you enter the underwater area to use 'run', you may switch to other boot slot items after entering the water and retain the ability to 'run'). If a weapon is equipped, you will not be able to 'run' while swimming.
They can be stored in amagic wardrobein a player'scostume room. They can also be stored in atackle box. Flippers can be made intodark flippersby talking toPatchyonMos Le'Harmlesswhile carrying adark clawand500coins.
