[Weapon: Boots Of Lightness#Worn]


Boots of lightnessreduceweightby 4.535 kg when worn, and offer slight defensive bonuses. As they are easy to obtain in bulk, they may be safely used in thewilderness. While they grant a higherweight-reductionthan thegraceful boots, they do not give an increase torun energyrestoration rate.
The boots are found in the cellar of theTemple of Ikov dungeon, but doesnotrequire starting theTemple of Ikovquest.
