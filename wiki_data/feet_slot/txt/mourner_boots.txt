[Weapon: Mourner Boots]


Mourner bootsare part of themourner set. To obtain them, players must kill a level 11Mourneron the path toPrifddinas. However, if not killed in one hit, the Mourner will bring all combat stats down to 20, so it is recommended players bring astat restore potionbefore fighting.
If you've completedSong of the Elves, you can find these boots in aclosed chestin the basement of the Mourner headquarters.
