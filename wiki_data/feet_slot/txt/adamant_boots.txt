[Weapon: Adamant Boots]


Adamant bootsare a pair ofbootsmade ofadamantite. The player needs at least 30Defenceto wear these armoured boots. These are the first tier of metal boots to offer a strength bonus. Players without the required Defence level can choose to wearclimbing bootsinstead, which have one more strength bonus, but lower defence bonuses.
As with allmetal boots, this piece of armourcannotbe made using theSmithingskill.
