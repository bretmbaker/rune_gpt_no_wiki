[Weapon: White Boots]


Thewhite bootsare a pair ofWhite Knightmetal bootsthat requires 10Defenceand the completion of theWanted!questto wield. They have the same bonuses as theblack boots, with an added +1prayer bonus. White boots are the cheapest boots in game to offer aPrayerbonus.
Players can purchase them fromSir Vyvinafter achieving therankof Adept (800black knightkills). They can also be bought on the Grand Exchange or obtained through trading other players.
