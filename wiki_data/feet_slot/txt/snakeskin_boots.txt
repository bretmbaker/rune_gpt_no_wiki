[Weapon: Snakeskin Boots]


Snakeskin bootsaresnakeskin armourworn in thefoot slotavailable topay-to-playrangers. To wear snakeskin boots, a player must have 30Rangedand 30Defence. Snakeskin boots are very popular amongst low/mid-level rangers, because of their low requirements and lack of an affordable upgrade. They are also used as a cheap, disposable ranging footwear in risky situations, such as in theWilderness. For their negative magic attack bonus, they are sometimes used whensplashing.
This item can be stored in thearmour caseof acostume room, as part of thesnakeskin armourset.Ultimate Ironmenwill be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
