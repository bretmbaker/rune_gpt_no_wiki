[Weapon: Jad Slippers]


Jad slippersare an item that can be obtained from participating in anybirthday event, and were first obtained from the2023 Birthday event. They are styled afterTzTok-Jad, and have a "Change-style" option that switches them into a pair ofBob the cat slippers. Like all holiday items, they can be reclaimed fromDiangoif lost.
