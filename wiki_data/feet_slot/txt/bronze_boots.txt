[Weapon: Bronze Boots]


Bronze bootsaremembers onlyarmour that are worn in thefootwear slot. There are no requirements for wearing these boots. To obtain the boots, the player can either get them as an uncommon drop fromCave crawlers, which requireSlayerlevel 10 to kill, or buy them from another player. They have the same stats asFighting bootsandFancy boots.
As with allmetal boots, this piece of armourcannotbe made using theSmithingskill.
