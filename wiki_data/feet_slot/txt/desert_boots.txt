[Weapon: Desert Boots]


Desert bootsare boots suitable for desert environments such as theKharidian Desertsouth ofShantay Pass. Wearing the boots delays thedesert heateffect by 6 additional seconds. The boots have no other bonuses.
A full set ofdesert clothingis needed for theThe Tourist Trapquest. They are given to a slave miner in exchange for a set ofslave robeswhich permit the player to access a deeper area of the mining camp.
