[Weapon: Shoes]


Shoesare aquest itemin theSpirits of the Elidquest. They are inNardah's mayor's house. When aknifeis used on them, twosolesare obtained. One of these must be given to theGenie, who lives in the crevice west of Nardah, in exchange for theStatuette of Elidinis. They can be worn but have no bonuses.
