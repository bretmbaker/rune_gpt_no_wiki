[Weapon: Spiked Manacles]


Spiked manaclesare a rare reward obtained frommediumTreasure Trails. They are tied witharanea bootsfor the best-in-slot strength bonus for footwear that has no requirements to equip. It has equivalent strength bonus todragon bootsand aranea boots, all of which are only surpassed byprimordial boots, though without any of the defensive bonuses.
In comparison toclimbing boots, which also have noDefencerequirement to equip, Spiked manacles have +2 higher strength bonus. However, Spiked manacles have some negative bonuses whereas the Climbing boots have none, and Climbing boots also offer +2 Slash and +2 Crush melee defence bonuses. Aranea boots have slight Ranged and Magic attack bonuses, no negative defence bonuses, and 1 Prayer bonus, making them slightly better than spiked manacles.
