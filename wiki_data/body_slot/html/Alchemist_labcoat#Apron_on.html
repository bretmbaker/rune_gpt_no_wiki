The alchemist labcoat is a piece of the alchemist's outfit purchased from the Mixology Rewards shop with 2,250 Mox resin, 2,800 Aga resin, and 3,700 Lye resin, which are earned from participating in the Mastering Mixology minigame.

They are purely cosmetic and provide no bonuses when worn, and can be changed to don an apron if desired.

This item can be stored in the armour case of a costume room, as part of the alchemist's outfit set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
