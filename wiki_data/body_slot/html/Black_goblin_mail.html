Black goblin mail is made by using black dye on goblin mail. Like all goblin mail, it is too small for players to normally wear as armour.

During the Land of the Goblins quest, the player can turn into a goblin using a transmogrification potion and wear the black goblin mail in order to access the Huzamogaarb tribal room of the Goblin Temple.
