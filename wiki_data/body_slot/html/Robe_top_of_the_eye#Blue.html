The robe top of the eye is a part of the Raiments of the Eye set, purchased from the Guardians of the Rift reward shop for 350 abyssal pearls. It grants 10% more runes when Runecrafting, and is increased to 60% when the full set is worn.

The outfit can be recoloured using abyssal dyes (received from the Rewards Guardian) to a red, blue, or green colour scheme. Each dye recolours one piece of the outfit, so three in total are needed to recolour a set, as the boots can't be recoloured.

This item can be stored in the magic wardrobe of a costume room, as part of the Raiments of the Eye sets. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
