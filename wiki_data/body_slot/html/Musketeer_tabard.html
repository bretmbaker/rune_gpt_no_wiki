The musketeer tabard is a rare reward from elite Treasure Trails, and is part of the Musketeer outfit. It provides no stats and is purely cosmetic.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room, as part of the Musketeer outfit set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
