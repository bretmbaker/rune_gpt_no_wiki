The fighter torso (called the Penance torso in the Barbarian Assault Reward Shop) is armour that requires 40 Defence to equip and is a reward from the Barbarian Assault minigame. It can be purchased from Commander Connad for 375 honour points in all four roles, and one Penance Queen kill. It can be sold back to Connad for 300 honour points in all four roles by using it on him.

It will remain in the player's inventory if they die below level 20 Wilderness, but will be unusable until repaired by using 150,000 coins on Perdu or on an armour stand in a PvP world.

Note: It is possible to obtain at any level, but low level players may struggle specifically with the attacker role.

This item can be stored in the armour case of a costume room.

Advantages[edit | edit source]

The fighter torso is one of the most popular and frequently used armour pieces for melee training, where maximizing damage output via strength bonus outweighs the benefit of any other equipment bonus. Its strength bonus is tied with the Bandos chestplate, Blood moon chestplate and Inquisitor's hauberk but only costs time playing Barbarian Assault instead of coins. Its defence bonuses sit in between that of the adamant and rune platebodies, except for its slash defence bonus which is higher than the rune platebody and even the granite body.

Additionally, it has the highest negative magic accuracy possible in the body slot, making it an effective armour for splashing.

Treasure Trails[edit | edit source]

  • The fighter torso is the item Falo requires players to show him when he sings about: Penance healers runners and more, obtaining this body often gives much deplore.
