Bronze platebody (t) is a part of bronze trimmed armour and a reward from easy Treasure Trails. It has the same stats as a normal Bronze platebody. This item cannot be made via the Smithing skill.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room, as part of the bronze trimmed armour. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
