A swampbark body is a piece of swampbark armour worn in the body slot, requiring level 50 Magic and 50 Defence to equip. The body can be made with level 48 Runecraft by bringing a splitbark body and 500 nature runes to the Nature Altar. Doing so requires players to have first learnt the secret to infuse the armour via reading the runescroll of swampbark.

In addition to their stats, the helm, body, and legs increases the duration of bind spells by 2 ticks (1.2 seconds), with the full set increasing the duration by 6 ticks (3.6 seconds). For comparison, Ice Barrage binds players for 32 ticks (19.2 secs).

  • Bind: 8 ticks (4.8 secs) to 14 ticks (8.4 secs)
  • Snare: 16 ticks (9.6 secs) to 22 ticks (13.2 secs)
  • Entangle: 24 ticks (14.4 secs) to 30 ticks (18.0 secs)

It can be stored in a magic wardrobe in a player-owned house.
