The guild hunter top is a piece of the guild hunter outfit that can be looted from hunters' loot sacks, which are obtained upon completing Hunters' Rumours.

When worn, it increases the catch rate of hunter creatures by 0.8%, increased to 2.5% when wearing the full set. It also increases the chance of receiving a rare creature part by ?%, or 5% with the full set.

This item can be stored in the armour case of a costume room, as part of the Hunters Outfit. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.

Duplicate pieces can be given to Verity for a master-level hunters' loot sack which excludes uniques.
