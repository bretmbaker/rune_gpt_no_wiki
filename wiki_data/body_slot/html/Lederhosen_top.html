Lederhosen top is a clothing item received as a random reward from the Freaky Forester random event, and is a part of the Lederhosen outfit. Like other random event rewards, the top gives no bonuses and simply serves as a cosmetic item.

This item can be stored in the fancy dress box of a costume room, as part of the Lederhosen Outfit set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
