Brown aprons are used to enter the Crafting Guild. They can be purchased from Thessalia's Fine Clothes store in the centre of Varrock for 2 coins.

A brown apron can be obtained from the Tool store 3 built in the Workshop of a player-owned house. Members can also claim them from Malignius Mortifer after the Swan Song quest.
