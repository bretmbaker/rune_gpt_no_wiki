A rune platebody (h5) is an item obtained from hard Treasure Trails. All of the rune heraldic platebodies have the same stat bonuses as the rune platebody. As with all Treasure Trails and rune armour, this cannot be made with the Smithing skill. It requires 40 Defence to equip, as well as completion of Dragon Slayer I.
