The black platebody is an armour used in melee with the same stat bonuses as a white platebody, excluding the Prayer bonus. It requires 10 Defence to equip and provides greater protection than a black chainbody. Like all platebodies, however, it gives a crippling negative bonus to the user's Ranged and Magic stats.

It cannot be made using the Smithing skill, though it's easily obtainable as it can be bought from Horvik, Zenesha or another player.

A black platebody is used in King's Ransom and Heroes' Quest if the player joins the Black Arm Gang.
