The dragon chainbody (g) is a dragon chainbody with a dragon chainbody ornament kit attached to it. The attached ornament kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the chainbody. It costs 241,828 for both the parts to make the dragon chainbody (g).

While the chainbody is untradeable in its ornamented state, it can be dismantled anytime, returning the tradeable chainbody and ornament kit.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room, as part of the dragon gold-trimmed armour set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
