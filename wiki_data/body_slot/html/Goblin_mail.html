Goblin mail is armour for goblins. Although this item has the "Wear" option, this item cannot be equipped by players as it is too small to fit humans. However, during the Land of the Goblins quest, the player can turn into a goblin using a transmogrification potion and wear the goblin mail.

It can be dyed into various colours, in which certain colours are used for the Land of the Goblins quest.

Goblin mail is obtained as an uncommon drop from goblins. In addition, three goblin mails can be found in Goblin Village, north of Falador. One can be found in a crate in a building west of General Bentnoze and General Wartface, another in a crate on the balcony up the ladder near the entrance, and a third one in a crate just north of the building of Bentnoze and Wartface. Three are used in the Goblin Diplomacy quest. During the Land of the Goblins quest there are crates which contain infinite goblin mail in the Goblin Temple.
