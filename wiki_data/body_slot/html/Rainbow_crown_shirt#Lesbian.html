The rainbow crown shirt is an event item unlocked by completing the 2024 Pride event.

There are nine unique versions of the crown shirt representing different variants of sexual orientation or gender identity, whose colours can be changed at any time by right-clicking the "Change" option on the crown shirt. When switched to another style other than the pride (rainbow) style, the item is renamed to a colourful crown shirt.
