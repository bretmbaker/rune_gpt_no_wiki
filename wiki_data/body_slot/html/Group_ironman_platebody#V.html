Group ironman platebody is a platebody obtainable only by players who chose to be a standard group ironman in Group Ironman game mode. It is obtained by using the Armour right-click option on the Group Ironman tutor. It has equivalent stats to an iron platebody, which is the best melee armour a starting player can use.
