[Weapon: Iron Platebody]


Theiron platebodyis an armour used inmeleewith stat bonuses betweenbronzeandsteel. It has noDefencerequirement to equip, which makes it the best platebody for players just starting out. It also provides greater protection than aniron chainbody. Like allplatebodies, however, it gives a crippling negative bonus to the user'sRangedandMagicstats.
If playing as anIronman, anironman platebodywith equivalent stats can be acquired by talking to theIronman tutorinLumbridge.
