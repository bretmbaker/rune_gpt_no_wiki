[Weapon: Green D%27Hide Body (T)]


Agreen d'hide body (t)is part of thegreen d'hide trimmed armourset, along withgreen d'hide chaps (t). Its stats are identical to agreen dragonhide body, but it has green trim added to it. To wear a green dragonhide body (t), a player must have 40Rangedand 40Defence, as well as completion of theDragon Slayer Iquest.
This item is a reward frommediumTreasure Trails. Unlike normaldragonhide armour, this item cannot be made viaCraftingor any other skill.
