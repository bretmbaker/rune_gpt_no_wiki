[Weapon: Bronze Chainbody]


Thebronze chainbodyis a chainbody that can be made at an anvil with 3bronze barsand a hammer with aSmithinglevel of 11, granting 37.5 Smithing experience. Since it requires aDefencelevel of 1 to wear, it can be worn by any player. It is one of the weakest armour chest pieces in the game and is seldom worn. The bronze chainbody can be obtained through a spawn located in theDwarven Mine.
