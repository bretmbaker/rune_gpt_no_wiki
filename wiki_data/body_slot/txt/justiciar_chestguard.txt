[Weapon: Justiciar Chestguard]


TheJusticiar chestguardis part of thejusticiar armourset requiring 75Defenceto equip. It is a rare drop from theTheatre of Blood.
Wearing the full Justiciar armour set grants thepassive effect: all non-PvPcombat damage is reduced bybonus3000, wherebonusis the player's current defence bonus being rolled against by the attacker'sattack style. For example, if an enemy attacks withcrushand the player has a crush defence bonus of +450, then 15% (450/3,000) of the damage is reduced.
