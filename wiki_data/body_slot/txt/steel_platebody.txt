[Weapon: Steel Platebody]


Thesteel platebodyis an armour used inmeleewith stat bonuses betweenironandmithril(not including theblackandwhiteplatebodies). It requires 5Defenceto equip and provides greater protection than asteel chainbody. Like allplatebodies, however, it gives a crippling negative bonus to the user'sRangedandMagicstats.
Players with level 48Smithingcan make one by using 5steel barson ananvilwhile carrying ahammer, granting 187.5experience. Alternatively, they can be bought fromHorvik's SmithyorZenesha's Plate Mail Body Shop.
