[Weapon: Ornate Top]


Theornate topis obtained from completing a clue during theCrack the Clue IIevent. It is part of theornate armourset, and is obtainable only once if you already have it in your inventory or bank. If you happen to accidentally destroy it, you will need to discover it again. Its bonuses are equal to those of aniron chainbody.
In order to (re)obtain the top, the player must perform theShrugandCheeremotesin succession east of theBlack Knights' Fortress.
