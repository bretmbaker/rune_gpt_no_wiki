[Weapon: Frog-Leather Body]


Thefrog-leather bodyis a piece offrog-leather armourthat can be bought fromReldak's Leather Armourin the market place ofDorgesh-Kaan. It can also be looted fromDorgesh-Kaan average chests, specifically from the chests inZanik's house, which require 52Thievingto unlock. Frog-leather armour cannot be created through theCraftingskill.
Wearing the body requires level 25 inDefenceandRanged.
