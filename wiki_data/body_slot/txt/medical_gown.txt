[Weapon: Medical Gown]


Themedical gownis aquest itemused inBiohazardandA Tail of Two Cats. It is found in thecupboardofNurse Sarah's house, south-west of the west Ardougne church.
While it is equipped, the player is allowed to enter theMourners' Headquartersin the north-east corner ofWest Ardougneafter poisoning the stew in the back yard with arotten apple. This item may also be used as part of a medical disguise inA Tail of Two Cats.
