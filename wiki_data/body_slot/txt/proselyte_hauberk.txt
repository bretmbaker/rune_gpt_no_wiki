[Weapon: Proselyte Hauberk]


Theproselyte hauberkis a piece ofTemple Knight Armourthat requires 30Defence, 20Prayer, and completion ofThe Slug Menacequest to equip. It shares the second highestPrayer bonusof any body slot equipment with the3rd age druidic robe topand theElite black platebody, only beaten by theSunfire fanatic cuirass. It has similar bonuses to those of anAdamant platebodywith a +8 Prayer bonus, and is slightly lighter. Oddly enough, it does not stopAva's devicesfrom gatheringarrows.
Like otherProselyteequipment, it is white with a dark-gold trim.
