TheVoid Knight topis a piece ofVoid Knight equipment. It can be bought from aVoid Knightfor 250Void Knight commendation pointsthrough thePest Controlmini game, although a player must have at least 42Attack,Strength,Defence,Hitpoints,Ranged, andMagic, along with 22Prayerto buy and wear one.
It is worn in combination with the other pieces of Void Knight Armour to achieve the designated set effect, depending on whichhelmis worn. This chestpiece does not interfere withAva's devices. Unlike theVoid knight robe, the Void knight top doesn't have more Magic Defence thanits blessed d'hide counterparts(+45, as opposed to a blessed d'hide body's +50).
