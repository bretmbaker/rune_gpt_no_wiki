[Weapon: Saradomin Robe Top]


TheSaradomin robe topis part of theSaradomin vestment setand can be obtained by completingeasyTreasure Trails. To wear, the player needs at least 20Prayer. It can be used as aSaradomin iteminGod Wars Dungeon, but it is unadvised to use due to its nonexistent defensive bonuses. However, it can also be used if you're praying against your target, as it provides a decent prayer bonus of +6.
This item can be stored in thetreasure chestof acostume room, as part of theSaradomin vestment set.Ultimate Ironmenwill be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
