[Weapon: Karil%27S Leathertop#50]


Karil's leathertopis a degradable piece ofranged armourworn in thebody slotthat is part ofKaril the Tainted's setofbarrows equipment. To wear Karil's top, a player must have 70Defenceand 70Ranged.
Being part of Karil's barrows set, if Karil's leathertop is worn along with the other three pieces of Karil's set, the player's attacks gains a special effect: on successful attacks withKaril's crossbowagainst another player, there is a 25% chance that it may temporarily lower that player'sAgilitylevel by 20%. Additionally, if anamulet of the damnedis worn alongside the set, there is also a 25% chance for each attack to deal a second hit for half the damage of the first.
