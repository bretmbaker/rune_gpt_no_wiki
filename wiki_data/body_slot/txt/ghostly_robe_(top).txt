[Weapon: Ghostly Robe (Top)]


Theghostly robeis part of theGhostly Robesset obtained from theCurse of the Empty Lordminiquest. It is a piece of clothing that belonged toValdez, the ghost found nearGlarial's Tomb, which players can obtain after starting the miniquest.
There are no requirements to wear and, when worn with the other pieces, the player appears 'ghostlike' (i.e. transparent). After initially obtaining the piece, the player can talk to any of the ghosts to get more pieces. A lost robe can be purchased fromPerdufor 45,000 coins.
