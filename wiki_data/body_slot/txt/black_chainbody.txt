[Weapon: Black Chainbody]


Theblack chainbodyis stronger than thesteel chainbody, and can be worn by players with 10Defence. Players can purchase this item at theKeldagrimandFaladorChainmail shops. This item cannot be made using theSmithingskill, and has the same bonuses as awhite chainbody, except it lacks aprayer bonus.
Sold atWayne's Chainsshop in southern Falador for 1,440 coins, and atSaro's armour shop in Keldagrim for 1,872 coins.
