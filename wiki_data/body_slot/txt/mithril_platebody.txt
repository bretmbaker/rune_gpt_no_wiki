[Weapon: Mithril Platebody]


Themithril platebodyis an armour used inmeleewith stat bonuses betweensteelandadamant(not including theblackandwhiteplatebodies). It requires 20Defenceto equip and provides greater protection than amithril chainbody. Like allplatebodies, however, it gives a crippling negative bonus to the user'sRangedandMagicstats.
Players with level 68Smithingcan make one by using 5mithril barson ananvilwhile carrying ahammer, granting 250 Smithingexperience. Alternatively, they can be bought from a handful of armour shops.
