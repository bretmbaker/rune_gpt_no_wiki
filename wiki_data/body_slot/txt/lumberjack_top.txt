[Weapon: Lumberjack Top]


Thelumberjack topis a part of thelumberjack clothingset that can be purchased from theForestry Shopfor 1,500anima-infused bark, 60 notedyew logs, 120 notedmagic logs, and 120 notedredwood logs. These logs can be bought on theGrand Exchangefor139,440. It is also dropped byundead lumberjacksin theTemple Trekkingminigame. It provides a +0.8% boost inWoodcuttingexperience, and is increased to +2.5% when the full set is worn.
It can be stored in theForestry kitwith aClothes pouch.
