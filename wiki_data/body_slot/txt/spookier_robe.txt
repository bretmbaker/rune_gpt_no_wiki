[Weapon: Spookier Robe]


Thespookier robeis a cosmetic reward from the2019 Halloween event. It is part of thespookier outfit, and can be obtained as a reward from the Halloween events that occur once per year. It is unlocked by using thespooky robeon theswampy sinkoutside the toolshed in theLumbridge Swamp, or on theEctofuntusoutsidePort Phasmatys.
It is meant to mimic theectoplasm-covered bedsheetobtained during theGhosts Ahoyquest, which players have often requested that it be wearable outsidePort Phasmatysbut cannot due to restricted interaction checks in the game code.
