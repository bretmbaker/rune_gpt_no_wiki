[Weapon: Zamorak Robe Top]


TheZamorak robe topis part of theZamorak vestment setand can be obtained by completingeasyTreasure Trails. To wear, the player needs at least 20Prayer. It can be used as aZamorak iteminGod Wars Dungeon, but it is unadvised to use due to its very low defensive bonuses. However, it can be used in conjunction withprotection prayersas it provides a Prayer bonus of +6.
This item can be stored in thetreasure chestof acostume room, as part of theZamorak vestment set.Ultimate Ironmenwill be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
