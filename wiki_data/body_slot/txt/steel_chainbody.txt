[Weapon: Steel Chainbody]


TheSteel chainbodyis the third weakest chainbody in theRuneScape, and it can be worn by a player with level 5Defence.
A player with level 41Smithingcan forge the steel chainbody by using threesteel barson ananvilwith ahammerin their inventory, granting 112.5Smithingexperience. It can also be bought fromWayne's ChainsinFalador, orHorvik's Armour ShopinVarrock.
