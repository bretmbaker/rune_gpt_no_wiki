[Weapon: Spined Body]

To obtain a spined body, a player can either bring 3dagannoth hides, 1flattened hide, and 10,000 coins toSigli the Huntsmanin Rellekka, receive it as a drop fromDagannoth Supreme, ortradewith another player.
