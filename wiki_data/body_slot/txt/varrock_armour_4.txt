[Weapon: Varrock Armour 4]


Varrock armour 4is a reward from completing the eliteVarrock Diary. The armour is received fromTobyinVarrockand can be retrieved from him for free if lost. Its stats are almost identical to those of aadamant platebody, but significantly lighter while providing a higher range bonus.
A number of other benefitsare always active upon claiming rewards from the elite tier of the Varrock diary.
