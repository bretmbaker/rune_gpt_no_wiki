[Weapon: Iron Chainbody]


Theiron chainbodyis the second weakestchainbodyinRuneScape, and can be worn by a player with 1Defence. A player with 26Smithingcan make the chainbody by using threeiron barson ananvilwhile carrying ahammer, granting 75 Smithingexperience.
An iron chainbody is required for theBlack Knights' Fortress,King's Ransom, andWhile Guthix Sleepsquests.
