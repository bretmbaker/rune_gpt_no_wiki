[Weapon: Varrock Armour 3]


Varrock armour 3is a reward from completing thehard Varrock Diary. The armour is received fromTobyinVarrockand can be retrieved from him for free if lost. Its stats are almost identical to those of amithril platebody, but significantly lighter while providing a higher range bonus.
A number of other benefitsare always active upon claiming rewards from the hard tier of the Varrock diary.
