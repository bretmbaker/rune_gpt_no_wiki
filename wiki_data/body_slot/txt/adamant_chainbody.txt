[Weapon: Adamant Chainbody]


Adamant chainbodyis piece ofarmourmade fromadamantite. A player can create an adamant chainbody by using anadamantite baron ananvilwhile ahammerand 3 adamantite bars are in the player's inventory. ASmithinglevel of 81 is required to make an adamant chainbody and doing so will grant the player 187.5 Smithing experience. Level 30Defenceis required to wear an adamant chainbody. It can also be bought fromWayne's Chainsin southFalador. One adamant chainbody is required forSong of the Elves.
