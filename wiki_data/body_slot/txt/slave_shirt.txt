[Weapon: Slave Shirt]


Theslave shirtis an item of clothing that is required to enter the mine undetected inThe Tourist Trapquest. Players obtain this item by trading a desert shirt with a slave during the quest.Rowdy slavescan also be killed in the mine to drop it.
Wearing the slave shirt will delay the onset ofdesert heatby 6 seconds.
