[Weapon: 3Rd Age Platebody]


The3rd age platebodyis a part of the3rd age melee set. Requiring level 65Defenceto wear, it has the third highest defence bonuses of all non-degradable melee body slot items, only surpassed by thejusticiar chestguardandTorva platebody. The 3rd age platebody is a possible reward fromhard,eliteandmastertierTreasure Trailsand cannot be made through theSmithingskill.
Its bonuses resemble that of achainbody, emphasising crush defence bonus. It has no negative range attack bonus and retrieves ammunition with Ava's devices. Due to its price and lack of offensive bonuses, it is very rarely used for combat.
