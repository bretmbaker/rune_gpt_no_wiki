[Weapon: Black Desert Shirt]


Ablack desert shirtis an item of clothing that players may find useful when doing theShadow of the Stormquest. They can make it by usingblack dyeon a normaldesert shirt. This can also be done with adesert robeto create ablack desert robe.
The quest requires players to be wearing at least three black garments, in order to look evil. Players may already be wearing a desert shirt (since the main part of the quest takes place in theKharidian Desert), andblack mushroomscan be found locally.
