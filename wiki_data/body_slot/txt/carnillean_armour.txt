[Weapon: Carnillean Armour]


Carnillean armouris obtainableonlyduringCeril's side of theHazeel Cultquest by killing Alomone and looting the nearby chest. It is possible to get multiple armour pieces by looting the chest multiple times. Carnillean requires aDefencelevel of 1 to wear but is worse than aniron platebody. Some mages or archers may wish to use Carnillean armour due to it not lowering magic or ranged attacks. This piece of armour will not interfere withAva's devices.
