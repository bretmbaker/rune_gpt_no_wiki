[Weapon: Armadyl Chestplate]


TheArmadyl chestplateis an item that can be obtained fromKree'arraand his bodyguards in theGod Wars Dungeon. Along with theArmadyl helmetandchainskirt, it is part of theArmadyl armourset, and requires 70DefenceandRangedto wear.
It penalises melee attack bonuses which may make it undesirable in situations where multiple attack styles are used.
