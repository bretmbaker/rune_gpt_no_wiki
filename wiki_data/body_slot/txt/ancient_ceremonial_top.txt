[Weapon: Ancient Ceremonial Top]


Theancient ceremonial topis an item dropped by all NPCs in theAncient Prison, excludingNex. Part of theancient ceremonial robesset, the top has no requirements to wear. In addition, as aZarosian item, it will also grantgod protectiontowards Zarosian NPCs in theGod Wars Dungeon. It has the same stats as amonk's robe top.
Unlike the version of the robes set inRuneScape, wearing themwill notallow players to bypass the Zarosiankill countto reach Nex's chamber, as theecumenical keyalready exists to do so.
