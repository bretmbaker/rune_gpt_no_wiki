[Weapon: Lunar Torso]


Thelunar torsois a piece oflunar equipmentworn in thebody slot. 65Magicand 40Defenceare required to wear the lunar torso. The lunar torso is created during theLunar Diplomacyquest by using aneedleandthreadonSuqah leather, but it may be bought from theOneiromancerafter a player has completed the quest for a price of120,000 coins. It may also be purchased fromPerdufor140,000 coins. It can be stored in amagic wardrobein a player-owned house.
As with all lunar equipment, it is always lost on death.
