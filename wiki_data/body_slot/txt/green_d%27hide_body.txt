[Weapon: Green D%27Hide Body]


Thegreen d'hide bodyis a part of thegreen dragonhide armourset. In order to wear the body, a player must first have completed theDragon Slayer Iquest and have level 40RangedandDefence. It offers the best ranged accuracy bonus among allfree-to-playbody armours, although its defensive bonuses are surpassed by thegilded d'hide body.
Although players must complete Dragon Slayer I in order to wear a green d'hide body, completion of this quest isnotrequired to equipblue,red, andblackd'hide bodies.
