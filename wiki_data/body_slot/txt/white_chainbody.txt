[Weapon: White Chainbody]


Thewhite chainbodyis a piece ofWhite Knight armourthat requires 10Defenceand the completion of theWanted!questto wield. It has the same bonuses as theblack chainbody, with an added +1prayer bonus.
Players can purchase it fromSir Vyvinafter achieving therankof Novice (100black knightkills). It can also be bought on the Grand Exchange or obtained through trading other players.
