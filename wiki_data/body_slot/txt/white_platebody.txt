[Weapon: White Platebody]


Thewhite platebodyis a piece ofWhite Knight armourthat requires 10Defenceand the completion of theWanted!questto wield. It has the same bonuses as theblack platebody, with an added +1prayer bonus. It also provides greater protection than awhite chainbody. Like allplatebodies, however, it gives a crippling negative bonus to the user'sRangedandMagicoffensive stats.
Players can purchase it fromSir Vyvinafter achieving therankof Adept (800black knightkills). It can also be bought on the Grand Exchange or obtained through trading other players.
