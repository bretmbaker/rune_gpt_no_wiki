[Weapon: Group Ironman Platebody#Iii]


Group ironman platebodyis a platebody obtainable only by players who chose to be a standard group ironman inGroup Ironmangame mode. It is obtained by using theArmourright-click option on theGroup Ironman tutor. It has equivalent stats to aniron platebody, which is the best melee armour a starting player can use.
