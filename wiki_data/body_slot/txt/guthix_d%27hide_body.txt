TheGuthix d'hide bodyis a piece ofRanged armouraligned withGuthix(does not count as agod iteminGod Wars Dungeon). At least 40Defenceis required to wear this body, along with 70Ranged. It has the same offensive stats as ablack dragonhide body, with an additional +1prayer bonus, and has higher defensive bonuses.
Along with otherblessed dragonhide armourpieces, itcannotbe made using theCraftingskill. Players can receive them as a reward from completinghardTreasure Trails.
