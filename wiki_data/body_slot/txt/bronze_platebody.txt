[Weapon: Bronze Platebody]


Thebronze platebodyis an armour used inmeleeand the weakest of allplatebodies. It has noDefencerequirement to equip and provides greater protection than abronze chainbody. Like all platebodies, however, it gives a crippling negative bonus to the user'sRangedandMagicstats.
Players with level 18Smithingcan make one by using 5bronze barson ananvilwhile carrying ahammer, granting 62.5experience. Alternatively, they can be bought fromHorvik's SmithyorZenesha's Plate Mail Body Shop.
