[Weapon: Black Platebody]


Theblack platebodyis anarmourused inmeleewith the same stat bonuses as awhite platebody, excluding thePrayerbonus. It requires 10Defenceto equip and provides greater protection than ablack chainbody. Like allplatebodies, however, it gives a crippling negative bonus to the user'sRangedandMagicstats.
It cannot be made using theSmithingskill, though it's easily obtainable as it can be bought fromHorvik,Zeneshaor another player.
