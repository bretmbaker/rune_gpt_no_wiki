Sinhaza shroud tier 3 is a cosmetic cape that can be obtained from the Mysterious Stranger by the entrance to the Theatre of Blood after completing 1,000 Theatre of Blood raids.

Players can use the right-click "Change" option to alter the cape's appearance to other tiers, providing that they have met the completion count required for them.

This item can be stored in the cape rack of a costume room.
