The Moonclan cape is part of the Moonclan robes set. It can be purchased from the clothing store on Lunar Isle during or after Lunar Diplomacy. It is currently the only cape to have a negative defence and attack bonus for any style. It can be stored in a magic wardrobe in a player-owned house.
