The fire max cape is the result of combining a fire cape with a max cape, during which the max hood required to be in the player's inventory will automatically convert to the fire max hood.

Like the other max cape variants (a max cape combined with another cape slot item), the fire max cape does not have the max cape's stats or perks with the following exceptions when worn:

  • It acts as a Cooking cape to allow access to the Cooks' Guild without a chef's hat, golden chef's hat, Varrock armour 3 or 4
  • It acts as a Crafting cape to allow access to the Crafting Guild without a brown apron or golden apron
  • It acts as a Runecraft cape to prevent essence pouches from degrading (but does not allow access to runic altars)
  • It acts as a Firemaking cape to work as a piece of warm clothing
  • It can be used to perform the Skill Cape emote.

Mac is able to refund a max cape, along with its variants, for 1,821,600 coins each. Note that items combined with the max cape will be lost upon refund and will need to be reobtained.

If the player dies above level 20 Wilderness and the cape is unprotected, it will be converted into coins when dropped. If they die below level 20 Wilderness, it will remain in their inventory; however, it will be unusable and must be repaired by Perdu for 99,000 coins.

A fire max cape cannot be gambled for a chance at Tzrek-jad.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Unlike the fire cape, the fire max cape's lava textures are not animated when dropped.
  • The only max cape variants that were considered "Warm Clothing" at Wintertodt were this variant and the Infernal max cape, until the TzHaar-Ket-Rak's Challenges update, at which point all variants inherited the "warm" status.
  • A common nickname from players for the cape is a "max cheese cape".
