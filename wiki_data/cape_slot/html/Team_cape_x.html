A team cape x is a rare reward from easy Treasure Trails. It can be purchased by free-to-play players from other players or the Grand Exchange.

This item can be stored in the cape rack of a costume room.

This item counts as a Team Cape for the Wilderness Easy Achievement Diary Task.
