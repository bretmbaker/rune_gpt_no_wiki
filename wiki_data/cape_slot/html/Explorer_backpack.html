The explorer backpack is a reward from hard Treasure Trails. It offers no stat bonuses and is purely cosmetic.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The examine info is a reference to the Backpack Song from the children's television series Dora the Explorer.
  • The backpack bears a strong resemblance to Tolna's backpack in A Soul's Bane as well as the Mountain Guide's backpack.
