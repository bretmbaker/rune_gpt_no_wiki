The diving apparatus is used for diving into the Mogre Camp in the waters of Port Khazard, during the Freeing Pirate Pete subquest of the Recipe for Disaster quest. It is used to get to the sea floor. It is also used in the underwater tunnel on Harmony Island during The Great Brain Robbery, as well as the underwater area of Fossil Island.

It is worn in the cape slot, and if worn alongside the fishbowl helmet, players' oxygen will not deplete while underwater. If the apparatus is lost, Murphy will provide another one upon choosing the diving option when talking to him. He will not give the apparatus if the player has an apparatus stored elsewhere. It can be stored in a tackle box.
