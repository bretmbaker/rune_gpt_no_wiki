Fremennik purple cloaks can be bought from Yrsa's Accoutrements in western Rellekka. They can be found in eleven different colours: cyan, brown, blue, green, red, grey, yellow, teal, purple, pink, and black. These capes have the same bonuses as a normal cape.

These capes can only be bought after a player has completed The Fremennik Trials quest.

This item can be obtained when the player is chasing Solus during the Wanted! quest.
