The Ardougne max cape is the result of combining the Ardougne cloak 4 with a max cape, during which the max hood required to be in the player's inventory will automatically convert to the Ardougne max hood.

Like the other max cape variants (a max cape combined with another cape slot item), the Ardougne max cape does not have the max cape's stats or perks; it only acts as a cosmetic upgrade to the Ardougne cloak 4 with the following exceptions when worn:

  • It acts as a Cooking cape to allow access to the Cooks' Guild without a chef's hat, golden chef's hat, Varrock armour 3 or 4
  • It acts as a Crafting cape to allow access to the Crafting Guild without a brown apron or golden apron
  • It acts as a Runecraft cape to prevent essence pouches from degrading (but does not allow access to runic altars)
  • It acts as a Firemaking cape to work as a piece of warm clothing
  • It can be used to perform the Skill Cape emote.

Mac is able to refund a max cape, along with its variants, for 1,821,600 coins each. Note that items combined with the max cape will be lost upon refund and will need to be reobtained.

Unlike the Ardougne cloak 4, the Ardougne max cape can be stored on the cape hanger in a player-owned house.

If the player dies above level 20 Wilderness and the cape is unprotected, it will be converted into coins when dropped.
