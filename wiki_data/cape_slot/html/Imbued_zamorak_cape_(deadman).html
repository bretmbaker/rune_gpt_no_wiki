The Deadman imbued Zamorak cape is an ornamented variant of the imbued Zamorak cape, made by giving Nigel an Armageddon cape fabric to unlock the god cape transformations, and using the cape on him. This cape, like its base component, counts as a Zamorak item for God Wars Dungeon.

This process can be undone by speaking to Nigel, returning the original cape back.
