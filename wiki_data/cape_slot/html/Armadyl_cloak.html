The Armadyl cloak is part of the Armadyl vestment set and can be obtained by completing medium Treasure Trails. A minimum of level 40 Prayer is required to wear one.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room, as part of the Armadyl vestment set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
