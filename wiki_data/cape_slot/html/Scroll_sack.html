The scroll sack is obtained after completing 100 master Treasure Trails. It is a large sack containing many clue scrolls, with a spade on the side of it.

It can be stored in the treasure chest in a player-owned house. If lost, it can be re-obtained by speaking to Watson or by completing another master clue scroll.

As of 30 March 2025, there are 26,865 players that have completed at least 100 master Treasure Trails.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The reward for completing 100 master clue scrolls was largely inspired by player design, and multiple rewards were polled (in Round 1 and Round 2).
  • When worn with a Godsword or Clan vexillum, the scrolls at the top of the sack will blow in the wind, similar to the bottom half of most normal capes swaying.

  • The initial three proposed rewards. It was not well received by players.

  • The six proposed rewards put into the Master Clue reward item poll.

  • The clue scroll sack scrolls swaying in the wind.
