The mourner cloak is part of the mourner set. To obtain them, players must kill a level 11 Mourner on the path to Prifddinas. However, the Mourner will bring all combat stats down to 20, so it is recommended players bring a stat restore potion before fighting. They give no bonuses.

If you lose this cape, you can get another one by killing a level 11 Mourner again. If you've completed Song of the Elves, you can find this cloak in a closed chest in the basement of the Mourner headquarters.

This item can be stored in the armour case of a costume room.
