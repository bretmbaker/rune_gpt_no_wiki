The clue hunter cloak is a cloak obtained from completing a clue during the Crack the Clue! event. It is part of the clue hunter outfit.

In order to obtain/reobtain the cloak, the player must dig between two willow trees south-east of Yanille.

This item can be stored in the armour case of a costume room, as part of the Clue hunter outfit set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Its examine text is a nod to George R.R. Martin's fantasy novel A Song of Ice and Fire.
  • Unlike most capes, the cloak appears to be furled up in the player's inventory and when dropped on the ground.
