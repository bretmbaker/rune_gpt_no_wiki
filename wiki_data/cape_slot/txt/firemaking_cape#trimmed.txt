[Weapon: Firemaking Cape#Trimmed]


AFiremaking capeis aCape of Accomplishment. It can be purchased for 99,000coinsalong with theFiremaking hoodfromIgnatius Vulcan(who can be found south ofSeers' Village) by players who have achievedskill masteryin theFiremakingskill.
Like allskill capes, the Firemaking cape gives +9 to alldefensive stats, and also a +4 bonus toprayerif it is trimmed, which requires a separate skill at level 99. The cape is automatically trimmed if this condition is met.
