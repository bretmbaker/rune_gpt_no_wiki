[Weapon: Forestry Basket#Closed]


TheForestry basketis an upgraded variant of theForestry kitwith the functionality of thelog basketadded to it. Creating it requires level 75SmithingandWoodcutting, the aforementioned items, and thesturdy harness. Like the Forestry kit, theclothes pouchcan be attached to the Forestry basket.
Like the log basket, players can right-click to open the Forestry basket to have logs that the player picks up or chop be automatically placed inside the basket, provided there is space for it.
