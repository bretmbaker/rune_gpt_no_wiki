[Weapon: Ornate Cape]


Theornate capeis obtained from completing a clue during theCrack the Clue IIevent. It is part of theornate armourset, and is obtainable only once if you already have it in your inventory or bank. If you happen to accidentally destroy it, you will need to discover it again.
In order to obtain/reobtain the cape, the player must dig by the entrance to theChaos Templein theWildernesswith agoblin mailin their inventory.
