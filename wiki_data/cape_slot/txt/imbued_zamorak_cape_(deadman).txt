[Weapon: Imbued Zamorak Cape (Deadman)]


TheDeadman imbued Zamorak capeis an ornamented variant of theimbued Zamorak cape, made by givingNigelanArmageddon cape fabricto unlock the god cape transformations, and using the cape on him. This cape, like its base component, counts as a Zamorak item for God Wars Dungeon.
This process can be undone by speaking to Nigel, returning the original cape back.
