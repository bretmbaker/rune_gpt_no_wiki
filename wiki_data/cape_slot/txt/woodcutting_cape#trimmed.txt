[Weapon: Woodcutting Cape#Trimmed]


AWoodcutting capecan be purchased fromWilfred(found south of the furnace inLumbridge) for 99,000coinsand comes with theWoodcutting hoodonly for players who have achieved level 99Woodcutting.
Like allskill capes, the Woodcutting cape gives +9 to all defensive stats, and also a +4 bonus to prayer if it is trimmed, which requires a separate skill at level 99. The cape is automatically trimmed if this condition is met.
