[Weapon: Ranging Cape#Untrimmed]


Theranging capeis aCape of Accomplishmentawarded to players who have achieved 99Ranged. The skillcape and its respective hood can be bought from Aaron theArmour salesmanin theRanging Guildfor 99,000 coins.
Like allskill capes, the Ranging cape gives +9 to all defensive stats, and also a +4 bonus to prayer if it is trimmed, which requires a separate skill at level 99. The cape is automatically trimmed if this condition is met.
