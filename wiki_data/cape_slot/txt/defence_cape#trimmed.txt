[Weapon: Defence Cape#Trimmed]


TheDefence capeis aCape of Accomplishmentthat players can purchase only when they've reachedlevel 99in theDefenceskill. It can be bought fromHarlan, the Melee combat tutor, for 99,000coinsfrom where he resides, close to theLumbridgegeneral store.
Like allskill capes, the Defence cape gives +9 to alldefensive stats, and also a +4 bonus toprayerif it is trimmed, which requires a separate skill at level 99. The cape is automatically trimmed if this condition is met.
