[Weapon: Diving Apparatus]


Thediving apparatusis used for diving into theMogre Campin the waters ofPort Khazard, during theFreeing Pirate Petesubquest of theRecipe for Disasterquest. It is used to get to the sea floor. It is also used in the underwater tunnel onHarmony IslandduringThe Great Brain Robbery, as well as theunderwater areaofFossil Island.
It is worn in thecapeslot, and if worn alongside thefishbowl helmet, players' oxygen will not deplete whileunderwater. If the apparatus is lost,Murphywill provide another one upon choosing the diving option when talking to him. He will not give the apparatus if the player has an apparatus stored elsewhere. It can be stored in atackle box.
