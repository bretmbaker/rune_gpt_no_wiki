[Weapon: Mourner Cloak]


Themourner cloakis part of themourner set. To obtain them, players must kill a level 11Mourneron the path toPrifddinas. However, the Mourner will bring all combat stats down to 20, so it is recommended players bring astat restore potionbefore fighting. They give no bonuses.
If you lose this cape, you can get another one by killing a level 11Mourneragain. If you've completedSong of the Elves, you can find this cloak in aclosed chestin the basement of the Mourner headquarters.
