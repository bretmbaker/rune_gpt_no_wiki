Blessed Dizana's quiveris an upgraded variant ofDizana's quiver, made by bringing it and 150,000sunfire splintersto aShrine of Ralos.
The blessed quiver permanently gains the effects ofDizana's Sunfire, where an additional +1Ranged Strengthand +10Ranged accuracyis applied to storedarrowsandbolts- essentially acting as a variant of the original with unlimited charges. The upgraded variant also retains its level 75Rangedrequirement to equip.
