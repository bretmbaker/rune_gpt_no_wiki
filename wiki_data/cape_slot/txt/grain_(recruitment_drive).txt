[Weapon: Grain (Recruitment Drive)]


The sack ofgrainis used duringSir Spishyus's trial inRecruitment Drive. It is found on the east side of theprecarious bridge.
The grain cannot be left with thechickenalone on one side of the bridge, or else it will eat the entire sack. If this occurs, the player will fail and is returned toFalador.
