[Weapon: Monkey (Monkey Madness Ii)]


Monkeyis an item obtained after the completion ofMonkey Madness II. Players can get this item by searching the westernmostcrateat theCrash Site Cavernentrance. Upon doing so, a dialogue box will appear in chat stating, "There's a sweet little monkey hiding in the warm crate. You gently pick it up."
Players are able to Release the Monkey via right-click, prompting a dialogue box to appear stating, "The monkey scampers back to the warmth of its crate." If players wish to retrieve the Monkey again they will have to search the crate back at the Crash Site Cavern entrance.
