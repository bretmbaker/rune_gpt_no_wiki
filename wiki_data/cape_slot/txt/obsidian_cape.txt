[Weapon: Obsidian Cape]


Theobsidian capeis a piece of theObsidian armour. Due to its lack of requirements and high defensive bonuses, it is a popular choice for players who lack afire capeor aCape of Accomplishment. It is one of few capes to count aswarm clothingat theWintertodt, although for this purpose some players may instead opt for the free, easily obtainableclue hunter cloak.
Areversed colour versionmay be obtained as amaster clue scrollreward fromTreasure Trails, which is statistically identical but the colours are inverted; it is red with black trim, as opposed to black with a red trim.
