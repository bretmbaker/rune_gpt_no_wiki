[Weapon: Fish Sack]


TheFish sackis an item that can be purchased for 1,000Molch pearlsfromAlry the AngleronMolch Island. The Fish sack is worn in place of a cape and is considered cosmetic apart from the minor defence bonuses.
Players can combine the fish sack with afish barrelto create thefish sack barrel, which is an equipable version of the fish barrel. There is no confirmation message when this occurs, but it can be split apart by selecting the "Dismantle" option after right-clicking "Configure".
