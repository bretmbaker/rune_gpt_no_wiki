[Weapon: Bonesack]


Thebonesackis a large sack filled with bones worn in thecape slot. Alongside theram skull helm, it is given to the player as a reward for completingRag and Bone Man II. If lost, theOdd Old Manwill offer a replacement for free.
Despite appearing to hold bones, the bonesack cannot store any bones.
