[Weapon: Attack Cape#Trimmed]


Anattack capeis aCape of Accomplishmentthat can be bought for 99,000coinsfromAjjatat theWarriors' Guildby any player who has achievedlevel 99in theAttackskill.
Like allskill capes, the Attack cape gives +9 to alldefensive stats, and also a +4 bonus toprayerif it is trimmed, which requires a separate skill at level 99. The cape is automatically trimmed if this condition is met.
