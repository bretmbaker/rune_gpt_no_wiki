[Weapon: Mixed Hide Cape]


Amixed hide capeis a part of themixed hide armourset, requiring level 60Rangedand 50Defenceto wear. It provides bonuses that complement thehunter's spear.
Players with level 68Craftingcan create the cape with amixed hide baseand ajaguar fur, alongside aneedleandthread. Players without the required level may instead pay 20,000coinstoPellemat theHunter Guildto make one on their behalf, whilst providing ajaguar fur.
