[Weapon: Graceful Cape (Kourend)#Worn]


TheKourend graceful capeis one of many variants of thegraceful cape. It is recoloured from a graceful cape by using it onOsteninShayzien(in the building east of the bank), costing 15marks of grace. Doing so requires completion of all five house quests in theGreat Kourend quest series:The Queen of Thieves,The Depths of Despair,The Ascent of Arceuus,The Forsaken Tower, andTale of the Righteous.
This item can be stored in themagic wardrobeof acostume room.
