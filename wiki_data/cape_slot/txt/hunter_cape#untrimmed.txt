[Weapon: Hunter Cape#Untrimmed]


TheHunter capeis theCape of Accomplishmentfor theHunterskilland can be purchased for 99,000coinsalongside theHunter hoodfrom theHunting expertlocated in theFeldip Hills, by players who have achievedlevel 99.
Like allskill capes, the Hunter cape gives +9 to alldefensive stats, and also a +4 bonus to prayer if it is trimmed, which requires a separate skill at level 99. The cape is automatically trimmed if this condition is met.
