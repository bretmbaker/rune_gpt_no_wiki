Clan cloaksare capes that players can obtain from theClerkin theClan Hub. Players must be in aclanto claim a clan cloak andclan vexillum.
The clan motif that is on the cloak can be customised in theClan Settingsinterface by those with the appropriate clan permissions. Upon closing of the customisation interface, the new clan motif will automatically update for all clan members.
