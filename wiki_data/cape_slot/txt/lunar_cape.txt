[Weapon: Lunar Cape]


Thelunar capeis thecapeof thelunar equipmentset, requiring 65Magicand 40Defenceto equip. During theLunar Diplomacyquest,Pauline Polarisgives it to the player once they correctly guess her name, Jane Blud-Hagic-Maid. After the quest is completed it is purchased from theOneiromancerorPerdu'sLost Property shop. It can be stored in amagic wardrobein a player-owned house.
As with all lunar equipment, it is always lost on death.
