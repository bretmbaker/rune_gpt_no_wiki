[Weapon: Red Cape (Soul Wars)]


Ared capeis a cosmetic cape worn by players during a game ofSoul Wars. The cape, alongside ared icon, are automatically equipped by players in the red team upon the start of a game, and cannot be unequipped. When a game of Soul Wars ends or the player leaves the tutorial, both the cape and icon are removed from the player.
Nomadcan upgrade the player'sSoul Wars capeswithammunitionsaving effects whenAva's devices(or theirmax capevariants) are used on him.
