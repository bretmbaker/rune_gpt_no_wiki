[Weapon: Icthlarin%27S Shroud (Tier 3)]


Icthlarin's shroud (tier 3)is a cosmetic cape that can be obtained by searching the chest across from theGrouping Obeliskin the main lobby after completing 1,000Tombs of Amascutraids in Normal/Expert Mode.
Players can use the right-click "Change" option to alter the cape's appearance to other tiers, providing that they have met the completion count required for them.
