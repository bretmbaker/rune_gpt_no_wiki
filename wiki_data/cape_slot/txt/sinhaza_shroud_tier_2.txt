[Weapon: Sinhaza Shroud Tier 2]


Sinhaza shroud tier 2is a cosmetic cape that can be obtained from theMysterious Strangerby the entrance to theTheatre of Bloodafter completing 500 Theatre of Blood raids.
Players can use the right-click "Change" option to alter the cape's appearance to other tiers, providing that they have met the completion count required for them.
