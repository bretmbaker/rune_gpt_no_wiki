[Weapon: Santa%27S List]


TheSanta's listis a cosmetic reward from the2022 Christmas event. It can be obtained as a reward from the Christmas events that occur once per year.
Using theCheckoption yields one of the following text lines to appear in the player's chatbox, differing depending on whether the player is on the "nice list" or the "naughty list". This is chosen at random the first time you check. If a player fails the first festive game, "Pass the Coal," three times they will be caught cheating and be put on the naughty list. Members can pet thedogat theMuseum Campto be put on the nice list.  Free-to-play accounts can pet thedogin the chicken coop on the south-west corner ofFalador farmto be put on the nice list.
