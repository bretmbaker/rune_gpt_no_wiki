[Weapon: Farming Cape#Trimmed]


TheFarming capecan be purchased for 99,000coinsalongside theFarming hoodfromMartin the Master GardeneratDraynor Villageby players who have achievedskill mastery(level 99) inFarming. It is theCape of Accomplishmentfor the Farmingskill.
Like allskill capes, the Farming cape gives +9 to alldefensive stats, and also a +4 bonus toprayerif it is trimmed, which requires a separate skill at level 99. The cape is automatically trimmed if this condition is met.
