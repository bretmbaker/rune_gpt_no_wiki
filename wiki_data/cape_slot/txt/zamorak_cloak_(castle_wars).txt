[Weapon: Zamorak Cloak (Castle Wars)]


TheZamorak cloakis an item used in theCastle Warsminigameto indicate a player's team. Players are given their team's cloak when they enter any of the threeGodportals:Zamorak,Zamorak, orGuthix. Because of this cloak players arenotallowed to wear anything in the cape slot.
The cloak allows the player to teleport back to their team's starting room by using the 'Surrender' option, providing their current hitpoints are below 15%. Similarly to dying, this fully replenishes the player's stats and run energy.
