[Weapon: Log Basket#Open]


Alog basketis an item that allows players to store a combined total of 28 logs of any type. The basket can be purchased from theForestry Shopfor 5,000anima-infused bark, as well as 300 notedoakandwillow logs. Players (excludingUltimate Ironmen) can sell the basket back to the rewards shop for a 80% return of the cost to purchase.
Players can automatically store logs into their basket whilst chopping a tree if players select the "Open" option. Players can check the basket's contents by selecting the "Check" right-click option. This tells the number and type of logs the player has inside and an option to empty the logs into the player's inventory. The basketdoes notstore fungi collected through cuttingsulliuscep,Bruma rootsobtained atWintertodt, or any type ofPyre logs.
