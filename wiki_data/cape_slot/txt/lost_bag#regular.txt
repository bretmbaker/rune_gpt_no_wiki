[Weapon: Lost Bag#Regular]


Thelost bagis a cape slot item that can be obtained from anintricate pouch, which can be received from theRewards Guardianafter completing a round ofGuardians of the Rift. It has no stat bonuses and is purely cosmetic.
The bag can be recoloured usingabyssal dye(received from theRewards Guardian) to a red, blue, or green colour scheme.
