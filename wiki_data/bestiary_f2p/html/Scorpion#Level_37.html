Scorpions are monsters historically iconic for inhabiting free-to-play areas, but have been increasingly more widespread in modern updates. They can be dangerous to low level players training Mining.

Scorpions are a relatively weak variety of monster, with the strongest being located in the Stronghold of Security (level 59).
