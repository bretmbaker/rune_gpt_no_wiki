Lesser demons are the second weakest standard demon, above the imp. They are sometimes killed by Free-to-play players, as they are one of the strongest monsters on those servers and can drop the desirable rune med helm. Lesser demons have a somewhat low Defence, which can make them a good source of combat experience. They are susceptible to the effects of demonbane weapons.

The Chasm of Fire is one of the best locations for slaying lesser demons. The fairy ring (DJR) is very close, and a Dwarf Multicannon can be used to speed up the task; players can also safespot in this location. Although combat experience per hour can be above average, lesser demons' sparse drops make them a below average Slayer task.

A unique Lesser demon can be found in the basement of Melzar's Maze, and is fought during the Dragon Slayer I quest.
