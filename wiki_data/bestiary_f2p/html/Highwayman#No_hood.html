Highwaymen are a level five NPC which are aggressive towards players below a combat level of ten. One will always exclaim Stand and deliver! when they target a player. Players with a Combat level below 10 are advised to quickly run past when encountering them.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Even though the hooded highwayman appears to have a slash attack style, and his attack makes a slashing sound, his attacks are still counted as stab attacks. This means the player's stab defence affects the chance of getting hit.
