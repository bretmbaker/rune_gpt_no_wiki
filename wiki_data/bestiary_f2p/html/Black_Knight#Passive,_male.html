Black knights, also known as the Kinshra, are Zamorakian warriors found mainly in their fortress and stronghold. They are central to the Black Knights' Fortress quest.

Once Wanted! has been completed, black knights can be slain in order to increase the player's White Knight rank. As their rank gets higher, they unlock more white equipment. Even though the quest, as well as white equipment, is only for members, non-members are still able to increase their rank.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Black knights are some of the oldest monsters in RuneScape. When the game was first released, they were the strongest foes at level 46, and only three of them existed.
  • Killing the black knight in the Port Sarim jail does not contribute to the player's White Knight rank.
  • While black knights appear to wear black equipment, they are incapable of dropping it.
