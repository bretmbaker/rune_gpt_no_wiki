Moss giants are large giants, found in areas that are tied to nature.

Moss giants are very commonly trained on, since their Defence level is rather low in comparison to other monsters of the same combat level, and they are vulnerable to Fire spells.

They can drop the mossy key, which is required to fight the moss giant boss Bryophyta.

Note that the level 84 Moss Giants in Iorwerth Dungeon have a magic attack that is not protected by Protect from Magic.
