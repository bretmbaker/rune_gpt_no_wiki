Ogress Shamans are found in the Corsair Cove Dungeon.

These ogresses only utilise magical attacks. They share a similar drop table to Ogress Warriors.

Ogress Shamans can only reach 4 squares away with their magic attack, which means you can safespot them with Ranged or Magic as long as you are 5 or more squares away from them.
