Big frogs are monsters found in the Lumbridge Swamp, the Lumbridge Caves, the Dorgesh-Kaan South Dungeon and the Lizardman Settlement. They play a minor role in the Rag and Bone Man I quest, as their bones are on the Odd Old Man's "wish list".

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The lower level big frogs were released for free-to-play players with the permanent free-to-play update to help provide more choices of training for the huge amounts of new players.
