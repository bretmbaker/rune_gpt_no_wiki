Seagulls are small birds that can be found on various docks in Gielinor. Their low max hit and negative defensive stats make them a prime target for beginners.

Seagulls are also a good way to start training Prayer, as their only drop of bones can be buried later. A unique variant can be found at the Icyene Graveyard which also drops one feather.
