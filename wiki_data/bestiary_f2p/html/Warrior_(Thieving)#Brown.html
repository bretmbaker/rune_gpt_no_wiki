Warriors are human soldiers in the employ of certain cities of Gielinor. They can be found patrolling the streets of East Ardougne, but also maintain a presence in the north-western wing of the Varrock Palace, as well as the outer walls of the Ardougne Castle. A group of four warriors can also be encountered on the outskirts of the city, in a camp just north-west of the castle.

In terms of level and stats, warriors are very similar to guards, yet their lack of drops make them an uncommon target for most players.

However, warriors are an infrequently used alternative to thieving from stalls at lower levels. They may be pickpocketed at level 25 Thieving, giving 18 coins and 26 Thieving experience, while hitting the player for 2 damage if they are caught in the act. Warriors that keep watch over the Ardougne Market are quite convenient to rob, as any damage they may deal in retaliation can be redressed by stealing from the Ardougne Baker's Stall instead.

As with guards, knights, paladins, and heroes, warriors can be lured into buildings in order to pick their pockets with greater ease.
