Reinforced goggles are a piece of Slayer equipment worn in the head slot. The goggles (or a slayer helmet) must be worn when fighting sourhogs.

One pair is obtained for free during A Porcine of Interest, but additional pairs can be purchased after the quest from any Slayer Master for 100 coins.
