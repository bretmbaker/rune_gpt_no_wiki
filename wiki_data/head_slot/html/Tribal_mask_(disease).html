Disease tribal masks are obtained by killing pale-yellow-skinned broodoo victims around Tai Bwo Wannai village while playing the Tai Bwo Wannai Cleanup minigame.

While the mask is purely cosmetic, players should note that if they attempt to speak with any of the villagers in Tai Bwo Wannai, they will refuse to talk them, afraid that the player is coming to kill them. This will prevent the player from interacting with them until they take the mask off.

They can be made into an disease broodoo shield at level 35 Crafting by using a hammer on the mask, while having 8 nails of any kind and two snakeskin in the inventory.
