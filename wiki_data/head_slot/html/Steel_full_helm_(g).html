Steel full helm (g) is a part of steel gold-trimmed armour and a reward from easy Treasure Trails. It has the same stats as a normal steel full helm, and requires level 5 Defence to wear. This item cannot be made via the Smithing skill.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room, as part of the steel gold-trimmed armour. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
