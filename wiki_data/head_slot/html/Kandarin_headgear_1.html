The Kandarin headgear 1 is a reward from completing the easy Kandarin Diary tasks given by The 'Wedge' outside of Camelot castle, and can be retrieved from him for free if lost. Its stats are similar to an iron med helm, except for a -1 Ranged attack bonus and a significantly lower weight.

Benefits[edit | edit source]

When worn
  • Doubled regular logs when cutting normal trees (no additional experience is given)
When worn or in the player's inventory
  • Functions as a light source

A number of other benefits are always active upon claiming rewards from the easy tier of the Kandarin diary.
