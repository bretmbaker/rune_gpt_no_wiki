The red bandana eyepatch is a piece of headwear that is made by taking a red pirate bandana and a right eye patch to Patchy on Mos Le'Harmless, and paying him 500 coins to sew them together. He can also remove them from each other for 600 coins.

When asked why it costs more to take the items apart, he will state that it is due to "wear and tear on scissors".
