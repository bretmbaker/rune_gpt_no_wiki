The Void melee helm (or) is a Void melee helm with a Shattered relics void ornament kit added onto it. The kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the helm.

Whilst in its ornamented state, the helm can be reverted anytime, returning the Void melee helm and kit.

A trouver parchment can be brought to Perdu alongside the item to "lock" it, preventing it from being lost in the Wilderness for one death.
