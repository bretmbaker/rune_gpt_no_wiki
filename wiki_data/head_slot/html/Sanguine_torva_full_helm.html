The sanguine torva full helm is a Torva full helm modified by an ancient blood ornament kit and 20,000 blood runes; the kit is not consumed. The ornament gives no additional bonuses, and is purely cosmetic.

While the item is untradeable in its sanguine state, it can be reverted anytime. However, the blood runes will not be returned.
