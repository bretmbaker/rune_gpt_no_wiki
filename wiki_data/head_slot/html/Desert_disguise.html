The desert disguise is used in The Feud quest. It can be made by combining a fake beard and a Kharidian headpiece, both of which can be bought from Ali Morrisane's stall just north of Al Kharid. Upon completing the quest, the disguise can still be created and the materials will still be obtainable.

Wearing the disguise will delay the onset of desert heat by 12 seconds.
