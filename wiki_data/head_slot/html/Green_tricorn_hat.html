A green tricorn hat can be purchased from Honest Jimmy for 500 Pieces of eight. It is for decoration only, and has no combat bonuses. It cannot be sold back to the shop.

Tricorn hats can also be stored in the Player-owned house Costume room along with a Naval shirt and Navy slacks of the same colour, as part of the green naval set.
