The red pumpkin is an event item unlocked by completing the 2024 Halloween event, after which it can be obtained from Diango or retrieved from a toy box built in the costume room.

The pumpkin is one of 63 variants of the carved pumpkin head, spanning seven colours and nine carvings each. The colour and carvings can be changed at any time by right-clicking the "Colour" or "Carve" options on the pumpkin head. When switching the colour and carvings, the item is renamed to reflect the chosen options.
