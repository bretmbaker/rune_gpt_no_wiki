The imbued saradomin max hood is a hood created by using an imbued saradomin cape on a max cape. When the imbued saradomin max cape is created, the max hood that is required to be in the player's inventory during the cape's creation is automatically converted to the imbued saradomin max hood.

If players are refunding the imbued saradomin max cape via Mac, the player must also have the imbued saradomin max hood in their inventory.

Should players lose the hood, they may reclaim it from Mac for free.
