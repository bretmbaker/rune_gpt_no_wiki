The adamant med helm is a medium helmet made of adamantite. This helm requires 30 Defence to wear. It provides more protection than its mithril counterpart. It can be made at level 73 Smithing using an adamantite bar, granting 62.5 experience.

Though the helmet provides a smaller Defence bonus than that of an adamant full helm, it has a lower penalty to Magic and Ranged attack. This means it is potentially more useful than the full helm to players who are training the Magic skill.
