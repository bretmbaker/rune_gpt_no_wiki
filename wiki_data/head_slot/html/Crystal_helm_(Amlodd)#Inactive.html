The Amlodd crystal helm is a cosmetic variant of the crystal helm made by using the crystal of Amlodd on any variant of the helm.

The helm's colours, based on that of the Amlodd Clan, may be changed to other colours of the Elven Clans by buying crystals from Lliann's Wares in Prifddinas, which cost 500,000 coins each.
