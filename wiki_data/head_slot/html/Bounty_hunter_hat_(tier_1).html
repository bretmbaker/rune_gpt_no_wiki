The tier 1 Bounty Hunter hat is a cosmetic hat that can be claimed from Corrupted Warriors in Ferox Enclave or Daimon's Crater, once players obtain one target kill in the Bounty Hunter minigame.

It can be stored in a magic wardrobe in a player-owned house.
