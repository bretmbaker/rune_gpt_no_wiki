A spiny helmet is a helmet designed to protect the player from the attacks of wall beasts, located in the Lumbridge Swamp Caves. It requires 5 Defence to equip and has the same stats as a steel full helm.

It can be bought from any Slayer Master for 650 coins. This is one of the six items used to craft a Slayer helmet.
