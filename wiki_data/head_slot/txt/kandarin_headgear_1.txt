[Weapon: Kandarin Headgear 1]


TheKandarin headgear 1is a reward from completing the easyKandarin Diarytasks given byThe 'Wedge'outside ofCamelotcastle, and can be retrieved from him for free if lost. Its stats are similar to aniron med helm, except for a -1Rangedattack bonus and a significantly lower weight.
A number of other benefitsare always active upon claiming rewards from the easy tier of the Kandarin diary.
