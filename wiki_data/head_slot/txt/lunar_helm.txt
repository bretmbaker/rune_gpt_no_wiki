[Weapon: Lunar Helm]


Thelunar helmis a piece oflunar equipmentworn in thehead slot. 65Magicand 40Defenceare required to wear the lunar helm. It is statistically similar to asplitbark helm. The lunar helm is created during theLunar Diplomacyquest by using alunar baron ananvilwhile the player has ahammer, granting 25Smithingexperience.
A lunar bar is created by using alunar oreon a furnace, lunar ores can be mined from stalagmites found in the cave at the north-east end of theLunar Isle. The lunar helm may also be bought for15,000 coinsfrom theOneiromancerafter a player has completed the Lunar Diplomacy quest if the player has lost their helm. The lunar helm is the only piece of lunar equipment that can be remade after the quest. It can be stored in amagic wardrobein a player-owned house.
