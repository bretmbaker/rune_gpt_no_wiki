[Weapon: Witch Hat]


Thewitch hatis a cosmetic reward from the2022 Halloween event. It is part of thewitch costumeset, and can be obtained as a reward from the Halloween events that occur once per year. Notably, wearing the hat will change the player's nose to that of a stereotypical witch.
If destroyed, a replacement can be obtained fromDiangoinDraynor Villageor from thetoy boxof acostume room, as part of the2022: Witch Costumeset.Ultimate Ironmenwill be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
