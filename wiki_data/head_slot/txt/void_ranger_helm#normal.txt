[Weapon: Void Ranger Helm#Normal]


TheVoid ranger helmis a piece ofVoid Knight equipmentobtained as a reward from thePest Controlminigame. It can be bought from aVoid Knightfor 200commendation points; players also must have at least 42Attack,Strength,Defence,Hitpoints,Ranged,Magicand 22Prayerto buy and wear one.
If worn alongside the other three equipment pieces (Void knight top,Void knight robeandVoid knight gloves), the Void ranger helm grants the player a 10% boost to Ranged damage (12.5% withElite equipment) and a 10% boost to Ranged accuracy. TheVoid knight maceisnotrequired for the bonus.
