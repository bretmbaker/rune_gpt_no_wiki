Ahrim's hoodismagic armourworn in theheadwear slotthat is part ofAhrim the Blighted's setofbarrows equipment. To wear Ahrim's hood, a player must have 70Defenceand 70Magic.
Players may opt to use afarseer helminstead of Ahrim's hood, unless the set effect or damage bonus is desired. Compared to the farseer helm, Ahrim's hood has higher level requirements to wear but no quest requirements, and provides +1% Magic damage bonus. However, the hood provides an identical magic attack bonus and incurs repair costs.
