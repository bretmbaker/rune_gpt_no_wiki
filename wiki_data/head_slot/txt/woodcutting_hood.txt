[Weapon: Woodcutting Hood]


TheWoodcutting hoodis the skillcape hood associated with having achievedskill masteryin theWoodcuttingskill. Players may buy the hood alongside thewoodcutting capefrom theWoodsman tutorinLumbridgefor 99,000coins, provided that they have level 99 Woodcutting. The hood itself provides absolutely no offensive or defensive bonuses and may be worn for purely aesthetic purposes.
While the hood is bought with the skillcape, it is not needed to perform theemoteassociated with it; the skillcape is the only essential item.
