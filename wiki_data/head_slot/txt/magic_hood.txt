[Weapon: Magic Hood]


The Magic Hood is the hood that accompanies theMagic Skillcapeas one of the Skillcapes for reaching level 99, and can be purchased alongside theSkillcapefromWizard Sininafound in theWizards' GuildinYanillefor 99,000coins. The hood itself provides absolutely no offensive or defensive bonuses and may be worn for purely aesthetic purposes.
While the hood is bought with the skillcape, it is not needed to perform theemoteassociated with it; the skillcape is the only essential item. Along with the cape, the hood may be stored in theCape rackof aplayer-owned house.
