[Weapon: Chef%27S Hat]


Achef's hatis a white hat that grants players access to theCooks' Guildwith aCookinglevel of 32. Aside from this, it has little practical use and is purely cosmetic.
The hat is dropped by several monsters, often to fill space in their drop tables.
