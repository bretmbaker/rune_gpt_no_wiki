[Weapon: Shayzien Helm (5)]


TheShayzien helm (5)is a helmet that requires 20Defenceto equip, and is part of theShayzien armour (tier 5)set. It is dropped by thetier 5 soldierat theCombat Ringin theShayzien Encampment. It can also be created at an anvil with 89Smithingand 2lovakite bars.
Upon completion of thehard Kourend & Kebos Diary, players can speak toCaptain Cleiveto teach them how to use aslayer helmetin place of a Shayzien helm when fightinglizardman shamans. After completion of theKourend and Kebos Hard Diarywhere the helmets are no longer as useful in combat, the Shayzien helms can be stored in anArmour case.
