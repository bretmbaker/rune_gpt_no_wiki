[Weapon: Eclipse Moon Helm#New]


Theeclipse moon helmis an item that requires level 75Rangedand 50Defenceto equip, which is obtained as a possible reward from theLunar ChestinNeypotzli.
As with allMoon equipmentit is degradeable, much likeBarrows equipment. The armour starts at 3000 charges and loses 1 charge for every 54 seconds in combat, meaning it lasts for 45 hours of continuous combat before breaking. Having an NPC such asBobandDunstanrepair it costs 1,500,000 coins when fully broken, while repairing it yourself at anarmour standadds a discount of 7,500 coins perSmithinglevel, reducing the price to 757,500 coins at level 99.
