[Weapon: Dark Infinity Hat]


Thedark infinity hatis aninfinity hatwith adark infinity colour kitattached to it. The attached colour kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the hat. It costs1,647,340for both the parts to make the dark infinity hat. It can be stored in amagic wardrobein a player-owned house.
While the hat is untradeable in its recoloured state, it can be dismantled anytime, returning the tradeable hat and ornament kit.
