[Weapon: Adamant Med Helm]


Theadamant med helmis amedium helmetmade ofadamantite. This helm requires 30Defenceto wear. It provides more protection than itsmithrilcounterpart. It can be made at level 73Smithingusing anadamantite bar, granting 62.5experience.
Though the helmet provides a smaller Defence bonus than that of anadamant full helm, it has a lower penalty toMagicandRangedattack. This means it is potentially more useful than the full helm to players who are training the Magic skill.
