[Weapon: Santa Hat]


Santa hatsare tradeable cosmetic items obtained by completing one of theChristmasholiday eventsthat occur once per year.
Santa hats were first obtainable on2 August2013, in theFalador Party Room. There were multiple ways of obtaining them there: either by depositingcoins,Easter eggsorpumpkinsinto the chest, then pulling the lever. The maximum amount of Santa hats that would ever spawn at once was 5.
