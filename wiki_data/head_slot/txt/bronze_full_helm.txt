[Weapon: Bronze Full Helm]


Thebronze full helmetis the weakestfull helmetinOld School RuneScape. The bronze full helmet grants greater defensive bonuses than itsmedium helmet counterpart, though at a cost of offensive bonuses forRangedandMagic. There are noDefencerequirements for wearing this helmet.
To create a bronze full helmet the player needs at least 7 Smithing, ahammerand 2bronze bars. To make a helmet use one of the bars on ananvilwith the hammer in the inventory. This grants 25 Smithing experience.
