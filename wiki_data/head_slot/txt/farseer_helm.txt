[Weapon: Farseer Helm]


Thefarseer helmis a piece ofFremennik armour. To wear a farseer helm, players must have completedThe Fremennik Trialsquest and have aDefencelevel of at least 45. To obtain this helmet you can buy one fromSkulgrimeninRellekkafor 78,000 coins, trade with another player or receive one as a drop fromDagannoth. It can also be obtained through gambling points in theBarbarian Assaultminigame.
