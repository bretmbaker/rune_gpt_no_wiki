[Weapon: Mystic Hat (Dark)]


Amystic hat (dark)is a component of the darkmystic robes setthat is worn in thehead slot. It comes in blue, light, and dark. Each may differ in price and aesthetics, but they all offer the same bonuses. The mystic hat require 40Magicand 20Defenceto wear. It can be stored in amagic wardrobein a player-owned house.
The dark mystic hat is a drop frominfernalandmalevolent mages.
