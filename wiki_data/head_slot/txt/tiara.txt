Atiarais an item that can be created at afurnacefrom asilver baralong with atiara mouldand level 23Crafting, granting 52.5 Crafting experience.
They are a purely cosmetic head slot item, though they can be turned into a talisman tiara, used in theRunecraftskill, acting as an equipabletalisman(head slot), saving an inventory space over a normal talisman. It also allows the player to enter a particularrunic altarby left-clicking on the corresponding mysterious ruins, saving time.
