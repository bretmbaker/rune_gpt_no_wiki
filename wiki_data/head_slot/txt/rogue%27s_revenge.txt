[Weapon: Rogue%27S Revenge]


Therogue's revengeis a cosmetic hat that can be claimed fromCorrupted WarriorsinFerox EnclaveorDaimon's Crater, once players has more non target kills than target kills, and at least 1 target kill.
The hat alters its appearance depending on the amount of target kills (assigned target kills in theBounty Hunterminigame) and rogue kills (non-target kills on Bounty Hunter worlds) the player has; theHunter's Honouris chosen if the player has more target kills than rogue kills, and the Rogue's Revenge is chosen if the player has more rogue kills than target kills.
