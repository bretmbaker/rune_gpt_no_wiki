[Weapon: Zealot%27S Helm]


Thezealot's helmis a piece of theZealot's robesset that can be obtained from looting gold chests within theShade Catacombs.
The helmet grants a 1.25% chance to prevent various remains from being consumed whilst trainingPrayer; wearing the full set will increase this chance to 5%, and stacks with the bone-saving effect of the altar in theChaos Temple. However, this does not apply at theForthos Dungeon'sSacred Bone Burner, nor when castingSinister Offering.
