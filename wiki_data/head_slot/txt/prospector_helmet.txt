[Weapon: Prospector Helmet]


Theprospector helmetis a helmet acquired from theMotherlode Minein exchange for 40golden nuggets, or from theVolcanic Minefor 26,000 points. The helmet grants 0.4% bonusMiningexperience. This is increased to 2.5% when wearing the fullProspector kit. Despite having two lights attached to it, the Prospector helmet doesnotact as a light source.
Wearing the prospector helmet while entering theMining Guildis a task in the hardFalador Diary.
