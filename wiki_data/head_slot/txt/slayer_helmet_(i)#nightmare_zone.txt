[Weapon: Slayer Helmet (I)#Nightmare Zone]


TheSlayer helmet (i), requiring aDefencelevel of 10 to wear, is a piece of equipment that is worn in the helmet slot. It provides the bonuses of multiple pieces of protective and beneficialSlayerequipment. It functions in the same manner as itsunimbued variant(with the 16.67%Meleebonus), except that it also provides an extra 15% boost toRangedaccuracyandRanged damage, and a 15% boost toMagicaccuracyandMagic damage, against monsters assigned as the player's Slayer task.
It has defensive bonuses equivalent to therune full helm, with the exception of providing +11 higher relative magic defence (+10 vs. -1) as well as positiveMagicandRangedattack bonuses. It will prevent infection fromNex's virus attack, and can be used aswarm clothingforWintertodt.
