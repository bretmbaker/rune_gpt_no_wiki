[Weapon: Black Beret]


Theblack beretis a hat that can be obtained fromEasyTreasure Trails. They provide no stat bonuses and are worn purely for cosmetic purposes. There's other variants: thewhite beret,blue beretandred beret.
Black berets can be attached to amime maskto make aberet maskby talking toPatchyonMos Le'Harmless, along with 500coins. Doing so requires the completion ofCabin Feverand aBook o' piracy.
