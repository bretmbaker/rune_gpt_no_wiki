[Weapon: Kandarin Headgear 4]


Kandarin headgear 4is a reward obtained after completing the eliteKandarin Diaryand speaking toThe 'Wedge'inCamelot. It has similar stats to amithril med helm, except that it weighs significantly less and has a -1Rangedattack bonus.
A number of other benefitsare always active upon claiming rewards from the elite tier of the Kandarin diary.
