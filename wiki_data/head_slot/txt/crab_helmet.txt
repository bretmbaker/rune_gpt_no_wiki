[Weapon: Crab Helmet]


Acrab helmetis a piece ofarmour. There are no level requirements to wear the crab helmet. The crab helmet has similar stats to theiron med helm, except the slash defence and stab defence stats are switched, and it has negative ranged attack bonus.
Crafting this helmet has a chance to fail producing abroken crab shell.
