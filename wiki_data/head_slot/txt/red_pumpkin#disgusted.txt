[Weapon: Red Pumpkin#Disgusted]


Thered pumpkinis anevent itemunlocked by completing the2024 Halloween event, after which it can be obtained fromDiangoor retrieved from atoy boxbuilt in thecostume room.
The pumpkin is one of 63 variants of thecarved pumpkin head, spanning seven colours and nine carvings each. The colour and carvings can be changed at any time by right-clicking the "Colour" or "Carve" options on the pumpkin head. When switching the colour and carvings, the item is renamed to reflect the chosen options.
