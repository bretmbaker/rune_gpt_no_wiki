[Weapon: Gnome Goggles]


Gnome gogglesare a possible reward from theGnome Restaurantminigame. Players have a 1/17 chance to obtain them as a reward fromCaptain DaerkinorCaptain Ninto, which are accessible with hard deliveries.[1]The gnome goggles are part of the gnome pilot set which also includes thegnome scarf.
Goggles can be combined with theBomber capfrom theEnlightened Journeyquest by talking withAuguste, resulting in aCap and goggles. As the cap and goggles can be stored, though not the goggles themselves, this provides a means of storing the goggles in thearmour case(as part of thebomber jacket costume).
