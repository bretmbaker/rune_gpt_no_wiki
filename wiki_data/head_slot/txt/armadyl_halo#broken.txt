[Weapon: Armadyl Halo#Broken]


TheArmadyl halois an item worn in the head slot that can be bought fromJustine's stuff for the Last Shopper Standingfor 450Last Man Standingpoints. It acts as anArmadylitem, and thus prevents Armadylean followers from attacking its wearer in theGod Wars Dungeon.
The defensive bonuses of the halo are similar to those of ablack full helm, excluding negative attack bonuses and Ranged defence bonus. In addition, the halo gives aprayer bonusand magic defence while the full helm does not.
