[Weapon: Nose Peg]


Thenose pegis a piece ofSlayer equipment, requiring level 60Slayerto wear, making it the second highest level slayer item. It can be bought from anySlayer Master. It is one of the items used to craft aSlayer helmet, which also has its nose protection ability.
When worn, the peg protects the player from the special attack of theaberrant spectrewhich, if not equipped, will cause a rapid and significant reduction in the player'sstatsandhitpoints.
