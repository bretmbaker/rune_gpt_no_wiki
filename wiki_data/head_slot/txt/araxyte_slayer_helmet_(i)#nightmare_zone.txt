[Weapon: Araxyte Slayer Helmet (I)#Nightmare Zone]


Thearaxyte slayer helmet (i)is a piece of equipment that is worn in the helmet slot. It is a cosmetic upgrade to theSlayer helmet (i). It is required to be worn in order to proceed through the steps to recolour theAmulet of rancourto itssilver variant.
It can be created by adding anaraxyte headafter purchasing the abilityEye see youfor 1,000Slayer reward pointsfrom anySlayer master. This is reversible, and the head will be returned to the inventory along with the slayer helmet's components. The head will be lost on death in PvP.
