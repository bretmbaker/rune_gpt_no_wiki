[Weapon: Guthix Full Helm]


TheGuthix full helmis a decoratedrune full helmthat is affiliated with the godGuthix. Like all god affiliated rune items, its bonuses are the same as its rune counterpart, but also offers a +1Prayer bonus. Visually, the only difference is the colour of the plume which is green for the Guthix full helm and purple for the rune full helm.
The full helm requires 40Defenceto wear andcannotbe made using theSmithingskill. Players can obtain a Guthix full helm as a reward from completinghardTreasure Trails.
