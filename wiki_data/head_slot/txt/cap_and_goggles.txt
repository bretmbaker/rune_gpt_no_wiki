[Weapon: Cap And Goggles]


Cap and gogglesare a combination ofgnome goggles, obtained duringGnome Restaurant, and thebomber capreward from theEnlightened Journeyquest. The items can be combined by talking withAugusteon the island ofEntrana. To separate the combined cap and goggles, right click and choose "split".
If you die with this item, the goggles will disappear.
