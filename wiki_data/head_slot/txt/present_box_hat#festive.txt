[Weapon: Present Box Hat#Festive]


Thepresent box hatis a cosmetic reward from the2024 Christmas event. It can be obtained as a reward from the Christmas events that occur once per year. Its appearance can be freely changed between the four different styles by using Change-option from inventory or while worn prompting aYou change the style of your Present box hat.message.
