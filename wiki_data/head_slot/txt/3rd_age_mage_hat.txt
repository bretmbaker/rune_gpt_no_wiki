[Weapon: 3Rd Age Mage Hat]


The3rd age mage hatis a piece of the3rd age mage setworn in thehead slot. Requiring level 65Magicand 30Defenceto wear, it has the highest Magic attack bonus of all mage hats, tied withVirtus maskandancestral hat. It has aMagic damagebonus of 1%.
The 3rd age mage hat is a possible reward fromhard,eliteandmastertierTreasure Trailsand cannot be made through any skills.
