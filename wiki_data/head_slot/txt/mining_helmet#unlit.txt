[Weapon: Mining Helmet#Unlit]


Amining helmetis an item primarily dropped byCave goblin miners. It is dropped unlit, but can be lit by players who have aFiremakinglevel of 65. It can also be obtained by showing thebroochtoMistagafter completingThe Lost Tribequest, or purchased fromMiltog's Lampsin theDorgesh-Kaanmarket place. It has the same stats as an iron med helm.
This is one of sixlight sourcesthat can be equipped; the others being theKandarin headgear, theFiremaking cape, themax cape, theAbyssal Lantern, and theBruma torch.
