[Weapon: Cooking Hood]


TheCooking hoodis the skillcape hood associated with having achievedskill masteryin theCooking skill. Players can buy the hood alongside theCooking skillcapefrom theHead chefin theCooks' Guildif they have a Cooking level of 99 for 99,000 coins. The hood itself provides absolutely no offensive or defensive bonuses and may be worn for purely aesthetic purposes. Similar to the cape, it can be worn to enter theCooks' Guild.
While the hood is bought with the skillcape, it is not needed to perform theemoteassociated with it; the skillcape is the only essential item.
