[Weapon: 3Rd Age Range Coif]


The3rd age range coifis a part of the3rd age range set. Requiring level 65Rangedand 45Defenceto wear, it has the third highest ranged attack bonus of all head-slot items, surpassed by theMasori maskandArmadyl helmet.
The 3rd age range coif is a possible reward fromhard,eliteandmastertierTreasure Trailsand cannot be made through any skills.
