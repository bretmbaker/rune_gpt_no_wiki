[Weapon: Saradomin Full Helm]


TheSaradomin full helmis a decoratedrune full helmthat is affiliated with the godSaradomin. Sharing the same stats as the rune full helm with a +1prayer bonus, the Saradomin full helm also looks visually similar. Its appearance differs in that the helm features an ornamental white trim around the symbol of Saradomin. The full helm requires 40Defenceto wear andcannotbe made using theSmithingskill. Players can obtain a Saradomin full helm as a reward from completinghardTreasure Trails.
Wearing any piece of this armour set will causefollowers of Saradominto become unaggressive upon entering theGod Wars Dungeon.
