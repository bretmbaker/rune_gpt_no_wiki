[Weapon: Facemask]


Afacemaskis aSlayeritem worn on the headgear armour slot, requiring level 10Slayerto wear, which cannot be boosted. The facemask can be bought from anySlayer Masterfor 200 coins. There is a facemask spawn inside a locked cage on the top floor ofKourend Castle, however the only way to pick it up is withtelegrab.
It must be worn when fightingdust devilsandsmoke devils, otherwise they will dramatically decrease players' stats.
