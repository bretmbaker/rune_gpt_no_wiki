[Weapon: Turquoise Slayer Helmet (I)#Soul Wars]


Theturquoise Slayer helmet (i)is a piece of equipment that is worn in the helmet slot. It is a cosmetic upgrade to theSlayer helmet (i).
It can be created by addingVorkath's headafter purchasing the abilityUndead Headfor 1,000Slayer reward pointsfrom anySlayer master. This is reversible, and the head will be returned to the inventory along with the slayer helmet's components. The head will be lost on death in PvP.
