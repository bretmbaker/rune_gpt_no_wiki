[Weapon: Kandarin Headgear 2]


TheKandarin headgear 2is a reward from completing themedium Kandarin Diarytasks given byThe 'Wedge'outside ofCamelotcastle, and can be retrieved from him for free if lost. Its stats are similar to that of asteel med helm, except for a -1Rangedattack bonus and a significantly lower weight. The headgear does not need to be worn in order to gain moremarks of grace, gain more experience frommaple trees, or exchangeflax.
A number of other benefitsare always active upon claiming rewards from the medium tier of the Kandarin diary.
