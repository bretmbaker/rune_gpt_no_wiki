[Weapon: Achievement Diary Hood]


Theachievement diary hoodis the skillcape hood associated with having completed allAchievement Diaries. Players may buy the hood alongside theAchievement diary cape (t)from theTwiggy O'KorninDraynorfor 99,000coins. The hood itself provides absolutely no offensive or defensive bonuses and may be worn for purely aesthetic purposes.
While the hood is bought with the skillcape, it is not needed to perform theemoteassociated with it; the skillcape is the only essential item.
