[Weapon: Helmet Of The Moon]


Thehelmet of the moonis an item obtainable as a rare drop from themoon chest. It is identical to the helmet worn byChosen Aluinus, and wearing it whilst investigating the greencampfireatShipwreck Covewill result in unique dialogue from them.
The helmet's melee defence stats are the same as aniron full helm, but with improved magic attack and defence, akin to thegnome hatsand their equivalents. Additionally, the +3 prayer bonus puts it in line withhalos. Offensively, its stats are slightly inferior to amitrebut without prayer and magic requirements.
