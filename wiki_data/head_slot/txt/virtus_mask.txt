[Weapon: Virtus Mask]


TheVirtus maskis a part of theVirtus robesset that is worn in thehead slot, requiring level 78 inMagicand 75Defenceto equip. It can be obtained as a rare drop fromThe Leviathan,The Whisperer,Vardorvis, andDuke Sucellus.
Each piece of Virtus robes gives a 2%magic damagebonus for a total of 6%. When using combat spells fromAncient Magicks, the magic damage bonus is increased to 5% for each piece for a total of 15%.
