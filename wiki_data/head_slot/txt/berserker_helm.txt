[Weapon: Berserker Helm]


Theberserker helmis a mid-levelled melee-based helmet. Players must have level 45Defenceand also have completed the questThe Fremennik Trialsin order to wear it. It can be purchased fromSkulgrimen's Battle Gearshop inRellekkafor 78,000 coins. It's also a possible reward fromBarbarian AssaultHigh Gamblesor a drop from a Dagannoth.
This helmet is favoured amongcombat puresfor being the second lowest level headgear to offer aStrengthbonus, which offers the same strength bonus as ahelm of Neitiznotand anObsidian helmet.
