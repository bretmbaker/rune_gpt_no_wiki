[Weapon: Bunny Ears]


Bunny earsare an untradeable cosmetic holiday item worn on the head. A pair of bunny ears can be obtained by completing one of theEasterholiday eventsthat occur once per year. This also rewards the player with all other holiday items associated with Easter.
Previously, they were obtainable during the2013 Rare Dropsevent (from14 Julyto21 July) as part of arare item drop.
