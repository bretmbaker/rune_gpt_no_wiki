[Weapon: Catalytic Tiara]


Acatalytic tiarais ahead slotitem used inRunecraft. It is created at anycatalytic runic altar(except for theAstral Altar,Soul AltarorKourend's Blood Altar) by using agold tiaraorcatalytic talismanon the altar with both in your inventory. This consumes the catalytic talisman and yields 60 Runecraftexperience. As with all other tiaras, catalytic runic altars accessed through Guardians of the Riftcannotbe used to for imbuement.
The Catalytic runes are: Cosmic runes, Mind runes, Body runes, Chaos runes, Nature runes, Law runes, Death runes, Blood runes, Soul runes and Wrath runes.
