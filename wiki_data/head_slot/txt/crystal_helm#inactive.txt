Acrystal helmis a piece ofcrystal armourrequiring level 70Defenceand 50Agilityto equip. When equipped, a 2.5% damage bonus and 5% accuracy bonus is applied when using thecrystal boworBow of Faerdhinen. This bonus stacks additively with thecrystal legsandcrystal body's bonuses, for a total of 15% damage and 30% accuracy.
Players can create a crystal helm via asinging bowlby combining 50crystal shardsand acrystal armour seed. Doing so requires level 70SmithingandCraftingtosingthe crystal (the levels can beboosted), and grants 2,500 experience in both skills. Players can configure the charges while the helm is in their bank by right-clicking on Configure-Charges, as long as players have the required materials. If the player does not meet the Smithing and Crafting requirements, they can haveConwennaorReesesing the crystal for them for an extra 60 crystal shards, for a total of 110.
