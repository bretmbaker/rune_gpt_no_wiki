[Weapon: Initiate Sallet]


Theinitiate salletis a piece ofTemple Knight Armourthat requires 20Defence, 10Prayer, and completion of theRecruitment Drivequest to equip. It provides a smallprayer bonusthat matches that of ahelm of Neitiznot, while retaining the defensive bonuses of amithril full helm, and is commonly used duringSlayer tasksthat require the use ofprotection prayers, as the armour reduces the drain of prayer points.
If lost, another sallet can be bought for 6,000 coins, but is usually much cheaper to buy from theGrand Exchange.
