[Weapon: Rock-Shell Helm]


ARock-shell helmis a members-only melee helmet that requires 40 Defence and the completion ofThe Fremennik Trialsquest to wear. To obtain a rock-shell helmet, a player must bring 1dagannoth hide, 1rock-shell chunk, and 5,000 coins toSkulgrimeninRellekka. Aside from appearance, it is identical to therune full helm.
Jarvaldwears a rock-shell helm and will inform the player on how to obtain one.
