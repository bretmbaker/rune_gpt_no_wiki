[Weapon: Lumberjack Hat]


Thelumberjack hatis a part of thelumberjack clothingset that can be purchased from theForestry Shopfor 1,200anima-infused bark, 200 notedyew logs, and 100 notedmagic logs. These logs can be bought on theGrand Exchangefor158,800. It is also dropped byundead lumberjacksin theTemple Trekkingminigame. It increases experience by 0.4% whenWoodcutting. This is increased to +2.5% when wearing the whole set. It is the only piece of the lumberjack outfit that counts aswarm clothingwhen fighting theWintertodt.[1]
It can be stored in theForestry kitwith aClothes pouch.
