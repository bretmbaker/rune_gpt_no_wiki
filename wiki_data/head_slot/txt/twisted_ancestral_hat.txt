[Weapon: Twisted Ancestral Hat]


Thetwisted ancestral hatis anancestral hatwith atwisted ancestral colour kitattached to it. The attached colour kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the hat.
While the hat is untradeable in its recoloured state, it can be dismantled anytime, returning the ornament kit and tradeable hat. It can be stored in amagic wardrobein a player-owned house.
