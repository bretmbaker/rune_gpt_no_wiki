[Weapon: Hell Cat Ears]


Hell cat earsare a head-slot cosmetic obtained as a reward from the2020 Birthday event. They can be obtained by using the right-click 'chase' option of regularcat earswhile nearHell-Rats.
While the hell cat ears are afree-to-playitem, there is currently no known way to transform the cat ears into hell cat ears on a free-to-playworld.
