[Weapon: Fighter Hat#Normal]


Thefighter hatis a piece ofmembers-onlyarmourthat requires 45Defenceto wear, and is obtained through theBarbarian Assaultminigame. Players with 275honour pointsin each role and who have killed thePenance Queencan purchase the hat fromCommander Connad, located on the ground floor of theBarbarian Outpost.
The fighter hat is sometimes used in place of thewarrior helmandberserker helm. The warrior helm provides slightly better defensive bonuses, but offers an attack bonus toslashonly, while the fighter hat also provides attack bonuses tostabandcrush. In fact, the +5 stab attack bonus makes the fighter hat the best-in-slot item for stab attack. The berserker helm has slightly better defensive bonuses and provides a strength bonus (+3), but provides nomeleeattack bonuses. The fighter hat is a popular choice amongobsidian maulersthat are unable to obtain theInquisitor's great helm.
