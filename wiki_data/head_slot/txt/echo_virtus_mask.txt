[Weapon: Echo Virtus Mask]


Theecho Virtus maskis aVirtus maskwith anecho virtus ornament kitadded onto it. The kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the mask.
Whilst in its ornamented state, the item can be reverted anytime, returning the mask and kit. It can be stored in amagic wardrobein aplayer-owned house.
