[Weapon: Swampbark Helm]


Aswampbark helmis a piece ofswampbark armourworn in theheadwear slot, requiring level 50Magicand 50Defenceto equip. The helm can be made with level 46Runecraftby bringing asplitbark helmand 250nature runesto theNature Altar. Doing so requires players to have first learnt the secret to infuse the armour via reading therunescroll of swampbark.
In addition to their stats, the helm, body, and legs increases the duration of bind spells by 2ticks(1.2 seconds), with the full set increasing the duration by 6 ticks (3.6 seconds). For comparison,Ice Barragebinds players for 32 ticks (19.2 secs).
