[Weapon: Spirit Angler Headband]


TheSpirit angler headbandis an upgraded version of theangler hat. It can be purchased fromGita Prymesby exchanging anangler hatalong with 1,200spirit flakes. The headband grants 0.4% bonusFishingexperience. Tempoross has a skill requirement of 35 Fishing; therefore, the headband requires level 35 Fishing to wear.
Wearing the completeSpirit angler's outfitacts as aropefor tethering tototem polesandmastswhile battlingTempoross, and grants a total of 2.5% bonus fishing experience.
