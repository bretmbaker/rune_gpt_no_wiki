Awintumber treeis a holiday item that can be planted in aplayer-owned house. It can be obtained by completing one of theChristmasholiday eventsthat occur once per year. This also rewards the player with all other holiday items associated with Christmas. The tree is untradeable and may be planted on any free non-hotspot space in theGardenor on any free outdoor space of a player-owned house. If lost or destroyed, the player can obtain another fromDiangoinDraynor Village.
