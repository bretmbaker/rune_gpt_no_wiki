Cooked meatis a type offoodthat restores 3Hitpointswhen eaten. It can be obtained by cooking variousraw meatson afireorcooking range, granting 30experiencewhen successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting inburnt meat. Theburn ratedecreases as the player'sCookinglevel increases. Cooked meat is one of a number of foods with a reduced burn rate while using theLumbridge Castle rangewithCook's Assistantcompleted. It will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 34, or 31 when using the Lumbridge range.
