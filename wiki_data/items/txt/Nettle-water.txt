Nettle-wateris an item created in the process of makingnettle tea. This item is made by addingnettlesto abowl of water. This requires aCookinglevel of 20. Historically, this item gave 1 cooking exp per creation, however this is no longer the case. The player is healed 1 Hitpoint when drank. However, it is not advised to consume this item because it has potential to heal more when made into tea. Nettle-water can be cooked on a range or fire to produce nettle tea. This item is usually only used during theGhosts Ahoyquest and theSkippy and the Mogresminiquest.
