Theoccult necklacefromLast Man Standingis a minigame-exclusive variant of the normaloccult necklace. It can be obtained from opening thechestswith abloody keyorbloodier key, or looting thecratesthat spawn at random locations on the map. It is dropped on death to whoever killed you. Wearing it increases the player'sMagic damageby 10%, and is the only amulet to give such a bonus. This 10% bonus will also stack with existing boosts, such as thestaff of the dead.
