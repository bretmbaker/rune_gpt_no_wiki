Theash sanctifieris a reward from completing theHard Kourend & Kebos Diary, where players can then claim it fromTyssnear theDark Altar. To use it, it must be charged withdeath runes, gaining 10 charges per death rune at a cost of16per charge. Players can configure the charges while the sanctifier is in their bank by right-clicking on Configure-Charges, as long as players have the required materials. Un-charging it will return the unused death runes.
