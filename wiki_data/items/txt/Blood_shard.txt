Theblood shardis a drop fromvyrewatch sentinelsand from pickpocketingvampyresinDarkmeyer. The blood shard can be added to anamulet of fury, making it anamulet of blood fury. This amulet has the same stats as a regular amulet of fury, but grants a 20% chance of healing 30% of the damage dealt to a targetwhen using melee. A blood shard provides the amulet with 10,000 charges, losing one for everysuccessful hit. Once the amulet reaches 0 charges the blood shard is destroyed, but the amulet of fury remains.
