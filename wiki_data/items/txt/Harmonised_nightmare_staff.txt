Theharmonised nightmare staffis astaffthat is created by attaching aharmonised orbto theNightmare staff. Requiring level 82Magicand 50Hitpointsto wield, it can autocast offensivestandard spells, butcannotautocast any other spells (includingAncient Magicksand theArceuus spellbook) unlike its other variants. Like theNightmare staffit gives a 15% bonus inmagic damage. The upgraded staff is untradeable, but it can be reverted to its tradeable components at any time. If lost on death in theWilderness, the killer will receive the staff and the orb.
