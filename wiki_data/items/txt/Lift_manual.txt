Alift manual, orAMCE Lift-In-A-Box User Manual, is obtained during theRoyal Troublequest. It is a guide to using the Lift-In-A-Box system, which is being used by a number ofdwarvesin their efforts to expand the caves beneathMiscellania. The Lift-In-A-Box is a product ofAli Morrisane Commercial Enterprises Ltd., one of the many projects of famed entrepreneurAli Morrisane.
