Abridge sectionis an item used in theTrouble Brewingminigame that will repair bridges damaged by fires. A bridge section can be found on the supply table located in the team's main base. When a round ofTrouble Brewingbegins, the table will have an initial stock of 10 bridge sections. Players can make more by usinglogson the crafting table, which is conveniently located next to the supply table, doing so will reward the player with 12.5 Crafting experience. Logs can be gathered from the log store (also located within your base) or by chopping down the varioustreesaround your base with anaxe.
