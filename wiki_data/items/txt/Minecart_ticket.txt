Aminecart ticketis an item that allows players to ride the Mine carts to and fromKeldagrim. The carts can travel to theDwarven Mine(listed asIce Mountain) and the tunnels underWhite Wolf Mountain, after theFishing Contestquestis completed. It is also possible to use the carts for travel between theGrand Exchangeand Keldagrim, although this service is free and doesn't require a ticket.
