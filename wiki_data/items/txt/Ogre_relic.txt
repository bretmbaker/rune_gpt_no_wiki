Anogre relicis aquest itemthat appears in theWatchtowerquest. During the quest, it is given as a gift to the ogre guards, who stand at the gates ofGu'Tanoth. In return, they grant the player permanent access to the city. It should also be noted from a lore point of view that upon gifting the relic to the guards, they recognise it as a statue of the ogre warrior,Dalgroth.
