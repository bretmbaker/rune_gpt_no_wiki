Asuper magic potionis apotiononly usable in theNightmare Zone, costing 250 points per dose. It boosts one'sMagicstat by 15% + 5. Because of this, it makes spells a great deal more accurate while providing lower levels the ability to use spells far above their Magic level. For example, one with 78 Magic could sip this potion to be able to castIce Barrage. At 99 Magic, this potion will boost one's Magic level to 118. You cannot use this to enchant jewellery.
