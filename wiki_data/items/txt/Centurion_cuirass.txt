TheCenturion cuirasswas a proposed reward for thePvP Arenaminigame that was available from thesuppliestable in atournament worldas a test armour for players. It would have had slightly higher defensive stats compared to theFighter torso, at the cost of only being able to be used inPvPcombat. Like the Fighter torso, it would have required aDefencelevel of 40 to be worn.
