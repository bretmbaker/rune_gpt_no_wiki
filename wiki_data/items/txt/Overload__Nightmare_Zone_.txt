AnOverloadis a potion purchased fromDom Onion's Reward Shopfor 1,500Nightmare Zonepoints per dose. Overloads cannot be used outside theNightmare Zone. Drinking an overload raises a player'sAttack,Strength,Defence,Ranged, andMagiclevels by 5-19 (5 + 15% of the player's level, rounded down) for five minutes, while causing them to take 50hitpointsworth of damage over the course of several seconds. This damage is not absorbed if the player has used anAbsorptionpotion.
