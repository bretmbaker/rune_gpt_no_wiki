Themusic sheetis a quest item used during theGrim Talesquest. It is obtained by searching themusic standin thewitch's housebasement inTaverley, after passing an electrified gate while wearingleather gloves. The sheet shows how to play the piano in a special combination that will open a secret compartment in the piano to reveal ashrinking recipe, ato-do listand 2shrunk ogleroots.
