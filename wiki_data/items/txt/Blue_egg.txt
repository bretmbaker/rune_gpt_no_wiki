Blue eggsare used inBarbarian Assaulttostunpenance monsters. If loaded into anegg launcherand shot atpenancecreatures, a blue egg will stun its target for 6 seconds. During this time, the monster will not move, and cannot be targeted with food, weapons or the egg launcher. A maximum of five blue eggs may be loaded into the egg launcher at any given time. Both egg launchers share the same reserve of eggs.
