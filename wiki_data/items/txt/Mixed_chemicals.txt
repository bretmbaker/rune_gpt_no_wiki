Mixed chemicalsare used in theThe Dig Siteto create thechemical compound. It is made by combiningnitroglycerinwithammonium nitrate, granting 2Herbloreexperience. The chemicals can then be combined withground charcoal, granting 2.5 Herblore experience, to make step 2 of the compound. Finally, it can be combined with anarcenia rootto make thechemical compound, granting 3 Herblore experience. Due to the nitroglycerin, dropping the mixed chemicals will cause an explosion that destroy the item and deals a significant amount of damage to the player. The chemicals deal 45 damage after step 1, or 55 damage after the addition of ground charcoal in step 2, but the damage is not lethal.
