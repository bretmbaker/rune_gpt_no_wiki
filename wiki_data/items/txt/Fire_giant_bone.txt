Thefire giant boneis an item on theOdd Old Man's wish list duringRag and Bone Man II. It is dropped byfire giants. After the quest is completed you can no longer obtain this as a drop. The bone must be put into apot of vinegarand boiled to create apolished fire giant bonebefore giving it to the Odd Old Man. The Fire Giants inside the Slayer Stronghold do not drop the Fire Giant bone.
