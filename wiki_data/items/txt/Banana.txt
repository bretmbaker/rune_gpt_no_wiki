Abananais a type offoodthat restores 2Hitpointswhen eaten. It can be found on trees in tropical locations, such asKaramjaandApe Atoll, or bought from various stores. Six bananas can be picked at a time from  abanana treegrown by planting abanana saplingin a fruit tree patch with 33Farming. They can also be obtained by using the spellBones to Bananas, which convertsbonesof all types in the caster's inventory to bananas.
