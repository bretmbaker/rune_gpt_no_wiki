TheSailing bookis anunobtainable itemreleased alongsideThe Tourist Trapquest. It appears in the chatbox when searching a bookshelf inCaptain Siad's office upstairs in theDesert Mining Camp. After having seen the Sailing book, players can talk to the Captain about his interest in sailing. While telling his many stories about sailing he becomes distracted, allowing players to steal theTechnical plansfrom his chest.
