Ancient shardsare obtained from monsters found within theCatacombs of Kourend(excludingghosts). Three shards can be used with aDarklightat thealtarin the centre of the catacombs to create anArclight, a degradeable variant of the darklight that provides stats superior to theabyssal tentaclewhen used against demons. Ancient shards are also used to charge Arclight. One shard provides 333 charges, three shards provide 1,000 charges; up to a maximum of 10,000.
