Red topaz boltsaresteel boltstipped withred topaz bolt tips. Red topaz bolts can be created through theFletchingskill. A player must first have 48 Fletching and take a cutred topazand use achiselwith it to make 12red topaz bolt tips, granting 3.9 experience. Attaching red topaz bolt tips also requires 48 Fletching, and each red topaz bolt tip attached to a steel bolt gives 3.9 experience. Once the red topaz bolt tips have been attached, the steel bolts become red topaz bolts.
