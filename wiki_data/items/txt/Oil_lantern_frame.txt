Anoil lantern framecan be made bymemberswith level 26Smithingfrom aniron bar, giving 25 Smithingexperience. It is used to make anoil lanternwhich can be made with level 26Craftingby using anoil lampwith the frame then filling the resultingempty oil lanternwithlamp oilat theChemist's west ofRimmington. Oil lanterns can be lit with aTinderboxat level 26Firemaking. They will eventually go out and require more lamp oil.
