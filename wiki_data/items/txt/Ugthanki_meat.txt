Ugthanki meatis a type offoodthat restores 3Hitpointswhen eaten. It can be obtained by cookingraw ugthanki meat, granting 40 experience when successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting inburnt meat. Theburn ratedecreases as the player'sCookinglevel increases. Ugthanki meat is unusual as despite lacking a cooking requirement, it never stops burning altogether even at level 99. At levels up to 82, the chance to burn this meat is higher on arangethan on afire.
