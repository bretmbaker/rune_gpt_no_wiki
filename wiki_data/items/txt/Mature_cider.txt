Mature cideris a type ofalethat restores 2Hitpointsandtemporarily boostsFarmingby 2, whiledrainingAttackandStrengthby 5. It is the stronger version of regularcider, created throughbrewing. Each batch of cider has a chance to produce mature cider. Unlike other ales "The stuff"can notbe added to try to increase the chance of brewing mature cider.
