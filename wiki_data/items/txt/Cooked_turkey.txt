Cooked turkeywas obtained by during the2013 Thanksgiving eventby speaking to the gnomeChef. Players could only possess two cooked turkeys per inventory. They couldn't be banked, when attempting them a message would appear in the game chat sayingA magical force prevents you from banking this item!Consuming them heals two hitpoints and you receive the messageIt heals some health, for which you feel thankful.in the game chat.
