Cooked dashing kebbitis a type ofhunter meatthat restores a total of 23Hitpointswhen eaten, 13 are restored immediately,  10 are restored after a delay of 7ticks(4.2s).  The secondary heal also restores 10 points ofrun energy. Eating another hunter meat too quickly will waste any pending healing, as the most recent delayed heal will replace older effects that have not completed yet. Standard foods and potions do not interrupt this pending heal.
