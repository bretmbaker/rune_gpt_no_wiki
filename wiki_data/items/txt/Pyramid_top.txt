Thepyramid topis an artefact found at the top of theAgility Pyramidcourse. To obtain it, players have to go through a series of obstacles to reach the top. Once at the highest point on the pyramid, the pyramid top can be seen above the exit doorway. To reach it, climb the climbing rocks at the side of the doorway. Then proceed around the top level and you will arrive at the centre of the south face of the pyramid.
