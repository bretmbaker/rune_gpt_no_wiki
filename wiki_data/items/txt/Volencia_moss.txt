Volencia mossis aherbwhich requires level 3Herbloreto clean, yielding 2.5experience. It is used in theJungle Potionquest as the fourth herb that the player bringsTrufitus. Players can make volencia moss by cleaning thegrimy volencia mossobtained by searching theblack rocksat theKaramja Jungle mine, north ofShilo Village.  In order to be able to collect the herb, the player must first have started theJungle Potionquest.
