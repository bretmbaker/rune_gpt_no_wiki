TheTrailblazer reloaded alchemy scrollis a consumable item purchased from theLeagues Reward Shopfor 2,000 League points. It can be used to unlock aTrailblazer Reloaded-themedalchemy spellanimation override.  To enable it, players can right click "Animation" on either theLow Level AlchemyorHigh Level Alchemyicon in thestandard spellbookand select "Leagues IV - Trailblazer Reloaded".
