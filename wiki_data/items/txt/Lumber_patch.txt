Thelumber patchis used in theTrouble Brewingminigame to repairhopperswhich have been burnt by other players. It can sometimes require more than one (up to three) patches to repair the hopper, how many it takes depends on how long the hopper was on fire for. As a large amount of games are commenced with the agreement to not sabotage the other team (as it benefits no-one), these are often not needed.
