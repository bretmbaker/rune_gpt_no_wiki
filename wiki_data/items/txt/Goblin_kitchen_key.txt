Thegoblin kitchen keyis aquest itemin theObservatory Quest. It found in a chest in theObservatory Dungeonand is used to open theKitchen gatein the same dungeon. The chest in which the key is found is different for every player, but remains the same once found. Attempting to obtain another from the chest while one is in the inventory yields the message:Another one will be of little use.However, it is possible to obtain duplicates if it is banked. It can be reobtained after the quest.
