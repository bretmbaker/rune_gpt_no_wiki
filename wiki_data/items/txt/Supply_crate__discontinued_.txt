Asupply cratewas a reward item given for subduing theWintertodtafter earning at least 500 points. It was replaced by theReward Cart, but existing supply crates were not deleted and can still be opened or kept. It can reward many useful items for skilling such as ores, logs, herbs, seeds, gems, and fish. The supply crate also gives the opportunity for items only obtainable through defeating the Wintertodt, such as thetome of fire,pyromancer outfit, and thephoenixpet.
