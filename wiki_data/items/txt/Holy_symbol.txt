Aholy symbolis an amulet donning the symbol ofSaradomin. They are made by having anunblessed symbolblessed byBrother Jeredin theEdgeville Monastery, or by blessing it via using aholy bookorbook of balanceon anunblessed symbolat 50Prayer, requiring a few prayer points. As Brother Jered is located on the upper floor of the monastery, blessing the symbol effectively requires a minimum of level 31 Prayer due to the entry requirement.
