Stewis a type offoodthat restores 11Hitpointswhen eaten. It can be obtained by cookinguncooked stewon afireorcooking range, requiring level 25Cookingand granting 117experiencewhen successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting inburnt stew. Theburn ratedecreases as the player'sCookinglevel increases. Stew is one of a number of foods with a reduced burn rate while using theLumbridge Castle rangewithCook's Assistantcompleted. It will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 58, or 54 when using the Lumbridge range.
