White toy horseysare items made as a joke byJagex, poking fun at the requests made by players to have horses. Toy horseys are obtainable fromDiangofor 150 coins, and come in 4 different colours: white, black, grey, and brown. They can also be made from aplankon anycrafting tablein aplayer-owned house, which requires level 10 or higherCrafting. When you play with the horsey, an animation showing your character playing with it appears, and text will appear above your head. Said text will beJust say neigh to gambling!
