TheTranslation book, orGnome-English Translation Dictionary, is a book written byAnita. It is obtained duringThe Grand Treequest to help players translate whatHazelmere, who only speaks in theGnomic tongue, is telling them. It is also used to help solve a puzzle later in the quest. DuringThe Eyes of Glouphriehowever, players don't need the book to talk to Hazelmere, as he will use magic to communicate with the player instead.
