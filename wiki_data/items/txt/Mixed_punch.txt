Amixed punchis an intermediate step in making aPineapple punch. It is created by clicking the "Mix-cocktail" option on a cocktail shaker, with twopineapples, alemonand anorangein your inventory, requiring level 8Cooking. If a player hasLime chunks,Pineapple chunks,Orange slicesand acocktail glassin their inventory, they can choose the "Pour" option, creating aPineapple punch.
