Theanger swordis a weapon obtained during the questA Soul's Baneused to killAngry unicorns. The anger sword is one of four weapons to choose from theweapon rackwhile completing the Anger Room in Tolna's Rift. This weapon cannot be taken from the room, and has a special ability that will automatically activate upon filling the Anger Bar. This special ability will send energy out to all NPCs in the room, instantly killing them.
