Tarn's diary, orThe Diary ofTarn Razorlor, is a book that chronicles how Tarn came to be in the dungeon where he is and how he got involved in the dark arts ofnecromancy. Additionally, the book contains some magic elements: when used with aSalve amuletor an imbued salve amulet, it will enchant it and increase the benefits gained to a 20% boost in strength and accuracy against theundead.
