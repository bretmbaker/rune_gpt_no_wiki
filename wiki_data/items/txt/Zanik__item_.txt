Zanikis obtained duringDeath to the Dorgeshuun. She is obtained (as an item) when theH.A.M. membersdispose of her. She cannot be dropped or destroyed and attempting to do so will result in the player saying "I can't just leave Zanik's body lying around! I should take her toJuna." When brought back to life, if asked how it was when she was dead she says, "One minute I was at H.A.M. camp, and the next I was here!".
