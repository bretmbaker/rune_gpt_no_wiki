Iron oxideis a quest item used in theOne Small Favourquest. It is obtained by talking toTindel MarchantinPort Khazard. The player is asked to stuff the man'sstodgy matressin return for the iron oxide. The oxide must then be taken toWizard CrompertyinArdougnein order to complete the next step in the quest. If lost, Marchant will charge 200 coins for a new one.
