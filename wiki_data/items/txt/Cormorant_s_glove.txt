Cormorant's gloveis an item obtained for free by speaking toAlry the AngleronMolch island. Requiring both theHunterandFishingskill to obtain, it can be used inAerial fishingto catchBluegills,Common tenches,Mottled eel, andGreater siren. Alongside the fish, players can also catchMolch pearlswhich can then be traded to Alry for a number of items which can assist in standard fishing.
