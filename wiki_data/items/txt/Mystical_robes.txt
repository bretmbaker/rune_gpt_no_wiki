Themystical robesare found during theGhosts Ahoyquest. After showing Necrovarus thePetition formwith 10 signatures, he burns it to ashes and then drops thekey, which you use to enter the room upstairs. Once in the room, open and search the coffin to find the robes. Upon giving theOld Cronethe robes, any duplicates will be removed from the inventory or bank; however, completing the quest does not eliminate any duplicates.
