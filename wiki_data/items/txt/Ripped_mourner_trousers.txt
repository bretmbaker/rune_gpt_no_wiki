Ripped mourner trousersare an item received during theMourning's End Part Iquestafter ambushing aMourneron theArandarpass. Players must take two silks and a bear fur toOronwen, the Elven Seamstress inLletya, to have the trousers repaired. Using the trousers onOronwenwith the appropriate items will not repair them. You have to talk to her and ask her if she fixes clothes.
