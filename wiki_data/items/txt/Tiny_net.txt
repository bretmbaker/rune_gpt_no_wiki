Atiny netis used to obtain apet fish, can be stored in atackle box, and can be purchased fromHarryinCatherbyfor 10 gold, provided the player has afishbowlfilled with both water andseaweedin their inventory. When meeting this criteria, a dialogue option about getting a fish and buying a tiny net becomes an option. AFishinglevel of 10 or higher is required. Obtaining a pet fish from Harry is required for theEasy Kandarin Diary.
