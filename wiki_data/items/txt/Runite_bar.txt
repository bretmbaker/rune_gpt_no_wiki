Runite barsare used in theSmithingskill to smith various runiteweaponryandarmour. At level 85 Smithing, players can smelt a runite bar by usingrunite oreand 8 pieces ofcoalwith afurnace, granting 50Smithingexperiencefor smelting and 75 Smithing experience for smithing, yielding a net of 125 experience.
