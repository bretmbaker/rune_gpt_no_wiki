Thesmall pickis a quest item found duringThe Fremennik Trialsquest. It is found while searchingPeer the Seer's house while trying to solve his challenge to get his vote. Although the player will also find ared herringwhile searching, the small pick is the real red herring as it serves no purpose during the quest. When you use the pick on the chest in the room, you will get the message: "You might be able to break the chest open using this pick...
... if you stood here tapping at it for twenty years or so."
