Enchanted milkis aquest itemused to free theLumbridge GuideinRecipe for Disaster. It is one of the three ingredients of acake of guidance, the others being theenchanted eggandenchanted flour. It is made from abucket of milkby WizardTraiborn, who lives on the1floor2floorof theWizards' Tower. The player must first complete a small test where they answer three general questions aboutOld School RuneScapein order to have it enchanted.
