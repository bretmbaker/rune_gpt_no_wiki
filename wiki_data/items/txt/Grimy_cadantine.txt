Agrimy cadantineis aherbthat has not yet been cleaned to make acadantine. Grimy cadantines may be grown from acadantine seedwith aFarminglevel of 67, giving 120 Farming experience per herb harvested. It is also dropped by certainNPCs. Cleaning a grimy cadantine requires aHerblorelevel of 65 and gives 12.5 Herblore experience. Cadantines are used to makesuper defence potionswith a Herblore level of 66.
