Red dragonhide(also calledRed d'hidebytanners) is a 100% drop fromred dragonswhen they die. The hide can be tanned to becomered dragon leatherby paying atanneror by using theTan Leatherlunar spell. A tanner will charge 20 coins for each hide, or 45 in Canifis. Tanners can be found inAl Kharid, theRanging Guild,Canifis,Forthos Dungeon, and theCrafting Guild.
