Thebonecrusher necklaceis a necklace that can be created by combining thebonecrusheranddragonbone necklacewith ahydra tail(which can be reversed). When combined, the charges that the bonecrusher held will be added to the bonecrusher necklace. Players cannot have a bonecrusher and a bonecrusher necklace at the same time. It requires level 70 inPrayerto equip and shares the same +12prayer bonusas the dragonbone necklace.
