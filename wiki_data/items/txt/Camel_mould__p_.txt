TheCamel mould (p)is aquestitem used during theEnakhra's Lamentquest. The (p) means positive mould. It is obtained by using a piece ofsoft clayin a cavity on the pedestal on the upper floor of the underground temple south of theBandit Camp. The mould can then be used to carve a newstone headfrom a block ofgranite. The player should then insert the head into the cavity on the pedestal in order to continue the quest.
