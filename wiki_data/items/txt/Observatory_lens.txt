Theobservatory lensis aquest itemmade and used during theObservatory Quest. During thequest, alens mouldand a piece ofmolten glassmust be given to the professor in the Observatory. The professor will then attempt to craft the lens but be unable to do so. He will then return the mould and glass to the player and request it be crafted. Once made, the lens should be given to the professor so that the telescope can be repaired.
