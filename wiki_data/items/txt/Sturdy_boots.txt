Sturdy bootsare a quest item used inThe Fremennik Trialsquest. They are obtained by giving afiscal statementtoYrsainRellekka. The boots can then be given toOlaf the Bardwho will then compose aFremennik ballad, which you will then have to give to thesailor. He will then give you anexotic flowerwhich when given toSigmund the Merchant, will be exchanged for his vote.
