Theaddress formis an item that is used in theKing's Ransomquest. The address form is received as a piece of evidence when the player breaks into theSinclair MansionnearSeers' Village. It is taken from the library on the1floor2floorof the mansion and cannot be telegrabbed. It proves that the Sinclair family had left for good and wasn't going to tell anybody where or why they were going. Once obtained from the house the player must then take the form back to the guard they talked to near the start of the quest. It has no other real use.
