Amonkeyis used inTrouble Brewingto gatherbitternuts. They are found in the island in between the two bases, and they can be caught awarding the player with 15AgilityandHunterexperience. Abowl of red wateror abowl of blue watermust be used on the monkey, making it ared monkeyor ablue monkey. The dyed monkey can then be used on aBitternut Tree, and it will throw down a bitternut to the player. If a monkey from the opposing team is in the tree as well, the two monkeys will fight, only one will throw down a bitternut, and the other will disappear.
