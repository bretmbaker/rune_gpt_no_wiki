Karambwan vesselsarefishinginstruments used for catchingkarambwanin the northern coastal waters ofKaramja. The player receives one during theTai Bwo Wannai Trioquest, and they can later be purchased fromTiadeche'sKarambwan StallinTai Bwo WannaiVillage for 2coins. It requires level 5 Fishing to use, although karambwan cannot be caught until 65.
