Half certificatesare used during theShield of Arravquest. When a player returns their half of the Shield toCurator Haig Halenof theVarrock Museum, they will receive one half of the certificate, depending on whether they joined thePhoenix Gangor theBlack Arm Gang. There are two different certificate halves. Once combined, one can not trade the fullcertificate. The full certificate must be given toKing Roaldto complete the Shield of Arrav quest.
