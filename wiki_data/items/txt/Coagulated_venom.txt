Coagulated venomis a tertiary drop fromAraxxorthat can be used onNidto allow it tometamorphoseintoRax. The drop is guaranteed if Araxxor is killed under 1:15. Once used on Nid, it cannot be obtained again. Nid must be following the player, you cannot use this while Nid is in your inventory, or in the player's pet menagerie in their player-owned house.
