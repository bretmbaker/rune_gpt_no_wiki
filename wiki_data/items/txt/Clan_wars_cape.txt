Clan wars capesareunobtainable itemsthat are equipped in thejaw slotduring a game ofClan Warsin order to achieve the team cape's effect without needing to wear one. The capes have 16 variants to account for possible facial hair, where fake beards are built into the item to make it appear as if they had not been removed. There are two separate versions for each team, although all the purple team's capes except the default have the same icon as the yellow team.
