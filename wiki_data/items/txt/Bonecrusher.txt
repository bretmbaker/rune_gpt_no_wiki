Thebonecrusheris a reward from thehard Morytania Diary. Players can obtain a bonecrusher from aghost disciplein theEctofuntusbuilding whilst wearing aGhostspeak amulet(orMorytania legs). When charged withecto-tokensand carried in the inventory, if the player kills a monster that drops bones, the bonecrusher will automatically crush them, granting the playerhalfthePrayerexperiencethey would have received from burying the bones themselves, or the full amount if theelite Morytania Diaryis completed, and consuming one charge. This effect can be toggled with the right-click "Activity" option.
