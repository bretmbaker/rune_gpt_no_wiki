Sacred oilis a vial of blessedolive oilthat is used to makepyre logs. It is obtained from theShades of Mort'ton minigame, by using vials of olive oil on thefire altar, once the temple has been fully repaired and the altar lit. Converting olive oil to sacred oil costs sanctity points, which are gained by repairing the temple's walls or by killing theloar shadesthat attack the temple. Each dose of olive oil costs about 0.9% sanctity to convert; a 4-dose vial of olive oil will cost about 3.6% sanctity to convert into sacred oil. Additionally, players must have at least 10% sanctity in order to convert olive oil into sacred oil.
