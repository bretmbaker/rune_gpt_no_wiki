Theugthanki kebabis a type offoodwhich restores 19Hitpoints. It can be obtained by chopping atomato, anonion, and a piece of cookedugthanki meatinto abowlwith aknife, then using the resultingkebab mixwith a bakedpitta bread. Level 58Cookingis required to make the pitta bread, but the rest may be made at Cooking level 1. There is a chance to fail when creating one with a low Cooking level, instead creating aUgthanki kebab (smelling).
