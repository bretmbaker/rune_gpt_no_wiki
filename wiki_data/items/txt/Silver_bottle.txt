Thesilver bottleis a quest item used in theMurder Mysteryquest. It is obtained from a barrel inCarol's bedroom in theSinclair Mansion. It has Carol's fingerprints on it and is used to determine if Carol is the murderer or not. If it is used on the flour barrel, it will create a dusted silver bottle, and ifflypaperis used on it, it will createCarol's print, which can be compared with theunknown printto see if Carol is the killer.
