Blood essenceis an item dropped by certain NPCs in theAncient Prison, a possible reward from theTombs of Amascut, as well as a potential reward when opening anintricate pouch. When one is activated, it becomes untradeable. While activated and in the inventory, it will give a 50% chance to craft an extrablood runeperpure essence,daeyalt essence,guardian essenceordark essence fragmentfor no additional experience. Each blood essence can craft an extra 1,000 blood runes before being destroyed. Blood essence works inGuardians of the Rift, however, the additional blood runes crafted will not provide additionalcatalytic guardian stones. Blood essence does not provide benefit nor consume charges at theOurania Altar.
