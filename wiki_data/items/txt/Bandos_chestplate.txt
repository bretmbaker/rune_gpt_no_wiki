TheBandos chestplateis a part of theBandos armourset, which requires 65Defenceto wear. It is dropped byGeneral Graardorand his bodyguards in theGod Wars Dungeon. The Bandos chestplate provides a +4Strength bonus, and more defensive bonuses than comparable body armours such as thefighter torsoandinquisitor's hauberk. Despite seemingly being made of metal, the Bandos chestplate does not interfere withAva's devices.
