Mushroom pieis  a type ofpiethat restores 16Hitpointsin total, 2 bites each healing for 8. It alsotemporarily boostsCraftingby 4, the second highest boost available, and the highest reliable boost. A pie dish will remain after both bites are consumed. It can be obtained by baking auncooked mushroom pieon acooking range, requiring level 60Cookingand granting 200 experience when successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting inburnt pie. Theburn ratedecreases as the player'sCookinglevel increases. Mushroom pie is one of a number of foods with a reduced burn rate while using theLumbridge Castle rangewithCook's Assistantcompleted. It will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 89, or 60 when using the Lumbridge range. Alternatively, casting theBake Piespell from thelunar spellbookwill always create a pie without burning it.
