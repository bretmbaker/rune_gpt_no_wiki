It can be obtained by baking araw summer pieon acooking range, requiring level 95Cookingand granting 260experiencewhen successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting inburnt pie. Theburn ratedecreases as the player'sCookinglevel increases. Summer pies never stop burning altogether even at level 99, unless the player is wearing aCooking cape.
