TheOnyx amuletis made by using anonyxand agold baron afurnacewith anamulet mouldin your inventory to create anonyx amulet (u). This requires aCraftinglevel of 90 and provides 165 Craftingexperiencewhen made. The amulet must then be strung before wearing by using aball of woolor theString Jewelleryspell from theLunar spellbook. Stringing provides an additional 4 Crafting experience.
