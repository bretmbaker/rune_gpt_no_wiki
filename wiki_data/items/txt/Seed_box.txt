Theseed boxis an item that can store up to 2,147,483,647seedsof six different types. It is purchased fromFarmer Gricollerat theTithe Farmfor 250 points. This item cannot be used directly on afarming patch; players must remove the seeds they want to use in order to plant them. Players can right-click to open the box, which will have seeds that the player picks up be automatically placed inside the box, provided there is space for it.
