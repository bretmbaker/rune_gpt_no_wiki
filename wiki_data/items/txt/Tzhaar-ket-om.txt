TheTzhaar-ket-om, also known as theobsidian maul, is a weapon that requires aStrengthlevel of 60 to wield. It is a two-handed weapon. It is affected by theBerserker necklace, receiving a 20% damage increase when the necklace is worn. It is also affected by theObsidian armour(helmet,platebodyandplatelegs) which will increase the melee attack and melee strength by 10%. These damage bonusesdostack with each other.
