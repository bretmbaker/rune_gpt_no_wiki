Aniron sheetis made by placing anIron barinto the press at thePiscatoris Fishing Colonyfurnace building. It is used to repair the colony wall during the questSwan Song. It has no further uses after the quest, and can be sold toFranklin Caranosfor 20 coins after the quest. It can be made using theSmithingskill, which grants 20 Smithingexperience.
