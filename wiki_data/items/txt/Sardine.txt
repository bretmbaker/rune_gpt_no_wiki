Sardineis a type of fish that restores 4Hitpointswhen eaten. It can be obtained by cookingraw sardineon afireorcooking range, granting 40experiencewhen successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting in aburnt sardine. Theburn ratedecreases as the player'sCookinglevel increases. Sardine is one of a number of foods with a reduced burn rate while using theLumbridge Castle rangewithCook's Assistantcompleted. It will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 38, or 34 when using the Lumbridge range.
