Cooked rabbitis a type offoodthat restores 5Hitpointswhen eaten. It can be obtained by cooking araw rabbiton afireorcooking range, granting 30 experience when successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting inburnt meat. Theburn ratedecreases as the player'sCookinglevel increases, and it will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 34 on both ranges and fires.
