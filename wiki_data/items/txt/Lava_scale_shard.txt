Lava scale shardsare the result of using apestle and mortaron alava scale, which is dropped bylava dragonsandbrutal black dragons. Grinding a scale yields between 3-6 shards, with higherHerblorelevels yielding a higher average. If thehard Wilderness Diaryis complete, this amount will be increased by 50%, rounded down, meaning that players can receive either 4, 6, 7, or 9 shards per scale.
