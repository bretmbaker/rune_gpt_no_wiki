Agrimy marrentillis a herb that has not yet been cleaned to make amarrentill. Grimy marrentills may be grown from aMarrentill seedwith aFarminglevel of 14. It is also dropped by certainmonsters. Cleaning a Grimy marrentill requires aHerblorelevel of 5 and gives 3.8 Herblore experience. Clean marrentills are used in the making ofantipoisonsat level 5Herblore,Guthix restsat level 18 Herblore, andmarrentill tarat level 31. Marrentill can also be used to light incense burners inplayer-owned houses.
