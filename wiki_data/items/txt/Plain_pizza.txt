Plain pizzais a type ofpizzathat restores 14Hitpointsin total, 2 bites each healing for 7. It can be obtained by baking anuncooked pizzaon acooking range, requiring level 35Cookingand granting 143 experience when successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting inburnt pizza. Theburn ratedecreases as the player'sCookinglevel increases, and it will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 68.
