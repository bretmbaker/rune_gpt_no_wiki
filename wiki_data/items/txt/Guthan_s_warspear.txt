Guthan's warspearis a two-handedmelee weaponthat is part ofGuthan the Infested's setofbarrows equipmentand requires 70Attackto wield. It is the second most accurate spear in game, surpassed only by theZamorakian spear/hasta, which has +85 Stab. However, it is slower than most spears, having an attack rate of 5 ticks, rather than 4 ticks, and it cannot be poisoned.
