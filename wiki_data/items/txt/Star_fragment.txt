Star fragmentis an item that allows one piece of theprospector kitto be recoloured to agold variant. It can be purchased fromDusuri's Star Shopfor 3,000stardust. As it is a one time use per prospector item, a total of 4 are needed to recolour the full set, costing 12,000 stardust. Just like its uncoloured variant, the full set grants 2.5% extra mining experience; As such, these changes are purely cosmetic.
