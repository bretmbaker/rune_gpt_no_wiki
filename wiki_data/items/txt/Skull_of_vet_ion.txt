Theskull of Vet'ionis an item dropped byVet'ionandCalvar'ion. It is used to upgradeThammaron's sceptreinto anaccursed sceptre. This process requires level 85Crafting; players without the required level to combine the items may askPhabelle BileinFerox Enclaveto do so instead, for a fee of500,000 coins. The uncharged weapon may be dismantled to separate the sceptre and skull.
