The barrel ofRotten applesis a quest item used in theMourning's End Part Iquest. It is obtained by using abarrelon aRotten Apple Pilein the orchard south of theGnome Strongholdand west ofCombat Training Camp. The barrel of apples should then be mashed using the press to turn it into anapple barrel. Then it is to be mixed with distilled naphtha to obtain aNaphtha apple mix.
