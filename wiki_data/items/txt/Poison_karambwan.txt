Poison karambwanis a type of untradeablefoodthat damages the player for 5poisondamage.  It can be obtained by cookingraw karambwan, granting 80 experience when successful. Prior to completingTai Bwo Wannai Trioand speaking toTinsay, poison karambwan will always be made when successfully cooking raw karambwan. After this event, raw karambwan can be made into either poison karambwan or intocooked karambwan, which heals for 18Hitpoints. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting inburnt karambwan. The burn rate decreases as the player'sCookinglevel increases, and it will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 99 on both ranges and fires.
