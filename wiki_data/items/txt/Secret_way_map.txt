TheSecret way mapis a quest item used in theDeath Plateauquest. It can be obtained by travelling toTenzing's mountain-side cabin nearBurthorpeand giving him a pair ofspiked bootsmade byDunstanthe blacksmith earlier in the quest. The map should then be taken toDenulth, the leader of theImperial Guardto complete the quest. If the map is lost before completion of the quest,Tenzingwill draw another map for the player without requiring another pair of spiked boots.
