Karamjan rumis the local drink ofKaramja. It can be bought fromKaramja Wines, Spirits, and Beersshop on Karamja and theDead Man's ChestinBrimhaven.Memberscan use the rum on a piece ofsilkto create acleaning cloth, which is used to removepoisonfromweapons. Drinking the rum heals 5Hitpointsand also boostsStrengthequal to 1 + 5% of the player's base Strength level, rounded down. Drinking it also temporarily lowers the drinker'sAttackequal to 3 + 2% of the player's current Attack level, rounded down. Upon drinking the rum, the chatbox displays the messageYou drink the rum. You feel slightly reinvigorated... ...and slightly dizzy too..
