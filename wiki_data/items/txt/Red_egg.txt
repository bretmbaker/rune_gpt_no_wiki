Ared eggis used inBarbarian Assaultto damage penance monsters. If loaded into the egg launcher and shot at penance creatures, it will explode and deal 3 damage to the primary target, and 0 to 1 damage to any penance monster within 3 squares of the primary target.A maximum of five red eggs may be loaded into the egg launcher at any given time. Both egg launchers share the same reserve of eggs.
