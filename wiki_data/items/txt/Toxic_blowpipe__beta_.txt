Thetoxic blowpipe (beta)is a variant of thetoxic blowpipethat is available in anunrestricted worldduring theEquipment Rebalancing Beta, as a means to test its proposed stats reductions in theEquipment Rebalancing Changesblog, in which the blowpipe's Ranged Attack bonus is proposed to be reduced by 30, and its Ranged Strength reduced by 15, with the dart's bonuses being reduced to match.
