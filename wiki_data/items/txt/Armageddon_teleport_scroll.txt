TheArmageddon teleport scrollis an item that can be purchased from theDeadman Reward Storefor 9,000Deadman Points, which is earned from participating inDeadman: Armageddon. It can be used to unlock a Deadman: Armageddon-themedHome Teleportanimation override. To enable it, players can right click "Animation" on the Home Teleport spell icon in their spellbook and select "Deadman: Armageddon."
