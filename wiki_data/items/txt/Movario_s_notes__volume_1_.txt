Movario's notes (volume 1)are the first half ofMovario's documentation of his search for theStone of Jas. He begins in the Wilderness, deeming it unworthy of further searching, and then moves toMorytania, theTemple of Ikov, theDigsite, andLunar Isle, where he learns part of the stone's true name. He then proceeds toShilo Village,Menaphos, and theAncient Cavern, where he learns another name for the stone, the Eye of Saradomin, as well as remnants ofdragonkinarchitecture. Finally, he travels to theLumbridge Swamp Cavesand theWizards' Tower, where he learns of another name for the stone, the Fist of Guthix.
