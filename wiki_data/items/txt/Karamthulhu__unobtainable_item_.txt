Karamthulhuis anunobtainable itemreleased alongside theRum Dealquest. It is very similar to the obtainablekaramthulhuitem, but has a different inventory icon, value, and examine text. While its examine text suggests it was intended to be linked to theunreleased Karamthulhu pet, the pet NPC was linked to the obtainable karamthulhu item instead, leaving the purpose of this one unknown.
