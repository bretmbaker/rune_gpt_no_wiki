Nettlesare the key ingredient innettle tea. They can be found in various locations throughout Runescape. Players must wearglovesin order to pick nettles (Beekeeper's gloveswill not work). If no gloves are worn, no nettles will be obtained and the player will be dealt up to 2Hitpointsof damage. Although the resulting hitsplat is green, similar to poison, the player will not be poisoned.
