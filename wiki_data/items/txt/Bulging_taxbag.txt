Thebulging taxbagis an item that is unobtainable via normal gameplay. DuringThe Fremennik Isles, in the part of the quest where players collect taxes forKing Gjuki Sorvott IV, the tax bag becomes bulging when they collect at least 33,000 coins in taxes from the citizens ofJatizso. However, players can only collect a maximum of 29,000 coins in taxes, resulting in ahefty tax bag.
