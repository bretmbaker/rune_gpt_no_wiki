Thechunk of crystalis a quest item used during theLegends' Quest. It is obtained by killing one of three knights—Ranalph Devere,Irvig SenayorSan Tojalon—in the dungeon underKharazi Jungle. When combined with thelump of crystal, and thehunk of crystal(obtained by killing the other two knights), they form theheart crystalrequired to unlock a door leading further into the dungeon.
