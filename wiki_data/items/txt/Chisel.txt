Achiselis a tool used in theCraftingskillto cut and shape hard non-metals, mainlygemsandlimestone. Chisels are also used inFletchingto cut gems intobolt tips. Chisels can be purchased for 1coinat allgeneral stores, except for those inDorgesh-KaanandArdougne. There is afree-to-playchisel spawn in theCrafting Guild.
