Anemerald amulet (u)can be made at level 31Craftingby using agold baron afurnacewhile carrying a cutemeraldand anamulet mould. This grants the player 70 Crafting experience. The amulet can then be strung with aball of woolto create a strungemerald amulet, granting an additional 4 experience. The strung amulet can be enchanted into anamulet of defenceusing theLvl-2 Enchantspell, requiring level 27Magic.
