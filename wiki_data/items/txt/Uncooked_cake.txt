Uncooked cakecan be made with theCookingskill by using anegg, abucket of milk, and apot of flourwith acake tin. To bake a cake, players must use it on arangewith level 40+ Cooking. This will either turn the uncooked cake into aCakeor it will burn the cake (burnt cakeshave no use). After cooking the cake, successfully or burnt, the player will receive back their cake tin. Optionally, at Cooking level 50+, players may then use achocolate bar(which can be found in theCooks' Guild) orchocolate duston the cake to make achocolate cake.
