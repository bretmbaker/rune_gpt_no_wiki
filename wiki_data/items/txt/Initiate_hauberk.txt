Theinitiate hauberkis a piece of Temple knight armour that requires 20Defence, 10Prayer, and completion of theRecruitment Drivequest to equip. It provides a small prayer bonus that matches theMonk's robe top, while retaining the defensive bonuses of aMithril platebody, and is commonly used duringSlayer tasksthat require the use ofprotection prayers, as the armour reduces the drain of prayer points.
