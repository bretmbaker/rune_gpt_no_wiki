TheGoblin symbol book, orA History of the Goblin Race, is aquest iteminThe Lost Tribequest. The player showsReldoabrooch, which prompts him to tell the player to search for the book on the northernbookcaseon the west wall in theVarrock Palace Library. It has symbols in it that help the player navigate the maze of tunnels leading to theDorgesh-Kaan mine.
