Thegriffin featheris a quest item obtained during theGrim Talesquest. The player must steal it fromGrimgnash, the griffin that lives on the east side ofWhite Wolf Mountain. In order to obtain the feather, the player has to first send Grimgnash to sleep with a most horrid bedtime story. The feather must then be taken toSylasinTaverleyalong withRupert's helmetin order to exchange them for somemagic beans.
