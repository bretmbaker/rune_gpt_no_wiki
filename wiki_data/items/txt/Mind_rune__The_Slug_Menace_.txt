Amind runeis a special type ofmind runeused duringThe Slug Menace. It is made by using ablank mind runeon theMind Altarin the normal way and is used to unlock a mysterious door in the newly discovered shrine beneathWitchaven. In order to unlock the door the player must also have made aspecial air rune, aspecial earth rune, aspecial fire runeand aspecial water rune. Unlike normal mind runes, this cannot be used to cast a spell.
