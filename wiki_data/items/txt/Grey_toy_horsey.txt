Grey toy horseysare items made as a joke byJagex, poking fun at the requests made by players to have horses. Toy horseys are obtainable fromDiangofor 150 coins, and come in 4 different colours: white, black, grey, and brown. They can also be made from aplankon anycrafting tablein aplayer-owned house, which requires level 10 or higherCrafting.
