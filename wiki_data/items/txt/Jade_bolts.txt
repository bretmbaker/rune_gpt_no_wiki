Jade boltsareblurite boltstipped withjade. Jade bolts can be created through theFletchingskill. A player must first have a Fletching level of 26 and take a cutjadeand use a chisel with it to make 12Jade bolt tips, granting 2 Fletching experience. Attaching jade bolt tips also requires a Fletching level of 26, and for each jade bolt tip attached to a blurite bolt, a player is granted 2.4 Fletching experience. Once the jade bolt tips have been attached, the blurite bolts become jade bolts.
