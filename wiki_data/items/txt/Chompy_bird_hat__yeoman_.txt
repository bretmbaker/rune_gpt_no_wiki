Theyeoman hat(in-game:Chompy bird hat) is amembers-onlydecorative hat. To wear this hat the player needs at least 5Fletchingand 30Ranged. To obtain this hat the player must first kill at least 70Chompy birds, then talk toRantzwith the ogre bow in the inventory. As this hat is purely cosmetic, it gives no bonuses.
