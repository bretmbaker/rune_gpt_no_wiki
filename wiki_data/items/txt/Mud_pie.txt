Amud pieis a special type ofpiethat does not restoreHitpoints, and instead can be used as an equipableweaponforPvP. The pie does no damage and is not stackable, but can be thrown at other players to reduces the target's currentrun energyby 25%, rounded down.Mud pies have no effect onNPCs. When throwing a mud pie, apie dishwill drop underneath the target. As mud pies are considered a thrown weapon, a player targeted by mud pies should wear armour with high Ranged defence to lower their accuracy.
