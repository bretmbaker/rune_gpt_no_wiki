Thehand mirroris a quest item used in theMourning's End Part IIand theSong of the Elvesquest. It is obtained by pulling a lever in theTemple of Lightnear the start of the quest and is needed to solve the light puzzle in the Temple. As the player progresses through the puzzle they are rewarded with more mirrors in order to unlock more complicated parts of the puzzle. The mirrors redirect light to the place it needs to go if you put it at the right angle.
