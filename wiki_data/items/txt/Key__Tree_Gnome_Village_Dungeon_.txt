Thekeyis an item found in theTree Gnome Village Dungeon. Prior to starting theRoving Elvesquest, it can be found in a crate in the storeroom to the north-east of the dungeon entrance. It opens the door to the jail, found north-west of the dungeon entrance. Upon finding it, theplayerwill receive the message "You found a large key". The crate can be searched for another key if the player loses it. It cannot be stored on aSteel key ring.
