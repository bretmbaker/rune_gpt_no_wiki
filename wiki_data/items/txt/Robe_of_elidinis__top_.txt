TheRobe of Elidinis (top)is a robe top that is required in theSpirits of the Elidquest. The robes are created by using aneedleon thetorn robesfound in the shrine inNardahduring or after the quest. The player must wear the robes in order to enter theWater Ravine Dungeonfarther north along theRiver Elid. After the quest, players may use the robes to continue to access the dungeon, although few do.
