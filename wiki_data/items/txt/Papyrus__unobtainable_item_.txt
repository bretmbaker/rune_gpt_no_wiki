Usedpapyrusis an unobtainable item that was released during theRuneScape 2beta. It is a version ofpapyrusthat has been used to write something usingcharcoal. Presumably, it was intended to be used duringShilo Village, which had been slightly reworked during the transition to RuneScape 2. However, for unknown reasons the used papyrus was never added to the game.
