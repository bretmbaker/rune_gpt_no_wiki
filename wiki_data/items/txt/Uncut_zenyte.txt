Anuncut zenyteis the highest tier ofgeminOld School RuneScape. It can be made by combining a cutonyxwith azenyte shardat thewall of flamebeneath theTemple of MarimboonApe Atoll, while wearing azombie monkey greegree(small or big). This requires completion ofMonkey Madness IIand level 70Crafting, and grants 15experience. It can then be cut with level 89 Crafting intozenyte, granting 50 experience.
