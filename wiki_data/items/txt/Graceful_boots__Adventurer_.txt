Theadventurer's graceful bootsare one of many variants ofgraceful boots. They are recoloured from graceful boots by using it on a pair ofadventurer's boots (t3), which is purchased from theSpeedrunning Reward Shop, alongside the rest of theoutfit, for 1,800Quest Speedrunningreward points. After purchasing the set once, you can claim unlimited sets from the Speedrunning Reward Shop. You can speak toOstenin Shayzien to revert your Adventurer's Graceful back to the default appearance for free.
