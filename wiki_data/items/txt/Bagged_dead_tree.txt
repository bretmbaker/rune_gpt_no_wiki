Abagged dead treecan be bought inFaladoror theFarming Guildfrom theGarden supplierfor 1,000 coins. It can be planted in thegardenof aplayer-owned house, requiring aConstructionlevel of 5 or higher, providing 31 experience to both Construction andFarming. The player must have a filledwatering canin their inventory to plant it. It is only for decoration and cannot be cut down to obtain logs.
