Acrate of steel arrowtipsis an item in theSailing alpha.  It is the cargo used for the "In need of arrowtips" and "GPDT fast-track required"courier tasks. It is used when transporting cargo from one port to another as one of several chosen cargo crates when doing a courier task. They can also be placed inside of a shipscargo holdon a player's ship to avoid needing to be held and to allow for usage of ship modules. The crate must be picked up at theledger tablein the starting port and must be placed at the ledger table at the destination port to complete the task.
