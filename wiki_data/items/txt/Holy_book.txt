Theholy bookis a book held in place of ashield, and is thegod bookaligned withSaradomin. God books are commonly used for their prayer bonus, something few shields offer. The Holy book is obtained from theHorror from the Deepquest. The book is received from the quest as just a cover, and requires 4Saradomin pagesobtained fromTreasure Trailsto be completed. The book will not give any bonuses (with the exception of the +5 prayer bonus) until it is completed. An incomplete holy book does count as a Saradomin item in theHallowed Sepulchre. With 50Prayerand a completed book, players can enchantunblessed symbolsintoholy symbols.
