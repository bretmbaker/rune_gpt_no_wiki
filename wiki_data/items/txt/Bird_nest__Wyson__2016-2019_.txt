The discontinuedbird nestfromWysonwas an old variant of theWyson seed nestobtainable before the release of theFarming Guild. After this update, the existing nests were discontinued, and newly obtained nests would instead be the current variant. All nests previously obtained would remain in banks and inventories, and the new nests obtained would providesnape grass,dragonfruit,celastrus, andredwood tree seeds. This change was made to prevent players from obtaining large quantities of the new seeds immediately upon release of the content.
