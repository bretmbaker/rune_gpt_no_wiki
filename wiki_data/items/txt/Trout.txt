Troutis a type of fish that restores 7Hitpointswhen eaten. It can be obtained by cookingraw trouton afireorcooking range, requiring level 15Cookingand granting 70experiencewhen successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting in aburnt trout. Theburn ratedecreases as the player's Cooking level increases. Trout is one of a number of foods with a reduced burn rate while using theLumbridge Castle rangewithCook's Assistantcompleted. It will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 49, or 45 when using the Lumbridge range.
