Thebone shortbowis a weapon requiring level 50Rangedto wield. It is made by combining ayew shortbowwithScurrius' spine, requiring aknifeand level 35Fletchingto do so; players without the required level can alternatively payHistorian Aldo50,000coinsto create it on their behalf. The bow has identical offensive stats to themagic shortbow, and can fire up toamethyst arrows.
