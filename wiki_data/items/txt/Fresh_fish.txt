Fresh fishwas obtained from barrels of fish found throughoutPort Piscarilius. Players must deposit these into thecooling chestinFrankie's Fishing Emporiumin the south-east corner of the docks. When handing in at least 2 fresh fish at once, there is a 20% chance for each fish to yield 0.1% Piscariliusfavoureach.Every fish handed in beyond the first will grant 10Fishingexperience.
