Raw troutare fish that can be caught by luring Lure/Bait fishing spots with aFishinglevel of 20, granting 50 Fishing experience per catch. It can be cooked intotroutwith aCookinglevel of 15. Players will stop burning trout at level 49 if using a range, or 50 if using a fire. Successfully cooking a raw trout gives 70 Cooking experience.
