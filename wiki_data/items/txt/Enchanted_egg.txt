Theenchanted eggis aquest itemused to free theLumbridge Guidein theRecipe for Disasterquest. It is one of the three ingredients of acake of guidance, the others beingenchanted milkandenchanted flour. It is made by having a regulareggenchanted by WizardTraiborn, who lives on the1floor2floorof theWizards' Tower. The player must complete a small test in which they guess the names of three NPCs who appear from giant eggs.
