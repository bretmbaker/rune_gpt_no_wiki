Aferretis an item used with arabbit snarein order to huntwhite rabbitsin thePiscatoris Hunter area. Players can obtain a ferret by catching one of thewild ferretsusing abox trapslightly south-west of the southern rabbit holes. Once caught, the player can use a ferret to flush out a white rabbit from its hole and towards a waiting rabbit snare. A ferret is also caught during the questEagles' Peakto be given toCharlie.
