Aweapon frameis an item used in theCorrupted Gauntlet. It can be obtained as a drop from any level monster. The weapon frame is used with 20corrupted shardsto create the basiccorrupted halberd,boworstaff. These items can be attuned to tier 2 by using 60 more corrupted shards. Depending on which weapon is created, the attuned weapons can be perfected to tier 3 by using a unique item (either thecorrupted spike,orborbowstring).
