Black dragonhide(also calledBlack d'hidebytanners) is a 100% drop fromblack dragons,brutal black dragons,lava dragons, and theKing Black Dragon. The hide can be tanned to becomeblack dragon leatherby paying atanneror by using theTan Leatherlunar spell. A tanner will charge 20 coins for each hide, or 45 in Canifis. Tanners can be found inAl Kharid, theRanging Guild,Canifis, theCrafting GuildandForthos Dungeon.
