Anensouled dragon headis an item which can be dropped by chromaticdragons. It is used to gainPrayerexperience by using the level 90MagicspellMaster Reanimationfrom theArceuus spellbookusing 4Soul runes, 2Blood runes, and 4Nature runes. When a player reanimates an ensouled dragon head, areanimated dragonwill appear and grant 1,560 Prayer experience after being killed. The reanimated dragon breathes fire, sodragonfireprotection or a safespot should be used.
