Cooked jubblyis an ogre delicacy obtained by cookingraw jubblyon thespitroastlocated nearRantzinFeldip Hillsor the one located atJiggig, requiring level 41Cookingand granting 160 experience when successful. Players may burn the jubbly while cooking one, resulting inburnt jubbly; theburn ratewhile cooking these will decrease as players reach higher Cooking levels.
