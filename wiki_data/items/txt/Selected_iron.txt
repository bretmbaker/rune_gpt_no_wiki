Selected ironis a quest item used in theAnimal Magnetismquest. The player must give theWitchinDraynor Manorfive regulariron barsand she will then return the bar that is most suitable for turning into abar magnet. The player must then take this bar to the mine nearRimmingtonand hit the bar with ahammer, making sure to face in the north direction, to turn it into a bar magnet. This will grant the player a token amount ofSmithingexperience.
