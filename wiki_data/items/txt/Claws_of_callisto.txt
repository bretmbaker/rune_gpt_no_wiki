Theclaws of Callistoare an item dropped byCallisto. They are used to upgradeViggora's chainmaceinto anursine chainmace. This process requires level 85Smithing; players without the required level to combine the items may askAndros MaiinFerox Enclaveto do so instead, for a fee of500,000 coins. The uncharged weapon may be dismantled to separate the chainmace and claws.
