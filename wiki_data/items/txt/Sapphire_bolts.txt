Sapphire boltsaremithril boltstipped withsapphire bolt tips. Sapphire bolts can be created through theFletchingskill. A player must have level 56 Fletching to use achiselwith a cutsapphireto make 12 sapphire bolt tips and to attach them to the mithril bolts, giving 4.7 Fletching experience per bolt. Once sapphire bolt tips have been attached to mithril bolts, they become sapphire bolts.
