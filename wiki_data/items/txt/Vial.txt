Vialsare typically filled up with water and are most commonly used in theHerbloreskill to make potions or mixes. Upon finishing a potion, the empty vial is left in the player's inventory; however with completion ofAlfred Grimhand's Barcrawl, players may speak to thebarbarian guardoutside of theBarbarian Outpostto learn how to automatically smash vials upon finishing a potion. This feature also works in free-to-play. The player may also toggle this feature from the settings menu.
