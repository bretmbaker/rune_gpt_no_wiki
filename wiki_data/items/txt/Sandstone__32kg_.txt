Sandstone (32kg)is a quest item used during theEnakhra's Lamentquest. It is obtained by speaking toLazimin the desert south of theBandit Campand then giving exactly 32 kg of individual sandstone blocks. The blocks can be mined from the quarry immediately south-west of Lazim. After giving the blocks to Lazim he will magically join them to create the 32 kg block. The player can then use achiselon this to make thesandstone basefor the statue needed in the quest.
