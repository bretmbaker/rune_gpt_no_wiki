Esoteric emblemsare items that can be traded in to theEmblem TraderforBounty Hunterpoints that can be used to purchase rewards from theBounty Hunter Store. A tier 1 emblem will drop on your first target kill, or it can be bought from the Bounty Hunter Store for two points. Each target kill will rank up your own emblem. Dying with an emblem will reduce its tier by 1. If the emblem has a tier of 1 upon death, it will be removed.
