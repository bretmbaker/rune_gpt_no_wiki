Anunfired potis made by usingsoft clayon apotter's wheel, which requires level 1Craftingand yields 6.3 Crafting experience. The unfired pot can be fired in apottery ovento produce apot, granting 6.3 Crafting experience upon success. Unlike shaping the clay, firing it has no Crafting level requirement. At lower levels, an unfired pot can crack in the oven, destroying the pot and granting no experience.
