Theblank mind runeis aquestitemused inThe Slug Menace. It is made by right-clicking onPage 3ofBrother Maledict's holy book and choosing the "shape mind" option with arune essenceorpure essencein the inventory. During this process the player must also make ablank air rune, ablank earth rune, ablank fire rune, and ablank water runeusingpage 1andpage 2. The player needs achiselin their inventory to make these runes.
It is recommended that players make more than one blank because players can fail while chiselling the shape of the rune, as well as when binding it with the element of the rune.
