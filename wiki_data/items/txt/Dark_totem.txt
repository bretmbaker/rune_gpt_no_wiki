Thedark totemis an item that can be created by combining thedark totem base,dark totem middle, anddark totem top. The totem pieces are dropped in that order and duplicates drop after all three are obtained. It is used on the altar in the centre of theCatacombs of Kourendto access the lower level of the catacombs to fightSkotizo. The totem is consumed when using it on the altar regardless of the outcome of the fight.
