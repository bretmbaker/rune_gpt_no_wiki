Mackerelis a type of fish that restores 6Hitpointswhen eaten. It can be obtained by cookingraw mackerelon afireorcooking range, requiring level 10Cookingand granting 60experiencewhen successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting in aburnt fish. Theburn ratedecreases as the player's Cooking level increases. Mackerel is one of a number of foods with a reduced burn rate while using theLumbridge Castle rangewithCook's Assistantcompleted. It will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 45, or 41 when using the Lumbridge range.
