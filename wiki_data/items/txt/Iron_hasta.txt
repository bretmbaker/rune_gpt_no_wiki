Theiron hastais a one-handedmeleeweapon, similar to thespear. To wield this weapon, players need to have completed theBarbarian Trainingminiquestthrough theSmithingsection. Although hastae offer negative defensive bonuses, the fact that they can be used with ashieldordefenderprovides more defence than a normal spear. To obtain an iron hasta the player can either create one using theSmithingskill or trade with another player. It can bepoisoned.
