Amethyst darts can bepoisonedand are one-handed, so they can be wielded with ashieldorgod bookfor protection. To fletch amethyst darts, the player must have at least 90 Fletching.Amethyst dart tipsare combined withfeathers, awarding 21 Fletching experience per dart.
