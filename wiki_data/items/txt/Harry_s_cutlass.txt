Harry's cutlassis a specialweaponsold bySmithon the docks of the island ofMos Le'Harmless. It originally belonged to a pirate namedBrass Hand Harry, who lost his right hand in a battle off the coast ofKaramja. He had Smith create a cutlass with a gauntlet fixed to the hilt in order to continue fighting. Smith will sell one to the player for 1,040coins. It requires anAttacklevel of 20 to wield. It has stats similar to those of aMithril scimitar.
