Loar remainsare an item found and used in theShades of Mort'tonminigame. To obtain these remains, the player must killloar shades, located aroundMort'ton. To burn the loar remains, the player needs some pyre logs (see table for more info) and atinderbox. First place the pyre logs on afuneral pyre, followed by the loar remains, then light the pyre. This will grant the playerFiremakingandPrayerexperience (see table).
