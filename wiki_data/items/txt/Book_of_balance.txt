Thebook of balanceis a book held in place of a shield, and is thegod bookaligned withGuthix. It can be received as a reward from theHorror from the Deepquest. Books of balance must be made using all four tornGuthix pageswhich can be purchased from other players or obtained fromTreasure Trails. It also allows players with aPrayerlevel of 50 or greater to blessholy symbolsandunholy symbols, at the cost of 6 prayer points per blessing (compared to 4 points lost when using the other two maingod book).
