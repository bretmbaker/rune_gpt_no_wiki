Thedark bowis a two-handedranged weaponthat requires level 60 inRangedto wield. It can only be obtained as a drop fromdark beastsand their superior variant,night beasts. With anattack speedof 9 (5.4 seconds), and 8 on rapid (4.8 seconds), this bow stands out as the slowest weapon in the game. It fires two arrows per attack which roll independent accuracy checks.It has the maximum possibleattack rangeof 10, so thelongrangeattack style will not increase its attack range.
