Cooked fishcakesare a type offoodthat can be obtained by cooking araw fishcakeon afireorcooking range, requiring level 31Cookingand granting 100 experience when successful. Players may burn the fishcake while cooking one, resulting inburnt fishcake; theburn ratewhile cooking these will decrease as players reach higher Cooking levels. It will stop burning entirely atCookinglevel 62 on both ranges and fires.
