Themodel shipis aquest itemgiven to the player during theGhosts AhoyQuest. The player must add a flag on it and dye the flag with the colours on the flag of the Ghost Ship found north-west ofPort Phasmatys; the colours are different for each player. It is given to the player by theOld Crone. After dyeing it the appropriate colour, players can then give it to theOld man, who is actually the Old Crone's son, to continue the quest.
