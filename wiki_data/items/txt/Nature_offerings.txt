Nature offeringsare a stackable item used inWoodcutting. When in the inventory or stored in aforestry kit, there is a 50% to 60% chance of receiving an additional log after receiving logs from chopping trees, this does not work when choppingSulliusceps; offerings are consumed regardless of whether the player receives an additional log. Chopping with other players on the same tree will increase the average by 2% with one other chopper, up to 20% more with the maximum +10 Woodcutting player bonus. No additional experience is awarded when receiving an additional log. However, the player must have at least two open inventory slots to receive the extra log; this extra log willnotdrop on the ground.
