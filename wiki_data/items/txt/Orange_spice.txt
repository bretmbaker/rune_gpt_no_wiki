Orange spiceis one of four spices used onstewto makespicy stew. Completion of theFreeing Evil Davesubquest ofRecipe for Disasteris required to obtain the spices inEvil Dave'sBasement of DoominEdgeville. The simplest method to obtain these spices is to send yourcatto catchHell-Rats, which will yield between 1 to 4 doses of any of the spice colours. Another option is to kill theHell-Rat Behemothin the north-western room, which will always drop a 4 dose of the orange spice. It is possible to combine lower doses of the orange spice on each other to create 4 dose shakers. Once all doses are used it becomes anempty spice shaker.
