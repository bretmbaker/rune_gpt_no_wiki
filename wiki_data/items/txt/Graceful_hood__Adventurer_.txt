Theadventurer's graceful hoodis one of many variants of thegraceful hood. It is recoloured from a graceful hood by using it on anadventurer's hood (t3), which is purchased from theSpeedrunning Reward Shop, alongside the rest of theoutfit, for 1,800Quest Speedrunningreward points. After purchasing the set once, you can claim unlimited sets from the Speedrunning Reward Shop. You can speak toOstenin Shayzien to revert your Adventurer's Graceful back to the default appearance for free.
