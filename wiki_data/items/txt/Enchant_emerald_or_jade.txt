Theenchant emerald or jadetablet is an item that can be used by any player to enchantemeraldorjadejewellery. Players can create this item on either ademon lectern, ateak demon lectern, or amahogany demon lecternas long as they have 1cosmic rune, 3air runes, and 1soft clay, and at least level 27Magic. This tablet is built using theConstructionskill and works regardless of yourMagiclevel. This single-use enchantment tablet enchants emerald jewellery.
