Thecomfy mattressis a quest item used inOne Small Favour. It is made by taking thestodgy mattressfrom a man inPort Khazardand adding the feathers given byRantz. The player should then return the mattress to the man in Port Khazard in exchange for someiron oxide. This must then be taken toWizard CrompertyinArdougnein order to proceed in the quest.
