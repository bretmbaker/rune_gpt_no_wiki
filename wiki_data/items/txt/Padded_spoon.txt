Padded spoonswere a stackable item used inWoodcutting. When in the inventory or stored in aForestry kit, there was a chance of aPheasant ControlForestry eventappearing; this item was a requirement for the event to reward agolden pheasant egg. The chance of the event occurring scaled based on the number of players present; when a spoon is consumed while training Woodcutting, a game message in the chatbox statedYou encounter some nesting pheasants and prepare your Padded spoon.When retrieving eggs for an event that players did not trigger, a spoon was consumed on the firstpheasant eggbut no chatbox message appeared.
