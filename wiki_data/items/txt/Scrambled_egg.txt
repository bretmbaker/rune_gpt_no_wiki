Scrambled eggsare a type offoodthat can be obtained by cooking anuncooked eggoncooking rangeorfire, requiring level 13Cookingand granting 50 experience when successful. Players may burn the egg while cooking one, resulting in aburnt egg; theburn ratewhile cooking these will decrease as players reach higher Cooking levels. It will stop burning entirely atCookinglevel 48 on both ranges and fires. Eating it heals 5 hitpoints.
