Thebow of Faerdhinenis a magical bow that once belonged toFaerdhinen, an elite elven warrior. It is created from anenhanced crystal weapon seed. Requiring level 80Rangedand 70Agilityto wield, it provides the highest Ranged Attack and Ranged Strength bonus out of any weapon for itsattack speed. To prevent the bow from degrading, it can be made into aBow of faerdhinen (c)at asinging bowlwith 2,000Crystal shards.
