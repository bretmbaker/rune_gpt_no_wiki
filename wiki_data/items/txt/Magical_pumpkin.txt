Themagical pumpkinis a reward for the2020 Halloween event. The item is used to plant apumpkinin agardenorformal gardenof aplayer-owned houseusing awatering can. Interacting with the magical pumpkin causes the player to take and eat pieces of differentcoloured candy. After eating the candy, the player will sayOoh piece of candy.. The candy does not provide any boosts, nor does it heal any missing health.
