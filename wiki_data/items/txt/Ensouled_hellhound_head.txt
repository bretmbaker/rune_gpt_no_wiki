Anensouled hellhound headis an item which can be dropped byhellhoundsandCerberus. It is used to gainPrayerexperience by using the level 72MagicspellExpert Reanimationfrom theArceuus spellbookusing 3nature runes, 1blood rune, and 2soul runes. When a player reanimates an ensouled hellhound head, areanimated hellhoundwill appear and grant 1,200 Prayer experience after being killed.
