TheUnholy symbolis a quest item obtained upon entering the burial pyramid during theIcthlarin's Little Helperquest. When thewanderer(Amascut) hypnotises the player, she hands over the symbol and says they will know what to do with it when the time comes. During one of the flashbacks, the player learns they placed this item in one of the sarcophagi so Amascut may enter the pyramid at ease. In the end of the quest, this led to her appearing during the ceremony, controlling apriestand attempting to kill/destroy all present at the ceremony. The adventurer defeats the priest and Amascut teleports away.
