Thenature talismanis atalismanused to gain access to theNature Altar, located north ofShilo Villageon the island ofKaramja, where players can craftnature runes. The talisman can also be combined with atiaraat the Nature Altar by having both the nature talisman and tiara in your inventory, and using the tiara on the altar. This action grants the player 45Runecraftexperience; some players choose to do this in order to save an extra inventory space and make quicker trips when crafting nature runes, as the Nature Altar may be accessed simply by left-clicking on it while the player is wearing thenature tiara.
