Thecup of teais a discontinued item that previously could be found at theTea stallin Varrock. InRuneScape Classicthis item was known asdisplay tea, and could be obtained by casting telekinetic grab. As the item was frequently used in scams, it was made untradeable with the release of RuneScape 2 and renamed, whereas the stall was changed to display a regularcup of tea. However, it still can be distinguished from the normal tea by its unique examine information.
