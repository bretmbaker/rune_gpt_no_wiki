Dagon'hai history(also known asThe Fall of the Dagon'haiorHistory of the Dagon'hai) is a book found in theVarrocklibrary. It can be found in the north-east corner of the room (the two bookcases placed south, with the book in the western bookcase). It tells of the fall of theDagon'haiand is used in thequestWhat Lies Below. After the quest it can also be found in abookcasein aplayer-owned house.
