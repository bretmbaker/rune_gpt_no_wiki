Fragment 1is a quest item used in theSlug Menacequest. It is given to the player byCol. O'Niall, along withFragment 2andFragment 3. The player must glue the fragments together usingsea slug glueand doing a small jigsaw puzzle to formPage 3of Maledict's holy book. This page is used in conjunction withPage 1andPage 2of the book in order to determine how to make the special runes needed to open a mysterious door in the newly discovered shrine beneathWitchaven.
