Thetrolleyis a quest item given to the player during theGarden of Tranquillityquest byQueen Ellamaria. The player must use the trolley to transport two heavy statues fromFaladorandLumbridgeto her garden behindVarrock Palace. The first is by the crossroads just north of the White Knights' Castle in Falador. The second is on the south pillar of the archway intoLumbridge Castle.
