Thecastle sketch 3is a quest item made during theDarkness of Hallowvalequest. It is a sketch of the southern part ofCastle Drakan. It is made by using a piece ofcharcoalon a sheet ofpapyruswhile standing on the South side of the castle (just a few steps west from where Safalaan give you the mission to sketch out the castle) at the north end ofMeiyerditch. The sketch must be given toSafalaanalong withcastle sketch 1andcastle sketch 2to complete the intelligence-gathering part of the quest.
