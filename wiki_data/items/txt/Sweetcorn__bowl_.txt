Abowl of sweetcorn(in game name "Sweetcorn") is bothfoodand a cooking ingredient. It can be made by using abowlon acooked sweetcorn; this does not require aknife, nor does it provide anyCookingexperience. Asfood, when eaten, it provides healing identical tocooked sweetcorn, namely 1+10% of total hitpoints, rounded down. A bowl ofsweetcornis called "Sweetcorn" in-game. This may cause confusion among players asraw sweetcornhas the same in-game name.
