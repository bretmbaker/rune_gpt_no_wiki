Anensouled giant headis anensouled headdropped bygiants. It is used to gainPrayerexperience by using the level 41MagicspellAdept Reanimationfrom theArceuus spellbook. When a player reanimates an ensouled giant head, areanimated giantwill appear and grant 650 Prayer experience after being killed. Killing this giant will only count towards theHill GiantSlayer task.
