Salmonis a type of fish that restores 9Hitpointswhen eaten. It can be obtained by cookingraw salmonon afireorcooking range, requiring level 25Cookingand granting 90experiencewhen successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting in aburnt fish. Theburn ratedecreases as the player's Cooking level increases. Salmon is one of a number of foods with a reduced burn rate while using theLumbridge Castle rangewithCook's Assistantcompleted. It will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 58, or 55 when using the Lumbridge range.
