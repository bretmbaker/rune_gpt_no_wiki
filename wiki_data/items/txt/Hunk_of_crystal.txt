Thehunk of crystalis aquest itemused during theLegends' Quest. It is obtained by killing one of three knightsRanalph Devere,Irvig SenayandSan Tojalonin the dungeon under theKharazi Jungle. When combined with thechunk of crystaland thelump of crystal(obtained by killing the other two knights) they form theheart crystalrequired to unlock a door that leads further into the dungeon.
