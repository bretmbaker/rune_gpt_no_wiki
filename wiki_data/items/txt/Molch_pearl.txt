Molch pearlsare a stackable item which can be obtained whileAerial fishing. Each catch has a 1/100 to 1/75 chance of giving Molch pearls in addition to the player's catch, which scales based on the player'sFishingandHunterlevels. Players can also exchange agolden tenchwithAlry the Anglerfor 100 Molch pearls. They can be stored in atackle box.
