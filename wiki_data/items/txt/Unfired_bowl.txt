Anunfired bowlis made by usingsoft clayon apotter's wheelwhich requires 8Craftingand yields 18 Crafting experience. The unfired bowl can be fired in apottery ovento produce abowl, granting 15 Crafting experience upon success. Unlike shaping the clay, firing it has no Crafting level requirement.
