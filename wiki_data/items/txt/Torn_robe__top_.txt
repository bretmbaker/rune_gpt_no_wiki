Torn robesare aquest itemin theSpirits of the Elidquest. They are found by searching the cupboards in theElidinis Statuettebuilding inNardah. They must be repaired with aneedleandthreadto make theRobe of elidinis, which the player must wear to enter theWater Ravine Dungeonfarther north along theRiver Elid. Thecostume needlewill not work for this.
