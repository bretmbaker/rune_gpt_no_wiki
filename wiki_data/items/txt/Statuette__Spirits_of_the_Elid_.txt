TheStatuetteis an icon of thegoddessElidinisand is obtained during theSpirits of the Elidquest. The statuette is said to have been made by Elidinis herself and was given toNardarine, future founder ofNardahwho swore loyalty to Elidinis, after Elidinis offered to save her from the desert heat.
The statuette must be retrieved from the evilgeniein thecreviceto the west ofNardah. The genie will give the player the statuette if they present him with thesoleof the mayor of Nardah.
