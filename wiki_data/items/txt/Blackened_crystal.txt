Theblackened crystalis aquest itemused in theMourning's End Part IIquest. It is obtained by using achiselon a large black rock in theTemple of Lightat the start of the quest. The player must give the crystal toArianwyn, who will then turn it into anewly made crystal, which is needed to complete the final part of the quest. If a player has lost their blackened crystal, they must carve a new piece from the giant black crystal in order to get it back.
