Forinthry sigilswere an item that could be dropped byCallisto,Venenatis, andVet'ion, along with their weaker versions (Artio,Spindel, andCalvar'ion), at a rate of 1/24. To be eligible to receive them, players must have talked toSir Pear Visorin the centre of theOld School Museumbeneath theFalador Party Room, and have agreed to help him. After agreeing to help him, a message appeared in the player's chatbox stating:You have enabled additional drops from activities across the game. To disable these speak to Sir Pear Visor in the Falador museum.
