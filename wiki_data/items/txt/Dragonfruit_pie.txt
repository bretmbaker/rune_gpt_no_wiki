Dragonfruit pieis a type ofpiethat restores 20Hitpointsin total, 2 bites each healing for 10. It alsotemporarily boostsFletchingby 4, the second highest boost available, and the highest reliable boost. A pie dish will remain after both bites are consumed. It can be obtained by baking auncooked dragonfruit pieon acooking range, requiring level 73Cookingand granting 220 experience when successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting inburnt pie. Theburn ratedecreases as the player'sCookinglevel increases. Dragonfruit pies never stop burning altogether even at level 99, unless the player is wearing aCooking capeor using theHosidius kitchen. Alternatively, casting theBake Piespell from thelunar spellbookwill always create a pie without burning it.
