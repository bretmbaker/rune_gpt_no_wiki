Astuffed snakeis made during theFreeing King Awowogeisubquest ofRecipe for Disaster, and requires level 70Cookingto make. It is made from stuffing asnake corpse(obtained by killing aBig Snake) with a slicedred bananaandtchiki nut pasteto obtain araw stuffed snakeand then cooked on the snake-shaped rock in dungeon belowTemple of Marimbo. A stuffed snake heals 20Hitpoints. After completion of Freeing King Awowogei, players can no longer procure the ingredients for a stuffed snake, but can cook more from those left over from the quest.
