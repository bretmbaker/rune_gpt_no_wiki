Abird nestwith an egg can be found while woodcutting most types oftreesand several other activities. Bird nests contain one of a red, blue, or greenbird's egg, and also provide anempty nest. The chance of getting a bird nest from a tree is 1/256 each time you would normally get a log, regardless of the type of tree. If a nest appears while a player is cutting a tree, a chirping sound will usually be heard, and the player will get a message in the chat box stating, "A bird's nest falls out of the tree." The nest is dropped on the ground and will despawn after 2 minutes if not picked up.Redwood treesdo not drop egg nests, and instead only dropclue nests.
