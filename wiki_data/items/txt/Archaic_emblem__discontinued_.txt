TheArchaic emblemsare former discontinued items that could be obtained as a reward fromBounty Hunter. Originally, the Tier 1 and 10Mysterious emblemsreceived were tradeable and could be turned into banknotes. In 2019, Bounty Hunter was reworked; the Mysterious emblems were converted intoArchaic emblemsand made untradeable. Any player who had one in banknote form was given this unique discontinued variant; it was identical to the real Archaic emblems, except stackable.
