Ground bat bonesis an item used in theWatchtowerquestand in theHerbloreskill. They are made by using apestle and mortaronbat bones. Provided that the player has level 14Herblore, when combined with aGuam potion (unf)it will explode (dealing 5 damage), and will leave the emptyvialonly. Should the player have their hitpoints less than 8, it will not kill them and will deal less than 5 damage. Additionally, when the item is combined with a vial of water, the water will evaporate followed by a dialogue box with the player saying, "Oh dear,  the mixture has evaporated." This also leaves an empty vial. The item can be created before, during, and after the quest; however, it serves no purpose outside of the quest.
