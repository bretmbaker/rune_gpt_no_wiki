Thecave goblin skullis an item on theOdd Old Man's wish list duringRag and Bone Man II. It isonlydropped bycave goblinsin theLumbridge Swamp CavesandDorgesh-Kaan mine. The bone must be put into apot of vinegarand boiled to create apolished cave goblin skullbefore giving it to the Odd Old Man. After the quest is completed you can no longer obtain this as a drop.
