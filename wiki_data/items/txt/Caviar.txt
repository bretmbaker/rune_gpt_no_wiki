Caviaris a food and enhancing ingredient used in the Barbarian Herblore to makeBarbarian mixes. Caviar can be used to mix with all potions, as opposed toroe, which cannot be used with potions fromEnergyand up. It can also be eaten on its own to heal 5Hitpoints. It can be obtained when players cut open theleaping sturgeon, obtained throughBarbarian Fishing, with a knife, granting 15Cookingexperience. The chance of receiving caviar from leaping sturgeon scales with Cooking level, with a 1/80 chance at level 1 Cooking, and a 100% chance at level 80.
