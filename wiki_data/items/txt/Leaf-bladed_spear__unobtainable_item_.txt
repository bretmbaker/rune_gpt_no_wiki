ALeaf-bladed spearis an unobtainable version of theLeaf-bladed spear. Spears could previously be traded and turned into bank notes; however, they were made untradeable in 2007 due to frequent use in scams, where players conflated them withGuthan's warspear. Any player who had a noted leaf-bladed spear was given this now discontinued item. Originally, it was identical to the real spear, except stackable.
