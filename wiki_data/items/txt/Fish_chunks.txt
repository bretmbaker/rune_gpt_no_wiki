Fish chunksare a product of cutting open aBluegill,Common tench,Mottled eel, orGreater sirenusing aknife. They can be stored in atackle box. Fish chunks can be used as a substitute forKing wormswhen feeding thecormorantinaerial fishing. Cutting the fish has no skill requirements, but provides a small amount ofCookingexperience. When catching fish, there is a 50% chance for a fish chunk to be consumed after every third catch. If it is not consumed on the third, it is guaranteed to be consumed on the fourth, meaning each chunk is consumed every 3.5 catches on average.
