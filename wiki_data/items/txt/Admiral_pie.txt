Admiral pieis a type ofpiethat restores 16Hitpointsin total, 2 bites each healing for 8. It alsotemporarily boostsFishingby 5, the highest available other than the invisible boost active within theFishing Guild. A pie dish will remain after both bites are consumed. It can be obtained by baking araw admiral pieon acooking range, requiring level 70Cookingand granting 210 experience when successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting inburnt pie. Theburn ratedecreases as the player'sCookinglevel increases, and it will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 94. Alternatively, casting theBake Piespell from thelunar spellbookwill always create a pie without burning it.
