Agrape seedcan be planted in avine patchin order to grow agrape vinewhich will producegrapes. It requires 1 grape seed per patch, and there are 12 patches available at the vinery, thus requiring 12 grape seeds to fill up all the patches. Planting the seeds requires aFarminglevel of 36 to do so. It can be obtained from theTithe Farmfor 2 points per seed or by trading other players.
