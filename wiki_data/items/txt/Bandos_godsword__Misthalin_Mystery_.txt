TheBandos godswordis anunobtainableweapon that appears during theMisthalin Mysteryquest. It is modeled after theBandos godsword, but unlike the regular version of the item, the one during the quest only serves as an unobtainable decoration. It was added in case players were ever able to obtain the godsword using glitches, and it is in unequipable, untradeable and it has an alchemy value of 0 coins, rendering this item useless in the hands of any potential player that might obtain it.
