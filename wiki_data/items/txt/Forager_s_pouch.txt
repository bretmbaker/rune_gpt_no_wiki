Theforager's pouchis an item that was obtainable during theRaging Echoes League, by unlocking theFriendly Foragerrelic. It is equipable in theshield slotand can hold up to 30 of the standard grimy herbs, very similar to that of theherb sack. If players destroy their pouch whilst a grimy herb is still inside the pouch, the player can retrieve another pouch fromThe Sageand any grimy herbs that were in the pouch before will still appear inside.
