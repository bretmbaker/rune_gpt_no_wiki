ThisKeyis used duringThe Lost Tribequest. It is obtained bypickpocketingSigmund. It can be used to open the chest in the room next toDuke Horacio's room, and disappears once the chest is unlocked. The chest is Sigmund's and containsH.A.M clothing, revealing that he is aHumans Against Monstersmember. If you search the chest after receiving the clothing, you also get a 1-doseAntipoison.
