Chaos coresare the corrupted cores ofChaos Golems, imbued with the power of theChaos Altar. They can be used in place ofpure essence, but only for crafting chaos runes. They act as if the player was carrying eleven essence instead of one, meaning each core gives 85Runecraftexperience and 11chaos runesor 2,380Runecraftexperience and 308chaos runesfor a complete inventory.
