Thepressure gaugeis aquest itemused only inErnest the Chicken. It is found submerged in the waters of the piranha-infestedfountainin the south-western area ofDraynor Manor. To get this item, the player must kill the piranhas usingpoisoned fish food. If the player does not use poisoned fish food, the piranhas will deal 1 hitpoint of damage.
