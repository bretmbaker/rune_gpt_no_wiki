TheCriminal's daggeris a questitemthat is found at the murder scene during theMurder Mysteryquest. This dagger cannot be equipped and is used to help gather evidence against the culprit during the quest. In the quest, you must use apot of flouron the dagger to dust it, then useflypaperon the dagger to get theunknown print. You must do the same with the items belonging to the suspects. Then compare them by using a suspect's fingerprints on the criminal's fingerprint to get thekiller's print.
