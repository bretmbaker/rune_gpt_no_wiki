AGold amulet (u)is made with theCraftingskill by using agold baron afurnacewhile carrying anamulet mould. Following that, it can be strung with aball of wool, resulting in astrung gold amulet. Making a gold amulet (u) requires level 8 Crafting and players gain 30 experience for making the amulet, and then 4 experience for stringing it.
