Crystal chunkswere an item that could be received from any activity that gives the playercrystal shards. To be eligible to receive them, players must have talked toSir Pear Visorin the centre of theOld School Museumbeneath theFalador Party Room, and have agreed to help him. After agreeing to help him, a message appeared in the player's chatbox stating:You have enabled additional drops from activities across the game. To disable these speak to Sir Pear Visor in the Falador museum.
