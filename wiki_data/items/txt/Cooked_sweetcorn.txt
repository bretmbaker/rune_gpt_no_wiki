Cooked sweetcornis a type offoodthat can be obtained by cookingsweetcornon afireorcooking range, requiring level 28Cookingand granting 104 experience when successful. Players may burn the sweetcorn while cooking one, resulting inburnt sweetcorn; theburn ratewhile cooking these will decrease as players reach higher Cooking levels. It will stop burning entirely atCookinglevel 54 on both ranges and fires. When eaten it heals 1+10% of total hitpoints, rounded down.
