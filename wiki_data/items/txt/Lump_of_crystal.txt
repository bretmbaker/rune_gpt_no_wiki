Thelump of crystalis a quest item used during theLegends' Quest. It is obtained by killing one of three knightsRanalph Devere,Irvig SenayandSan Tojalonin the dungeon underKharazi Jungle. When used with thechunk of crystaland thehunk of crystal(obtained by killing the other two knights) they form theheart crystalrequired to unlock a door that leads further into the dungeon to continue further on in the quest.
