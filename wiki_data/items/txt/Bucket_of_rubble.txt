Thebucket of rubbleis a quest item made by the player during theIn Aid of the Myrequequest. It is made by mining the rubble using apickaxein the basement of the bar inBurgh de Rott, and then using a spade or a pot on the remaining bits of rubble with a bucket in the player's inventory. To empty it, players either have to empty it next to the rubble pile outside the bar, or anywhere outside Burgh de Rott. It has no use after the quest.
