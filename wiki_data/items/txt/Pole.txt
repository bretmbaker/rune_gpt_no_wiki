Apoleis aquest itemused inMountain Daughter. It can be found in the goat's pen in theMountain Camp, east ofRellekka. It is used to catapult the player across the stones in the middle of the lake near the Mountain Camp; however, any staff will work for this as well. There is also aBroken polefound on the south-western side of the lake which cannot be repaired and has no known use.
