Like ordinarypineapples, it has an 'Eat' option, but cannot be consumed whole, and it is possible to use aknifeon it, giving the option to cut it intopineapple ringsorpineapple chunks. It can also be placed in acompost binto createsupercompost. If it is lost, it can be re-obtained fromAl Shabimuntil The Tourist Trap is completed.
