Thesunlight spearis a two-handed weapon that was dropped from the Echo variant ofSol Hereditduring theRaging Echoes League. Every time the player attacks using the spear, they will gain a "Sunlight Stack", which can be stacked up to 20. Players will lose a  Sunlight Stack if they have not attacked for 50 ticks (30 seconds). Additionally, teleporting will reset the Sunlight Stacks to zero.
