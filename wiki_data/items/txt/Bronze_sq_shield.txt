TheBronze sq shieldis the lowest level defencesquare shieldinOld School RuneScape. It can be equipped to give a player better defence bonuses in combat. Its recommended use is inmeleecombat. Players can make a bronze square shield with 2bronze barsat level 8Smithing, granting 25 Smithingexperience. It can also be bought fromCassie's Shield ShopinFalador.
