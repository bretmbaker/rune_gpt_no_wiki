Movario's notes (volume 2)are the second half ofMovario's documentation of his search for theStone of Jas. In them, he details how Lucien took interest in his search, detailing the events of theTemple of Ikovquest. He then mentions laying a false path toDraynor Villagefor Lucien's spies, as well as learning that theTemple of the Eyeonce held the Eye of Saradomin. He concludes by realizing the full power of the stone.
