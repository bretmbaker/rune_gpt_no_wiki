Thegold helmetis ahelmetthe player must smith during theBetween a Rock...quest using 3gold bars. It requires level 30Defenceto wear. Smithing the helmet requires 50Smithingand gives 30Smithingexperience. More than one helmet can be made by using thedrop trickand castingTelekinetic Grabon any helmets dropped on the ground. The helmet must be worn to useDondakan's cannon and to enter the massiveArzinian Mines, found in the mines south ofKeldagrim.
