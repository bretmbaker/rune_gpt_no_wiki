Urt saltis a type ofsaltthat can be mined in theSalt MineunderneathWeissafter the completion ofMaking Friends with My Arm. Along withTe saltandEfh salt, it can be used to lightfire pitsthat provide benefits (such as a permanentlight source), and it is also used to createstony basalts. Mining Urt salt requires 72Miningand is mined in chunks of 2-7 scaling with the player's level, granting the player 5 experience per salt mined.Mining enhancersdonotwork here.
