Thespinning plateis a toy bought fromDiangoinDraynor Village. It is a white plate with a red picture of a dragon on it. The player can click on the plate to have their character spin the plate on a stick. This has no in-game effect and is purely cosmetic. The spinning plate is one of the items theRowdy Dwarfcan request from the player duringForgettable Tale of a Drunken Dwarf.
