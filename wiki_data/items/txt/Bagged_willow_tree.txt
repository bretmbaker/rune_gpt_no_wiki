Abagged willow treecan be bought inFaladoror theFarming Guildfrom aGarden supplierfor 10,000 coins. They can be planted in thegardenof aplayer-owned house, requiring aConstructionlevel of 30, providing 100 experience to both Construction andFarming. The player must have a filledwatering canin their inventory to plant it. This tree is only for decoration and cannot be cut for logs.
