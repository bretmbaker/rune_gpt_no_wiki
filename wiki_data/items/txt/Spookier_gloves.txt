Thespookier glovesare a cosmetic reward from the2019 Halloween event. They are part of thespookier outfit, and can be obtained as a reward from the Halloween events that occur once per year. They are unlocked by using thespooky gloveson theswampy sinkoutside the toolshed in theLumbridge Swamp, or on theEctofuntusoutsidePort Phasmatys.
