Karambwanji pasteis made by grinding upkarambwanjiwith apestle and mortar. DuringTai Bwo Wannai Triothe paste must be used withburnt jogre bonesto createpasty jogre bones (burnt raw). The pasty bones will then be cooked intomarinated j' bonesand given toTinsay. The karambwanji paste can incorrectly be used withjogre bonesthat have not been burnt, which will create a useless version of thepasty jogre bones.
