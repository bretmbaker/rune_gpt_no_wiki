Bronze key blackis a key and reward from theShades of Mort'tonminigame. To obtain it, the player must kill aPhrin Shadeand then burn itsremainson a pyre. Burning aLoar Shadewill never generate this key as a reward. The key may be used to enter theShade Catacombsand then open thecorrectly coloured chest. It will open a bronze-framed chest with a black lock, however it can only be used to open bronze doors within the tomb.
