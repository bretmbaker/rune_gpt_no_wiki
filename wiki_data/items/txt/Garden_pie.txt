Garden pieis a type ofpiethat restores 12Hitpointsin total, 2 bites each healing for 6. It alsotemporarily boostsFarmingby 3. A pie dish will remain after both bites are consumed. It can be obtained by baking araw garden pieon acooking range, requiring level 34Cookingand granting 138 experience when successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting inburnt pie. Theburn ratedecreases as the player'sCookinglevel increases, and it will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 68. Alternatively, casting theBake Piespell from thelunar spellbookwill always create a pie without burning it.
