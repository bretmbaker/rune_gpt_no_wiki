Theconsecration seedis an item used to perform an elven consecration on the tomb ofGlarialin theRoving Elvesquest. The item can be obtained by killing one of theMoss GuardiansinGlarial's Tomb.Elunedcan enchant the seed to prepare it for the consecration. If you lose the seed during the quest, just go back to Eluned and you will get a new consecration seed.
