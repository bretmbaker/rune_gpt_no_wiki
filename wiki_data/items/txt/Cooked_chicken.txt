Cooked chickenis a type offoodthat restores 3Hitpointswhen eaten. It can be obtained by cookingraw chickenon afireorcooking range, granting 30experiencewhen successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting inburnt chicken. Theburn ratedecreases as the player'sCookinglevel increases. Cooked chicken is one of a number of foods with a reduced burn rate while using theLumbridge Castle rangewithCook's Assistantcompleted. It will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 34, or 31 when using the Lumbridge range.
