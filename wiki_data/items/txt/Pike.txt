Pikeis a type of fish that restores 8Hitpointswhen eaten. It can be obtained by cookingraw pikeon afireorcooking range, requiring level 20Cookingand granting 80experiencewhen successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting in aburnt pike. Theburn ratedecreases as the player's Cooking level increases. Pike is one of a number of foods with a reduced burn rate while using theLumbridge Castle rangewithCook's Assistantcompleted. It will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 54, or 49 when using the Lumbridge range.
