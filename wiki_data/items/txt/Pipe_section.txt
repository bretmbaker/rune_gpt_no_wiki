Thepipe sectionis an item used in theTrouble Brewingminigame to repair sabotagedbamboo pipesand water pumps. In the beginning of the game some pipes are set on the supply table. This item can also be made by using a regularlogon a workbench.Logscan be gathered from the log store (also located within your base) or by chopping down the varioustreesaround your base with anaxe. Creating a bamboo pipe rewards 12.5 crafting experience, and actually using it rewards the same amount.
