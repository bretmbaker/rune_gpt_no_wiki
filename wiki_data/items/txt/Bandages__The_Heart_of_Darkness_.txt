Bandagesare used to heal the player andPrince Itzla Arkanduring the second trial in the quest,The Heart of Darkness. These bandages can be taken out of the fivemedical boxesthat contain five bandages each. When choosing the 'Heal' option on Prince Itzla Arkan, he cannot take any of the player's food, thus it is important to have enough inventory space to hold some bandages.
