Awhite apronis a piece of clothing that gives no stat bonuses and is largely worn for fashion. It is used in thePirate's TreasureandMourning's End Part IIquest. A white apron respawns inGerrant's Fishy Businessevery 10 ticks, and another spawns inWydin's Food StoreinPort Sarim, though players must be wearing a white apron to grab the one in Wydin's Grocery.
