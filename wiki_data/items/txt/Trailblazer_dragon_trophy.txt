TheTrailblazer dragon trophyis a cosmetic reward that can be obtained by being in the top 1% of players in the Leagues Hiscores by the end of theTrailblazer League (2020), which required having at least 56,310 League points to do so. Consequently, this means there were 2,842 players eligible for the trophy. When equipped, the trophy can be right-clicked to perform the Trophy Emote.
