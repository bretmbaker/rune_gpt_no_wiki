Themaple blackjack(d)is a weapon mainly used to lure/knockout NPCs. It is the best defensive blackjack available. 30 Defence and 30 Thieving are required to equip and use it. To obtain one, the player must have completed the blackjack section of theRogue Traderminigame. When the blackjacks are set to 'defensive', they can be purchased fromAli Morrisane.
