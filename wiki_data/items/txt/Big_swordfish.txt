Abig swordfishis an abnormally largeswordfishcaught while fishing for swordfish at anycage/harpoon fishing spot, requiring 50Fishingto catch (or 70 Fishing and 50Strengthforbarehanded fishing). Players have a 1/2,500 chance to receive a big swordfish in place of a raw swordfish;catching one results in the game messageYou catch an enormous swordfish!instead of the usualYou catch a swordfish.If fishing with afish barrel, a big swordfish will be placed into the inventory.Additionally, big swordfish cannot be cooked.
