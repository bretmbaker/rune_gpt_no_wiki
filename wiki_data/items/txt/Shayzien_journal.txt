TheShayzien journalis an old journal written by King Shayzien VII, who ruledGreat Kourendfrom the 32nd to 37th decade. Recovered from theTower of Magicduring the uprising againstXericat the end of theAge of Strife, it was sent via parcel by an unknown party toPhileas Rimor's house, and kickstarts the events of thequestTale of the Righteous.
