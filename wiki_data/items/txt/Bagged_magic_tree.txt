Abagged magic treecan be bought from aGarden supplierinFaladoror theFarming Guildfor 50,000coins. They can be planted in thegardenof aplayer-owned housewith aConstructionlevel of 75, providing 223 experience to both Construction andFarming. The player must have a filledwatering canin their inventory to plant it. Players can temporarily boost their construction level from 72 by using the tea obtained from teak shelves 2. This tree is only for decoration and cannot be cut for logs.
