Thescorpion cageis an item obtained fromThormacat the beginning ofScorpion Catcher. This item is used in the quest to capture the scorpions which are obtainable from:Taverley Dungeon,Barbarian OutpostandEdgeville Monastery. According to Thormac, these scorpions are rare. If you lose the cage, you can get another by talking to Thormac again.
