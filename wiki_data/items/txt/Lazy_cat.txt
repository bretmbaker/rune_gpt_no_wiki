Alazy catis a special variant of a petcat, which players gain access to by completing theRatcatchersquest. After completion, players can speak toFelkrashto have her train their overgrown cat into awily cat. After a random amount of time, usually under an hour, regardless of the amount of interactions performed, a wily cat will become a lazy cat. Lazy cats can only catch rats, and have around a 41% success rate.
