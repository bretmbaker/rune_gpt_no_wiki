ASteel baris an item used for creating steelweaponryandarmour. It can be smelted using theSmithingskill at level 30 Smithing by using 1iron oreand 2coalon afurnace, providing 17.5experiencepoints. Steel bars can be used with an anvil to makesteel armourandweaponry, granting 37.5 Smithing experience per bar, unless the bar is used with a furnace andcannonball mouldto makecannonballs, granting 25.6 experience per bar instead, which is profitable experience popular among low and mid-level smiths. Steel bars are also used in theMahogany Homesminigame.
