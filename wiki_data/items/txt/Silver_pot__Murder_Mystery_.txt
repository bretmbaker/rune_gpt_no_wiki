Thesilver potis a quest item used in theMurder Mysteryquest. It is obtained from a barrel inFrank's bedroom in theSinclair Mansion. It has Frank's fingerprints on it and is used to determine if Frank is the murderer. If it is used on the flour barrel, it will create a dusted silver pot, andflypaperis used on it, it will createFrank's print, used to see if Frank is the killer.
