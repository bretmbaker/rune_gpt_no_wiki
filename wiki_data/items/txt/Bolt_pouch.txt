Abolt pouchallows a player to carry multiple kinds ofboltsin one inventory slot, includingbolt racks, as well asmith grapples, and swap between two types of bolts mid-combat. It can be purchased for1,500fromHirko, who runs thecrossbow shopinKeldagrimon the east side of the river. Players must ask Hirko about ammo in order to be given the option to purchase a pouch; it cannot be bought in Hirko's normal shop.
