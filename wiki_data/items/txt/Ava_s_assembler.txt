Ava's assembleris the upgraded version ofAva's accumulatorthat requires level 70Rangedto equip. The assembler gives the second best Ranged attack bonus for thecape slotand is one of the few cape slot items that provides aRanged Strengthbonus. To obtain the assembler, players must take 75mithril arrows,Ava's accumulator(or 4,999 coins), andVorkath's head(which can only be obtained by killingVorkathafter completion ofDragon Slayer II) toAvainDraynor Manor.
