Ugthanki dungis an unobtainable variant of the regularUgthanki dung. Occasionally,Ali the Camelwill produce a green dung afterred hot sauceis poured into his trough. However, the player cannot use the spade to pick it up unlike the normal dung; attempting to do so results in the player stating"I wouldn't pick that up for all the runes in Gielinor!"
