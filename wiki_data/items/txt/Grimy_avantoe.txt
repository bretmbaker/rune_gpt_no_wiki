Agrimy avantoeis a herb that has not yet been cleaned to make anavantoe. Grimy avantoes may be grown from anavantoe seedwith aFarminglevel of 50. It is also dropped by certainNPCs. Cleaning a grimy avantoe requires aHerblorelevel of 48 and gives 10 Herblore experience. Clean avantoes are used to makefishing potions,super energy potionsandhunter potions, requiring levels 50, 52, and 53 Herblore respectively.
