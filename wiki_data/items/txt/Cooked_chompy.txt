Cooked chompyis an ogre delicacy obtained by skewering araw chompywith aniron spit, then cooking theskewered chompyon a fire, requiring level 30Cookingand granting 100 experience when successful. Players may burn the chompy while cooking one, resulting in aruined chompy; theburn ratewhile cooking these will decrease as players reach higher Cooking levels. It will stop burning entirely atCookinglevel 99.
