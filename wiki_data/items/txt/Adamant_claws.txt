Adamant clawsare a two-handed melee weapon. They can be made with twoadamantite barsand a hammer at any anvil granting 125Smithingexperience. They can only be made by players who have completed theDeath Plateauquest and have a Smithing level of at least 83. They have anAttackrequirement of 30 to wield. They are not a very popular weapon due to the fact they require 2 hands to wield and provide inferior stats to that of theadamant scimitar.
