Codis a type of fish that restores 7Hitpointswhen eaten. It can be obtained by cookingraw codon afireorcooking range, requiring level 18Cookingand granting 75experiencewhen successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting in aburnt fish. Theburn ratedecreases as the player's Cooking level increases. Cod is one of a number of foods with a reduced burn rate while using theLumbridge Castle rangewithCook's Assistantcompleted. It will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 51 on a fire, 49 on a range, or 46 when using the Lumbridge range.
