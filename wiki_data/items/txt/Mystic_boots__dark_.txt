Mystic boots (dark)are an item part of theMystic robes setworn in the feet slot. They are a type of foot armour for mages. It comes in blue, white and black, but all offer the same bonuses. All mystic items require 40Magicand 20Defenceto wear. The dark mystic boots can be bought from other players or rarely dropped byInfernal Mages, which require 45 Slayer to kill. It can be stored in amagic wardrobein a player-owned house.
