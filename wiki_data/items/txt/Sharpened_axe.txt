Thesharpened axeis a quest item used in theOne Small Favourquest. It is obtained by giving theblunt axetoBrianinPort Sarimafter helping his friendAggiefree her apprentice,Jimmy the Chisel. The player must take the sharpened axe back to the forester south-east ofShilo Villagein order to proceed in the quest. Brian, unlike many other people in the quest whom you must do favours for, will be happy to supply you with another axe without charge should you lose the first one.
