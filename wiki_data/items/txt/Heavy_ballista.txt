Theheavy ballistais a two-handed ranged weapon that usesjavelinsas ammunition, requiring level 75 inRangedand the completion of the questMonkey Madness IIto wield. Players must also accept bothDukeandDaero's training in order to make use of it, unlike thelight ballista. While there is no direct Defence requirement for the heavy ballista, completing the required training from Daero afterMonkey Madness Iwill provide a minimum of 20,000 Defence experience. This corresponds to an effective level requirement of 33 Defence.
