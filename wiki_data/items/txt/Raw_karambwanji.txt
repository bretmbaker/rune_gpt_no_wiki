ARaw karambwanjiis a brightly coloured fish used as bait to catchraw karambwan. Players must load a raw karambwanji into akarambwan vesseland then use the vessel with one of the karambwan fishing locations in the north-east ofKaramjato fish karambwan. They cannot be stored in afish barrel. They can, however, be stored in atackle box, as they are considered bait.
