Ateapot with leavesis an item used tomake teain thekitchenof aplayer-owned houseallowing the player to temporarily boostConstructionby up to 3 levels. A player can obtain this item by using ateapotwithtea leaves. Requiring aCookinglevel of 20, players can use this item on ahot kettle, producing apot of teaand gaining 52 experience. The contents can then be poured into up to 4 emptyclay,porcelain, ortrimmed porcelain cups.
