A jug of wineis a type ofdrinkthat restores 11Hitpointsand temporarily lowersAttackby 2, leaving behind an emptyjugafter consumption. It can be obtained by usinggrapeswith ajug of water, requiring level 35Cooking.The process of fermenting the jug of wine takes 12 seconds and grants 200 Cookingexperience; this timer resets each time another grape is added to another jug of water. There is also a chance to fail, resulting in ajug of bad wine. The fail rate decreases as the player's Cooking level increases, and it will stop failing entirely at Cooking level 68.
