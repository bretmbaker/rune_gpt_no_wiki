Elemental oreis an ore that may only be obtained by killing a level 35earth elementalafter trying to mine theelemental rock. This ore can only be obtained during and after theElemental Workshop Iquest, and turned intoelemental metals. Elemental ore is only found in theElemental Workshop dungeonbeneath theSeers' Village, in the house with theanvil, next to the bank. Earth elementals are found in the western area on the first level of the workshop.
