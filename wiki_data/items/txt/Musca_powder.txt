Musca powderis the result of using apestle and mortaron amusca mushroom,  where between five and six powders are given per mushroom with the amount being tied to yourHerblorelevel. The amount of powder produced is based on the equation⌊level−2010⌋, and is capped between 1 and 6 powders. Due to the required 62 Herblore forDesert Treasure II, the only amounts players will normally see are 4, 5, and 6 powders. Six are mixed in the asylum'sFermentation Vat, alongside sixArder powderand sixSalax salt, to create oneArder-musca poison, which are used to wakeDuke Sucellus.
