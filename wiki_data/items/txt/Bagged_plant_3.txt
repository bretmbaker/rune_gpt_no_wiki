Bagged plant 3is a type of bagged plant which may be bought inFaladoror theFarming Guildfrom aGarden supplierfor 10,000 coins. This type of bagged plant can be planted in thegardenof aplayer-owned house, requiring aConstructionlevel of 12, providing 100 experience to both Construction andFarming. The player must have a filledwatering canin their inventory to plant it. It is only for decoration.
