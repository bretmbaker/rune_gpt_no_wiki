Thegolden bowlis a quest item used during theLegends' Quest. It is made by first smithing agold bowlfrom 2gold bars, granting 30 Smithing exp, and then giving it toGujuoin theKharazi Jungleto bless it, making it into ablessed gold bowl. The player must then head to the pond in the middle of the jungle, cut ahollow reedby using amacheteon the tall reeds next to the pond, and use the reed on the pond to turn the bowl into a golden bowl containing sacred pure water.
