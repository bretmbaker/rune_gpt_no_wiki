Thejail keyis dropped by theJailer, found in theBlack Knights' Base, deep withinTaverley Dungeon. The key gives access to the jail cell containingVelrak the explorer, who will in turn give players theDusty keywhich may be used to travel deeper into the dungeon. It can also be used to unlock the door that holds twodwarves, opposite toVelrak the explorer's cell.
