Thesteel dart tipis adart tipmade with theSmithingskill. Players can craft this item when using asteel baron ananvilwith ahammerin theirinventoryand a Smithing level of 34. Smithing one set of 10 dart tips will provide the player with 37.5 Smithing experience. They can be fletched intosteel dartsat level 37Fletchingwhen used withfeathers, granting 75 Fletching experience per 10 darts made.
