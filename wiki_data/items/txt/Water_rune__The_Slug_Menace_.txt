Awater runeis a special type ofwater runeused duringThe Slug Menace. It is made by using ablank water runeon theWater Altarin the normal way and is used to unlock a mysterious door in the newly discovered shrine beneathWitchaven. Sometimes the blank water rune can break while trying to fuse it with the Water Altar. In order to unlock the door the player must also have made aspecial air rune, aspecial earth rune, aspecial fire rune, and aspecial mind rune. Unlike normal water runes, this cannot be used to cast a spell.
