Agrimy lantadymeis aherbthat has not yet been cleaned to make alantadyme. Grimy lantadymes may be grown from alantadyme seedwith aFarminglevel of 73. It is also dropped by certainNPCs. Cleaning a grimy lantadyme requires aHerblorelevel of 67 and gives 13.1 Herblore experience. Clean lantadymes are used to makeantifirepotions andMagic potionwith a Herblore level of 69 and 76 respectively. When planting and harvesting lantadymes you will receive 134 experience for planting, and 151.5 experience for harvesting.
