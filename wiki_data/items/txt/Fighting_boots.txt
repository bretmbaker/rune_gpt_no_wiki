Fighting bootsare a pair of low level boots with stats identical tobronze boots(except weighing significantly less),fancy bootsandfancier boots. They are obtained from theCradle of Lifeon the fourth level of theStronghold of Securitydungeon provided the player's account has two-factor authentication enabled. They are used by new orfree-to-playplayers, as the centre of each level rewards a combined total of10,000coinsalong with the choice between fancy boots, fighting boots or fancier boots (at the fourth and final level).
