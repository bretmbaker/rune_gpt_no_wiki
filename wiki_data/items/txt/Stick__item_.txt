Thestickis an item that must be retrieved in theWerewolf Agility Course. Players have to go through a series of obstacles to reach the stick which has been thrown by theAgility Boss. Once obtained, players must complete the course to be able to give the stick to theAgility Trainerand start the process again. Retrieving the stick grants the player 380Agilityexperience.Note:If the player is holding multiple sticks, handing it in will result inALLthe sticks in the player's inventory being exchanged for the same amount of experience as only 1 stick.
