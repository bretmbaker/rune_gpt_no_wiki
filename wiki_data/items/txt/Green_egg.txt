Agreen eggis used inBarbarian Assaultto damage penance monsters. If loaded into theegg launcherand shot at penance creatures, it will deal 1 damage and poison the target. If shot at aPenance Runner,Penance Fighter, orPenance Ranger, the poison will hit every 3 seconds a total of 5 times unless healed. If shot at aPenance Healer, the poison will hit every 18 seconds a total of 5 times. Green egg poison is in addition to poisoned food damage, and will not reset, reduce, or delay damage. A maximum of five green eggs may be loaded into the egg launcher at any given time. Both egg launchers share the same reserve of eggs.
