Green dragon leatheris used in theCraftingskill to makegreen dragonhide armour. It can be created by giving agreen dragonhideto atanner. Tanners can be found inAl Kharid, theRanging Guild,Forthos Dungeon,Canifisand theCrafting Guild. Alternatively, green dragon leather can be made using theTan Leather spell. Crafting any piece of green dragonhide armour will grant the player 62 experience per hide.
