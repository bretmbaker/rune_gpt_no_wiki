Mature greenman's aleis a type ofalethat restores 2Hitpointsandtemporarily boostsHerbloreby 2, while alsodraining2AttackandStrengthwhen drunk. It can be obtained throughbrewingat level 29Cooking, granting 281 experience per batch. It is the stronger, or 'mature' version of agreenman's ale, with a  5% chance (increased to 64% with "the stuff") for your batch to become matured during brewing.
