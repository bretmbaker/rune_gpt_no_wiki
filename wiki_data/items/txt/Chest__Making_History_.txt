Thechestis aquest itemused duringMaking History. To obtain the chest, a player must rub anenchanted keyto find the location where the chest is buried, which is an area south of theObservatoryand north ofCastle Wars. The key will be steaming hot when the player is standing on the spot where the buried chest lies. Once the player has dug up the chest with aspade, the key is used on the chest to open it and getDrozal's journal.
