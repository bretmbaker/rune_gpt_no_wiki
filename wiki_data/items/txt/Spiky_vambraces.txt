Spiky vambracesareleather armourworn on the forearms, most commonly by low-level warriors, and often rangers (as there is no magic penalty), in place ofleather vambraces. They have the same stats as leather vambraces, except for a +2Strengthbonus. To make spiky vambraces, usekebbit clawson leather vambraces. This grants 6Craftingexperience. Level 32 Crafting is required to make any vambraces into spiked vambraces.
