Atorn enhanced quetzal whistle blueprintwill drop at 100 completed rumours if the player has not already received one. Note that certain drops scale with certain skill levels such as Woodcutting; players with a higher Woodcutting level have a better chance of obtaining a higher tier log. However, it is possible (though very unlikely) for a player with a low skill level to obtain such items (i.e. a player with Woodcutting level 30 to receive aYew log.)
