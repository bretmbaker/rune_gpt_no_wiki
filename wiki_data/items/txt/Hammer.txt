Ahammeris a tool used for smithing items on anAnvil, and it is vital to level theSmithingskill. Hammers can be bought from mostgeneral storesand will never break. Alternatively, players can obtain a hammer from talking to toMaster smithing tutorat the WestVarrockanvil.  A hammer is also used in theConstructionskill when making furniture, and it is required for some minigames.  At 15 Construction, members can make aTool store 1for the workshop room of their house; this tool store provides an unlimited supply of hammers.
