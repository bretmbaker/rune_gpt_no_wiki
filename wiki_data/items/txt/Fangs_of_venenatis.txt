Thefangs of Venenatisare an item dropped byVenenatis. They are used to upgradeCraw's bowinto awebweaver bow. This process requires level 85Fletching; players without the required level to combine the items may askDerse VenatorinFerox Enclaveto do so instead, for a fee of500,000 coins. The uncharged weapon may be dismantled to separate the bow and fangs.
