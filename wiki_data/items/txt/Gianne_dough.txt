Gianne doughis an ingredient ingnome cookingused to makegnomebowls,gnome crunchies, andgnome battas. It can be bought fromHudoin theGrand Treeor looted fromgnomes. Gianne dough cannot be cooked by itself; instead, players must use the dough on acrunchy tray,batta tin, orgnomebowl mouldand then precook it on arange. It was probably named afterAluft Gianne snr., the gnome cook. 100 Gianne dough is also given as a reward for completing theGnome Restauranttraining.
