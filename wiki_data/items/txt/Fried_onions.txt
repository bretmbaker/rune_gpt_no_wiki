Fried onionsare a type offoodthat can be obtained by cookingchopped onionson afireorcooking range, requiring level 42Cookingand granting 60 experience when successful. Players may burn the onions while cooking one, resulting in aburnt onion; theburn ratewhile cooking these will decrease as players reach higher Cooking levels. It will stop burning entirely atCookinglevel 77 on both ranges and fires. When eaten, fried onions heal 5hitpoints.
