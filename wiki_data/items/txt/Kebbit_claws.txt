Kebbit clawsare obtained from hunting ofWild kebbitsfrom Deadfall Traps. They can be attached to any leather or regular dragonhidevambraces(excludingblessed,gilded,snakeskinandthird age) makingspiked vambraces. These add a +2 strength bonus to allvambracesand in the cases of the dragonhide versions a slight increase in physical defensive bonuses as well. Wild Kebbits are found near the Falconry area, and at level 23Hunter, can be caught in adeadfall trap.
