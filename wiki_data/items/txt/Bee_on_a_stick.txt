Bees on a stickwas a stackable item used inWoodcutting. When in the inventory or stored in aForestry kit, there was a chance of theflowering treeeventappearing. The chance of this occurring was scaled based on the number of players present; when a stick was consumed, a game message in the chatbox stated,Your Bee on a stick buzzes excitedly as it detects pollen-rich plants nearby., and the player will receive roughly double the anima-infused bark for each pollination as well as double bark rewarded at the end of the event.
