Blighted super restorepotions are a variant ofsuper restorepotion that can only be drunk while inside theWilderness,Ferox Enclave,Mage Arena bank,PvP worlds,Castle Wars, andClan Wars. If aPrayer capeorring of the gods (i)is equipped or a Prayer cape orholy wrenchis in the player's inventory, the Prayer restore increases to 8 + 27% of the player's current Prayer level, rounded down. This does not affect the rate of restoration of stats other than Prayer.
