Thesilver bookis a quest item used in theMurder Mysteryquest. It is obtained from a barrel inDavid's bedroom in theSinclair Mansion. It has David's fingerprints on it and is used to determine if David is the murderer or not. If it is used on the flour barrel, it will create the dusted silver book, andflypaperis used on it, it will createDavid's print, which can be compared with theunknown printto see if David is the killer.
