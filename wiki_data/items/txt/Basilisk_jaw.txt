Thebasilisk jawis a unique drop frombasilisk knights. It can be combined with thehelm of Neitiznot, after the completion ofThe Fremennik Exiles, to create an untradeableNeitiznot faceguard. Doing so requires askingOlaf the Bardfor stories about basilisk jaws while having it in their inventory. Completion ofThe Fremennik Exilesis also required to equip the helmet.
