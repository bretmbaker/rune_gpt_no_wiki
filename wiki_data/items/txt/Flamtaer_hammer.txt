TheFlamtaer hammeris an item used during theShades of Mort'ton minigame. It is a replacement for the regular hammer when building the temple, and is approximately 75% faster. It acts as an invisible +40Craftingboost while rebuilding the temple in the Shades of Mort'ton minigame.The Flamtaer hammer can be bought fromRazmire Keelganat theMort'tongeneral store or fromRasolo the Wandering MerchantnearBaxtorian Falls.
