Thejar generatorcan be obtained fromElnock Inquisitor, and can be used to create eitherbutterfly jarsorimpling jars. Only one jar generator can be owned at a time, and the player must use up or destroy their current generator before obtaining another. Attempting to obtain another while the player has one will lead to the choice to recharge the currently-owned generator instead, which costs the same as buying a new one. It is always lost upon death.
