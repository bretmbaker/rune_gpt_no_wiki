Thenewly made crystalis a quest item used in theMourning's End Part IIquest. It is obtained by using achiselon a large black rock in theTemple of Lightat the start of the quest and then giving this toElunedafter talking toArianwyn. He will then turn it into an uncharged crystal which must be charged on theDeath Altarin order to be able to seal the Temple for 1,000 years.
