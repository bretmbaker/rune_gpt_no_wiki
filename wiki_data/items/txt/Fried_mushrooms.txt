Fried mushroomsare a type offoodthat can be obtained by cookingsliced mushroomson afireorcooking range, requiring level 46Cookingand granting 60 experience when successful. Players may burn the mushrooms while cooking one, resulting in aburnt mushroom; theburn ratewhile cooking these will decrease as players reach higher Cooking levels. It will stop burning entirely at level 90 Cooking on both ranges and fires. When eaten, fried mushrooms heals 5Hitpoints.
