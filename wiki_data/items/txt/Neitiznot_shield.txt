TheNeitiznot shield, also commonly called the Fremennik roundshield, is ashieldmade of wood that requires level 25Defenceto wield. It can be made during and after completion ofThe Fremennik Islesquestwith level 56Woodcuttingon thewoodcutting stumpnear the bank inNeitiznot. This requires twoarctic pine logs, abronze nail, arope, and ahammerin the inventory. Making the shield grants 34Craftingexperience.
