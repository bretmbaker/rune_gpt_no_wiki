Thepharaoh's sceptreis a discontinued item that previously had similar functionality to itsmodern incarnation. It was changed with theupdatethat introducedBeneath Cursed Sands. In that update, the sceptre was updated to hold a vastly increased number of charges, and now functions in a manner similar toXeric's talisman. It held 3 charges by default, increased to 4 charges after theEasy Desert Diary, 5 after theMedium Desert Diary, 6 after theHard Desert Diary, and 8 after theElite Desert Diary.
