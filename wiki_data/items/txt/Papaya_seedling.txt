Papaya seedlingsare created by planting apapaya tree seedin aplant potwith agardening trowel. Once watered, these saplings will grow into anpapaya sapling, which can then be planted in afruit tree patch. You must have 57farmingto plant theseeds. Once the seedling is in the patch, you can pay the nearby farmer 10pineapplesto protect it. The farmer will accept noted pineapples.
