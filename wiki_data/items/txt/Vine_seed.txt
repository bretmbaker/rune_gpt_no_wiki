Thevine seedsare a quest item given to the player during theGarden of Tranquillityquest by the gardenerBernald. In order to obtain the seeds the player must help save thegrapevinesthat are growing in the garden behind Bernald's house in south-eastBurthorpe. To save the vines the player must mix somerune dustinto a magicalplant curethat can then be poured on the existing vines.
