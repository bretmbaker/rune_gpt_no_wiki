Along vineis a player-madeitemused to cross a river inTemple Trekking. To make this, players must use aknifeon a nearby set of hanging vines to obtain threeshort vines. The three vine sections are combined by using them on each other, forming a long vine which is then lassoed over a swamp tree to form a rope swing to cross the river. The long vine is lost in process, and therefore cannot be taken outside of theminigame.
