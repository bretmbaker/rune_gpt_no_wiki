Thecandle lanternis a type of members-only light source used in theFiremakingskill. To make a candle lantern, the player must first have an unlitcandle, anempty candle lantern, and at least level 4 Firemaking. The player places the white candle in the lantern frame and lights it, using a tinderbox. No experience is received when lighting a candle lantern. They cannot be traded whilst lit.
