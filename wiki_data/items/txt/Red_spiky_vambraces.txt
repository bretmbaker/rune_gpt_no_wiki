Red spiky vambracesarered dragonhide vambraceswithkebbit clawsattached, which doing so requires 32Craftingand grants 5.8 Crafting experience. Kebbit claws can be gained using theHunterskill. Red spiked vambraces can also be obtained by looting aneclectic implingthat has been caught. The difference in stats between spiked and non-spiked vambraces is a bonus to strength of +2 and a slight increase in physical defence for the spiked ones.
