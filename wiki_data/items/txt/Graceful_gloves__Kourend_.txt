TheKourend graceful glovesare one of many variants ofgraceful gloves. They are recoloured from a pair of graceful gloves by using them onOsteninShayzien(in the building east of the bank), costing 15marks of grace. Doing so requires completion of all five house quests in theGreat Kourend quest series:The Queen of Thieves,The Depths of Despair,The Ascent of Arceuus,The Forsaken Tower, andTale of the Righteous.
