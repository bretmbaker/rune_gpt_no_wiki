It is also used to make awater tiaraif you have atiaraand a water talisman and use them on the altar, granting 30Runecraftexperience. If you are a member, it can be used to runecraftmist runesat theAir Altar,steam runesat theFire Altar, ormud runesat theEarth Altar. The water talisman is consumed in the process.
