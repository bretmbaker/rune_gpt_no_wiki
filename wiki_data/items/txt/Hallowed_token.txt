Hallowed tokenis an item used to add an extra minute of time during aHallowed Sepulchrerun, it is consumed upon use. If a player wants to add the extra minute to the run, the token needs to be activated before the timer runs out. It can be purchased from theMysterious Hallowed Goodsshop for 10hallowed marks. You may use more than one token per attempt at the Sepulchre.
