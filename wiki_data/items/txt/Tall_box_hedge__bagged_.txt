Abagged tall box hedgecan be bought inFaladoror theFarming Guildfrom aGarden supplierfor 100,000coins. They can be planted in theformal gardenof aplayer-owned house, requiring aConstructionlevel of 80, providing 316 experience to both Construction andFarming. The player must have awatering canwith at least one charge in their inventory to plant it and it will consume that charge. It is only for decoration.
