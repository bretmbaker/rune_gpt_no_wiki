Agrimy ranarr weedis a herb that can be cleaned to make aranarr weed. Grimy ranarr weeds may be grown from aranarr seedwith aFarminglevel of 32 and gives 30.5 Farming experience. Cleaning a grimy ranarr requires aHerblorelevel of 25 and gives 7.5 Herblore experience. Clean ranarr weeds are used to makedefence potionsandprayer potionswith a Herblore level of 30 and 38, respectively.
