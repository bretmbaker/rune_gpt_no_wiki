Green d'hide chaps, short for green dragonhide chaps, is a type oflegweardesigned forrangers. They are part of thegreen dragonhide armourset, and can be worn at level 40Ranged. They offer the best ranged accuracy bonus out of allfree-to-playleg armours, although their defensive bonuses are surpassed by thegilded d'hide chaps. Notable for noDefencerequirement, this piece of armour is commonly used by thefree-to-play combat pures.
