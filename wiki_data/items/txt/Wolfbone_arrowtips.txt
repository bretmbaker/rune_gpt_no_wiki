Wolfbone arrowtipsare used as to create theogre arrowsused in theBig Chompy Bird Huntingquest. Players must partially complete Big Chompy Bird Hunting in order to fletch the arrowtips. They require level 5 inFletchingto make. Using achiselwithwolf boneswill result in 2-6 arrowheads and gives 2.5 Fletching andCraftingexperience per arrowtip. The amount of arrowtips carved out of each wolf bone is random, and is not influenced by Fletching or Crafting level.
