Ajug of blessed sunfire wineis an item created by blessing ajug of sunfire wineat theexposed altarfound south ofthe Teomat, which is made by using twosunfire splinterson ajug of winewith a pestle and mortar in your inventory. Sunfire splinterscannotbe added to ajug of blessed wine; it must be added to an unblessed jug first. It is used in the process of sacrificingblessed bone shardsat thelibation bowl, where one jug can sacrifice 400 shards. Players will also need 2Prayer pointsper sacrifice.
