Thepresentwas an item used during the 3rdbirthday event. It was given to the player bySir Tiffy Cashien, who assumes they were the courier he summoned. The present was intended for his grandson, Ebel Lum, whom the player was supposed to deliver to, but it was brought to thegnome childinLumbridgeinstead. Attempting to open it before speaking to the gnome child resulted in the messageThis isn't for me to open!
