Willow logsare logs, which can be cut fromwillow treesusing theWoodcuttingskill. Cutting willow logs from a willow tree requires anaxeand level 30Woodcuttingand grants 67.5 Woodcuttingexperienceper log. Players can burn willow logs using theFiremakingskill and atinderbox. This requires level 30 Firemaking and grants 90 Firemaking experience per log, or 30 experience per log if burned at aforester's campfire.
