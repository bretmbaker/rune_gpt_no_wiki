Brandyis an alcoholic drink which can be purchased atJossik's store in theLighthouse, at theTree Gnome StrongholdinHeckel Funch's store or atthe Asp & Snake BaratPollnivneach. It is an ingredient in the gnome cocktailBlurberry special. It heals 5hitpoints, increases a player'sStrengthby 5% + 1 and decreasesAttackby 2% - 3 when drunk. Unlike the other types of alcoholic drinks bought from stores, brandy has a cone-shaped bottle.
