Cakeis a type offoodthat restores 12Hitpointsin total, 3 bites each healing for 4. It can be obtained by baking anuncooked cakeon acooking range,  requiring level 40Cookingand granting 180 experience when successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting inburnt cake. Theburn ratedecreases as the player'sCookinglevel increases, and it will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 74.
