TheKourend graceful hoodis one of many variants of thegraceful hood. It is recoloured from a graceful hood by using it onOsteninShayzien(in the building east of the bank), costing 15marks of grace. Doing so requires completion of all five house quests in theGreat Kourend quest series:The Queen of Thieves,The Depths of Despair,The Ascent of Arceuus,The Forsaken Tower, andTale of the Righteous.
