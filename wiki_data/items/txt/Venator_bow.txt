Thevenator bowis a two-handedranged weaponthat was once used by the venators, who were hunters and scouts that served theZarosian Empire. Requiring level 80 inRangedto wield, it is made by combining fivevenator shards. It can fire any type of arrow, includingdragon arrows, and has anattack rangeof 6 (8 when using the "Longrange" combat style).
