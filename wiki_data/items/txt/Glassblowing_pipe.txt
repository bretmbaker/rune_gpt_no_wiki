Aglassblowing pipeis a tool used to make items frommolten glassusing theCraftingskill. It is required to make glass items. The item can be obtained fromFritz the Glassblower's house found in the south-western area ofEntrana, taken from atool store 3in aplayer-owned house(required 35Construction), fromGrandpa Jack's house (fromFishing Contestquest), bought from thePiscatoris Fishing Colonyafter finishingSwan Song, or bought fromcharter shiplocations (from the crew).
