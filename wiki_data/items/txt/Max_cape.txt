Themax capeis a cape available to players who have attained level 99 in all 23skills. It is sold byMacfor 2,277,000 coins (99,000 for each of the 23 skills). Mac is located onan islandwest of theWarriors' Guild, beyond the ladder that leads down to level 106Cyclopes. To reach him, the player needs a pickaxe and an axe, as well as level 99 inAgility,Crafting,Construction,Defence,Mining, andWoodcutting.
