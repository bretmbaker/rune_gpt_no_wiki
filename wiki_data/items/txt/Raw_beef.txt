Raw beefis an uncookedfood. Raw beef is one of the requirements of theDruidic Ritualquest. Players require only level 1Cookingto cook raw beef. It gives 30 Cooking experience when cooked. When cooked, the raw beef may becomecooked meatorburnt meat. Players can buy it fromRufus' Meat EmporiuminCanifisand fromWydin's Food StoreinPort Sarim. Raw beef is always dropped fromcows. Members can convert raw beef intosinewby using it on arangeand choosing the "Make sinew" option.
