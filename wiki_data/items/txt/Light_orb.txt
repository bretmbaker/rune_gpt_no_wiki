Light orbscan be used to fixmagical lampsinDorgesh-Kaan. Level 52CraftingandFiremakingis needed to fix a lamp, giving 500 Crafting experience and 500 Firemaking experience. When a player has replaced 50 light orbs they will receive the message "You have replaced 50 orbs and have gained extra experience." The bonus received is 2,500 Crafting and Firemaking experience. This can be repeated indefinitely.
Broken lamps are found on all three floors. The large street lamps throughout Dorgesh-Kaan never need replacing. Broken lamps can be hard to distinguish from a distance. There are always ten broken lamps, and once one is fixed, a new one will break. Broken lamps are unique to each player and are the same for the player no matter what world the player is on.
