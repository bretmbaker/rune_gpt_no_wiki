Thesnow globeis obtained as a reward from the2016 Christmas event, as well as subsequent Christmas events. Its design is based off the original snowglobe obtained from the2007Christmas event, in which players obtained a snowglobe with a replica ofLumbridge Castleinside it. When shaken, the snowglobe would play a softer version of the songHarmony, create a pile ofsnow, and fill the player's inventory withsnow.
