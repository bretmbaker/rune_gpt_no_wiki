Fat snail meatis a type offoodthat restores 7 to 9Hitpointswhen eaten. It can be obtained by cookingfat snailon afireorcooking range, requiring level 22Cookingand granting 95 experience when successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting in aburnt snail. Theburn ratedecreases as the player's Cooking level increases. Fat snail meat is one of a number of foods with a reduced burn rate while using theLumbridge Castle rangewithCook's Assistantcompleted. It will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 56 or 51 when using the Lumbridge range.
