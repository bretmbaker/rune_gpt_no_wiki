Miazrqa's pendantwas given to her by her mother and is of great sentimental value toMiazrqa. The player is asked to retrieve the pendant from a mouse which stole it from her at the witch's house inTaverley. To claim it back the player must first shrink themselves with ashrink-me-quickpotion in order to be able to follow the mouse down its hole, and find the pendant on the ground inside. In return for getting the pendant back,Miazrqawill releaseRupertfrom the tower he is being held captive in.
