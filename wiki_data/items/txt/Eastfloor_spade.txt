TheEastfloor spadeis a cosmetic reward obtained from the2023 Easter event, as well as subsequent Easter events. It can be used in place of a regularspadewhilst digging for clues as part ofTreasure Trails, but cannot be used for any other activity requiring a spade (includingX Marks the Spot). Attempting to use it outside of Treasure Trails results in the game messageThis spade is only sturdy enough for certain loose soil often found on the trail of treasure.
