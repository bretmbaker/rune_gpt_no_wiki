Silver orecan be mined at level 20Mining, providing 40 Mining experience. Aftersilver rocksare mined, it takes 60 seconds for them to respawn. Smelting silver ore will turn it into asilver bar, requiring 20 Smithing and giving 13.7Smithingexperience. The bar can then be crafted into various types ofjewellerythrough theCraftingskill at a furnace, rather than at an anvil like other metallic bars. Doing so requires the usage of amould, depending on the type of jewellery desired.
