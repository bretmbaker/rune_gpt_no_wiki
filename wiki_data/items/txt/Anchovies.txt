Anchoviesare a type offishthat restores 1Hitpointwhen eaten. It can be obtained by cookingraw anchovieson afireorcooking range, granting 30 experience when successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting inburnt anchovies. The burn rate decreases as the player'sCookinglevel increases. Anchovies are one of a number of foods with a reduced burn rate while using theLumbridge Castle rangewithCook's Assistantcompleted. It will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 34, or 31 when using the Lumbridge range.
