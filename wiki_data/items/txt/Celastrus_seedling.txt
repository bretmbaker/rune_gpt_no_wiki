Celastrus seedlingsare the result of planting aCelastrus seedin aplant potwith agardening trowel. Once watered, these seedlings will take approximately 5 minutes to grow into aCelastrus sapling. 85Farmingis needed to plant the seed. When it has grown, players may place it in a Celastrus tree patch to grow aCelastrus tree. Players can pay 8Potato Cactifor the farmer to look after it.
