Khali brewis aquest itemin theFight Arenaquest. Once the player has spoken to theHead Guardin the south-eastern room of the jail, it is possible to buy the beer for5coins from theKhazard barmanin the bar in the south-west corner of theFight Arena. The Khazard barman suggestslying down before drinking it so there is less distance to collapse; however, there is no option for the player to drink it. Giving it to the Head Guard will get him drunk and allow you to steal theKhazard cell keys.
