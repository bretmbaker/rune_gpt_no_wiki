Raw boar meatcan be obtained by killingboars, found in the woods west ofKourend Castleand south ofThe Node. It can be cooked at level 1Cooking, granting 30 Cooking experience if successful. After successfully cooking it, it will simply becooked meat, while burning it will result inburnt meat(burning can be achieved at any level by cooking it twice). The raw boar meat can also be made intosinewat level 1 Cooking by using it on a range.
