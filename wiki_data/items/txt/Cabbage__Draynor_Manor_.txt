Acabbagepicked atDraynor Manoris unlike othercabbages; it appears slightly darker, and when eaten, it restores 1Hitpointand grants atemporary skill boosttoDefence. The item cannot be traded on theGrand Exchangeand can only be obtained from one of sevencabbage plantslocated in between the west side of the house and the fence directly north of the farming patch on the minimap.
