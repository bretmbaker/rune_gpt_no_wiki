Thecelestial ringis amining enhancerfrom theShooting Starsactivity, purchased fromDusuri's Star Shopfor 2,000stardust. Players can charge the ring with stardust, with each one added giving one charge (up to a maximum of 10,000). Players can configure the charges while the ring is in their bank by right-clicking on Configure-Charges, as long as players have the required materials. Uncharging the ring will remove all charges with the remaining stardust returned to the player.
