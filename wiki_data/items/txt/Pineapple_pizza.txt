Pineapple pizzais a type ofpizzathat restores 22Hitpointsin total, 2 bites each healing for 11. It can be obtained by usingpineapple chunksorpineapple ringson a cookedplain pizza, requiring 65Cookingand granting 45 experience. It is recommended to cut the pineapple into rings, as this yields four rings for use on multiple pizzas, compared to the single chunk if dicing it.
