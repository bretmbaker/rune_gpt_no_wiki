TheKourend graceful legsare one of many variants of thegraceful legs. They are recoloured from graceful legs by using it onOsteninShayzien(in the building east of the bank), costing 15marks of grace. Doing so requires completion of all five house quests in theGreat Kourend quest series:The Queen of Thieves,The Depths of Despair,The Ascent of Arceuus,The Forsaken Tower, andTale of the Righteous.
