Ava's accumulatoris an item awarded to the player by completing the questAnimal Magnetismif the player is above level 50Ranged(players with lower than 50 Ranged will instead receiveAva's attractor). It is equipped in thecape slot, can pick up a player's ranged ammunition after they fire it, and every 3.5 minutes, will attract an assortment of metal ammunition. The accumulator gives the fourth best Ranged attack bonus for thecape slot.
