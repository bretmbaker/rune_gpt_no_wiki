Flourwas an item that could be obtained by killingblack knightsinRunescape Classic. It could not normally be picked up, and players were intended to use apoton it in order to obtain apot of flour. However, it was possible to obtain the heap of flour by castingTelekinetic Grab. Upon the release ofRuneScape 2, knights were changed to drop a regular pot of flour instead, initially making it one of the rarest discontinued items in the game.
