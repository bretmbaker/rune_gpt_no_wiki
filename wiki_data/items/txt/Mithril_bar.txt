AMithril baris a bar of refinedmithril ore. It can be created through theSmithingskill by using amithril oreand 4 pieces ofcoalon afurnace, granting 30experience. TheSuperheat Itemspell can also be used to make mithril bars. Both ways of creating mithril bars require level 50 Smithing. It can also be created in theBlast Furnace, which can be considerably cheaper as it halves the amount of coal needed.
