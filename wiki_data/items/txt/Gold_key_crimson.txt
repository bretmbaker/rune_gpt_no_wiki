Gold key crimsonis a key and reward from theShades of Mort'tonminigame. To obtain it, the player must kill aUrium Shadeand then burn itsremainson a pyre. The key may be used to enter theShade Catacombsand then open the correctly coloured chest. It will open agold-framed chest with a crimson lock. This type of key can be used to open any door within the tombs, giving the player unrestricted access to all areas.
