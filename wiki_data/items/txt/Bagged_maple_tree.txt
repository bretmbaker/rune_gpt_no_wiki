Abagged maple treecan be bought inFaladororFarming Guildfrom aGarden supplierfor 15,000coins. They can be planted in thegardenof aplayer-owned house, requiring aConstructionlevel of 45, providing 122 experience to both Construction andFarming. The player must have a filledwatering canin their inventory to plant it. This tree is only for decoration and cannot be cut for logs.
