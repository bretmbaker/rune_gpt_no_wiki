Theiron dart tipis adart tipmade with theSmithingskill. Players can craft this item using aniron baron ananvilwith ahammerin theirinventoryand a Smithing level of 19. Each iron bar creates 10 iron dart tips and gives the player 25 Smithing experience. They can be fletched intoiron dartsat level 22 Fletching when used withfeathers, granting 38 Fletching experience per 10 darts made.
