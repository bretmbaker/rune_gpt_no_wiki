TheTrailblazer reloaded vengeance scrollis a consumable item purchased from theLeagues Reward Shopfor 7,000 League points. It can be used to unlock aTrailblazer Reloaded-themedVengeanceanimation override. To enable it, players can right click "Animation" on the Vengeance spell icon in thelunar spellbookand select "Leagues IV - Trailblazer Reloaded".
