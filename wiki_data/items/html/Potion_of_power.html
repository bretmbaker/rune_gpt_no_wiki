A potion of power is a potion exclusive to Soul Wars, obtained from the table of potions of power at the player's base. The potion combines the effects of a super combat, super ranging, super magic, and super restore potion. Unlike most potions, the potions of power cannot be combined with one another.

When a game of Soul Wars ends, all gameplay items in the player's inventory are removed, including the potion of power. All boosts acquired from drinking it are removed as well.
