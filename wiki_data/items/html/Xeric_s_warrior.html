Xeric's warrior is a cosmetic cape that can be obtained from Captain Rimor by the entrance to the Chambers of Xeric after completing 500 Chambers of Xeric in Challenge Mode.

This item can be stored in the cape rack of a costume room. It can also be mounted on a cape hanger in an Achievement Gallery.
