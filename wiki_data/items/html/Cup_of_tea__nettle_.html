A Cup of tea (nettle) is a drink that restores 3 Hitpoints and 5% run energy, provided you have missing hitpoints. It can be obtained by using nettle tea on an empty cup.

A bucket of milk may also be added to the tea to create Cup of tea (milky nettle) which has the same effects and is used during the Ghosts Ahoy quest.

This particular kind of tea cannot be added to a tea flask.

Steps
1. Pick some nettles while wielding gloves
2. Fill a bowl with water
3. Use the nettles on the bowl of water to obtain nettle-water
4. Cook the nettle-water by using it on a range or fire to make nettle tea
5. Use the nettle tea on an empty cup to make a cup of tea
6. Optional: Use a bucket of milk on the nettle tea or cup of tea to create the milky version
