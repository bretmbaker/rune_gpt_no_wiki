Cooked crab meat are a type of food that can be obtained by cooking crab meat on a fire or cooking range, requiring level 21 Cooking and granting 100 experience when successful. Players may burn the crab meat while cooking one, resulting in burnt crab meat; the burn rate while cooking these will decrease as players reach higher Cooking levels. It will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 62 on both ranges and fires.

Cooked crab meat provides 5 bites healing for 2 hitpoints each. The small amounts of hitpoints it gives per bite can also be used to "fine tune" the hitpoints of someone using Dharok the Wretched's equipment. However, it is not recommended for general healing, as tuna is one-bite, heals just as much, and costs significantly less.
