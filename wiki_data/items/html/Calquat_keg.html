A calquat keg is used to collect completed brewing ales and cider from the barrel next to fermenting vats. Two calquat kegs are required to empty a barrel, each keg containing four pints compared to beer glasses single pint.

They are created by using a knife on a calquat fruit picked from calquat trees, requiring 72 Farming to grow.

Ales in beer glasses cannot be used to fill a calquat keg.
