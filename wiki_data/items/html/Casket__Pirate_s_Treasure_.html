The casket (also known as One-Eyed Hector's chest) is a quest item reward in Pirate's Treasure. It is found by digging with a spade next to the statue of Saradomin in Falador Park.

A gardener will spawn and attack the player who digs there. The gardener can be killed or despawned by running away. Afterwards, dig in that location again to obtain the casket and complete the quest. It was buried by One-Eyed Hector and the pirate message leads the player to the location of the treasure. The casket can only be obtained one time.

It can be opened to receive 450 coins, a gold ring, and an emerald.
