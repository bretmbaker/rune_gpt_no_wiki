A chisel is a tool used in the Crafting skill to cut and shape hard non-metals, mainly gems and limestone. Chisels are also used in Fletching to cut gems into bolt tips. Chisels can be purchased for 1 coin at all general stores, except for those in Dorgesh-Kaan and Ardougne. There is a free-to-play chisel spawn in the Crafting Guild.

At level 15 Construction, members can make a tool store 1 for the workshop room of their house, which will provide an unlimited supply of chisels.
