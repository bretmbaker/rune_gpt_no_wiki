The drake tooth is an item obtained from drakes found within the Karuulm Slayer Dungeon. It can be combined with the holy sandals to create devout boots, which require level 60 Prayer to wear and increase the prayer bonus from +3 to +5.

When attempting to create the devout boots, a warning will appear in the chatbox stating that the process cannot be reversed, and a confirmation will appear afterwards asking if players would like to proceed with the alchemical combination. Should the player confirm, the chatbox will state You successfully combine the Holy sandals and Drake's tooth to create Devout boots.
