Bandages are a healing item that can only be obtained in Soul Wars. After the game has ended, or if the player left the game early, all bandages in a player's inventory will disappear.

Bandages are found on the table in the start room. They restore 1 + 15% of a player's hitpoints (rounded down) and 100% of a player's run energy. They have the ability to cure poison and venom effects. They do not cure corruption. It is possible to use bandages on other players to heal them.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • With the update on 16 February 2022, the aid symbol on the bandages changed from a red cross to an asterisk to comply with the International Red Cross's emblem trademarks. This is a different approach than was taken to change it in 2010 in Runescape, where it instead changed the colour to green, rather than the shape.
