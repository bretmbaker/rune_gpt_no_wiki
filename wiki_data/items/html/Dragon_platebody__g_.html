The dragon platebody (g) is a dragon platebody with a dragon platebody ornament kit attached to it, and is part of dragon gold-trimmed armour. The attached ornament kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the platebody. It costs 12,407,676 for both the parts to make the dragon platebody (g).

While the platebody is untradeable in its ornamented state, it can be dismantled anytime, returning the tradeable platebody and ornament kit.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room, as part of the dragon gold-trimmed armour set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
