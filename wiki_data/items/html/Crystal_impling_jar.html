The crystal impling jar is the result of successfully catching a crystal impling, while also having an empty impling jar. Level 80 Hunter is required to catch them.

Loot[edit | edit source]

At current Grand Exchange prices, opening a crystal impling jar gives 66,979.00 worth of loot on average. If you include growing the Crystal acorns, you can use Crystal shards to create Divine potions, this increases the value to 85,786.00.

The following drop rates are provided by Jagex, unless otherwise specified.

Impling jar[edit | edit source]

An empty impling jar is returned when removing the loot, with a 10% chance of it breaking.[1]
