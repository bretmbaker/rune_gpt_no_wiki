Vorkath's head is an item dropped by Vorkath. The head has various uses, such as being used to upgrade Ava's accumulator, as a cosmetic upgrade to the Slayer helmet, or sacrificed for Prayer experience. For any given kill, the drop rate of the head is 1/50, with the exception of the 50th kill, when the drop is guaranteed.

Players can take the head along with 75 mithril arrows, and either Ava's accumulator or 4,999 coins (for a new device), to Ava in Draynor Manor to obtain Ava's assembler, which gives increased stats and improved ammo return. Players can also use the head on a ranging or max cape to give the enhanced ammo-returning effect to those capes. Additionally, players can bring the assembler and Dizana's Quiver to Ava to grant the quiver the ammo-returning effect.

Uses[edit | edit source]

Aside from creating the assembler, it can be stuffed at the taxidermist in Canifis by using the item on her and paying 50,000 coins, returning Vorkath's stuffed head. When players take it to the her, she will tell players "This blue dragon smells like it's been dead for a remarkably long time. Even by my standards, it smells awful."

Once stuffed, it can be mounted at a gilded display within the skill hall in a player-owned house, granting 223 Construction alongside 200 Attack, Strength, Defence, Magic, and Ranged experience, with players being given the option to decline the combat experience before mounting the heads.

Alternatively, the head can be sacrificed to the Dark Altar north of Arceuus, granting 1,000 Prayer experience. However, players with level 90 Magic can instead opt to cast Master Reanimation on it, giving 1,560 Prayer experience instead after killing the reanimated dragon.

The head can also be combined with a slayer helmet (or imbued) to create a turquoise slayer helmet; doing so requires purchasing the ability Undead Head for 1,000 Slayer reward points. After the claiming the elite tier rewards from the Combat Achievements system, the heads can additionally be combined with the dragon hunter crossbow to create a turquoise variant.

Lastly, the head (or a stuffed head) can be used to enhance the ornate undead combat dummy, enabling it to act as a draconic monster.

Prayer info[edit | edit source]

This experience is granted by using the head on the Dark Altar. It can also be reanimated using the Master Reanimation spell, for which it functions identically to an ensouled dragon head.
