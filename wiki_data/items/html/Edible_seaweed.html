Edible seaweed is an edible version of seaweed that restore 4 Hitpoints. Edible seaweed can be cooked on a range or fire to create soda ash (an ingredient in molten glass), granting no experience.

The item is one of the possible random items required during the quest Fairytale I - Growing Pains.
