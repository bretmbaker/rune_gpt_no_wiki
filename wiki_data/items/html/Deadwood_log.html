Deadwood logs are used in the 2015 Halloween event, during which the player must prevent the Anti-Santa from re-entering the house. Deadwood trees can be found behind the Grim Reaper's mansion. The logs must be converted into deadwood planks in the coffin workshop west of the mansion.
