The tier 1 adventurer's top is an item that is part of the tier 1 adventurer's outfit, which can be purchased for 200 Quest Speedrunning reward points from the Speedrunning Reward Shop.

This item can be stored in the armour case of a costume room, as part of the Adventurer's outfit (tier 1). Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
