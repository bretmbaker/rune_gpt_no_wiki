Cooked rabbit is a type of food that restores 5 Hitpoints when eaten. It can be obtained by cooking a raw rabbit on a fire or cooking range, granting 30 experience when successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting in burnt meat. The burn rate decreases as the player's Cooking level increases, and it will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 34 on both ranges and fires.

A Cooked rabbit is required for the quest Regicide.
