The Ancient robe top is part of the ancient vestment set and can be obtained by completing easy Treasure Trails. To wear, the player requires a minimum of level 20 Prayer.

Curiously, the ancient robe top weights 0.005 kg, whereas all other vestment robe tops weigh 1.8 kg.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room, as part of the ancient vestment set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
