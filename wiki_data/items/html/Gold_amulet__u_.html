A Gold amulet (u) is made with the Crafting skill by using a gold bar on a furnace while carrying an amulet mould. Following that, it can be strung with a ball of wool, resulting in a strung gold amulet. Making a gold amulet (u) requires level 8 Crafting and players gain 30 experience for making the amulet, and then 4 experience for stringing it.

Unlike gem amulets, gold amulets cannot be enchanted, making them purely cosmetic.
