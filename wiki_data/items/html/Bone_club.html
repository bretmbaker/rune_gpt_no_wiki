The bone club can be purchased from Nardok's Bone Weapons store, run by Nardok in the Dorgeshuun Mines after completing The Lost Tribe quest. It may also be obtained as a drop from either a cave goblin or cave goblin guard. Its power is between that of an iron warhammer and a steel warhammer.
