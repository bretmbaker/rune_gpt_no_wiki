The seed box is an item that can store up to 2,147,483,647 seeds of six different types. It is purchased from Farmer Gricoller at the Tithe Farm for 250 points. This item cannot be used directly on a farming patch; players must remove the seeds they want to use in order to plant them. Players can right-click to open the box, which will have seeds that the player picks up be automatically placed inside the box, provided there is space for it.

Like Gricoller's can, players can only own one seed box at a time. It is useful to bring to Slayer tasks and bosses which drop a large variety of seeds commonly, such as nechryael, to save inventory space. Ultimate Ironman players may find the seed box very useful, as it saves up to five inventory spaces while training Farming and Herblore.

Upon death to an NPC, including deaths below level 20 Wilderness, the seed box will go to the gravestone with all of its contents still stored. However, if the player dies above level 20 Wilderness, the seed box will be destroyed but the contents preserved within the next box acquired. For Ultimate Ironman players, deaths above level 20 Wilderness will result in the seed box being destroyed, and all of its contents dropping to ground. The Seed Box is always lost upon death in PvP.

In Deadman Mode, the seed box is lost upon death, even when stored in the bank and empty.

Note: The seeds within the seedbox can be safely ghostbanked by dying whilst it is in a deathbank interface such as Hespori.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
20 July 2022
(update)

The seed box now lists its content in chat when checked, even if the interface doesn't remain open. If it is open, the list will appear when closing the interface, thus giving the most up-to-date status of the box.

14 April 2021
(update)

Upon death above level 20 wilderness the seed box will now be destroyed but its content preserved within the next box acquired. This does not work for UIM Accounts

16 April 2020
(update | poll)

Seeds pickpocketed from Master Farmers will now be directly placed into an open seed box.

23 January 2020
(update | poll)
  • Open and close options have been added, which allows seeds that the player picks up be automatically placed inside the box while opened, providing there is space for it.
  • The item's name was changed from "Seed box" to "Open seed box", while the closed version was given the open box's item name.
  • The item was graphically updated.
24 January 2019
(update)

Shift-clicking now empties the contents into the inventory.

14 April 2016
(update)

The "Check" and "Fill" options were swapped, so Fill is now left-click.

11 February 2016
(update)

The weight of the seed box was reduced from 6kg to 1 kg.
