The present box hat is a cosmetic reward from the 2024 Christmas event. It can be obtained as a reward from the Christmas events that occur once per year. Its appearance can be freely changed between the four different styles by using Change-option from inventory or while worn prompting a You change the style of your Present box hat. message.

If destroyed, a replacement can be obtained from Diango in Draynor Village or from the toy box of a costume room.
