The mithril full helm is the fourth best full helmet. It requires 20 Defence to equip. It offers better defensive stats than its med helm counterpart, at the cost of having lower magic and ranged attack, and greater weight. One can be made by a player with level 57 Smithing at any anvil with a hammer and 2 mithril bars.
