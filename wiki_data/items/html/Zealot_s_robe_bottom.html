The zealot's robe bottom is a piece of the Zealot's robes set that can be obtained from looting gold chests within the Shade Catacombs.

The robe bottom grants a 1.25% chance to prevent various remains from being consumed whilst training Prayer; wearing the full set will increase this chance to 5%, and stacks with the bone-saving effect of the altar in the Chaos Temple. However, this does not apply at the Forthos Dungeon's Sacred Bone Burner, nor when casting Sinister Offering.

This item can be stored in the magic wardrobe of a costume room, as part of the Zealot's robes set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.

The Items Kept on Death value of zealot's robes has been increased to rival superior dragon bones, so that they are kept upon death more reliably.

Zealot's robes now gives players a chance to save bonemeal and buckets of slime.
