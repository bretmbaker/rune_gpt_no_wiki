The Slayer ring (eternal) is a special slayer ring that is made with an eternal gem instead of an enchanted gem. It provides unlimited teleport charges, compared to the eight of its normal counterpart. It is only obtainable through Crafting, and is otherwise crafted the same way as the normal slayer ring and has the same requirements (namely 75 Crafting and having purchased the Ring bling unlock from any Slayer master for 300 Slayer reward points).

This is an irreversible process, and the ring cannot be traded or reverted to the gem.

Players can obtain an eternal gem by killing superior slayer monsters, which are monsters that rarely spawn after slaying a Slayer monster if the player has purchased Bigger and Badder for 150 Slayer reward points from any Slayer master.

Teleports[edit | edit source]

The ring will teleport players to any of the locations displayed below. Similar to enchanted dragonstone jewellery, the slayer ring will allow players to teleport at up to level 30 Wilderness.

Location Destination Requirements Notes
Stronghold Slayer Cave Outside the cave, close to Nieve/Steve. None Close to a bank. Those who use NPC Contact may also find this teleport useful as they can teleport to Nieve to cancel/block their tasks with her, while using NPC Contact to contact other Slayer masters (such as Duradel) to try to get the task they want.
Slayer Tower Ground floor, just inside the entrance. Priest in Peril Can be used for easy access to Canifis.
Fremennik Slayer Dungeon Just inside the entrance. None Fourth closest teleport to a fairy ring (the quest point cape teleport being the closest, Salve Graveyard teleport (Arceuus Spellbook) second and the necklace of passage teleport to the Wizards' Tower being the third closest).
Tarn's Lair Empty room before the Terror dogs' chamber. Haunted Mine Can be used to skip a large portion of the Lair of Tarn Razorlor miniquest.
Dark Beasts Dark Beasts' chamber in the Mourner Tunnels. Mourning's End Part II Can be used to access the Mourner Tunnels without Mourner clothing.
