The Adamant pickaxe is a pickaxe that is stronger than the mithril pickaxe, but weaker than the rune pickaxe. It requires an Attack level of 30 to wield, and a Mining level of 31 to mine with.

You can buy this item in Nurmof's Pickaxe Shop located within the Dwarven Mines for the price of 3200 coins.

The adamant pickaxe mines on a 4-tick cycle, unlike other pickaxes. This allows it to be used for tick manipulation while mining shooting stars.
