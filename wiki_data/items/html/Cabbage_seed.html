Cabbage seeds are the third seeds a player can plant with the Farming skill. 3 seeds are sown in an allotment patch to grow cabbages, which yield the cabbage food item used in Cooking. They take 40 minutes to grow.

A nearby gardener will watch over growing cabbages for 1 sack of onions. Fully grown rosemary or white lilies in a nearby flower patch will also protect the cabbages from disease.

Many medium and low-level monsters drop cabbage seeds, including guards, farmers, and hill giants. These seeds can be stolen from master farmers and seed stalls. They are purchasable from the Draynor Seed Market or Amelia's Seed Shop in the Farming Guild. There is a small chance to obtain a cabbage seed while picking cabbages from a field.

Sacks of cabbages are needed to protect growing tomatoes (x2), Krandorian hops (x3), and redberry bushes (x4).

Average harvest yield[edit | edit source]

Cabbage has a minimum of 3 harvest lives, increasing by 1 for each tier of compost used (for a maximum of 6 when using ultracompost). While harvesting cabbage, there is a chance to not consume a harvest life dependent on the player's Farming level. This chance scales from 42.2% at level 1 to 70.7% at level 99, interpolating linearly in between.

Magic secateurs (equipped or in inventory) increase the chance of receiving additional yield by 10%.
