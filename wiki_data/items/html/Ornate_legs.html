The ornate legs are obtained from completing a clue during the Crack the Clue II event. Giving the same bonuses as iron platelegs, they are part of the ornate armour set, and are obtainable only once if you already have them in your inventory or bank. If you happen to accidentally destroy them, you will need to discover them again.

In order to obtain/reobtain the legs, the player must dig East (Near blue flower) of the entrance to the Air Altar south of Falador with a raw herring in their inventory.

The ornate legs are a required item to solve Step 3 (Match-Catch) and Step 5 (Start-South-Numbers) of Crack the Clue III.
