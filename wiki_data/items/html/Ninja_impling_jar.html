A ninja impling jar is obtained as the result of successfully catching a Ninja impling, while also having an empty impling jar in your inventory. A Hunter level of 74 is required to catch ninja implings.

This jar can be traded in singularly to Elnock Inquisitor for 3 empty impling jars, but this is not recommended due to its high value.

Loot[edit | edit source]

At current Grand Exchange prices, opening a ninja impling jar gives 13,104.00 worth of loot, on average. This means opening ninja impling jars makes an average profit of −44,890.00 per jar, however this does not include loot from clue scrolls.

On average, one would profit −1,122,238.00 to get a hard clue scroll from ninja impling jars at current Grand Exchange prices, excluding the rewards from the completed Treasure Trails.

The following drop rates are provided by Jagex, unless otherwise specified.

Impling jar[edit | edit source]

An empty impling jar is returned when removing the loot, with a 10% chance of it breaking.[1]
