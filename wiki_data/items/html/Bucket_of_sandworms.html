A bucket of sandworms is obtained by digging sandworm castings north of Tynan's Fishing Supplies, granting 10 experience in Hunter. While digging sandworm castings, players may fail to find any sandworms, resulting in a bucket of sand instead.

In order to obtain buckets of sandworms, players require at least level 15 Hunter while carrying a spade and empty bucket. There is a 58-70% chance of obtaining a bucket of sandworm, depending on the player's Hunter level.

Each bucket holds between 1 and 5 sandworms as anglerfish bait.
