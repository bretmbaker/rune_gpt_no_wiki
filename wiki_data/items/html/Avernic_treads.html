Avernic treads are boots requiring level 80 in Defence, Strength, Ranged, and Magic to wield, dropped by Osto-Ayak.

The boots are dropped in their base form, and can be further upgraded by adding primordial boots, pegasian boots and eternal boots to them, along with a set amount of demon tears. Once any of the three boots have been added, the boots become untradeable until removed, which returns the base materials apart from the tears.

Development[edit | edit source]

The treads were originally required to have all three boot sets (primordial, pegasian and eternal) which would be combined with an avernic catalyst. Player concerns quickly arose about the requirement of pegasian boots, as ranger boots were much harder to acquire compared to dragon boots and infinity boots respectively. The new requirements were revealed on 11 March 2025, accounting for player feedback about the treads.
