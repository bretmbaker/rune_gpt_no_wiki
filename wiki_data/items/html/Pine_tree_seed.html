Pine tree seeds were obtained by searching various evergreen trees in north-east area of the WWF conservation area during the Big Cats & WWF holiday event. The trees would take several hours to grow, and could require secateurs to cure them of disease.

Once fully grown, players were able check their health and receive 284.5 farming experience. This would cause stags, does, male, and female amur leopards to populate the area.

Free-to-play players could obtain this item, but planting the seeds was a members only feature.
