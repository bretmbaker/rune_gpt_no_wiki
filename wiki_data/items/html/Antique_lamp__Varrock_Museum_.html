The antique lamp is an item obtained from the Varrock Museum upon giving Historian Minas various information for their displays:

  • Infinite can be obtained as a possible reward from cleaning: 500xp in a skill of at least 10.
    • Three from giving information about the quests Making History, Merlin's Crystal, and Shield of Arrav:[1] 1,000xp in a skill of at least level 20.
    • Giving information about the Defender of Varrock awards one antique lamp granting 5,000xp in a skill of at least 30.
    • Giving information about the Curse of the Empty Lord awards one antique lamp granting 10,000xp in a skill of at least 50.
