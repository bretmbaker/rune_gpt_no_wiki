The ring of suffering (i) is an upgraded version of the regular ring of suffering. It can be imbued as a reward from the Nightmare Zone, costing 725,000 reward points or 362,500 with the hard tier of Combat Achievements, at Soul Wars for 300 Zeal Tokens, or by using a scroll of imbuing purchased from the Emir's Arena. Its stats are double those of the ring of suffering.

The ring can also be charged with rings of recoil to give it the recoil effect, renaming the item as ring of suffering (ri). Each ring of recoil provides 40 charges, assuming the ring of recoil has not been drained prior. A maximum of 100,000 charges can be stored, equivalent to 2,500 rings and costing 2,387,500. Players can configure the charges while the ring is in their bank by right-clicking on Configure-Charges, as long as players have the required materials. If a player uncharges the ring, the recoil rings stored will not be refunded.

If the wearer dies to a monster, the ring will retain its imbued status even if not protected.
