Torn robes are used during the Spirits of the Elid quest. In order to get them, players must search the cupboards in the Elidinis Statuette in Nardah. There is no other way to get them, due to them not being tradeable. They must be repaired using a needle and thread to turn them into the Robe of elidinis, which the player must wear in order to enter the dungeon farther north along the river. The costume needle will not work for this. The torn version cannot be worn.
