The enchant dragonstone tablet is an item that can be used by any player to enchant dragonstone jewellery. Players can create this item on a mahogany demon lectern as long as they have 1 cosmic rune, 15 earth runes, 15 water runes, and 1 soft clay, and at least level 68 Magic. You gain 58.5 Magic experience for each tablet you create.

This tablet can be made regardless of the player's current spellbook. Using a Mud battlestaff can greatly reduce the cost of casting the spell.

Using this tablet for the elite clue scroll task does not work.
