A bird nest with seeds from Wyson can be obtained by trading mole claws and mole skins with Wyson in Falador Park. One nest is received for each claw or skin. They appear identical to regular seed nests obtained from Woodcutting, but have a separate drop table and stack separately in the bank.

Wyson will accept noted mole claws and mole skins, and will reward nest boxes if there isn't enough room in the inventory for all the nests.

Discontinued nests[edit | edit source]

The first variant of Wyson nests were released along with the Giant Mole. These nests originally rewarded relatively low level seeds, and were considered to not be valuable enough compared to the difficulty of the content. To improve the nests, Wyson nests were updated to a second variant on 18 February 2016 to reward more valuable seeds. The original obtained prior to this update could still exist in player banks and inventories and can be searched to provide seeds from the original reward table.

With the release of the Farming Guild, the current Wyson nests were introduced. All nests previously obtained would remain in banks and inventories, and all new nests obtained can reward snape grass, dragonfruit, celastrus, and redwood tree seeds. This change was made to prevent players from obtaining large quantities of the new seeds immediately upon release of the content.
