Bags of salt can be purchased from any Slayer Master, and are used to finish off rockslugs that have less than 5 Hitpoints. When used, the rockslug shrivels up and dies, consuming the bag of salt. Using a bag of salt on a rockslug with 5 or more Hitpoints has no effect but will still consume a bag of salt.

A bag (or pile) of salt is required during Icthlarin's Little Helper.
