Goutweed is a rare herb that is not currently used in Herblore, but rather traded to Sanfew in Taverley for random herbs at a 1:1 ratio.

Goutweed is obtained in three ways:

  • By sneaking past troll guards in the storeroom under Burntmeat's kitchen during (and after) the Eadgar's Ruse quest.
  • By growing from gout tubers in a herb patch, which are obtained occasionally from the Tai Bwo Wannai Cleanup minigame. This is not recommended as gout tubers are valued at 521,831 on the Grand Exchange.
  • Rare reward from Brambickle in the Gnome Restaurant minigame.

It is an item required in the Eadgar's Ruse, Dream Mentor and Dragon Slayer II quests. It is therefore recommended to gather at least three goutweed when you go to the storeroom for the first time, so that you do not have to go back multiple times.

Obtaining goutweed[edit | edit source]

Main article: Eadgar's Ruse § Getting goutweed

During and after Eadgar's Ruse, goutweed can be freely obtained in the Troll Stronghold. The goutweed is in the storeroom and guarded by trolls that must be avoided.

  • How to access the goutweed

  • Map of the Troll Stronghold with the storeroom on the right

Exchanging with Sanfew[edit | edit source]

Goutweed can be traded to Sanfew in Taverley in exchange for a herb, which is rolled off the standard herb drop table. The average goutweed exchange is worth 1,268.27.
