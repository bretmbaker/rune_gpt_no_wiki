The splitbark body is a piece of splitbark armour worn in the body slot. 40 Magic and 40 Defence are required to wear it. A splitbark body can be created by using a needle and thread on four pieces of fine cloth and four bark, requiring 62 Crafting. Alternatively, it can be made without the Crafting requirement by giving four bark, four fine cloth, and 37,000 coins to Wizard Jalarast at the Wizards' Tower. An alternative to this is the Lunar torso which has slightly lower defence stats.

It can be stored in a magic wardrobe in a player-owned house.
