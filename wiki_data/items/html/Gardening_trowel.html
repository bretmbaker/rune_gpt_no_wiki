A gardening trowel is used in the members only skill Farming. It is used both to fill a plant pot with earth from a weeded patch, and to plant a tree seed into that plant pot to begin the process of growing a tree.

These can be bought from Farming shops, or obtained by trading other players.

Players can withdraw unlimited amounts of gardening trowels from the tool store 5.

The tool leprechaun can store 100 of these tools for you.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
11 July 2005
(update)
  • The item was made obtainable.
  • The item's examine was changed from "Farming skill coming in July!" to "Not suitable for archaeological digs."
  • The item's value was increased from 3 to 12.
6 June 2005
(update)

The item was added to the game cache.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The gardening trowel was supposed to be released alongside other farming tools on 6 June 2005, and its examine info was updated on this day to reflect this. However, it did not actually become available to players until the release of Farming, presumably because Jagex forgot to add it to the drop table of Farmers.
