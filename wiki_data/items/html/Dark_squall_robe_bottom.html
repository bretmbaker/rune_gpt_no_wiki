The Dark squall robe bottom is a piece of Dark Squall robes obtained during While Guthix Sleeps, from a wardrobe in the Dark Squall's inner base within the Black Knight Catacombs. They are worn to disguise the player as the Dark Squall, later revealed to be Surok Magis; once acquired, they can also be used to enter the Black Knights' Fortress.

It has stats similar to that of a Zamorak monk bottom, and can be stored in a magic wardrobe in a player-owned house. After completion of While Guthix Sleeps, they can be reclaimed from Silif if lost.

The Dark squall robe bottom counts as a Zamorak item in the God Wars Dungeon.

On Death, even killed by a player with Skull (status) in The Deep Wilderness, the Dark Squall robes are not lost, and remain equipped.
