The corrupted plateskirt is an item introduced during the Christmas corrupt cluefest. To obtain the plateskirt, the player must bring an acorn and bones to Louie Legs in Al Kharid. The corrupted platelegs will also be obtained at the same time.

It has stats identical to that of an iron plateskirt.

This item can be stored in the armour case of a costume room, as part of the Corrupted armour set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
