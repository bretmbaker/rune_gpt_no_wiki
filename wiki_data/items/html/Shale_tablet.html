The shale tablet is an item obtained during The Curse of Arrav in the tunnels beneath Zemouregal's Fort, and is one of four tablets made by Lamistard, who dug the tunnels in an attempt to avoid being chosen as a sacrifice in the Mahjarrat's rituals.
