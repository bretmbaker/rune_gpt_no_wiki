Treacle is an item obtained during the 2018 Easter event. Goldie wants the player to solve a riddle involving different jars during the event to give them nuggets.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
12 April 2018
(update)
  • The item was graphically updated with the removal of the 2018 Easter event.
  • The item was renamed from "Treacle" to "Null".
