A cosmic talisman is a talisman needed to enter the Cosmic Altar in the southern part of Zanaris. It can be combined with a tiara to make a cosmic tiara for 40 Runecraft experience at the Cosmic Altar by using the tiara on the altar. This makes the talisman disappear, but the tiara can be used in just the same way and can be worn. Both the tiara and the talisman allow players to craft cosmic runes at level 27 Runecraft.

To enter the altar the player needs to have completed the Lost City quest or Enter the Abyss if using the Abyss.

Cosmic talismans can be obtained from monsters and also a possible reward from random events, as well as from caskets that are obtained from big fishing nets or killing Rock Crabs, Sand Crabs, and Ammonite Crabs.
