The bervirius notes is a quest item that is obtained during the Shilo Village quest by searching the dolmen in Tomb of Bervirius. The notes must be given to Trufitus in Tai Bwo Wannai Village.

When clicked, it will give you an option to read a Tattered Yellow papyrus, a Crusty Orange papyrus, or a Decayed White papyrus.

After the quest the notes can be sold to Yanni Salika for 100 coins.

If dropped flavour text reads, As you drop the delicate scrolls onto the floor, they disintegrate immediately.

Tattered Yellow papyrus

...and rest like your mother who is silent in the peace of her tomb far to the North of Ah Za Rhoon. Near the sea, and under the hills deep in the underground to watch all nature from the darkness of her final resting place.

Crusty Orange papyrus

...the sphere is activated when power of a spiritual nature is expended upon it, this can be very draining on the body...

...Rashiliyia did so love objects of beauty. Her tomb was adorned with crystals that glowed brightly when near to each other.
