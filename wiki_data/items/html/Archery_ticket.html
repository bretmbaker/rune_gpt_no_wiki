An archery ticket is the reward given for using the target range at the Ranging Guild. The quantity received varies from 0-100 tickets per 10 shots at the cost of 200 coins. They can be exchanged with the Ticket Merchant within the guild for prizes, most notably rune arrows and green d'hide bodies.

The archery ticket is one of the items that may be asked for during Mourning's End Part II.

Ticket exchange[edit | edit source]

You buy the whole quantity of items for the indicated price at the Ranging Guild Ticket Exchange. That is, for example, you buy 30 barb bolttips at once for 140 Archery tickets.
