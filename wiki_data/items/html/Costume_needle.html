The costume needle is an item obtained from the costumer in the backstage of Villa Lucens' theatre on Aldarin after the completion of Death on the Isle. Using it in place of a needle allows players to craft most items without any thread.

The following items/objects are known to not work with the costume needle:

  • Torn robes from Spirits of the Elid
  • Bellows in the Elemental Workshop
  • The new body of Lord Rologarth in Creature of Fenkenstrain
