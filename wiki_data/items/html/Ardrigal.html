Ardrigal is the clean form of grimy ardrigal, a herb obtained by searching the palm trees with large leaves on the north-eastern peninsula of Karamja, on the south side of the tree grapple shortcut. It is the second herb Trufitus asks the player to bring him during Jungle Potion, and is also used during Legends' Quest in combination with snake weed to make a bravery potion.

Trufitus will buy clean ardrigals for a random amount of coins between 1 and 4. This can only be done by using the herb on him, as there is no dialogue option for selling them to him. He will not buy grimy ardrigals, his reasoning being that he cannot identify what the herb is.

Ardrigal grows back 60 seconds after being picked.
