Apple mush is made by putting four cooking apples into an apple press, crushing them, and filling a bucket with the resulting product.

Brewing cider requires four apple mush in the fermenting vat, therefore 16 cooking apples are needed in total to make a full brew of eight beer glasses or two calquat kegs of cider or mature cider.
