Rocks are items created when a chisel is used on a piece of limestone in an attempt to craft a limestone brick unsuccessfully. 12 Crafting is required to do so, but there is a chance to fail. Failing to craft a limestone brick yields 1.5 experience. Players stop failing at level 40 Crafting.

Rocks can also sometimes be found inside barrels in the Shaman Caves.

This rock is obtained during In Aid of the Myreque, where the player must shift a great number of rubble from the basement of a building in Burgh de Rott during the quest, in order to prepare the area for use as the Myreque's new base of operations.
