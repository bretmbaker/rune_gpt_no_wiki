A master scroll book is an item obtainable from any level Treasure Trail except beginner. If a reward casket would reward any type of teleport scroll, there is a 1/23 chance for it to reward a master scroll book instead.[1]

The master scroll book can store up to 1000 of every type of teleport scroll, which is done by "using" the scroll with the book. Currently, to fill the entire book with 1000 teleports it would cost 212,302,000 coins, plus 1000 key master teleport scrolls which are untradeable.

Players can open their book to show an interface where they can either use or take out any of the stored teleport scrolls. They can also set one of the scrolls in it as the default teleport option, which becomes the left-click option of the book.

Players can exchange a master scroll book with Watson to receive 10-20 Watson teleport scrolls. This currently results in a profit of between −75,244 and 61,076 coins per master scroll book exchanged, with an average profit of −7,084 coins. Exchanging a master scroll book with scrolls inside does not cause the player to lose the scrolls, though the player must have or obtain another book to access them.
