A flyer is a virtually useless item that, when examined, advertises Bob's Brilliant Axes. Its only use is as a possible required item to exchange for a Kelda seed in the Forgettable Tale... quest.

It can be dropped by barbarians and imps or obtained as a reward from a mystery box from the Quiz Master random event. Talking to men or women also gives rise to a 1/128 chance of being offered a flyer.[1]

This item cannot be traded on the Grand Exchange.
