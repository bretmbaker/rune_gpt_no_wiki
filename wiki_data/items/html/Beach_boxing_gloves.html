Beach boxing gloves are holiday items released on 2nd July, 2013 to celebrate summer. They were found at the 2013 Midsummer event by Catherby beach, Mudskipper Point, and south of the Al Kharid bank. The gloves come in two colours: yellow and pink. The player can now purchase both sets of gloves from Diango in Draynor Village for 75gp each.

The gloves are similar to regular boxing gloves that can be found in a player-owned house once a boxing ring is constructed, albeit with having the additional capability of being able to be carried anywhere.
