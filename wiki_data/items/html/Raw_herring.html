Raw herring is an item that is obtained through the Fishing skill by using a fishing rod with fishing bait on a net/bait fishing spot using the bait option. Herrings can only be caught in fishing spots by the sea. Catching a raw herring grants 30 Fishing experience. It can be cooked into herring with a Cooking level of 5, granting 50 Cooking experience.

This fish can be caught at level 10 Fishing and cooked at level 5 Cooking.

River fishing spots do not provide raw herring.

Fishing locations[edit | edit source]

For all fishing spots, see Fishing spot (small net, bait).
  • Lumbridge Swamp: East coast
  • Draynor Village: South-west of Draynor bank
  • Al Kharid: South of Al Kharid bank
  • Mudskipper Point: South of Port Sarim, dotting the western shore of the peninsula, located behind the church and near Thurgo's shack
  • Karamja: North-east Karamja
  • Wilderness Bandit Camp: The small pond
  • Catherby beach: On the beach, south-east of the bank
  • Entrana: Platform to the south-east
  • Feldip Hills: West of the hunter area
  • Fishing Platform: Various spots
  • Rellekka: The port just north from Peer the Seer, north-west of the fishing stall
  • Port Sarim: West of altar (F2P possibility to sell fish in nearby shop)

Treasure Trails[edit | edit source]

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • One of Old School RuneScape's Monty Python references is revealed if players use a herring (cooked or raw) with almost any tree in Gielinor. The chatbox will state This is not the mightiest tree in the forest, in reference to one of the lines in Monty Python and the Holy Grail.
    • If used with a redwood tree that has fully grown in the Farming Guild, the chatbox will state This is not the mightiest tree in the forest; it is fairly mighty though.
    • If used with the Grand Tree, the chatbox will state It can't be done! in reference to another line in the movie.
    • If used with the blisterwood tree, the chatbox will state This is indeed a mighty tree. But alas, it is not the mightiest tree in the forest.
    • The Christmas Tree has no herring messages.
      • During the 2020 Christmas event, the message was indeterminable. The chatbox may state: I can't reach that! or have no message at all where the player may step towards the tree and step back again.
      • During the 2021 Christmas event, the chatbox states: Nothing interesting happens.
    • Several other ungatherable trees do not have any herring messages:
      • Evergreen tree from the 2021 Halloween event
      • Mac's evergreen
      • Strong Yew
      • Tree (Draynor guard)
      • Tree (tropical)
