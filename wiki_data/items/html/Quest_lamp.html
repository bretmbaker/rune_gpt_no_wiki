A quest lamp is given to players in Deadman: Apocalypse upon entering a new combat bracket. It is used to choose between one of two choices of questlines to complete.

Despite being a lamp, it does not provide the experience these quests would have given.

If the player's inventory is full when switching brackets the quest lamp will be placed in the player's bank.
