Unicorn horn dust is a Herblore ingredient obtained by using a unicorn horn with a pestle and mortar or as a drop from cave bugs and cave crawlers. It can be used with a marrentill potion (unf) to make antipoison or with an irit potion (unf) to make superantipoison. It is also one of the ingredients required to turn super restores into Sanfew serums.
