Kebbit teeth dust is obtained by grinding kebbit teeth, obtained by deadfalling a sabre-toothed kebbit at level 51 Hunter, with a pestle and mortar. The dust is a secondary ingredient for the Hunter potion, which can be created at level 53 Herblore.
