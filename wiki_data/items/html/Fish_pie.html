Fish pie is a type of pie that contains trout, cod and a potato. A fish pie restores 12 hitpoints in total with 2 bites each healing for 6. It also temporarily boosts Fishing by 3, less than the 5 given by the admiral pie. A pie dish will remain after both bites are consumed. It can be obtained by baking a raw fish pie on a cooking range, requiring level 47 Cooking and granting 164 experience when successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting in burnt pie. The burn rate decreases as the player's Cooking level increases, and it will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 74. Alternatively, casting the Bake Pie spell from the lunar spellbook will always create a pie without burning it.

Fish pie is a possible task from Romily Weaklax in the Cooks' Guild.

Steps
1. Use a pastry dough on a pie dish to make a pie shell
2. Use a trout on the pie shell to make a part fish pie (trout)
3. Use a cod on the part fish pie (trout) to make a part fish pie (cod)
4. Use a potato on the part fish pie (cod) to make a raw fish pie
5. Cook the raw fish pie on a range to finish a fish pie
