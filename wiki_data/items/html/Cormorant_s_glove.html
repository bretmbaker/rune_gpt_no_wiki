Cormorant's glove is an item obtained for free by speaking to Alry the Angler on Molch island. Requiring both the Hunter and Fishing skill to obtain, it can be used in Aerial fishing to catch Bluegills, Common tenches, Mottled eel, and Greater siren. Alongside the fish, players can also catch Molch pearls which can then be traded to Alry for a number of items which can assist in standard fishing.

In order to use the cormorant's glove, players must have the appropriate bait in their inventory. King worms can be found scattered around the island which can be used, however should the player bring a knife to the island or pick up the one that spawns there, they can use it to obtain Fish chunks from the many varieties of fish caught there. The player received 1 Fish chunk for each fish cut, regardless of the type.
