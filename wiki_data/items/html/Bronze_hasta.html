The bronze hasta is a one-handed melee members-only weapon, similar to the spear. It is the weakest hasta in Old School RuneScape. To wield this weapon, players need to have completed the Barbarian Training miniquest up through the Smithing section. To obtain a bronze hasta the player can either create one using the Smithing skill or trade with another player. It can be poisoned.
