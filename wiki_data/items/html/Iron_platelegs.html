Iron platelegs are melee leg armour made of iron. There is no Defence requirement to wear them, as is the same with bronze armour. As such, it is recommended to skip bronze tier items and start out with iron tier items. They have the same defensive stats as an iron plateskirt but weigh more.

They can be made at level 31 Smithing with 3 iron bars, granting 75 experience.
