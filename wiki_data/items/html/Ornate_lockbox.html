Ornate lockboxes are boxes found within the Camdozaal Vault, typically found towards the back of the vault. Players must exit the vault before the one minute timer runs out to keep their lockboxes; otherwise, they will lose all lockboxes they have acquired whilst inside. Lockboxes the player was carrying before entering the vault will not be lost.

Ornate lockboxes have a 6/50 (~1/8.33) chance to roll the gear drop table, which contains weapons and armour ranging from black to adamant.
