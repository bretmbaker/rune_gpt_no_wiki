A plank is a common Construction item and is used in several quests. Made from normal logs, it is the lowest-level type of plank. Each plank used while training Construction yields 29 experience. They are also used to repair fishing cranes, which yields Crafting and Construction experience equal to 4 times the player's current level.

Quests[edit | edit source]

  • Dragon Slayer I: 3 × planks to repair hole
  • Monk's Friend: 1 × plank (alternatively logs)
  • Underground Pass: 1 × plank
  • Observatory Quest: 3 × planks
  • Horror from the Deep: 2 × planks
  • In Search of the Myreque: 6 × planks to repair rope bridge
  • Mountain Daughter: 1 × plank
  • In Aid of the Myreque: 11 × planks
  • Royal Trouble: 1 × plank
  • Darkness of Hallowvale: 2 × planks
  • Cold War: 1 × plank to make clockwork suit
  • The Great Brain Robbery: 18 × planks
  • Getting Ahead: 2 × planks to make mounted head

  • Daddy's Home: 10 × planks

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Plank spawns in the Wilderness and elaborate or ornate lockboxes in the Camdozaal Vault, they are the only source for free-to-play players prior to starting Dragon Slayer I.
  • There are no uses for planks in free-to-play except for the Dragon Slayer I quest.
