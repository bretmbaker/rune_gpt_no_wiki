The King's message is a quest item that players receive from the King's messenger 4 to 12 minutes after logging in, once the player has completed the Underground Pass quest.[1] When players receive it, it is a signal to them that they can start the Regicide quest.

Transcript[edit | edit source]

The following text is transcluded from Transcript:King's message.

Squire [player name].

You are needed to serve the Kingdom of Kandarin. The Well of Voyage has been reopened. We must discuss your next commission.

His Majesty, King Lathas

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
17 – 24 November 2004

The item was renamed from "Kings[sic] message" to "King's message".

14 October –
2 November 2004

The item's examine was changed from "A summons from King Luthas."[sic] to "A summons from King Lathas."

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Although this item is used for a members' quest, players can still view and receive it in free-to-play worlds.
