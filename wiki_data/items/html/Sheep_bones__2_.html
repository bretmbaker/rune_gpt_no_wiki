Sheep bones (2) is a quest item used only in Sheep Herder. It is dropped by a Green Sheep that is in the pen and after feeding it sheep feed.

The bones cannot be buried but are instead incinerated.

Date Changes

  • The item was renamed from "Bones" to "Sheep bones (2)".
  • The item's examine was changed from "These sheep remains are infected." to "The suspicious-looking remains of a suspicious-looking sheep."
