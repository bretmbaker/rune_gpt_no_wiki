The Zamorak monk top is part of the Zamorak monk robes set. They are bright red, and like the related Zamorak monk bottom they are dropped by disciples of Iban, necromancers and monks of Zamorak.

Zamorak monk robes are commonly used by mages in the Wilderness due to providing decent Prayer and Magic bonuses and being easy to replace if lost. They can also be used to telegrab Wine of Zamorak at the Chaos Temple (Asgarnia) without being attacked.

One of only three free-to-play bodies with a magic attack bonus, the Zamorak monk top gets -1 Magic attack and +3 Prayer bonus compared to the Black robe, Blue wizard robe, and their ornamental variants.

The Zamorak monk top counts as a Zamorak item in the God Wars Dungeon.
