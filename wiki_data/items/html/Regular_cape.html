Regular cape

From Old School RuneScape Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

A regular cape is any cape which can be coloured with dye. Despite being similar in appearance, the Fremennik cloaks are not capable of being dyed. All capes are available for free-to-play players except for the pink cape, which can only be dyed by members who have started the quest The Hand in the Sand.

Recolouring[edit | edit source]

The following combinations can be created by using dyes on any of the regular capes:

Cape colour Dye needed Sold in Dropped by
Black cape Black dye Barkers' Haberdashery, Shayzien Styles Highwaymen (always)
Blue cape Blue dye Barkers' Haberdashery, Valaine's Shop of Champions, Shayzien Styles, Floria's Fashion Not dropped
Green cape Green dye Barkers' Haberdashery, Floria's Fashion Not dropped
Orange cape Orange dye Not sold Not dropped
Pink cape Pink dye Not sold. Not dropped
Purple cape Purple dye Not sold Not dropped
Red cape Red dye Barkers' Haberdashery Some goblins (1/128)
Yellow cape Yellow dye Barkers' Haberdashery, Fancy Clothes Store, Shayzien Styles, Floria's Fashion Not dropped
