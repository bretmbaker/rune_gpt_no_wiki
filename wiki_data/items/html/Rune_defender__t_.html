The rune defender (t) is a rune defender with a rune defender ornament kit attached to it. The attached ornament kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the defender.

The trimmed defender can be dismantled anytime, returning the defender and the tradeable ornament kit. Lorelai will not recognise the rune defender (t) as a rune defender for access to the basement cyclopes.

A trouver parchment can be brought to Perdu alongside the item to "lock" it, preventing it from being lost in the Wilderness for one death. If locked upon a PvP death, the killer will receive the value of the trouver parchment and the ornament kit, while the unornamented defender will be returned to the owner.
