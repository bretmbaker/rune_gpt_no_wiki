The polar camo top is a members only piece of the polar camouflage gear. To make one, bring two polar kebbit furs and 20 coins to the Fancy Clothes Store in Varrock, to Pellem in the Hunter Guild, or to Prifddinas' Seamstress.

When worn, it reduces weight by 3 kg. Additionally, though the examine claims so, it does not affect the trapping of hunter creatures.

This item can be stored in the armour case of a costume room, as part of the Camouflage gear: Polar set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
