The crystal of Cadarn is a crystal that can be bought from Lliann's Wares in the Ithell district of Prifddinas. It is used on the crystal crown, corrupted Blade of Saeldor, corrupted Bow of Faerdhinen and crystal armour to give them the green colour of Clan Cadarn.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
8 February 2023
(update | poll)

The crystal can now be used to recolour crystal armour.

7 July 2021
(update)

The time it takes for the Elven Clan crystals to fully restock has been drastically decreased. Previously, it took three days, eleven hours, and twenty minutes for one crystal to restock.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The previous shop restock time was equal to 500,000 game ticks, one tick per coin that the item costs.
