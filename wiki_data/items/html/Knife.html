A knife is a multi-functional tool used for a large variety of tasks, primarily used for fletching logs in the Fletching skill and cutting food in the Cooking skill. It sees secondary usage in the Crafting skill. Additionally, it can be used to cut through webs and setup Hunter traps.

They are sold in many general stores, at Trader Stan's Trading Post on Karamja, or the Ardougne Spice Stall. A knife can also be found by searching a sack between two webs in front of the lever to the Mage Arena bank, if the player does not have a knife in their inventory.

Uses[edit | edit source]

Quests[edit | edit source]

  • Plague City: 1 × knife to make chocolate dust (alternatively pestle and mortar)
  • Elemental Workshop I: 1 × knife to make slashed book and battered key
  • Recipe for Disaster (Freeing Pirate Pete subquest): 1 × knife to make breadcrumbs
  • A Porcine of Interest: 1 × knife (alternatively any slash weapon except abyssal whip, abyssal tentacle, or dragon claws)
  • Below Ice Mountain: 1 × knife to make steak sandwich

Miniquests[edit | edit source]

  • Skippy and the Mogres: 1 × knife to make chocolate dust (alternatively pestle and mortar)

Other[edit | edit source]

  • Slashing webs. (Note: As opposed to the knife, the Wilderness sword 1 or better has a 100% success-rate)
  • Cutting cacti in the Kharidian Desert to fill waterskins.
  • Setting up deadfall and pitfall traps with the Hunter skill.
  • Cutting open Giant egg sac and Scaly blue dragonhide (while at a bank).

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Before RuneScape first launched in 2001, the knife was originally intended to be wieldable as a weapon. Although the option to wield it was removed before launch, its code with weapon stats still remained. For a brief time there was a bug where players could wield the knife, and also wield multiple knives at once, stacking their stats and giving them an insane strength bonus. This glitch has since been patched. (See knife bug)
