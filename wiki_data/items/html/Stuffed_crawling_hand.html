A stuffed crawling hand is obtained by bringing a crawling hand and 1,000 coins to the taxidermist in Canifis; this process is reversible by selecting the "Unstuff" option on the item.

Once stuffed, it can be mounted at a teak, mahogany, or gilded display within the skill hall in a player-owned house, granting 31 Construction and 261 Slayer experience.
