The blue pirate bandana is a piece of headwear. Pirate bandanas can be attached to a right eye patch to make a blue bandana eyepatch by talking to Patchy on Mos Le'Harmless, along with 500 coins. Doing so requires having the book o' piracy in the player's inventory.
