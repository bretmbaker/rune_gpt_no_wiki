The Sigil of the Menacing Mage is a tier 2 combat Deadman Mode sigil. Like all sigils, the un-attuned version is dropped by most monsters (via the Deadman Mode drop table) and is tradeable on the Grand Exchange. It can be activated by clicking Attune.

Effect[edit | edit source]

Event Effect
Deadman: Armageddon Upon dealing Magic damage, you have a 10% chance to curse the target. The curse deals 18 damage, 33% less damage in the 3 to 50 bracket, over the next 6 seconds and heals you for the same amount. A target can only be under the effect of one curse at a time.
Deadman: Apocalypse Upon dealing Magic damage, you have a 20% chance to curse the target. The curse deals 12 damage over the next 6 seconds and heals you for the same amount. A target can only be under the effect of one curse at a time.
Deadman: Reborn Upon dealing Magic damage, you have a 20% chance to curse the target. The curse deals 12 damage over the next 6 seconds and heals you for the same amount. A target can only be under the effect of one curse at a time. You also gain an additional 50% XP in the Magic skill.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
23 August 2023
(update)

The sigil no longer gives a 50% XP bonus in Magic for Deadman: Apocalypse.
