The enhanced crystal teleport seed is a crystal seed that can be used with 100 crystal shards at a singing bowl to create an eternal teleport crystal—which functions as a teleport crystal with unlimited teleport charges.

If the player does not meet the 80 Smithing and 80 Crafting requirements, they can have Conwenna or Reese sing the crystal for them for an extra 50 crystal shards, for a total of 150.

Players can also exchange the enhanced crystal teleport seed for 150 crystal shards by speaking to Amrod in Prifddinas' Hefin Inn. They must be unnoted.

Crystal teleport seeds cannot be exchanged with Amrod for crystal shards and only function to allow players to have multiple teleport crystals.

Note: The effect of the rogue equipment does apply to this item; the player will have a chance to receive two enhanced seeds if they are wearing parts of the outfit, with it guaranteed if the full set is worn.
