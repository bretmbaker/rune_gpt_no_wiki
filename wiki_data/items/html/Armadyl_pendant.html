The Armadyl pendant is a quest item in the Temple of Ikov quest. A Guardian of Armadyl in the Temple of Ikov dungeon gives the player the pendant if the player sides with them. Equipping it allows players to attack Lucien, once he is dead, the quest is complete.

This pendant is the easiest obtainable protection against Armadyl and followers of Armadyl in the God Wars Dungeon.

If lost it can be reobtained by:

  • Talk to a Guardian of Armadyl if sided with Guardians of Armadyl during the quest.
  • Killing a Guardian of Armadyl if sided with Lucien during the quest.

A shiny key is needed for the fastest method. Down the ladder of the house in McGrubor's Wood, walk west until there is a wall with the "Push" option. Depending on alignment, kill or talk to a Guardian for a replacement pendant.

Multiple Armadyl pendants can be obtained through the drop trick.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The Armadyl pendant shares its design with the pendant of lucien and the unobtainable Lathas' amulet, the only difference being the colour of the inner gem. The pendant of lucien's gem is green for males, whereas the Armadyl pendant's gem is silver on males. Both pendants have an identical red gem for females.
  • The amulet's original examine text, Yet another amulet., was joking at the fact that you receive the pendant of lucien at the beginning of the quest, and then you receive another pendant later on if siding with the Guardians of Armadyl.
  • Queen Sigrid and Princess Astrid both appear to be wearing Armadyl pendants.
