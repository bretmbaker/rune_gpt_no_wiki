A bolt of cloth is used in the Construction skill to build objects such as wooden beds, curtains and also rugs. Bolts of cloth can be purchased from a Sawmill Operator and provide 15 Construction experience each when used.

One bolt of cloth is used in the quest Making Friends with My Arm, and five are used in the Daddy's Home miniquest.
