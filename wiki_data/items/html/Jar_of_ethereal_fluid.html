Jars of ethereal fluid are an item that was involved in the 2015 Halloween event, during which the player must evict the Anti-Santa for the Grim Reaper. It was found in the kitchen and searching the refrigerator. They must be used to catch souls in the graveyard, to obtain a trapped soul.
