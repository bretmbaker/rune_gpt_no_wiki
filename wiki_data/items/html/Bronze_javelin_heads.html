Bronze javelin heads are items that can be made at level 6 Smithing, granting 5 javelin heads and 12.5 experience per bronze bar. They can be fletched into bronze javelins at level 3 Fletching when used with javelin shafts, which are made by using regular logs with a knife, granting 15 experience per 15 javelins made.
