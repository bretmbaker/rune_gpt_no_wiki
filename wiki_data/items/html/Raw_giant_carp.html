A raw giant carp is a quest item used only in Fishing Contest. It requires Level 10 Fishing and is caught with a fishing rod and red vine worms after stuffing the pipe with a garlic.

It is given to Bonzo in order to win the Fishing trophy.

Bonzo will take all copies of raw giant carp in the player's inventory. After receiving the fishing trophy more fish can no longer be caught. Catching one gives no Fishing experience. It can be cooked into a giant carp, but this gives no Cooking experience. A cooked giant carp heals 6 Hitpoints.
