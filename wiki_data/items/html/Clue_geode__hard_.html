A clue geode (hard) is randomly obtained from Mining. Opening the geode gives a hard clue scroll.

The ring of wealth (i) doubles the rate of clue geode drops above beginner clue geode from Mining in the Wilderness.

Item sources[edit | edit source]

The exact rate of obtaining a clue geode is based on your Mining level and the completed reward tier of Combat Achievements. For more precise rates, use the calculator on the clue geodes page.
