Sweetcorn seeds can be planted at level 20 Farming. 3 seeds are sown in an allotment patch to grow sweetcorn plants, which yield sweetcorn: an item used in Cooking. They take 1 hour to grow.

A nearby gardener will watch over growing sweetcorn plants for 10 jute fibres. Fully grown white lilies in a nearby flower patch will also protect the sweetcorn from disease. Alternatively, a scarecrow may be placed in a flower patch for the same effect.

Many medium and low-level monsters drop sweetcorn seeds, including guards, farmers, and hill giants. These seeds can be stolen from master farmers and seed stalls. There is a small chance to obtain a sweetcorn seed while picking sweetcorn from a field.

9 sweetcorn is needed to protect growing apple trees.

Average harvest yield[edit | edit source]

Sweetcorn has a minimum of 3 harvest lives, increasing by 1 for each tier of compost used (for a maximum of 6 when using ultracompost). While harvesting sweetcorn, there is a chance to not consume a harvest life dependent on the player's Farming level. This chance scales from 34.8% at level 1 to 70.7% at level 99, interpolating linearly in between.

Magic secateurs (equipped or in inventory) increase the chance of receiving additional yield by 10%.
