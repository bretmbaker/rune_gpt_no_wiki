A mithril battleaxe is a battleaxe made from 3 mithril bars, requiring 60 Smithing, yielding 150 Smithing experience. 20 Attack is required to wield it. It is stronger than the black battleaxe, but weaker than the adamant battleaxe.

Players can purchase it from Brian's Battleaxe Bazaar in Port Sarim, Bob's Brilliant Axes in Lumbridge, or Gulluck and Sons in the Tree Gnome Stronghold.
