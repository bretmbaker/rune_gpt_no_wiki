Fat snails are dropped by Bruise blamish snails and Blood blamish snails in Mort Myre Swamp. They are made into fat snail meat with 22 Cooking yielding 95 Cooking experience. It heals 7-9 Hitpoints. These can be used for Temple Trekking.

It may also be used in Fairytale I - Growing Pains as a quest item for magic secateurs.

There is a right-click option to 'Grind' the fat snail. This is used to produce Blamish snail slime used in the Blamish oil for the Heroes' Quest. You are required to have a sample bottle in your inventory to collect it.
