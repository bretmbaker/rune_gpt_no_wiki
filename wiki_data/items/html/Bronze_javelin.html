The bronze javelin is a type of member's only Ranged ammunition used by the light and heavy ballistas. They are the weakest type of javelin. As with most javelins, they can be poisoned.

They can be fletched by attaching bronze javelin heads to javelin shafts at level 3 Fletching, granting 15 experience per 15 javelins made. They can also be obtained by buying them from the Ranging Guild, the Void Knight Archery Store or from Oobapohk's Javelin Store after Monkey Madness II.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
31 October 2016
 Hotfix

The Ranged Strength bonus of bronze javelins has been decreased from +30 to +25.[1]

6 May 2016
(update)
  • Bronze javelins are no longer equipable in the Weapon slot, now being equipable in the Ammunition slot as ammo for ballistae.
  • The Ranged Attack bonus has been removed; previously, it was +5.
  • The Ranged Strength bonus has increased from +6 to +30.
10 December 2014
(update)

The super strong poisoned variant was renamed from "Bronze jav'n(p++)" to "Bronze javelin(p++)".

20 September 2006
(update)
  • The extra strong poisoned variant was renamed from "Bronze javelin(+)" to "Bronze javelin(p+)".
  • The super strong poisoned variant was renamed from "Bronze javelin(s)" to "Bronze jav'n(p++)".
29 March 2004
(update)

The item became permanently available with the launch of RuneScape 2.

21 January 2004
(update)

The item became obtainable in the RuneScape 2 Beta.

1 December 2003
(update)

The item was added to the RuneScape 2 Beta cache.
