The book of the dead is an equipable book obtained as a reward from completing A Kingdom Divided. Having it equipped or in the player's inventory is required when casting resurrection spells.

As it is an improved version of Kharedst's memoirs, the book is able to teleport to the same locations as the memoirs, and holds up to 250 teleport charges (150 + 20 per page added) compared to the memoirs' 100 (20 per page added).

If lost, the book of the dead can be reclaimed from Commander Fullore, who is located on the ground floor of the Kourend Castle. Multiple copies can be purchased from Perdu at the cost of 9,500 coins.

Recharging[edit | edit source]

In order to recharge the book, players must inspect the Old Memorial north of Land's End.

Each charge costs 1 law, body, mind, and soul rune (315 per charge), granting the player 10 Magic experience per charge added. The maximum amount of charges the book can hold is 250 (150 + 20 per page added). In order to fully charge the book, you will need 250 law, body, mind and soul runes, costing in total 78,750

Location of the Old Memorial.

Players can reach the memorial via the following:

  • Teleporting to the Kourend Woodland via Rada's blessing, then head north-west.
  • Teleporting to Xeric's Lookout via Xeric's talisman, then head south-west.
  • Teleporting to Shayzien via Book of the dead (History and hearsay), then head south-east.
  • Taking a ship to Land's End by talking to Cabin Boy Herbert at Port Piscarilius or Port Sarim, then head north.
