The address label is an item used during Tribal Totem. Players can obtain it by searching the double-stacked crates south-east of the southernmost bank in East Ardougne in the GPDT Depot.

The player must then use the label on the teleport block created by Wizard Cromperty so that it is delivered to Lord Handelmort's Mansion, after which the player can teleport into his house to continue the quest.
