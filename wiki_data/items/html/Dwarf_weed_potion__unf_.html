A dwarf weed potion (unf) is an unfinished potion made by using a dwarf weed on a vial of water, requiring 72 Herblore. Using a wine of zamorak on a dwarf weed potion (unf) with 72 Herblore yields a ranging potion(3) and 162.5 Herblore experience. Using nihil dust on a dwarf weed potion (unf) with 85 Herblore yields an ancient brew(3) and 190 Herblore experience. Using a lily of the sands on a dwarf weed potion (unf) with 88 Herblore yields a Menaphite remedy(3) and 200 Herblore experience.
