A reward casket (medium) is received after completing the final clue of a medium clue scroll trail, and contains the final reward for the trail.

While players can only have one of each type of clue scroll in their possession, they can possess unlimited amounts of reward caskets, and are eligible to receive clue scrolls again once it is obtained.

Rewards[edit | edit source]

Medium clues generate a minimum of 3 rewards and a maximum of 5, granting an average of 4 rewards per casket. The rewards consist of generic items, shared treasure trail items and items that are unique for medium clues. Additionally, players have a 1/30 chance to receive a master clue scroll upon opening a medium casket.

All droprates are confirmed by Mod Tide.[1][2] The listed rarities are per roll.

The average loot roll is worth 42,396.00, for an average value per casket of 169,587.00. The 1/30 chance of receiving a master clue scroll adds another 37,653.00, for a total expected value per clue of 204,839.00.

However, these averages are heavily skewed by the extremely high value of ranger boots compared to the rest of possible rewards. When they are removed from consideration, the average loot roll drops to 12,194.00, and the average medium clue drops to 48,778.00 when not factoring master clues.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
15 August 2019
(update | poll)

The locks on the reward casket have been changed to reflect the tier of Treasure Trail it comes from.

11 April 2019
(update | poll)

All Treasure Trails caskets were changed to be stackable. To bolster each tier of clue with new cosmetic items, nine new items were added as medium clue scroll rewards.

6 July 2016
(update | poll)

All of the lower tier clues are getting many new additions to the drop tables.

14 August 2014
(update)

Bandos cloaks and mitres are now available from medium clue scrolls after accidentally being left off the reward list.

26 June 2014
(update | poll)

The Holy sandals are now available as a reward from medium clue scrolls.

12 June 2014
(update | poll)

There are over 180 new items available to be discovered by solving clue scrolls including third age weaponry, new outfits and a host of other items. There are new rewards and brand new clues for every level of treasure trail.

6 March 2014
(update | poll)

Clue scrolls and caskets now state the levels in their names.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Yew comp bows used to be as common a reward as yew shortbows, while players were not able to receive yew longbows as a reward at all. However after a game update on 25 January 2018, players started reporting yew longbows as a new common reward from their medium clue scrolls. Since then, yew comp bows also seem to be rarer to get as a reward. No changes to the Treasure Trails rewards table were mentioned in the official update post.
  • Along with the Treasure Trail expansion on 6 July 2016, the Ancient, Armadyl and Bandos vestment sets were completed with mitres, cloaks, stoles and croziers. These items were added as a reward for medium clue scrolls, even though Saradomin, Zamorak and Guthix stoles and croziers are rewards of hard clue scrolls.
  • With the Treasure Trail expansion on 12 June 2014 update, 43 new unique rewards were added to medium clue scrolls. Two weeks later, Holy sandals were added in a 26 June 2014 update. The Treasure Trail expansion on 6 July 2016 added another 12 unique rewards to medium clue scrolls.
  • Mod Mat K revealed in a 2016 data stream[3] that on average 7,000 medium clue scrolls were completed every day.
