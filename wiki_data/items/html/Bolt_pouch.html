A bolt pouch allows a player to carry multiple kinds of bolts in one inventory slot, including bolt racks, as well as mith grapples, and swap between two types of bolts mid-combat. It can be purchased for 1,500 from Hirko, who runs the crossbow shop in Keldagrim on the east side of the river. Players must ask Hirko about ammo in order to be given the option to purchase a pouch; it cannot be bought in Hirko's normal shop.

Within the bolt pouch, there are four slots that can each hold 10,000 bolts of a single type, or one Mith grapple. To store items in the pouch, players must use them on the pouch. Left-clicking on the pouch will open an interface showing what bolts are in the pouch and what the player has equipped in their ammo and extra ammo slots. Bolts can be moved between slots via left click and dragging.

In addition to storing ammo, the bolt pouch allows players to seamlessly swap between their equipped ammo and one extra ammo slot. This can be done via left click from the Worn Equipment tab without interrupting the player's current action, including attacking enemies. This does not work with non-ammo items, such as Ghommal's penny or Rada's blessing - these items can only be equipped in the primary ammo slot, and switching ammo from the extra slot into the primary slot will unequip them.

If the player has Dizana's quiver equipped or in their inventory it will disable the bolt pouch's functionality to swap between their equipped ammo and the extra ammo in the bolt pouch. The Worn Equipment tab will not show the extra ammo in the bolt pouch—only the ammo in the quiver. If the player attempts to move ammo from the bolt pouch into their ammunition slot using the bolt pouch interface, it will not work, and a message will warn the player You cannot change ammo from your bolt pouch with Dizana's quiver on your person.

Attempting to use an Alchemy spell on the bolt pouch fails and results in the message The dwarven pouch resists your magic. appearing in the chatbox.

If a player dies while carrying a bolt pouch in the Wilderness above level 20, they will lose the bolt pouch. If a player dies in PVP, the bolt pouch will be destroyed. When destroyed the contents will be preserved, but will be dropped to the player who killed them if not protected.

If an Ultimate Ironman loses the bolt pouch on death, either though despawning from a pve deathpile or being wiped from an item retrieval service, its contents will be preserved and can be reclaimed by buying a new bolt pouch from Hirko.

The bolt pouch is not obtainable on Deadman Mode worlds.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
17 March 2021
(update | poll)
  • The bolt pouch interface has been updated, adds a new Extra Ammo slot to the pouch.
  • The bolt pouch is no longer destroyed on death.
  • Mith grapples and Bolt racks can now be stored in the pouch.
  • The item's value was decreased from 5,000 to 10.
17 August 2017
(update | poll)

The amount of bolts that can be stored per slot was increased from 255 to 10,000.

9 January 2014
(update)

The bolt pouch will now store broad bolts.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Kebbit bolts, long kebbit bolts, Sunlight antler bolts, and Moonlight antler bolts cannot be stored in the pouch.
