A chef's hat is a white hat that grants players access to the Cooks' Guild with a Cooking level of 32. Aside from this, it has little practical use and is purely cosmetic.

The hat is dropped by several monsters, often to fill space in their drop tables.

Members can obtain an infinite supply from Teak shelves 2 in a player-owned house using the Construction skill.
