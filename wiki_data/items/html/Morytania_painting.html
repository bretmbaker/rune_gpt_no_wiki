The Morytania painting is an item that players can buy from Sir Renitee in White Knights' Castle for 2000 coins, provided that they have completed Shades of Mort'ton, Haunted Mine, Creature of Fenkenstrain and Ghosts Ahoy.

It is used in the Quest Hall of a player-owned house (along with three teak planks) to build Morytania in the landscape painting hotspot. Doing so requires level 44 Construction, and grants 314 experience.
