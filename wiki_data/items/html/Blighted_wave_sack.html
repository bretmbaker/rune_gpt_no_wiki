A blighted wave sack was an item purchased from the Emir's Arena reward shop. It allowed players to cast any wave spells without needing the runes for it. However, it could only be used inside the Wilderness while on the standard spellbook.

The spell sack was consumed upon use, regardless of a successful hit.

On PVP death, unprotected sacks would be converted to 120 coins and dropped as loot for the killer.

Now the Blighted surge sack is able to cast wave spells, making the wave sack redundant.
