The golden feather is a quest item used in the Eagles' Peak quest. It is found on a pedestal inside the caves under Eagles' Peak.

The player must use the golden feather along with a bronze feather and silver feather in order to open a locked door that leads deeper into the caves. The feather can also be obtained after the quest and multiple copies can be obtained by banking the duplicates.
