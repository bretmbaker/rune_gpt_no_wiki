The honourable blessing can be found as a reward from all levels of Treasure Trails, except beginner. The scroll's examine text reflects the philosophy of Armadyl.

The scroll can be equipped in the ammunition slot, and provides a +1 prayer bonus. In addition, it provides protection from Armadylean forces in the God Wars Dungeon.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room.
