The ancient staff is a one-handed magical weapon that can be used to autocast spells from the Ancient spellbook and standard spellbook. It requires 50 Attack and 50 Magic to wield, along with completion of Desert Treasure I. The staff is decorated with the symbol of Zaros.

It is frequently used in high-level player-killing due to its high magical accuracy. It, along with its upgraded versions, are the only items that have negative Prayer bonus.

Similarly to bladed staves, this staff has an attack speed of 4, making it a suitable weapon for melee combat, displaying similar stats as those of the rune scimitar with slightly higher strength bonus and lower attack bonus. It is outclassed in melee combat by the granite hammer, which also requires 50 Attack to wield, with an additional requirement of 50 Strength.

The staff may be bought once from Eblis for 80,000 coins after completing Desert Treasure I. For this reason, Ironmen are not recommended to alch or drop it because it is used in a master clue step.

The ancient staff can be upgraded to the ancient sceptre by bringing an ancient staff and an ancient icon to Eblis. This process is irreversible and the sceptre is untradeable.

Treasure Trails[edit | edit source]

The ancient staff is required for a master clue step requiring the player to have a negative prayer bonus. It is one of six items in the game with a negative prayer bonus, the others being its upgraded version, the ancient sceptre, and the upgrades to the ancient sceptre.
