The nature impling jar is the result of successfully catching a Nature impling, while also having an empty impling jar. A player needs level 58 Hunter to catch them.

This jar can be traded in singularly to Elnock Inquisitor for 3 empty impling jars, but this is not recommended due to its high value.

You can trade one of these jars as well as three essence impling jars and two eclectic impling jar for a jar generator.

Loot[edit | edit source]

At current Grand Exchange prices, opening a nature impling jar gives 1,967.00 worth of loot, on average. This means opening nature impling jars makes an average profit of −4,695.00 per jar, however this does not include loot from clue scrolls.

On average, one would profit −469,515.00 to get a hard clue scroll from nature impling jars at current Grand Exchange prices, excluding the rewards from the completed Treasure Trails.

The following drop rates are provided by Jagex, unless otherwise specified.

Impling jar[edit | edit source]

An empty impling jar is returned when removing the loot, with a 10% chance of it breaking.[1]
