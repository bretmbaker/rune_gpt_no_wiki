Saradomin's blessed sword is a two-handed sword which requires 75 Attack to wield. It is created by combining Saradomin's tear with the Saradomin sword. The blessed sword has +18 Slash and +6 Strength more than the normal variant.

If the sword is not protected upon death, it will fully degrade into Saradomin's tear. However, if it is protected on death, it will remain in its regular state as Saradomin's blessed sword.

Special attack[edit | edit source]

Saradomin's blessed sword has a special attack, Saradomin's Lightning, which deals a Magic-based attack (Magical melee) that increases the player's max hit by 25%, consuming 65% of the player's special attack energy.

The special attack rolls against the opponent's Magic defence.[1] Under perfect conditions, the special attack can hit up to 72. Despite being Magic-based, it will still inflict damage on Cyclops in the Warriors' Guild and soldiers inside the Shayzien combat ring.

Degradation[edit | edit source]

Saradomin's blessed sword degrades as it is used. After 10,000 hits with the weapon (400 minutes of continuous combat), it will degrade and consume the Saradomin sword, leaving the player with Saradomin's tear.

When using the items on each other there will be a warning message that states A blessed Saradomin sword is untradeable, with an Attack requirement of 75. After 10,000 hits, the sword crumbles to dust, and you get the Tear back. Each hit costs 15.35, costing 23,029.20 per hour of constant use.
