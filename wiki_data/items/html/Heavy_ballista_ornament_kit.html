The heavy ballista ornament kit is an untradeable ornament kit purchased from the Bounty Hunter Store for 50 points. It can change the appearance of the heavy ballista, turning it into a heavy ballista (or).

Once attached, the ornamented item would become untradeable, but the kit could be detached from the item.
