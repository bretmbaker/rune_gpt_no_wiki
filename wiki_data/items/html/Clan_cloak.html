Clan cloaks are capes that players can obtain from the Clerk in the Clan Hub. Players must be in a clan to claim a clan cloak and clan vexillum.

The clan motif that is on the cloak can be customised in the Clan Settings interface by those with the appropriate clan permissions. Upon closing of the customisation interface, the new clan motif will automatically update for all clan members.
