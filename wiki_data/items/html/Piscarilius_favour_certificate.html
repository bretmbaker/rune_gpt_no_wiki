The Piscarilius favour certificate was a quest reward for completing The Queen of Thieves. Reading this granted the player 10% Piscarilius favour, where there would be a confirmation message before doing so. Should the player have wished to use it some other time, they could destroy it (if bank space was an issue) and reclaim it from Lady Shauna Piscarilius

The certificate was removed alongside the entire Kourend Favour system on 10 January 2024 with the release of Children of the Sun.

  • The examine text was changed; previously, it was "I can use this to gain Piscarilius Favour."

(update)

The examine text was changed; previously, it was "I can use this to gain favour with Piscarilius House."
