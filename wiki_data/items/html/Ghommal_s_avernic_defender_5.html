Ghommal's Avernic defender 5 is an Avernic defender with Ghommal's hilt 5 attached to it. The defender retains the benefits given by the hilt and the bonuses given by the Avernic defender.

The defender can be dismantled anytime, returning the defender and the hilt.

A trouver parchment can be brought to Perdu alongside the item to "lock" it, preventing it from being lost in the Wilderness for one death.
