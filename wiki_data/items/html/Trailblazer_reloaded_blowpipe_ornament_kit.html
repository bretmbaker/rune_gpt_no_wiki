The Trailblazer reloaded blowpipe ornament kit is an ornament kit that changes the appearance of an empty toxic blowpipe to a blazing blowpipe. This process can be reverted at any time returning the item and ornament kit.

It can be purchased from the Leagues Reward Shop for 6,000 League Points.
