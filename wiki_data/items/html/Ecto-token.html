Ecto-tokens are a currency used in Port Phasmatys. In addition to being used to enter the town, they have several uses in and around the area, and can also be used to charge a bonecrusher. Players may obtain them by offering bonemeal to the Ectofuntus.

Obtaining Ecto-tokens[edit | edit source]

Main article: Ectofuntus

Ecto-tokens can be earned by worshipping the Ectofuntus, granting 5 tokens and a certain amount of Prayer experience per worship.

Players need any type of bones, a pot, and a bucket of slime to complete the ritual, with one of each required for every offering. Slime can be collected in the basement, using empty buckets to collect the pool of slime. Players must grind the bones into a pot using the Bone grinder on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] of the Ectofuntus. Then, head to the ground floor and worship the ectofuntus. The bones and slime will be consumed, leaving you with the empty bucket and empty pot. This will grant four times the usual Prayer experience. Speaking with a ghost disciple while wearing a ghostspeak amulet or Morytania legs 2+ allows the player to claim the tokens. Ghost disciples can hold a maximum of 1,000 tokens before forcing players to collect them. 13 bonemeal and 13 buckets of slime will yield 65 tokens.

Uses[edit | edit source]

Before completing the Ghosts Ahoy quest, players need to pay a toll of 2 ecto-tokens whenever they want to enter the fallen city Port Phasmatys. However, this toll can be ignored by chartering a ship to the city instead. After the quest, no more toll has to be paid.

Ecto-tokens are always required for two quests:

  • Between 25 and 31 tokens for Ghosts Ahoy, depending on how you enter the city.
  • 20 tokens for Animal Magnetism.

They also have several more uses:

  • Purchasing ale yeast from Metarialus for 5 tokens. However, this may also be bought from Blandebir or Lucrezia for only 25 coins.
  • Purchasing an undead chicken from Malcolm after the Animal Magnetism quest for 10 tokens.
  • Travelling to Dragontooth Island costs 25 tokens, reduced to 10 while wearing the ring of charos (a). You may also give the ghost captain 500 tokens to permanently unlock free trips to the island. After completion of the Ghosts Ahoy quest, the island's only use is as a potential destination requirement for certain elite and master clue scrolls.
  • Charging the Bonecrusher and Bonecrusher necklace for 25 charges per token used. This tool is a reward from the hard Morytania Diary and, when charged, automatically buries bones of slain enemies instantly for half or full amount of their normal experience dependent on the completion of the Morytania Hard and Elite diaries respectively.
