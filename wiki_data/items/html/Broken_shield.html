The broken shield is one of the two halves of the restored Shield of Arrav.

In the Shield of Arrav quest, the player must combine both parts of the broken shield to get the complete shield. One player must join the Black Arm Gang and retrieve it from a cupboard upstairs in the base. The other player must be in the Phoenix Gang and must get it from the warehouse.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • In RuneScape Classic, the icon on the shield was a pegasus as confirmed in the Shield of Arrav book.[1] This icon was graphically updated when the game transitioned to RuneScape 2.
