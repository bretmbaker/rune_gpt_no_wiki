The Magical orb is a quest item given to the player during the Hand in the Sand quest. It is used to record the interrogation with Sandy, where he is under the influence of the truth serum.

Activating the orb when not besides Sandy will trigger the "Nothing interesting happens!" instead of the usual "Nothing interesting happens."

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
23 October 2024
(update)

The item was recoloured to its original intended colour, as part of a fix involving the implementation of detecting malformed recolours in the game's configs.

17 February 2021
(update)

You can now reclaim your Magical Orb from Zavistic Rarve if you lose it during the Hand in the Sand quest.
