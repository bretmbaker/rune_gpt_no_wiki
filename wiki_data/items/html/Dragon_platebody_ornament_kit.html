The dragon platebody ornament kit is obtained as a possible reward from master Treasure Trails. It is purely decorative, and can be attached to the dragon platebody, making it a dragon platebody (g).

Once attached, the ornamented armour becomes untradeable, but the kit can be safely retrieved by selecting the option "Dismantle" on it.
