The Ursine chainmace is a mace that requires level 70 in Attack to wield. One of the proposed rewards from Callisto, it is only available from the supplies table in an unrestricted world as a test weapon for players.

The chainmace is identical in function to Viggora's chainmace, but with increased stats and a special attack.

Special attack[edit | edit source]

The Ursine chainmace has a special attack, Bear Down, which consumes 50% of the player's special attack energy. The special attack hits an enemy with doubled accuracy, and upon a successful hit, also deals 20 damage over 6 seconds, prevents running for 3.6 seconds (6 ticks) and reduces the target's Agility level by 20.
