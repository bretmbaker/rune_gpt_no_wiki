Raw tuna is a type of fish, obtained by harpoon fishing at a cage/harpoon fishing spot. Raw tuna require 35 Fishing to catch and award the player 80 experience when caught. They can be sold, or cooked with a Cooking level of 30 to produce tuna, granting 100 experience in Cooking and will no longer be burned after level 63. The cooked version is often used by mid-levelled players when training combat.

Raw tuna can also be bought from Flosi Dalksson in Jatizso or thieved from the Fish stalls in The Warrens, Rellekka, Miscellania, and Etceteria, requiring a Thieving level of 42.
