Mithril full helm (g) is a part of mithril gold-trimmed armour and a reward from medium Treasure Trails. It has the same stats as a normal Mithril full helm, and requires level 20 Defence to wear. This item cannot be made via the Smithing skill.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room, as part of the mithril gold-trimmed armour set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
