An unlit torch may be used with a tinderbox or used on a range to get a lit torch. It is useful for dark tunnels and smoking Hunter traps once lit. 39 Hunter is required to smoke traps. Smoking traps increases the chance of catching a creature by 2% (additive).[1]

Unlit torches are dropped by many cave dwelling creatures including the level 49 and level 76-78 Catablepons, and Cave slimes.

Unlit torches can sometimes be found inside barrels in the Shaman Caves.

They can also be purchased from these stores:

  • Aleck's Hunter Emporium in Yanille
  • Gunslik's Assorted Items in Keldagrim
  • Obli's General Store in Shilo Village
  • Nardah Hunter Shop in Nardah
  • Jiminua's Jungle Store near Brimhaven
  • Imia's Supplies in the Hunter Guild
  • Sunset Coast General Store in Sunset Coast
  • Dorgesh-Kaan General Supplies in Dorgesh-Kaan
  • Miltog's Lamps in Dorgesh-Kaan
  • Cam Torum General Store in Cam Torum
  • Darkmeyer Lantern Shop in Darkmeyer

When lit, a torch can be used as a light source in dark dungeons and in some quests such as Shilo Village. Multiple torches (up to 3) can be used at the same time for brighter lighting (this also works with candles).

This can be a dangerous source of light in some places such as the Lumbridge Swamp Caves due to the fact that its open flame can ignite the "swamp gases" within the caves. If the player does not leave the swamp gas area immediately, or does not extinguish the flame, then the swamp gas may explode, damaging the player and putting out all light sources in the player's inventory.

Lit torches are also used in the Construction skill, as a lighting option in Dungeons, Oubliettes, and Treasure Rooms in a Player-owned house.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Unlit torches found at Trouble Brewing will be destroyed when dropped on ground with a humorous message telling that "a monkey will scamper off with it".
  • A lit torch is given to the player at end of the Death to the Dorgeshuun quest by Zanik, if the player doesn't have any light sources in inventory.
  • An unlit torch is given to the player as part of the Sea Slug quest.
