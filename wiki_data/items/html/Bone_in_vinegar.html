Bone in vinegar is an item that players use during the Rag and Bone Man I and Rag and Bone Man II quests. It is made by using certain one-time drop bones (such as a wolf bone) with a pot of vinegar. Due to the fact that each bone in vinegar contains a different bone, they do not stack in the inventory or bank.

All the bones in vinegar look identical, but the examine text differs for each one. For example:

  • There is a cave goblin skull in here.
  • There is a seagull wing in here.
  • There is a ghoul bone in here.

Take care not to accidentally empty the pot during your quest or when cleaning the bones. You will be left with only the empty pot, forcing you to retrieve the bone all over again.
