A Menaphite remedy is a stat renewal potion that restores 6 + 16% of the player's combat stats every 15 seconds, over the course of five minutes.

It can be made by a player with 88 Herblore by adding a Lily of the Sands to an unfinished dwarf weed potion. This grants the player 200 Herblore experience.

Although this potion will restore combat stats if they are below their base level, it will not boost your combat stats back up if they were previously boosted.

Additionally, consuming this potion will remove all divine effects of existing boosts, such as from the saturated heart or a divine super combat potion, making them tick down at the usual rate of -1 level per 60 seconds (90 if praying Preserve).

This property can prolong the usefulness of these boosts by consuming the remedy at any point before the boost runs out. A stat boosted by a divine effect will return to its base level after the divine effect expires, but removing it with the Menaphite remedy will retain the boosted stat, allowing it to tick down normally.

If new divine boosts are applied after the remedy has been consumed, they will correctly apply their divine status. This strategy can be useful at a bank to prepare for scenarios where the player's stats are frequently lowered, such as Nex, where Saradomin brews are often used. This allows the player to benefit from the stat restoration of the Menaphite remedy while retaining the benefits of their divine potions or saturated heart.
