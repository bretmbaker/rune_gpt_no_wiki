An oyster pearl is a small, round, milky-coloured semi-precious gem obtained by opening oysters. They can also be collected by fishing with a big fishing net, participating in the Fishing Trawler minigame, panning in the river at the Digsite, or by killing various monsters.

Players with 41 Fletching can use a chisel on this item to gain 6 pearl bolt tips, which can be attached to iron bolts or dragon bolts to make pearl bolts or pearl dragon bolts.
