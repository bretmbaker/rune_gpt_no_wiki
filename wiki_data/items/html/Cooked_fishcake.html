Cooked fishcakes are a type of food that can be obtained by cooking a raw fishcake on a fire or cooking range, requiring level 31 Cooking and granting 100 experience when successful. Players may burn the fishcake while cooking one, resulting in burnt fishcake; the burn rate while cooking these will decrease as players reach higher Cooking levels. It will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 62 on both ranges and fires.

The cooked fishcake heals 11 hitpoints when eaten, and is needed to complete the Pirate Pete subquest of Recipe for Disaster. It is made with breadcrumbs, ground cod, ground kelp, and ground crab meat, creating a raw fishcake that is then cooked.
