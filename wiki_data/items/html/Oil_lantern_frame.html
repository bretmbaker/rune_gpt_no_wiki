An oil lantern frame can be made by members with level 26 Smithing from an iron bar, giving 25 Smithing experience. It is used to make an oil lantern which can be made with level 26 Crafting by using an oil lamp with the frame then filling the resulting empty oil lantern with lamp oil at the Chemist's west of Rimmington. Oil lanterns can be lit with a Tinderbox at level 26 Firemaking. They will eventually go out and require more lamp oil.
