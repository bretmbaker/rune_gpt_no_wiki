A seasoned sardine is a quest item used only in Gertrude's Cat. It is made by using doogle leaves on a raw sardine.

It is given to Fluffs to calm her down.

A player cannot feed it to their own pet kitten, nor can it be fed to Fluffs after the quest, as this simply gives the message Nothing interesting happens. This item cannot be cooked for human consumption on a range or fire unlike a raw sardine. Additionally it cannot be reverted back to a raw sardine, so it serves no further use after the quest.
