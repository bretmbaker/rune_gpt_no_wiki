The bronze arrow is an arrow made of bronze. These arrows are the weakest type of arrows in Old School RuneScape. Any bow is capable of shooting bronze arrows. As with most arrows, they can be poisoned.

In order to create this item players must first smelt bronze bars. Then using a hammer and anvil, the player must turn the bar into bronze arrowtips. Creating the bronze bar has no Smithing requirement, while a player must have level 5 Smithing to create the arrowheads. Then players should cut logs, and using a knife, turn them into arrow shafts. After attaching feathers to the arrow shafts (creating headless arrows) players can then use the arrowheads on the headless arrows to create bronze arrows. This entire process requires level 1 Fletching.

In addition, arrows can be purchased from a variety of general and archery stores.
