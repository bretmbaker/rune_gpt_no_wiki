Jered's empty wine bottle is an item received from Brother Jered during the 2021 Halloween event.

It is used to scare Oziach out of buying the Edgeville Monastery by making ghostly sounds with it. If players attempt to make scary sounds with it before climbing a tree, the chatbox will state "I should wait until I'm in a tree to do this!" If players destroy the bottle at crucial moments when trying to scare Oziach, the chatbox will state "You destroyed the bottle, meaning you have no way of scaring Oziach anymore." and the player returns to their former location.
