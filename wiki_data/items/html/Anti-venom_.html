Anti-venom+ is a potion that instantly cures venom and poison. It provides immunity to poison for 15 minutes and venom for approximately 3.6 minutes.

Players can make this potion at 94 Herblore by using a torstol or an unfinished torstol potion on an anti-venom, granting 125 Herblore experience.

Additionally, anti-venom+ can be upgraded to an Extended anti-venom+ by mixing one Araxyte venom sack per dose.
