Lozar's Crandor Map part is a quest item used only in Dragon Slayer I. This map part is either dropped by Wormbrain, arrested in the Port Sarim jail, after killing him and obtaining the part with telekinetic grab or paying him 10,000 coins.

When all three map parts are in the inventory, using any two pieces on each other will combine all three into the Crandor map, which is given to Ned.

Studying the map part results in:

This is a piece of map that you got from Wormbrain, the goblin thief. You will need to join it to the other two map pieces before you can see the route to Crandor.
