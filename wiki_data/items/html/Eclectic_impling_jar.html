An eclectic impling jar is the result of successfully catching an eclectic impling in the Impetuous Impulses minigame.

This jar can be traded in singularly to Elnock Inquisitor for 3 empty impling jars, but this is not recommended due to its high value.

You can trade two of this jar, three essence impling jars and one nature impling jar for a jar generator.

Loot[edit | edit source]

At current Grand Exchange prices, opening an eclectic impling jar gives 1,375.00 worth of loot, on average. This means opening eclectic impling jars makes an average profit of −3,970.00 per jar, however this does not include loot from clue scrolls.

On average, one would profit −99,251.00 to get a medium clue scroll from eclectic impling jars at current Grand Exchange prices, excluding the rewards from the completed Treasure Trails.

The following drop rates are provided by Jagex, unless otherwise specified.

Impling jar[edit | edit source]

An empty impling jar is returned when removing the loot, with a 10% chance of it breaking.[1]
