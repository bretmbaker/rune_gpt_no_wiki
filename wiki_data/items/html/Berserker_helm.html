The berserker helm is a mid-levelled melee-based helmet. Players must have level 45 Defence and also have completed the quest The Fremennik Trials in order to wear it. It can be purchased from Skulgrimen's Battle Gear shop in Rellekka for 78,000 coins. It's also a possible reward from Barbarian Assault High Gambles or a drop from a Dagannoth.

This helmet is favoured among combat pures for being the second lowest level headgear to offer a Strength bonus, which offers the same strength bonus as a helm of Neitiznot and an Obsidian helmet.

The berserker helm's defensive bonuses are roughly on par with the rune full helm. More specifically, the berserker helm is slightly better than the rune full helm in both stab and crush defence, but is slightly worse in slash defence.

Skulgrimen's Battle Gear will buy this helmet from a player for 42,000 coins, which is more than the High Level Alchemy price of 36,000 coins.
