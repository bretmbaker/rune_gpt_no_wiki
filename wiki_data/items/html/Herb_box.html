Herb boxes are a reward from both Nightmare Zone and Tithe Farm. They cost 9,500 points in the Nightmare Zone reward shop and cost 30 points in the Tithe Farm reward shop. It contains an assortment of ten random grimy herbs. Only 15 of these may be bought per day from the Nightmare Zone; this limit does not exist for the Tithe Farm. Players can right-click a herb box to place all the herbs into a bank. Ironmen cannot purchase herb boxes from the Nightmare Zone but can from Tithe Farm.

Profit analysis[edit | edit source]

Based on the percentages below, you can discern the approximate amount of profit you will make per box. Because prices are constantly fluctuating, the profit can vary, but it is highly unlikely that you will lose profit when buying herb boxes.

The estimated profit per box based off the percentages in the table is 12,682.73 coins, for a value of 1.34 coins per Nightmare Zone point.

Assuming that you open the highest number of herb boxes possible per day (which is 15) for 142,500 Nightmare Zone points, you may get a total average profit of 190,241.02 coins.
