The black plateskirt has the same stats as black platelegs but weighs less. The black plateskirt requires 10 Defence to wear. One can be bought from Ranael's Super Skirt Store in Al Kharid.

As with all black and white equipment, the black plateskirt cannot be made from raw materials using the Smithing skill.
