Bottle of sorodamin's bru is an item in the Sailing alpha. It is acquired by prying open a Sealed crate. Drinking the Bottle of sorodamin's bru deals 5 damage to the player.

It drain's the player's Defence by 2 + 20% of their current level and drains the player's Attack, Strength, Ranged, and Magic by 2 + 10% of their current level.
