Kruk monkey greegree (also known as a large monkey greegree) is used for transforming into Kruk on Ape Atoll during Monkey Madness II. To make one, a player must take a monkey talisman and Kruk's paw to Zooknock, located at the very end of the Ape Atoll Dungeon.

A glitch exists where if the player gets caught and they try to transform back, there is a small chance they will lose their greegree; however, this glitch is not common. If the player has a full inventory, and teleports from Ape Atoll without removing their greegree, they will lose it.

After Monkey Madness II, it can be reobtained from Zooknock in the Tree Gnome Stronghold, north-west of the Gnome Ball Field, if lost by selecting the 'Large Ninja' greegree, as it is not listed as Kruk monkey greegree. No materials are required.

The greegree can be used for training Agility at the Ape Atoll Agility Course or to hunt maniacal monkeys.
