A barricade is an item used in the Soul Wars minigame as portable obstacles to restrict enemy movement.

The barricades have a set-up option that places the item on the ground below the player, turning it into an attackable object with 50 hitpoints. Each team in the minigame can have a maximum of 10 barricades set up. If the limit is reached, players on that team cannot set up more barricades unless some of the previously set up barricades are destroyed.

Players cannot walk through placed barricades, and must destroy it by attacking it or using an explosive potion on it.

If you have reached your barricade limit, a message in your chat box will inform you so. They cannot be poisoned.
