Huge snowballs were items received by rolling some snow seven times on the ground during the 2021 Christmas event, in order to make snowmen for Larry's children, Buoy and Gurl.

Dropping the snowball resulted in it melting, and the chatbox stated "The snowball melts and vanishes as it hits the ground."
