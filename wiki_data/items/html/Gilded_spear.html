The Gilded spear is a gold-plated version of the rune spear. It has the same stats as the rune spear, and only differs in appearance. It requires 40 Attack to wield.

It can be obtained as a reward from hard, elite and master tier Treasure Trails and cannot be made through any skill.

Unlike every other metallic spear, the gilded spear cannot be poisoned.
