A herb sack is an item that can be bought from Slayer masters for 750 Slayer reward points or from Farmer Gricoller's Rewards for 250 Tithe Farm points.

Buying one requires level 58 in Herblore (cannot be boosted) and allows the player to store up to 30 of each of the grimy standard herbs, for a total of 450 stored. A full herb sack is worth 1,270,470.

Players cannot store noted or cleaned herbs in the herb sack.

The herb sack can be manually filled by using the left-click 'Fill' option, which will deposit any grimy herbs from your inventory, unless it is full.

It can be opened or closed with the right-click options 'Open' or 'Close' respectively. An open sack will automatically deposit any grimy herbs that are picked up by the player, as well as those harvested from a herb patch and Herbiboars, provided there is space for it. A closed sack will not automatically deposit herbs.

The 'Empty' option deposits herbs into the inventory, starting from the lowest level herb, and with the bank open it will deposit all of the contents into the bank.

Using the 'Check' option will list all currently stored herbs within it in the chat box. Using the 'Check' option while the sack is empty will result in the messages You look in your herb sack and see: The herb sack is empty.

The herb sack is useful for killing monsters that often drop valuable herbs, such as chaos druids, flesh crawlers, aberrant spectres, and deviant spectres, since it only takes up one inventory slot. The herb sack can also be used when lighting incense burners during open altar house parties, reducing the frequency of banking or unnoting Marrentill at Phials.

Herbs stored in the sack are not lost upon death. Herbs will stay in the herb sack upon death, unless the player has untoggled this setting.

Attempting to use an item on the herb sack that is not a grimy herb will result in the message You can't possibly put that in there, it would spoil your sack.

Like other untradeables, the herb sack is lost on death beyond level 20 Wilderness. If the herb sack is one of the item kept on death the herbs inside the herb sack are also kept on death when killed by a player.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
20 September 2023
(update)

On death during PvM, herbs will remain in the herb sack. In PvP, they will appear for the player that killed you.

25 June 2020
(update)

On death, grimy herbs within the herb sack are no longer deleted. Instead, the herbs either go to the player's Grave or to a PKer's loot pile

16 April 2020
(update | poll)

Players can now purchase the herb sack from Farmer Gricoller's Rewards for 250 points.

23 January 2020
(update | poll)
  • Open and closed versions have been added, which allows herbs that the player obtains be automatically placed inside the sack while opened, providing there is space for it.
  • "Open" was added as a right-click option.
22 February 2018
(update | poll)

Emptying the contents of the herb sack into the inventory can now be done by holding down shift and left-clicking it if shift-click dropping is enabled.

20 August 2015
(update)
  • It is now possible to use a herb on the herb sack (or vice versa) to place that herb into the sack.
  • Some game messages relating to adding and removing herbs from a herb sack can now be filtered.
