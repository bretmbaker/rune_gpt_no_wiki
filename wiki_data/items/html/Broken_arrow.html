A broken arrow can be found in certain item spawn points, such as in the Ranging Guild and playing the Fishing Trawler minigame. An Ava's accumulator can also randomly attract one to the player's inventory. If it is caught during Fishing Trawler, then the player will receive 5 Fishing experience. Searching certain boxes on the table during the King's Ransom quest provides unlimited broken arrows.

Broken arrows, along with broken staves and broken armour can be repaired at a repair bench in a player-owned house. Successfully repairing a broken arrow gives 8 Fletching experience and creates up to a mithril arrow. Failing to repair the broken arrow destroys it.

When shown to Terry Balando, he says "No doubt this arrow was shot by a strong warrior!"
