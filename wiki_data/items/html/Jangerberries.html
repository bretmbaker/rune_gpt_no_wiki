Jangerberries can be harvested from a bush patch after planting jangerberry seeds. They are used in Herblore to make Zamorak brews or as protection payment for snape grass (x5).

Jangerberries restore 2 hitpoints, 1 prayer point, temporarily boosts Attack by 2 and Strength by 1, and reduces Defence by 1. Adding them to a compost bin makes supercompost, though this is not recommended as watermelons or pineapples are far cheaper and have the same effect.

Uses in quests[edit | edit source]

  • Watchtower: 1 x jangerberries to make a potion.
  • Fairytale I - Growing Pains: possible item from a list to obtain magic secateurs. (If doing this quest, bring extra, as ghasts can turn them into rotten food.)
