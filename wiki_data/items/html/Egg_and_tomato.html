Egg and tomato is a food and cooking ingredient. It can be created by players with a cooking level of 23 by combining a scrambled egg with a tomato. This does not require a knife or range. Consuming it heals for 8 hitpoints.

At level 51 Cooking, it can be combined with a potato with butter to make an egg potato. Doing so provides 45 Cooking experience and does not require a range or fire.
