Curry saplings are saplings that can be used to grow curry trees at level 42 Farming.

They can be made by planting a curry tree seed in a filled plant pot, with a gardening trowel in your inventory, and then watering the curry seedling with a watering can or by using Humidify. When watered, the seedling will sprout into a sapling in one crop cycle (0-5 minutes).

Planting the sapling in a fruit tree patch, with a spade in your inventory, will give 40 Farming experience. It will grow into a curry tree after 960 minutes (16 hours), which gives 2,906.9 Farming experience.

Players can ask the gardener to protect their tree from disease whilst it grows, which requires payment of 5 baskets of bananas. This payment can be noted.

A curry tree's leaves can be used with uncooked stew to make curry.
