Unstrung symbols can be made at 16 Crafting with a silver bar and a holy mould at a furnace, granting 50 crafting experience. It can be used with a ball of wool to create an unblessed symbol. This can then be taken to Brother Jered who will bless it at level 31 Prayer (including F2P), or it can be blessed at level 50 Prayer using a completed Holy book.
