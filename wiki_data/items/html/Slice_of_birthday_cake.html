A slice of birthday cake is an item available during some of Old School RuneScape's birthday events by taking a slice off the birthday cake. Eating the cake heals 3 hitpoints. This item can not be banked.

  • During Old School RuneScape's first birthday, it was blue in colour, and was available by taking a slice off the birthday cake in Prifddinas.
  • During Old School RuneScape's second birthday, it was available by taking a slice off the birthday cake in Varrock Square, although the colour of the cake has been changed to yellow.
  • During Old School RuneScape's fifth birthday, it was available by taking a slice off the birthday cake in the Falador Party Room, although the colour of the cake has been changed to purple, adding alongside decorations of runes.
  • During Old School RuneScape's seventh birthday, it was available by taking a slice off the birthday cake in Juliet's house. The cake has the same appearance as in 2018.
  • During Old School RuneScape's eleventh birthday, it was available by taking a slice off the birthday cake in the Falador Party Room, although the colour of the cake has been changed to green.
  • During Old School RuneScape's twelfth birthday, it was available by taking a slice off the birthday cake in the Falador Party Room, although the colour of the cake has been changed to black.
