The Armadyl godsword is a minigame-exclusive variant of the normal Armadyl godsword. It can be obtained from opening chests with a bloody key or bloodier key, or looting the crates that spawn at random locations on the map. It is dropped on death to whoever killed you. Upon leaving the island, it is removed from the player and thus cannot be taken out of the minigame.

Special attack[edit | edit source]

The Armadyl godsword has a special attack, The Judgement. In addition to doubled accuracy, it inflicts damage with a 37.5% (125% multiplied by the hidden 110% every godsword special possesses, the calculation uses integer values after the multiplication of 1.1 the value gets rounded down and then multiplied by 1.25) higher maximum hit than a normal attack, consuming 50% of the player's special attack energy.

The Judgement is capable of dealing a very large amount of damage; under ideal conditions, a player can potentially deal over 70 damage in a single blow. In PvP scenarios the Armadyl godsword is most commonly used as a "knock-out" weapon, as the special is often capable of finishing an injured player in one large blow.
