A ball of wool is used in the Crafting skill. It is made by using a spinning wheel.

Tip: The shop in Neitiznot has the largest stock of any store with a bank in the same building. After Monkey Madness II the crafting shop on Marim is a close spot to a bank but not faster.

Uses[edit | edit source]

It can be used on a kitten for increased attention duration.

Quests[edit | edit source]

  • Sheep Shearer: 20 × balls of wool
  • Prince Ali Rescue: 3 × balls of wool to make a wig
  • Regicide: 4 × balls of wool to make a strip of cloth
  • Monkey Madness I: 1 × ball of wool to make a M'speak amulet
  • Recipe for Disaster (Freeing Skrach Uglogwee subquest): 1 × ball of wool to make a balloon toad
  • Enlightened Journey: 1 × ball of wool
