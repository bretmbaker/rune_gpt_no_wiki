The calcified acorn is a reward from the Colossal Wyrm Agility course that can be purchased from Worm Tongue for 900 Termites. Using it on a giant squirrel will allow it to metamorphose into a bone squirrel.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Using the calcified acorn on various NPCs will result in varying interactions identical to that of the dark acorn.
    • If used on c0lect0r890's giant squirrel, a chatbox message will state "You shouldn't feed someone's pet without their permission."
    • If used on squirrels on Fossil Island, they'll respond "That Acorn looks nasty, no squirrel in their right mind would eat that!"
    • If used on Maz, it will result in her saying *chitter*
