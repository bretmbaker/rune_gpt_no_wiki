Bottles are obtained during Defender of Varrock within Zemouregal's Base, where they are used to collect red mist from defeated armoured zombies in order to obtain a bottle of mist.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
28 February 2024
(update)

Bottles are no longer duplicated on the ground when picked up.
