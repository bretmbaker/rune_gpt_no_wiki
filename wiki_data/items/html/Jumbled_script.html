Jumbled script is an item used in the 2025 Easter event. It is found in the old bookcase backstage in the rabbit hole. After showing the script to Fry, it can be sorted.

The order of the script does not matter for the performance during the 2025 Easter Event. The item is removed from your inventory after the performance and attempting to obtain another will just show the message "You don't need another script at the moment."
