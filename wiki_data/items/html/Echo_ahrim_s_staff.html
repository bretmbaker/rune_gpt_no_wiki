The echo Ahrim's staff is an Ahrim's staff with an echo ahrim's ornament kit added onto it. The kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the staff.

Whilst in its ornamented state, the item can be reverted anytime, returning the staff and kit.
