The Fremennik blade is a slash weapon equivalent to an adamant scimitar. This weapon is dropped by various Fremennik people in Rellekka, Fremennik warriors, and some monsters on Waterbirth Island. Completion of The Fremennik Trials and 30 Attack is required to equip it. The sword is not tradeable.

Bardur, in the Waterbirth Island Dungeon, will trade a cooked shark for any Fremennik blade, shield, or helm brought to him.

The player will be awarded a Fremennik blade if they successfully defeat Koschei the deathless' final form. However, they still need to complete the quest in order to wield it.
