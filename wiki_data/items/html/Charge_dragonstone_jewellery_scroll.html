The charge dragonstone jewellery scroll is a consumable scroll obtained as a reward from all levels of Treasure Trails, excluding beginner clue scrolls. In order to use the scroll, players must first have completed the Heroes' Quest.

The scroll allows a player to charge any amulet of glory, combat bracelet, and skills necklace with 4 charges, although the former requires completion of Heroes' Quest and the latter two can only be charged after completion of the Legends' Quest. One scroll will charge all uncharged and partially charged unnoted jewellery in the inventory, as well as any equipped jewellery.

This item cannot charge the ring of wealth; only the Fountain of Rune in the deep Wilderness can do so.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
Date unknown

The scroll was changed to require completion of Heroes' Quest in order to recharge an Amulet of glory.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • This item used to have the longest item name in all of Old School RuneScape at 35 characters. On 11 April 2019, its record was beaten by 3 characters with the addition of the Rune scimitar ornament kit (saradomin). The record of 49 characters is currently held by Corrupted morrigan's leather chaps (bh)(inactive), which were added to the game on 24 May 2023.
