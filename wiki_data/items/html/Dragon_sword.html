The dragon sword is a one-handed stab weapon obtained as a rare drop from wyrms, and requires 60 Attack to wield. It has the highest stab attack bonus of the standard shortswords, and the third highest stab attack bonus of all shortswords. The dragon sword provides 2 less stab attack than the leaf-bladed sword, but provides 13 more strength bonus.

As with most dragon equipment, the dragon sword cannot be made from raw materials using the Smithing skill.

Special attack[edit | edit source]

The dragon sword has a special attack, Wild Stab, that consumes 40% of the player's special attack energy to hit the target with 25% increased accuracy and damage. If the target is using Protect from Melee, the special attack will ignore the prayer for one attack. This special rolls off of the opponents stab defence regardless of which mode it is in.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
19 March 2025
(update)

The dragon sword special attack will now work correctly in PvP when the player's opponent is not using the Protect from Melee protection prayer.

10 January 2019
(update | poll)

Following the release of Kebos Lowlands, dragon sword was moved from the Chambers of Xeric drop table to wyrms.
