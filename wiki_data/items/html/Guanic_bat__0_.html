Guanic bat (0) is a food item made by using raw guanic bat (0) on a fire within the Chambers of Xeric. Eating a guanic bat heals the player for 5 hitpoints.

Cooking guanic bat yields 10 Cooking experience, and requires a Cooking level of 1.
