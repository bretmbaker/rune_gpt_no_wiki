The white scimitar is a White Knight weapon that requires 10 Attack and the completion of the Wanted! quest to wield. It has the same bonuses as a black scimitar, with an added +1 prayer bonus.

Players can purchase it from Sir Vyvin after achieving the rank of Master (1,300 black knight kills). It can also be bought on the Grand Exchange or obtained through trading other players.

As with all white weapons, it cannot be made using the Smithing skill.
