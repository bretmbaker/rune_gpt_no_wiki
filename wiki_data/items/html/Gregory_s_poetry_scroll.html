Gregory's poetry scroll is an item obtained from Gregory during the 2023 Pride event. Players must fill in the missing phrases of the poem based on the available chat options whilst speaking to him; namely, "spring" for the first line and "soil" for the second.

After talking to Love, Gregory will give the player a new scroll with a new "Directions" option. Selecting this option displays the riddle on the back: "Just South of town can my champion be found, tending cautiously to fertile ground."
