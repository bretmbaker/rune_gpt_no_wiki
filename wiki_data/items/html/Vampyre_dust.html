Vampyre dust are remains dropped by vampyres. It is primarily used on Saradomin Braziers in the Hallowed Sepulchre to pass through holy barriers, where players can then loot the coffin past it. It is also an item that may be asked for during the quest Forgettable Tale... It cannot be used with the ash sanctifier. Using 20 vampyre dust on an ornate undead combat dummy enables it to function as a vampyre.
