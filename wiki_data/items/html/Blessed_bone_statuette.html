Blessed bone statuettes are items that may occasionally be obtained from stealing valuables. Though considered as contraband in Varlamore, players may break them down with a chisel to obtain 125 blessed bone shards (similar to that of Blessed superior dragon bones at 121 blessed bone shards) and 5 Crafting xp.
