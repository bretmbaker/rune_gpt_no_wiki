A Sea slug is an item that players can pick up using the Telekinetic Grab spell on a sea slug that Kent knocks off the player during the Sea Slug quest.

Without Telekinetic Grab, it will merely damage the player for 3 hitpoints, causing them to exclaim "Ouch!" If dropped, players can only pick it up again using another Telekinetic Grab spell.

Attempting to cast alchemy spells on a sea slug will fail with the message Ugly parasites have feelings too!
