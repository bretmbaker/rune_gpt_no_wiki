Red topaz is a semi-precious gem used mainly in Crafting. It can be cut from uncut red topaz with a chisel at level 16 Crafting, yielding 25 experience. There is a chance to unsuccessfully cut the red topaz, resulting in a crushed gem and 6.3 experience.

3 red topaz can be given to Safta Doc alongside a gout tuber and 1,200 trading sticks in exchange for a red topaz machete.
