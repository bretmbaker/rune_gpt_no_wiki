Cake is a type of food that restores 12 Hitpoints in total, 3 bites each healing for 4. It can be obtained by baking an uncooked cake on a cooking range, requiring level 40 Cooking and granting 180 experience when successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting in burnt cake. The burn rate decreases as the player's Cooking level increases, and it will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 74.
