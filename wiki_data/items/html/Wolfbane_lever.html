The Wolfbane lever was an unobtainable item that previously could be found in the game cache. It was created to replace an item used by Jagex moderators for testing features of the Werewolf Agility Course called the "Werewolf Agility Arena helper". Since items could not be fully removed once created, the Wolfbane lever was added in a hidden update in 2005 to fill this slot.

The name and examine were designed to be inconspicuous, but the actual item was just a copy of a pink skirt. When in the inventory, players had the option "Push", "Pull", "Abuse", or "Pluck" it. If players attempted to view the Wolfbane lever in game by using cheating software, they would be given a Hex edit detected instead.

In RuneScape, the item later became viewable via Quick Chat, causing much confusion.

The item was never obtainable in Old School RuneScape, and was replaced by Amethyst broad bolts in 2017.
