Incomplete stew is made by using a potato, cooked meat, or a cooked chicken on a bowl of water, requiring level 25 Cooking. The final product is the same regardless of which of the two types of meat is used. The ingredients can be added in any order. Adding the last ingredient creates an uncooked stew.
