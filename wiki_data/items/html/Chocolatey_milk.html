Chocolatey milk is a type of untradeable drink that restores 4 Hitpoints when drunk. It can be obtained by using chocolate dust on a bucket of milk. Chocolate dust can be obtained by using a knife or pestle and mortar on a chocolate bar. Unlike other dairy products, it cannot be made using a dairy churn.

Chocolatey milk can be used with snape grass to create a Hangover cure which is required for the Plague City quest and the Skippy and the Mogres miniquest.
