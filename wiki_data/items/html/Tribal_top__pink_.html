The pink tribal top is a shirt that can be purchased with 360 trading sticks from Gabooty's Tai Bwo Wannai Cooperative. If players are wearing Karamja gloves 1 or better, the price is reduced to 300 trading sticks per top.

It can be stored in a magic wardrobe in a player-owned house.
