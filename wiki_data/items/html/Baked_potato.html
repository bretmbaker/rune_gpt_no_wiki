A baked potato is a type of food that heals 4 Hitpoints when eaten. It can be obtained by baking a potato on a cooking range, requiring level 7 Cooking and granting 15 experience when successful. Potatoes cannot be cooked on fires.

Players may burn the potato, resulting in a burnt potato. Players burn potatoes less at higher levels, and will stop burning them entirely at Cooking level 41.

Types of baked potatoes[edit | edit source]

To add toppings to a baked potato, a pat of butter must first be added to make a potato with butter. After that, further toppings can be added.

Types of potatoes
Type Level Heals
Burnt potato 7 0
Baked potato 7 4
Buttered potato 39 14
Chilli potato 41 14
Cheese potato 47 16
Egg potato 51 16
Mushroom potato 64 20
Tuna potato 68 22

Due to players wanting to add toppings to potatoes, it can be lucrative to bake and sell potatoes. A guide for making money with this method can be found here: Baking potatoes.

The field north of Ardougne is good option to make baked potatoes in bulk, since it's close to a bank and a range. The potatoes can be picked and cooked directly before banking, saving the trouble of cooking them later.
