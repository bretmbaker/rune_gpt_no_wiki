Cooked kyatt is a type of hunter meat that restores a total of 17 Hitpoints when eaten, 9 are restored immediately, 8 are restored after a delay of 7 ticks (4.2s). Eating another hunter meat too quickly will waste any pending healing, as the most recent delayed heal will replace older effects that have not completed yet. Standard foods and potions do not interrupt this pending heal.


It can be obtained by cooking raw kyatt on a fire or cooking range, requiring level 51 Cooking and granting 143 experience when successful. 25 Hunters' Rumours must be completed before they may be cooked. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting in burnt large beast. The burn rate decreases as the player's Cooking level increases, and it will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 80 on ranges and level 86 on fires.
