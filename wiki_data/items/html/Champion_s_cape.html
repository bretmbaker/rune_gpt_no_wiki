The champion's cape is a cosmetic cape that is rewarded after defeating all 11 champions available in the Champions' Challenge. It is worn by Leon d'Cour, the human champion, Dimintheis, a Varrockian noble, as well as Toby, the taskmaster of the Varrock Diary.

It can be mounted on a cape hanger in an Achievement Gallery of a player-owned house.

This item can be stored in the cape rack of a costume room.

An unlimited number of additional capes can be obtained by speaking to Larxus.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The cape depicts three heraldic lions, similar to those found on the Royal Arms of England.
  • The cape is likely blue as a reference to the Blue cape purchased within the Champions' Guild, which was originally referred to as the "Cape of Champions" in the Wanted! quest.
