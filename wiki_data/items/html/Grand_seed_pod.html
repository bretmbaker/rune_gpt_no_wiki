The grand seed pod is a single-use teleport item that can be obtained as a rare reward from Wingstone, Penwie, Brambickle, and Professor Manglethorp in the Gnome Restaurant minigame.[1][2] Seed pods are stackable. They can take players to the Grand Tree, and have two right-click options: launch and squash.

Launching the seed pod will take players to the top floor of the Grand Tree, through Captain Lamdoo and a glider, but it can only be used outdoors. This option will also give the player 100 Farming experience. Squashing the seed will take players to the Grand Tree (to the ground floor by King Narnode Shareen) faster than Launching it, but the Farming experience will not be given, and 5 levels will temporarily drain their Farming level. Neither method can be used past level 30 Wilderness.

The upgraded version of this item, the Royal seed pod, is obtained upon completion of Monkey Madness II, and has unlimited charges. It does not offer the Launch option and does not affect Farming (experience or level drain).

This item was once commonly used to lure players. Although scams involving this item have fallen out of favour, players should still exercise caution when the grand seed pod is involved. Most of these lures involve the victim dropping valuable items and then proceeding to Squash or Launch the pod.
