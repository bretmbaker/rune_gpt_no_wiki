The yew composite bow is a bow that requires level 40 Ranged to equip. Yew comp bows can be obtained from completing a medium difficulty Treasure Trail. The yew comp bow is essentially an upgraded yew longbow with a one tick faster attack speed and slightly improved accuracy.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room.
