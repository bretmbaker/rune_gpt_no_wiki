Amylase pack is an item pack containing 100 amylase crystals used in making stamina potions. One amylase crystal is needed to create one dose of stamina potion, meaning one amylase pack is good for 25 stamina potion(4).

These packs can be bought from Grace's Graceful Clothing for 10 marks of grace, from the Agility Arena Store for 60 Brimhaven vouchers, or from Worm Tongue's Wares for 100 Termites.

Amylase packs can only be sold back to Grace's Graceful Clothing for 8 marks of grace, even if it was brought from the Agility Arena Store or Worm Tongue's Wares.

Therefore, if using amylase packs to convert between currencies, one would have to exchange 12.5 or 7.5 to receive 1.

The total Grand Exchange value of the crystals contained in a single pack is 98,300.
