The holy Ghrazi rapier is a Ghrazi rapier with a holy ornament kit attached to it. The ornament kit gives no additional stat bonuses, but it does make the rapier count as a Saradomin item in the God Wars Dungeon.

While the rapier is untradeable in its ornamented state, it can be dismantled anytime, returning the ornament kit and tradeable rapier.
