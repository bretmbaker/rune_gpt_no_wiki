Blasted ore is obtained after detonating a hard rock face in the blast mine. Players must first excavate the hard rock using a chisel, then using a pot of dynamite on the resulting cavity. Then, players must light the dynamite and run away from it, or large amounts of damage will be taken.

Once the dynamite explodes, blasted ore will appear where the dynamite exploded. It should then be deposited in an ore sack near a blonde-haired operator. Players have three minutes before the blasted ore disintegrates, so it is encouraged to deposit the ore frequently.

Should the player pick up but not deposit the blasted ore in the Ore sack in time, the blasted ore will disappear from the inventory and a message will appear in the chat interface "Your blasted ore has disintegrated."

Like pay-dirt, blasted ore cannot be deposited in a bank.
