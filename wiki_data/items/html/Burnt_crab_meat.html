Burnt crab meat is the result of accidentally burning crab meat while attempting to make cooked crab meat. The burn rate while cooking these will decrease as players reach higher Cooking levels, stopping entirely at level 62.

The burnt crab meat has no use whatsoever. Unlike most burnt food, it is not tradeable.
