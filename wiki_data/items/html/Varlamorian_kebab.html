Varlamorian kebabs can be purchased from Emelio after completion of Meat and Greet. Like other kebabs, they heal a variable amount, varying between 3-30 hitpoints. Unlike other kebabs, they cannot drain your stats, and even have a chance to boost Attack, Defence, and Strength levels by 2.

On average, players will be healed by approximately 4 + 10% of their hitpoints level (based on current estimates) when eating a Varlamorian kebab. This is more than a normal kebab, with no risk of stat drain.

Like other kebabs, Varlamorian kebabs cannot be made through the Cooking skill.
