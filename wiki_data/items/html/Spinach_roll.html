Spinach roll is a food item that restores 2 hitpoints when eaten. It is received as a reward from random events such as the Mysterious Old Man and Rick Turpentine, or looted from opening a Crystal chest, Christmas cracker or Barrel.

During Demon Slayer, Traiborn will give players a spinach roll if they choose chat options ( 3•2•1)…

3I need to get a key given to you by Sir Prysin.
2He's one of the king's knights.
1Yes Please.

When eaten, the messages You eat the spinach roll. and It tastes a bit weird, but it fills you up. are displayed.
