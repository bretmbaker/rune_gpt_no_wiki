An uncut zenyte is the highest tier of gem in Old School RuneScape. It can be made by combining a cut onyx with a zenyte shard at the wall of flame beneath the Temple of Marimbo on Ape Atoll, while wearing a zombie monkey greegree (small or big). This requires completion of Monkey Madness II and level 70 Crafting, and grants 15 experience. It can then be cut with level 89 Crafting into zenyte, granting 50 experience.

Cut zenyte is used to make enchanted zenyte jewellery, which are some of the most powerful in the game. These are amulet of torture, necklace of anguish, ring of suffering, and tormented bracelet.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Zenyte appears to be derived from Zenith which means "the highest point", referenced by being the highest level gemstone.
  • This item is similar to the uncut hydrix, which is present in RuneScape. Both require an onyx to produce a complete gem, which is used to craft the strongest jewellery items available.
