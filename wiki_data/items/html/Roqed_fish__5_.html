Roqed fish (5) is a food item made by using raw roqed fish (5) on a fire within the Chambers of Xeric. Eating a roqed fish heals the player for 20 hitpoints.

Cooking roqed fish requires a Cooking level of 75, and yields 25 Cooking experience for each successfully cooked fish. For the list of known burn levels, refer to the burn level article.
