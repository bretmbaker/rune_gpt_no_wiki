A premade dr' dragon is a drink that can be bought at Blurberry Bar in the south-east corner of the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] of the Grand Tree. Premade drinks cannot be used in the Gnome Restaurant activity. When drunk, it increases Strength by 1-7, decreases Attack by 4, and raises a few hitpoints, just like the Drunk dragon drink that can be made with the Cooking skill.
