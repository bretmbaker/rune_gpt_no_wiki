A mithril crossbow is a crossbow that is stronger than the steel crossbow but weaker than the adamant crossbow. It requires a Ranged level of 36 to wield, and can fire bolts up to and including mithril bolts. It has an attack range of 7 increased to 9 with longrange.

Mithril crossbows can be made through the Fletching skill at level 54; a player must first cut a maple stock for the crossbow from maple logs, granting 32 Fletching experience. Then, a player must add mithril limbs to the stock with a hammer in their inventory, granting 64 experience and making an unstrung mithril crossbow. Finally, a player must string the crossbow with a crossbow string, granting 32 experience. Creating a mithril crossbow from raw materials grants 128 Fletching experience in total.
