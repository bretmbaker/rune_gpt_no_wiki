Plundered salvage is a type of salvage recovered from pirate shipwrecks located in the Feldip Strait, using a salvaging hook. 30 Sailing XP is received for each plundered salvage recovered.

Salvage can be stored in a ship's cargo hold and sorted at a salvaging station to receive various useful drops, although this does not yield further XP.
