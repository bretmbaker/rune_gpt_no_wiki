The elder maul (or) is a cosmetic variant of the elder maul with an elder maul ornament kit added to it. It can be dismantled at any time, returning both parts back to the player. It is a two-handed crush weapon that requires Attack and Strength levels of 75 to wield.

Along with the non-cosmetic variant, it is the highest tier crush weapon available in Old School RuneScape. It overtakes the godswords as the weapon with the highest strength bonus. Despite the strength bonus, the abyssal bludgeon often has higher overall damage output against low-defence monsters because of its quicker attack speed, making it only useful in places such as Tekton. Because of its high strength bonus, it is often used as a finisher in player killing.
