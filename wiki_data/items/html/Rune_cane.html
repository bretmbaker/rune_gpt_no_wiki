The rune cane is obtained as a reward from hard Treasure Trails. As with standard rune equipment, level 40 Attack is needed to equip this item. It is not used often as a weapon as it has the same stats as a rune mace with a 1-tick slower attack speed.

Rune canes cannot be made by players. This cane is topped with a cut dragonstone gem.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room.
