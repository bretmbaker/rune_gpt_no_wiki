Beekeeper's boots are an item gained from the Beekeeper random event and are part of the beekeeper's outfit. They give no bonuses apart from their cosmetic appeal.

This item can be stored in the fancy dress box of a costume room, as part of the Beekeeper Outfit set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
