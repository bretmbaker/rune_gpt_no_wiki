Lye paste is a reagent used to make Varlamorian potions in Mastering Mixology. It can be obtained by using mid-level herbs or unfinished potions made with said herbs on the refiner.

The mixology lab notes (found at lab bookshelf near the bank) can be referenced for combinations used for each potion.
