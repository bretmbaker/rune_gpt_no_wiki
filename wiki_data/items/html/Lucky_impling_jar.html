The lucky impling jar is the result of successfully catching a lucky impling via the Hunter skill. This impling has the highest requirement to catch, requiring level 89 Hunter or 99 Hunter if caught barehanded.

The lucky impling is approximately ten times rarer than a dragon impling in Gielinor, and closer to nine times rarer in Puro-Puro.[1]

Loot[edit | edit source]

Main article: Treasure Trails ยง Rewards

Looting a lucky impling gives players one roll on the drop table of an easy, medium, hard, elite, or master clue scroll. There is an equal chance of rolling into each tier's clue scroll table.[2]

At current Grand Exchange prices, opening a lucky impling jar gives 64,702 coins worth of loot, on average.

Although looting a lucky impling has a chance of rolling the master clue scroll table, it is not possible to receive a bloodhound pet when doing so.[3] Similarly, it is not possible to receive a master clue scroll as a result of opening a lucky impling jar.[4][5]

The following drop rates are provided by Jagex, unless otherwise specified.

Impling jar[edit | edit source]

An empty impling jar is returned when removing the loot, with a 10% chance of it breaking.[6]
