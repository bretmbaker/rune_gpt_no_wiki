An ensouled bloodveld head is an item which can be dropped by bloodvelds. It is used to gain Prayer experience by using the level 72 Magic spell Expert Reanimation from the Arceuus spellbook. Killing the reanimated bloodveld requires level 50 Slayer and will grant the player 1,040 Prayer experience.

Ensouled heads can be reanimated anywhere as long as the head is on the ground within the 31x31 area centred on its initial drop location and has not been put into the bank, trade screen, Grand Exchange, or price checker.[1][2] Otherwise, it must be taken to the Dark Altar in Arceuus to be reanimated.
