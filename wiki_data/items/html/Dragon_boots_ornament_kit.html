The dragon boots ornament kit is a possible reward from hard Treasure Trails. It is purely decorative, and can be attached to a pair of dragon boots, making it a pair of dragon boots (g).

Once attached, the ornamented item becomes untradeable, but the kit can be detached from the item and sold.
