The Iced gingerbread shield is an item from the 2019 Christmas event, created when the player enters the Icelord's cage while wielding the Gingerbread shield. The colour of the iced shield is random, and unlike the Green gingerbread shield earned as a reward from completing the event, the iced gingerbread shields can not be transformed between the three colours.
