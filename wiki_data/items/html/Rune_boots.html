Rune boots are a pair of boots made of runite. They require a Defence level of 40 to equip. Players without the required Defence level can choose to wear climbing boots instead, which have the same strength bonus but lower defence bonuses.

As with all metal boots, this piece of armour cannot be made using the Smithing skill.
