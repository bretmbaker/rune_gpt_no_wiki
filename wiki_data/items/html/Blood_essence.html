Blood essence is an item dropped by certain NPCs in the Ancient Prison, a possible reward from the Tombs of Amascut, as well as a potential reward when opening an intricate pouch. When one is activated, it becomes untradeable. While activated and in the inventory, it will give a 50% chance to craft an extra blood rune per pure essence, daeyalt essence, guardian essence or dark essence fragment for no additional experience. Each blood essence can craft an extra 1,000 blood runes before being destroyed. Blood essence works in Guardians of the Rift, however, the additional blood runes crafted will not provide additional catalytic guardian stones. Blood essence does not provide benefit nor consume charges at the Ourania Altar.

The activated blood essence is destroyed upon death, and cannot be recovered from an Item Retrieval Service.

As a blood rune costs 307 coins each, each blood essence could add an additional 307,000 coins in total. Factoring in the cost of each essence, one can expect to earn 225,366 coins for every blood essence used.

Date Changes

(update)
  • The value of the item was halved from 200,000 to 100,000, lowering its high alch value from the previous 120,000.
  • The active version of the item can no longer be alchemised.

(update | poll)

The item is now obtainable.

16 December 2021
(update)

The item was added to the game, but was unobtainable.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • This item was likely derived from Blood essence in RuneScape, which is used with potions rather than in runecrafting.
