The corrupted kiteshield is an item introduced during the Christmas corrupt cluefest, obtained by speaking to Phantuwti Fanstuwi Farsight with an empty vial and oak logs in your inventory.

It has stats identical to that of an iron kiteshield.

This item can be stored in the armour case of a costume room, as part of the Corrupted armour set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
