Ugthanki meat is a type of food that restores 3 Hitpoints when eaten. It can be obtained by cooking raw ugthanki meat, granting 40 experience when successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting in burnt meat. The burn rate decreases as the player's Cooking level increases. Ugthanki meat is unusual as despite lacking a cooking requirement, it never stops burning altogether even at level 99. At levels up to 82, the chance to burn this meat is higher on a range than on a fire.

Ugthanki meat is used to make ugthanki kebab which restores 19 hitpoints.
