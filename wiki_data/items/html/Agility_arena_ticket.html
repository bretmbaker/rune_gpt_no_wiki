Agility Arena tickets are earned by taking part in the Brimhaven Agility Arena. One ticket and one Brimhaven voucher is earned for tagging a ticket dispenser. They are exchanged at the Agility Arena Store by talking to Pirate Jackie the Fruit for 345 Agility experience each, or 379.5 experience each with Karamja gloves 2 or higher equipped.

Only 1 ticket per minute is possible as the dispenser respawns about every minute (98 ticks). Never missing a dispenser, without Karamja gloves 4, it takes at least 1 hour and 37 minutes for 100 tickets and around 16 hours for 1,000 tickets.

When doing Mourning's End Part II, Thorgel will not accept the discontinued Agility Arena Ticket. You must go to the Brimhaven Agility Arena and get a new ticket.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
8 May 2024
(update)
  • The original Agility arena ticket is now discontinued, and no longer rewarded from ticket dispensers.
    • Players with the old Agility arena tickets can still redeem the tickets at the former Brimhaven Agility Arena Ticket Exchange shop run by Pirate Jackie the Fruit.
  • Players no longer need to tag two pillars back-to-back to earn tickets.
  • Tickets are now only redeemable for 345 Agility experience each. Items are now purchased in exchange for Brimhaven vouchers.
  • Agility experience rewarded from exchanging tickets no longer scale based on the amount exchanged.
5 March 2015
(update | poll)

There is now a 10% chance to receive double tickets in the Brimhaven Agility Arena after completing the elite Karamja Diary.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Agility Arena tickets have the same model as castle wars tickets.
