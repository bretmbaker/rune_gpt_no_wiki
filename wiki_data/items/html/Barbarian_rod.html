The barbarian rod is a heavier version of the fishing rod used during the Fishing portion of Barbarian Training. When you start that portion of the training Otto Godblessed will let you take this fishing rod from underneath the bed in his cabin; if you lose or drop it, get another from the bed. It uses fishing bait, feathers, fish offcuts, roe, or caviar as bait. It is the only fishing rod capable of catching leaping trout, leaping salmon and leaping sturgeon. It can only be used in the fishing spots at Otto's Grotto, and Mount Quidamortem in the Kebos Lowlands. Since barbarian fishing gives marginal Strength and Agility experience with each catch, it is more efficient than other training methods such as fly fishing.

The equipable version of the barbarian rod, the pearl barbarian rod, can be bought with molch pearls from Alry the Angler.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
9 March 2022
(update)

The player will now appear to be holding a pearl barbarian rod instead of a normal fishing rod when fishing with a barbarian rod.

7 July 2021
(update)

The barbarian rod's position on female players has been adjusted so that they appear to be held properly when fishing.

1 June 2017
(update)

The "Drop" option was replaced with a "Destroy" option, due to players commonly dropping it by accident.

8 September 2016
(update)

The item's value was increased from 1 to 76, so it would always appear above a leaping sturgeon.
