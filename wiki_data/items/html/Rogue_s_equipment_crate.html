Rogue's equipment crates are obtained as a reward from the Rogues' Den minigame. Searching it gives the player the choice to either receive one piece of rogue equipment, or a rogue kit.

If the player chooses to receive rogue equipment, they can choose which piece to receive.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • This item was introduced for Ultimate Ironman accounts. Previously, the minigame would check a player's bank to determine which rogue's equipment piece to give. However, since Ultimate Ironmen cannot access their banks, they would repeatedly receive the same piece, leaving them unable to acquire the full set.
  • When taking a rogue top from the crate, the chat dialogue has "...how convenient." appended to the message.
