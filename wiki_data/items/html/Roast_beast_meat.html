Roast beast meat are a type of food that can be obtained by cooking a skewered beast on a fire, requiring level 21 Cooking and granting 82.5 experience when successful. Players may burn the meat while cooking one, resulting in burnt beast meat; the burn rate while cooking these will decrease as players reach higher Cooking levels. It will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 99.

When eaten, roast beast meat heals 8 hitpoints.
