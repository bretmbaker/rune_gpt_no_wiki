Opal dragon bolts (e) are opal dragon bolts that are enchanted via the Enchant Crossbow Bolt (Opal) spell by a player with level 4 Magic. The spell enchants 10 bolts per cast, and uses 1 cosmic and 2 air runes. These bolts can only be used with a Dragon crossbow or better.

Special effect[edit | edit source]

Enchanted opal dragon bolts have a chance (5%) of triggering the Lucky Lightning effect, which results in a lightning bolt striking your opponent and causing extra damage equal to 10% of your visible ranged level. For example, at 112 ranged level, the extra damage is 11.

If the player has completed the hard Kandarin Diary, there is a 10% increase of the likelihood that the special effect of an enchanted bolt will occur, giving enchanted opal dragon bolts an overall 5.5% chance of the special effect activating.[1]

This effect rolls its chance of activation regardless of whether or not it passes an accuracy roll.
