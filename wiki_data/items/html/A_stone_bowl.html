A stone bowl[sic] is a quest item used only in Elemental Workshop I. It is found by searching the boxes north-east of the hatch in the Elemental Workshop.

It is used to make a stone bowl filled with lava from a lava trough. The full stone bowl is then used on the furnace to heat it up.

Duplicates can be drop tricked by searching the boxes to obtain another.
