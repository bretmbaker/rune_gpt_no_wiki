The Eye of ayak is a powered staff requiring level 83 Magic to wield, dropped by Osto-Ayak.

It is the second strongest powered staff, between the Sanguinesti staff and Tumeken's shadow in power. It is intended to be useful even after obtaining a Tumeken's shadow, with the utility of its special attack and faster attack speed.

Special attack[edit | edit source]

Special Attack: Consumes 50% special attack energy. Has an attack speed of 5 ticks, but with doubled Accuracy and a 30% higher Max Hit. Additionally, it drains Magic Defence equal to damage dealt, but it cannot drain below 0. NPCs will not regenerate lost Magic Defence.
