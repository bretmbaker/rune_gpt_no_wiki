Dust is an item obtained from searching the northern skeleton in the ground level of the Uzer Mastaba, in the tunnel connecting to it. Players can only have one in their inventory, unless dropping all dust in the inventory before searching the skeleton again.

It currently has no use, but is notably purchasable by free-to-play players on the Grand Exchange.
