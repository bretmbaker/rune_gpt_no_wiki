The bronze full helmet is the weakest full helmet in Old School RuneScape. The bronze full helmet grants greater defensive bonuses than its medium helmet counterpart, though at a cost of offensive bonuses for Ranged and Magic. There are no Defence requirements for wearing this helmet.

To create a bronze full helmet the player needs at least 7 Smithing, a hammer and 2 bronze bars. To make a helmet use one of the bars on an anvil with the hammer in the inventory. This grants 25 Smithing experience.
