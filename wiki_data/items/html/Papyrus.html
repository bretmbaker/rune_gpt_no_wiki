Papyrus is an item used in building various furniture in a player-owned house. Additionally, after completing Enlightened Journey, using a ball of wool and unlit candle on a papyrus results in an origami balloon.

Papyrus is notably required for the following quests:

  • Darkness of Hallowvale (given during quest)
  • Three for Enlightened Journey
  • At least three for Legends' Quest to map the Kharazi Jungle
  • One for The Golem quest to write him a new program
  • Zogre Flesh Eaters (given during quest)
  • Shilo Village
  • While Guthix Sleeps
