Goat dung is an item used in the quest Making Friends with My Arm. It is obtained by using a bucket on a pile of goat poo in the north-easternmost building in Weiss.

It is given to Snowflake to fertilise two herb patches in Weiss: one for the Weiss trolls to grow goutweed, and another for players to use after the quest, which, like the Troll Stronghold herb patch, will never be diseased.
