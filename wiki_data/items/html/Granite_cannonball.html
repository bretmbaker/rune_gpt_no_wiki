Granite cannonballs are enhanced cannonballs created by using a regular cannonball on granite dust. This requires 50 Smithing and gives no experience.

These cannonballs increase the max hit of the dwarf multicannon from 30 to 35, equating to an approximate damage per second increase of 16.67%.

When a player creates granite cannonballs, a message in the chatbox will state You apply a thick coating of granite dust to your cannonballs.

If a player is killed by another player in the Wilderness or a PvP world, the killer will receive regular cannonballs.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Granite cannonballs had a festive override to look like christmas puddings during the 2017, 2018, and 2019 Christmas events. The override had a darker colour in 2018 compared to 2017 and 2019. Upon the 2019 event ending, the colour was accidentally changed to be identical to regular cannonballs, which was fixed the following week.
