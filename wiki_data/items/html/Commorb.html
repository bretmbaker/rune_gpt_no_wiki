The Commorb is a quest item in the Wanted! quest. Upon joining the White Knights, Sir Tiffy Cashien gives one to the player for 10,000 or makes one for 1 law rune, 1 molten glass, and 1 enchanted gem. It is used to contact Savant, who also helps player during The Slug Menace.

The Commorb is upgraded to the Commorb v2 by Sir Tiffy Cashien which starts The Slug Menace quest.
