Revenant ether is always dropped by revenants in the Revenant Caves. The bracelet of ethereum has a feature to automatically absorb ether from defeated revenants.

Revenant ether is always dropped on death in the Wilderness, even when it is stored as charges within weapons or a bracelet of ethereum.

Uses[edit | edit source]

Item charging[edit | edit source]

Revenant ether is used to charge the following wilderness weapons and other items:

  • Craw's bow
  • Webweaver bow
  • Thammaron's sceptre(autocasting)
  • Accursed sceptre(autocasting)
  • Viggora's chainmace
  • Ursine chainmace
  • Bracelet of ethereum

Combat dummy variant[edit | edit source]

500 ether can be used to enhance an ornate undead combat dummy, enabling it to act as a monster in the Wilderness.
