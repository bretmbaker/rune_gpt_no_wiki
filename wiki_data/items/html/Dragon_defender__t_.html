The dragon defender (t) is a dragon defender with a dragon defender ornament kit attached to it. The attached ornament kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the defender.

The trimmed defender can be dismantled anytime, returning the defender and the tradeable ornament kit.

If the user dies in PvP with the trimmed defender and it is not protected, the ornament added will be lost on death. If the death also took place at or beyond level 20 Wilderness, the dragon defender itself will be lost as well. A trouver parchment can be brought to Perdu alongside the item to "lock" it, preventing it from being lost in the Wilderness for one death.

If the user dies a Player versus monster death anywhere with an unprotected dragon defender (t), including at or beyond level 20 Wilderness, both the ornament kit and the defender will be Items Kept on Death. This will prevent the dragon defender from dropping to the player's gravestone as coins, as it otherwise would in the case of a PvM death at wilderness level 20 if untrimmed. This effectively saves the defender in this situation. It will protect regardless of if the user is skulled or praying Protect Item. However, both the defender and the ornament kit will be lost if the user's death is attributed to another player, which is likely to happen if one were in combat with another player at any point relatively close to when they died.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The dragon defender (t) appears to lack the reddish glow of its regular counterpart.
  • Despite being gold-trimmed, the item's name is inconsistent with most other ornamented dragon equipment, which have (g) at the end of their names rather than (t). The defender shares this inconsistency with the dragon scimitar (or).
  • The dragon defender (t) originally had a slightly different appearance in the inventory. This was changed in response to a reddit thread because the sprite was slightly clipping the border of the offhand slot in the equipment menu.
