Raw slimy eel is a fish that can be caught with a fishing rod and fishing bait. They require a Fishing level of 28 to catch, and grant 80 Fishing experience per catch when caught in the Lumbridge Swamp Caves, Dorgesh-Kaan South Dungeon, or the Ruins of Camdozaal. They grant 65 Fishing experience when caught in the Mort Myre Swamp. It can be cooked into cooked slimy eel with a Cooking level of 28.

Raw slimy eel is one of the required items in Land of the Goblins, where it is used as bait to catch a Hemenster whitefish. It is also a possible item needed to obtain the magic secateurs in Fairytale I - Growing Pains.

Fishing locations[edit | edit source]

There are 4 fishing locations at which slimy eels can be caught:

  • Mort Myre Swamp and south of Mort Myre: Requires Priest in Peril.
  • Dorgesh-Kaan South Dungeon: Requires Death to the Dorgeshuun, as well as a suitable light source. Players can also net fish for frog spawn.
  • Lumbridge Swamp Caves: Requires a suitable light source. Players can also net fish for frog spawn.
  • Ruins of Camdozaal: Requires Below Ice Mountain
