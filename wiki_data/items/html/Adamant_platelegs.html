Adamant platelegs are platelegs made of Adamant and, as such, require a Defence level of 30 to wear. These platelegs have the same stats as the Adamant plateskirt, but are 1kg heavier.

Adamant platelegs can be smithed using 3 adamant bars at level 86 Smithing, granting 187.5 experience.
