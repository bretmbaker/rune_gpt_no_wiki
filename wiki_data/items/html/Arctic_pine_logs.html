Arctic pine logs are cut from arctic pine trees, found on the island of Neitiznot. A Woodcutting level of 54 is required to cut them, giving 40 experience per log; 42 Firemaking is needed to make a fire using these type of logs, granting 125 experience per log. Burning them at a forester's campfire grants 41.6 Firemaking experience. A Woodcutting level of 56 is required to split these logs.

They are also used to make Fremennik round shields, requiring a level of 56 in Woodcutting. Two of these logs, a bronze nail, and a rope are required to make one, granting 34 Crafting experience.

Players can also use the logs on a woodcutting stump to make split logs for the quest The Fremennik Isles.
