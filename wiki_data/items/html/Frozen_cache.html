The frozen cache is a drop received from defeating the Phantom Muspah. When opened, it gives a reward from the Muspah's drop table.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • If players receive a frozen cache as loot, the chatbox will state You open the frozen cache and find... another frozen cache inside! How peculiar.
  • If a player tries to open a frozen cache on Entrana or in the Entrana Dungeon, they receive a message saying "You can't open that here."
