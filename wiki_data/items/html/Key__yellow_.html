The yellow key is a quest item used only in Dragon Slayer I. It is dropped by the skeleton wielding a round shield on the 2nd floor[UK]3rd floor[US] floor of Melzar's Maze.

Is it used to unlock the yellow doors, where it will disintegrate upon being used.
