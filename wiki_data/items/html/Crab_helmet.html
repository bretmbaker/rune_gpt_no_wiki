A crab helmet is a piece of armour. There are no level requirements to wear the crab helmet. The crab helmet has similar stats to the iron med helm, except the slash defence and stab defence stats are switched, and it has negative ranged attack bonus.

Crafting this helmet has a chance to fail producing a broken crab shell.
