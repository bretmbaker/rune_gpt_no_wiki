A bagged fancy hedge can be bought in Falador or the Farming Guild from a Garden supplier for 25,000 coins. They can be planted in the formal garden of a player-owned house, requiring a Construction level of 72, providing 158 experience to both Construction and Farming. The player must have a filled watering can in their inventory to plant it. It is only for decoration.
