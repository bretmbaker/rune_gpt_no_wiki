A gourmet impling jar is the result of successfully catching a Gourmet impling, while also having an empty impling jar. It requires 28 Hunter to catch a gourmet impling.

This jar can be traded in singularly to Elnock Inquisitor for 3 empty impling jars.

One gourmet impling jar, along with three baby impling jars and two young impling jars, can be traded to Elnock for a vial of Imp repellent. Alternatively, three gourmet impling jars, along with two earth impling jars and one essence impling jar, can be traded for a magic butterfly net.

Loot[edit | edit source]

At current Grand Exchange prices, opening a gourmet impling jar gives 881.00 worth of loot, on average. This means opening gourmet impling jars makes an average profit of −3,505.00 per jar, however this does not include loot from clue scrolls.

On average, one would profit −87,625.00 to get an easy clue scroll from gourmet impling jars at current Grand Exchange prices, excluding the rewards from the completed Treasure Trails.

The following drop rates are provided by Jagex, unless otherwise specified.

Impling jar[edit | edit source]

An empty impling jar is returned when removing the loot, with a 10% chance of it breaking.[1]
