Marlo's crate is a quest item reward in Daddy's Home. It is obtained from Marlo after building all the furniture required for Old Man Yarlo.

If used in Construction the materials hold a potential of 1,545 Construction experience.

This item cannot leave a placeholder. If lost on death, you can reclaim it for free from Marlo.
