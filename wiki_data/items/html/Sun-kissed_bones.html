Sun-kissed bones are a type of blessed bones obtained from various activities in Varlamore. They are broken down with a chisel to obtain 45 blessed bone shards and 5 Crafting experience, which can then be sacrificed at the libation bowl at the Teomat.

As each shard sacrificed gives 5 Prayer experience, each sun-kissed bones gives 225 Prayer experience overall. If using a jug of blessed sunfire wine at the libation bowl instead of a regular jug of blessed wine, this is increased to 6 Prayer experience per shard, or 270 Prayer experience per bone.
