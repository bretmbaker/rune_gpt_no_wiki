A bolt mould is an item that is used for making silver bolts through the Crafting skill. It can be purchased from the crafting stores in Rimmington, Al Kharid, or Civitas illa Fortis for 25 coins.

Crafting silver bolts with the bolt mould requires level 21 Crafting, provides 50 Crafting experience points per silver bar used, and produces ten silver bolts (unf) per bar.
