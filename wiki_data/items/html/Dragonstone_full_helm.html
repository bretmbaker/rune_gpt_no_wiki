The dragonstone full helm can be found as a rare reward upon opening the Elven Crystal Chest within Prifddinas. It has the same stats as rune full helm and requires 40 Defence to equip.

This item can be stored in the armour case of a costume room, as part of the Dragonstone armour set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
