A bowl of hot water can be created by using a normal bowl on a water source to turn it into a bowl of water and then heating this on a stove, a range, or a fire. When added to an empty cup, a cup of hot water is made.

A bowl of hot water is used to make Guthix rest and also used in the quest One Small Favour.
