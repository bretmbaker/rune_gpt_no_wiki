The clue compass is an item that was obtainable during the Raging Echoes League, given to players upon unlocking the Clue Compass relic. The compass is equipped to the ammo slot.

The item allows players to teleport to any STASH unit (with the exception of 3 in Varlamore near the Twilight Temple), and Falo the Bard, provided that the destination is in a region the player has unlocked. The player may also teleport to the location of the clue step they have last read.

This item ignores wilderness teleport restrictions.

For Falo the Bard clue steps, "use" the clue compass on Falo the Bard to fulfil the item requirement.

This item also acts as a light source.

If lost or destroyed, the clue compass can be reclaimed from The Sage.
