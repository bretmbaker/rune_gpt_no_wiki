Jade bolts (e) are blurite bolts tipped with enchanted jade. Jade bolts can be enchanted into enchanted jade bolts through the spell Enchant Crossbow Bolt (Jade) by a player with level 14 Magic. The spell enchants 10 jade bolts at a time. These bolts are untradeable.

Special effect[edit | edit source]

Jade bolts (e) have a 6% chance of triggering the Earth's Fury effect, which results in the bolts knocking their target to the ground for 8 ticks (4.8 seconds), although agile opponents may resist this.

One could conceivably use the Jade bolts (e) special ability for tactical purposes in Castle Wars as a stuns to limit the flag carrier's mobility, serving as a low-level ranged alternative to Ice Barrage or the Dragon spear. However, flag carriers with high Agility will be very resistant to this.

Unlike other enchanted bolts, jade bolts actually roll twice when used against players in order to activate: the rate of the special effect occurring, then rolling against the target's Agility level. In addition, Protect from Magic will also fully block Earth's Fury from activating when used by a target.[1] As such, the chance of it activating overall may be significantly lower than other enchanted bolts.

This effect rolls its chance of activation regardless of whether or not it passes an accuracy roll.

If the player has completed the Hard Kandarin Diary, there is a 10% increase of the likelihood that the special effect of an enchanted bolt will occur, resulting in a first roll chance of 6.6%. This effect is always active regardless if the headgear is equipped.
