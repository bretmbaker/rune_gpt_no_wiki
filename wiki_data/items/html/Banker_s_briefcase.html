The banker's briefcase is an item that was obtainable during the Raging Echoes League, given to players upon unlocking the Bank Heist relic. The briefcase is equipped to the cape slot.

The item allows players to teleport to any bank, bank chest, or bank deposit box, providing that the destination is in a region the player has unlocked.

This item ignores wilderness teleport restrictions.

If lost or destroyed, the banker's briefcase can be reclaimed from The Sage.
