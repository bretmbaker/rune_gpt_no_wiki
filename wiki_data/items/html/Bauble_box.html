A bauble box was a container used to store baubles during the 2014 Christmas event. Full boxes could be given to the pixies in exchange for a scarf. Taking five boxes to the tree in Varrock and decorating it would give the player a Marionette, Santa outfit, and tradeable Christmas rares.
