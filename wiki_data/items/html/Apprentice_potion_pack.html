Apprentice potion packs are an item purchased from the Mixology Rewards shop with 420 Mox resin, 70 Aga resin, and 30 Lye resin, which are earned from participating in the Mastering Mixology minigame.

Purchasing apprentice potion packs from the shop requires level 60 Herblore (not boostable).

Loot[edit | edit source]

Drop rates estimated based on 1,030 samples from the Drop Rate Project, unless otherwise cited.

Each potion pack rolls the loot table 4 times, giving the player 2-4 noted 3-dose potions per roll.

The average Apprentice potion pack opening is worth 5,692.24.
