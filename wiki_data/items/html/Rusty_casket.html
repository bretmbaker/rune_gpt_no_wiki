The rusty casket is a quest item found by the player during the Horror from the Deep quest after killing the Dagannoth Mother. After completing the quest, players can take this to Jossik by going up the stairs of the lighthouse and he will tell them something is written on it.

Note: dropping the casket and trying to pick it back up, the game will yield the message, It is too old and fragile to pick up. Additionally, Telekinetic Grab cannot be cast on it. If the casket is dropped, Jossik will state that he found it and prompt the player to select a prayer book.

Depending on which answer they give, he will give them a damaged prayer book in one of three colours, which correspond to the three major gods of Gielinor: Guthix, Saradomin, and Zamorak. You can buy the other books from him afterwards for 5000 coins each, including those for Armadyl, Bandos, and Zaros.

The pages for these books can be received from all levels of Treasure Trails, except for beginner clues.

Should you lose this book Jossik will give you the old book back. All pages previously put in these books will still be there.
