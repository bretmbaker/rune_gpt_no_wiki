A Banner is a decorative flag made on a Banner easel in a Player-owned house with the crest of the Humans Against Monsters cult. They can be created at 48 Crafting with 1 plank, 1 bolt of cloth, and the appropriate crest assigned by Sir Renitee.

Sir Renitee will not assign a crest unless the player has over level 16 Construction. Players can use a Banner easel in another player's house if they do not have one.
