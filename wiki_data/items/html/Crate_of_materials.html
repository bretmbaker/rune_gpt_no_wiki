A crate of materials is an item in the Sailing alpha. It is picked up from the ledger table during the Pandemonium quest and must be delivered to Junior Jim. Upon picking it up it is automatically equipped, and attempting to unequip it prints the message You can't remove the cargo. You'll need to find a safe place to put it down first. It can be set down at the ledger table.
