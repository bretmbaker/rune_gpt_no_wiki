Large reef shark fin was part of the March 2025 Sailing Alpha.
It was only available on temporary alpha worlds, and can't be found in the main version of Old School RuneScape.
This article contains information about a subject known only to exist in the game APIs or cache.
The content is not known to be accessible in game, or may be reserved for use in a future update.
