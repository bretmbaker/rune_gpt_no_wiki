The Varlamore graceful top is one of many variants of the graceful top. It is recoloured by purchasing the Graceful crafting kit from Worm Tongue at the Colossal Wyrm Agility Course in exchange for 650 termites; this requires having the full set.

This item can be stored in the magic wardrobe of a costume room, as part of the graceful outfit. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
