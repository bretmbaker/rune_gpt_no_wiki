The sticky red goop is a quest item used in The Fremennik Trials quest when helping Peer the Seer.

The goop is made by heating the red herring found upstairs in his house, and is then used on a wooden disk, also found in his house, to turn it into a red disk. The red disk is then used on an abstract mural downstairs, along with another old red disk, in order to obtain the vase lid needed to complete Peer's challenge.
