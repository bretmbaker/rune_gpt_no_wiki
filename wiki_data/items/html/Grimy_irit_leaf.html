A grimy irit leaf is a herb that has not yet been cleaned to make an irit leaf. Grimy irit leaves may be grown from an irit seed with a Farming level of 44. It is dropped by certain NPCs. Cleaning a grimy irit requires a Herblore level of 40 and gives 8.8 Herblore experience. Clean irits are used to make super attack, super anti-poison potions, irit tar, and antidote++s with Herblore levels of 45, 48, 55, and 79 respectively. They are dropped inside the Edgeville Dungeon and Taverley Dungeon by the Chaos druids.

When cleaned, the message You clean the Grimy irit leaf. is displayed.
