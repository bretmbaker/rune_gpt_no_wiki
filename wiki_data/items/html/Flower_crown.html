The flower crown is an item obtained from Gilbert in the flower field south-east of Barbarian Village. It was first obtained from him as a reward for completing the 2022 Pride event.

There are nine unique versions of the crown representing different variants of sexual orientation or gender identity, whose colours can be changed at any time by right-clicking the "Change" option on the crown.

If destroyed, a replacement can be obtained from Diango in Draynor Village or from the toy box of a costume room.
