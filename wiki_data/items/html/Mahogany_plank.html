Mahogany planks are the highest level planks used in the Construction skill, used to make some of the highest-level furniture. Each plank gives 140 experience when used in a player-owned house, and an average of 346.1 experience when used in expert Mahogany Homes contracts (including completion bonus). Mahogany planks are worth 350 experience when used to fix the broken bridges in the Hallowed Sepulchre, also requiring rune nails.
