A red marionette torso was used in the 2014 Christmas event. It could be found in the crates in Diango's Workshop. If used with the crate that holds red marionette heads, it would become a red marionette (head). When completed and strung, the marionette can be placed inside a puppet box to be handed over to the Bearded stranger at Taverley gate.
