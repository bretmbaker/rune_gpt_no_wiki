Adamantite bars can be smelted with 6 coal and 1 adamantite ore at level 70 Smithing. Smelting the bar grants 37.5 Smithing experience, and any item that is smithed from adamantite bars grants 62.5 Smithing experience per bar, yielding a net of 100 experience.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Adamantite (referred to as adamant) was used as one of the tiers of post count badges on the official Runescape Forums. Players needed to have made 2,500 posts to be given the adamant badge.
