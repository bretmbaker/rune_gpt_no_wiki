Burnt swordfish is the result of accidentally burning a raw swordfish while attempting to make a swordfish. The burn rate while cooking these will decrease as players reach higher Cooking levels, stopping entirely at level 86; wearing cooking gauntlets will stop them from burning at level 81 instead.

The burnt swordfish has no use whatsoever. Like most other burnt food, it is tradeable to other players but not on the Grand Exchange.
