A crate of live lobsters is an item in the Sailing alpha. It is the cargo used for the "Live lobster delivery" courier tasks. It is used when transporting cargo from one port to another as one of several chosen cargo crates when doing a courier task. They can also be placed inside of a ships cargo hold on a player's ship to avoid needing to be held and to allow for usage of ship modules. The crate must be picked up at the ledger table in the starting port and must be placed at the ledger table at the destination port to complete the task.
