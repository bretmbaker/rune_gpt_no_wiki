Asyn remains are an item found and used in the Shades of Mort'ton minigame. To obtain these remains, the player must kill asyn shades, located behind a solid black door in the Shade Catacombs. Shades encountered during Temple Trekking may also drop asyn remains.

To burn the asyn remains, the player needs yew pyre logs or better, level 65 Firemaking, and a tinderbox. First place the pyre logs on a funeral pyre, followed by the asyn remains, then light the pyre. This will grant the player Firemaking and Prayer experience (see table). Once burned, a black or silver shade key, or some coins, will appear next to the pyre. The keys can be used to either enter the catacombs or open the corresponding coloured doors to chests containing valuable items.
