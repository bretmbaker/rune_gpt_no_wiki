Dark dye allows one piece of the graceful outfit to be recoloured to a black and red variant. Six are needed to recolour the full graceful set, as the dye is consumed with one use.

It can be purchased from the Mysterious Hallowed Goods shop for 300 hallowed marks.

Recolouring[edit | edit source]

The recolouring, obtainable by using dark dye on a piece of the outfit, costs 300 Hallowed marks at the Hallowed Sepulchre per piece of Graceful, costing a total of 1800 hallowed marks.
