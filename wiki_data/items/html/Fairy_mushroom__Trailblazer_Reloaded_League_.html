The fairy mushroom is an item obtained within the Trailblazer Reloaded League, given to players at a bank upon unlocking the Fairy's Flight relic. The fairy mushroom is equipped to the shield slot.

The item allows players to teleport to any fairy ring, spirit tree, or tool leprechaun, providing that the destination is in a region the player has unlocked.

If lost or destroyed, the fairy mushroom can be reclaimed from The Sage.
