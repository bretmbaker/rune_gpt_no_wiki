The Shayzien boots (5) are boots that require 20 Defence to equip, and are part of the Shayzien armour (tier 5) set.

It is dropped by the tier 5 soldier at the Combat Ring in the Shayzien Encampment. It can also be created at an anvil with 87 Smithing and 1 lovakite bar.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
10 January 2024
(update | poll)
  • The "Destroy" option has been replaced with "Drop". Previously, the destroy text stated "You fought a Shayzien soldier for this. You'll have to repeat the fight for a replacement."
  • The item can now be created through the Smithing skill with the removal of Kourend Favour.
30 June 2021
(update | poll)

The tier 5 Shayzien boots has had its Ranged Attack bonus increased from -1 to +4.

10 January 2019
(update)

The examine text was modified, the old examine text was "Dress like a tier 5 soldier of the Shayzien House."
