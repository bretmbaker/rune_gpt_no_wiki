Corrupted paddlefish are food obtained within the Corrupted Gauntlet, healing 16 hitpoints. Like cooked karambwan, they can be eaten instantly with no delay after eating a paddlefish.

It can be made with 10 corrupted shards and a paddlefish at the singing bowl.
