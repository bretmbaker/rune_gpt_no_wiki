The Piscarilius graceful gloves are one of many variants of graceful gloves. They are recoloured from a pair of graceful gloves by using them on Osten in Shayzien (in the building east of the bank), costing 15 marks of grace. Doing so requires completion of The Queen of Thieves.

This item can be stored in the magic wardrobe of a costume room.
