A Castle Wars bracelet is a bracelet that aids the player in Castle Wars. It is obtained by casting Lvl-2 Enchant on an emerald bracelet.

When players are wielding this item at the moment they enter the game, they do an extra 20% damage against a bearer of a flag, and bandages heal 50% more. Players only need to be wearing it up until the time the game starts. After that, the charge is already given, and the player can switch to any item to be worn in the hand slot. Players instead may opt to give bracelets to Lanthus to have a charge used automatically when a game is started without needing to wear one at all.

The bracelet can have a maximum of 3 charges. Each game will use up one charge, and after all charges are gone it will be destroyed. The number of charges remaining is displayed in brackets in the item name.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
2 April 2025
(update | poll)

Castle Wars bracelets can now be handed to Lanthus to store charges, automatically consuming one per game.

31 August 2017
(update)

Partially charged versions of the item could now be turned into bank placeholders.

10 August 2017
(update)

The item was made available to free-to-play players.

26 February 2015
(update)
  • The item was renamed from "Castlewars brace" to "Castle wars bracelet".
  • Partially charged versions of the item were made untradeable on the Trading Post/Grand Exchange.
10 December 2014
(update)

Charged versions of the item were renamed from "Castlewar brace(n)" to "Castlewars brace(n)".

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Prior to an update on 10 August 2017, Castle wars bracelets were not available to free-to-play players. Despite this, enchanting it still requires membership.
