Tokens are items that can be found from completing a variety of specific daily tasks around Varlamore.

Players may only gain a token from a singular source per account per day, which is based on the player's character name and the day of the week (see below). For instance, on Mondays, players with 4-letter names can only get a token from the pickpocketing activity described below. A player cannot receive a token from a given source if a token from the same source is in their inventory. They can receive a duplicate if the token is in their bank, however. Whether in the bank or inventory, these tokens do not stack.

By wearing one or two (but not all three) of the required items for the easy emote clue, and yawning within the Grand Museum in Civitas illa Fortis, the Collector will appear with special dialogue related to the tokens, reacting if the player has at least one token in their inventory, responding that they need more. With all 7 tokens in the inventory, the player will be given a nice key in exchange for the tokens.

Token sources

The following table shows the sources of each token and their daily rotation.

Day Activity Location Additional information Image
1 Colosseum Wave 3 of the Fortis Colosseum (Show map) Completing wave 3 of the Colosseum while wearing a partyhat (the silver partyhat does not work). The partyhat must be equipped before the wave 3 starting menu appears.
2 Killing Buffalo In the buffalo pen east of the Outer Fortis farming patch (Show map) Players must be wearing gloves that provide protection whilst picking nettles; only the buffalo that respawns at the specific location (in the image to the right) will drop the token.
3 Digging South-east of Ralos' Rise mine (Show map) The dig location is the intersection of two arrows hidden in Varlamore. One can be found under plants near the onion field north-east of the Hunter Guild; the other is located at the base of the hill east of Ralos' Rise.

The sextant coordinates of the dig location are 3 degrees 1 minute south, 29 degrees 22 minutes west.

4 Mining calcified rocks Within the Cam Torum mine (Show map) The music track Scape Main must be playing whilst mining the rock. The rocks do not need to have flowing water.
5 Pickpocketing a citizen In the house south-east of the Sunrise Palace (Show map) The citizen must be pickpocketed whilst she is standing at the specific location (in the image to the right), one tile west of the food basket.
6 Fishing for bream Within the Streambound Cavern in Neypotzli (Show map) The player's Quest List must be sorted by Storyline in the interface settings prior to fishing for bream.
7 Breaking down blessed bat bones Anywhere To create them bat bones must be blessed on the Exposed altar atop Ralos' Rise. The token is received upon crafting Blessed bone shards. There is no specific location.

Eligibility for tokens

The length of a player's character name determines which token they are eligible to receive on a given day, thus players can only obtain more than one activity's token in a day by changing their name. It is currently Friday.(update)

2 or 9 characters Bones Colosseum Buffalo Digging Mining Pickpocketing Fishing
3 or 10 characters Fishing Bones Colosseum Buffalo Digging Mining Pickpocketing

Trivia

  • The token appears to be a recoloured version of the red disk used in the Fremennik Trials quest. The ground item model is slightly skewed, but the inventory icon is identical. Ultimately, both use an upscaled version of the item model of a regular coin.
  • The arrows that point to the location where the digging token can be obtained are identical to the arrows found in the Skullball course in the Werewolf Agility Course.
  • Bat bones could not be acquired in Varlamore on initial release. This is no longer the case as several bats spawn on Aldarin, which was released in Varlamore: The Rising Darkness.
