A ranger hat is a piece of headgear available as a reward from Barbarian Assault. It resembles the head of a Penance Ranger.

Equipping the hat requires level 45 Defence. Players who have killed the Penance Queen and have 275 honour points in all four roles can purchase a Ranger hat from Commander Connad, who is located on the ground floor[UK]1st floor[US] of the Barbarian Outpost.

If the player dies below level 20 Wilderness, it will remain in the player's inventory. However, the item will be unusable and must be repaired for a cost of 45,000 by using the item on Perdu.

This item can be stored in the armour case of a costume room.
