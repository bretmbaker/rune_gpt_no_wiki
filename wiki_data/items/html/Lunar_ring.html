The lunar ring is a piece of lunar equipment worn in the ring slot. The lunar ring can be obtained during the Lunar Diplomacy quest, and its location is revealed to players through a riddle told by Selene, located near the centre of the Lunar Isle. A player must take a spade and travel outside the south side of the village to the east until they come to a bridge, and over that bridge is a blue flower which the player must dig on top of. The lunar ring may also be bought from the Oneiromancer after a player has completed the quest, however if you don't own the other lunar equipment you will have to purchase those first. It can be stored in a magic wardrobe in a player-owned house.

Compared to other quest rewards, the lunar ring has the same magic bonus as the beacon ring, and 3 less magic bonus than the ring of shadows.

As with all lunar equipment, it is always lost on death. If the player loses their ring, they can obtain one from the Oneiromancer for 2,000 (or for free if lost before the quest is completed); this requires a seal of passage or completion of the elite Fremennik diary or the player will be teleported off the island. Players may also obtain a replacement ring from Perdu for 8,000.
