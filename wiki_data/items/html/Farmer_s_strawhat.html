The Farmer's strawhat is a strawhat acquired from the Tithe Farm minigame in exchange for 75 points. It is a part of the farmer's outfit. The strawhat grants 0.4% bonus Farming experience while equipped.

The appearance of the farmer's strawhat differs based on the player's gender.

This item can be stored in the armour case of a costume room, as part of the Farmer's outfit. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored. It can alternatively be stored in a STASH unit as it is required for an elite clue step.
