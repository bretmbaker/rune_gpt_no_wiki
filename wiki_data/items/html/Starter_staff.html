The starter staff is a powered staff obtained from a Deadman starter pack, along with the starter sword and bow. It has no requirements to wield, and is intended to be used to help a player get started in a seasonal Deadman Mode server.

It is equipped with a built-in Fire Strike, being able to cast 500 of them before fully degrading.

As with all starter weapons, it cannot be used against other players.
