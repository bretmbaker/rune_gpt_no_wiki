Boxing gloves are items that can only be obtained from a glove rack in a player-owned house. They come in two different colours: red and blue. They count as a two-handed slashing weapon, and deal a max hit of 12 to other players in the boxing ring. Other gloves cannot be worn while wielding the boxing gloves, and anything worn in the glove slot will be automatically unequipped when putting them on.

When walking with these equipped your character holds them up to their face in a typical boxing stance. They are the only weapon that can be used in a boxing ring in a player's Combat Room, and they are destroyed if a player tries to leave a house with them. Fighting with them in a player-owned house is their only use.
