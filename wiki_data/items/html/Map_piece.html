Map pieces are obtainable from multiple locations on the House on the Hill during the Dragon Slayer II quest. 24 pieces can be merged into a map that leads the player to Lithkren.

  • There are five on a stone chest in the basement.
  • There are three on a stone chest at the top floor.
  • There are four in the fungi next to the staircase outside.
  • There are seven scraps in a hook briar south of the house.
  • There are five on a mushtree just east of the house, near the strange stone.
