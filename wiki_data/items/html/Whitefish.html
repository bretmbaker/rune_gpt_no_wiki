Whitefish are caught within the Fishing Contest area, located in Hemenster, as part of the Land of the Goblins quest. Level 40 Fishing is required, along with a fishing rod and raw slimy eels for bait.

It is given to Aggie, the witch in Draynor Village, as a bleaching agent to remove the dye from goblin mail and turn it white.
