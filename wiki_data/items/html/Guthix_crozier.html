The Guthix crozier is part of the Guthix vestment set. It requires at least 60 Prayer to wield. The crozier is equipped as a staff in the weapons slot.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room, as part of the Guthix vestment set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.

It should be noted that despite being obtained from a Reward casket (hard), it is stored in the easy rewards section of the treasure chest.
