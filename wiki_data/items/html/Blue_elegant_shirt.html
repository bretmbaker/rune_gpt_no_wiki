Blue elegant shirt is a formal, fancy item of Elegant clothing that can be obtained from Easy Treasure Trails.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room, as part of the blue elegant clothes. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
