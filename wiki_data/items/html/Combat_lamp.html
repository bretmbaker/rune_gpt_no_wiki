A combat lamp is a quest reward for completing the Contact! quest. It grants two experience boosts in combat skills of the player's choice. If the lamp is lost or destroyed before experience is gained from it, it can be reclaimed from the Sophanem High Priest.

This lamp can be banked.

It grants two wishes of 7,000 experience each to any of the following skills:

  • Attack
  • Strength
  • Defence
  • Hitpoints
  • Ranged
  • Magic

After the second wish, The lamp dissipates into smoke that drifts away. appears in the dialogue box. Once it has been used up, it cannot be reclaimed.

***

Making any wish with the lamp yields unique text in the chat-box:

Wishing for Attack XP yields the following message in the chatbox:

You wish for strengthened Attack - your wish comes true as you gain 7000 XP.

Wishing for Strength XP yields the following message in the chatbox:

You wish for strengthened Strength - your muscles bulge as you gain 7000 XP.

Wishing for Defence XP yields the following message in the chatbox:

You wish for strengthened Defence - your wish comes true as you gain 7000 XP.

Wishing for Magic XP yields the following message in the chatbox:

You wish for strengthened Magic skill - your mind feels expansive as you gain 7000 XP.

Wishing for Ranged XP yields the following message in the chatbox:

You wish for strengthened Ranged skill - your eye becomes keener as you gain 7000 XP.

Wishing for Hitpoints XP yields the following message in the chatbox:

You wish for strengthened Hitpoints - you feel tougher as you gain 7000 XP.
