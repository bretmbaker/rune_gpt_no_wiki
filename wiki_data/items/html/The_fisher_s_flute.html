The fisher's flute is a quest item reward in The Queen of Thieves.

It is used on Kharedst's memoirs to allow teleportation to Port Piscarilius. If lost before being added to Kharedst's memoirs, a replacement can be claimed from Lady Shauna Piscarilius in The Warrens.

Inspecting The fisher's flute
  • Player: This looks like one of the pages to Kharedst's Memoirs. Maybe I should try adding it to the book.
  • (End of dialogue)
Using The fisher's flute on Kharedst's memoirs
  • You add an entry to Kharedst's Memoirs.
  • (End of dialogue)
