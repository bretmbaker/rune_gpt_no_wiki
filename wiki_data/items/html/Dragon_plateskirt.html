The dragon plateskirt is a rare piece of armour that requires a Defence level of 60 to be worn. It has the same stats as the dragon platelegs.

Dragon plateskirts can be combined with a dragon legs/skirt ornament kit to make a gold-trimmed dragon plateskirt (g). This cosmetic enhancement won't change the stats in any way.

As with most dragon armour, players cannot make this item using the Smithing skill.
