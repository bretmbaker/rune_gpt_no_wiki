Mysterious pages are pages of the strange hallowed tome that can be obtained from looting any coffin within the Hallowed Sepulchre. There are five pages in total, one for each floor, with a 10% chance of being obtained provided that the player hasn't already obtained one.[1] The collection log tracks which pages have been obtained.

The pages can be added into the strange hallowed tome, which is obtained by investigating the skeleton in the south-west corner of the minigame lobby.

If lost, the tome can be re-obtained from the skeleton with all previously added pages still present.
