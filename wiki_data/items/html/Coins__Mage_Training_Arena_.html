Coins in the Mage Training Arena are produced by using alchemy spells (High Level Alchemy or Low Level Alchemy) in the alchemist's section of the minigame. For every set of 100 coins deposited into the Coin Collector, the player will have 10 coins deposited into their bank (or inventory if an Ultimate Ironman).
