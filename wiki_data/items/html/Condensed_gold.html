Condensed gold is an item used in Construction, in which ten are required to build a gold sink for a player-owned house kitchen. It can be bought from the Stonemason in south-western Keldagrim or Metla in Stonecutter Outpost for 10,400,000 coins (Note: the price does increase when buying multiple at once). It cannot be made by players.
