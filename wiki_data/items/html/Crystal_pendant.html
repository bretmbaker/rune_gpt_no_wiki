Crystal pendant is an item used in Regicide. It is given to players by Lord Iorwerth so they can prove they're working for him. The crystal pendant has no uses after finishing the quest, aside from being aesthetically unique.

Warning: Pendants in the player's bank and equipped to their character will be removed when speaking to Lord Iorwerth and King Lathas during Regicide. The pendant is silently taken from the player when talking to Lord Iorwerth to receive the Big book of bangs, as well as any future dialogue with Lord Iorwerth during the quest, and finally when speaking with King Lathas to complete the quest. As such, using the drop trick is necessary when talking to them for players who want to keep this pendant after the quest.
