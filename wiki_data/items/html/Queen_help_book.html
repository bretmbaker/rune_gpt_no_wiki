The Queen help book or Queen help is obtained during the Barbarian Assault minigame after The Final Battle tutorial with Captain Cain.

If players destroy the book, it can be reclaimed from Captain Cain after viewing the same tutorial. It is also available in a bookcase of a player-owned house.

Transcript[edit | edit source]

The following text is transcluded from Transcript:Queen help book.

1) Collector: Pick up yellow egg. Pass to healer. Take omega egg from Defender. Load into turret.

2) Healer: Take yellow egg from Collector. Poison egg in pool. Pass to Attacker.

3) Attacker: Take poisoned, yellow egg from healer. Add spikes from mushroom. Pass to Defender.

4) Defender: Take poisoned, spiked, yellow egg from Attacker. Dunk in lava. Pass to Collector.
