The runescroll of bloodbark is a scroll obtained from chests within the Shade Catacombs. Higher tier chests give a higher chance of obtaining the scroll, as well as wearing a ring of wealth. Reading the scroll unlocks the ability to craft bloodbark armour from splitbark armour.

The following text is transcluded from Transcript:Runescroll of bloodbark.
Read for the first time
  • Reading through the scroll reveals the secret to infusing Splitbark armour with Blood runes at a Blood Altar to create Bloodbark armour!
  • As you close the scroll it starts to disintegrate in your hands.
  • (End of dialogue)
Read after the first time
  • You've already gained the knowledge to infuse Splitbark armour with Blood runes.
  • (End of dialogue)
