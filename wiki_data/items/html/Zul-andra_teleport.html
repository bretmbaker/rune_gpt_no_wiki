Zul-andra teleport scrolls are consumable teleport scrolls that teleport the player to Zul-Andra. They can be obtained from defeating Zulrah.

This scroll is only usable after completing the Regicide quest. Attempting to use the scroll without meeting the requirements displays the game message: You need to complete the Regicide quest before you can teleport to Zul-Andra.

Price history[edit | edit source]

On release, Zul-andra teleports were dropped in quantities of 50 per drop which was shortly reduced to 20. This caused an oversupply of teleport scrolls, and the drop rate was silently reduced to one scroll per drop on 15 October 2015, limiting the quantity of scrolls that enter the game economy. As a result the teleport scroll rapidly increased in price. This caused controversy within the Old School RuneScape community, as players who had hoarded teleport scrolls prior to the update were able to profit greatly due to the higher prices. On the day of the update, 9,092,122 scrolls were traded on the Grand Exchange.

Shortly after the 25 May 2017 update that decreased the average value of loots from Zulrah by 30%, on 15 June 2017 the drop quantity was again increased from 1 to 4.
