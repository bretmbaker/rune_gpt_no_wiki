Void Knight gloves are a piece of Void Knight equipment. They can be bought from a Void Knight for 150 commendations, although a player must have at least 42 Attack, Strength, Defence, Hitpoints, Ranged, and Magic, along with 22 Prayer to buy and wear them.

If the player dies below level 20 Wilderness, it will remain in the player's inventory. However, the item will be unusable and must be repaired with 120,000 coins by using the item on Perdu.
