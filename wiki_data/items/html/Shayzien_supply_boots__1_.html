Shayzien supply boots (1) were unequippable tier 1 Shayzien supply armour that required 30% Lovakengj favour, 47 Smithing, and 1 lovakite bar to smith, yielding 10 Smithing experience.

When delivered to armourer (tier 1), 0.1% Lovakengj favour, 45 Mining experience, and 80 Smithing experience were rewarded. However, it was more efficient to deliver a Shayzien supply set (1), rewarding 1.5% Lovakengj favour, 675 Mining experience (691.9 wearing full Prospector kit), and 1200 Smithing experience.
