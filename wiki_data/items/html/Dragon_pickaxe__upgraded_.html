The dragon pickaxe (upgraded) is a dragon pickaxe with a dragon pickaxe upgrade kit attached to it. The kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the pickaxe. It costs 1,473,654 coins for both the parts to make the cosmetic dragon pickaxe.

While the pickaxe is untradeable in its ornamented state, it can be reverted anytime, returning the dragon pickaxe. However, the kit will not be returned.

If a player dies with the pickaxe and it is not one of the player's protected items, the pickaxe will revert to its default appearance, and players must use another dragon pickaxe upgrade kit to enhance its appearance again.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The model for this item was originally polled as a graphical update for the dragon pickaxe in Content Poll #22. It gained 72.2% yes votes, 2.8% short of the passing threshold required. The model was then polled as a cosmetic upgrade kit in the Old School Bounty Hunter Rewards 2 poll, and passed by 79.7%.
