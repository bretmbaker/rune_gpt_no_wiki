A Golden feather is a quest item used only in Priest in Peril. It is obtained by using a feather on the east Monument in the Paterdomus mausoleum.

It serves no purpose in the quest, and has no uses outside the quest, as it cannot be used in Fishing or Fletching. However, players can cast Low Alchemy or High alchemy on it.

It cannot be obtained once Drezel has moved to the dungeon after the player has exhausted all dialogue with him after using the blessed water on the coffin.
