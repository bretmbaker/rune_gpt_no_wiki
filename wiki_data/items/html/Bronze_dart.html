Bronze darts are Ranged throwing weapons made from bronze that are only available to members. Bronze darts only require level 1 Ranged to wield. They can be obtained by either using the Smithing and Fletching skills or trading with another player. Bronze darts can be poisoned and can be wielded with a shield or god book for protection.

To create bronze darts the player must have completed the Tourist Trap quest and have at least level 4 Smithing and level 10 Fletching. Players can smith bronze bars into bronze dart tips. Each bronze bar creates 10 bronze dart tips and gives the player 12.5 Smithing experience. Feathers then must be added to create the bronze darts. 1.8 Fletching experience is rewarded per bronze dart.


Darts are among the fastest weapons in the game (along with throwing knives), and are commonly used for player-killing, training Ranged, or tagging enemies for barraging.
