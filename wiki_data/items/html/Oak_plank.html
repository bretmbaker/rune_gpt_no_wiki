Oak planks are a type of plank in the Construction skill. Each plank gives 60 Construction experience when used.

A single oak plank is worth on average of ~175 experience when used in novice Mahogany Homes contracts (including completion bonus), and 150 experience when used to fix the broken bridges in the Hallowed Sepulchre, also requiring mithril nails.

Oak planks are most commonly obtained from hunting Eclectic implings, fighting Vet'ion/Calvar'ion, and as a common reward from elite clue scrolls.
