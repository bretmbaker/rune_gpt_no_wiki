The magic dolmen is an item obtained from placing the appropriate herblore ingredients on the statue of magic within the Ancient Guthixian Temple. It is used on the stone table in the centre of the temple in order to open the huge door leading to the Stone of Jas.

As there are 13 possible statues, of which 8 are randomly chosen by the game for the player to obtain dolmens from, they may not obtain this item at all.
