The zombie helmet is a crush-focused piece of headgear that requires 50 Strength and Defence to equip. It is created by taking a broken zombie helmet, which can be obtained as a drop from armoured zombies in Zemouregal's Fort, to an anvil and repairing it. Doing so requires level 70 Smithing.

The zombie helmet has better stats overall compared to the dwarven helmet, alongside having a +8 crush attack bonus; this makes it the second highest for crush attack bonus after the inquisitor's great helm out of the entire game.
