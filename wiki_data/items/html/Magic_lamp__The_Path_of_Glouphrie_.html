Magic lamps are an item obtained as a reward after completing The Path of Glouphrie. Four are given to the player, and each one grants differing amounts of experience:

  • 30,000 Strength experience
  • 20,000 Slayer experience
  • 5,000 Thieving experience
  • 5,000 Magic experience

The lamp cannot be banked. However, if the player prefers to use it at a later date, it can be destroyed and reclaimed from Hazelmere whenever the player wishes to do so. Note that if players choose not to claim it before Hazelmere's appearance during While Guthix Sleeps, they will need to reclaim them from King Narnode Shareen instead.
