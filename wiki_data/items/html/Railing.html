A railing is a quest item used only in Dwarf Cannon. Six railings are obtained from Captain Lawgof.

It is used to repair the six bent scenery railings surrounding the Coal Trucks with a hammer. There is a chance to fail when repairing sections of the fence, and the player may receive small amounts of damage which will deal 1-2 Hitpoints. The player will also receive a message saying either You cut your hand on the rusty old railing., You strain your back trying to handle the railings., or You accidentally crush your hand in the railing.

Repairing success rate[edit | edit source]
