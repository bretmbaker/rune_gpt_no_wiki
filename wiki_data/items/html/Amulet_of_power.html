An amulet of power offers a good balance of attack and defence bonuses for all classes of combat. It is created by casting Lvl-4 Enchant on a diamond amulet, and has no requirements to be worn.

This item is a less expensive option for players who do not wish to buy an amulet of glory. It lacks the glory's teleportation capabilities and has weaker attack and Prayer bonuses, but has the same strength bonus as well as higher defence bonuses.

This amulet is currently the best overall amulet for F2P players, although the amulet of strength is better in some cases, such as fighting low defence targets with melee.

A helpful way for P2P Ironmen to obtain one is by looting the H.A.M. Store rooms for a diamond amulet, and then enchanting it with 57 Magic.
