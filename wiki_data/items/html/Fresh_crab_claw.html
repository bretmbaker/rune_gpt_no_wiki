A fresh crab claw can be crafted into a crab claw with a chisel and 15 Crafting, yielding 32.5 Crafting experience. However, there is a chance of failure so that a broken crab claw is crafted instead, yielding no experience. The chance of successfully crafting a crab claw scales from 15% at level 1 Crafting to 94% at level 99 Crafting.[1]

The Freeing Pirate Pete subquest of Recipe for Disaster must be partially completed to be able to access the Mogre Camp where the crabs that drop the fresh crab claw are located.
