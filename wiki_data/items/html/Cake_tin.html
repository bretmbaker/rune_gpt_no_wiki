A cake tin is an item used to bake cakes. Players need to add an egg, a pot of flour and a bucket of milk on to the cake tin and used in an oven.

They can be found in the Cooks' Guild, bought from any general store (except for the ones in Port Phasmatys, Ardougne, Port Khazard, Canifis, Keldagrim, and Burthorpe), bought from the Culinaromancer's Chest in Lumbridge, or taken from the wooden shelves from a player-owned house. The cake tin also respawns at the house with an anvil in the level 21 Wilderness.

Once baked, the cake tin will separate from the cooked cake, unlike the pie dish, where it comes with the pie.

One cake tin is needed for the quest Troll Romance to make wax.
