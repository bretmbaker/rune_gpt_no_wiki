The mystic hat (dusk) is a part of the dusk Mystic robes set worn in the head slot. Mystic hats come in many colours but all share the same bonuses, and all mystic items require 40 Magic and 20 Defence to wear. It can be stored in a magic wardrobe in a player-owned house.
