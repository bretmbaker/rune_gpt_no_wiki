The ghostly robe is part of the Ghostly Robes set obtained from the Curse of the Empty Lord miniquest. It is a piece of clothing that belonged to Valdez, the ghost found near Glarial's Tomb, which players can obtain after starting the miniquest.

There are no requirements to wear and, when worn with the other pieces, the player appears 'ghostlike' (i.e. transparent). After initially obtaining the piece, the player can talk to any of the ghosts to get more pieces. A lost robe can be purchased from Perdu for 45,000 coins.

This item can be stored in the magic wardrobe of a costume room, as part of the ghostly robes set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
