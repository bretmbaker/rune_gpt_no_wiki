Ranger boots are a rare reward received from the completion of medium Treasure Trails. It is part of the ranger kit, and requires 40 Ranged to wear; it can also be stored in a treasure chest. Ranger boots are the second best Ranged footwear in terms of ranged attack bonus.

The drop rate of ranger boots is 1/1,133 per slot.[1] With each medium clue reward, a player will receive 3-5 slots of items; this makes the effective drop rate ~1/283.6 per casket. The ranger boots are the most valuable drop from medium clues.

With level 60 in Runecraft and Magic, ranger boots can be combined with a pegasian crystal to create a pair of pegasian boots, which has increased stats, and remains tradeable. The process cannot be reversed.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room.
