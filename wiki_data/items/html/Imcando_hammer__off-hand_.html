Imcando hammer (off-hand) is a variant of the Imcando hammer that can be equipped in the shield slot. The hammer functions identically to the normal variant. The off-hand variant is particularly useful at Motherlode Mine as it allows the player to repair broken struts while still having a pickaxe equipped, and without taking up any inventory space.

The hammer can be swapped back into its normal variant by using the "Swap" option.

Although the main hand variant can be handed back to Ramarno for Barronite shards, attempting to return the off-hand hammer will not work. This item can be stored in the magic wardrobe of a costume room.
