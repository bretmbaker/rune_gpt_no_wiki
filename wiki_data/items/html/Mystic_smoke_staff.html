The mystic smoke staff is an elemental staff requiring 40 Attack and 40 Magic to wield. In addition to increased Melee and Magic Attack bonuses, the staff provides unlimited amounts of air and fire runes as well as the autocast option when equipped.

Like the smoke battlestaff, it provides a 10% increase in accuracy and damage when casting spells from the standard spellbook; this bonus also applies to curse spells.[1] The bonus also works in F2P.

This staff can be created after the completion of the quest Scorpion Catcher by taking a smoke battlestaff and 40,000 coins to Thormac. After completion of the Hard Kandarin Diary and Elite Kandarin Diary, Thormac will only require 30,000 coins or 20,000 coins respectively.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes

The Magic Attack and Defence bonuses of mystic staves have been increased from +10 to +14.

15 October 2015
(update)

The examine text for the Smoke battlestaves has been updated.

8 October 2015
(update | poll)

The smoke battlestaff and mystic smoke staff now provide a 10% increase in accuracy and damage when used to cast spells from the normal spellbook.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Due to the strength of fire spells and the popularity of splashing, the mystic smoke staff became the most expensive mystic staff almost immediately after it was introduced to the game. On 10 September 2015, splashing was nerfed, and the price of the mystic smoke staff plummeted to less than 25% of its peak. To compensate for this nerf and to make the staff still worth using, it was given its 10% bonus to magical accuracy and strength in a buff on 8 October 2015.
  • There has yet to be a graphical update properly showing the smaller orbs of the staff moving whilst equipped, instead of only when dropped.
