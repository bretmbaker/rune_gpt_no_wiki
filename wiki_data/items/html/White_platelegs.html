The white platelegs are a piece of White Knight armour that requires 10 Defence and the completion of the Wanted! quest to wield. They have the same bonuses as the black platelegs, with an added +1 prayer bonus, and have the same stats as a white plateskirt but weigh more.

Players can purchase them from Sir Vyvin after achieving the rank of Novice (100 black knight kills). They can also be bought on the Grand Exchange or obtained through trading other players.

As with all white armour, they cannot be made using the Smithing skill.
