A cup of tea is an item made during the Ghosts Ahoy quest. Players pour nettle tea into a porcelain cup given to them by the Old Crone. She refuses to drink the tea until the player adds milk. The player uses a bucket of milk on this item which only changes the colour of the liquid and the examine text. Once given to the Old Crone, she is able to recall some aged memories for the player.
