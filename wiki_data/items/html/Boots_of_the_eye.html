The boots of the eye is a part of the Raiments of the Eye set, purchased from the Guardians of the Rift reward shop for 250 abyssal pearls. It grants 10% more runes when Runecrafting, and is increased to 60% when the full set is worn.

Unlike other pieces of the outfit, the boots cannot be recoloured. Attempting to do so anyway results in a chatbox message: You try to apply the dye to your boots but for some reason they seem to repel the liquid. You manage to return the dye to the bottle. Perhaps it would be better used on something else?

This item can be stored in the magic wardrobe of a costume room, as part of the Raiments of the Eye. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
