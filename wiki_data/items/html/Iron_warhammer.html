The iron warhammer is a warhammer stronger than the bronze warhammer. It can be wielded at level 1 Strength. Players can make an iron warhammer with the Smithing skill at level 24 using 3 iron bars, giving 75 Smithing experience.

One spawns in the ruined castle near the bandits just south-west of the Hosidius Mess.

The iron warhammer can be used as a substitute for a regular hammer when banging the big door at the entrance of Bandos' Stronghold in the God Wars Dungeon.

(update)

The weapon's position on female players has been adjusted so that they appear to be equipped properly.

21 April 2021
(update)
  • Iron warhammers now require level 1 Strength to wield instead of level 1 Attack.
  • The iron warhammer's strength bonus was increased from 9 to 11.

(update)

The item became permanently available with the launch of RuneScape 2.

(update)

The item was added to the RuneScape 2 Beta.
