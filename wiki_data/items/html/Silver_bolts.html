Silver bolts are ranged ammunition that can be fired from iron crossbows or better. They have a Ranged Strength bonus higher than blurite bolts but lower than iron bolts.

To produce silver bolts, players with at least 21 Crafting must use a silver bar on a furnace with a bolt mould in their inventory. This will return 10 unfeathered silver bolts and 50 Crafting experience. Attaching feathers to the bolts with 43 Fletching will complete them and grant the player 25 Fletching experience.

They are a type of silver weaponry and are one of the few ammo types able to be used to effectively kill Vampyre Juvinates.
