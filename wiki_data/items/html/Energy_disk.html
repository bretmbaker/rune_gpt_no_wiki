The energy disks are quest items found during The Forsaken Tower quest. They are used to solve the energy pylons puzzle on the 2nd floor[UK]3rd floor[US] which unlocks the Dinh's hammer display case.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • If the player manages to drop one of the disks and attempts to pick it up outside of the puzzle room, the item will be deleted, and the player will receive a message via chat, You have no use for that.
    • The disks cannot be picked up using Telekinetic Grab.
