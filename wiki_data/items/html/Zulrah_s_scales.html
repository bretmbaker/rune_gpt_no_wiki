Zulrah's scales are items used to charge serpentine helms, toxic blowpipes, toxic staves of the dead, and tridents of the swamp. It can also be used to create anti-venom at 87 Herblore by using them on an Antidote++, with 5 scales used per dose.

They are primarily obtained as a drop from Zulrah. They can also be obtained by dissecting sacred eels with a knife, which requires level 72 in Cooking and level 87 in Fishing. A player's Cooking level affects how many scales are received when they are dissected.

In addition, players can receive 20,000 scales from dismantling the following items:

  • Tanzanite fang
  • Toxic blowpipe (empty)
  • Serpentine visage
  • Serpentine helm (uncharged)
  • Magic fang

On average, each Zulrah kill will provide 220 scales, or 337 if including scales gained by dismantling the aforementioned drops.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Zulrah's scales have been the featured item on the Grand Exchange's official website since its release, having been manually chosen.[1] They were briefly replaced by the Santa hat on December 17th, 2018, but reverted shortly afterwards.
  • They are the second most traded Old School-exclusive item on the Grand Exchange, after Ancient essence.
