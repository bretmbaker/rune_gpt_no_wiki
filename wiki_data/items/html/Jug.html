A Jug is an item that can be filled with water to create a jug of water. Wine can also be made by adding grapes to a jug of water.

Jugs can be bought at general stores for one coin each. Jugs are not sold at the Catherby, East Ardougne, or West Ardougne general store. However, jugs are sold by the charter crew members in Catherby. Wine Shop in Draynor Village sells them for the same price as the general stores, and they can be thieved from the stall. Hassan at the Al Kharid palace may also give free jugs of water if the player has not completed Prince Ali Rescue.

Jugs can also be found at their spawn points in the basement of Lumbridge Castle, the Crafting Guild, the Cooks' Guild (top floor), and some other kitchens.

Jugs can only be filled with water from sinks, fountains and with the Lunar spell Humidify. They cannot be filled with water from wells; upon attempting to do so, a player receives the message "If I drop my jug down there, I don't think I'm likely to get it back." Also, jugs can be filled by filling another vessel with water and then using it on the jug (e.g. filling a bucket from a well, then using the bucket of water on the jug).
