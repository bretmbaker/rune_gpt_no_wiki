A moonlight potion is a potion created by adding moonlight grub paste to a vial of water. A dose of the moonlight potion provides a temporary skill boost to Attack, Strength, Defence, as well as restoring prayer points; the values of the boost vary depending on the player's Herblore level.

The potion can have 2-4 doses on creation, with chances of each varying depending on the player's Herblore level.

The potion cannot be taken out of Neypotzli. Doing so will revert the potion into a vial of water and the player will receive following message: The resources from Neypotzli fizzle to dust as they are severed from its magic.

At level 99 Herblore, 99 Attack, 99 Defence, 99 Strength, 99 Prayer, you will get the following boosts. In addition, the defence boost will reset itself at the end of its duration like a divine potion.

Prayer points 36

Defence boost 26
Strength boost 19

Formulas[edit | edit source]

The moonlight potion calculates its boosts based on the player's base Prayer, Attack, Defence, and Strength levels, as well as the player's current Herblore level when the potion is drunk.

For prayer points, the game calculates two formulas, one based on the player's Prayer level: ⌊PrayerLevel×0.25⌋+7, and another based on the player's Herblore level: ⌊HerbloreLevel×0.3⌋+7 and takes the greater result as the amount of prayer points to restore. If the player's current Herblore level is less than 38 when the potion is drunk, it will not restore any prayer points.

Prayer points formula (whichever is higher)
⌊PrayerLevel×14⌋+7 ⌊HerbloreLevel×310⌋+7

For the Attack boost, if the player has less than 45 Herblore the moonlight potion behaves like an attack potion, and if the player has 45 Herblore or greater it behaves like a super attack potion.

Herblore level Attack boost formula
3-44 ⌊AttackLevel×110⌋+3
45+ ⌊AttackLevel×15100⌋+5

For the Strength boost, if the player has less than 55 Herblore the moonlight potion behaves like a strength potion, and if the player has 55 Herblore or greater it behaves like a super strength potion.

Herblore level Strength boost formula
12-54 ⌊StrengthLevel×110⌋+3
55+ ⌊StrengthLevel×15100⌋+5

For the Defence boost, if the player has less than 66 Herblore the moonlight potion behaves like a defence potion, and if the player has between 66 and 69 Herblore it behaves like a super defence potion. If the player has 70 or higher Herblore, the defence boost is even stronger than a super defence potion, and will reset at the end of its duration like a divine potion.

Herblore level Defence boost formula
30-65 ⌊DefenceLevel×110⌋+3
66-69 ⌊DefenceLevel×15100⌋+5
70+ ⌊DefenceLevel×210⌋+7
