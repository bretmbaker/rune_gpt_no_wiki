The clothes pouch blueprint is an item purchased from the Forestry Shop for 10,000 anima-infused bark, as well as 300 noted willow and maple logs. These logs can be bought on the Grand Exchange for 30,000.

With level 50 Woodcutting and Crafting, players can craft a clothes pouch by using a piece of leather on the blueprint whilst having thread in the inventory.
