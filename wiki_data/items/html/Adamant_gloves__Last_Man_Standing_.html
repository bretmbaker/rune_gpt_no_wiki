Adamant gloves are an unobtainable item added with the Last Man Standing beta. Presumably, they were intended to be obtained from loot crates like Mithril gloves and Rune gloves. However, it was never actually added to the minigame, presumably as it was believed the item would be redundant given the availability of two other pairs of gloves.
