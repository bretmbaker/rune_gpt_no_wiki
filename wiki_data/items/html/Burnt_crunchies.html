Burnt crunchies is the result of accidentally burning raw crunchies while attempting to make a half baked crunchy. The burn rate while cooking these will decrease as players reach higher Cooking levels, stopping entirely at level 38.

The burnt crunchies has no use whatsoever, although it can be emptied to reclaim a crunchy tray. Like most other burnt food, it is tradeable to other players but not on the Grand Exchange.
