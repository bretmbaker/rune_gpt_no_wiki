Mushroom pie is a type of pie that restores 16 Hitpoints in total, 2 bites each healing for 8. It also temporarily boosts Crafting by 4, the second highest boost available, and the highest reliable boost. A pie dish will remain after both bites are consumed. It can be obtained by baking a uncooked mushroom pie on a cooking range, requiring level 60 Cooking and granting 200 experience when successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting in burnt pie. The burn rate decreases as the player's Cooking level increases. Mushroom pie is one of a number of foods with a reduced burn rate while using the Lumbridge Castle range with Cook's Assistant completed. It will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 89, or 60 when using the Lumbridge range. Alternatively, casting the Bake Pie spell from the lunar spellbook will always create a pie without burning it.
