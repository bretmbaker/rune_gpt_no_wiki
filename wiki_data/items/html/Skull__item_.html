A Skull is an item that can be found in various places, and is used as a material for Construction in player-owned houses. The player can use them to make a skeleton throne (level 88), skull torches (level 94), and hanging skeletons (level 94).

Skulls are involved in Mourning's End Part II, where Thorgel's item list includes a skull.
