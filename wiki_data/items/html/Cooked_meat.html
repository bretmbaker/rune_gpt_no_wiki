Cooked meat is a type of food that restores 3 Hitpoints when eaten. It can be obtained by cooking various raw meats on a fire or cooking range, granting 30 experience when successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting in burnt meat. The burn rate decreases as the player's Cooking level increases. Cooked meat is one of a number of foods with a reduced burn rate while using the Lumbridge Castle range with Cook's Assistant completed. It will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 34, or 31 when using the Lumbridge range.

Cooked meat is used in the making of stews, pies, and steak sandwiches, or as a topping on pizzas.

Unlike most food, players can deliberately burn cooked meat by using it on a fire or cooking range. One burnt meat is required for Witch's Potion.
