Nature offerings are a stackable item used in Woodcutting. When in the inventory or stored in a forestry kit, there is a 50% to 60% chance of receiving an additional log after receiving logs from chopping trees, this does not work when chopping Sulliusceps; offerings are consumed regardless of whether the player receives an additional log. Chopping with other players on the same tree will increase the average by 2% with one other chopper, up to 20% more with the maximum +10 Woodcutting player bonus. No additional experience is awarded when receiving an additional log. However, the player must have at least two open inventory slots to receive the extra log; this extra log will not drop on the ground.

Offerings can be created by purchasing ritual mulch from the Forestry Shop and combining it with one of the following herbs: avantoe, kwuarm, snapdragon, cadantine, lantadyme, dwarf weed, or torstol. However, both clean and grimy huasca do not work with ritual mulch. Creating nature offerings requires levels 68 Woodcutting and 50 Farming, with each set producing 40 nature offerings.

Bug:
Prices shown for this item are highly inaccurate due to faulty logic in Jagex's Grand Exchange database. See the wiki's real-time prices for more accurate information.
