Mangled bones are obtained in the Ancient Cavern, either from certain monsters or rummaging the skeletons found on the ground. If rummaging a skeleton, at least 2 inventory slots must be free because of the random chance of finding an ancient page; otherwise, the bones will "disintegrate". The chance of obtaining a mangled bone from a skeleton is 11/202.[1]

Unlike other types of bones, mangled bones cannot be buried, and as such a bonecrusher will not activate when receiving them.

Mangled bones do NOT work for completing the Firemaking section of the Barbarian Training miniquest; only chewed bones work.

With any type of logs, an axe, and a tinderbox, players can use it on the burnt space (pyre site) to make a pyre ship. When burned, this will release a level 166 ferocious barbarian spirit, which must be killed to receive a reward. However, players cannot receive a dragon full helm, as it is only obtainable from chewed bone pyre ships or as a drop from mithril dragons.
