Castle Wars tickets are tickets that are awarded in the Castle Wars minigame to players who spend at least 15 minutes in the game. The amount of tickets earned are dependent on whether the player's team wins, loses or ties, as well as whether the opposing team has scored, and also on whether the player is in a dedicated Castle Wars world.

Dedicated Castle Wars worlds:

  • 2 tickets are earned if the player's team loses.
  • 2 tickets are earned if scores are tied at 0-0.
  • 3 tickets are earned if scores are tied at any number other than 0.
  • 3 tickets are earned for winning if the opposing team has scored.
  • 4 tickets are earned for winning if the opposing team had 0 points.

Non-Castle Wars worlds:

  • 1 ticket is earned if the player's team loses.
  • 1 ticket is earned if scores are tied at 0-0.
  • 2 tickets are earned if scores are tied at any number other than 0.
  • 2 tickets are earned for winning if the opposing team has scored.
  • 3 tickets are earned for winning if the opposing team had 0 points.

Tickets can be spent on items in the Castle Wars Ticket Exchange shop.

Items can be sold back for a full ticket refund.
