Brown toy horseys are items made as a joke by Jagex, poking fun at the requests made by players to have horses. Toy horseys are obtainable from Diango for 150 coins, and come in 4 different colours: white, black, grey, and brown. They can also be made from a plank on any crafting table in a player-owned house, which requires level 10 or higher Crafting. When you play with the horsey, an animation showing your character playing with it appears, and text will appear above your head. Said text will be Just say neigh to gambling!A Brown toy Horsey can also be assigned as a random item from Rowdy Dwarf in the Forgettable Tale... quest

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
18 December 2014
(update)

Fixed a typo on a toy horse.

10 December 2014
(update)

The item was renamed from "Toy horsey" to "Brown toy horsey".

18 March 2013
(update)

As part of a policy change on unofficial gambling games, using a horsey will always cause the player to say "Just say neigh to gambling!".

Previously, on using a horsey a player would randomly say one of the following phrases: "Come on Dobbin, we can win the race!", "Hi-ho Silver, and away" or "Neaahhhyyy! Giddy-up horsey!"
