The Fremennik sea boots 1 are a piece of footwear obtained by completing the easy Fremennik Diary. The boots are given out by Thorodin, and can be reclaimed from him if lost. Its stats are identical to that of Iron boots, however the Fremennik sea boots 1 weigh less.
