The dragon thrownaxe, like all throwing axes, is a members-only Ranged throwing weapon and is the most powerful type of throwing axe. It is obtained as a drop from wyrms, drakes, hydras and the Alchemical Hydra.

To wield a dragon thrownaxe, the player must have at least 61 Ranged.

Special attack[edit | edit source]

The dragon thrownaxe has a special attack, Momentum Throw, which consumes 25% of the player's special attack energy. It improves the player's accuracy by 25% along with guaranteeing that the player will attack on the next game tick.

The special attack could be used to attack several times in quick succession with improved accuracy. However, unlike the granite maul's special attack, it would not be possible to use all of these special attack hits at once in the same game tick. Only one can be launched per game cycle.

Note that in addition to itself attacking on the next game tick, using the special attack counts as a normal attack, resetting the player's attack delay to 5 ticks, or 4 using rapid. Even factoring in the additional tick to throw the thrownaxe, this can result in an increased attack speed for slower weapons such as the heavy ballista or dark bow, and even melee weapons such as a crystal halberd.

Dragon thrownaxes are instantly consumed when they are thrown using the special attack if not used with an Ava's device.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes

(update)

The item's value was decreased from 300 to 3.

10 January 2019
(update | poll)

Following the release of Kebos Lowlands, dragon thrownaxes were moved from the Chambers of Xeric drop table to wyrms, drakes, hydras and Alchemical Hydra.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The dragon thrownaxe model is similar to the battleaxe held by the Red Axe dwarf, Colonel Grimsson in the quest Forgettable Tale of a Drunken Dwarf.
