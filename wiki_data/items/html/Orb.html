An orb is an item used in the Enchanting Chamber of the Mage Training Arena. They can be made by casting an enchant jewellery spell on icosahedrons, cylinders, cubes, pentamids, and dragonstones found within the chamber. If the player leaves the Enchanting Chamber, all orbs will disappear from their inventory.

Orbs can be deposited in the hole in the centre of the chamber. Every 20 orbs deposited will reward the player with three blood, death, or cosmic runes.
