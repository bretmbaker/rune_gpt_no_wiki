Bandit's brew is a type of ale that restores 1 Hitpoint and temporarily boosts Thieving and Attack by 1, while draining Strength and Defence by ⌊Level×6100⌋+3 (see table below)

It can be purchased at The Big Heist Lodge in the Kharidian Desert Bandit Camp for 650 coins. Unlike other ales, it cannot be made through brewing.

One bandit's brew is required for the quest Desert Treasure I.
