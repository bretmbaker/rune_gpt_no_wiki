The drunk parrot is a quest item used in the Eadgar's Ruse quest. It is obtained by giving alco-chunks to a parrot in Ardougne Zoo.

The player must hide the parrot underneath the rack near the prison cells in the Troll Stronghold. By doing this, the parrot listens to the sounds of humans screaming from the cells, and copies them. This makes the parrot sound like a human to the trolls.

The player must then give the drunk parrot to Eadgar in Trollheim, along with a dirty robe, 10 grain, a log, and 5 raw chicken. He will then turn them into a fake man that the player can give to Burntmeat, a troll cook, in order to obtain some information about goutweed.

Dropping the drunk parrot prints the message You release the parrot, and it flies away.. It can be reobtained by using more alco-chunks on the parrots in the zoo, or from Eadgar if the parrot has already learned to speak like a human.
