Gnome goggles are a possible reward from the Gnome Restaurant minigame. Players have a 1/17 chance to obtain them as a reward from Captain Daerkin or Captain Ninto, which are accessible with hard deliveries.[1] The gnome goggles are part of the gnome pilot set which also includes the gnome scarf.

Goggles can be combined with the Bomber cap from the Enlightened Journey quest by talking with Auguste, resulting in a Cap and goggles. As the cap and goggles can be stored, though not the goggles themselves, this provides a means of storing the goggles in the armour case (as part of the bomber jacket costume).
