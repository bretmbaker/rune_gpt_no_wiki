The sack of grain is used during Sir Spishyus's trial in Recruitment Drive. It is found on the east side of the precarious bridge.

The grain cannot be left with the chicken alone on one side of the bridge, or else it will eat the entire sack. If this occurs, the player will fail and is returned to Falador.

It is automatically equipped when picked up and does not exist as an item in the player's inventory.
