A reward casket (beginner) is received after completing the final clue of a beginner clue scroll trail, and contains the final reward for the trail.

While players can only have one of each type of clue scroll in their possession, they can possess unlimited amounts of reward caskets, and are eligible to receive clue scrolls again once it is obtained.

The beginner reward casket, unlike other reward caskets, cannot be opened on Entrana. If players attempt to do so, the chatbox will state "You cannot open this on Entrana, the monks are watching you too closely." Furthermore, accounts that are less than 10 hours old will not be able to receive any unique rewards from these caskets.[1]

Rewards[edit | edit source]

Beginner clues generate a minimum of 1 reward and a maximum of 3, granting an average of 2 rewards per casket. The rewards consist of generic items, and items that are unique for beginner clues. On average it will take 597 beginner clues to obtain every unique, not counting the black pickaxe which you can get from easy clues at a higher rate.

All droprates are confirmed by Mod Tide.[2] The listed rarities are per roll.

The average loot roll is worth 3,969.00. On average, beginner clues are worth 7,939.00.
