The reagent pouch is a storage item purchased from the Mixology Rewards shop with 13,800 Mox resin, 11,200 Aga resin, and 15,100 Lye resin, which are earned from participating in the Mastering Mixology minigame.

The pouch, requiring level 81 Herblore to use, holds up to 26 of each Herblore secondary ingredient. When making herblore potions, the secondary ingredients will be used directly out of the bag, allowing for 26 potions to be made at a time instead of 14. However, it is not possible to use unfinished potions directly on the pouch, necessitating a single unit of the secondary in question to make potions. For example, should the player want to make prayer potions, they would need the pouch (full), 1 snape grass, and 26 unfinished ranarr potions in their inventory. The player would then use the snape grass on the ranarr potion with the pouch open to start making potions.

Players can also right-click to open the pouch, which will have secondaries that the player acquires to be automatically placed inside the pouch, provided there is space for it. Players can check the contents of the pouch by selecting the "Check" right-click option, which tells the number of secondaries the player has inside. Players may empty the pouch by right-clicking "Use" on the item within a bank or whilst the bank or deposit box interface is open.

Bug:
The pouch does not work at bank chests or other non-standard banks (e.g. Emerald Benedict) when making potions.
Bug:
Lily of the Sands does not work while in Reagent Pouch.

Methods[edit | edit source]

Here is an easy method for making 26 potions per inventory with minimal management:

Setup:

  1. Withdraw the reagent pouch and lock the inventory slot it occupies to prevent it from being deposited.
  2. Withdraw amount should be set to at least 27 or just use "All".
  3. Have the reagent pouch in your inventory filled with secondary ingredients, along with 1 secondary in your inventory.
    • Optional but recommended: While in bank, use the official client's minimenu reorder or RuneLite's menu entry swap plugin to change the left click of the reagent pouch to 'use'.

Cycle:

  1. Withdraw 26 unfinished potions.
  2. Make 26 potions.
  3. Deposit all (everything except the reagent pouch should go to the bank).
  4. While in bank, 'use' the reagent pouch to empty it into bank. (If using Prescription goggles)
  5. Take out 27 secondaries.
  6. While still in bank, 'use' the pouch (this should leave 1 secondary in the bottom-right slot of your inventory)
  7. Repeat the cycle.

Note: When creating a variety of potions that use different secondaries, multiple sets of 26 secondaries can be stored inside the reagent pouch, allowing for more than 26 potions to be made before the pouch needs to be emptied again.

Notable non-storeable items[edit | edit source]

  • Un-crushed versions of items, such as unicorn horns and superior dragon bones.
  • Stackable secondary ingredients, such as nihil dust and amylase crystals.
  • Some quest-only secondary ingredients, including blamish snail slime, pharmakos berries, and ground bat bones.
  • Herbs used as secondaries, such as those used in Guthix rest, and torstol used in divine super combat potions and anti-venom+.
  • Any flower, as used in imp repellent.
  • Snake weed, as used in Relicym's balm and Sanfew serum.
  • Cave nightshade, as used in weapon poison++.
  • Garlic, as used in Guthix balance.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
16 October 2024
(update)

Caviar and roe can now be added to a reagent pouch.

2 October 2024
(update)
  • The resin costs for the pouch has been adjusted:
    • Mox resin: 16,350 -> 13,800
    • Aga resin: 10,650 -> 11,200
    • Lye resin: 22,600 -> 15,100
  • The following items are now compatible with the reagent pouch:
    • Ashes
    • Gorak claw powder
    • Jangerberries
    • Kebbit teeth dust
    • Lily of the sands
    • Nail beast nails
    • Poison ivy berries
    • Silver dust
27 September 2024
 Hotfix

Players can now only withdraw supplies from the reagent pouch while at a bank.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Upon reaching level 81 Herblore, the level-up message incorrectly names the Reagant pouch as the "Secondary Pouch."
  • When chopping Sulliusceps, collected Mort myre fungi is not automatically placed inside an open pouch, and must be manually filled.
