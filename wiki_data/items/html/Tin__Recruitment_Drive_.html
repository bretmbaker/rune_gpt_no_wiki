A Tin is a quest item used only in Recruitment Drive. It is found by searching a crate during Miss Cheevers's trial.

It is used as a mould to create the bronze key needed to escape the room.

Duplicates can be drop tricked by searching the crate to obtain another.

Icon Examine Directions
I could probably pour something into this. Found on shelves.
A tin layered with some stuff from a vial. Use gypsum on the first tin.
It's full of a white lumpy mixture that seems to be hardening. Use vial of liquid on second tin.
There is an impression of a key embedded in it. Use third tin on chained key.
There is an impression of a key, filled with tin ore. Use tin ore powder on fourth tin.
There is an impression of a key, filled with copper ore. Use cupric ore powder on fourth tin.
There is an impression of a key, filled with tin and copper ore. Use cupric ore powder on fifth tin, or tin ore powder on sixth tin.
There is a bronze key surrounded by plaster in this tin. Use seventh tin on bunsen burner. Use a knife, chisel or bronze wire on it to get bronze key.
There is a strange concoction filling this tin. Add any incorrect ingredients.
