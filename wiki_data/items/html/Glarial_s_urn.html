Glarial's urn is an item used in the Waterfall Quest and has no known use after the quest. The urn can obtained by searching the tomb in the Glarial's tomb. After the completion of the Waterfall Quest, the urn becomes empty.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
25 July 2019
(update)

The empty urn had its name changed from "Glarial's urn" to "Glarial's urn (empty)" to prevent confusion.

5 December 2005
(update)

A missing period was added to the items' examines.

14 October –
2 November 2004
  • The items were renamed from "Glarials[sic] urn" to "Glarial's urn".
  • The full urn's examine was changed from "An urn containing glarials[sic] ashes"[sic] to "An urn containing Glarial's ashes"[sic]
  • The empty urn's examine was changed from "An empty urn made for glarials[sic] ashes"[sic] to "An empty urn made for Glarial's ashes"[sic]
29 March 2004
(update)
  • On RuneScape 2s launch, all existing Glarial's urns became members-only. In RuneScape Classic, the item was classified as free-to-play, presumably by mistake, since it was only obtainable in member's worlds.
