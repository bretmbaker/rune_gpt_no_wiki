An air orb is a glass orb that has been charged at the Obelisk of Air by using the Charge Air Orb spell, requiring 66 Magic, on the obelisk. The spell requires an unpowered orb, 30 air runes, and 3 cosmic runes, yielding 76 Magic experience. The Air Obelisk is located in level 7 Wilderness, north of Edgeville. To get there, climb the ladder north of the black demons in Edgeville Dungeon.

An air orb can be used on a battlestaff, requiring 66 Crafting, yielding an air battlestaff and 137.5 Crafting experience.
