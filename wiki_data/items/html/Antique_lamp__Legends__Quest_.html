The antique lamp is an item obtained from Radimus Erkle after completing Legends' Quest. Four are given to the player, and each grants 30,000 experience each in four skills of their choice: Attack, Defence, Strength, Hitpoints, Prayer, Magic, Woodcutting, Crafting, Smithing, Herblore, Agility, and Thieving.

The lamps cannot be banked. However, if the player prefers to use them at a later date, they can be destroyed and reclaimed from Radimus whenever the player wishes to do so.
