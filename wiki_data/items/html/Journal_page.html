The journal page is obtained by searching the mud mound north of the Icyene graveyard with a spade, which yields the journal page and an ancient armour.

Transcript[edit | edit source]

The following text is transcluded from Transcript:Journal page.
From the stadium to Everlight, crowds lined the streets. I'd never seen anything like it. It seems that the royal family have spared no expense. It makes sense though. This is the first time that the Dominion Games have taken place here. The queen clearly wants to make everything as close as possible to the ones held back home. It seems to be working so far, but only time will tell for sure...

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The journal page, the ancient armour, and the mud mound are references to the Archaeology skill of RuneScape, which revolves around excavating soil and uncovering artefacts and ancient texts. The journal page also refers to Everlight; a large, recently resurfaced lighthouse in RuneScape, east of Darkmeyer and Meiyerditch, which was sunk by Lowerniel Drakan during the Conquest of Hallowvale.
