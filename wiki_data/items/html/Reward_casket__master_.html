A reward casket (master) is received after completing the final clue of a master clue scroll trail, and contains the reward for the trail.

While players can only have one of each type of clue scroll in their possession, they can possess unlimited amounts of reward caskets, and are eligible to receive clue scrolls again once it is obtained.

Rewards[edit | edit source]

Master clues generate a minimum of 5 rewards and a maximum of 7, granting an average of 6 rewards per casket. The rewards consist of generic items, shared treasure trail items, and items that are unique for master clues. Additionally, players have a 1/1,000 chance to receive the Bloodhound pet upon opening a master casket.

There is also a 1/15 chance of the reward casket being a mimic, which can be taken to the strange casket in Watson's house to fight The Mimic. Players are only eligible to receive mimics if they have spoken to the strange casket to enable the chance of a reward casket turning into a mimic. If a player attempts to open a master reward casket for the first time before speaking to the strange casket, they will be asked whether they want a chance of a Mimic boss fight or not, and wanting it will result in the chatbox directing players to the strange casket.

All droprates (except for the Mimic rewards) are confirmed by Mod Tide.[1][2] The listed rarities are per roll, except for the Mimic rewards. The average loot roll is worth approximately 146,250.98.[a]

On average, master clues are worth approximately 877,505.89, if the Mimic fight is not enabled. In comparison, the expected value of the easy through elite clues given to Watson is 1,080,748.03, including the value of potential master clues from the caskets themselves.

The Mimic has a significantly boosted chance to reward a piece of 3rd age equipment, adding an expected value of 252,100.78 for a total of 1,129,606.67 per master reward casket. The expected value of the clues given to Watson is 1,260,183.52, when additionally including Mimics from the elite casket and potential master clues.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
15 August 2019
(update | poll)

The locks on the reward casket have been changed to reflect the tier of Treasure Trail it comes from.

11 April 2019
 Hotfix

The chance of a master reward casket being a mimic was changed from 1/30 to 1/15.[5]

11 April 2019
(update | poll)

All Treasure Trails caskets were changed to be stackable. To bolster each tier of clue with new cosmetic items, thirteen new items were added as master clue scroll rewards.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • On release, players could also get rewarded Botanical pies from master clues, but this drop was quickly removed after this reward crashed the market of botanical pies.
  • With the 25 Jan 2018 update 3 new unique rewards were added to master clue scrolls, being the Anguish ornament kit, Dragon platebody ornament kit and Dragon kiteshield ornament kit.
  • In 2016 Mod Mat K revealed in a data stream that on average 700 master clue scrolls were completed every day.
  • This casket cannot be opened on Entrana. The following message is received "You cannot open this on Entrana, the monks are watching you too closely."
