Hallowed ring is a reward from the Hallowed Sepulchre. It can be purchased from the Mysterious Hallowed Goods shop for 250 hallowed marks. When equipped, it negates all damage taken from failing environmental obstacles like the knight statues' swords and the crossbowman statues' bolts, and reduces the recovery time by teleporting the player back to the start of the obstacle should they fail. It has no effect on the recovery time for failing the grapple pillars and does not negate damage taken for failing to enchant portal frames.

The ring also counts as a Saradomin item for the purposes of recharging your run energy at the Magical Obelisks at the end of each floor and as a follower item in God Wars Dungeon.

The Hallowed ring can be stored in the Hallowed Sepulchre Equipment Storage run by the Mysterious Stranger.
