The Avernic defender is a defender wielded in the shield slot, requiring 70 Attack and Defence to wield. It is obtained by combining a dragon defender with an Avernic defender hilt. This process can be reversed to return the dragon defender, but the hilt will be destroyed in the process. This defender boasts the highest strength bonus of any shield slot equipment, being 1 higher than the dragonfire shield. It also has the highest melee attack bonuses of any shield slot equipment. The defensive bonuses are comparable to that of an adamant kiteshield.

Ghommal's hilt 5 or 6 can be attached to it, creating Ghommal's avernic defender 5 or 6 respectively, giving the bonuses of the Avernic defender and the additional benefits of the hilt. This process is reversible, returning both the hilt and the defender.

If the player dies below level 20 Wilderness, it will remain in the player's inventory, but will be unusable and must be repaired with 1,000,000 coins by using the item on Perdu. If the player is killed by another player, below 20 Wilderness, 1,000,000 coins will be dropped, and above 20 Wilderness, 600,000 coins will be dropped.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
20 September 2023
(update)

The amount of coins dropped in place of the Avernic Defender when a player is killed by another player above level 20 Wilderness has been increased from 300,000 to 600,000 coins.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The term Avernic refers to a race of demons, which are composed of the Tsutsaroth (demons such as K'ril Tsutsaroth and Porazdir), Alyaroth (greater demons and black demons) and Byzroth (lesser demons) tribes.
    • Currently, the only demon confirmed to be an Avernic in Old School RuneScape is Thammaron.[1]
