Bruma herbs are obtained from picking Sprouting Roots found within the lobby and the east and west side of the Wintertodt's prison. They are used on a rejuvenation potion (unf) to create a rejuvenation potion, yielding Herblore experience equal to 10% of the player's Herblore level, that heals the pyromancers. They can only be harvested during a game of Wintertodt, not while it is subdued.

Date Changes
9 October 2024
(update)

Picking bruma herbs no longer grants Farming experience. Previously, picking one granted experience equal to 10% of the player's Farming level.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Brūma is the Latin word for winter solstice and by extension, winter cold; it is the root of the English word brumation.
