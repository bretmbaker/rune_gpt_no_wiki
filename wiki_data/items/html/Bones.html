Bones are the remains of most creatures with an animalistic or humanoid appearance.

Bones give 4.5 Prayer experience when buried. This can be increased by offering the bones in different ways:

  • 13.5 XP when casting Sinister Offering.
  • 13.5 XP when using the sacred bone burner after the appropriate faith level has been achieved.
  • 15.7 XP when offered at a gilded altar with two burners lit or at the Chaos Temple.
  • 18 XP when offered at the Ectofuntus in the form of bonemeal.
  • 20 XP when blessed, broken down into blessed bone shards and offered at the libation bowl with jug of blessed wine, or 24 XP when offered with a jug of blessed sunfire wine. Virilis outside the altar will exchange noted bones for 10 coins each.

Players may also use the Bones to Bananas or Bones to Peaches spells to turn normal bones to edible bananas or peaches.

25 regular bones are used in the Demon Slayer quest.
