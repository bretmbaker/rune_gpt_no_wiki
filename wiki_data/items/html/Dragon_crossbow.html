The dragon crossbow is a crossbow that is stronger than the rune crossbow. It requires a Ranged level of 64 to wield, and can fire bolts up to dragon bolts. It has an attack range of 7 increased to 9 with longrange.

Dragon crossbows can be made through the Fletching skill at level 78; a player must first cut a magic stock for the crossbow from magic logs, granting 70 Fletching experience. Then, a player must add dragon limbs to the stock with a hammer in their inventory, granting 135 experience and making an unstrung dragon crossbow. Finally, they must string the crossbow with a crossbow string, granting 70 experience. Creating a dragon crossbow from raw materials grants 275 Fletching experience in total. A dragon crossbow can be changed cosmetically by using a Bounty hunter ornament kit on it to become a dragon crossbow (cr). The ornament kit can be obtained by participating in Bounty Hunter and earning 50 Bounty Hunter Points needed to purchase one from the Bounty Hunter Store.

Special attack[edit | edit source]

The crossbow has a special attack, Annihilate, which drains 60% of the special attack bar and hits up to 9 enemies in a 3x3 area. The player will roll for accuracy, and if it succeeds, then rolls for a hit between 0 and the usual maximum hit from the regular attack. The primary target of Annihilate will take 20% extra damage, and all other targets will take 20% less damage. For example, if a hit of 25 is rolled, the primary target will get 30 damage and the other targets will get 20 damage. If used in a single-way combat area, the attack will still work but only hit one target with 20% extra damage.

Enchanted bolts cannot activate their effects when using the dragon crossbow's special attack.[1]
