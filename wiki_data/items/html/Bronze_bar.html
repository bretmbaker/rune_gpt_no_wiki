A bronze bar is a bar of refined bronze. It can be created through the Smithing skill at level 1 by using a copper ore and a tin ore on a furnace, granting 6.2 Smithing experience. A bronze bar can be smithed on an anvil through the Smithing skill to create bronze weapons and armour. A list of bronze items that can be smithed from bronze bars and the Smithing levels required can be found here. When smithed, each bar gives 12.5 experience, with a maximum of 62.5 experience given for the bronze platebody.

Members can purchase bronze bars Shantay Pass Shop at the Shantay Pass south of Al Kharid for 8gp each or from Jiminua's Jungle Store north of Tai Bwo Wannai village for 12gp each.

4-6 bronze bars are required for quests. Observatory Quest requires 1, The Tourist Trap requires 1-3 (dependent on your luck), Prince Ali Rescue requires 1 and One Small Favour requires 1. Furthermore, Creature of Fenkenstrain and Recipe for Disaster/Freeing Pirate Pete require 3 bronze wires each, for a total of 10-12 bronze bars.
