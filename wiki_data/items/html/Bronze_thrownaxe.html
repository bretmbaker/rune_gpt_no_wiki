The bronze thrownaxe is a Ranged throwing weapon. There are no requirements to wield a bronze throwing axe. They can be obtained either by buying from the Tribal Weapons store at the Ranging Guild or trading with another player. The bronze throwing axe can be wielded with a shield or prayer book for protection.
