Brown spice is one of four spices used on stew to make spicy stew. The Freeing Evil Dave subquest of Recipe for Disaster must be completed to obtain the spices, which can be found in Evil Dave's Basement of Doom in Edgeville. Stews containing brown spice temporarily boost or drain the stats of Farming, Fishing, Herblore, Mining, and Woodcutting.

Obtaining spice[edit | edit source]

Sending your cat to catch Hell-Rats yields between 1 to 4 doses of any of the spice colours. The Hell-Rat Behemoth in the southern room always drop 4 doses of the spice they guard. Spices of the same colour can be used on each other to combine up to 4 doses to save space. Once all doses are used it becomes an empty spice shaker.

The locations of the coloured spices the Behemoths guard.

Comparison to guaranteed stat boosts[edit | edit source]

A single dose of brown spice in a stew causes the affected stats to increase or decrease by a maximum of 1. Two doses affect those stats by -3 or +3, and three doses bring the maximum up to -5 or +5. Because of the uncertainty of the boost, players may prefer boosts that give a guaranteed boost every usage.
