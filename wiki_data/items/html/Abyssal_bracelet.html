An abyssal bracelet is made by casting the Lvl-4 Enchant spell on a diamond bracelet, requiring a Magic level of 57. Diamond bracelets can be made at level 58 Crafting.

Wearing the bracelet will prevent the player from being skulled when entering the Abyss. Each bracelet holds 5 charges, and 1 charge is used each time the wearer enters the Abyss. When all charges are used, the bracelet crumbles to dust.
