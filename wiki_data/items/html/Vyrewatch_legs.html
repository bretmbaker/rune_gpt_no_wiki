Vyrewatch legs are a part of the Vyrewatch clothing set. They may be bought from Trader Sven in Meiyerditch and reduce the chances of being spotted by the Vyrewatch patrols when worn.

In the quest, Sins of the Father, the Vyrewatch legs are upgraded to the Vyre noble legs by Polmafi Ferdygris.
