The ardrigal mixture is an item used during Legends' Quest to make a bravery potion. It is made by using an ardrigal on a vial of water. Snake weed can then be added to make the bravery potion, which is used to enter the dark cave during the quest. It can be made after completion of Legends' Quest, although it has no use.
