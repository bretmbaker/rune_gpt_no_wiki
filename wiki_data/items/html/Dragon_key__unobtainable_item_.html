The dragon key is an unobtainable item that can be seen in a cutscene during Dragon Slayer II. It is dropped by a slain dragonkin during the assault on Lithkren in the Fourth Age by Robert the Strong, Aivas, Camorra, and Tristan.

While the key appears visually identical to the real dragon key used to open the Lithkren Vault, it is in reality a different version of the item to prevent sequence breaking in case a player somehow manages to access the cutscene and take the item.
