Limestone bricks can be crafted through the Crafting skill at level 12 by using a chisel with limestone, granting 6 Crafting experience. Note that you may inadvertently create a rock if you are too heavy-handed with the chisel.

Bricks can be purchased for 26 coins from the Stonemason in Keldagrim or Metla in the Stonecutter Outpost, or for 21 coins from Razmire at his builders store in Mort'ton. Rasolo, the wandering merchant south-east of Baxtorian Falls, also sells two for 40 coins.

Limestone bricks are used in the Construction skill, providing 20 Construction experience each. They are also used in rebuilding the Mort'ton temple during the Shades of Mort'ton quest and minigame, as well as to construct additional Quetzal Transport System landing zones, requiring 3 limestone bricks each. The Flamtaer bag, obtainable from chests within the Shade Catacombs is able to store up to 60 limestone bricks.
