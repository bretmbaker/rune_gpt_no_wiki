Soul runes are one of the runes used to cast spells in the Magic skill. It is used for the strongest curse spells, the teleport-other spells, Enchant Crossbow Bolt (Dragonstone), Blood Barrage, the shadow spells and most Reanimation spells. Soul runes are also used in charging Tumeken's shadow and the soul bearer, as well as creating Elidinis' ward (f).

Runecraft info[edit | edit source]

With 90 Runecraft, players can craft soul runes from dark essence fragments at the Soul Altar in northern Arceuus, granting 29.7 experience per fragment.
