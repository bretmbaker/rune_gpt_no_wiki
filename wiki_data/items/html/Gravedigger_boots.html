Gravedigger boots are an untradeable cosmetic item, part of the gravedigger outfit, which can be obtained by completing one of the Halloween holiday events that occur once per year.

During the 2015 Halloween event, they were given by the Grim Reaper in order to disguise the player to prevent Anti-Santa from becoming suspicious.

If destroyed, a replacement can be obtained from Diango in Draynor Village or from the toy box of a costume room, as part of the 2015: Undead Gravedigger set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
