Bronze nails are nails made with a bronze bar on an anvil with a hammer in the player's inventory, requiring level 4 Smithing, yielding 15 bronze nails and granting 12.5 Smithing experience per bar. Bronze nails can be purchased from the sawmill operator for 2 coins each, however they break very easily.

Bronze nails can be used to construct certain types of wooden furniture. With 7 Fletching, they can be used on flighted ogre arrows to create bronze brutal arrows, granting 1.4 Fletching experience per arrow. With 41 Crafting, fifteen bronze nails can be used with an oak shield and two pieces of hard leather to craft a hard leather shield, granting 70 Crafting experience per shield. One bronze nail is required in The Fremennik Isles quest in order to make a Neitiznot shield.

Since nails cannot be bent while constructing STASH units, bronze nails are the most economical option for them.
