A bucket of milk is a Cooking ingredient used to make a variety of drinks and foods such as choc saturdays and cakes, as well as several feeders. Players can use a bucket on a dairy cow or dairy buffalo to create a bucket of milk, or simply click on the cow or buffalo with at least one empty bucket in the inventory.

Players can turn a bucket of milk into chocolatey milk by adding chocolate dust, or add milk to nettle tea (though this does not add any effects). Depending on their Cooking level, Members can use a dairy churn to turn a bucket of milk into a pot of cream, a pat of butter, or a piece of cheese.

A bucket of milk is required to make a Hangover cure during the Plague City quest and the Skippy and the Mogres miniquest. It is also needed in the Gertrude's Cat quest.

Uses[edit | edit source]

Quests[edit | edit source]

  • Cook's Assistant: 1 × bucket of milk
  • Plague City: 1 × bucket of milk to make chocolatey milk
  • Gertrude's Cat: 1 × bucket of milk
  • Ghosts Ahoy: 1 × bucket of milk
  • A Tail of Two Cats: 1 × bucket of milk
  • Ratcatchers: 1 × bucket of milk
  • Recipe for Disaster (Freeing the Mountain Dwarf subquest): 1 × bucket of milk

Miniquests[edit | edit source]

  • Skippy and the Mogres: 1 × bucket of milk to make chocolatey milk

Pets[edit | edit source]

A bucket of milk can also be used to feed kittens. If fed to a hellcat, it will turn back into a normal cat.
