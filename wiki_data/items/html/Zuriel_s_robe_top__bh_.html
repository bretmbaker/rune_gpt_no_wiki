Zuriel's robe top (bh) is a piece of armour requiring 78 Defence to wear, and is purchased from the Bounty Hunter Store for 650 points. When worn alongside Zuriel's hood (bh) and robe bottom (bh), the accuracy of magical attacks is increased by 25%. It can be converted into its corrupted variant by speaking to a Corrupted Warrior.

As with all Ancient Warriors' equipment from Bounty Hunter, it is initially purchased in inactive form and must be charged with 5,000,000 coins before it can be used by the player. In addition, it can only be used within Daimon's Crater, Castle Wars, Clan Wars, and Soul Wars; attempting to attack anything outside the listed areas will result in the player being interrupted from doing so.

Upon dying in PvP, the coins used to activate the item are given to the killer, and players are left with the inactive variant.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
29 January 2025
(update)
  • The robe top's Magic attack bonus was increased from +35 to +58.
  • The set effect of Zuriel's equipment (bh) was changed from a 15% accuracy bonus to special attacks of magic weapons to a 25% accuracy bonus for both magic spells and special attacks of magic weapons.
11 September 2024
(update | poll)

Bounty Hunter rewards can now be used within Castle Wars, Clan Wars, and Soul Wars.

14 June 2023
(update)

Zuriel's robe top has been given a 3% Magic Damage bonus.
