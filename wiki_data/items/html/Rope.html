Rope has many uses in RuneScape. It is used in many quests and in the creation of certain items. It can be obtained by purchasing at a shop, crafted by Ned in exchange for 4 balls of wool, or by a player spinning yak hair on a spinning wheel.

The best store to buy rope, without requirements, is at Ned's shop in Draynor Village. However, you can get a full inventory of rope for free from the Tempoross minigame in the ropes container. A convenient place to get rope is in the Ardougne general store (helpful for those doing the Waterfall Quest and Plague City) where there is a stock of 20, but Martin Thwait's Lost and Found at Rogues' Den is a far better option since it stocks 50 and is next to a bank (although it requires 50 Thieving and 50 Agility to access his shop). Khazard General Store is also within close proximity of a bank.

Quests[edit | edit source]

  • Prince Ali Rescue: 1 × rope to tie up Lady Keli
  • Plague City: 1 × rope to open grill
  • The Dig Site: 2 × rope
  • Spirits of the Elid: 1 × rope
  • Watchtower: 2 x rope
  • The Fremennik Isles: 9 x rope
  • Recipe for Disaster (Freeing King Awowogei subquest): 1 × rope to get red banana
  • In Aid of the Myreque: 1 × rope to make Rod of ivandis
  • A Porcine of Interest: 1 × rope to enter the strange hole
  • Waterfall Quest: 1 x rope to traverse the waterfall
  • Tale of the Righteous: 1 × rope to enter the Quidamortem Cave
  • Underground Pass: 1 x rope to traverse the pit in the Underground Pass_(dungeon)
  • Regicide: 1 x rope to traverse the pit in the Underground Pass_(dungeon)

Passage[edit | edit source]

  • Entering the Lumbridge Swamp Caves
  • Entering the Kalphite Lair
  • Entering the God Wars Dungeon, and Saradomin's Encampment from there
  • To ride on eagles using the Eagle transport system
  • Entering the Waterfall Dungeon
  • Getting through the Underground Pass
  • To go around the guard on the way to the Mountain Camp during Mountain Daughter
  • Repairing the net in the Fishing Trawler minigame
  • To get to the Ogre Island east of Castle Wars
  • Entering the rift in A Soul's Bane
  • Used to access the baby dragon area within the Evil Chicken's Lair

Other[edit | edit source]

  • Net trapping salamanders with the Hunter skill
  • Constructing a well for the Museum Camp on Fossil Island
  • Crafting a mith grapple for Agility related courses

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Ned leads you to believe that he makes rope from wool. During The Fremennik Isles quest, however, you discover that Ned trades with the Fremennik in Neitiznot, exchanging the wool he receives for rope. The Fremennik are amused that you would believe sturdy rope could be made from something as flimsy as wool rather than tough yak hair.
  • After The Fremennik Isles quest, if you buy a rope from Ned, your character will notice that it smells strange, and if you continue through the dialogue, Ned will give you a second rope for free, as a payment for your silence.
