Beautiful yellow pansy seeds are a special type of seed obtained from Wolf during the 2022 Pride event. One is given to Gilbert to progress the event.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Despite their destroy text claiming "they will be gone for good", it is always possible to reclaim the seeds if the player destroys them. The seeds only stop being reclaimable once they have been successfully handed in to Gilbert.
