The hallowed hammer is an item that will never break nails inside the Hallowed Sepulchre (regardless of the plank and nail combination used). It can be purchased from the Mysterious Hallowed Goods shop for 100 hallowed marks. Unlike the other hallowed equipment, the hammer does not need to be equipped in order for its effect to work.

The hallowed hammer cannot be used in place of a normal hammer outside the Hallowed Sepulchre. The hammer also counts as a Saradomin item for the purposes of recharging your run energy at the Magical Obelisks at the end of each floor, as well as in God Wars Dungeon.

The Hallowed hammer can be stored in the Hallowed Sepulchre Equipment Storage run by the Mysterious Stranger.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
2 July 2020
(update)
  • The hallowed hammer is now labelled as a member's item.
  • The examine text on the hallowed hammer has been slightly changed; previously, the examine text was "Removes the chance of bending nails in the Hallowed Sepulchre."
25 June 2020
(update)

The examine text on the hallowed hammer has been changed to reflect its buff from the previous week's update. Previously, the examine text was "Reduces the chance of bending nails in the Hallowed Sepulchre."

18 June 2020
(update)

The Hallowed hammer now has a 100% chance to not bend nails regardless of the plank and nail combination used.

12 June 2020
(update)

All Hallowed items can no longer be alchemised.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The hallowed hammer does not work outside the Hallowed Sepulchre as making it usable in other areas of RuneScape would require rewriting all hammer-related code across the entire game, a very large and time-consuming endeavor. Whilst this was later done for the Imcando hammer, the hallowed hammer remains usable only in the Hallowed Sepulchre.[1]
