Attuned crystal legs are a piece of crystal armour created within the Gauntlet, made from 60 crystal shards, 1 basic crystal legs, 1 crystal ore, 1 phren bark, and 1 linum tirinum.

It can be upgraded into a pair of perfected crystal legs by using 80 crystal shards, 2 crystal ore, 2 phren bark, and 2 linum tirinum.
