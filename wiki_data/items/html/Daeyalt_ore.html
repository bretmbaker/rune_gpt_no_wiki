Daeyalt ore is a rare ore that can only be mined from daeyalt rocks by players with 20 Mining during and after the Darkness of Hallowvale quest. It cannot be smelted into a bar using the Smithing skill.

Daeyalt ore is found in the Meiyerditch mine in north-western Meiyerditch in the Sanguinesti region and in the Daeyalt essence mine in Darkmeyer. A player gets 3 ores from each rock wall.

If players are sent to the Meiyerditch mine by the Vyrewatch in Meiyerditch, they must mine 15 ores and place them in the mine cart, then ask to be released from the mine. This can be skipped by wearing a vyre noble outfit, requiring partial completion of Sins of the Father.
