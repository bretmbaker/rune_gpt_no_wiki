Atlatl darts are ammunition used exclusively by the eclipse atlatl, which is obtained as a possible reward from the Lunar Chest in Neypotzli.

The atlatl dart has no bonuses as the eclipse atlatl uses the player's melee strength bonus to deal damage.

Despite being called darts, atlatl darts cannot be loaded into the toxic blowpipe, and they deal standard ranged damage.[1]

Despite being equipped in the ammo slot, they can not be stored in Dizana's quiver. Attempting to do so displays the following message: "You have nothing in your worn quiver to fill your Dizana's Quiver with."
