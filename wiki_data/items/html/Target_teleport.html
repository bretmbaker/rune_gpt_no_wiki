The Target teleport is a magic tablet that can be broken by players to teleport to their assigned target. They can be purchased at Justine's stuff for the Last Shopper Standing for 1 Last Man Standing point.

In order to be able to use the tablet, players must have unlocked Teleport to Target via reading the Target teleport scroll. In addition, the restrictions for casting the teleport spell also apply to the tablet.
