Pot lids are items that when used on pots create an airtight pot, an item required for the One Small Favour and Swan Song quests. They can be created by using a piece of soft clay on a Potter's wheel and then firing the unfired pot lid in a Pottery oven.

Success chance[edit | edit source]

At lower levels, there is a chance that an unfired pot lid will crack when placed inside a pottery oven. Players with level 13 Crafting or higher will always succeed at firing a pot lid.[1]
