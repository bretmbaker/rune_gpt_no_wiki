The enriched snapdragon seed is an item created by the player during While Guthix Sleeps, by using a rose-tinted lens on a normal snapdragon seed inside Betty's Magic Emporium. For the enrichment process to work, the player must stand on the tile immediately inside the doorway of the shop and then use the rose-tinted lens on the shop counter.

The seed is then planted in a specially prepared patch at the top of White Knights' Castle (accessible from the western stairs) in order to grow the enriched snapdragon needed for the replacement super truth serum.
