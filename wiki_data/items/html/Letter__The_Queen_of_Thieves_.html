A letter is a quest item used only in The Queen of Thieves. It is found in a locked chest, that can pickpocketed, on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] floor of Councillor Hughes' home in Kingstown.

It is given to Tomas Lawry so he can arrange for her arrest.

Transcript[edit | edit source]

The following text is transcluded from Transcript:Letter (The Queen of Thieves).

Child of Yama,

I have left the items you requested at the usual drop-off, courtesy of the Kourend Treasury. It goes without saying, but I trust you will make our master aware of who is funding his return.

Regards,
Sophia Hughes
