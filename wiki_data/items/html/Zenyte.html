Zenyte is the highest tier of gem in Old School RuneScape. Zenyte shards are dropped from monsters, then combined with uncut onyx to produce an uncut zenyte, which can be cut to form a zenyte.

Cutting an uncut zenyte with a chisel requires level 89 Crafting and yields 50 experience. It can then be made into zenyte jewellery by using a furnace along with a gold bar and a mould.

Zenyte jewellery can be enchanted to make some of the most powerful jewellery in the game. Wearing any piece of enchanted zenyte jewellery requires level 75 Hitpoints.

Training[edit | edit source]

While zenyte can be used for training, it is highly impractical due to its scarcity causing extremely quick price fluctuations when it is traded in abnormal volumes. Any profit shown below reflects merely theoretical values, and should not be expected to be maintainable as the trade volumes of zenyte and unenchanted zenyte jewellery are exceedingly low.

Magic[edit | edit source]

Zenyte jewellery can be enchanted using Lvl-7 Enchant, requiring level 93 Magic. The spell requires 1 cosmic rune, 20 soul runes, 20 blood runes and the piece of zenyte jewellery that is to be enchanted.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Zenyte appears to be derived from Zenith which means "the highest point", referenced by being the highest level gemstone.
  • Despite zenyte jewellery appearing to be made from silver, all of the jewellery is made using gold bars.
