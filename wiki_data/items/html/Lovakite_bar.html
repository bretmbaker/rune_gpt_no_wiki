A lovakite bar is a bar of refined lovakite ore made by smelting one lovakite ore and two pieces of coal at the Lovakite furnace in the centre of Lovakengj, granting 20 Smithing experience.

It can be smelted with a Smithing level of 45.

It cannot be smelted in a normal furnace. If players try, it'll say it's not the right temperature. Players must use the Lovakite furnace just south of the blast mine bank (accessible instantly by using Xeric's talisman to teleport to Xeric's Inferno). However, the Superheat Item spell will work as normal.
