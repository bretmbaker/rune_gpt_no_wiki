An orange is a fruit that can be grown as part of the Farming skill. Oranges are used in several gnome cooking recipes, and can be used with a knife to create orange slices or orange chunks. You can give a basket of oranges to a farmer as payment to protect a maple tree (farming). It heals 2 hitpoints regardless of the state the orange is in (whole, sliced or chunks).
