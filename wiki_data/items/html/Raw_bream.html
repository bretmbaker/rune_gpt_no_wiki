Raw bream are a type of fish caught from fishing spots within the Streambound Cavern in Neypotzli, requiring level 20 Fishing and a big net; occasionally, the bream may slip past the net instead of being caught. During Perilous Moons, players must catch one and use a knife on it to obtain bream scales.

Players with level 30 Cooking can cook raw bream to obtain a cooked bream. Up to two bream can be cooked in a single action, with the chance depending on the player's Cooking level.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
3 April 2024
(update)

Whilst fishing, aligning the net with the stream now guarantees one raw bream, with two being received if the player passes the skilling success roll.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The fishing method used to obtain raw bream was inspired by the 2001 video game Jak and Daxter which was revealed by Mod Rice in a March 2024 Q&A.[1]
