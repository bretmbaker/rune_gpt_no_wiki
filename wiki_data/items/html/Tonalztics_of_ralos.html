The Tonalztics of Ralos is a one-handed thrown weapon that requires level 75 Ranged to equip, which is obtained by participating in the Fortis Colosseum. Unlike most thrown weapons, it is not stackable and effectively provides unlimited "ammo," though it has an additional effect when charged. The weapon rolls against a monster's light ranged defence.

In its uncharged state, the weapon hits a target for 0-75% of the player's maximum ranged hit once. However, when charged with Sunfire splinters, the weapon will hit twice instead, with the second damage roll independent from the first. It can hold a maximum of 20,000 charges. One charge is consumed per throw, whether or not the special attack is used, and regardless of the amount of hits that land. Players can configure the charges while the weapon is in their bank by right-clicking on Configure-Charges, as long as players have the required materials.

Special attack[edit | edit source]

The Tonalztics of Ralos has a special attack, Division, costing 50% special attack energy. Division reduces the target's Defence level by 10% of its Magic level on a successful hit. This effect occurs for each individual hit, meaning a charged special attack can potentially reduce the target's defence two times (20% of Magic level) with a potential four time reduction if using two specials in a row (40% of Magic level).

This effect makes it uniquely useful for high-defence targets who have an accompanying high-magic level; Or for draining the defence of targets who are otherwise unreachable with standard methods (like a Dragon warhammer). An example being Zulrah: with her 300 Magic/Defence, Division can potentially drain up to 120 Defence (30 x 4) immediately and greatly increase accuracy for the duration of the fight.

When used on NPCs, the defence reduction from a successful first hit will apply for the calculation of the second hit. However, this is not the case when used in PVP [1].

The special attack animation differs depending on whether the weapon is charged.

  • A player performs Division with the uncharged weapon.

  • A player performs Division with the charged weapon.

Charging[edit | edit source]

To unleash the Tonalztics of Ralos' full potential, it must be charged with Sunfire splinters, holding up to 20,000 maximum charges. Each charge requires 1 Sunfire splinter, costing 437 coins per attack. The total cost to fully charge the weapon with 20,000 splinters is 8,740,000 coins. Assuming non-stop use, on the rapid attack style, one attack every 3.6 seconds and 1,000 attacks per hour, the weapon costs 437,000 coins per hour to use.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The weapon was initially proposed as the "Glaive of Ralos" but was changed to its current name after player feedback. The term "glaive" historically refers to a polearmed weapon, and was popularised as a term describing thrown weapons that return to the user after the 1983 film Krull, which features a similarly shaped weapon as its MacGuffin. Indeed, RuneScape also has a similarly named weapon, the shadow glaive, as well as a proper glaive named the Death Lotus Glaive.
  • The word "tonalztics" is made up of morphemes from the Nahuatl language. The root "tona-" evokes the warmth of the Sun, as in Tōnatiuh, a deity who carries the Sun across the sky, or tonalli, an animating force and aspect of one's soul that manifests in one way as internal body temperature. Many Nahuatl nouns and adjectives end in "-ztic".
