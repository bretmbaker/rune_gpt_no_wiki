Bronze bolts (unf) are unfinished bolts made from using a bronze bar on an anvil with a hammer, requiring 3 Smithing, yielding 10 bronze bolts (unf) and 12.5 Smithing experience. Using a feather on one makes a bronze bolt, requiring 9 Fletching, and yielding 0.5 Fletching experience per bolt.
