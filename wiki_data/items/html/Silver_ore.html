Silver ore can be mined at level 20 Mining, providing 40 Mining experience. After silver rocks are mined, it takes 60 seconds for them to respawn. Smelting silver ore will turn it into a silver bar, requiring 20 Smithing and giving 13.7 Smithing experience. The bar can then be crafted into various types of jewellery through the Crafting skill at a furnace, rather than at an anvil like other metallic bars. Doing so requires the usage of a mould, depending on the type of jewellery desired.

Wearing mining gloves, superior mining gloves, or expert mining gloves allows 1, 2, or 3 additional ores (respectively) to be mined before depletion.
