The echo Virtus robe bottom is a Virtus robe bottom with an echo virtus ornament kit added onto it. The kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the robe bottom.

Whilst in its ornamented state, the item can be reverted anytime, returning the robe bottom and kit. It can be stored in a magic wardrobe in a player-owned house.

As with its normal variant, it provides protection from Zaros' followers within the God Wars Dungeon's Ancient Prison.
