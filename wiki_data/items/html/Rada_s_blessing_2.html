Rada's blessing 2 is an Achievement diary reward for completing all medium Kourend & Kebos Diary tasks. It is given by Elise in the Kourend Castle courtyard, and can be retrieved for free if lost. It can be stored in a tackle box.

Destination[edit | edit source]

Benefits[edit | edit source]

When worn
  • 4% chance to catch two fish at once everywhere when equipped (no additional XP is given), excluding Tempoross
  • +1 to blessing's prayer bonus compared to the Rada's blessing 1
When worn or in the player's inventory
  • 5 daily teleports to the Kourend Woodland

A number of other benefits are always active upon claiming rewards from the medium tier of the Kourend and Kebos diary.
