Adamant bolts are a ranged projectile that can be fired from an adamant crossbow or higher, effectively requiring 46 Ranged to use.

An alternative for players with 55 Slayer and 61 Ranged is broad bolts, as these are equally strong. However, broad bolts require a rune crossbow or higher to use. As with most bolts, they can be poisoned, however this will prevent the addition of bolt tips.

Adamant bolts can be smithed at level 73 Smithing using an adamantite bar, creating 10 adamant bolts(unf) per bar. Level 61 Fletching is required to complete the bolts. When adding feathers, each ten bolts yields 70 experience. Players can also choose to attach ruby or diamond bolt tips at level 63 and 65 Fletching, respectively, and then enchant the bolts to make ruby bolts (e) or diamond bolts (e), which are useful for boss fights.

Ironmen who lack the 73 Smithing requirement may receive adamant bolts as an uncommon drop from iron dragons (1/21.33) and skeletal wyverns (1/42.67), or as rewards for the Hallowed Sepulchre and Soul Wars minigames.
