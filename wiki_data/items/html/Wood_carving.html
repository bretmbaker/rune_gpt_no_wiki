Wood carvings are items obtained during The Garden of Death, where players find them found throughout the dungeons built by the Old Ones, an ancient race who inhabited modern-day Varlamore.

The carvings can be inspected to view illustrations and words in a previously unknown language, which are used as context clues for players to infer the meaning behind the words, and attempting a possible translation for them via the word translations scroll.
