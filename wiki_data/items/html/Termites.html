Termites are insects in a jar which are obtained while training Agility at the Colossal Wyrm Agility Course. They are scooped up from small circles of termites that occasionally spawn on the course, which are picked up automatically without need for additional input from the player, in contrast to Marks of Grace.

While using basic obstacles in the course, players will receive 8-10 termites per scoop, whilst obstacles in the advanced section of the course give 12-15.

If running the advanced course, players can expect between 3.5 and 4 termites per lap on average, meaning roughly 400 laps are required to accumulate enough termites to fill all the collection log slots.

Termites serve as the currency for Worm Tongue's Wares, and are eaten by Worm Tongue, an anteater.

Rewards[edit | edit source]

Reward Cost Description
Amylase pack 100 A pack containing 100 crystals of amylase.
Colossal wyrm teleport scroll 40 A teleport to the Colossal Wyrm Remains in the Avium Savannah.
Graceful crafting kit 650 Gives your graceful set a Varlamore-themed makeover.
Calcified acorn 900 A brittle looking acorn. Perhaps a squirrel would be interested in this?

Players will need a total of 1,590 Termites to complete the Colossal Wyrm Agility collection log entry (as the Amylase pack is not part of the collection log).

You will get 1 dose of Stamina potion per Termite if crafting them from the Amylase packs purchased here (before factoring in the Amulet of chemistry or Alchemist's amulet).
