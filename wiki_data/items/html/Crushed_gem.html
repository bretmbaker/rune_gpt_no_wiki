A crushed gem is produced when a player fails to cut a semi-precious gems (opal, jade and red topaz). Players will gain a small amount of Crafting experience when a gem is crushed. The chance of obtaining crushed gems decreases with higher Crafting levels.

Crushed gems may be one of the items required in the Fairytale I - Growing Pains quest to make the magic secateurs.

The item is also tradeable but cannot be sold on the Grand Exchange.
