The dragon boots (g) are dragon boots with a dragon boots ornament kit attached to it. The attached ornament kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the boots. It costs 211,180 for both the parts to make the dragon boots (g).

While the boots are untradeable in its ornamented state, it can be dismantled anytime, returning the tradeable boots and ornament kit. This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room.
