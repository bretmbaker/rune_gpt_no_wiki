Old notes can be obtained throughout the Karuulm Slayer Dungeon. The notes are written by a Dragonkin named Karuulm, noting their experiments to recreate the Wyrm and the creation of the Drakes and Hydras. There are seven different notes, and can be combined into the Mount Karuulm diary.

Old notes locations[edit | edit source]

The old notes can be found by searching various stone crates in the Karuulm Slayer Dungeon, marked in the maps below.
