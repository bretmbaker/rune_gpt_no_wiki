Barronite deposits are found whilst mining Barronite within the Ruins of Camdozaal, each deposit mined yields 32 Mining experience. Mining barronite does not consume celestial ring charges, however the +4 invisible Mining boost still applies.

Using them on the Barronite Crusher anvil, near Ramarno, with a hammer yields various rewards, including barronite shards, and 30 Smithing experience.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
5 May 2021
(update)

Smithing experience gained from crushing a Barronite deposit increased from 10.2 to 30.

16 April 2021
(update) 		Hotfix
  • Mining experience gained from obtaining a Barronite deposit increased from 24 to 32.
  • The chance of successfully mining Barronite at high Mining levels has been decreased, but the chance at low Mining levels has been increased.
  • The extra chance of success afforded by the Charm of Mining has been increased from 5% to 10%.
15 April 2021
(update) 		Hotfix
  • The base chance of getting a Barronite deposit increased from 3% to 24%.
  • Mining experience gained from obtaining a Barronite deposit reduced to 24 from 250.
  • Smithing experience gained from crushing a Barronite deposit reduced to 10.2 from 37.5.
  • Unique items from Barronite are now 8x rarer to receive, with the Broken hammer being 3x rarer.
  • The Charm of Mining now gives an extra 5% chance of success when mining, rather than 5% additional deposits.
