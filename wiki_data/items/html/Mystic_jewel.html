The mystic jewel is given by Brian O'Richard to players when starting the Rogues' Den minigame. When used, it teleports players from the maze back to the waiting room, the starting point of the game.

Date Changes
24 August 2017
(update | poll)

The mystic jewel is now automatically given when entering the Rogues' Den maze.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Previously, Brian O'Richard would give the gem prior to entering the maze. Activating the mystic jewel outside of the maze resulted in Brian O'Richard telling the player they don't require help as they aren't in the maze.
