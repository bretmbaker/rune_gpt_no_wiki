The crocodile emblem is an item obtained during the quest Beneath Cursed Sands. The emblem depicts Crondis, one of the four minor gods of the Menaphite Pantheon.

The crocodile emblem, along with the scarab, baboon, and human emblem, are used to solve a puzzle in the tomb below the Ruins of Ullek.
