Pharmakos berries are used in making goblin potions, which are used during the Land of the Goblins quest and Hopespear's Will miniquest. They can be picked from pharmakos bushes near the Makeover Mage.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • In Greek, "Pharmakos" means "medicine", although in other contexts it can sometimes also mean "poison", ironically.
  • In RuneScape, Pharmakos berries are given directly to the player by the Makeover Mage rather than being harvested from nearby bushes.
