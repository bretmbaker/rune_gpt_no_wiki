The Onyx amulet is made by using an onyx and a gold bar on a furnace with an amulet mould in your inventory to create an onyx amulet (u). This requires a Crafting level of 90 and provides 165 Crafting experience when made. The amulet must then be strung before wearing by using a ball of wool or the String Jewellery spell from the Lunar spellbook. Stringing provides an additional 4 Crafting experience.

The Onyx amulet can be enchanted into an amulet of fury through the use of the Lvl-6 Enchant spell which requires level 87 Magic, or an enchant onyx tablet made from a lectern in a player-owned house.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The ball of wool used to make this amulet seems to be stained black in the making of this amulet. Additionally, despite having to use a gold bar to make an onyx amulet, there is no gold visible on the model of the amulet.
