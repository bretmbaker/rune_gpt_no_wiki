The tier 1 twisted boots are a tradeable cosmetic item. They are part of the tier 1 tier 1 Twisted Relic Hunter, which can be purchased for 1,000 League points from the Leagues Reward Shop.

This item can be stored in the armour case of a costume room. It can also be displayed (with the rest of the outfit) by using it on furniture built in the League hall's outfit stand space.
