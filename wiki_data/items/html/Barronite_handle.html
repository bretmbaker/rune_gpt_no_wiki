The barronite handle is one of the three parts of the barronite mace, found while fishing at a spot in the Ruins of Camdozaal.

Alongside 1,500 barronite shards, a barronite head, and barronite guard, it can be restored to a barronite mace by Ramarno.

Alternatively, the handle can be exchanged for 100 Barronite shards by showing it to Ramarno. However, casting High Alchemy on it is usually preferable.

Note: The fastest way to obtain a barronite handle is small net fishing. Many players choose to drop acquired fish as this speeds up the initial collection process. Offering fish to the altar does not provide any rolls on the drop table.

Activating the Charm of Luck buff from Ramarno for 3000 Barronite shards will give a 5% boost of getting this item.
