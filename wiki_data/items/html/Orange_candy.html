Orange candies are candies that can be taken from a pumpkin in a player-owned house. When eaten, the player will say Ooh piece of candy. The candy will not heal the player.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
11 November 2020
(update)

The item could now be turned into a bank placeholder.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The line "Ooh piece of candy." is likely a reference to Family Guy where James Woods is depicted as following a trail of candy, saying this line each time he eats one.
