Mourner boots are part of the mourner set. To obtain them, players must kill a level 11 Mourner on the path to Prifddinas. However, if not killed in one hit, the Mourner will bring all combat stats down to 20, so it is recommended players bring a stat restore potion before fighting.

If you've completed Song of the Elves, you can find these boots in a closed chest in the basement of the Mourner headquarters.

This item can be stored in the armour case of a costume room.
