The wig is an item used in the Prince Ali Rescue quest. Ned creates it if three balls of wool are given to him. A yellow dye can be used on a grey wig to turn it blonde. The player may ask Ned to create as many as they want.

The wig is one of the necessary items to disguise Prince Ali as Lady Keli, as part of the rescue operation.

The wig is removed from the inventory after talking to Prince Ali.
