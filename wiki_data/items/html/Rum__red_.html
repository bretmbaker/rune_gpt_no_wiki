Rum is only available for purchase from Honest Jimmy at Trouble Brewing, at a cost of 20 pieces of eight. It serves as a single-use teleport back to Trouble Brewing. Upon drinking the rum, the player will become intoxicated and collapse, after which point they will awaken outside the lobby of the minigame. The rum can not be consumed beyond level 20 Wilderness.

While under the intoxicating effects, the player will exclaim: Oh gods! It tastes like burning!

Attempting to drink the rum while already at Trouble Brewing will result in the message: It hardly seems worth drinking the 'rum' here.

It is often more time-efficient to use the minigame teleport to get to Trouble Brewing, as it costs nothing, and can be used every 20 minutes.

Creation[edit | edit source]

During a game of Trouble Brewing, rum is created with 5 buckets of water and 3 bowls of red water. The coloured water is obtained by placing red flowers into the proper kettle and using an empty bowl on the kettle. Sweetgrubs, obtained by luring with meat, and scrapey tree bark, obtained by using a knife on scrapey tree logs, are needed for the rum as well. One sweetgrub and one scrapey tree bark are needed per bottle of rum.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Unlike most drinks, the bottle can be seen in the player's hand as they consume it.
  • The line "it tastes like burning" is likely a reference to The Simpsons episode "Das Bus", where Ralph eats berries he found and exclaims they "taste like burning".
