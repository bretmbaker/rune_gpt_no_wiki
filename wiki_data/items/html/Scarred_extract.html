Scarred extracts are a catalyst used in runecrafting when crafting blood, soul, and wrath runes, by giving 60 extra runes per extract.

The extract can be obtained from the extract orifice by depositing tainted essence chunks into a ventriculus opening, and having 24,000 coins within Hagus' coffer to convert the tainted essence into a scarred extract.

Because using scarred extracts result in a cost of 400 coins for each rune crafted, it is not recommended to use extracts as a means of making money; rather, it is intended for ironman players who plan to use the runes for themselves.

When used in combination with a blood essence, their effects stack additively: both the essence and the extract will act on the base number of blood runes crafted.[1]

Scarred extracts can be used at either the traditional runecrafting altars, or at the Zeah blood or soul altars.

If players happen to make too many extracts, they can be refunded for 90% of their original cost by taking them to Hagus in the Scar.
