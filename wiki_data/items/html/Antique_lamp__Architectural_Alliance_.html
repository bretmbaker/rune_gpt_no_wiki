The antique lamp was an item obtained from Hosa after completing the Architectural Alliance miniquest. It granted 10,000 experience in a skill of the player's choosing above level 40. The lamp could not be banked, but could be destroyed and reclaimed from Hosa at a later point in time.

Upon removal of the Architectural Alliance miniquest on 10 January 2024, the antique lamp was added as a reward from completing A Kingdom Divided instead. It was also recoloured in order to resemble the other antique lamp awarded by that quest. Players that have completed Architectural Alliance prior to this date without claiming an antique lamp as a reward can still do so.

Players who have completed the miniquest prior to the release of the antique lamp can claim one from Hosa.
