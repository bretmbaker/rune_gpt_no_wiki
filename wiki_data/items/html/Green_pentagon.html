The green pentagon is an item used in The Eyes of Glouphrie and The Path of Glouphrie. It has a value of 20 in Oaknock's and Yewnock's machine, as calculated by multiplying the value of the shape's colour and the number of sides the shapes have:

Below is the value of each shape:
Colour Value
Circle Triangle Square Pentagon
Red 1 3 4 5
Orange 2 6 8 10
Yellow 3 9 12 15
Green 4 12 16 20
Blue 5 15 20 25
Indigo 6 18 24 30
Violet 7 21 28 35
