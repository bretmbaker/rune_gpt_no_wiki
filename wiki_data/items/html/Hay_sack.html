A hay sack is made by using an empty sack on a hay bale or on any haystack. It is combined with a bronze spear and a watermelon to make a Scarecrow. Level 23 Farming is required for either of these steps.

To fill your sack, you can go to:

  • The chicken coop north of Port Sarim
  • The haystack north of Ardougne
  • The haystack in the building next to the Emblem Trader in Edgeville
  • The hay bales north-west of Lumbridge
  • The "inner" Barbarian Outpost (Agility training course)
  • You can also fill sacks from the hay bales north-east of Draynor Village, north-west of Lumbridge, north of Ardougne, North of Hosidius, and south of the Farming Guild.
  • The hay bales in the northern building of the Ortus Farm
