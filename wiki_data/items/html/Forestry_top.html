The forestry top is a cosmetic variant of the lumberjack top, purchased from the Forestry Shop for 1,250 anima-infused bark, the lumberjack top, and 60 noted oak, willow, teak, maple, mahogany, arctic pine, yew, magic, and redwood logs.

Like the lumberjack top, the forestry top provides a +0.8% boost in Woodcutting experience, and is increased to +2.5% when the full forestry outfit is worn. Additionally, the outfit can be stored in a clothes pouch in order to receive the outfit's benefits without having to wear them. However, bonuses from other outfits, such as the graceful outfit, will override the clothes pouch if worn.
