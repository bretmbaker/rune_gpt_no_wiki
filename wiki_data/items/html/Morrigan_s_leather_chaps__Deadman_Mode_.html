Morrigan's leather chaps is a piece of armour exclusive to Deadman seasonals, requiring 78 Defence to wear. It is part of Morrigan's equipment, a set of Ancient Warriors' equipment, and is dropped by any monster found in the Wilderness as part of a global loot table, the chance being relative to the combat level of the monster.
