The angler hat is obtained from aerial fishing by purchasing it from Alry the Angler for a price of 100 Molch pearls or acquired randomly from completing a game of Fishing Trawler. The hat grants 0.4% bonus Fishing experience. A Fishing level of 34 is required to wear it. The angler hat is 1 of 4 pieces of the angler's outfit.

When a player receives it from the Fishing Trawler minigame, a message in the chat box states The luck of the sea is in today. You spot a piece of fishing gear caught up in the netting!

This item can be stored in the armour case of a costume room, as part of the Angler outfit set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored. It can also be stored in a tackle box.
