A broken bronze pickaxe was a bronze pickaxe that had been broken by an exploding rock. It could be fixed at Nurmof's Pickaxe Shop in the Dwarven Mine.

If the broken pickaxe was equipped before being broken, it would remain in the weapon slot. Once unequipped, it could not be equipped again.

After an update on 11 September 2014 making random events optional, all existing broken pickaxes were automatically repaired.
