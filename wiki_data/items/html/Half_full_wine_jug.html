Half full wine jugs are rewards given out during various holiday events. Drinking a half full wine jug will restore 7 Hitpoints, and like normal wine, will temporarily decrease Attack by 2.

A regular jug of wine cannot be drunk to make a half full wine jug, nor can two half full jugs be decanted to make a full jug. Interestingly, the half full wine jug heals more than half of a regular jug of wine.

It is not advised to drink half full wine jugs as they are more expensive than most other food items that heal just as much or more. They are more valuable as a collector's item.

Obtaining[edit | edit source]

Previously, half full wine jugs were obtainable during the 2013 Rare Drops event (from 26 August to 2 September) as part of a rare item drop. It did not spawn very frequently and was only found by a few hundred players at the time, due to being paired with the zombie head that spawned a lot more often than it did.

It was then made an annual reward from midsummer events until 2017, where they were occasionally given out by Jagex Moderators en masse via use of items such as the holiday tool.

Half full wine jugs were then given out as birthday event rewards from 2020 onwards, rewarding 2 noted half full wine jugs each year.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Similarly to some other event rewards in Old School RuneScape such as partyhats, the half full wine jug is a discontinued item in RuneScape.
