The Lizardkicker is a type of ale that temporarily boosts Ranged by 4 while draining Strength and Magic by 2 + 4%. Unlike other ales, it cannot be created through brewing.

It can be purchased at the The Cloak and Stagger in the city of Shayzien.

Upon drinking the ale, a message in the chatbox will state You drink the Lizardkicker. It tastes like swampwater, but you feel like your eyesight has improved slightly.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • A herpetarium is a zoological exhibition space for reptiles and amphibians.
  • According to Oswald and Sheryl, the Lizardkicker is made from wheat, hops, yeast, and reptile scales.
  • Lizardkicker is similar to the Ranger's Aid ale in RuneScape because they are both the only drink to boost Ranged.
