Yellow pumpkins are a variety of pumpkin planted by Marika during the 2024 Halloween event. They are one of seven varieties that the player can choose to take from the storage bin, which are carved into one of nine variants at the pumpkin carving table to be placed on the nearby scarecrow.

Upon completing the event, players can choose to recolour their carved pumpkin head into the yellow variety.
