Mixed hide boots are part of the mixed hide armour set, requiring level 60 Ranged and 50 Defence to wear. It provides bonuses that complement the hunter's spear.

Players with level 69 Crafting can create the boots with a mixed hide base and one sunlight antelope fur, alongside a needle and thread. Players without the required level may instead pay 17,500 coins to Pellem at the Hunter Guild to make one on their behalf, whilst providing the aforementioned materials.

This item can be stored in the armour case of a costume room, as part of the mixed hide armour set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
