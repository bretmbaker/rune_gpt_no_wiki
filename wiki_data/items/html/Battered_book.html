The battered book, titled Book of the Elemental Shield is a quest item used in Elemental Workshop I. It is found by searching the east bookcase in the building with a cooking range, south of the building with anvils in it in Seers' Village.

It is read to start the quest. When a knife or any slash weapon is used on it, the player will receive a battered key to get into the Workshop and the book will become a slashed book. The battered or slashed book also acts as a tool in the player's inventory to make elemental shields. After the Elemental Workshop II quest, mind shields can also be smithed.

Duplicates can be drop tricked by searching the bookcase to obtain another. If the player searches the bookcase after creating the slashed book, all copies in the player's inventory and bank are deleted. Duplicates cannot be obtained after creating the slashed book. It can also be found in the bookcase in your house after getting it from Seers' Village.

Transcript[edit | edit source]

The following text is transcluded from Transcript:Battered book.

Within the pages of this book you will find the secret to working the very elements themselves.

Early in the fifth age, a new ore was discovered. This ore has a unique property of absorbing, transforming or focusing elemental energy. A workshop was erected close by to work this new material. The workshop was set up for artisans and inventors to be able to come and create devices made from the unique ore, found only in the village of the Seers.

After some time of successful industry the true power of this ore became apparent, as greater and more powerful weapons were created. Realising the threat this posed, the magi of the time closed down the workshop and bound it under lock and key, also trying to destroy all knowledge of manufacturing processes. Yet this book remains and you may still find a way to enter the workshop within this leather bound volume.
