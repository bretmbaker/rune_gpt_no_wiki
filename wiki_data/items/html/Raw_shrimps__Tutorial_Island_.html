Raw shrimps are a unique variant of raw shrimps that can only be obtained on Tutorial Island. They are obtained by fishing at a Tutorial Island fishing spot. The Survival Expert teaches the player how to fish for shrimp using a small fishing net; they must catch and cook some shrimp in order to proceed to the next part of the tutorial.

A second variant of the raw shrimps could be obtained from the alternate Tutorial Island available to some players in 2020.
