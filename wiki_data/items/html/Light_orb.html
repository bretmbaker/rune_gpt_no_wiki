Light orbs can be used to fix magical lamps in Dorgesh-Kaan. Level 52 Crafting and Firemaking is needed to fix a lamp, giving 500 Crafting experience and 500 Firemaking experience. When a player has replaced 50 light orbs they will receive the message "You have replaced 50 orbs and have gained extra experience." The bonus received is 2,500 Crafting and Firemaking experience. This can be repeated indefinitely. Broken lamps are found on all three floors. The large street lamps throughout Dorgesh-Kaan never need replacing. Broken lamps can be hard to distinguish from a distance. There are always ten broken lamps, and once one is fixed, a new one will break. Broken lamps are unique to each player and are the same for the player no matter what world the player is on.

In addition to attaching cave goblin wire to empty light orbs via the Crafting skill (requires 87 Crafting, granting 104 experience), complete light orbs are sometimes obtained from Dorgesh-Kaan Average Chests, specifically from the chest on the 2nd floor in Oldak's house. Empty orbs are obtained by looting a chest only slightly more frequently than completed light orbs.

A light orb is needed for a Sherlock elite clue scroll step, and one must be crafted for a master clue scroll.

Using stamina potions to replace light orbs will give roughly 90,000 experience per hour in both Crafting and Firemaking. Because of the added cost of stamina potions, instead it may be useful to make fires in the city and take quick glances at nearby lamps in hope of a broken lamp being found.

Treasure Trails[edit | edit source]

  • Fixing a magical lamp in Dorgesh-Kaan with a light orb is required to complete a elite clue scroll skill challenge assigned by Sherlock.
  • Crafting a light orb in the Dorgesh-Kaan bank is required to complete a master clue scroll skill challenge assigned by Sherlock.
