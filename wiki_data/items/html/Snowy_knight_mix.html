A snowy knight mix is a type of hunter's mix that players can drink to restore 8 Hitpoints per dose (16 hitpoints total). If drunk in a multicombat area, up to three players who are standing within two tiles of the drinker will also be healed, provided that they have Accept Aid toggled on.

The snowy knight mix is the only hunter's mix that has a weaker per-use effect than releasing the moth or butterfly directly. The jarred snowy knight is a single-use item that restores 15 hitpoints in a single action, whereas the hunter mix restores a total of 16 hitpoints across two actions.

They are made by using a jarred snowy knight with certain meats acquired from hunter creatures; doing so requires a pestle and mortar, and does not appear to have any skill requirements associated to mix them.
