An essence impling jar is the result of successfully catching an essence impling, while also having an empty impling jar. It requires 42 Hunter to catch an essence impling.

This jar can be traded in singularly to Elnock Inquisitor for 3 empty impling jars.

One essence impling jar of this jar, along with three gourmet impling jars and two earth impling jars, can be traded to Elnock for a magic butterfly net. Alternatively, players can trade three of this jar, two eclectic impling jars and one nature impling jar for a jar generator.

Loot[edit | edit source]

At current Grand Exchange prices, opening an essence impling jar gives 1,001.00 worth of loot, on average. This means opening essence impling jars makes an average profit of −1,553.00 per jar, however this does not include loot from clue scrolls.

On average, one would profit −77,633.00 to get a medium clue scroll from essence impling jars at current Grand Exchange prices, excluding the rewards from the completed Treasure Trails.

The following drop rates are provided by Jagex, unless otherwise specified.

Impling jar[edit | edit source]

An empty impling jar is returned when removing the loot, with a 10% chance of it breaking.[1]
