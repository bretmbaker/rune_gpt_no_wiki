The Armadyl crossbow is a ranging weapon that requires a Ranged level of 70 to wield, and can fire up to and including dragon bolts. It is dropped only by Commander Zilyana. This item counts as an Armadyl item in the God Wars Dungeon.

The crossbow has an attack range of 8, and the longrange combat style will increase it by 2. It is one of two crossbows with a Prayer bonus, along with its upgraded version, the Zaryte crossbow.

The Armadyl crossbow can be combined with a nihil horn and 250 nihil shards to create the Zaryte crossbow.

Special attack[edit | edit source]

The Armadyl crossbow has a special attack, Armadyl Eye, which consumes 50% of the player's special attack energy. Armadyl Eye will, for one shot, double the player's overall accuracy (level + gear accuracy), as well as doubling the base chance of an enchanted bolt's special effect to occur.

For example, when performing the special attack with enchanted diamond bolts, the bolt's special effect, Armour Piercing, would have a 20% chance of activating instead of 10%. If the Hard Kandarin Diary has been completed, the additional chance given from the diary reward is also added,[1] meaning the chance of activating for that shot is increased from 10% to a total of 21%.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • While pretending to form an alliance with Armadyl, Commander Zilyana stole the Armadyl crossbow from Kree'arra during the God Wars, keeping it as a trophy of her cunning, explaining why it is dropped by Zilyana instead of Kree'arra.
  • The Armadyl crossbow is one of four unique boss drops that are not dropped by the respective bodyguards (excluding hilts or pets), the others being steam battlestaff, Saradomin's light, and staff of the dead.
  • The special attack sound is recycled in other content, likely as easter eggs:
    • When cutting trees down, if a bird nest falls from it, there is a 1/250 chance for the crossbow special attack sound to play instead.
    • If using the Dream spell with a jar of dreams in the inventory, the sound will be used alongside The Nightmare's face popping up.
    • There is a small chance that the goose found north of Lumbridge will make the sound when petted.
    • There is a small chance that Huberte will make the sound when talked to.
  • The Armadyl crossbow uses the same model as the hunters' crossbow, albeit recoloured.
