An unfired pot is made by using soft clay on a potter's wheel, which requires level 1 Crafting and yields 6.3 Crafting experience. The unfired pot can be fired in a pottery oven to produce a pot, granting 6.3 Crafting experience upon success. Unlike shaping the clay, firing it has no Crafting level requirement. At lower levels, an unfired pot can crack in the oven, destroying the pot and granting no experience.
