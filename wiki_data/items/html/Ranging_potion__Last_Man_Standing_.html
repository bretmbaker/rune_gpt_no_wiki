A ranging potion is a potion found in the Last Man Standing minigame. A dose of ranging potion provides a temporary skill boost to Ranged equal to 4 + 10% of the player's current Ranged level, rounded down. Unlike normal ranging potions, these lack an "Empty" option.

Effects[edit | edit source]

Ranged boost is calculated with: ⌊RangedLevel×110⌋+4

1-9 +4 50-59 +9
10-19 +5 60-69 +10
20-29 +6 70-79 +11
30-39 +7 80-89 +12
40-49 +8 90-99 +13
