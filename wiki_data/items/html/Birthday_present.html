The Birthday present is an item obtained during birthday events.

  • During the 2014 Birthday event, it was available on a table in Prifddinas, and gave players a cow mask.
  • During the 2015 Birthday event, it was available on a table in Varrock Square, and gave players the cow outfit.
  • During the 2016 Birthday event, players were given a present instead of this item, giving them the gnome child hat alongside the cow outfit and war ship.
  • During the 2018 Birthday event, it was available by completing the event, and gave players all previous birthday event rewards.
