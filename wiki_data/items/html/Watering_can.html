Watering cans are used in the Farming skill to water allotment, flower, and hops patches, as well as to water seedlings to grow saplings. Watering reduces the risk of disease occurring at the end of that growth cycle by varying amounts.[1] During the next growth cycle, the plant moves from being watered back to normal and returns to the standard rate of disease once more, unless it is watered again.

A watering can holds eight doses. It can be refilled at any water supply (except wells), by using a bucket of water, bowl of water, or jug of water on it, or by casting Humidify. Gricoller's can, an untradeable reward from Tithe Farm, holds 1,000 charges.

Watering cans can be bought from farming shops, obtained as a drop from farmers, or withdrawn from a tool store 5.

Watering cans with water inside can be used to extinguish the flame walls in the Chambers of Xeric.
