Green dragon leather is used in the Crafting skill to make green dragonhide armour. It can be created by giving a green dragonhide to a tanner. Tanners can be found in Al Kharid, the Ranging Guild, Forthos Dungeon, Canifis and the Crafting Guild. Alternatively, green dragon leather can be made using the Tan Leather spell. Crafting any piece of green dragonhide armour will grant the player 62 experience per hide.
