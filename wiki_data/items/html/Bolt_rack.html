Bolt racks are ammunition exclusively used by the Karil's crossbow. They are a reward from Barrows with at least 881[1] reward potential, purchased from Ak-Haranu's Exotic Shop on the Port Phasmatys docks or the Last Man Standing shop.

Unlike other bolts, they always disappear after being fired and cannot be retrieved by Ava's devices.
