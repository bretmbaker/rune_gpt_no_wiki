Larran's key is a key obtained from killing monsters while on a Slayer task given by Krystilia. It is used to open Larran's small and big chests.

The exact odds of receiving a key scales with the monster's Combat level. For levels 1-80, the probability follows a curve between 1/1972 at level 1 to 1/100 at level 80.[1] For levels 81 or higher, the odds are between 1/99 and 1/50, scaling linearly and capping at level 350, as reflected in the equation below.[2]

If it concerns a Slayer monster (meaning it requires a certain Slayer level to kill), the drop chance is increased by 20%.[3][4] In addition, any monster killed within the Wilderness Slayer Cave has a further 15% increased chance to drop a key.[5] Keys are dropped on death. Superior slayer monsters that spawn as part of Krystilia's tasks will always drop a key on death.

Larran's key is always dropped on player death, even if there are fewer than 3 items kept on death. If killed by another player, the key is lost to the killer. If killed in PvM, the key always goes to your gravestone.

21 October 2020
(update)
  • All the creatures in the Wilderness Slayer Cave will have a boosted chance to drop Larran's Keys.
  • The keys can now be dropped instead of destroyed. The Destroy option text was "You'll have to fight more creatures to find more keys."
27 June 2019
(update)

Larran's keys will now always be lost on death, and are also destroyed slowly if dropped manually, similar to bank keys.
