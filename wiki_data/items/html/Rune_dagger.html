The rune dagger is a dagger made from rune. Rune daggers can be made from a single rune bar at level 85 Smithing. Each rune dagger created grants 75 experience. Rune daggers are the third-strongest daggers in RuneScape, behind the dragon dagger and the abyssal dagger. Like most rune weapons they require 40 Attack to wield. As with most daggers, this weapon can be poisoned.
