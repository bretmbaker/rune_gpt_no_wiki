Snapdragon is a herb which can be cleaned at level 59 Herblore. It may be grown from a snapdragon seed with a Farming level of 62, granting 98.5 farming experience for every snapdragon grown. Once planted, the herb takes 80 minutes to fully grow.

Snapdragons are used to make super restore potions and sanfew serums with a Herblore level of 63 and a Herblore level of 65 respectively. To create a potion, you need to add the cleaned herb to a vial of water to create a snapdragon potion (unf), and then add the secondary ingredient.

According to the book Gielinor's flora - herbs, snapdragon is native to the rocky areas of Tirannwn, and is supposedly named due to its resemblance of the face of a dragon.
