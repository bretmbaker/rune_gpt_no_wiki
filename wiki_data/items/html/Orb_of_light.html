An orb of light, also known as a Sphere of Light, is a magical orb used in Underground Pass quest. The player destroys four of these orbs in a furnace to progress into the next part of the dungeon. Destroying an orb yields the message, You feel a cold shudder run down your spine as the light of the orb fades.

They were placed in the Underground Pass by the warrior, Randas, in a failed attempt to ward off the darkness prior to the player's involvement.

Orbs of light cannot be banked, and duplicates cannot be held or picked up after that version is destroyed. The item spawn cannot be seen by Telekinetic Grab, however, a player-dropped variant can, but the spell cannot be used on it.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
25 July 2019
(update)

The "Destroy" option was replaced with a "Drop" option. The northern 3 orb of light spawns are replaced by orb of light scenery objects that have the appearance of item spawns and are no longer visible after destroying the orbs.

15 June 2017
(update)

The "Drop" option was replaced with a "Destroy" option. The orb of light scenery object that provides the southernmost orb was changed to have the appearance of an item spawn, as part of preventing a bug that could lead to picking up a Dead orb.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • In RuneScape Classic, each orb of light had a different colour.
