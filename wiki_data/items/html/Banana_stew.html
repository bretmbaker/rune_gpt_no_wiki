Banana stews can be purchased at Solihib's Food Stall on Ape Atoll for 300 coins. Banana stews cannot be made through the Cooking skill.

A banana stew heals 11 hitpoints when consumed. This can be a useful food source when training Agility at the Ape Atoll Agility Course if you are near death, since the stall is near the course.

Banana stews weigh less than bowls (0.25 kg vs 0.453 kg), meaning that upon eating one, the player's weight effectively increases.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Unlike most other food, eating banana stew results in a message You eat the banana stew. even if the game messages are filtered. The message is the same regardless of whether it heals any health. This behaviour is shared with monkey bars and monkey nuts.
