Branch is an item obtained by using a knife or an axe on a spiny bush, giving between 2 and 4 branches. They have no use, and are removed from the inventory upon teleporting.

Prior to the removal of the bog event in Temple Trekking, they were used on the bog to see whether it's firm or not.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • This item can be obtained in free-to-play worlds, where it shows up as a members object
