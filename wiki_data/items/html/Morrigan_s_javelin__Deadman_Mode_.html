Morrigan's javelin is a ranged throwing weapon requiring level 78 Ranged to wield. Unlike other javelins, they cannot be used with ballistae and are a standalone weapon. They may also be retrieved with Ava's devices.

On seasonal and permanent Deadman Mode worlds, they are dropped by any monster found in the Wilderness as part of a global loot table, the chance being relative to the combat level of the monster. It is part of Morrigan's equipment, a set of Ancient Warriors' equipment.

Special attack[edit | edit source]

Morrigan's javelin has a special attack, Phantom Strike, that consumes 50% of the player's special attack energy and applies a damage over time effect to the opponent after the special attack is performed. This special attack has no effect on NPCs.

Every tick (0.6 seconds) after the special attack is performed, the opponent will take an additional 10 hitpoints of damage until 75% of the damage dealt by the special attack has been delivered (for example, if hitting for 40 damage, the target will bleed for 3 hits of 10). When this occurs, the target will receive a message in their chatbox stating "You start to bleed as a result of the javelin strike." Subsequent damage will result in the chatbox stating "You continue to bleed as a result of the javelin strike."
