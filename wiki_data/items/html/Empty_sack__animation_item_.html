The Empty sack is an animation item added to the game with Animal Magnetism. It is wielded by Malcolm during a cutscene in which he attempts to catch an Undead chicken for the player.

It may have originally been intended for players to obtain this item first, then give it to Malcolm to use.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Although the item is referred to internally as an "empty chicken sack", and the cutscene depicts the catching of a chicken, the item's examine states it to be a sack with no cows in it.
