Miazrqa's pendant was given to her by her mother and is of great sentimental value to Miazrqa. The player is asked to retrieve the pendant from a mouse which stole it from her at the witch's house in Taverley. To claim it back the player must first shrink themselves with a shrink-me-quick potion in order to be able to follow the mouse down its hole, and find the pendant on the ground inside. In return for getting the pendant back, Miazrqa will release Rupert from the tower he is being held captive in.
