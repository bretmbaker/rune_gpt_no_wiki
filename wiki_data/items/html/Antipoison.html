An antipoison is a potion made by using unicorn horn dust on a marrentill potion (unf), requiring 5 Herblore, yielding an antipoison(3) and 37.5 Herblore experience. A dose of antipoison cures poison and provides immunity to poison for 90 seconds. Drinking two doses of antipoison will cure venom. The first dose will reduce the venom to poison and the second will remove the poison.

The next cheapest alternative, superantipoison, cures poison and provides immunity to poison for 6 minutes.

After Barbarian Herblore Training, roe or caviar can be added to an antipoison(2), requiring 6 Herblore, yielding an antipoison mix and 12 Herblore experience. An antipoison mix has 2 doses, cures poison and provides immunity to poison for 90 seconds, and heals 3 Hitpoints.

Obtaining[edit | edit source]

  • An antipoison(1) spawn can be found in a chest in the Observatory Dungeon north-east of the stairs leading up to the Observatory.
  • An antipoison(3) can be purchased from The Spice Is Right for 432 coins, Jiminua's Jungle Store for 432 coins, or 374 coins when wearing any tier of Karamja gloves.
  • An antipoison(4) can be purchased from the Apothecary in Varrock for 5 coins, a cadava berry, and a limpwurt root.
  • An antipoison(4) is also a reward from Jekyll and Hyde random events if a marrentill is given to Dr Jekyll.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Antipoison was made available in free-to-play, when Bryophyta was released, since she was supposed to be the first poisonous F2P-monster. However the idea was scrapped and Bryophyta was not made poisonous on F2P-servers, thus making antipoisons redundant in F2P.
