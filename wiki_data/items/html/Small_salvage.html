Small salvage is a type of salvage recovered from small shipwrecks in the Bedabin Basin, using a salvaging hook. 13 Sailing XP is received for each small salvage recovered.

Salvage can be stored in a ship's cargo hold and sorted at a salvaging station to receive various useful drops, although this does not yield further XP.
