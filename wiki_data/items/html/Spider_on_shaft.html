A spider on shaft can be made on Karamja during the Tai Bwo Wannai Cleanup minigame. Kill a jungle spider to get a spider carcass, then use an arrow shaft with the spider carcass. Alternatively, a skewer stick can be used instead, resulting in a spider on stick. The raw version cannot be purchased on the Grand Exchange; only the cooked one is available.

When the raw spider is on the shaft, it can be cooked, requiring level 16 Cooking and granting 80 experience when successful. Players may burn the spider while cooking one, resulting in a burnt spider; the burn rate while cooking these will decrease as players reach higher Cooking levels. It will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 48 on both ranges and fires. When eaten, it heals a random amount of health between 7 and 10, and the shaft is destroyed.

Hudo's shop Grand Tree Groceries sells cooked spiders on shafts for 40 coins, although they are never in stock.

Cooking a spider on a shaft (or alternatively, a spider on a stick) is a medium task for the Karamja achievement diary. It does not need to be cooked on Karamja to complete this task.
