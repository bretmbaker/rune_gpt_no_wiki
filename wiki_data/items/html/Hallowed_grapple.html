Hallowed grapple is an item that has a 100% chance of succeeding a grapple shortcut inside the Hallowed Sepulchre. It can be purchased from the Mysterious Hallowed Goods shop for 100 hallowed marks. The grapple does not work outside of the Hallowed Sepulchre.

The grapple also counts as a Saradomin item for the purposes of recharging your run energy at the Magical Obelisks at the end of each floor, as well as in God Wars Dungeon.

The Hallowed grapple can be stored in the Hallowed Sepulchre Equipment Storage run by the Mysterious Stranger.

Date Changes

(update)

The examine text on the hallowed grapple has been changed to reflect its buff from the previous week's update. Previously, the examine text was "Reduces the chance of failing a grapple shortcut within the Hallowed Sepulchre."

(update)

The Hallowed grapple now has a 100% chance to grapple successfully.
