Yew leaves are obtained from cutting yew trees, at a rate of 1 in 4 when the Forestry kit is in the player's inventory or equipped. They are used in creating forester's rations, as well as compost in compost bins.

They can also be obtained by using secateurs to cure a planted yew tree of disease.

28 June 2023
(update | poll)
  • Yew leaves are now obtainable from cutting yew trees at a rate of 25%.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The price of yew leaves was stuck at the guide price of 17 coins between its previous update on 25 October 2023, and it being manually fixed on 23 October 2024. This was due to a fault in the G.E pricing logic, which was also fixed on 22 October 2024.
