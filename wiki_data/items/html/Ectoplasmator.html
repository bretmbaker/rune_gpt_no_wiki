The Ectoplasmator is a reward from the Soul Wars minigame. Requiring 40 Prayer, it can be purchased from Nomad on the Isle of Souls for 250 Zeal Tokens.

This item can not be taken into Soul Wars.

It has a similar effect to the bonecrusher and ash sanctifier, yielding Prayer experience upon killing spectral creatures, equal to 20% of the monster's max health, rounded down. This does not restore prayer points in the Catacombs of Kourend, as the Catacombs only grant prayer points for bones.

The ectoplasmator stacks with the bonecrusher when killing a spectral monster that also drops bones, like ankou.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Ectoplasm is a term used in spiritualism to describe spiritual energy or substance that is external to living creatures or physical objects.
  • The Ectoplasmator was originally released on 24 October 2012 in RuneScape, as a reward from the 2012 Halloween event.
