Spells are a series of unobtainable items that exist in the game cache. Presumably, they are used in the Spellbook interface. While originally a Spell item existed for every magic spell, since 2014 this has not been necessary.

In an update in 2018 which reworked the Spellbook, the original scroll appearance of the items was removed.
