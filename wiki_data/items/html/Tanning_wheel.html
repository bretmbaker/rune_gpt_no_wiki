Tanning wheel is an item obtained after searching the pile of junk during the 2022 Easter event. It can be used to create the churning machine.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The tanning wheel's examine text references the song You Spin Me Round (Like a Record) by the British pop band Dead or Alive.
