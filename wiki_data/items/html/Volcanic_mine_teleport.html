The Volcanic mine teleport is a magic tablet that can be broken by players to teleport to the Volcanic Mine entrance on the Fossil Island Volcano.

They can be purchased at Petrified Pete's Ore Shop for 200 Volcanic Mine reward points.

Bone Voyage is required to use this tablet, although it is not possible to obtain it without completing the quest under normal circumstances. Attempting to use the tablet without the requirements displays the game message: You need to have completed the Bone Voyage Quest to use this spell.
