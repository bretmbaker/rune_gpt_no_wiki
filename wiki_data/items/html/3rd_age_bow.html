The 3rd age bow is a part of the 3rd age range set. It is a possible reward from elite and master Treasure Trails and cannot be made via the Fletching skill. Requiring 65 Ranged to wield, the 3rd age bow can fire arrows up to dragon arrows at the same speed as that of a shortbow whilst having a longer attack range.

It is considered to be one of the more useful 3rd age equipment pieces; it can be utilised to shoot faraway monsters during Fight Caves and Inferno speed runs and is also valued in the Barbarian Assault attacker role.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room.
