A pointed blamish ochre shell is a 100% drop from the pointed variety of Ochre Blamish Snails in the Mort Myre Swamp.

Players with level 15 in Crafting can use a chisel on the shell to craft it into an pointed ochre snelm, granting 32.5 experience.
