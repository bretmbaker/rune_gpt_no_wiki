The double ammo mould is an item that makes cannonballs twice as fast compared to a regular ammo mould, making 8 every 8 ticks (4.8 seconds) rather than 4 (using two steel bars every interval).

It can be purchased from the Giants' Foundry reward shop for 2,000 Foundry Reputation if Dwarf Cannon is complete. On average, using Steel-Mithril longswords with only optimal moulds (requiring up to 81 Smithing), obtaining one mould should take around 75 minutes of gameplay. With only default moulds, it takes 15 minutes more.

This mould can also be used to create the gold cannonball used in the Between a Rock... quest.

At max speed a player can smelt roughly 6000 cannonballs per hour, or 4,800 if they are also using the blast furnace to create their own steel bars.
