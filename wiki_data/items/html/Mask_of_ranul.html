The mask of Ranul is worn by the Undead Druids in the Forthos Dungeon and can be received from them as a drop.

The mask appears to be a depiction of Ranul, the god of the moon. Its combat stats are identical to a Wizard hat, while also being slightly lighter.

If worn while inspecting the campfire at Shipwreck Cove, Chosen Aluinus will appear and give the message Hmm... Such potential. Such a waste.... He seems to go on a minute cooldown before appearing again.
