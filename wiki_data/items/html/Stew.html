Stew is a type of food that restores 11 Hitpoints when eaten. It can be obtained by cooking uncooked stew on a fire or cooking range, requiring level 25 Cooking and granting 117 experience when successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting in burnt stew. The burn rate decreases as the player's Cooking level increases. Stew is one of a number of foods with a reduced burn rate while using the Lumbridge Castle range with Cook's Assistant completed. It will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 58, or 54 when using the Lumbridge range.

Stew is the starting ingredient for spicy stew.

A maid in a player-owned house can make a stew for the player.

Many stews are needed for the Freeing Evil Dave Recipe for Disaster sub-quest, as well as one stew for the The Queen of Thieves quest.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Using stew on the tree with a guard, located in Draynor near the bank, will cause the guard to thank you and donate 30gp to you for each stew you give him. He will also drop the bowl near by. Note that players cannot give him a second stew until he has dropped the bowl from the tree.
  • When making stews and adding potato it will say "You cut up the potato and put it into the bowl." and when adding meat/chicken it will say "You cut up the meat/chicken and put it into the stew." in the chatbox, even if you don't have a knife in the inventory.
