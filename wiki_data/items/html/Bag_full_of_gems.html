A bag full of gems is obtainable from Prospector Percy's Nugget Shop for 40 golden nuggets, from Belona's Mining Guild Mineral Exchange for 20 unidentified minerals, or from Dusuri's Star Shop for 300 stardust. Each bag contains an assortment of 40 noted uncut gems.

The chances of an individual gem being an uncut onyx is 1/100,000,000, making the chance per bag approximately 1/2,500,000. In terms of probability, this makes it the rarest drop in the game.[1]

The bags are exclusive to the stores they are bought from and therefore cannot be sold to another store in exchange for a different currency.

Percentages[edit | edit source]

These probabilities are based on a sample of 21,237 bags full of gems, all of which were purchased from Dusuri's Star Shop.

Item Quantity Rarity Price High Alch

Uncut emerald1 (noted)40 × 3,459/10,00042030
Uncut ruby1 (noted)40 × 1,202/10,00096760
Uncut diamond1 (noted)40 × 299/10,0002,254120
Uncut dragonstone1 (noted)40 × 50/10,00016,397600
Uncut onyx1 (noted)40 × 1/100,000,0002,460,473120,000

All three variants of the bag full of gems have the same distribution of gem drops.[2]

Based on the above estimated distribution, the average bag full of gems contains 19.960 uncut sapphires, 13.836 uncut emeralds, 4.808 uncut rubies, 1.196 uncut diamonds, 0.200 uncut dragonstones, and negligible uncut onyxes. At current GE prices, the average bag full of gems is worth 21,466.54. The uncut onyx adds just 0.98 coins per bag due to its rarity.

On average, a player can expect 2,496.8 Crafting experience per bag if they were to cut all the gems. If a player were to process the cut gems into jewellery, the expected Crafting experience would increase to between 4,525 and 5,334 experience, depending on what jewellery is made.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The first player to obtain an uncut onyx from a bag full of gems was enneUni on 15 June 2020, more than four years after release.[3][4]
  • Bags full of gems were exclusive to Old School RuneScape prior to being added to RuneScape on 16 December 2024. In RuneScape, each bundle of gems only gives five uncut gems as opposed to 40, and also includes semi-precious gems. However, the chance of obtaining an uncut onyx is much higher.
