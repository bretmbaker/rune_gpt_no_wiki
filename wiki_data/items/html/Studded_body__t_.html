Studded body (t) is a piece of the trimmed studded leather armour and only obtained by completing Easy Treasure Trails or trading. It gives identical bonuses as the regular Studded body, and as such is mostly effective for low-levelled pures. It requires 20 Ranged and Defence to wear.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room, as part of the trimmed studded leather armour. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
