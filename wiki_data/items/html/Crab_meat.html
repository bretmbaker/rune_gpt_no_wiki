Crab meat is a 100% drop from crabs. It is used in Recipe for Disaster to make a cooked fishcake to give to Pirate Pete. Crab meat require level 21 Cooking to cook, and will give 100 Cooking experience. When cooked it will become cooked crab meat. This provides 5 bites of 2 Hitpoints each. It can be burned even at level 60 (rarely).

When used with a pestle and mortar, it becomes ground crab meat.
