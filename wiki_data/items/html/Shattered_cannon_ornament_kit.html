The Shattered cannon ornament kit is an ornament kit that changes the appearance of the dwarf multicannon. This process can be reverted at any time returning the item and ornament kit.

A pack of four can be purchased from the Leagues Reward Shop for 4,500 League Points. They cannot be purchased individually. All four must be used on the various cannon parts to allow the cannon to actually be set up; ornamented and non-ornamented parts cannot be mixed and matched.
