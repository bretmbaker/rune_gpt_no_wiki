The black full helm is a full helmet that requires 10 Defence to equip. As with all black armour, it cannot be made using the Smithing skill. It can be bought at Valaine's Shop of Champions in the Champions' Guild for 1,372 coins. It can also be obtained as a Treasure Trails reward.

A black full helm is used in King's Ransom and, if you're part of the Black Arm Gang, Heroes' Quest.
