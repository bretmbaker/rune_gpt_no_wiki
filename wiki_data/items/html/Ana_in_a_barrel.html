Ana in a barrel is the result of using a Barrel on Ana, which is used to smuggle her out of the mining camp she has been imprisoned in, and is a quest item used by the player during The Tourist Trap quest. If a player drops the barrel, Ana will climb out of it, and she will appear in the mines again. Players who attempt to teleport out while carrying her will drop the barrel first.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Ana herself weighs 22 kg; this can be seen by checking your weight before and after putting her in the barrel. She weighs exactly the same as Zanik although Zanik is a lot smaller.
  • Should players attempt to alchemise the barrel with her, a message appears in the chatbox saying, "You can't do that to the poor girl!"
  • Ana in a barrel's chathead (image) is considerably larger than it appears in the chatbox.
