Sliding pieces are the individual pieces that can be seen in a Puzzle box. The pieces themselves are actually items, although they can only be seen in the puzzle box interface and can never be obtained by players.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
11 January 2018
(update)
  • The item was renamed from "Sliding piece" to an entirely blank name.
  • The item's examine was changed from "A piece of the puzzle" to an entirely blank examine.
  • The "Move" option was replaced with a "Drop" option.
