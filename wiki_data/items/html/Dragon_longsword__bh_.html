The dragon longsword (bh) is a weapon that requires level 60 in Attack to wield. It is created by using a dragon longsword imbue scroll on the dragon longsword, which is purchased as a reward from Bounty Hunter.

In comparison to the regular longsword, the imbued longsword has a +4 increased stab and +6 slash bonus, and a +25% accuracy to the special attack. In addition, the special attack consumes only 15% special attack energy rather than 25% and is one tick faster (2.4 seconds instead of 3.0 seconds).

In addition, it can only be used within Daimon's Crater, Castle Wars, Clan Wars, and Soul Wars; attempting to attack anything outside the listed areas will result in the player being interrupted from doing so.

Special attack[edit | edit source]

The dragon longsword (bh) has a special attack called Cleave, which raises both the player's maximum hit and accuracy by 25%, as well as temporarily increasing the attack speed of the weapon by one tick (from 3.0 seconds to 2.4 seconds) for one hit after performing the special attack; potentially allowing the player to use multiple special attacks in quicker succession than the regular dragon longsword. This consumes 15% of the player's special attack energy.

The accuracy roll for Cleave is based on the attack bonus of the player's currently select style, which is rolled against an opponent's slash defence.[1]
