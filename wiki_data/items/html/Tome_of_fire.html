The tome of fire is a book held in place of a shield that is a possible reward from the Reward cart upon subduing the Wintertodt. The tome of fire requires level 50 in Magic to wield.

The tome is only tradeable when empty. When it is empty, its only function is its magic attack and magic defence equipment bonuses.

Empty tomes can be traded in for 250 burnt pages per tome (value of 402,750) by speaking to Ignisia.

This item counts as a warm item at the Wintertodt.

Charging[edit | edit source]

The tome can hold 1000 pages and players can configure the amount of charges on withdrawal, by right-clicking the tome while the tome is in the bank and selecting Configure-Charges. As long as players have the required pages.

The tome can be charged with burnt pages, with each page adding 20 charges, at a cost of 80.55 per charge. (It costs 1,611,000 to fully charge.)

The tome can alternatively be charged with searing pages, with each page adding 20 charges, at a cost of 667.55 per charge. (It costs 13,351,000 to fully charge)

Pages can be removed from the tome, but if one or more charges are used from a page, the page will be entirely consumed upon removal.

Effects[edit | edit source]

  • When the tome has at least one charge, it acts as an infinite source of fire runes while equipped. Using it as a source of fire runes for non-combat spells does not consume any charges.
  • Casting fire spells with a charged tome equipped will increase the spell's damage by 10% against NPCs and 50% against players (multiplicative with magic damage bonus from equipment) and consume one charge per spell. The bonus applies only to the standard spellbook's fire spells (Fire Strike, Fire Bolt, Fire Blast, Fire Wave and Fire Surge). Other spells that use fire runes such as Iban Blast, Flames of Zamorak, Undead Grasp, Demonbane, smoke spells, and non-combat spells do not benefit from the bonus and do not consume any charges.
  • When charged with searing pages, the minimum hit increases from 0 to 10% of the spell's maximum hit.
Bug:
The +10% damage bonus from the Tome of fire is not included when calculating the minimum hit gained from Searing pages.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
5 March 2025
(update)

The Tome of fire supports a bank item option for changing charges.

5 June 2024
(update) 		Hotfix

The tome of fire now calculates damage correctly during games of Last Man Standing.

29 May 2024
(update)

The tome's damage boost against NPCs has been decreased from a 50% boost to a 10% boost to account for elemental weaknesses.

24 April 2024
(update)

Checking the charges on the tome will now specify whether burnt pages or searing pages were used.

24 March 2021
(update)

The "Add or remove pages" option and the "Add pages" option were both renamed to "Pages" to be consistent across the full and empty tomes.

8 April 2020
(update | poll)

Ignisia will now trade an empty tome of fire for 250 burnt pages, up from 100.

11 January 2018
(update | poll)

You can now talk to Ignisia, found within the Wintertodt camp, to trade in empty tomes of fire for 100 burnt pages per tome.

1 June 2017
(update)

The " Add or remove pages"[sic] option while equipped had the space at the start removed.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • In the Old School Content Poll #46, the tome of fire was proposed to have dragonfire protection as well, similar to an anti-dragon shield. It only received 56.7% votes in favour, 18.3% short of passing the poll.
