A mithril pickaxe (nz) is a Nightmare Zone exclusive version of the mithril pickaxe, and shares its combat stats. It spawns on the ground if Slagilith is present in a Nightmare Zone session, as using a pickaxe is the only possible means of damaging the boss, and the player's Attack level is lower than 40 to be able to equip a rune pickaxe.

As with all items with the (nz) suffix, it cannot be taken outside of the Nightmare Zone, and if discarded, can be retrieved from the same place it spawned.
