Barb bolttips are bolt tips that are made into barbed bolts when used with bronze bolts, requiring 51 Fletching, yielding 95 Fletching experience per 10 bolts. 30 barb bolttips can be purchased for 140 archery tickets from the Ranging Guild Ticket Exchange in the Ranging Guild.
