Marinated jogre bones are a quest item that are made by cooking pasty jogre bones on a range or fire. They are used in the Tai Bwo Wannai Trio quest. They can be buried for 17 Prayer experience each.

Image Examine Components Notes
Burnt Jogre bones marinated in a lovely Karambwanji sauce. Perfect. Burnt jogre bones and Raw Karambwanji paste Required
Jogre Bones marinated in Karambwanji sauce. Not quite right. Jogre bones and Raw Karambwanji paste Useless
