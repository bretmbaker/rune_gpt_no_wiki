Spiked manacles are a rare reward obtained from medium Treasure Trails. They are tied with aranea boots for the best-in-slot strength bonus for footwear that has no requirements to equip. It has equivalent strength bonus to dragon boots and aranea boots, all of which are only surpassed by primordial boots, though without any of the defensive bonuses.

In comparison to climbing boots, which also have no Defence requirement to equip, Spiked manacles have +2 higher strength bonus. However, Spiked manacles have some negative bonuses whereas the Climbing boots have none, and Climbing boots also offer +2 Slash and +2 Crush melee defence bonuses. Aranea boots have slight Ranged and Magic attack bonuses, no negative defence bonuses, and 1 Prayer bonus, making them slightly better than spiked manacles.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room.
