A mirror is used in the Nature Spirit quest to prove to Filliman Tarlock that he is dead. It can be found under the washing bowl on a table near the entrance to the Nature Grotto south of the Mort Myre Swamp. It is also obtainable after the quest, but it serves no purpose.
