The echo Ahrim's ornament kit is an ornament kit that changes the appearance of Ahrim the Blighted's equipment. This process can be reverted at any time returning the item and ornament kit.

A pack of four can be purchased from the Leagues Reward Shop for 6,000 League Points.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
4 February 2025

A fourth ornament kit has been added to players' banks if they bought one from the Leagues shop prior to 22 January update.

22 January 2025
(update)
  • The ornament kit can now be used on Ahrim's staff.
  • Players will now receive four ornament kits instead of three when purchased from the Leagues Reward Shop.
  • The word "robes" in the item's examine text was changed to "pieces".
15 January 2025
(update)

The item is now obtainable.

27 November 2024
(update)

The item was added to the game.
