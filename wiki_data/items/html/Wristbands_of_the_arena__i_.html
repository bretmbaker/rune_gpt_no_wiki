Wristbands of the arena (i) were a proposed reward from the Emir's Arena that were available from the supplies table in a tournament world as a test item for players. They would have been created by using a scroll of imbuing on a pair of Wristbands of the arena. It would have required a Defence level of 40 to be worn, slightly lower than the minimum Defence requirement of 41 to wear Barrows gloves (when factoring quest requirements and experience rewards).

They would have been statistically superior to the Barrows gloves, with slightly higher accuracy bonuses, but could only be used in PvP combat.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Strangely, despite being imbued wristbands, the broken version of this item refers to them as "hardened wristbands".
  • The broken version of this item, "Hardened wristbands of the arena (broken)", had the longest item name in all of Old School RuneScape, totalling 41 characters. The previous record was held by Trailblazer relic hunter (t1) armour set (alongside the t2 and t3 sets) at 40 characters, and the record was next set by Corrupted morrigan's leather chaps (bh)(inactive) with 49 characters.
