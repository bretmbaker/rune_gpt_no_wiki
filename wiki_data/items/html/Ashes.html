Ashes are a common item that are produced when a fire burns out. Ashes are used as a Herblore secondary, and are needed in a number of quests. They are commonly discarded, and can often be found where players are lighting fires for Firemaking training. If a player acquires multiple of Old man's message and starts a new task at the Wise Old Man, the duplicates will explode into ashes when attempting to read them.

Ashes can be crushed into ground ashes using a pestle and mortar.

Uses[edit | edit source]

Quests[edit | edit source]

  • Used in Prince Ali Rescue to make skin paste.
  • Used in and after Shades of Mort'ton to make Serum 207 in combination with a Tarromin potion (unf), to cure Afflicted villagers in Mort'ton.
  • Used in Recipe for Disaster Another Cook's Quest to make a dirty blast.
  • Used in Desert Treasure I to fix the mirrors.
  • Possibly required in "Forgettable Tale..." to obtain a kelda seed from the Rowdy Dwarf.

Other[edit | edit source]

  • Can be crushed with a pestle and mortar to make ground ashes, which are used to dust the character's hands. This is used when throwing a shotput in the Warriors' Guild for better grip.
