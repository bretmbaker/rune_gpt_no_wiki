The sapphire bracelet is made by using a gold bar, a sapphire and a bracelet mould on a furnace. It requires a Crafting level of 23 and gives 60 experience when made. It offers no combat bonuses when worn.

Players can convert this item into a bracelet of clay by casting the spell Lvl-1 Enchant on it, requiring level 7 Magic and grants 17.5 Magic experience.
