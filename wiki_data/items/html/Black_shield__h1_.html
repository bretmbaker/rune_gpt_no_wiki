A Black shield (h1) is obtainable as a reward from easy Treasure Trails. It has exactly the same bonuses as a regular Black kiteshield, and has no significant differences besides the heraldic design on the front. It requires 10 Defence to equip.

Players cannot make this item via the Smithing skill.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room.
