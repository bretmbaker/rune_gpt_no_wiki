A Satchel is obtained during Monkey Madness II. After navigating the maze, the player will discover a crate which they can search. The player needs a total of six satchels to complete that section of the quest. After six are obtained, the player must re-navigate the maze to find the explosives which can only be stored within the satchel.

After all the explosive satchels are placed at weak point, the player can leave the construction site and detonate the explosives.
