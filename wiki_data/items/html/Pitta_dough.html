Pitta dough is created by mixing a pot of flour with either a bucket of water, bowl of water, or jug of water. It can be cooked on a range (not a fire) to make pitta bread.

Unlike many cooking recipes, this item cannot be burned, even though the burnt version exists. This is because players can no longer burn it by the time they have the level required to cook it.[1]

While this item is tradeable, it cannot be sold on the Grand Exchange.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Creating pitta dough used to give 1 Cooking XP, thus it was one of the lowest methods of gaining experience in that skill.
