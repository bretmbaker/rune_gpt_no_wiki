Cooked jubbly is an ogre delicacy obtained by cooking raw jubbly on the spitroast located near Rantz in Feldip Hills or the one located at Jiggig, requiring level 41 Cooking and granting 160 experience when successful. Players may burn the jubbly while cooking one, resulting in burnt jubbly; the burn rate while cooking these will decrease as players reach higher Cooking levels.

Eating cooked jubbly will heal 15 Hitpoints, and one is required to complete the Skrach Uglogwee subquest for Recipe for Disaster.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Its examine text is a reference to the British sitcom Only Fools and Horses.
  • The cooked jubbly is tied for the heaviest food at 10kg, alongside the cooked chompy. It also appears uncommonly large on the ground.
