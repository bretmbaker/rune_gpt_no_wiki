Castle wars supply crates can be purchased from the Castle Wars Ticket Exchange for one plaudit. Plaudits are awarded for Castle Wars games played on dedicated Castle Wars worlds. Crates can not be opened on Entrana.

9 April 2025
(update)
  • Castle wars supply crates have now been re-enabled for purchase and opening.
  • Runes and coins have been removed from the drop table.

(update) 		Hotfix

Purchasing supply crates has been temporarily disabled.
