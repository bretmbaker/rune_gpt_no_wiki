Hallowed sacks are obtainable from Mysterious Hallowed Goods for 100 Hallowed marks. When opened, hallowed sacks give loot found in coffins within the Hallowed Sepulchre.

Each sack gives five rolls from the low-tier loot table, one roll from the mid-tier loot table, and two rolls from the high-tier loot table. The average value of a hallowed sack is 78,253.35.
