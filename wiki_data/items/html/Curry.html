Curry is a type of food that restores 19 Hitpoints when eaten. An empty bowl will remain after consumption. It can be obtained by cooking uncooked curry on a fire or cooking range, requiring level 60 Cooking and granting 280 experience when successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting in burnt curry. The burn rate decreases as the player's Cooking level increases, and it will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 74.

At 50 Construction, a Demon butler can be hired, who can serve curry in Player-owned houses. The number of curries served is equal to the number of people in the house, up to 10.

Curry is popular for fighting the chaos elemental or chaos fanatic as the empty bowl prevents their special attacks from un-equipping Worn Equipment due to a full inventory.

Steps
1. Add a potato to a bowl of water to make an incomplete stew
2. Add cooked meat or cooked chicken to the incomplete stew to make an uncooked stew
3. Add 1 spice or 3 curry leaves to the uncooked stew to make an uncooked curry
4. Cook the uncooked curry on a range to make a finished curry
