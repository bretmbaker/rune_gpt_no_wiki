A pearl fly fishing rod is an equipable version of the fly fishing rod. It is purchased for 120 Molch pearls from Alry the Angler on Molch island. This rod can be used to fish for trout, salmon (both with feathers), and rainbow fish (with stripy feathers).

The pearl fly fishing rod can only be obtained by members, but can be equipped and used in a free to play world.

It can be stored in a magic wardrobe in a player-owned house.
