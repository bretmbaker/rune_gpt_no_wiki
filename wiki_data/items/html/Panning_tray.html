The panning tray is a quest item used in the digsite to pan for gold. One spawns in the tent near the south-eastern shore of the digsite.

It can also be used before the quest if you bribe the Panning guide with a cup of tea.

It is needed in the The Dig Site quest to retrieve a special cup, and there is a chance of finding an opal, which is also needed in the quest.

When panning, players can find gold nuggets, an oyster, an uncut opal, an uncut jade, and coins. Panning provides 2 Fishing experience and 2 Mining experience.
