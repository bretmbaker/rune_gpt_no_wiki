The nest box is an item that holds bird nests. It can be obtained from trading in noted mole claws or mole skins to Wyson the gardener who can be found in Falador park.

There are three different types of nest boxes.

  1. Nest box (seeds) - Contains birds nests with seeds in them.
  2. Nest box (ring) - Contains bird nests with rings in them.
  3. Nest box (empty) - Contains bird nests with nothing in them.

Each box will contain various amounts of nests depending on what each nest contains.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Upon release, nest boxes could be extracted anywhere. However, it was changed in a hotfix in 15 October 2018 so that it could be only be extracted within banks or the Grand Exchange[1]. This was done after players devised a method involving nest boxes, the Humidify spell and herb sacks to create Saradomin brews on-the-go during boss trips.
