The monkey dentures are a quest item used during the Monkey Madness I quest.

Monkey dentures are obtained by searching the stacked crates on the light brown floor inside the house south of the marketplace in Marim, the village on Ape Atoll. In the dungeon underneath this house, you can find the m'amulet mould. After finishing chapter 2 of the Monkey Madness I quest, the player can buy monkey dentures from Tutab's Magical Market while in monkey form.

During Monkey Madness I, the player must take the monkey dentures, a gold bar and the m'amulet mould to Zooknock, the Royal Guard mage, at the end of the Ape Atoll Dungeon. Zooknock will then infuse the magical powers of the dentures into the gold bar and return an enchanted bar.
