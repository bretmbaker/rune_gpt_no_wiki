Runite bolts (or rune bolts) are a type of crossbow ammo. They can be fired using a rune, dragon, dragon hunter, Armadyl, or Zaryte crossbow. Runite bolts provide +115 Ranged strength, making them tied with amethyst broad bolts for fourth strongest type of crossbow ammunition in the game, being surpassed by dragon bolts, onyx-tipped, and dragonstone-tipped rune bolts which provide +122, +120, and +117 respectively. They require a Ranged level of 61 to equip.

Runite bolts can be made by first smithing runite bolts (unf). This requires a Smithing level of 88 and a runite bar. Each bar will make 10 unfinished runite bolts and grant 75 Smithing experience. Attaching feathers to each bolt requires a Fletching level of 69, granting 10 Fletching experience per bolt. As with most bolts, they can be poisoned, however this will prevent the addition of bolt tips.

Unlike all other runite equipment in the game, runite bolts do not use the shorthand 'rune' in their name.
