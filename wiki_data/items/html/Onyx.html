An onyx is the second rarest gem in Old School RuneScape, behind zenyte (which is created from an onyx and a zenyte shard). Cutting an uncut onyx with a chisel requires level 67 Crafting and yields 167.5 experience. It can then be made into onyx jewellery by using a furnace along with a gold bar and a mould.

Onyx jewellery can be enchanted to make some of the most powerful jewellery in the game without any requirements to wear.

Six of these are used to build a volcanic theme at level 85 Construction in a player's superior garden. In addition, one is used to build an obsidian decorative bench at level 98 Construction.

Training[edit | edit source]

While onyxes can be used for training, it is strongly not recommended due to the likelihood of losing coins doing so, and the difficulty of sourcing them in large volumes. Any profit shown below should be treated with extreme caution as it is likely due to the low trade volume of onyxes and unenchanted onyx jewellery.

Magic[edit | edit source]

Onyx jewellery can be enchanted using Lvl-6 Enchant, requiring level 87 Magic. The spell requires 1 cosmic rune, 20 fire runes, 20 earth runes and the piece of onyx jewellery that is to be enchanted.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Onyx was used as one of the tiers of post count badges on the now defunct official Runescape Forums. Players needed to have made 1,000,000 posts to be given the onyx badge.
