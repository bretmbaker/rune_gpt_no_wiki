The Raging echoes mithril trophy is a cosmetic reward that can be obtained by having at least 20,000 League points by the end of the Raging Echoes League. When equipped, the trophy can be right-clicked to perform the Trophy Emote.

The trophy can be displayed by using it on furniture built in the League hall's pedestal space.

If lost, players can reclaim it from the Leagues Assistant, or from furniture built in the League hall's trophy case space.
