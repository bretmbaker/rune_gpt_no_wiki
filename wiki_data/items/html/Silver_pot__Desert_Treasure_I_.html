The Silver pot is a quest item used in the Desert Treasure I quest, obtained by giving Ruantun a silver bar.

It is filled with blood when talking to Malak where he will attack the player for 5 Hitpoints. The damage will decrease so that it does not kill the player and instead leave them with at minimum 1 Hitpoints. Spice and garlic can then be added. It can be blessed at any point (whether empty, with blood, etc.) by talking to the High Priest on Entrana. Once it has been blessed and all ingredients have been added, it can be used to summon Dessous.

Note: Dessous can be summoned with just the silver pot filled with blood, but as long as all the steps are not completed he cannot be killed.

After the player completes their task from Malak and collects the blood diamond, all variants of the pot will be taken. To avoid this, the copies must be dropped before talking to him. If the blood diamond is lost and needs to be reclaimed, the duplicated pots need to be dropped again to avoid confiscation.
