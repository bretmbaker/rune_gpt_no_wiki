The training manual is obtained from Captain Ginea as part of the Tackle organised crime event after giving her an intelligence report, dropped by gangsters and the gang boss during one of their meetings. Reading the book gives the player the option to gain experience in either Hitpoints, Attack, Strength, or Defence. This item cannot be banked. Dying with the book in your inventory will cause it to be permanently lost.
