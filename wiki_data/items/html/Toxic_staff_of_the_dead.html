The toxic staff of the dead is a bladed staff made by using a magic fang on a staff of the dead, requiring 59 Crafting to do so. This process is reversible, provided that the staff is uncharged. It also requires 75 Attack and 75 Magic to wield.

Aside from increased stats, the toxic staff shares the same features as the regular staff of the dead, with one extra benefit; players using combat spells when wielding the staff have a 25% chance of inflicting venom on their opponent. However, if the opponent is an NPC and the serpentine helm is worn in conjunction with the staff, there is a 100% chance of envenoming them. The player can also envenom opponents using the staff's melee attack.

Special attack[edit | edit source]

The staff has a special attack, Power of Death, that halves all of the opponent's melee damage, consuming 100% of the player's special attack energy. Upon activating the special attack, a message will appear in the player's chatbox: "Spirits of deceased evildoers offer you their protection."

The effect lasts for one minute after using the special attack, but is immediately lost if players unequip the staff. In addition, the effect stacks with the PvP damage reduction effect of the Protect from Melee prayer.

Charging[edit | edit source]

The staff can always be used to cast spells, however when charged the staff provides a +8 magic attack bonus and a chance to inflict venom on opponents struck by the staff or by spells cast while wielding it. Similar to Serpentine helm, the staff will immediately use 10 scales when the player enters combat and will use another 10 if the player is still in combat after a minute has passed. A continuous hour of combat uses 600 scales, costing 152,400 coins per hour or 2,540 coins per minute.

Resource Cost per charge
1 x Zulrah's scales 254
Total cost per charge 254

Fully charging the staff with 11,000 Zulrah's scales costs 2,794,000 coins. Players can configure the charges while the weapon is in their bank by right-clicking on Configure-Charges, as long as players have the required materials.

Modifying the staff by removing the fang, or trading the staff to other players is only possible while the staff is uncharged. Players may uncharge the staff at any time to recover all unused scales.

If a player dies with the staff in PvP scenarios and it is not protected, any scales that were used to charge it will appear on the floor along with the uncharged staff.
