Elidinis' ward is a magical shield requiring 80 Magic, Defence, and Prayer to equip. Obtained as a rare drop from the Tombs of Amascut, the ward once belonged to the goddess Elidinis herself, who gave it to Pharaoh Osmumten before disappearing to mourn the loss of her husband Tumeken at the end of the Kharidian–Zarosian War.

The ward can be fortified by attaching an Arcane Sigil to the ward, creating the Elidinis' ward (f); doing so requires level 90 Prayer, 90 Smithing, and 10,000 soul runes, granting 260 experience in both skills in the process. Players cannot pay Abbot Langley to fortify the ward, though he can remove the sigil from an arcane spirit shield.

The fortified ward is untradeable, but it can be dismantled to return the sigil and ward. However, the soul runes will not be returned.
