The berserker ring (i) is an upgraded version of the regular berserker ring. It is imbued using 650,000 Nightmare Zone reward points or 325,000 with the hard tier of Combat Achievements, 260 Soul Wars Zeal Tokens, or a scroll of imbuing purchased for 200 Emir's Arena reward points. Its stats are double those of the berserker ring.

If not protected, dying to another player will cause the imbued ring to lose its imbued status, but the player will be reimbursed the Nightmare Zone points, Zeal Tokens or Scroll of imbuing to re-imbue it via the aforementioned methods. Dying to a monster will not remove its imbued effect.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
10 April 2024
(update | poll)
  • Manually uncharging the Berserker ring (i) will refund 100% of the points/zeal, up from 80%
  • Dying with the Berserker ring (i) that is lost on death will now refund either the points or the Imbue Scroll used to create it.
20 November 2014
(update | poll)

The inventory models for imbued rings have now been updated to be a touch lighter than the standard version of the rings.
