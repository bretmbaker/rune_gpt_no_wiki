The steel arrow is an arrow made out of steel arrowtips, arrow shafts and feathers. A Fletching level of 30 is required to make steel arrows. You also have to have a Smithing level of 35 to make your own steel arrowtips. Steel arrows can be fired from oak bows or better. As with most arrows, they can be poisoned.

Ava's accumulator (from the Animal Magnetism quest) generates steel arrows in the ammo slot, and also randomly attracts items into your inventory.
