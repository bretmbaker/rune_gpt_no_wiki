A fishbowl can be filled up with water, and then filled with seaweed. With a filled fishbowl in the inventory, a tiny net can be purchased from Harry in Catherby for 10 gold. The tiny net can then be used to catch a fish in the aquarium to get a pet fish. This is one of the tasks in the Easy Kandarin Diary.

It is also used in the Recipe for Disaster quest, in which players make it into a fishbowl helmet. This is worn in the helm slot and allows the player to go diving near Port Khazard.

Fishbowls are sometimes used to train Magic with the Lunar spell Humidify, which fills any container in the player's inventory with water. Because the left-click option for a water-filled fishbowl is to empty it, the spell can be repeatedly cast with ease.
