Monodon bones is an item in the Sailing alpha, harvested from the variants of monodon found throughout. Due to the restricted area of the Sailing alpha, and naval combat not yet being enabled, these bones are seemingly unobtainable through conventional means. Monodon bones give 35 Prayer experience when buried. This can be increased by offering the bones in different ways:

105 XP when casting Sinister Offering. 105 XP when offered at the Sacred Bone Burner after the appropriate faith level has been achieved. 122.5 XP when offered at a gilded altar with two burners lit or at the Chaos Temple.
