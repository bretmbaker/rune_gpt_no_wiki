Amascut's Promise is a music track that is unlocked during the fight with Tumeken's Warden and Elidinis' Warden in the Tombs of Amascut .

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • A cover of Amascut's Promise was made available to play in mobile rhythm game Beatstar as part of a collaboration. [1]
