Temple of the Eye is a music track that is unlocked during the Temple of the Eye quest. It plays inside the Guardians of the Rift lobby and before the minigame's preparation round start after the quest completion.

Changes [ edit | edit source ]

Date Changes
16 March 2022
( update )

The track was erroneously released, though it was not yet visible in the Music Player . It was removed around four hours later, following a coldfix for an unrelated issue.
