Darkly Altared is a music track that is unlocked during the fight with Skotizo , beneath the Catacombs of Kourend .

To enter Skotizo's lair and unlock the track, players need to use a completed dark totem in the central altar of the Catacombs. The totem is made up of three pieces which are rarely dropped by monsters in the Catacombs (not including ghosts ). Superior slayer monsters within the Catacombs have a guaranteed drop for a totem piece.
