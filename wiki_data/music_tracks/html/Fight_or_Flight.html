Instruments Tubular Bells, Pizzicato Strings, Timpani, String Ensemble 2, Choir Aahs, Trumpet, Trombone, French Horn, Flute, Pad 1 (new age), Percussion

Fight or Flight is a music track that is unlocked in Mourner Tunnels near the Temple of Light .
