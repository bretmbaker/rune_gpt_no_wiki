Instruments Tubular Bells, Pizzicato Strings, Orchestral Harp, String Ensemble 1, String Ensemble 2, Choir Aahs, French Horn, Synth Brass 1, Flute

Expecting is a music track that is unlocked near the Ourania Altar or by falling through the swamp or from the rope swing in the Underground Pass .

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • According to data crowdsourced via RuneLite in 2020 , approximately 97.5% of players unlock the track at the Ourania Altar, while 2.5% unlock it in the Underground Pass.
