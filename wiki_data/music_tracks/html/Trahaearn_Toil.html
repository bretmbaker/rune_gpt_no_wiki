Trahaearn Toil is a music track that is unlocked upon entering Prifddinas for the first time after completing the Song of the Elves quest. It is also the default track that plays in the Grand Library , the Trahaearn mine , and the entrance to the Zalcano lair. It is the theme of the Trahaearn Clan .
