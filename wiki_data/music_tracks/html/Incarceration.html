Incarceration is a music track that is unlocked during the While Guthix Sleeps quest, during the first interrogation attempt on the shady stranger in White Knights' Castle .

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • This is a port of the RuneScape track of the same name , which was released on 11 March 2008 .
