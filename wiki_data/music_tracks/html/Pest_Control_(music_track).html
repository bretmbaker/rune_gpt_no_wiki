Pest Control is a music track that is unlocked while playing the Pest Control minigame, or during the first part of the naval assault in Dragon Slayer II .

Version Release Music Track

2 6 March 2007

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • According to data crowdsourced via RuneLite, approximately 84% of players unlock the track playing Pest Control, while 16% unlock it during Dragon Slayer II.
