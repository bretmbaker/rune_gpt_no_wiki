Zaros Zeitgeist is a music track that is unlocked in the encounter with Nex , which requires completion of the miniquest The Frozen Door to unlock.

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • The track was originally released alongside Nex on 10 January 2011, being played during the boss fight.
