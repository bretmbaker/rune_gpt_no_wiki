Jungle Troubles (#326)
Released 23 August 2005 ( Update )
Members Yes
Location Near the Jogres north-east or north of Tai Bwo Wannai
Unlock hint This track unlocks north of Tai Bwo Wannai.
Quest No
Instruments Marimba, Xylophone, Percussive Organ, Pan Flute, Ocarina, Steel Drums, Taiko Drum, Reverse Cymbal, Percussion

Composer Ian Taylor
Map
File:Jungle Troubles.ogg 23 August 2005This track unlocks north of Tai Bwo Wannai.

Jungle Troubles is a music track that is unlocked in the jungle north-east of Tai Bwo Wannai near the crashed gnome glider .

Changes [ edit | edit source ]

Date Changes
1 March 2018
( update )

Added unlock hint for this track: 'This track unlocks north of Tai Bwo Wannai.'

23 August 2005
( update )

Added to game and set to play in the jungle north-west of Karamjan Ship Yard , replacing Spooky Jungle . This change does not apply on a cutscene during Tai Bwo Wannai Trio quest.
