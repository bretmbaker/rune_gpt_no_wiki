The Forlorn Homestead is a music track that can be unlocked in Hosidius .

Playing this song to Cecilia may be required for an Easy Clue step.

Location before June 2021
Location in June-October 2021

Changes [ edit | edit source ]

Date Changes
13 October 2021
( update )
  • The Forlorn Homestead has been replaced with Beyond the Meadow in western parts of the Hosidius House.
16 June 2021
( update )
  • The Forlorn Homestead area has been changed to better resemble borders of Hosidius, including:
    • Slight expansion to the south-west.
    • Replacement with Country Jig at some farms south of Hosidius and peninsula east of Hosidius.
    • Expansion to the farms north of Hosidius, replacing Country Jig .
23 May 2019
( update )
  • The Forlorn Homestead area has been changed to play at majority area of Hosidius House including Hosidius town, coast east of it, Mess, plough farms, northern saltpetre deposits and Forthos Ruin as part of Hosidius rework.
10 January 2019
( update )

The Forlorn Homestead has been replaced with A Walk in the Woods in Kourend Woodland .

1 March 2018
( update )

Added unlock hint for this track: 'This track unlocks in Hosidius.' [ confirmation needed ]

17 November 2016
( update )

The Forlorn Homestead has been added to play in Kourend Woodland.

7 January 2016
( update )

Added to game and set to play in southern part of Hosidius House.
