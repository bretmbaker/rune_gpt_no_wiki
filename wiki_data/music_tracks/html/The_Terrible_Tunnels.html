The Terrible Tunnels is a music track that is unlocked during the quest Sins of the Father . It is unlocked when exploring the Meiyerditch Laboratories with Safalaan , and plays in the Laboratories after the quest.

It is based on the track The Terrible Tower that plays in the Slayer Tower .

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • The track was originally released on 22 April 2008 , being played during the quest Legacy of Seergaze in RuneScape .
