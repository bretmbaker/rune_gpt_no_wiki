Zanik's Theme is a music track unlocked during the cutscene of Grubfoot 's dream of Yu'biusk during the Land of the Goblins quest.

While the track is not unlocked until the fourth quest in the Dorgeshuun quest series , it is played in three cutscenes from the second quest in that series, Death to the Dorgeshuun , and in the Yu'biusk cutscene in Land of the Goblins .

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • The track was originally released on 12 November 2007, as part of the original Land of the Goblins quest.
