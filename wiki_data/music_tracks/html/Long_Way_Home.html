Long Way Home is a music track that can be unlocked outside Falador city walls, at the Falador Farm , Crafting Guild , and the Dark Wizards' Tower . In this area, the music player will also shuffle between playing Miles Away and Nightfall .

In Classic mode, the track plays in Rimmington .

Location before 2021 and on Classic mode
Location on Modern mode in 2021-2022 (excluding Falador Rooftop Course)
