Sorceress's Garden (#703)
Released 12 February 2007 ( Update )
Members Yes
Location Sorceress's Garden
Unlock hint This track unlocks in the Sorceress's Garden.
Quest None
Instruments Pizzicato Strings, Pad 2 (warm), Percussion
Duration 01:28
Composer Ian Taylor
File:Sorceress's Garden.ogg 12 February 2007This track unlocks in the Sorceress's Garden.

Sorceress's Garden is a music track that plays at the Sorceress's Garden . It can be heard in the centre of the garden near the fountain.
