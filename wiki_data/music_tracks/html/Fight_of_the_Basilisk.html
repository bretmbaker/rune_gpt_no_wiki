Fight of the Basilisk is a music track that is unlocked when fighting Typhor during The Fremennik Exiles quest. In the next stage of the fight, Reign of the Basilisk will play in this cave, and after completing the quest, Lair of the Basilisk will play in the entire cave.

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • The track was originally released on 1 April 2009, being played during the quest Glorious Memories in RuneScape . It was originally composed by Chris Jolley and was titled "Frost Fight".
