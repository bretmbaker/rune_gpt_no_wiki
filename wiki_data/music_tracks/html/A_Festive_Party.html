A Festive Party is a music track that used to unlock during the 2021 Christmas event , inside Falador city walls. Afterwards, it can only be unlocked by completing future Christmas events .

In Classic mode, the track also played outside Falador walls, replacing Arrival , Fanfare , Miles Away , Nightfall , Scape Soft , Wander and Workshop in total.

Former location on Classic mode

It also played during the 2022 Christmas event in The Nutcracker pub grounds, and during the 2024 Christmas event within Falador.
