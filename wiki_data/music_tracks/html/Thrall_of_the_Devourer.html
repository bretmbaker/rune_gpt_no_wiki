Instruments Bright Acoustic Piano, Electric Bass (finger), Pizzicato Strings, Orchestra Hit, Brass Section, Pad 4 (choir), Sitar, Shamisen, Percussion, Custom Choir
Duration 05:53
Composer Julian Surma
File:Thrall of the Devourer.ogg 27 April 2022This track unlocks during the Beneath Cursed Sands quest.

Thrall of the Devourer is a music track that is unlocked during the Beneath Cursed Sands quest . It plays during the fight with the Champion of Scabaras and the Menaphite Akh .
