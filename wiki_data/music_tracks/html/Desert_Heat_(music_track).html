Desert Heat is a music track unlocked in members part of Kharidian Desert , mostly at sands. In this area, the music player will also shuffle between playing this track, Arabian 3 , Desert Voyage , The Desert , Egypt , Scarab , Sphinx and Sunburn .

In Classic mode, the track plays in Uzer Oasis , Ancient Vault and the area west of these areas.
