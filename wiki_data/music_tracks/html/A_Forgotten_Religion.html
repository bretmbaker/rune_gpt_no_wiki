A Forgotten Religion is a music track that is unlocked upon entering the Forthos Dungeon .

Several tracks use a similar melody to this track, fittingly in areas associated with the Old Ones and Losanium :

  • The Guidance of Ralos
  • The Moons of Ruin
  • The Guardian of Tapoyauik

The track was also used on the unique login screen accompanying the release of Varlamore: The Rising Darkness .
