Gaol is a music track that is unlocked either in Gu'Tanoth or in the Skavid caves . Prior to the track's removal from an area north of the Bandit Camp in the Wilderness in 2021, this track was accessible to free-to-play players.

Date Changes

Gaol has been replaced with Wilderness music genre north of Wilderness Bandit Camp.

( update )

Added unlock hint for this track: 'This track unlocks in Gu'Tanoth.'

15 March 2004
( update )

Added to game and set to play in Gu'Tanoth, Skavid caves and north of Wilderness Bandit Camp.

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • According to data crowdsourced via RuneLite in 2020 , approximately 58% of players unlocked the track at Gu'Tanoth, while 42% unlock it at the Bandit Camp.
  • The word 'gaol' is an old British spelling of the word 'jail'.
