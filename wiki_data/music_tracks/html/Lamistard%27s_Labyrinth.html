Lamistard's Labyrinth is a music track unlocked during The Curse of Arrav quest. It plays within the tunnels beneath Zemouregal's Fort .

It is named after the Mahjarrat Lamistard and the tunnels that he built in an attempt to hide under the ritual stone , but accidentally tunneled into the basement of Zemouregal's Fort.

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • This is a port of the RuneScape track of the same name , which was released on 21 July 2009 for the original release of The Curse of Arrav.
