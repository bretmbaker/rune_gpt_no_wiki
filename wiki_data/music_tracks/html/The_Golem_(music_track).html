The Golem is a music track that is unlocked at the Ruins of Uzer .

In Classic mode, the track also plays at the Orange salamander hunting site.

Location before 2021, current Classic mode unlock area
Modern mode unlock area before 2022
Modern mode unlock area in 2022-2023

Changes [ edit | edit source ]

Date Changes
11 January 2023
( update )

The track's unlock area in Modern music area mode has been slightly expanded to the south-east.

27 April 2022
( update )
  • The Golem track was replaced with Desert music genre tracks at the Orange salamander hunting site and Uzer mine in Modern mode.
  • The hint was changed from 'This track unlocks near Uzer' to 'This track unlocks in the Ruins of Uzer'.
26 May 2021
( update )

On newly released Classic music mode, The Golem track was set to play at its original locations.

28 April 2021
( update )

The Golem track was set to play at Uzer mine .

11 April 2005
( update )

Added to game and set to play at Uzer and sands west of it.
