Beetle Juice
Beetle Juice (#437)
Released 10 January 2007 ( Update )
Members Yes
Location Sophanem Dungeon
Unlock hint This track unlocks during the Contact! quest.
Quest Contact!
Instruments Xylophone, Pizzicato Strings, Timpani, Piccolo, Pan Flute, Ocarina, Lead 3 (calliope), FX 7 (echoes), Sitar, Percussion

Composer Ian Taylor
Map
File:Beetle Juice.ogg 10 January 2007This track unlocks during the Contact! quest.

Beetle Juice is a music track that is unlocked in the battle with the Giant Scarab during the Contact! quest. After the quest, The Depths will play in this area.

10 January 2007

Added to game and set to play at the underground tunnel leading to Menaphos during the battle with the Giant Scarab during the Contact! quest.

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • The track's title is a reference to the movie Beetlejuice .
