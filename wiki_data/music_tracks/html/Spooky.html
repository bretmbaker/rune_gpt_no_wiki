Spooky is a music track that is unlocked at Draynor Manor or during the Gravedigger random event. It could also be unlocked during the 2021 Halloween event .

Although it is presumably not supposed to do so, Spooky is replaced with Scape Soft and Lightness in Modern mode after playing once within Draynor Manor fenced area.

Location before 2022, current Classic mode unlock area

Changes [ edit | edit source ]

Date Changes
27 April 2022
( update )
  • Spooky Draynor area was reduced to only play inside Draynor Manor borders in Modern mode.
  • The hint was changed from 'This track unlocks at Draynor Manor' to 'This track unlocks in Draynor Manor'.
17 March 2021
( update )

Removed ability to unlock this track by scrying through a Draynor Manor portal in a player-owned house.

18 October 2004
( update )

The track was renamed from "Spooky2" to "Spooky".

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • According to data crowdsourced via RuneLite in 2020, approximately 94% of players unlock the track at Draynor Manor, while 6% unlock it during the Gravedigger event.
