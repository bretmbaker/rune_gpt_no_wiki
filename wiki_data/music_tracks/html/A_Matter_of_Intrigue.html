Released 25 September 2024 ( Update )
Members Yes
Location Sunrise Palace / Villa Lucens
Unlock hint This track unlocks during the Twilight's Promise quest.
Quest Twilight's Promise
Duration 01:50
Composer Grace Docksey
File:A Matter of Intrigue.ogg 25 September 2024This track unlocks during the Twilight's Promise quest.

A Matter of Intrigue is a music track that is unlocked during the Twilight's Promise quest. It also plays on the 2 nd floor [ UK ] 3 rd floor [ US ] of Villa Lucens during the Death on the Isle quest, when the player starts the accusations.
