Released 13 September 2023 ( Update )
Members Yes
Location Tree Gnome Village Dungeon
Unlock hint This track unlocks during The Path of Glouphrie quest.
Quest The Path of Glouphrie
Duration 2:44
Composer Dan Atkinson
File:Bolrie's Diary.ogg 13 September 2023This track unlocks during The Path of Glouphrie quest.

Bolrie's Diary is a music track that is unlocked during the quest The Path of Glouphrie . It plays whilst viewing the cutscenes of King Bolrie 's diary.
