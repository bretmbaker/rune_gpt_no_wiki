Shattered Relics (#702)
Released 19 January 2022 ( Update )
Members No
Location None
Unlock hint This track unlocks automatically.
Quest None
Instruments Acoustic Grand Piano, Celesta, Music Box, Tubular Bells, Drawbar Organ, Pizzicato Strings, Choir Aahs, Pad 2 (warm), Pad 4 (choir), Pad 5 (bowed), FX 2 (soundtrack), Reverse Cymbal, Percussion
Duration 01:53
Composer James Rodgers
File:Shattered Relics.ogg 19 January 2022This track unlocks automatically.

Shattered Relics is a music track that played during opening cinematic cutscene of the Shattered Relics League . After an update on 27 April 2022 , it is automatically unlocked for all players.

Changes [ edit | edit source ]

Date Changes
27 April 2022
( update )

The music track is now listed in the Music Player .
