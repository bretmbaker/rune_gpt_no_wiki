The Chosen is a music track that is unlocked during the Recruitment Drive quest .

Version Release Music Track
1 31 May 2005

Analysis [ edit | edit source ]

The song takes the form of a very happy, epic piece of music driven by heavy brass and drums at a reasonable pace.
