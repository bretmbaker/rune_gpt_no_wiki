Xenophobe is a music track that is unlocked at the Waterbirth Island Dungeon . It plays in the deeper area from rooms 12-15, and in the lairs of the Dagannoth Kings .

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • A xenophobe is someone who has a dislike of foreigners, or more generally a fear of something foreign or strange.
