Instruments Acoustic Grand Piano, Electric Piano 1, Tubular Bells, Church Organ, Fretless Bass, Tremolo Strings, Timpani, French Horn, Pad 4 (choir), FX 3 (crystal), Taiko Drum, Reverse Cymbal, Percussion

File:Welcome to my Nightmare.ogg 7 June 2018This track unlocks in the Theatre of Blood.

Welcome to my Nightmare is a music track that is unlocked upon entering the Pestilent Bloat 's room in the Theatre of Blood .

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • The title of this track could be a reference to the album of the same name by Alice Cooper
