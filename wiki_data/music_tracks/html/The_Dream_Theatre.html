The Dream Theatre is a music track that is unlocked during the Defender of Varrock quest, when the player communes with the Sacred Forge .

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • This song is likely a reference to the American progressive metal band Dream Theater , since there are numerous key changes in the song, an aspect that Dream Theater frequently use in their works.
