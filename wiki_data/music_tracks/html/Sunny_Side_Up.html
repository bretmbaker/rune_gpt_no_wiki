Sunny Side Up is a music track unlocked during the 2024 Easter event at the Grand Exchange .

Changes [ edit | edit source ]

Date Changes
26 April 2024
Hotfix

The track is now automatically unlocked for players, after it was re-locked for various players who've already unlocked the track after the event was removed.

3 April 2024

Fixed a bug that prevented the track to be unlocked.
