Released 28 November 2005 ( Update )
Members Yes
Location Port Sarim 's Rat Pits
Unlock hint This track unlocks during the Ratcatchers quest.
Quest Ratcatchers
Instruments Marimba, Accordion, Tango Accordion, Acoustic Guitar (steel), Pizzicato Strings, English Horn, Piccolo, Fiddle, Percussion
Duration 03:19
Composer Ian Taylor
Map
File:Sarim's Vermin.ogg 28 November 2005This track unlocks during the Ratcatchers quest.

Sarim's Vermin is a music track that is unlocked by speaking to Felkrash at the end of the Ratcatchers quest. It can later be heard at the Port Sarim rat pits.
