Hells Bellsis amusictrack that is unlocked while sledding downTrollweiss Mountain. The player only needs to have completed theDeath Plateauquest to access the mountain.

The track can also be unlocked without completing Death Plateau via the entrance toKeldagrim, (south-west of fairy ringDKSand north-east ofRellekka). When entering the dungeon, do not crawl through the crack; instead, follow the southern path through thetrollsto the entrance of the snowy area. The track is unlocked by approaching the snowy area at the bottom of the slope.

Playing this song toCeciliamay be required for aHard Cluestep.
