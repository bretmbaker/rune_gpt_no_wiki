Still Nightis amusictrack that can be unlocked at locations outsideVarrockcity walls, including theCooks' Guild,Champions' Guild, andJolly Boar Inn. In this area, the music player will also shuffle between playingDoorways,Expanse, andGreatness.

In Classic mode, the track plays at themining areato the south-east ofVarrock.
