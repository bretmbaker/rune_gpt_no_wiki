You Have My Attentionis amusictrack that is unlocked during thequestSins of the Father. It plays during the cutscene whereLowerniel Drakanconfronts the player.

The track is split into two distinctive parts, the last part bears resemblance toA Forgotten Religion. During the cutscene, the track skips to the second part when Lowerniel starts subdueing the Vyrewatch Sentinel.

Trivia[edit|edit source]

The cache id number of the track is 666, which is a reference to the "Number of the Beast", as well as thecombat levelof Lowerniel Drakan inRuneScape; fittingly the track is played at Lowerniel Drakan's first appearance in the game.
