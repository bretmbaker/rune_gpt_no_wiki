Sea Shantyis amusictrack that unlocks aroundMusa PointandKaramja Volcano. In this area, the music player will shuffle between playing this track andJungle Island.

In Classic mode, the track plays atMusa Pointand its docks.

Changes[edit|edit source]

Date | Changes

26 May2021(update) | On newly released Classic music mode, Sea Shanty was set to play at its original Karamjan location as well as Musa Point docks.

28 April2021(update) | Sea Shanty was made part of Musa Point music genre. This also affects a tiny coastal area near Rimmington, setting to play nothing there unless already playing a track at the same session.

1 March2018(update) | Added unlock hint for this track: 'This track unlocks at Musa Point.'
