Faerieis amusictrack that is unlocked in westernZanarisand outside theCosmic Altar. To get there, the player must have completed theLost Cityquest.

It may also play in eastern Zanaris if moving near wheat field to playImpulsesand then moving out. It will not shuffle withCrystal Cavethere, though.

Playing this song toCeciliamay be required for aMedium Cluestep.
