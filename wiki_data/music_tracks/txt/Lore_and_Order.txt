Lore and Orderis amusictrack that is unlocked at theSeers' Village Courthouse, visited during theKing's Ransomquest.

Analysis[edit|edit source]

It is a remix of theKnightlytrack. There are only very few differences between the songs, with Lore and Order using most of Knightly's chords and sections.

Trivia[edit|edit source]

The title is a play on the phraselaw and order.Along withKnightmare, it was the last music track to be added to the game before the creation of theAugust 2007 Archive of RuneScape.
