Trivia[edit|edit source]

Although the track was released with the Music Player, it was not unlockable by players until the Underground Pass was added to Runescape 2 on15 April2004.Prior to an update on26 October2004, this track was calledUpass1.According to datacrowdsourcedvia RuneLite, approximately 97% of players unlock the track in the grid's pitfall trap, while 3% unlock it in the Underground Pass second area. (i.e. 3% of players clear the grid on their first attempt)
