The Terrible Tunnelsis amusictrack that is unlocked during thequestSins of the Father. It is unlocked when exploring theMeiyerditch LaboratorieswithSafalaan, and plays in the Laboratories after the quest.

It is based on the trackThe Terrible Towerthat plays in theSlayer Tower.

Trivia[edit|edit source]

The track was originally released on22 April2008, being played during the questLegacy of SeergazeinRuneScape.
