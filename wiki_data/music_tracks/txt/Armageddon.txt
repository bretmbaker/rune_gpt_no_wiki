Trivia[edit|edit source]

This music track seems to contain elements of the other God Wars Dungeon tracks. If the player listens closely, parts of it sound very similar toArmadyl Alliance,Bandos Battalion,Strength of Saradomin, andZamorak Zoo.The name of the song may refer to the site where an epic battle takes place at the end of times, according to the Abrahamic religions (Judaism, Christianity and Islam). At the place where this song is unlocked, theGod Wars Dungeon, an epic battle takes place as well.According to datacrowdsourcedviaRuneLite, approximately 71% of players unlock the track in the God Wars Dungeon, while 29% unlock it in the Wilderness God Wars Dungeon.
