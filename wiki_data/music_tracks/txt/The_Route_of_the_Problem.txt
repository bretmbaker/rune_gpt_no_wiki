The Route of the Problemis amusictrack that is unlocked during theWhile Guthix Sleepsquest, during the mercenary attack onMcGrubor's Wood.

It is a slower-paced variation ofThe Route of All Evil, making heavy use of harmonies.

Trivia[edit|edit source]

This is a port of theRuneScape track of the same name, which was originally released on15 January 2008.
