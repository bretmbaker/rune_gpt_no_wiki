Dreamis amusictrack that is unlocked when the player first arrives in Lumbridge. It can also be heard around the Lumbridge Swamp and farmlands. In this area, the music player will also shuffle between playingAutumn Voyage,Book of Spells,Flute Salad,Harmony, andYesteryear.

In Classic mode, the track plays on the path fromLumbridgetoDraynor Village.
