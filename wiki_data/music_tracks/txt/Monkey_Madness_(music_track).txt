Monkey Madnessis amusictrack that is unlocked at the island ofApe Atoll. In this area, the music player will also shuffle between playingAnywhereandIsland Life. It can later be heard at the airship platform duringMonkey Madness II.

In Classic mode, this track plays inMarim.
