Attack 2is amusictrack that is unlocked inElvarg's lair in theCrandor and Karamja Dungeon. It can later be heard inDragon Slayer II, during the cutscene of theDragonkinbattle on Lithkren.

It is the second in a set of six tracks known as the "Attack" series.
