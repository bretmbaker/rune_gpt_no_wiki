Fangs for the Memoryis amusictrack that is unlocked duringIn Aid of the Myreque, when the player transportsIvan Stromthrough theMort Myre swampand fights theVampyre Juvinates.

It can later be heard duringDarkness of Hallowvale, when Vanstrom Klause ambushes the player onCastle Drakan.

It plays again duringSins of the Father, while fighting the Vampyre Juvinates when escorting Ivan back through the swamp.
