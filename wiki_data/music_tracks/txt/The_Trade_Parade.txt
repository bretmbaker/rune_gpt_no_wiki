Trivia[edit|edit source]

The track was originally added to the game files on27 September2005, as the theme for anunreleased minigameset in the Trade Octagon. The minigame, which consisted of speculating on the prices of ores and bars, was not completed due to the developer's resignation.[1]A short, unlisted jingle plays when a buy/sell offer is completed and it uses the beginning melody in this track with other elements added.
