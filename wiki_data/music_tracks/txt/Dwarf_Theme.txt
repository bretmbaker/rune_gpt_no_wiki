Dwarf Themeis amusictrack that is unlocked near the surface camp above theDwarven Mine.

In Classic mode, the track plays in a wider area around the Dwarven Mine, on the path betweenFaladorand theBarbarian Village.

Changes[edit|edit source]

Date | Changes

26 May2021(update) | On newly released Classic music mode, Dwarf Theme has been set to play at its original location.

28 April2021(update) | Dwarf Theme has been expanded in southern part of Ice Mountain but replaced with north-east Asgarnia music genre tracks in areas outside the mountain.The hint has been changed from 'This track unlocks in the Dwarven Mines.' to 'This track unlocks at the entrance to the Dwarven Mines.'.

1 March2018(update) | Added unlock hint for this track: 'This track unlocks in the Dwarven Mines.'

6 March2007(update) | The track was renamed from 'Gnome Theme' to 'Dwarf Theme'.The file ID changed from 150 to 310, and the version was reset to 2.

15 March2004(update) | Added to game as 'Gnome Theme' and set to play at the Dwarven Mine, west of Barbarian Village.

Trivia[edit|edit source]

Until4 June2007, the track was known as Gnome Theme. This was presumably changed due to the fact that thegnomerace is not found in the area.
