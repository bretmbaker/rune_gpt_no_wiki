Winter Funfairis amusictrack that used to unlock during the2017 Christmas event, upon enteringSanta's workshop with theWise Old Man. Afterwards, it can only be unlocked by completing futureChristmas events.

It also played during the2022 Christmas eventinThe Nutcrackerpub grounds, and during the2024 Christmas eventwithin Falador.

Trivia[edit|edit source]

The track was originally released on16 December2008, being played during the quest Myths of the White Lands inRuneScape. It was originally titled "Winter Funfare".
