Scape Sadis amusictrack that is unlocked in theWildernessor during the ending cutscene ofDragon Slayer II. It also plays inWilderness Agility Course Dungeon.

In Classic mode, the track plays at theFountain of Runeand at theDemonic Ruinsin the Wilderness.
