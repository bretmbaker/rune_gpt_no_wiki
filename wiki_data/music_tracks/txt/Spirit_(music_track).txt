Spiritis amusictrack that can be unlocked insideVarrockcity walls, excluding theGrand Exchange. It also plays in thePhoenix Gang Hideoutand in the basement of theBlue Moon Inn. In this area, the music player will also shuffle between playingAdventure,Garden, andMedieval.

In Classic mode, this track plays in westernVarrock.
