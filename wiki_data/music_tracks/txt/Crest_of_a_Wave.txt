[Music Track: Crest_of_a_Wave]

Crest of a Waveis a music track that is unlocked automatically.

DuringThe Giant Dwarf, it can be heard during the cutscene when the player and theDwarven Boatmanfirst sail toKeldagrim. InDragon Slayer I, it plays during the cutscene when sailing toCrandorwithCaptain Ned.
