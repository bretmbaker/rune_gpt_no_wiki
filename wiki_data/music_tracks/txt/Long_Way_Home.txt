Long Way Homeis amusictrack that can be unlocked outsideFaladorcity walls, at theFalador Farm,Crafting Guild, and theDark Wizards' Tower. In this area, the music player will also shuffle between playingMiles AwayandNightfall.

In Classic mode, the track plays inRimmington.
