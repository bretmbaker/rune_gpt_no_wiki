Analysis[edit|edit source]

Like most music tracks unlocked in theKharidian Desert, City of the Dead draws influence from real-worldMiddle Eastern music. It is a slow, somewhat somber track, with some faster periods, reflecting Sophanem's struggling state following the arrival ofAmascut's plagues.
