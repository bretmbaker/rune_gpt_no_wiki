Attack 6is amusictrack that is unlocked at theGu'TanothOgre Enclavelair. It can also be unlocked and heard in the questThe Ribbiting Tale of a Lily Pad Labour Dispute, when the player fightsCuthbert, Lord of Dread.

It is the sixth track in the series of six "Attack" music tracks.
