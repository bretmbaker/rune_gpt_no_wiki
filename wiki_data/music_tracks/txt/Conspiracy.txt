Conspiracyis amusictrack that is unlocked while spying onRanis DrakanandVanstrom KlauseinVer SinhazaduringA Taste of Hope. It can later be heard in the basement of Serafina's house.

The track returns inSins of the Father. It is heard first when followingCarl, and again during the cutscene whenDamien Leucurteis confronted.

Trivia[edit|edit source]

The track was originally released on22 April2008, being played during the quest Legacy of Seergaze inRuneScape. It was originally titled "Conspiracy: Part 1".The track has been extended from 2 minutes and 8 seconds to 4 minutes and 18 seconds probably with the Darkmeyer update released on4 June2020.
