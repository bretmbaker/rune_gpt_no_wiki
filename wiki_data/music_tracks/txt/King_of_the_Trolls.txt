King of the Trollsis amusictrack that plays duringThe Fremennik Isles. It can be heard during the battle that takes places in theIce Troll Caves.

It can later be heard in theNightmare Zonewhen fighting theIce Troll King. Originally, the name "Ice Troll King" would appear in the music player, but no longer does so since 28 April 2021.
