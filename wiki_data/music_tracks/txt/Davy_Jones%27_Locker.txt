Trivia[edit|edit source]

The title of the track is an idiom fordeath at sea. The name Davy Jones is a nickname for what would be the devil, saint, or god of the seas.Unlike most songs in theRuneScapesoundtrack, Davy Jones' Locker is written in 3/4 time.
