Miles Awayis amusictrack that can be unlocked outsideFaladorcity walls, at theFalador Farm,Crafting Guild, and theDark Wizards' Tower. In this area, the music player will also shuffle between playingLong Way HomeandNightfall. It can also be unlocked in theFisher Realmafter partial completion ofHoly Grailquest.

In Classic mode outside the Fisher Realm, the track plays at theCrafting Guild.
