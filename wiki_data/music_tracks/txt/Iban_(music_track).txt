Trivia[edit|edit source]

Although the track was released with the Music Player, it was not unlockable by players until the Underground Pass was added toRuneScapeon15 April2004.The first version of the track featured a longer introduction and simply faded out at the end. At some point in January2006the track was updated to its current version.
