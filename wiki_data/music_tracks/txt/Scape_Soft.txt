Scape Softis amusictrack that is unlocked in the north-east ofAsgarnia, aroundIce Mountainor theMonasteryor east ofFalador. In this area, the music player will shuffle between playing this track andLightness.

In Classic mode, the track plays in a smaller area just north ofFalador.

Although it is presumably not supposed to do so, Scape Soft along withLightnessreplacesSpookywithinDraynor Manorfenced area after the track playing once in Modern mode.
