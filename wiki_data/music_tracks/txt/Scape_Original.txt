Scape Originalis amusictrack from the early days ofRuneScape 2. It was the first login screen music track before being replaced withScape Main. It is now unlocked automatically by all players upon their first login. It was also the track that played in theRealm of Memoriesduring the20th Anniversary event.

This music track is considered to be the "theme song" ofRuneScape. Many ofRuneScape's other songs make references to it, including Scape Main.
