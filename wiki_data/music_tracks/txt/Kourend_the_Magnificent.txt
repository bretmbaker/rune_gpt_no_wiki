Kourend the Magnificentis amusictrack unlocked upon arriving toGreat Kourend. It mainly plays inThe Legless Faun,KingstownandKourend Castle. The lyrics can be found by reading the inscription on the pillar in the centre of the courtyard.

Trivia[edit|edit source]

This composition seems to draw some elements from "Battle Hymn of the Republic."From the rhythm of the song it can be inferred that the part that was sung to the lyrics appears twice: from 0:06 to 1:44 and from 3:00 to 4:38.
