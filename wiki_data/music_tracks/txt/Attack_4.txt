Attack 4is amusictrack that is unlocked at theFight Arena. It can also be unlocked in theIorwerth Campcave duringSong of the Elves, or in parts of theYanille Agility Dungeon.

It is the fourth track in the series of six "Attack" music tracks.

Trivia[edit|edit source]

According to datacrowdsourcedvia RuneLite in2020, approximately 99.4% of players unlock the track at the Fight Arena. 0.4% unlock it during Song of the Elves, while 0.2% unlock it in the Yanille Agility Dungeon.Previously, you did not need to do Fight Arena to unlock this track. You simply had to walk into the vicinity of the Fight Arena. This was later changed to play within the quest only.
