Wild Isleis amusictrack that can be unlocked in theWildernessand also plays inWilderness Agility Course Dungeon.

Prior to anupdateon28 April2021, the track could be unlocked south ofLava Dragon Islein theWilderness. However, it can still be heard there if using Classic music mode.

Analysis[edit|edit source]

This is a disturbing, tension-building piece of music that is mostly quiet and features the sound of a raging storm with scattered, soft piano fading in and out randomly, the sound of growling, and a few eerie sound effects here and there.
