Box of Delightsis amusictrack that is unlocked during the fight withthe Mimic.

This is one of the more challenging music tracks to unlock, mainly for players making progress to theMusic cape. To access the Mimic fight and unlock the track, players must show amimic casketto thestrange casketinWatson's house. A mimic casket can be obtained fromeliteandmaster casketsduringtreasure trailsat 1/35 and 1/15 rates, respectively. Players must also ensure their ability to receive a mimic is enabled via the strange casket.
