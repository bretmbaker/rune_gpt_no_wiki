A Festive Partyis amusictrack that used to unlock during the2021 Christmas event, insideFaladorcity walls. Afterwards, it can only be unlocked by completing futureChristmas events.

In Classic mode, the track also played outside Falador walls, replacingArrival,Fanfare,Miles Away,Nightfall,Scape Soft,WanderandWorkshopin total.

It also played during the2022 Christmas eventinThe Nutcrackerpub grounds, and during the2024 Christmas eventwithin Falador.
