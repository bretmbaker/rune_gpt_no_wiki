Expanseis amusictrack that can be unlocked at locations outsideVarrockcity walls, including theCooks' Guild,Champions' Guild, andJolly Boar Inn. In this area, the music player will also shuffle between playingDoorways,Greatness, andStill Night.

In Classic mode, this track plays south of Varrock and in thePhoenix Gang Hideout.
