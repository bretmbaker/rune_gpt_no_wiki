Jungle Islandis amusictrack that unlocks aroundMusa PointandKaramja Volcano. In this area, the music player will shuffle between playing this track andSea Shanty. It can also be unlocked near the entrance to theKharazi Junglesouth ofShilo Village.

If using Classic music mode infree-to-playKaramjapart, it only plays at theKaramja Volcanoas well as coast north of it.
