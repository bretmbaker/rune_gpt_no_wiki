Arabianis amusictrack that is unlocked inAl Kharid, at theAl Kharid mineand theGiants' Plateau. In this area, the music player will also shuffle between playing this track,Al KharidandArabian 2.

In Classic mode, the track plays in several locations in northern Kharidian Desert:

On the path to theEmir's Arenanorth ofAl Kharid.At theCitharede Abbey.At theUzer mine.

Trivia[edit|edit source]

According to datacrowdsourcedviaRuneLitein2020, approximately 98.2% of players unlocked the track north of Al Kharid, whereas only 1.8% unlocked it elsewhere. The most common method of doing so was by teleporting using aNecklace of passage.
