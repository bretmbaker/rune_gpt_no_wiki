Aloneis amusictrack unlocked atIce Mountain, or in theClock Tower Dungeon.

In Classic mode, the track plays in a wider area around the Ice Mountain, as well as theEdgeville Monastery, as described in the first map with obvious square-like borders (not the next one) in case its caption is not visible.
