Trivia[edit|edit source]

Players can unlock the track in the Edgeville Dungeon by never visiting Edgeville, and entering the dungeon from theVarrock Sewersor with theBrass key. According to datacrowdsourcedvia RuneLite, approximately 1.2% of players unlock the track in this manner, as opposed to 98.8% at Edgeville.
