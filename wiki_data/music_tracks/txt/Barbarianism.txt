Trivia[edit|edit source]

Before an update on2021, to unlock the track in the Edgeville Dungeon, players had to enter the dungeon in Edgeville then take a very long route to reach the Hill Giants. According to datacrowdsourcedviaRuneLite, approximately 0.4% of players unlocked the track in this way, as opposed to 99.6% at the Barbarian Village.
