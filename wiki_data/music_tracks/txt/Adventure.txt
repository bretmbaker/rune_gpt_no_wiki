Adventureis amusictrack that can be unlocked insideVarrockcity walls, excluding theGrand Exchange. It also plays in thePhoenix Gang Hideoutand in the basement of theBlue Moon Inn. Within this area, the music player will also shuffle between playingGarden,Medieval, andSpirit.

In Classic mode, this track plays inVarrock Palace.
