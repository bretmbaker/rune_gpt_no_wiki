Incantationis amusictrack that is unlocked duringShadow of the Stormin the cutscene showing the summoning ofAgrith Naar. The track temporarily replacesThe Golem,Forgotten, andThrone of the Demonwhen Agrith Naar is not yet slain.

Analysis[edit|edit source]

It begins with plucked strings and melo toms. French horns start off with the melody while strings enter in with a countermelody. Later in the song, the flute takes the countermelody.
