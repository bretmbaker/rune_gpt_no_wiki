Knightmareis amusictrack that is unlocked at theBlack Knights' Fortress. It is also played during the escape fromKeep Le Fayeduring theKing's Ransomquest.

Prior to anupdateon28 April2021, the track could be unlocked in a small area around the fortress, and would keep playing anywhere within the same64x64 tile square, even when walking outside of the unlock area. Leaving the 64x64 tile area would cause the normal track to start playing again.

Trivia[edit|edit source]

The name of the song is a pun on the word "nightmare".Along withLore and Order, it was the last music track to be added to the game before the creation of theAugust 2007 Archive of RuneScape.
