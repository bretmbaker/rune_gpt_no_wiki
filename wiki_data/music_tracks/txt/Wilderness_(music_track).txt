Wildernessis amusictrack that is unlocked in theWildernessand also plays inWilderness Agility Course Dungeon.

If using Classic mode, it plays at theHobgoblin mine, west ofDark Warriors' Fortress, north ofGrand Exchange, as well as inDaimon's Crater.
