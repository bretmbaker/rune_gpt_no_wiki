Trivia[edit|edit source]

In the Bible, Fire and Brimstone (or, alternately, brimstone and fire, translated from the Hebrew גפרית ואש) are signs of God's wrath. They often appear in reference to the fate of the unfaithful. Brimstone is possibly the ancient name for sulphur.
