Nightfallis amusictrack that can be unlocked outsideFaladorcity walls, at theFalador Farm,Crafting Guild, and theDark Wizards' Tower. In this area, the music player will also shuffle between playingLong Way HomeandMiles Away.

In Classic mode, the track plays north ofRimmington.
