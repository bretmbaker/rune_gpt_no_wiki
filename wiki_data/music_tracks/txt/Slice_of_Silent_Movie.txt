Trivia[edit|edit source]

The name comes from the cliché during old silent movies where a hero would have to defeat a villain while the female was tied to the train tracks to be run over. The female, in this case, isZanik; and the villain,Sigmund. The "Slice of" is taken from the name of the quest:Another Slice of H.A.M..
