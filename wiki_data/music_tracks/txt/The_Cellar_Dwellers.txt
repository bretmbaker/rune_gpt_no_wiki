Trivia[edit|edit source]

The track is also scripted to play in the inaccessible section of the Ardougne Sewers; however, this can never be heard by players.[1]According to datacrowdsourcedvia RuneLite in2020, approximately 72% of players unlock the track in Elena's prison, 26% unlock it in the Ardougne Sewers, 2% unlock it in The Warrens, while only 0.16% unlock it in the Carnillean Mansion.
