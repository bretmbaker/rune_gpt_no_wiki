Land of Snowis amusictrack that used to unlock during the2017 Christmas event, in theLand of Snow. Afterwards, it can only be unlocked by completing futureChristmas events. It is an adaptation of the classical piece "Jupiter, the Bringer of Jollity", from the orchestral suiteThe Planets, by Gustav Holst.

It also played during the2022 Christmas eventinThe Nutcrackerpub grounds, and during the2024 Christmas eventwithin Falador.

Trivia[edit|edit source]

The track was originally released on18 December2007, being played during the2007 Christmas eventinRuneScape.
