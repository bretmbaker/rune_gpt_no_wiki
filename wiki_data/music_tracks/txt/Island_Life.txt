Island Lifeis amusictrack that is unlocked at the island ofApe Atoll. In this area, the music player will also shuffle between playingAnywhereandMonkey Madness.

In Classic mode, this track plays at the southern parts of Ape Atoll.

Analysis[edit|edit source]

It has a steady tempo starting out with staccato quarter note 60s organs. It begins in the key of A and its melody starts off with a mix between a piano and a marimba. Shortly after, an Oboe joins in as a countermelody.
