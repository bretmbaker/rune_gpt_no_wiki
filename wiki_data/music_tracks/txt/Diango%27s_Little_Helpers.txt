Diango's Little Helpersis amusictrack that is unlocked atDiango's Workshop, which has so far been accessible during the2013 Halloween event, the2014 Christmas event, and the2017 Christmas event.

It also played during the2022 Christmas eventinThe Nutcrackerpub grounds, and during the2024 Christmas eventwithin Falador.

Trivia[edit|edit source]

The track's title is a pun on the term "Santa's little helpers" which refers to the toy-making elves who work for Santa Claus (or Father Christmas) in Christmas stories.
