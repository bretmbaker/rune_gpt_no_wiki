Trivia[edit|edit source]

It is possible, but difficult, to unlock the track duringTemple Trekking. The player must complete theNature Spirit,In Search of the Myreque, andIn Aid of the Myrequequests without ever passing through the north-western part of theMort Myre Swamp.According to datacrowdsourcedviaRuneLite, approximately 99.81% of players unlock the track in the swamp, 0.17% unlock it east of the Digsite, and 0.02% unlock it during Temple Trekking.
