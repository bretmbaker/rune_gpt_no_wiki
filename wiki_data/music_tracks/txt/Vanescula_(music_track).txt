Trivia[edit|edit source]

The track was originally released on31 August2011as an unlisted track, being played during the quest The Branches of Darkmeyer. However, it is called "Vanescula Fight" inRuneScape.The original version of the track had choir crescendos which have been removed in this version of the track.
