Doorwaysis amusictrack that can be unlocked at locations outsideVarrockcity walls, including theCooks' Guild,Champions' Guild, andJolly Boar Inn. In this area, the music player will also shuffle between playingExpanse,Greatness, andStill Night. It can also be unlocked on theIsle of Souls.

If using Classic music mode infree-to-playarea, it only plays at theJolly Boar Innor theLumberyard.
