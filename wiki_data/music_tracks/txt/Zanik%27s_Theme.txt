Zanik's Themeis amusictrack unlocked during the cutscene ofGrubfoot's dream ofYu'biuskduring theLand of the Goblinsquest.

While the track is not unlocked until the fourth quest in theDorgeshuun quest series, it is played in three cutscenes from the second quest in that series,Death to the Dorgeshuun, and in theYu'biuskcutscene inLand of the Goblins.

The track was originally released on 12 November 2007, as part of the original Land of the Goblins quest.
