Cain's Tutorialis amusictrack that plays duringBarbarian Assault. It can be heard during the tutorial for the minigame presented byCaptain Cain.

If the player chose to skip the tutorial, the track can be obtained by selecting the "Getting Started" option from Cain's Tutorial menu.

It is a remix ofBarbarianism.
