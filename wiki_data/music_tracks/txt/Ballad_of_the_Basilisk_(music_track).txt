Ballad of the Basiliskis amusictrack that is unlocked duringThe Fremennik Exilesquest.

During the last cutscene after defeatingthe Jormungand,Olaf the Bardis on the stage in theRellekka Longhallperforming the ballad. He will sing the first four verses out loud.

Trivia[edit|edit source]

It is an instrumental version of a song inRuneScapeof the same name, which is composed to celebrate the return of the god-heroV. However, in the original lyrics fromRuneScape,V's weapon is ahalberdrather than alongsword, and his shield is akiteshieldrather than aroundshield.This track was also remixed into a new track titledReign of the Basilisk, transposed from G Minor to D Minor.
