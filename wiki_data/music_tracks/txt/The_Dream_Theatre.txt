The Dream Theatreis amusictrack that is unlocked during theDefender of Varrockquest, when the player communes with theSacred Forge.

Trivia[edit|edit source]

This song is likely a reference to the American progressive metal bandDream Theater, since there are numerous key changes in the song, an aspect that Dream Theater frequently use in their works.
