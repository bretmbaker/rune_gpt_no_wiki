Bandos Battalionis amusictrack that is unlocked inBandos' Stronghold. It can be unlocked outside the stronghold without the requirement of 70Strength.

Analysis[edit|edit source]

Throughout the track renditions of different tracks are featured, such as the theme music of theGoblin Villageand ofDorgeshuun City. This track features a part of themain music of the God Wars Dungeon, as doStrength of Saradomin,Armadyl Alliance, andZamorak Zoo.
