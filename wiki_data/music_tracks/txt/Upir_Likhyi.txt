Trivia[edit|edit source]

The track was originally released on31 August2011, being played during the questBranches of DarkmeyerinRuneScape. However, this version is notably more slower pace than the original.Upir' Likhyj(Упирь Лихый, in Russian Упырь Лихой, Old Russian Оупирь Лихыи) was the name of an 11th century Old Russian scribe and a priest. Direct translation of his name from the Old Russian would be "Bloodsucker the Foul" or "Bloodsucker the Wicked". Upir' could also be a Cyrillic version of the Swedish/Nordic Öpir or Öper, with Likhyj meaning "wicked/unworthy" in Old Russian, as in "Öpir the Unworthy".
