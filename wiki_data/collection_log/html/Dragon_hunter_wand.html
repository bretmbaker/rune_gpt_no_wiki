The Dragon hunter wand is a magic dragonbane weapon requiring level 65 Magic to wield. It is obtained as a rare drop from the the Hueycoatl.

The wand has a passive effect that increases accuracy by 50% and damage by 20% when fighting dragons (excluding Elvarg and revenant dragons) and other draconic creatures, such as wyrms, wyverns, and hydras. This stacks multiplicatively with the Slayer helm (i) and Elite Void but not salve (ei). The wand can also autocast spells from the Standard spellbook.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The wand was originally proposed as the Dueling wand, which, besides being able to cast normal combat spells, would have also had a unique spell called Duelling Shot, which would have been a 3-tick earth spell with a max hit of 15.
