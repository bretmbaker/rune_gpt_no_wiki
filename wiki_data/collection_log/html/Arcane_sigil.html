The arcane sigil is an item obtained as a rare drop from the Corporeal Beast. It can be attached to a blessed spirit shield to create an arcane spirit shield, as well as an Elidinis' ward to create Elidinis' ward (f).

Creating an arcane spirit shield requires level 90 Prayer and 85 Smithing. Alternatively, players can ask Abbot Langley to create it for them for 1,500,000 coins.

Meanwhile, fortifying Elidinis' ward requires level 90 Prayer and 90 Smithing, as well as 10,000 soul runes. Unlike the arcane spirit shield, Abbot Langley cannot help players to fortify the ward, though he can remove the sigil from an arcane spirit shield, destroying the blessed spirit shield in the process.
