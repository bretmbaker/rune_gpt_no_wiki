Bryophyta's essence is an item dropped by Bryophyta, which is used with a battlestaff with 62 Crafting to create Bryophyta's staff.

Free-to-play players, or those without the required Crafting level to create the staff, may bring the essence to Zaff in central Varrock to do so, for a fee of 50,000 coins.

Note: On death, the staff will lose all but one of its charges.
