Scurrius' spine is an uncommon drop from Scurrius. It can be combined with various weapons to create rat bone weapons.

Excess spines can be given to Historian Aldo outside the boss area in exchange for an antique lamp giving 5,000 experience in either Attack, Strength, Defence, Hitpoints, Magic, Ranged, or 3,500 experience in Prayer.
