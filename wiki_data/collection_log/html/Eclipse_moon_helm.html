The eclipse moon helm is an item that requires level 75 Ranged and 50 Defence to equip, which is obtained as a possible reward from the Lunar Chest in Neypotzli.

As with all Moon equipment it is degradeable, much like Barrows equipment. The armour starts at 3000 charges and loses 1 charge for every 54 seconds in combat, meaning it lasts for 45 hours of continuous combat before breaking. Having an NPC such as Bob and Dunstan repair it costs 1,500,000 coins when fully broken, while repairing it yourself at an armour stand adds a discount of 7,500 coins per Smithing level, reducing the price to 757,500 coins at level 99.

Set effect[edit | edit source]

When all pieces of the eclipse moon armour set are worn, the player gains the Eclipse set effect, granting successful attacks with the eclipse atlatl a 20% chance to inflict a burn on any non-immune target.

While burning, the target takes 10 burn damage over the course of 40 ticks or 24 seconds (Seen as 1 damage every 4 ticks or 2.4 seconds). Each burn has an individual duration and up to five burns can be applied to a target at a time. At maximum stacks, existing burns must expire before a new burns can be applied.

On average, this results in targets taking roughly 2 burn damage per successful hit of the weapon. However because burn deals damage over time, damage is lost if the burn is cleared or the target dies.
