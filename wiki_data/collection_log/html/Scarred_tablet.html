The scarred tablet is an item dropped by the Leviathan. It is used on the ring of shadows to unlock a teleport to the Scar.

The Leviathan593; 798; 1157 11/25.8

Transcript[edit | edit source]

The following text is transcluded from Transcript:Scarred tablet.
When inspected
  • You touch the scarred tablet. You feel a surge of energy run through your body, hearing the word 'Scar' echo in your mind.
  • (End of dialogue)
When used on the ring of shadows
  • You hold the scarred tablet against the ring of shadows causing the tablet to shatter, transferring its power into the ring.
  • (End of dialogue)
