The gauntlet cape is a cosmetic cape that is obtained after completing the Corrupted Gauntlet, after which it will automatically appear inside the players' inventory. If a player has one in their bank or cape rack, they will not receive another. The cape is lost upon death if not protected.

This item can be stored in the cape rack of a costume room.
