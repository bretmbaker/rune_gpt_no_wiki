The hill giant club is a two-handed melee weapon requiring 40 Attack to equip. It is currently the best-in-slot weapon for crush bonuses in free-to-play, possessing very similar stats to a rune 2h sword but with the slash and crush bonuses swapped, though the rune 2h sword has a slightly higher slash bonus than the crush bonus of the club.

It can be obtained as a drop by killing Obor the Hill Titan. The only way to gain access to Obor's Lair is to kill Hill Giants until they drop a giant key. Killing Hill giants in the Wilderness increases the chance of receiving a key.

This weapon is commonly used in free-to-play PvP activities as a knockout weapon, especially during the so-called risk fights, as well as safe activities such as Clan Wars and Castle Wars. It is notable for being more accurate against opponents in full rune armour than the rune 2h sword. It can be used as a slash weapon for cutting spider webs.
