Pyromancer outfit

From Old School RuneScape Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The pyromancer outfit is an experience-boosting set that grants bonus Firemaking experience when worn. It provides a small boost to experience received when training Firemaking - a total of 2.5% when the full set is equipped. Although warm gloves match the appearance of the pyromancer outfit, they do not count towards the set bonus. However, they do count as a piece of warm clothing when subduing the Wintertodt.

The pyromancer outfit does not currently give an experience boost when training Firemaking at Shades of Mort'ton nor when making funeral pyres in Mort'ton.[1]

Pieces of the outfit can be obtained from the Reward Cart, from which rolls are awarded after subduing the Wintertodt. Extra outfit pieces may be exchanged for an extra supply crate or 50 burnt pages via Ignisia. The full outfit can be exchanged at once for either an extra supply crate (with an additional roll on the loot table, for a total of 5 rolls) or 250 burnt pages.[2]

The entire set can be stored in a magic wardrobe in a player-owned house costume room. Warm gloves can be stored there separately. The outfit is converted to coins on death above level 20 Wilderness, but will be protected below level 20.
