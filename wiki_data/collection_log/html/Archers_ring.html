The archers ring is one of the Fremennik rings and is dropped by the Dagannoth Supreme in the Waterbirth Island Dungeon. The Fremennik rings are some of the very few rings in RuneScape to give stat bonuses.

It can be imbued using 650,000 Nightmare Zone reward points or 325,000 with the hard tier of Combat Achievements, 260 Soul Wars Zeal Tokens, or a Scroll of Imbuing, purchased for 200 Emir's Arena reward points, which doubles its bonuses.

Players can use a chisel on the archers ring to create an archer icon after learning how to combine ring icons from Peer the Seer. This process requires 80 Crafting (boostable), and is part of the process for creating the venator ring.
