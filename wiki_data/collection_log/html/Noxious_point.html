The noxious point is a rare drop from Araxxor. It can be combined with the noxious blade and noxious pommel to create the noxious halberd.

Transcript[edit | edit source]

The following text is transcluded from Transcript:Noxious point.
When combined with a noxious blade and pommel
  • Do you wish to combine all three pieces to create a noxious halberd? This process is non-reversible.
  • Do you wish to create a noxious halberd?
  • Yes.
    • You successfully create a noxious halberd.
    • (End of dialogue)
  • No.
    • (End of dialogue)
Attempting to combine the pieces without the level requirements
  • Creating a Noxious Halberd requires level 72 crafting & smithing.[sic]
  • (End of dialogue)
Attempting to combine only two pieces
  • Creating a noxious halberd requires at least one noxious [point/pommel/blade][sic]
  • (End of dialogue)

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • While untradeable and having a high alchemy value of 96,000 coins, when obtained, the clan broadcast gives it a value of 10,000,000 coins.
