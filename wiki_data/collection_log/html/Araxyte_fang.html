The araxyte fang is an untradeable add-on dropped by Araxxor. With a Crafting level of 86, it can be used to upgrade the amulet of torture into the amulet of rancour, giving 500 Crafting experience.

Based on Grand Exchange prices, the araxyte fang is worth 65,930,139 if used to upgrade an amulet of torture.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • While it is untradeable and has a high alchemy value of 120,000 coins, the clan broadcast that occurs when it is obtained gives it a value of 50,000,000 coins.
  • If lost in PvP, the Araxyte fang will be converted to 24,000 coins.
