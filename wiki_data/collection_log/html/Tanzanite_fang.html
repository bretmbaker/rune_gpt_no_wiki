The tanzanite fang is obtained from killing Zulrah. It can be used with a chisel to create the toxic blowpipe (empty), requiring level 78 Fletching and granting 120 Fletching experience.

Players can dismantle the fang to receive 20,000 Zulrah's scales, resulting in a profit of −1,747,772 coins.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Tanzanite is a variety of the mineral zoisite, and is only found in the Mererani Hills in Northern Tanzania. The colour of the fang reflects the colour of the actual mineral.
