The enhanced crystal weapon seed is an enhanced version of the crystal weapon seed that can be obtained as a rare drop from the Gauntlet. It can be sung into either the Blade of Saeldor or Bow of Faerdhinen.

It can also be obtained by reverting the inactive blade or bow at a singing bowl. Reverting an inactive blade or bow requires 250 crystal shards, 82 Smithing and 82 Crafting. Conwenna can also revert the inactive blade or bow, requiring 375 crystal shards.[1] There is no confirmation screen on the reversion menu.

Players can also exchange the enhanced weapon seed for 1,500 crystal shards by speaking to Amrod in Prifddinas' Hefin Inn.
