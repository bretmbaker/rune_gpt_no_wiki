Item: Chromium ingot
Summary: Chromium ingots are metal bars dropped by the Leviathan, the Whisperer, Vardorvis, and Duke Sucellus. They can also be smelted via a mixture of runite and adamant ores at the Blast Furnace. They are used in the process of creating the four ancient rings, each requiring three ingots.
