Item: Dragon harpoon
Summary: The dragon harpoon is a wieldable harpoon that requires 61 Fishing to use and 60 Attack to wield. The harpoon gives the player a 20% increased catch rate compared to the regular harpoon. Its combat stats are comparable to the dragon dagger, but it is 1 tick slower. It can be stored in a tackle box.
