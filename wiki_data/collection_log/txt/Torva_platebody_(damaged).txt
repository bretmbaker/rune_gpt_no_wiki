Item: Torva platebody
Summary: The Torva platebody  is part of the Torva armour set, requiring 80 Defence to wear. The platebody boasts the highest strength bonus of any body slot equipment, being 2 higher than the Bandos chestplate, Inquisitor's hauberk, fighter torso, and blood moon chestplate. As a Zarosian-aligned item, Torva armour will give Zarosian protection within the God Wars Dungeon.
