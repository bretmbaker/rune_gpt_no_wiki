Item: Inquisitor's mace
Summary: The Inquisitor's mace is a one-handed crush weapon requiring 80 Attack to wield. Received as a drop from The Nightmare, it has bonuses nearly identical to the Ghrazi rapier and blade of Saeldor, acting as the crush variant of these weapons; unlike those weapons, it also has +2 Prayer bonus.
