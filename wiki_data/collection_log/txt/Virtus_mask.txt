Item: Virtus mask
Summary: The Virtus mask is a part of the Virtus robes set that is worn in the head slot, requiring level 78 in Magic and 75 Defence to equip. It can be obtained as a rare drop from The Leviathan, The Whisperer, Vardorvis, and Duke Sucellus.

- Vardorvis (Level 572; 784; 1136, Quantity: 1, Rarity: 1/3,264)
- The Whisperer (Level 587; 791; 1146, Quantity: 1, Rarity: 1/1,536)
