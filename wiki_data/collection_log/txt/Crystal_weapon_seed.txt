Item: Crystal weapon seed
Summary: A crystal weapon seed is an elven crystal that can be turned into crystal weapons. Completing the Gauntlet will yield a chance of gaining these seeds. The crystal weapon seed can be bought from Justine's stuff for the Last Shopper Standing for 12 Last Man Standing points.
