Item: Dragon pickaxe
Summary: The dragon pickaxe was released with the Wilderness Rejuvenation on 13 March 2014. It requires 61 Mining to use and 60 Attack to wield. It is tied for the second strongest and second fastest pickaxe in-game with the 3rd age pickaxe, behind the crystal pickaxe.
