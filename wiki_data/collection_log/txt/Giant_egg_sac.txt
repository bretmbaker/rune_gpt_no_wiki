Item: Giant egg sac
Summary: A giant egg sac is an item obtained as a drop from Sarachnis, which can be opened with a knife while standing inside a bank (right click egg sac in bank interface, then click empty) to obtain 100 red spiders' eggs, which is worth 37,100. This means opening a full giant egg sac results in a profit of 1,679.
Sources:
- Sarachnis (Level 318, Quantity: 1, Rarity: 1/20)
