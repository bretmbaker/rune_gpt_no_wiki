Item: Torva platelegs
Summary: The Torva platelegs are part of the Torva armour set, requiring 80 Defence to wear. The platelegs boast the highest strength bonus of any legs slot equipment, being 2 higher than the Bandos tassets. As a Zarosian-aligned item, Torva armour will give Zarosian protection within the God Wars Dungeon.
