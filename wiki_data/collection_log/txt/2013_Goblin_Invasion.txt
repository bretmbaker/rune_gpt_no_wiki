Item: 2013 Goblin Invasion
Summary: The Goblin Invasion was a community focused event that ran over a series of 5 weeks. The progression of this community quest was determined by voting in polls to decide who fights with the players and where as well as collecting items needed to defeat never before seen bosses. The event was first teased on 10 May 2013, then released in successive weeks between 2 August - 5 September 2013 as the goblins attacked various locations across Gielinor.
