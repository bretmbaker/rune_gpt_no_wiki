Item: Spectral sigil
Summary: The spectral sigil can be attached to a blessed spirit shield to make a spectral spirit shield. To attach the sigil to the blessed spirit shield, players need 90 Prayer and 85 Smithing. Alternatively, players can ask Abbot Langley to attach it for 1,500,000 coins. It is a rare drop from the Corporeal Beast.
Sources:
- Corporeal Beast (Level 785, Quantity: 1, Rarity: 1/1,365)
