Item: Odium shard 1
Summary: The odium shard 1 is an item dropped by the Chaos Fanatic. When the two other shards, the odium shard 2 and 3 are obtained, they can be forged into an Odium ward on the Wilderness Volcano on the most north-east part of the Wilderness.
Sources:
- Chaos Fanatic (Level 202, Quantity: 1, Rarity: 1/256)
