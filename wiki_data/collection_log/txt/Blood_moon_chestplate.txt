Item: Blood moon chestplate
Summary: The blood moon chestplate is an item that requires level 75 Strength and 50 Defence to equip, which is obtained as a possible reward from the Lunar Chest in Neypotzli. With similar stats to a fighter torso, it is tradeable and has identical strength bonus, while having +50 more magic defence bonus.
