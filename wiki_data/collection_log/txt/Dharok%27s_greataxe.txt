Item: Dharok's greataxe
Summary: Dharok's greataxe is a two-handed melee weapon that is part of the Dharok the Wretched's set of barrows equipment. To wield the Dharok's greataxe, a player requires 70 Attack and 70 Strength. It cannot be used for Woodcutting, unlike other axes.
