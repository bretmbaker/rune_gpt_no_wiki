Item: Sunfire fanatic chausses
Summary: The sunfire fanatic chausses are an item that requires level 60 Prayer and 40 Defence to equip, obtained as a possible reward from participating in the Fortis Colosseum. Part of the sunfire fanatic armour set, they provide the best in slot prayer bonus for the legs slot, and also have identical defence bonuses to the rune platelegs.
