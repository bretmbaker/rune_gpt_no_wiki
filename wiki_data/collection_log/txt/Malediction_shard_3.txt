Item: Malediction shard 3
Summary: The malediction shard 3 is an item dropped by Scorpia. When the two other shards, the malediction shard 1 and 2 are obtained, they can be forged into a malediction ward on the Wilderness Volcano on the most north-east part of the Wilderness.
Sources:
- Scorpia (Level 225, Quantity: 1, Rarity: 1/256)
