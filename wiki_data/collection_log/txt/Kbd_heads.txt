Item: Kbd heads
Summary: Kbd heads are a rare drop from the King Black Dragon. It can be stuffed at the Taxidermist in Canifis by using the item on her and paying 50,000 coins, returning a stuffed kbd heads. When players take it to the her, she will tell players "Three?! This must be a King Black Dragon! I'll have to get out my heavy duty tools -- this skin's as tough as iron!"
Sources:
- King Black Dragon (Level 276, Quantity: 1, Rarity: 1/128)
