Item: Armadyl helmet
Summary: The Armadyl helmet is an item that can be obtained from Kree'arra and his bodyguards in the God Wars Dungeon, or as an extremely rare drop from an Armadylean/Bandosian guard from elite coordinate clues. Along with the Armadyl chestplate and chainskirt, it is part of the Armadyl armour set, and requires 70 Defence and Ranged to wear.
