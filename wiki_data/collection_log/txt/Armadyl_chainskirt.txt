Item: Armadyl chainskirt
Summary: The Armadyl chainskirt is an item that can be obtained from Kree'arra and his bodyguards in the God Wars Dungeon. Along with the Armadyl helmet and chestplate, it is part of the Armadyl armour set, and requires 70 Defence and Ranged to wear.
