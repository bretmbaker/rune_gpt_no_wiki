Item: Armadyl chestplate
Summary: The Armadyl chestplate is an item that can be obtained from Kree'arra and his bodyguards in the God Wars Dungeon. Along with the Armadyl helmet and chainskirt, it is part of the Armadyl armour set, and requires 70 Defence and Ranged to wear.

- Flockleader Geerin (Level 149, Quantity: 1, Rarity: 1/16,129)
