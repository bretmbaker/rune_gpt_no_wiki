Item: Ancient shard
Summary: Ancient shards are obtained from monsters found within the Catacombs of Kourend (excluding ghosts). Three shards can be used with a Darklight at the altar in the centre of the catacombs to create an Arclight, a degradeable variant of the darklight that provides stats superior to the abyssal tentacle when used against demons. Ancient shards are also used to charge Arclight. One shard provides 333 charges, three shards provide 1,000 charges; up to a maximum of 10,000. They are also used on the altar in the centre of the catacombs for quick transportation to several areas (and are consumed on use):
