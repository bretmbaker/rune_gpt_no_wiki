Item: Deadeye prayer scroll
Summary: The deadeye prayer scroll is a possible reward from looting Eldric the Ice King of the Royal Titans. Reading it requires level 62 Prayer, which in turn consumes the scroll and unlocks the Deadeye prayer, which replaces Eagle Eye within the prayer book.
Sources:
- Eldric the Ice King (Level 350, Quantity: 1, Rarity: 1/75; Never)
