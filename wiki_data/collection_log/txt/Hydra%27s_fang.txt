Item: Hydra's fang
Summary: The Hydra's fang is an item dropped by hydras and the Alchemical Hydra. It can be combined with the Hydra's eye and heart to create the brimstone ring.
Sources:

- Colossal Hydra (Level 309, Quantity: 1, Rarity: 1/360.72)
- Hydra (Level 194, Quantity: 1, Rarity: 1/1,800.72; 1/360.72)
