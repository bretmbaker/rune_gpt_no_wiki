Item: Uncut onyx
Summary: An uncut onyx is the second rarest gem in Old School RuneScape, behind zenyte. It can be cut with a chisel at level 67 Crafting, granting 167.5 experience. This makes a cut onyx, which can be used to make very powerful jewellery. The cut onyx can also be used with a zenyte shard to create an uncut zenyte.
