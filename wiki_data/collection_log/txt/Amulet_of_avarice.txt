Item: Amulet of avarice
Summary: The amulet of avarice is an amulet that can be received as a drop from revenants found within the Revenant Caves. The amulet has the same combat bonuses as the amulet of glory, but has an additional 20% increase in accuracy and damage when fighting revenants, similar to a Salve amulet. The effects of the amulet do not stack with the Slayer helmet (i).
