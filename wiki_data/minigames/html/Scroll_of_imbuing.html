The scroll of imbuing is an item purchased from the Emir's Arena reward shop for 200 points. It is used to imbue items similar to the Nightmare Zone and Soul Wars Reward Shop .

Note: Every item can be uncharged to return the scroll in its original form, allowing it to be swapped to other items freely.

Changes [ edit | edit source ]

Date Changes
10 April 2024
( update | poll )

Dying with an imbued item that is lost on death will now refund either the points or the Imbue Scroll used to create it.

20 July 2022
( update )

The price for a scroll of imbuing has been reduced from 500 points to 200. Players who purchased one prior to this update will be refunded their points.

13 July 2022
( update )

The item is now obtainable.

6 July 2022
( update )

The item was added to the game, but was unobtainable.
