Guides

The Morytania area covers the entirety of Morytania along with the island of Mos Le'Harmless , Braindeath Island , Dragontooth Island and Harmony Island . Instances of areas in Morytania and underground areas with an entrance in Morytania will also be included.

Restricted travel [ edit | edit source ]

The following travel options are restricted unless the appropriate area is unlocked:

  • The agility shortcut through the railing next to the Paterdomus Temple leading to Misthalin
  • The stairs leading from the Paterdomus temple up to Misthalin

Notable combat-related activities [ edit | edit source ]

  • Barrows
  • Cave horrors
  • Fenkenstrain's experiments
  • Grotesque Guardians
  • Haunted Mine
  • Morytania Spider Cave
  • The Nightmare of Ashihama
  • Lair of Tarn Razorlor
  • Shades of Mort'ton
  • Slayer Tower
  • Theatre of Blood
  • Vyrewatch Sentinels
  • Echo Boss : Grotesque Guardians

In Raging Echoes League, Theatre of Blood: Hard mode and Phosanis nightmare have the following changes:

  • Damage & Defence increased by 25%
  • 50% increased hitpoints on monsters
  • Unique chance will be further multiplied by 2.
  • In Theatre of Blood: Hard mode, if a unique is rolled for any player, everyone in a team will receive a unique item.

Achievement diary tasks [ edit | edit source ]

Automatically completed diary tasks upon area unlock
Morytania Diary
Easy Tasks

None

Medium Tasks
  • Make a batch of cannonballs at the Port Phasmatys furnace
Hard Tasks
  • Enter the Kharyrll portal in your POH through a Portal Chamber
  • Pray at the altar of nature in the nature grotto with piety activated
Elite Tasks
  • Catch a shark in Burgh de Rott with your bare hands
  • Fertilize the Morytania herb patch using lunar spells

Slayer [ edit | edit source ]

This region will add the following tasks to your slayer list ( Combat level where they are enabled):

  • Vampyres (35 - 100)
  • Banshees (20 - 50)
  • Gargoyles (80 - 126)
  • Bloodveld (50 - 126)
  • Nechryael (85 - 126)
  • Cave Horrors (85 - 126)
