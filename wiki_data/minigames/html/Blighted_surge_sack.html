A blighted surge sack is an item purchased from the Emir's Arena reward shop. It allows players to cast any surge spells or wave spells without needing the runes for it. However, it can only be used inside the Wilderness , Ferox Enclave , Mage Arena bank , PvP worlds , Castle Wars , Clan Wars , and Soul Wars , while on the standard spellbook .

The spell sack is consumed upon use, regardless of a successful hit .
