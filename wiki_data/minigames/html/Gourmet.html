Gourmets are cave goblins that one can sell food to in the Dorgesh-Kaan market trading minigame.

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

When selling food to the gourmets, there is a roughly 1-in-4 chance to be given a random piece of Dorgeshuun food.

Item Quantity Rarity Price High Alch
Loach 1 ~1/24 Not sold 30
Eel sushi 1 ~1/24 Not sold 30
Mushrooms 1 ~1/24 Not sold 30
Grubs à la mode 1 ~1/24 Not sold 30
Roast frog 1 ~1/24 Not sold 30
Fillets 1 ~1/24 Not sold 30
