The Hunter's Honour is a cosmetic hat that can be claimed from Corrupted Warriors in Ferox Enclave or Daimon's Crater , once players obtain one target kill in the Bounty Hunter minigame.

The hat alters its appearance depending on the number of target kills (assigned target kills in the Bounty Hunter minigame) and rogue kills (non-target kills on Bounty Hunter worlds) the player has; the Hunter's Honour is chosen if the player has more target kills than rogue kills, and the Rogue's Revenge is chosen if the player has more rogue kills than target kills.
