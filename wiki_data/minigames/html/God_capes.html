God capes

From Old School RuneScape Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search
God capes God cape

God capes are a set of Magic capes that can be awarded from the Mage Arena I miniquest . After defeating Kolodion in all four of his forms, the player can speak to him and step through the sparkling pool and pray to one of three gods : Saradomin , Zamorak , or Guthix . All god capes have the same stats, so choose based on which staff is required the desired spell or based on God Wars alignment (Guthix is not involved with the God Wars Dungeon ). After praying to a god, the corresponding cape will appear at the statue's feet and it may be picked up. The cape can be picked up multiple times to receive multiple capes.

The three types of god capes are:

  • Saradomin cape
  • Zamorak cape
  • Guthix cape

Wearing a god cape with the corresponding god staff can allow you to cast the spell Charge , which boosts the damage of god spell by 50% for seven minutes (with a boosted maximum hit of 30).

God capes offer the second highest Magic Attack bonus of any cape, outclassed only by their imbued variants. God capes require a Magic level of 60 to wear.

Once lost, a player can get a new cape by once again praying at the statue without needing to fight Kolodion again. It may be a good idea to grab multiple capes (from the same statue). Alternatively, players can purchase any one of the three capes from Perdu for 23,000 coins.

It is an essential of the corresponding god staff you choose also by talking to the Staff Trader .

Imbuing [ edit | edit source ]

Players with level 75 in Magic can imbue a god cape by taking a Justiciar's hand , Ent's roots and a Demon's heart to Kolodion during Mage Arena II miniquest . After the quest, players can obtain more imbued capes by simply bringing the respective component of the god cape that they wish to imbue; it is not necessary to kill all three god followers again to make another imbued cape. Alternatively, players can purchase any one of the three capes from Perdu for 250,000 .

The imbued capes are:

  • Imbued saradomin cape
  • Imbued zamorak cape
  • Imbued guthix cape

Used in recommended equipment [ edit | edit source ]

God capes and their imbued forms have identical stats irrespective of god alignment and are interchangeable in most situations outside of God Wars Dungeon bosses.

Changes [ edit | edit source ]

Date Changes
21 August 2024
( update )

The Deadman: Armageddon variants of the imbued god capes now correctly function with their respective god spells .

10 April 2024
( update | poll )

Players can now buy imbued god capes from Perdu .

7 December 2022
( update )

The imbued God capes now protect over more items on death.

12 September 2019
( update )

The cape is now returned in a broken form below level 20 Wilderness and can be repaired by bringing it to Perdu .

23 November 2017
( update ) 		Hotfix
  • Players can no longer obtain additional imbued god capes without giving the remains of another god being, and capes obtained in this manner were removed.
  • The imbued god capes can no longer be alchemised. Previously, casting High Level Alchemy resulted in 60,000 coins being given.
24 March 2016
( update | poll )

It is now possible to get multiple copies of your chosen god cape from the mage arena! It is still not possible to get different god capes at the same time, just multiple of one type.

18 June 2015
( update )

Degraded barrows equipment, mage arena capes and achievement diary reward items are now also protected on death, outside of PvP, in line with other untradeable items.

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • Prior to the release of Mage Arena II , the player could only hold capes from one god, as attempting to pick up another without disposing the old cape would lead to a message stating they would conflict.
