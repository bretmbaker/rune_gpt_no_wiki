A seed pack is a reward from Guildmaster Jane for completing a farming contract , or purchased from the Farmer Gricoller for 30 Tithe Farm points. Guildmaster Jane will also exchange a spirit seed , seedling , or sapling for 1 tier 5 seed pack.

Each farming contract has a difficulty from Tier 1 to Tier 5. For example, Tier 1 contracts represent short Easy contracts such as growing potatoes, whereas Tier 5 rewards would include long Hard contracts such as growing a magic tree . The tier of each contract is listed on Guildmaster Jane 's page.

Seed packs purchased from the Tithe Farm are equivalent to a tier 3 pack, with the addition of white lily seeds to its drop table.

Taking from a seed pack rewards the player with one roll on the reward table. The player may keep taking seeds until all seeds are removed from the seed pack. [1]

Upon death the seed pack will always be destroyed.

Changes [ edit | edit source ]

Date Changes
26 March 2025
( update | poll )

Seed packs can be purchased from the Tithe Farm , with the seed packs being equivalent to a tier 3 pack with the addition of white lily seeds to its drop table.

6 July 2022
( update )

Seed packs now have a right-click Take-all option.

23 September 2020
( update | poll )

Players can now exchange spirit seeds , seedlings , and saplings via Guildmaster Jane for tier 5 seed packs.

18 June 2020
( update )

Opening seed packs will no longer stop players from running or walking.

16 May 2019
( update )

Rebalanced seed rewards into a 5 tier based system.

7 February 2019
( update )

The seed pack received another graphical update .

17 January 2019
( update )

The item was renamed from "Seed box" to "Seed pack", alongside a graphical update .
