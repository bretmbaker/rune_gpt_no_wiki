Dragon arrows are the strongest type of arrow in the game. They are only usable with the dark bow , twisted bow , venator bow , scorching bow , and the 3rd age bow . As with most arrows , they can be poisoned .

They can be created through the Fletching skill at level 90 by using dragon arrowtips on headless arrows , granting 15 Fletching experience per arrow made. 15 arrows can be made at a time, giving 225 Fletching experience.

When used with a dark bow , dragon arrows will enhance its special attack .

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • The tips of dragon arrows are actually made from the claws of dragons instead of Orikalkum , this is unlike other arrows such as rune arrows which get their name from the metal they are tipped with.
  • Despite using Headless arrows which can only be fletched from wood, the shafts of dragon arrows appear to be made of Orikalkum due to the red coloration.
  • When the 3rd age bow was released, it was originally intended to shoot up to dragon arrows. However, a technical problem prevented this; it was later fixed with the release of the Chambers of Xeric .
