This article is about the wearable armour. For the discontinued armour that could be crated and handed in for favour, see Shayzien supply armour .

Shayzien armour is armour requiring 20 Defence to wear. Each tier grants slightly higher protection against the green acidic attack of Lizardman shamans . In order to obtain Shayzien armour, players must compete in the Shayzien Combat Ring within Shayzien , or create it by Smithing lovakite bars (11 bars for a full set). The collection log slots are filled regardless of how the armour is obtained. There are 5 different tiers of Shayzien armour:

  • Shayzien armour (tier 1)
  • Shayzien armour (tier 2)
  • Shayzien armour (tier 3)
  • Shayzien armour (tier 4)
  • Shayzien armour (tier 5)
