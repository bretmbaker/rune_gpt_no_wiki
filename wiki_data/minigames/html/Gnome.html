Gnomes are a race of small humanoids found in various parts of Gielinor . They can be found primarily in the Kandarin region, being most common in the Tree Gnome Village , Tree Gnome Stronghold , and Battlefield of Khazard . Gnomes fight with primarily either ranged or melee attacks, depending on the weapon they wield. They have different coloured hoods.

Gnomes can be pickpocketed at level 75 Thieving yielding 133.5 Thieving experience . It is not recommended to pickpocket them until level 85 Thieving, due to the high rate of failure until this level. Pickpocketing gnomes can be very convenient for those playing Gnome Restaurant so the player does not need to leave to get more king worms or toad legs . It is also a good source of medium clue scrolls however, keep in mind that you will not receive a clue from pickpocketing an NPC if your inventory is full.
