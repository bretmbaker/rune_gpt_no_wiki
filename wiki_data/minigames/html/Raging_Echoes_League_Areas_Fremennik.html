Raging Echoes
Guides

The Fremennik Province area covers the entire Fremennik Province (including the Fremennik Isles ) along with Lunar Isle and Weiss . Instances of areas in the Fremennik Province and underground areas with an entrance in the Fremennik Province will also be included. Access to Kandarin via the bridge south of Rellekka will be blocked by magical barriers.

Restricted travel [ edit | edit source ]

The following travel options are restricted unless the appropriate area is unlocked:

  • The log shortcut leading to Kandarin north of Sinclair Manor
  • The rubble separating the Fremennik Province and the Death Plateau
  • The cave entrance in the tunnel leading to Keldagrim that leads to the bottom of Trollweiss mountain
  • The basalt rock agility shortcut leading from the Lighthouse to the Barbarian Outpost

Slayer [ edit | edit source ]

This region will add the following tasks to your slayer list ( Combat level where they are enabled):

  • Trolls (60 - 126)
  • Dagannoth (75 - 126)
  • Turoth (60 - 100)
  • Basilisks (40 - 126)
  • Cockatrice (40 - 80)
  • Kurask (65 - 126)
  • Jellies (57 - 126)

In addition, the following items and abilities are unlocked after unlocking the area:

Items [ edit | edit source ]

  • Ice gloves

Mechanics [ edit | edit source ]

  • The ability to create super antifire potions.
