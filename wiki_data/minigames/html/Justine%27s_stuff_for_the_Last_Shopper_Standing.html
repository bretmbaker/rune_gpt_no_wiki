Justine's stuff for the Last Shopper Standing is the Last Man Standing reward store owned by Justine located in the Last Man Standing lobby. Players must have 48 hours of playtime as a member in order to buy from the store.

Victor's Capes are claimed by achieving a certain amount of Competitive Last Man Standing wins. Casual games do not count towards the win count to claim the Victor's cape.

Unlockable rewards [ edit | edit source ]

The only unlockable rewards are animation overrides for the Godsword special attack , changing the colour to gold, each override costs 75 Last Man Standing points.

Once unlocked, the gold trim can be toggled at the shop whenever the player desires.

  • Armadyl godsword

  • Bandos godsword

  • Saradomin godsword

  • Zamorak godsword

Changes [ edit | edit source ]

Date Changes
9March 2023
( update )

The name of the reward Kharyll Teleport Tablet changed to Kharyrll Teleport Tablet .

9March 2022
( update )

The amount of Last Man Standing points required to purchase a Trouver parchment has been further reduced from 30 to 18.

3June 2021
( update )

Purchasing rewards from the Last Man Standing store now requires 48 hours of playtime as a member.

7August 2020
Hotfix

Rune arrows and dragonstone bolts (e) have had their prices adjusted from 1 point to 3 points per purchase.

6August 2020
( update )
  • Rewards from Bounty Hunter 's Emblem Trader have been moved to Last Man Standing, except for Amethyst arrows which have been removed.
  • Each point will now be worth an average of 15,000 to 20,000 GP when exchanged for items in Justine's shop.
23January 2020
( update )

The amount of Last Man Standing points required to purchase a Trouver parchment has been reduced from 50 to 30.
