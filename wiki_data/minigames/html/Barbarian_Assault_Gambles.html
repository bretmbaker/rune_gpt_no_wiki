Rewards (Barbarian Assault)

Honour points can be gambled for a chance to receive armour, resources, and the rare pet penance queen . These gambles are known on mobile as lucky dips , and are spent from a single role of the player's choice.

Tiers [ edit | edit source ]

There are 3 levels of gambling, each with different costs. For low and medium gambles, the player needs to have progressed through a certain number of waves without resetting or completing Wave 10.

Level Honour points needed Required wave
Low 200 3 or higher
Medium 400 6 or higher
High 500 and 1 queen kill 1 or higher

Tertiary rewards (high gamble only) [ edit | edit source ]

The clue scroll (elite) and pet penance queen will always be rolled in addition to one of the main drops.

Item Quantity Rarity Price High Alch
Clue scroll (elite) 1 1/15 [d 1] Not sold 12
Pet penance queen 1 1/1,000 [1] Not sold N/A
  1. ^ The elite clue scroll drop rate increases to 1/14 after unlocking the elite Combat Achievements rewards tier.

Changes [ edit | edit source ]

Date Changes
22 August 2019
( update )

Gambles are now referred to as "Lucky dips" when playing on Old School RuneScape Mobile .

1 September 2017
Hotfix

The chance to get an elite clue scroll from a high gamble has been decreased from 1/10 to 1/15.

31 August 2017
( update | poll )

There is now a 1/10 chance to get an elite clue scroll alongside high gamble rewards.

5 March 2015
( update | poll )

You now receive 10% more reward points from Barbarian Assault if you have completed the Kandarin Hard Diary .

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • The odds of getting a dragon chainbody , as initially quoted from Jagex , are "About the same odds as Zamorak suddenly deciding to give up his evil ways." Mod Mat K later confirmed that the actual rate was 1 in 16,000.
