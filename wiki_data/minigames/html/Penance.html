Penance (race)
This article is about the race. For the special attack, see Abyssal bludgeon#Special attack .

Penance are a race only found within and fought through the minigame Barbarian Assault . They are a strange bunch of creatures, and some Penance can only be killed by certain strategies. They are lead by the deadly Penance Queen , also only fought during the minigame. All Penance have one eye.

Below are the types of Penance:

  • Penance Queen
  • Queen Spawn
  • Penance Fighter
  • Penance Ranger
  • Penance Healer
  • Penance Runner
