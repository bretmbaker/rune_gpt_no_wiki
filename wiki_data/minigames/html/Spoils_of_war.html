The spoils of war is a reward from the Soul Wars Minigame . It can be purchased from Nomad on the Isle of Souls for 30 Zeal Tokens .

Unlike supply crates and some impling jars which cannot be taken to Entrana nor opened there for possibly containing dangerous weapons and armour, the spoils of war can be taken to Entrana. While spoils of war can't be opened on the main island of Entrana, they can be taken through the law rift in the Abyss or mysterious ruins to the Law Altar and opened there, while the former two cannot be.

Drop mechanic [ edit | edit source ]

Spoils of war uses a special mechanic to determine items obtained. When the crate is opened, a drop will be rolled for until the roll results in a success, as described below:

  • Roll 1/120 for the dragon drop table.
  • If unsuccessful, roll 1/50 for the junk drop table.
  • If unsuccessful, roll 1/10 for the coins drop table.
    • If successful, roll 1/50 for 40,000-60,000 coins.
    • If unsuccessful, there is an equal chance to receive either 2,000-3000 coins or 20,000-30,000 coins.
  • If the roll for the coins drop table was unsuccessful, the standard table is rolled.
  • The aforementioned sequence occurs three times. However, if a player successfully rolled one of the three subtables, they cannot roll for them again. For example, if a player received a dragon scimitar, they cannot roll the dragon drop table for the remaining rolls.
  • Once the player rolls the entire drop table three times, roll 1/400 for Lil' Creator .

The average spoils of war opening is worth 76,588.44 .

There is a 1/120 chance of rolling the dragon drop table. If one of the player's three rolls on the spoils of war loot table rolled the dragon drop table, they cannot roll for it again. [1]

Changes [ edit | edit source ]

Date Changes
27 January 2021
( update )

The dragon sub-table was added to the spoils' loot table.

20 January 2021
( update )

Spoils of war can no longer be opened in Glarial's Tomb and the arena where players fight Koschei the deathless .

13 January 2021
( update )
  • A "Check" option has been added, which tracks how many players have opened so far.
  • It is no longer possible to open Spoils of War on Entrana.
  • Rebalanced the quantities of certain items in Spoils of War.
