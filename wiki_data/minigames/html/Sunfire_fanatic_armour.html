Sunfire fanatic armour

From Old School RuneScape Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Sunfire fanatic armour is a set of prayer armour obtained as a possible reward from participating in the Fortis Colosseum , starting at Wave 4. Players will receive one of each piece before receiving duplicates. It currently has the best Prayer bonus for the head, body, and legs equipment slots, with each armour piece having a +2 higher bonus compared to their proselyte armour equivalents.

In order to wear the armour, the player must have a Prayer level of 60 and a Defence level of 40. The armour shares the same stats with rune armour , barring the lighter weight and higher prayer bonus. Because of the negative ranged and magic bonuses the set provides, it is only ideal for combat that utilises prayer and where accuracy is not essential.

Equipping any piece of sunfire fanatic armour is necessary for an elite clue scroll emote step . This set can be stored in the armour case space of a costume room in a player-owned house . Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
