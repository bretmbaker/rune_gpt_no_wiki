Raging Echoes
Guides

The Kharidian Desert area covers the entirety of the Kharidian Desert . The Emir's Arena minigame will not be available during this League, although the building itself is accessible. Instances of areas in the Kharidian Desert and underground areas with an entrance in the Kharidian Desert will also be included.

Restricted travel [ edit | edit source ]

The following travel options are restricted unless the appropriate area is unlocked:

  • The gate northwest of Al-Kharid leading to Lumbridge
  • The grapple shortcut across the River Lum to Lumbridge
  • The stepping stone northwest of the Kalphite Lair leading to the Lumbridge Swamp

Notable combat-related activities [ edit | edit source ]

  • Bandits
  • Kalphite Queen
  • Smoke Dungeon
  • Tombs of Amascut
    • Tombs of Amascut has 2 new invocations, which means max raid level can go up to 1000.
    • At 500 invocation level and above, The chance of getting a unique item is further boosted by 2x. If a unique is rolled for any player, everyone in a team will receive a unique item.
  • The Leviathan
  • Tempoross
  • Echo Boss : Kalphite Queen

Players may create private instances to fight the Kalphite Queen.

Achievement diary tasks [ edit | edit source ]

Automatically completed diary tasks upon area unlock
Desert Diary
Easy Tasks

None

Medium Tasks
  • Travel to the Desert via the Eagle transport system
Hard Tasks
  • Slay a dust devil in the desert cave with a Slayer Helmet equipped
  • Refill your waterskins in the Desert using Lunar spells
Elite Tasks
  • Bake a wild pie at the Nardah clay oven
  • Speak to the Kq head in your POH [1]
  1. ^ This task will automatically complete when you receive a Kq head or Kq head(tattered) drop.

Slayer [ edit | edit source ]

This region will add the following tasks to your slayer list ( Combat level where they are enabled):

  • Dust Devils (70 - 126)
  • Kalphite (15 - 126)
  • Bandits (3 - 126)
