Keldagrim tasks are small quest -like tasks assigned only in Keldagrim , the capital of the dwarven realm.

To begin the tasks, players must speak to various dwarves in Keldagrim. Certain shopkeepers will often ask the player to retrieve items for them. For the most part these correspond to the dwarf's job. For instance, Agmundi , the owner of Agmundi Quality Clothes , will ask for items such as thread .

The player will have 10–25 minutes to retrieve the items. If the player brings the items to the dwarf in time, they will usually get to choose their reward: either a few hundred or thousand coins or 150-500 experience in a certain skill (s). The experience corresponds to the items retrieved, meaning that players can receive either Attack , Crafting , Defence , Mining , or Smithing experience depending on the task.

Task-givers [ edit | edit source ]

There are seven dwarves who can request tasks. Only one of them has a task to give at any point in time. Each time that a task is fulfilled or failed, a different dwarf from the previous one is randomly chosen to be the new task-giver. Each of the shopkeepers seems to have even chances of being chosen, while the rowdy dwarf is selected much less frequently. Once a task-giver is selected, the game will determine what kind of item will be requested (e.g. for Saro, this can be either bars, ores, or armour). Finally, any item on the table fitting that description can be rolled with even odds. [1]

If the player wishes to receive a different task rather than fulfilling their current one, there are a few ways to do so: either log out, change worlds or talk to the dwarf and refuse to finish the job. Doing either of these will randomly select a new task-giver. Note: These methods only work while you have a current task. You cannot change task-givers without first finding and speaking with the one currently selected for you.

  • Agmundi , owner of Agmundi Quality Clothes in the western marketplace
  • Gunslik , owner of Gunslik's Assorted Items in the western marketplace
  • Hervi , owner of Green Gemstone Gems in the eastern marketplace
  • Santiri , owner of Quality Weapons Shop in the north-western part of the city
  • Saro , owner of Quality Armour Shop in the south-western part of the city
  • Tati , owner of Pickaxe-Is-Mine in the eastern part of the city
  • The rowdy dwarf , drunkenly circling the Laughing Miner Pub

Item Requests [ edit | edit source ]

The following tables list the items that each dwarf can request, along with the XP awarded per item or the coins rewarded for each item. Players can choose which of the two types of rewards they want to receive. Items have to be unnoted.

  • Ores award either coins or Mining XP,
  • Bars award either coins or Smithing XP,
  • Armour awards either coins or Defence XP
  • Weapons award either coins or Attack XP
    • Pickaxes award either coins or both Attack and Mining XP.
  • Crafting items award either coins or Crafting XP

Experience and coins shown in the tables below are listed per item.
