A raw lobster is a type of crustacean which can be caught at level 40 Fishing with a lobster pot , resulting in 90 Fishing experience . It can be cooked into Lobster at level 40 Cooking , yielding 120 Cooking experience. Starting at level 74 Cooking, raw lobsters will no longer burn; wearing Cooking Gauntlets reduces this threshold to 64 Cooking instead.

Raw lobster is also used in Creature Creation together with a raw jubbly to make a jubster .

Fishing locations [ edit | edit source ]

There are several fishing locations at which lobsters can be caught. The same spots always contain a harpoon spot for raw tuna and raw swordfish .

Free-to-play [ edit | edit source ]

  • Karamja : Musa Point , north-east of the Karamja Volcano .
  • Wilderness : North-eastern coast in level 30+, south of Fountain of Rune . On Deadman worlds, only dark crab fishing spots are available at this location.
  • Feldip Hills : Corsair Cove Resource Area (requires completion of Dragon Slayer I ).

Members [ edit | edit source ]

  • Catherby : On the beach, south-west of the White Wolf Mountain .
  • Fishing Guild (requires 68 ): Players at 65 or higher with a fishing potion (63+ with an admiral pie ) may also fish for lobsters in the Fishing Guild.
  • Rellekka : Northern dock.
  • Hosidius : The peninsula south of the port (there are aggressive level 28 Hobgoblins nearby).
  • Port Piscarilius : North-west of the bank.
  • Land's End : West of the bank.
  • Jatizso : Along the southern shoreline and pier.
  • Isle of Souls : North-East side of the island.
  • Varlamore : Along the southern shore near Sunset Coast
