Raging Echoes
Guides

The Wilderness area covers the entirety of the Wilderness .

Last Man Standing and Bounty Hunter will not be accessible during this League .

For this League, deaths will behave differently in the Wilderness:

  • PvP deaths will be treated like a PvM death with your lost items moving to a gravestone . The gravestone will then move to your respawn location.
  • PvM deaths will be unaffected.
  • Loot keys are disabled on League Worlds.

Instances of areas in the Wilderness and underground areas with an entrance in the Wilderness will also be included.

All transportation and teleportation via Charter Ship , Customs officer , Gnome gliders , Fairy Rings , Balloons and other forms of transport require you to have unlocked access to the area your destination is in.

The following travel options are restricted unless you have access to the appropriate area:

  • Crossing the Wilderness Ditch will require access to whichever area is on the other side of it.
  • The lever at the Deserted Keep will work only if you have access to the area it takes you.

Access to the Revenant Caves will be free instead of requiring 100,000 coins to enter.

Access to the Wilderness Agility Course will be free instead of requiring 150,000 coins to enter.

Dying will not reset your lap counter in the Wilderness Agility Course .

Krystillia assigns tasks like any other Slayer Master , not assigning Wilderness-specific tasks.

Slayer targets defeated anywhere will have a chance to drop a Larran's key .

Unlocks [ edit | edit source ]

The following quest is automatically completed but will not award any completion XP:

  • Enter the Abyss

The following tasks in the Wilderness Achievement Diary are automatically completed:

Medium Tasks:

  • Smith a Gold Helmet in the resource area

Hard Tasks:

  • Take the Agility shortcut from Trollheim to the Wilderness
  • Fish a raw lava eel in the Wilderness

Elite Tasks:

  • Teleport to Ghorrock

This region will add the following tasks to your slayer list ( Combat level where they are enabled):

Echoes [ edit | edit source ]

Echo King Black Dragon

The King Black Dragon can be found in the Wilderness , just west of the Lava Maze . To fight it the player must use a wilderness echo orb , which is dropped by the regular King Black Dragon .

Items [ edit | edit source ]

Thunder khopesh

A powerful khopesh , imbued with the power of thunder and lightning.

  • Deals full damage to the Corporeal Beast .
  • Has a 20% chance to trigger an AoE lightning bolt under the target, hitting anything in a 1-tile radius, which rolls with 50% of the original attack's max hit.

Special Attack: 50% – Attacks twice. If either attack is successful, an additional lightning bolt is summoned, rolling up to 100% of the original attack's max hit.

This item has a drop rate of 1/25.

Thousand-dragon ward

A shield that has had over a thousand unique dragons encase it in flame.

Grants immunity to status effects, including Poison , Venom , Dragonfire , Icy breath , Swamp decay, Disease , Desert Heat , Ice Prison, Brimstone Dungeon Floor, Sulphur Clouds, Cold/Ice stat drain.

This item has a drop rate of 1/25.
