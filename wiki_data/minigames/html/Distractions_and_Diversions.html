Distractions and Diversions

Distractions and Diversions , also known as DNDs or D&Ds , are sporadic activities that, as the name suggests, are distractions and diversions from other content players may be engaged with. Unlike minigames , Distractions and Diversions are typically encountered by chance, and may not always be specifically sought after. For instance, a player may receive a Champion's scroll or Clue scroll while training Slayer , or encounter Shooting Stars while Questing .

There are currently three Distractions and Diversions, two of which, Shooting Stars and Treasure Trails , are available to free-to-play players. Though in both cases members have access to additional content that is unavailable in free-to-play worlds.

Distractions and Diversions can be identified with a yellow star icon on the map . These locations are where players can find more information about the activities, and not necessarily where they can engage with the activity itself.

List of Distractions and Diversions [ edit | edit source ]

Activity Released Classification Description Rewards
Champions' Challenge 12 December 2005 Dangerous Fight creatures of 10 races to have a chance at being challenged by their champions. Defeating the human champion proclaims players as champion of champions. Champion's lamps and champion's cape .

See: main article

Shooting Stars 17 March 2021 Dangerous (certain star locations can be found in PVP locations or close to monsters ) Players find meteors that have crashed landed on Gielinor to mine stardust to trade in for rewards. See the rewards section for more information.
Treasure Trails 5 May 2004 Dangerous (certain clues can spawn aggressive NPCs) Players follow cryptic clues on wild chases to obtain valuable rewards. Rewards include runes, god pages, trimmed armour, third-age equipment, and much more.
