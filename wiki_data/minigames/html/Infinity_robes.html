Infinity robe armour

Infinity robes are a set of magic armour that are rewards from the Mage Training Arena minigame . Each piece of the robes can be obtained from the Rewards Guardian on the top floor of the arena in exchange for Pizazz points . Infinity robes require 25 Defence and 50 Magic to wear. The hat, top and bottom gives a 1% Magic damage bonus for a total of 3%.

To buy full infinity robes with Pizazz points requires 1,495 Telekinetic, 1,695 Alchemist, 14,700 Enchantment and 1,495 Graveyard points. It can be stored in a magic wardrobe in a player-owned house.

It is possible to change the colour of the infinity robes. To recolour a piece, the player must have a light or dark infinity colour kit , and then use an infinity hat, top or bottom on the kit. Infinity gloves and boots cannot be recoloured.
