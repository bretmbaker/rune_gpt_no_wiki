Dragon arrowsare the strongest type ofarrowin the game. They are only usable with thedark bow,twisted bow,venator bow,scorching bow, and the3rd age bow. As with mostarrows, they can bepoisoned.

They can be created through theFletchingskillat level 90 by usingdragon arrowtipsonheadless arrows, granting 15 Fletchingexperienceper arrow made. 15 arrows can be made at a time, giving 225 Fletching experience.

When used with adark bow, dragon arrows will enhance itsspecial attack.

Trivia[edit|edit source]

The tips of dragon arrows are actually made from the claws of dragons instead ofOrikalkum, this is unlike other arrows such asrune arrowswhich get their name from the metal they are tipped with.Despite usingHeadless arrowswhich can only be fletched from wood, the shafts of dragon arrows appear to be made of Orikalkum due to the red coloration.When the 3rd age bow was released, it was originally intended to shoot up to dragon arrows. However, a technical problem prevented this; it was later fixed with the release of theChambers of Xeric.
