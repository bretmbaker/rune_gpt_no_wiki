TheFremennik Provincearea covers the entireFremennik Province(including theFremennik Isles) along withLunar IsleandWeiss. Instances of areas in the Fremennik Province and underground areas with an entrance in the Fremennik Province will also be included. Access toKandarinvia the bridge south ofRellekkawill be blocked by magical barriers.
