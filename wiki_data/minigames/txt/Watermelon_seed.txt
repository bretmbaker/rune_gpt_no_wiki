A nearbygardenerwill watch over growing watermelons (excludingHarmony Island) for 10curry leaves, either noted or unnoted. Fully grownnasturtiumsorwhite liliesin a nearbyflower patchwill also protect the watermelons fromdisease.

These seeds can bestolenfrommaster farmersandseed stalls. They may also be obtained fromTemple Trekking, theWintertodtreward cart,high-level gamblingat theBarbarian Assaultminigame, andFarming Guildseed packs.

Average harvest yield[edit|edit source]

Watermelons have a minimum of 3harvest lives, increasing by 1 for each tier ofcompostused (for a maximum of 6 when usingultracompost). While harvesting watermelon, there is a chance to not consume a harvest life dependent on the player's Farming level. This chance scales from 49.6% at level 1 to 70.7% at level 99, interpolating linearly in between.

Magic secateurs(equipped or in inventory) increase the chance of receiving additional yield by 10%.
