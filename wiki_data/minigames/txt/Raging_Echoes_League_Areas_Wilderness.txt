The Wilderness area covers the entirety of theWilderness.

Last Man StandingandBounty Hunterwill not be accessible during thisLeague.

For this League, deaths will behave differently in the Wilderness:

PvPdeaths will be treated like aPvMdeath with your lost items moving to agravestone. The gravestone will then move to your respawn location.PvM deaths will be unaffected.Loot keysare disabled on League Worlds.

Instances of areas in the Wilderness and underground areas with an entrance in the Wilderness will also be included.

All transportation and teleportation viaCharter Ship,Customs officer,Gnome gliders,Fairy Rings,Balloonsand other forms of transport require you to have unlocked access to the area your destination is in.

The following travel options are restricted unless you have access to the appropriate area:

Crossing theWilderness Ditchwill require access to whichever area is on the other side of it.

The lever at theDeserted Keepwill work only if you have access to the area it takes you.
