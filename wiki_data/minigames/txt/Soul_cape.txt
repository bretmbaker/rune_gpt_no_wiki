Asoul capeis a cape obtained from theSoul Warsminigame, costing 2,500Zeal Tokensto purchase from the reward shop. It requires level 70Prayerto purchase and equip, and offers the highestPrayer bonusin thecape slot. It is visually identical to the capes that are given to players upon entering a round in the Soul Wars minigame, however, the capes inside the minigame do not have any bonuses.

It is worth noting that even though the capes act as Ava's Devices in the minigame after showing any varietyAva's devicetoNomad, thereward capedoesnothave this effect throughout Gielinor.

Players can right-click "Toggle-colour" to switch between the cape's two colours.

This item can be stored in thecape rackof acostume room.

Trivia[edit|edit source]

The Soul cape resembles the design of theblue capeandred capefromRuneScape, which are given as a reward after the grandmaster questNomad's Requiem. Strangely, the Soul cape was (potentially mistakenly) styled after the updated versions of the capes that were implemented upon the release of theEvolution of Combatto fit the newly proportioned character model, rather than the original design of the capes from the release of Soul Wars which were made to fit the character model that still exists inOld School. As a result the Soul cape appears stylistically different and disproportionately longer than other capes in game.
