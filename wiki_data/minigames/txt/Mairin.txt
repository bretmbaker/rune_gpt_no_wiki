Mairinis a mermaid located in theunderwatersection ofFossil Island. She runs amarketwhich sells a variety ofunderwater-related items, bought withmermaid's tears. She can also train the player'sAgilityandThievingin exchange forglistening tears. If players exchange them for experience with more than 700 glistening tears in the inventory, the excess will bediscarded. Both tears are obtained by searching chests and clams in themermaid's domain.

Mairin objects tofishbowl helmetsand does not allow players to use them when talking to her or when going to the mermaid's domain, as her sister was once trapped in a fishbowl helmet by humans.
