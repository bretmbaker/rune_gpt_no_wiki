[Minigame: God_spells]

God spellsrefer to the three combat spells unlocked at theMage Arena. Each spell requires a staff that corresponds to its respective god, and players must have cast the spell at least 100 times within the Mage Arena before being able to cast it anywhere else. Being able to cast all three god spells anywhere is a requirement to start theMage Arena IIminiquest.

Casting one of these spells on another player in theWildernessis a requirement for thehard Wilderness diary.Splashingthe spell will not fulfil the requirement.

Max hit[edit|edit source]

The max hit of all god spells is 20 without any bonuses. This can be increased to 30 if players castCharge; this boost will last for seven minutes.

If casting Charge while wearing fullAhrim's, as well as anamulet of the damned, atormented bracelet, animbued god cape, and anElidinis' ward (f), players can hit up to 44, matching the max hit of Ice Barrage while in the same gear (but requiring 80 instead of 96 Magic).
