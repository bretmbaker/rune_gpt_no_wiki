Echo crystalsare a drop received from participating in theFortis Colosseum. It is used on a pair ofguardian bootsto createecho boots, which gives it an additional +2prayer bonusand a passive recoil effect.

Each echo crystal added to the boots gives it 6,000 recoil charges.
