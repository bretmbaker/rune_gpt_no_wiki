TheKebos and Kourendarea covers the entirety ofKebosandKourend. Instances of areas in Kebos and Kourend and underground areas with an entrance in Kebos and Kourend will also be included.

Colloquially this region is commonly calledZeahor 'Z, despite Zeah normally also including Varlamore.
