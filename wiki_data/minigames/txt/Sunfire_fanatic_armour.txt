[Minigame: Sunfire_fanatic_armour]

Sunfire fanatic armouris a set ofprayer armourobtained as a possible reward from participating in theFortis Colosseum, starting at Wave 4. Players will receive one of each piece before receiving duplicates. It currently has the best Prayer bonus for the head, body, and legs equipment slots, with each armour piece having a +2 higher bonus compared to theirproselyte armourequivalents.

In order to wear the armour, the player must have aPrayerlevel of 60 and aDefencelevel of 40. The armour shares the same stats withrune armour, barring the lighter weight and higher prayer bonus. Because of the negative ranged and magic bonuses the set provides, it is only ideal for combat that utilises prayer and where accuracy is not essential.

Equipping any piece of sunfire fanatic armour is necessary for anelite clue scroll emote step. This set can be stored in thearmour case spaceof acostume roomin aplayer-owned house.Ultimate Ironmenwill be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
