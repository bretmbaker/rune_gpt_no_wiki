TheAsgarniaarea covers the entire Kingdom ofAsgarniaalong with the island ofEntrana, thePest Controlislands, andTroll Country. Instances of areas in Asgarnia and underground areas with an entrance in Asgarnia will also be included. Access toCatherbyfromWhite Wolf Mountainwill be restricted and prevented through the use of magical barriers.
