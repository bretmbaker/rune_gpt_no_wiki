Iron orecan be mined fromiron rocksat level 15Mining, providing 35 Miningexperience. It can be smelted at afurnaceinto aniron barat level 15Smithing, or along with twocoalinto asteel barat level 30 Smithing. Smelting iron bars has a 50% success rate, unless the player is wearing aring of forging, using theSuperheat Itemspell, or using theBlast Furnace. Smelting iron ore into a bar gives 12.5 Smithing experience, and smelting a steel bar gives 17.5 Smithing experience.
