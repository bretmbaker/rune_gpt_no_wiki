Acyclops(pluralcyclopes) is a large one-eyedgiant. They are known for dropping manyalchabledrops, so bringingnatureandfire runesis helpful as it allows players to make longer trips and more money without banking or taking up inventory space. They can only be attacked with melee.

Cyclopes may be killed as a substitute forhill giantson aSlayer task.

Defenders[edit|edit source]

Cyclopes in theWarriors' Guildhave a rare chance of dropping adefender. These defensive weapons range frombronzetodragonand must be obtained in order, starting with bronze, by killing cyclopes on the top floor of the Warriors' Guild and then showing the defender toKamfreenaoutside the chamber. Once a player earns the rune defender, they can enter the basement of the Warriors' Guild and kill the stronger cyclopes found there for a chance at the dragon defender. As this is located in the Warriors' Guild, cyclopes found in these areas are immune toRangedandMagicattacks and must be fought with melee combat.

[Note]: You do not need to have the defender in your inventory to be able to get the next one. The only requirement is that you use the defender onKamfreenawhen you get the drop.
