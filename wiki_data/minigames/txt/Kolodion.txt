Kolodionis the shape-shifting master of theMage Arena. He is fought to gain entrance to the arena, where players fight for their god of choice.

When a player fights him during theMage Arena Iminiquest, he first starts out as aman, then transforms into anOgre, agiant ice blue spider, alarge ghost, and finally ablack demon.

Strategy[edit|edit source]

Kolodion attacks players with the threegod spells, which all have a maximum hit of 20, regardless of his form. For this reason, it is recommended that players achieve level 37Prayerto useProtect from Magic.

Players should bring the requiredrunesfor their most powerful combat spell, sincemeleeandrangedattacks cannot be used against him. In addition, it is recommended to either bring good food to heal with, or a fewprayer potions.

Since the fight takes place in theWilderness, it's not recommended to bring expensive gear;monk,wizardorXerician robesare a good budget option.

God Spells[edit|edit source]

After Kolodion is defeated, players may enter thesparkling pool, leading them to a chamber where they can then worship one of the three god statues. Players will then receive agod capebased on their choice of worshiping the statue ofGuthix,SaradominandZamorak; they can then claim thatgod's staffby speaking to thechamber guardianfor free. After obtaining their free staff, players can buy additional staves of any god for80,000each.
