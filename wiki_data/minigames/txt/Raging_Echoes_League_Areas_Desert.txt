TheKharidian Desertarea covers the entirety of theKharidian Desert. TheEmir's Arenaminigame will not be available during this League, although the building itself is accessible. Instances of areas in the Kharidian Desert and underground areas with an entrance in the Kharidian Desert will also be included.
