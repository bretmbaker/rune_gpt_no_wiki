[Weapon: White Platelegs]


Thewhite platelegsare a piece ofWhite Knight armourthat requires 10Defenceand the completion of theWanted!questto wield. They have the same bonuses as theblack platelegs, with an added +1prayer bonus, and have the same stats as awhite plateskirtbut weigh more.
Players can purchase them fromSir Vyvinafter achieving therankof Novice (100black knightkills). They can also be bought on the Grand Exchange or obtained through trading other players.
