[Weapon: 3Rd Age Robe]


The3rd age robeis a part of the3rd age mage set. Requiring level 65Magicand 30Defenceto wear, it has the fifth highest magic attack bonus of any leg-slot item, only surpassed byblue moon tassets,Ahrim's robeskirt,Virtus robe bottomandancestral robe bottom. It has aMagic damagebonus of 1%.
The 3rd age robe is a possible reward fromhard,eliteandmastertierTreasure Trailsand cannot be made through any skills.
