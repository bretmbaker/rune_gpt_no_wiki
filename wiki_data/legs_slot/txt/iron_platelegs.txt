[Weapon: Iron Platelegs]


Iron platelegsaremeleeleg armour made ofiron. There is noDefencerequirement to wear them, as is the same withbronze armour. As such, it is recommended to skip bronze tier items and start out with iron tier items. They have the same defensive stats as aniron plateskirtbut weigh more.
They can be made at level 31Smithingwith 3iron bars, granting 75 experience.
