Anelite void robeis a piece ofElite Void Knight equipment. It can be upgraded from avoid knight robefor 200Void Knight commendation pointsby speaking to theElite Void Knight, although thehard Western Provinces Diarymust be completed to upgrade it. When worn in combination with the other pieces of Void Knight armour, there is set effect that depends on whichvoid helmis worn. Compared to thevoid knight robe, it has an additional +3prayer bonusand a +2.5% damage boost to Ranged and +5% damage boost to Mage when worn with theelite void top.
If the player dies below level 20Wilderness, it will remain in the player's inventory. However, the item will becomeunusableand must be repaired with250,000coinsby using the item onPerdu.
