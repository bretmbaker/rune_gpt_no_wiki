[Weapon: Mithril Plateskirt]


Mithril plateskirtsgive the fifth best stat bonuses of allplateskirts. This piece of armour can be made with Level 66Smithingby using 3mithril barson ananvil, therefore granting the player with 150 smithing experience. At least 20Defenceis required to wear it. Mithril plateskirts have similar stats tomithril platelegsbut weigh less.
