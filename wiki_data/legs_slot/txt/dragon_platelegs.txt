[Weapon: Dragon Platelegs]


Dragon platelegsare a rare piece of armour that require aDefencelevel of 60 to be worn. They are dropped by mostmetal dragons,brutal black dragons,skeletal wyvernsandrevenants. They have the same stats as thedragon plateskirt, including  weight.
Dragon platelegs can be combined with adragon legs/skirt ornament kitto make gold-trimmeddragon platelegs (g). This cosmetic enhancement won't change the stats in any way.
