[Weapon: Spined Chaps]


Spined chapsare a piece ofRanged armour. To wear spined chaps, the player must have at least 40Defence, 40Ranged, and have completedThe Fremennik Trialsquest. Stat-wise, they are identical toGilded d'hide chaps.
To obtain spined chaps, a player can either bring 2dagannoth hides, 1stretched hide, and7,500toSigli the HuntsmaninRellekka, receive it as a drop fromDagannoth Supreme, ortradewith another player.
