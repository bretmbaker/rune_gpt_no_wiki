[Weapon: Saradomin Plateskirt]


TheSaradomin plateskirtis a decoratedrune plateskirtthat is affiliated with the godSaradomin. Sharing the same stats as the rune plateskirt (with the exception of a +1prayer bonusand weighing 1kg more), the Saradomin plateskirt also looks visually similar. Its appearance differs in that the skirt features an ornamental white trim around the symbol of Saradomin. The plateskirt requires 40Defenceto wear andcannotbe made using theSmithingskill. Players can obtain a Saradomin plateskirt as a reward from completinghardTreasure Trails.
Wearing any piece of this armour set will causefollowers of Saradominto become unaggressive upon entering theGod Wars Dungeon.
