[Weapon: Rock-Shell Legs]


Rock-shell legsare a piece ofmelee armour. To wear rock-shell legs, players must have at least 40Defenceand have completedThe Fremennik Trialsquest. Compared toRune platelegs, Rock-shell legs have a +4 ranged attack bonus. They are the same in every other aspect.
To obtain rock-shell legs, players can either bring 2dagannoth hides, 1rock-shell splinter, and 7,500coinstoSkulgrimeninRellekka, or receive them as a drop fromDagannoth Rex.
