[Weapon: 3Rd Age Platelegs]


3rd age platelegsare3rd Age armour, requiring 65Defenceto equip. They have the third highest defence bonuses of all non-degradable meleeleg slotitems, only surpassed by thejusticiar legguardsandtorva platelegs. 3rd age platelegs are only obtainable as a reward fromhard,elite, andmastertierTreasure Trails.
They have slightly better melee defence thanbandos tassets, however they lackstrength bonus. Due to that and their price, they are rarely used.
