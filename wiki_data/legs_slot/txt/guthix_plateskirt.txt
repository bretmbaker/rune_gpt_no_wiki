[Weapon: Guthix Plateskirt]


TheGuthix plateskirtis a decoratedrune plateskirtthat is affiliated with the godGuthix. Sharing the same stats as the rune plateskirt (with the exception of a +1prayer bonusand weighing 1kg more), the Guthix plateskirt also looks visually similar. Its appearance differs in that the skirt features an ornamental green trim around the symbol of Guthix.
The plateskirt requires 40Defenceto wear andcannotbe made using theSmithingskill.
