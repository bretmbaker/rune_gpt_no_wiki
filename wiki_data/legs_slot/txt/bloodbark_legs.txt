[Weapon: Bloodbark Legs]


Bloodbark legsare a piece ofbloodbark armourworn in thelegs slot, requiring level 60Magicand 60Defenceto equip. The legs can be made with level 81Runecraftby bringing asplitbark legsand 500blood runesto either the trueBlood AltarorKourend's Blood Altar. Doing so requires players to have first learnt the secret to infuse the armour via reading therunescroll of bloodbark.
Each piece of bloodbark armour equipped increases the amount thatblood spellsheal by 2%. When the full set of armour is equipped, blood spells will heal the caster'sHitpointsby 35% of the amount of damage inflicted. It can be stored in amagic wardrobein a player-owned house.
