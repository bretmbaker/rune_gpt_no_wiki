[Weapon: Mystic Robe Bottom (Light)]


Amystic robe bottom (light)is magic armour that is part of themystic robe setworn in the legwear slot. It comes in blue, white, black, and the specialenchantedversion, but all offer the same bonuses. All mystic robe bottoms require 40Magicand 20Defenceto wear. It can be stored in amagic wardrobein a player-owned house.
