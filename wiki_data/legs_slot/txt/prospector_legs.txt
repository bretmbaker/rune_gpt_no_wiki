[Weapon: Prospector Legs]


Theprospector legsare leggings which can be bought from theMotherlode Minein exchange for 50golden nuggetsorVolcanic Minefor 34,000 points. These leggings increaseMiningexperience by 0.6%.
Like the rest of the Prospector kit, the legs provide partial protection against theheat of the Kharidian desert, adding 12 seconds to the interval between required sips from a waterskin, or 34 seconds if the entire set is worn.
