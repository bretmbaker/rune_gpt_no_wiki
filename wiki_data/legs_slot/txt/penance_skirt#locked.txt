[Weapon: Penance Skirt#Locked]


Apenance skirtis a piece ofarmourthat requires level 60Rangedand level 40Defenceto wear. Through participating in theBarbarian Assaultmini-game, players who earn 375honour pointsin each of the four roles, and who have killed thePenance Queen, can purchase the skirt fromCommander Connad, located on the main floor of theBarbarian Outpost.
If the player dies below level 20Wilderness, it will remain in the player's inventory. However, the item will be unusable and must be repaired with 20,000 coins by using the item onPerdu.
