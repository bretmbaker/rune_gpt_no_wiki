[Weapon: White Plateskirt]


Thewhite plateskirtis a piece ofWhite Knight armourthat requires 10Defenceand the completion of theWanted!questto wield. It has the same bonuses as ablack plateskirt, with an added +1prayer bonus, and have the same stats as thewhite platelegsbut weighs less.
Players can purchase it fromSir Vyvinafter achieving therankof Page (300black knightkills). It can also be bought on the Grand Exchange or obtained through trading other players.
