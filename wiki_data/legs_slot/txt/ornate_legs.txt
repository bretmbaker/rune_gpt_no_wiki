[Weapon: Ornate Legs]


Theornate legsare obtained from completing a clue during theCrack the Clue IIevent. Giving the same bonuses asiron platelegs, they are part of theornate armourset, and are obtainable only once if you already have them in your inventory or bank. If you happen to accidentally destroy them, you will need to discover them again.
In order to obtain/reobtain the legs, the player must dig East (Near blue flower) of the entrance to theAir Altarsouth ofFaladorwith araw herringin their inventory.
