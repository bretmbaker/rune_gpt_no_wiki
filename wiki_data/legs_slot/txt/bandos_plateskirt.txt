[Weapon: Bandos Plateskirt]


TheBandos plateskirtis a decoratedrune plateskirtthat is affiliated with the godBandos. It can be obtained as a reward from completinghardTreasure Trails. Sharing the same stats as the rune plateskirt (with the exception of a +1Prayer bonusand weighing 1kg more), the Bandos plateskirt also looks visually similar. Its appearance differs in that the skirt features an ornamental brown trim around the symbol of Bandos. The plateskirt requires 40Defenceto wear andcannotbe made using theSmithingskill.
This item can be stored in thetreasure chestof acostume room, as part of theBandos rune armourset.Ultimate Ironmenwill be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
