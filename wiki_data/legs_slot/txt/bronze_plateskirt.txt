[Weapon: Bronze Plateskirt]


Abronze plateskirtis the weakestplateskirtinOld School RuneScape. It has identical stat bonuses to thebronze platelegsbut weighs less. A player with 16Smithingcan make it by using threebronze barson ananvilwhile carrying ahammer, granting 37.5 smithingexperience. They can be bought fromRanael's Super Skirt StoreinAl Kharid.
