[Weapon: Corrupted Statius%27S Platelegs (Bh)#Activated]


Corrupted statius's platelegs (bh)is a piece of armour requiring 20Defenceto wear, and is obtained by convertingStatius's platelegs (bh)via aCorrupted Warrior. When worn alongsideCorrupted statius's full helm (bh)andplatebody (bh), the accuracy of meleespecial attacksare increased by 15%.
When in inactive form, it must be charged with 1,500,000 coins before it can be used by the player. In addition, it can only be used withinDaimon's Crater,Castle Wars,Clan Wars, andSoul Wars; attempting to attack anything outside the listed areas will result in the player being interrupted from doing so.
