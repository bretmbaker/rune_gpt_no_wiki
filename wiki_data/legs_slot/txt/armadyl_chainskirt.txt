[Weapon: Armadyl Chainskirt]


TheArmadyl chainskirtis an item that can be obtained fromKree'arraand his bodyguards in theGod Wars Dungeon. Along with theArmadyl helmetandchestplate, it is part of theArmadyl armourset, and requires 70DefenceandRangedto wear.
The chainskirt is light in weight, however, it penalises melee attack bonuses which may make it undesirable in situations where multiple attack styles are used.
