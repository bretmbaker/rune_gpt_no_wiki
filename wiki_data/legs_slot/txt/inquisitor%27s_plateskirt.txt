[Weapon: Inquisitor%27S Plateskirt]


TheInquisitor's plateskirtis a drop fromThe Nightmare, requiring 70Strengthand 30Defenceto equip, and is part of theInquisitor's armourset.
When equipped, the plateskirt boosts damage and accuracy by 0.5% when using thecrushattack style. If all three pieces of the armour are equipped, the boost is increased to 2.5% (7.5% if using theinquisitor's mace). In addition, it counts as aZamorakianitem inGod Wars Dungeon.
