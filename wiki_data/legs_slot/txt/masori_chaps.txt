[Weapon: Masori Chaps]


TheMasori chapsare obtained as a rare drop from theTombs of Amascut. They are part of theMasori armourset, and require level 80Rangedand 30Defenceto equip. As with the rest of the armour set, the chaps boast the highestrangedattack bonus of any item in thelegs slot.
The chaps can be upgraded into afortified variantby attaching threeArmadylean platesonto it, increasing the Defence level to equip it to level 80. This process requires level 90Crafting, and is not reversible.
