[Weapon: Skeletal Bottoms]


Skeletal bottomsare a members-only piece ofmagic armour. To wear skeletal bottoms, the player must have at least 40Defence, 40Magic, and have completedThe Fremennik Trialsquest.
To obtain skeletal bottoms, a player can either bring 2dagannoth hides, 1fibula piece, and 7500 coins toPeer the SeerinRellekka, receive them as a drop fromDagannoth Prime, or trade with another player.
