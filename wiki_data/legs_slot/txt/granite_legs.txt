[Weapon: Granite Legs]


Granite legsareplatelegswhich require 50 defence and 50 strength to be worn. They can only be obtained by killingSkeletal Wyverns.
Compared todragon platelegsandrune platelegs, they have a greater ranged defence bonus at the cost of worse melee defences. They also have 10 more negative magic attack bonus compared to metal platelegs and plateskirts, which can be beneficial for splashing.
