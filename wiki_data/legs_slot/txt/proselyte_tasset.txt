[Weapon: Proselyte Tasset]


Theproselyte tassetis a piece ofTemple Knight Armourthat requires 30Defence, 20Prayer, and completion ofThe Slug Menacequest to equip. Along with itsplatelegs counterpart, it is tied with the3rd age druidic robe bottomsand theZealot's robe bottomfor the second highestPrayer bonusof any leg slot equipment, beaten only by theSunfire fanatic chausses.
It has the exact same bonuses as anAdamant plateskirt, except that it offers +6 Prayer bonus and weighs less. Like otherProselyteequipment, it is white with a dark-gold trim.
