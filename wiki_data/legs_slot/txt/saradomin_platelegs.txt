[Weapon: Saradomin Platelegs]


TheSaradomin platelegsare a decorated version ofrune platelegsthat are affiliated with the godSaradomin. Sharing the same stats as rune platelegs (with the addition of a +1prayer bonus), the Saradomin platelegs also look visually similar. Their appearance differs in that they feature an ornamental white trim around the symbol of Saradomin. The platelegs require 40Defenceto wear andcannotbe made using theSmithingskill. Players can obtain Saradomin platelegs as a reward from completinghardTreasure Trails.

Wearing any piece of this armour set causes followers of Saradomin to become unaggressive to the player.
