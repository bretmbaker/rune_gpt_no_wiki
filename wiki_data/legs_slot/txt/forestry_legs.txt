[Weapon: Forestry Legs]

Like the lumberjack legs, the Forestry legs provide a +0.6% boost inWoodcuttingexperience, and is increased to +2.5% when the fullForestry outfitis worn. Additionally, the outfit can be stored in aclothes pouchto receive the outfit's benefits without having to wear them. However, bonuses from other outfits, such as thegraceful outfit, will override the clothes pouch if worn.
