[Weapon: Swampbark Legs]


Swampbark legsare a piece ofswampbark armourworn in thelegs slot, requiring level 50Magicand 50Defenceto equip. The legs can be made with level 48Runecraftby bringing asplitbark legsand 500nature runesto theNature Altar. Doing so requires players to have first learnt the secret to infuse the armour via reading therunescroll of swampbark.
In addition to their stats, the helm, body, and legs increases the duration of bind spells by 2ticks(1.2 seconds), with the full set increasing the duration by 6 ticks (3.6 seconds). For comparison,Ice Burstbinds players for 16 ticks (9.6 secs), whileIce Barragebinds players for 32 ticks (19.2 secs).
