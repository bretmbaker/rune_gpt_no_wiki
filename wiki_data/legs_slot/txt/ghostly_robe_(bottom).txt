[Weapon: Ghostly Robe (Bottom)]


Theghostly robeis a piece ofmember's onlyarmour. Players can obtain one after speaking toLennissaduring theCurse of the Empty Lordminiquest. The robe bottom, according to Lennissa, was a spare robe that she wore during one of her assignments.
There are no requirements to wear and, when worn with the other pieces, the player appears 'ghostlike' (i.e. transparent). After initially obtaining the piece, the player can talk to any of the ghosts to get more pieces. A lost robe can be purchased fromPerdufor 43,000 coins.
