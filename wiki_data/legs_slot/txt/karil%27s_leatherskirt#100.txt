Karil's leatherskirtisRanged armourworn in thelegwear slotthat is part ofKaril the Tainted's setofBarrows equipment. To wear Karil's leatherskirt, a player must have 70Defenceand 70Ranged.
Karil's leatherskirt gives the third highestmagic defencefor the leg slot at +35, followingArmadyl chainskirt's +40, andMasori chaps (f)'s +46. The skirt has the same offensive bonuses as theblack d'hide chaps, while offering higher defence in all butSlashin which it is on par.
