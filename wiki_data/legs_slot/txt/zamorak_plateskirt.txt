[Weapon: Zamorak Plateskirt]


TheZamorak plateskirtis a decoratedrune plateskirtaffiliated with the godZamorak. Sharing the same stats as the rune plateskirt (with the exception of a +1prayer bonusand weighing 1kg more), the Zamorak plateskirt also looks visually similar. Its appearance differs in that the skirt features an ornamental red trim, manifesting as the symbol of Zamorak.
The plateskirt requires 40Defenceto wear andcannotbe made using theSmithingskill. Players can obtain a Zamorak plateskirt as a reward for completinghardTreasure Trails.
