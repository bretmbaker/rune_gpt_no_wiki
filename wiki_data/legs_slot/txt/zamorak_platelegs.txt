[Weapon: Zamorak Platelegs]


Zamorak platelegsare a trimmed version ofrune platelegswhich are associated with the godZamorak. Zamorak platelegs have the same stats as rune platelegs, apart from aPrayer bonusof 1. Like rune platelegs, these platelegs require 40Defenceto wear and cannot be made using theSmithingskill.
Players can obtain Zamorak platelegs as a reward from completinghardTreasure Trails. Wearing Zamorak platelegs in theGod Wars Dungeonwill cause followers of Zamorak to becomeunaggressive.
