[Weapon: Dark Squall Robe Bottom]


TheDark squall robe bottomis a piece ofDark Squall robesobtained duringWhile Guthix Sleeps, from a wardrobe in theDark Squall's inner base within theBlack Knight Catacombs. They are worn to disguise the player as the Dark Squall, later revealed to beSurok Magis; once acquired, they can also be used to enter theBlack Knights' Fortress.
It has stats similar to that of aZamorak monk bottom, and can be stored in amagic wardrobein a player-owned house. After completion of While Guthix Sleeps, they can be reclaimed fromSilifif lost.
