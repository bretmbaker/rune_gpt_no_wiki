[Weapon: Mourner Trousers]


Themourner trousersare part of themourner set. To obtain them, players must kill a level 11Mourneron the path toPrifddinas. However, the Mourner will bring all combat stats down to 20, so it is recommended players bring astat restore potionbefore fighting.
When the player first obtains the trousers they arerippedand must be repaired. Players need to take two silks and a bear fur toOronwen, the Elven Seamstress inLletya, to have the trousers repaired.
