[Weapon: Black Desert Robe]


Ablack desert robeis an item of clothing that players may find useful when doing theShadow of the Stormquest. They can make it by usingblack dyeon a normaldesert robe. This can also be done with adesert shirtto create ablack desert shirt.
The quest requires players to be wearing at least three black garments, in order to look evil. Players may already be wearing a set of desert robes (since the main part of the quest takes place in theKharidian Desert), andblack mushroomscan be found locally.
