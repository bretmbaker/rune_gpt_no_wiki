Purple elegant skirt is a formal, fancy item of elegant clothing that can be obtained from medium Treasure Trails.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room, as part of the purple elegant clothing set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
