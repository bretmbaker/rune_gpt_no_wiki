Skeletal bottoms are a members-only piece of magic armour. To wear skeletal bottoms, the player must have at least 40 Defence, 40 Magic, and have completed The Fremennik Trials quest.

To obtain skeletal bottoms, a player can either bring 2 dagannoth hides, 1 fibula piece, and 7500 coins to Peer the Seer in Rellekka, receive them as a drop from Dagannoth Prime, or trade with another player.

These bottoms can be stored in the magic wardrobe of a costume room, as part of the skeletal armour set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
