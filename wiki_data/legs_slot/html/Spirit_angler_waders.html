The Spirit angler waders are an upgraded version of the angler waders. They can be purchased from Gita Prymes by exchanging angler waders along with 1,200 spirit flakes. The waders grant 0.6% bonus Fishing experience. Tempoross has a skill requirement of 35 Fishing; therefore, the waders require level 35 Fishing to wear.

Wearing the complete Spirit angler's outfit acts as a rope for tethering to totem poles and masts while battling Tempoross, and grants a total of 2.5% bonus fishing experience.

This item can be stored in the armour case of a costume room, as part of the Spirit angler's outfit set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored. It can also be stored in a tackle box.
