The slave robe is obtained by trading a desert robe with a slave for use in The Tourist Trap quest. They are required to enter the mine undetected. Rowdy slaves can also be killed in the mine to drop it.

Wearing the slave robe will delay the onset of desert heat by 6 seconds.
