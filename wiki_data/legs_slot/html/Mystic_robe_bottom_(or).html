The mystic robe bottom (or) is a mystic robe bottom with a shattered relics mystic ornament kit added onto it. The kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the robe bottom.

The ornament kit is only compatible with the standard blue mystic robe bottom; other variants of the mystic robe bottom cannot be used.

Whilst in its ornamented state, the robe bottom can be reverted anytime, returning the mystic robe bottom and kit.
