The blue skirt (g) is obtainable by completing easy Treasure Trails, and is part of the blue gold-trimmed wizard robes. It is the gold-trimmed version of the blue skirt.

It is often worn for its visual appeal, as well as the fact that it can be used as a status symbol for free players that cannot wear or afford the slightly more expensive decorative rune platelegs (guthix platelegs, rune platelegs (g), etc).

Unlike the regular skirt, it cannot be combined with Black dye to try to create a Black skirt (g).

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room, as part of the blue gold-trimmed wizard robes. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
