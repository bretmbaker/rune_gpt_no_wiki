The Menaphite purple kilt is part of the Menaphite clothing set. Wearing it will delay the onset of desert heat by 12 seconds.

Players can obtain this kilt during, and after, the Rogue Trader miniquest by trading with Ali Morrisane after giving Siamun three dyes of any colour.

This kilt is available in purple and red, where red is worn in Sophanem and purple is worn in Pollnivneach.
