The Agility Arena graceful legs are one of many variants of the graceful legs. They are recoloured by speaking to Pirate Jackie the Fruit at the Brimhaven Agility Arena in exchange for 250 Brimhaven vouchers; this requires having the full set.

This item can be stored in the magic wardrobe of a costume room.
