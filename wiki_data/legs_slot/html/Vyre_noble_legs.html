Vyre noble legs are a piece of vyre noble clothing that has been scented with the blood of Damien Leucurte, a vyre noble.

Initially obtained by killing Damien Leucurte during Sins of the Father, it can be reclaimed from the chest in the Myreque Hideout under Old Man Ral's house.

The armour can be stored in the armour case of a player-owned house costume room.
