Zamorak platelegs are a trimmed version of rune platelegs which are associated with the god Zamorak. Zamorak platelegs have the same stats as rune platelegs, apart from a Prayer bonus of 1. Like rune platelegs, these platelegs require 40 Defence to wear and cannot be made using the Smithing skill.

Players can obtain Zamorak platelegs as a reward from completing hard Treasure Trails. Wearing Zamorak platelegs in the God Wars Dungeon will cause followers of Zamorak to become unaggressive.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room, as part of the Zamorak armour set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
