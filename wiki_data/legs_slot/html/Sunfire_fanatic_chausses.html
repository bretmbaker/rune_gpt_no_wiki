The sunfire fanatic chausses are an item that requires level 60 Prayer and 40 Defence to equip, obtained as a possible reward from participating in the Fortis Colosseum. Part of the sunfire fanatic armour set, they provide the best in slot prayer bonus for the legs slot, and also have identical defence bonuses to the rune platelegs.

This item can be stored in the armour case of a costume room, as part of the Sunfire fanatic armour set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
