[Page: Tutorial Island bank]

TheTutorial Island bankis the onlybankon theTutorial Island. The bank has three floors: immediately to the left of the booths is a ladder to a vault on the1stfloor[UK]2ndfloor[US]. Slightly behind and to the right of the booths is a ladder to another area on the1stfloor[UK]2ndfloor[US], which leads to a vault on the2ndfloor[UK]3rdfloor[US].

TheAccount Guideis also found here.
