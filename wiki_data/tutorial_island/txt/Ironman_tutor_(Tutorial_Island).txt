[Page: Ironman tutor (Tutorial Island)]

PaultheIronman tutoris found onTutorial Island, and teaches new players aboutIronman Mode. He can change the player's Ironman status freely before they leave Tutorial Island. Another Ironman tutor,Adam, is found inLumbridgeand can help players downgrade or lock their Ironman status once off Tutorial Island.

The player may also choose to create aGroup Ironmanwith other players when selecting their Ironman status. Paul will then instruct the player to continue to the tutorial and visitThe Node.
