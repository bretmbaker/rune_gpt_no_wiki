[Page: Chicken (Tutorial Island)]

Chickensare found onTutorial Islandas part of theMagictraining tutorial. TheMagic Instructorprovidesairandmind runesto castWind Strikeon a chicken to progress in the tutorial.

The chickens are located behind a fence, so they can't be attacked using melee, and any dropped bones can't be picked up.
