TheQuest Guideis the fourthnon-player characterwhom players meet inRuneScape. He gives players information aboutquestsand thequest journal. He also explains the differences betweenfreequests andmembersquests. He is only found onTutorial Island, and can never be encountered again. During his lecture, he makes references to the questsImp CatcherandDragon Slayer I.
