[Page: Shrimps]

Shrimpis a type of fish that restores 3Hitpointswhen eaten. It can be obtained by cookingraw shrimpon afireorcooking range, granting 30experiencewhen successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting inburnt shrimp. Theburn ratedecreases as the player'sCookinglevel increases. Shrimp is one of a number of foods with a reduced burn rate while using theLumbridge Castle rangewithCook's Assistantcompleted. It will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 34, or 31 when using the Lumbridge range.
Shrimp are one of the fish required byCalebin the questFamily Crest.
Rickcan make shrimps for the player in aplayer-owned house.
