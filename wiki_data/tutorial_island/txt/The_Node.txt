[Page: The Node]

The Nodeis a group of three small islands just south ofTutorial Islandthat are only accessible toGroup Ironmanplayers. The islands are connected to each other via bridges, resembling an upside-downIronman helm. Together, the islands form the southern-most part of the kingdom ofMisthalin.
Players can use either the boat on southern end of Tutorial Island or the portal near theLumbridge General Storeto reach the northern island of The Node, which connects via the docks to the central (largest) island of the area.
