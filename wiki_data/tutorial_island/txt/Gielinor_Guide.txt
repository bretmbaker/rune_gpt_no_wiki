[Page: Gielinor Guide]

TheGielinor Guide(formerlyRuneScape Guide) is the firstnon-player character(NPC) that players meet in the game. He is found in his house onTutorial Island. He gives a brief but informative chat about the game.

At this NPC, a player is required to choose theircharacter's looks.
These include:
