[Page: Magic Instructor]

TheMagic instructor, whose real name is Terrova, is the last instructor new players meet onTutorial Island. He teaches players aboutMagicand how to cast spells, and provided them with 5air runesand 5mind runes.

The Magic instructor is found in the building at the end of the dirt road onTutorial Island. He has a large cage withchickensin it for players to train with theruneshe provides to them (you could only userangedor magic attacks on the chickens because the cage is only made of walls and a metal fence, which doesn't have a gate).
