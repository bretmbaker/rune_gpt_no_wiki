Bread doughis made by mixing either abucket of water,bowl of water, orjug of waterwith apot of flour. It can be used on arangeto make a loaf ofbreadat level 1Cooking, granting 40 experience and healing 5hitpointswhen consumed. It is one of the most basic items used for the Cooking skill. Players will also get a pot of flour and a bucket of water to make bread dough onTutorial Island, as a tutorial for cooking.
