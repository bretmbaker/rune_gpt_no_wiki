The Tutorial Island bank is the only bank on the Tutorial Island . The bank has three floors: immediately to the left of the booths is a ladder to a vault on the 1 st floor [ UK ] 2 nd floor [ US ] . Slightly behind and to the right of the booths is a ladder to another area on the 1 st floor [ UK ] 2 nd floor [ US ] , which leads to a vault on the 2 nd floor [ UK ] 3 rd floor [ US ] .

The Account Guide is also found here.
