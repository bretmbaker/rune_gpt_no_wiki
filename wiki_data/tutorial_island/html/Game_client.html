Game client

There are several game clients , both official and unofficial, that are allowed for playing Old School RuneScape .

The official clients are:

  • The New Official Client , a C++ conversion of the client released through the Steam gaming platform and offered on the Jagex Launcher .
  • Old School RuneScape Mobile , the client for Android and iOS.

The officially approved third party clients are:

  • RuneLite
  • HDOS


Discontinued official client:

  • The Legacy Java Client , the original client written in Java which used to be embedded in browser.

Discontinued third party client:

  • OSBuddy
