Copper ore can be mined at level 1 Mining providing 17.5 Mining experience. After mining copper rocks , it will take 2.4 seconds until it reappears. Copper ore is used with tin ore to smelt bronze bars . Smelting a bronze bar gives 6.2 Smithing experience.
