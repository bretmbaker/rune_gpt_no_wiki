The Magic instructor , whose real name is Terrova, is the last instructor new players meet on Tutorial Island . He teaches players about Magic and how to cast spells, and provided them with 5 air runes and 5 mind runes .

The Magic instructor is found in the building at the end of the dirt road on Tutorial Island . He has a large cage with chickens in it for players to train with the runes he provides to them (you could only use ranged or magic attacks on the chickens because the cage is only made of walls and a metal fence, which doesn't have a gate).
