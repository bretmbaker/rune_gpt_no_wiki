Bread is a type of food that restores 5 Hitpoints when eaten. It can be obtained by baking bread dough on a cooking range , granting 40 experience when successful. When cooking there is a chance to fail, resulting in burnt bread . The burn rate decreases as the player's Cooking level increases. Bread is one of a number of foods with a reduced burn rate while using the Lumbridge Castle range with Cook's Assistant completed. It will stop burning entirely at Cooking level 38, or 34 when using the Lumbridge range. Bread may also be bought or stolen from the Ardougne Baker's Stall in the Ardougne marketplace.
