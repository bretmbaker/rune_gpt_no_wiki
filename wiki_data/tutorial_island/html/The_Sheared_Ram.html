The Sheared Ram is a small bar in Lumbridge that sells beer . Here, players can speak to Veos , an experienced adventurer from Great Kourend , a kingdom far west of the mainland. Players who are new to clue scrolls may also speak to Arthur the Clue Hunter to learn more about them.

Controversy [ edit | edit source ]

On release, the Sheared Ram replaced what was a house in Lumbridge containing a man and a woman . There was noted contention among some players resulting from the alteration of what some considered to be an iconic or memorable part of the RuneScape World map .

  • The exterior of the house that existed before being changed into a bar.

  • The interior of the house that existed before being changed into a bar.

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • The name of the bar comes from when new players try to complete the Sheep Shearer quest and almost inevitably left-click a ram while completely unprepared for combat.
  • When using an oculus orb in the Stranglewood 's mine , players can spot another branch of The Sheared Ram pub to the east.
