The Node is a group of three small islands just south of Tutorial Island that are only accessible to Group Ironman players. The islands are connected to each other via bridges, resembling an upside-down Ironman helm . Together, the islands form the southern-most part of the kingdom of Misthalin .

Northern island [ edit | edit source ]

Players can use either the boat on southern end of Tutorial Island or the portal near the Lumbridge General Store to reach the northern island of The Node, which connects via the docks to the central (largest) island of the area.

Central island [ edit | edit source ]

There are several structures on the main island:

Structure Description
Group ironman bank A small booth where the Group Storage tutor teaches Group Ironmen about their shared bank.
Group ironman pavilion A small building featuring the Group Ironman tutor , who gives information and armour, and can help players setup or invite other members to their group.
Statue A statue dedicated to Group Ironmen.

Southern island [ edit | edit source ]

This island contains boars , enraged boars , and The Node mine , where iron rocks can be found.
