A pot of flour is a Cooking ingredient used to produce several types of dough when combined with water .

Using an empty pot on a barrel of flour is a faster alternative to having to get grain and use a windmill. One barrel is in the kitchen of Sinclair Mansion after starting the Murder Mystery quest and another barrel is located on the 1 st floor [ UK ] 2 nd floor [ US ] of Gordon and Mary's farm .

Creation [ edit | edit source ]

Only one grain can be processed at a time, but the flour bin can store up to 30 flour before it needs to be emptied. Flour can be collected at any windmill except in Zanaris and on Harmony Island .

Steps
1. Put grain into the hopper at the top of a windmill
2. Activate the hopper controls
3. Use a pot on the flour bin on the ground floor [ UK ] 1 st floor [ US ] to collect a pot of flour
