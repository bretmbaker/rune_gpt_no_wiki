Adventurer Jon is a seasoned adventurer found outside The Sheared Ram pub in Lumbridge . He tells players about the Adventure Paths task system and is the person players go to to claim the combat path starter kit and combat path vouchers .

Players can only see him by being eligible for Adventure Paths, which requires telling the Gielinor Guide at the very beginning of Tutorial Island that they are either a new player or are a player that has played in the past, but not recently. In addition, ironmen cannot see him.
