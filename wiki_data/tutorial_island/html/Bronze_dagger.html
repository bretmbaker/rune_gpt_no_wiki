The bronze dagger is the most basic dagger in Old School RuneScape , and can be made at an anvil using the Smithing skill. This requires one bronze bar and a hammer , and yields 12.5 Smithing experience . There are no level requirements to create or wield a bronze dagger; like many melee weapons, it can be poisoned .

Changes [ edit | edit source ]

Date Changes
7July 2021
( update )

The position of the weapon and its variants on female players has been adjusted so that they appear to be equipped properly.

10December 2014
( update )

The super strong poisoned variant was renamed from "Br'ze dagger(p++)" to "Bronze dagger(p++)".

22August 2006
( update )
  • The extra strong poisoned variant was renamed from "Bronze dagger(+)" to "Bronze dagger(p+)".
  • The super strong poisoned variant was renamed from "Bronze dagger(s)" to "Br'ze dagger(p++)".

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • It is the third weapon that players obtain on Tutorial Island , after the bronze axe and bronze pickaxe .
    • It is the first weapon that players obtain which is not also a tool .
    • Despite becoming seemingly obsolete as the player is given a bronze sword in the next section of the tutorial, the bronze dagger does have some usefulness during the Magic Instructor 's task given its small magic attack bonus.
  • The bronze dagger is denounced by Vannaka as being a "butter knife". Because of this, it is commonly regarded as the "worst" weapon in the game. However, the bronze 2h sword arguably deals less damage per second.
  • It is the second-weakest dagger in the game, behind the Dark / Glowing dagger obtained during Legends' Quest , which has marginally superior accuracy bonuses but no strength bonus.
