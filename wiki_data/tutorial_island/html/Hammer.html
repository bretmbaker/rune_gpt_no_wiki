A hammer is a tool used for smithing items on an Anvil , and it is vital to level the Smithing skill. Hammers can be bought from most general stores and will never break. Alternatively, players can obtain a hammer from talking to to Master smithing tutor at the West Varrock anvil. A hammer is also used in the Construction skill when making furniture, and it is required for some minigames. At 15 Construction, members can make a Tool store 1 for the workshop room of their house; this tool store provides an unlimited supply of hammers.

A hammer is required to access Bandos' stronghold in the God Wars Dungeon ; warhammers are an acceptable substitute.

The Imcando hammer can be used instead of a hammer when performing tasks like Construction or Smithing .
