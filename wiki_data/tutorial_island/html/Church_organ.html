Church organs are interactive scenery found in churches throughout Old School RuneScape . Players can play the organ to hear one of two unique songs.

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • Slepe church has an organ on the 1 st floor [ UK ] 2 nd floor [ US ] , but it cannot be played.
