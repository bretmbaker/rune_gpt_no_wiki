Music Player

The Music Player tab displays what music theme is playing at the moment. The music is unlocked as you play the game and explore new places and contents.

Players can click on the "Rand" button at the top of the screen to shuffle between music the player has unlocked. Players can also click on green titles to choose which track to listen to. Titles in red have not been unlocked, and thus cannot be played. There are a total of 795 music tracks.

Changes [ edit | edit source ]

Date Changes
14 June 2023
( update )

The "LOOP" option has been replaced with a "Rand" option, which plays a random music track that the player has unlocked. The LOOP option has been moved to the Audio settings.

27 February 2014
( update | poll )

The Music Loop button now remembers its setting over logout.
