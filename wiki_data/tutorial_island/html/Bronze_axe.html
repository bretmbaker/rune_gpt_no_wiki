The Bronze axe is the weakest axe in Old School RuneScape . It can be wielded for combat, or used to cut trees in Woodcutting . It requires an Attack level of 1 to be equipped, and a Woodcutting level of 1 to be used.

Players can make a bronze axe with the Smithing skill at level 1 using one Bronze bar , giving the player 12.5 Smithing experience . Players can take one for free from logs found at various locations, such as at the chicken farm by the Lumbridge cow field , or Fred the Farmer 's house. One can also be obtained by talking to the Woodsman tutor .

Various activities require the use of an any axe, such as chopping Kraken tentacles on the Fishing Trawler , defeating the growthlings during the Bryophyta boss fight, or cutting bruma roots to fight Wintertodt . A bronze axe is available to the player from the nearby environment in these activities: on the Fishing Trawler from a crate marked axes , at Bryophyta from a pile of logs , and at Wintertodt from a crate .
