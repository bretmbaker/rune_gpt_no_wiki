Spria is a Slayer master and daughter of Turael . She lives in a house in Draynor Village and assists the player with Sarah 's bounty during the quest A Porcine of Interest .

After the quest, she acts as a Slayer master, assigning the same tasks as her father Turael, with the addition of being able to assign sourhogs . She is the only Slayer master who can assign them. However, she cannot change a Slayer task from another master into a less difficult one; she will instruct players to visit Turael to do so. Additionally, players cannot skip Spria's tasks using Turael.

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • In RuneScape , Spria replaces Turael as Burthorpe's Slayer Master during and after While Guthix Sleeps . As Spria was added prior to the Old School RuneScape release of While Guthix Sleeps, Aya is instead the replacement for Turael.
  • She appears to be wearing a binding necklace , Antisanta boots , leather chaps , and modified versions of Karil's crossbow and adamant platebody .
