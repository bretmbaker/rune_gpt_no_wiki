Item: Cap'n Izzy No-Beard
Summary: Cap'n Izzy No-Beard is a pirate captain and master of Agility who can be found at the entrance of the Brimhaven Agility Arena minigame. He has a pet parrot which he says has "cracked Jenny's tea cup." He and his parrot guard the entrance to the Agility arena, with an entry fee of 200 coins to use the arena.
