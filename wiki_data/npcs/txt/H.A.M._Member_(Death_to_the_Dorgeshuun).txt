Item: H.A.M. Member (Death to the Dorgeshuun)
Summary: A H.A.M. Member is a member of Humans Against Monsters seen carrying crates into the caves below Seth Groats's farm during Death to the Dorgeshuun. He picks up his crates near the dwarf by the riverbank, carries them into the cave, and returns the empty crates to a pile just south of the cave entrance.
