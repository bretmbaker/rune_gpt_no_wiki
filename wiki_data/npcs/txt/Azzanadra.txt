Item: Azzanadra
Summary: Azzanadra is a powerful Zarosian Mahjarrat, general and chief high priest of Zaros. He was trapped in the Ancient Pyramid during the God Wars. He is freed by the player during Desert Treasure I; in return he teaches the player how to use Ancient Magicks as a reward. He aids the player in Desert Treasure II - The Fallen Empire in disguise as Dr Banikan, himself being in search of the Frostenhorn artefact.
