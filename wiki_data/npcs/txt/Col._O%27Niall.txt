Item: Col. O'Niall
Summary: Colonel Jake O'Niall is a semi-retired Temple Knight residing in Witchaven. He still works with the Temple Knights and helps the player to investigate Witchaven during The Slug Menace quest. At the end of the quest, he is possessed by a Sea slug and has been ordered to be killed on sight. He later disappears from Witchaven, leaving behind only his chair.
