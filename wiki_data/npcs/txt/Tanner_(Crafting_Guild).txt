Item: Tanner (Crafting Guild)
Summary: Tanner is a tanner on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] of the Crafting Guild. He will tan hides for a small fee per item. Members who have claimed the rewards for completing the hard Falador Diary or have 99 Crafting can use the bank chest on the ground floor[UK]1st floor[US]. This is the fastest method of tanning hides. With maximum focus, up to 9000 leather can be tanned per hour.
