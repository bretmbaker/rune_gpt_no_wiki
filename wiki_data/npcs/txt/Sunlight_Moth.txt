Item: Sunlight Moth
Summary: The sunlight moth is a creature found in the Avium Savannah, directly south of the Hunter Guild, that players can catch at level 65 Hunter with a butterfly net and jar. Players with level 75 Hunter can catch sunlight moths barehanded. Sunlight moths may be assigned as a hunters' rumour task starting at level 75 by Guild Hunters Gilman, Cervus, or Teco.
