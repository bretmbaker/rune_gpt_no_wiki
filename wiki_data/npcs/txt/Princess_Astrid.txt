Item: Princess Astrid
Summary: Princess Astrid is the daughter of King Vargas, the ruler of Miscellania. She lives with her father and her brother Prince Brand in the castle on the western side of their island. Players must get her or her brother to agree to marry them during the Throne of Miscellania quest, although they don't get married immediately. She also features in the Royal Trouble quest. She is rather vain (a quality she shares with her brother) and loves flowers and archery.
