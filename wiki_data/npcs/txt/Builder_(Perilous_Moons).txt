Item: Builder (Perilous Moons)
Summary: Builder 1 and Builder 2 are a pair of builders that transport the building supplies used to make the campsites within Neypotzli during the Perilous Moons quest. Before the quest, they can be found outside the entrance to Cam Torum, blocked from entering by Attala. After the quest, they remain within the Neypotzli antechamber.
