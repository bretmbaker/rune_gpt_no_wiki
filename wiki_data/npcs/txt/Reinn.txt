Item: Reinn
Summary: Reinn is a Fremennik teenager trying to pass his trial of adulthood. To do this, he and his friends Armod, Hild, Signy, and Beigarth took part in a plot to create a war between Miscellania and Etceteria, which they could then settle, cheating their way through their trials. He  is involved in the Royal Trouble quest.
