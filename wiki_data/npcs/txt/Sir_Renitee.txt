Item: Sir Renitee
Summary: Sir Renitee is the chief herald of Falador, he is located on the east end of the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] of the White Knights' Castle in Falador. He can sell the player various paintings, maps, to place inside a player-owned house and tell one's heritage to paint as a crest on a steel, adamant or rune kiteshield or on steel, adamant or rune full helms.
