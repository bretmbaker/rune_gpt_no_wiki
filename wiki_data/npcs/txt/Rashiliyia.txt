Item: Rashiliyia
Summary: Queen Rashiliyia, wife of King Danthalas, mother of Bervirius, is the ghost who sent the undead ones to Shilo Village. 
Rashiliyia was a powerful sorceress and queen of an unknown kingdom on Karamja during the Third Age. Rashiliyia was in love with Zadimus, a high priest of Zamorak, who resided in the magnificent temple of Ah Za Rhoon. When Zadimus rejected her, she tortured and hanged him before collapsing the entire temple.
