Item: Ennius Tullus
Summary: Ennius Tullus is a servant of the Royal Family of Varlamore, dealing with administrative work alongside his twin sister Furia Tullus. Both were a part of the delegation visiting Varrock on behalf of the Kingdom of Varlamore during Children of the Sun. He is also involved in Twilight's Promise, recruiting the player's help in rounding up six knights to help discover the traitor among them.
