Item: Brother Jered
Summary: Brother Jered is a monk at the Edgeville Monastery. Players may speak to him to have their Unblessed symbol blessed one at a time. Since he is on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US], only those with a Prayer level of 31 can access him. Players who achieve level 99 Prayer may purchase the Prayer cape from him for 99,000 coins.
