Item: Boneguard
Summary: The Boneguards are spirits who have been bound to guard Enakhra's Temple against their will. They are encountered during Enakhra's Lament. The first Boneguard that the player encounters must be defeated with the use of the Crumble Undead spell, allowing the player to climb over its bones. The second Boneguard must be spoken to with the Protect from Melee Prayer activated in order to endure its attacks during the conversation.
