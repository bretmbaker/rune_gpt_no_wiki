Item: Archpriest of the Unicorn
Summary: The Archpriest of the Unicorn is a ghost who can be found while looting the Grand Hallowed Coffin in the Hallowed Sepulchre. There is a 1% chance of this occurring.[1] After this encounter, the Archpriest of the Unicorn can be found in the lobby of the Hallowed Sepulchre while the ring of visibility or ring of shadows is equipped.
