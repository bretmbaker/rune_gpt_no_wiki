Item: Osmumten
Summary: Osmumten was the last pharaoh of the line of pharaohs chosen by Tumeken, the God of the Sun. Besides being ruler, Osmumten was a passionate hunter, wielding his specially designed weapon, he adventured across the land, fighting the greatest beasts. Osmumten was the pharaoh somewhere around the Second Age, when the present-day Kharidian Desert was invaded by the Zarosian Empire.
