Item: Thurgo
Summary: Thurgo, the supposed last of the Imcando dwarf race, is found outside the Asgarnian Ice Dungeon, south of Port Sarim, and just north of Mudskipper Point. You can use the fairy ring code AIQ and run north to reach him. He is a master of Smithing and players who have achieved level 99 in Smithing can purchase the Smithing cape from him for 99,000 coins. Additionally, he is one of the main characters encountered during the The Knight's Sword quest.
