Item: Merlin
Summary: Merlin can be found on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] of Camelot castle in his workshop, accessed via stairs in the entry room. Note that his (yellow) minimap icon does not show up until you are in his workshop. If the player has not completed the Merlin's Crystal quest, he will be imprisoned within a block-shaped crystal on the top floor of the south-east tower.
