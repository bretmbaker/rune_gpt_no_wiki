Item: Jorral
Summary: Jorral is a historian located at his Outpost, which is surrounded by aggressive level 64 wolves and level 42 hobgoblins, north of West Ardougne. He starts the Making History quest. During Making History, Jorral enlists the help of the player to save the outpost from King Lathas by researching its past. After the player saves the outpost, Jorral turns it into a small museum.
