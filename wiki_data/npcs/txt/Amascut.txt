Item: Amascut
Summary: Amascut (Jagex pronunciation: AH-mass-cut), also known as "The Devourer", is the demi-goddess of destruction,[1] formerly the goddess of rebirth, and one of the four major gods of the Menaphite Pantheon. Amascut is the creation of Elidinis and Tumeken and the sister of Icthlarin.[2]
