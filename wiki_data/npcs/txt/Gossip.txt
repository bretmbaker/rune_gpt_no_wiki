Item: Gossip
Summary: The Gossip starts the King's Ransom quest and is partially involved with Murder Mystery. He does not talk much to players. During King's Ransom, he provides important background history on the Sinclair family, as well as Anna Sinclair. He can be found just around the guard you speak to in Murder Mystery. When you speak to him in Murder Mystery, he will tell you that he saw a poison salesman who came in the village and gave a huge sale on poison to the family.
