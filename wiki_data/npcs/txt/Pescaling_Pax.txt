Item: Pescaling Pax
Summary: Pescaling Pax is the head of research and development for the penguins under the Iceberg as well as one of the sons of The Pescallion. He plays a role in Cold War. His main scheme is to take over the world with an army of well-trained penguins and espionage. Therefore, he pretends to be a king and, although he is not, this desire is the most likely explanation for his crown and pose.
