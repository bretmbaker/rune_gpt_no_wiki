Item: Sarah (Port Phasmatys)
Summary: Sarah Corvo is an inhabitant of Port Phasmatys and descendant of the hero Tristan, she is met during the Dragon Slayer II quest. She gives information on the location of the Morytania dragon key piece, which she buried in the swamps to avoid letting the vampyres get it. She was relieved to get rid of the key piece, believing it to be best if it was lost and forgotten.
