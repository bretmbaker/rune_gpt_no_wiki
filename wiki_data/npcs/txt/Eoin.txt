Item: Eoin
Summary: Eoin is an elven child in the lodge of Lletya. He can easily be found in Lletya square playing tag with his sister, Iona. When talked to, he will say that he cannot stop to talk or else Iona will catch him. He plays tag with Iona commonly. According to his mother, Mawrth, he is 38 years old, although that is of child age for elves, as elves live longer and age more slowly than humans.
