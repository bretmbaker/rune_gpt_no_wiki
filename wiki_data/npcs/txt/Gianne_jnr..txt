Item: Gianne jnr.
Summary: Aluft ("Lufty") Gianne Jr is the son of Aluft Gianne snr., and the proprietor of the Gnome Restaurant minigame. Speak to him to start the minigame. Aluft Gianne jnr. is found in the north-west area of the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] of the Grand Tree, just a little west of the cafe. Among his restaurant staff includes the gnomes Timble, Tamble and Spang.
