Item: Beigarth
Summary: Beigarth is a Fremennik teenager trying to pass his trial to adulthood. To do this, he and his friends Armod, Hild, Signy, and Reinn took part in a plot to create a war between Miscellania and Etceteria, which they could then settle, cheating their way through their trials. He is involved in the Royal Trouble quest.
