Item: Sliske
Summary: Sliske is a mysterious Zarosian Mahjarrat responsible for giving the Barrows Brothers their equipment and subsequently raising them as undead wights. He is referred to as "Sliske the serpent-tongued, who delves the shadows" by Azzanadra in the 20th Postbag from the Hedge[1] and appears as a mysterious figure in the crumbling tome.
