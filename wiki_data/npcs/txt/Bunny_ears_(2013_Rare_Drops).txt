Item: Bunny ears (2013 Rare Drops)
Summary: Bunny ears was an item that spawned as part of the 2013 Rare Drops event. Picking up the spawn would provide the player with a bunny ears. This was the first introduction of bunny ears in Old School RuneScape, though they were later made available as part of the annual Easter holiday events. The spawns were available for 1 week and dropped at the same time as the reindeer hat.
