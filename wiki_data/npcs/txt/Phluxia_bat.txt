Item: Phluxia bat
Summary: Phluxia bats can be found inside the Chambers of Xeric where they can be caught using a butterfly net in order to obtain a Raw phluxia bat (3). They require a Hunter level of 45 to catch and grant 17 Hunter experience. Once caught, phluxia bats can be cooked into a Phluxia bat (3), requiring a Cooking level of 45.
