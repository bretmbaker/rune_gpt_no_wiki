Item: Brian O'Richard
Summary: Brian O'Richard is a non-player character who can be found in the Rogues' Den. He owns and operates a maze of Thieving and Agility traps inside the Rogues' Den which players can navigate, which is where players can receive rogue armour and rogue kits. Players can speak to Brian to get permission to enter the maze, which requires level 50 Thieving and Agility. (Cannot be boosted.)
