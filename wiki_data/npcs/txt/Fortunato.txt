Item: Fortunato
Summary: Fortunato is the owner of Wine Shop in Draynor Village. Free players can purchase jugs and jugs of wine from him, while members can additionally purchase bottles of wine and jugs of vinegar from him once they have started the Rag and Bone Man I quest. He will also stop players from stealing from his wine stall if he is near a player attempting to do so (requiring 22 Thieving and granting 27 experience per successful attempt).
