Item: Sir Tiffy Cashien
Summary: Sir Tiffy Cashien sits in the Falador Park, and he is head of recruitment for the Temple Knights, a division of the White Knights. He is also where one can start the Wanted! and The Slug Menace quests. He sells Initiate armour, Proselyte armour and can change the player's respawn points to Falador using the Gaze of Saradomin.
