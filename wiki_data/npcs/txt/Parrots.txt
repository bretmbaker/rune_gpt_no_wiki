Item: Parrots
Summary: Parrots are birds found on display at Ardougne Zoo. They are cared for by Parroty Pete, a zoo employee. They can be found in a large group in a display at the Zoo's north end. Parrots can only be interacted with during Eadgar's Ruse, when a parrot is fed Alco-chunks to steal it, obtaining a Drunk parrot.
