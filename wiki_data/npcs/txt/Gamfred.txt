Item: Gamfred
Summary: Gamfred is the dwarf in the Warriors' Guild that runs the catapult activity. He is in a tiny narrow room on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] of the Warriors' Guild. Immediately upon talking to him you will notice that he is very excited about his crossbow-catapult invention. Gamfred also made the dummy room downstairs. He can explain to you how to use the catapult, and you can also receive a defensive shield or Warrior guild tokens that you have earned from him.
