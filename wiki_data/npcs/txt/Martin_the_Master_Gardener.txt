Item: Martin the Master Gardener
Summary: Martin the Master Gardener is a member of the Group of Advanced Gardeners organisation and resides in the Draynor Village market by a pig pen owned by them. He is the starting point for two quests: Fairytale I - Growing Pains and also Fairytale II - Cure a Queen. As the master of Farming, Martin sells the Farming skill cape to players who have reached level 99 in Farming for a price of 99,000 coins.
