Item: Timfraku
Summary: Timfraku is the leader of the Tai Bwo Wannai village. He has three sons, Tamayu, Tiadeche and Tinsay, who you must bring back to the village during the Tai Bwo Wannai Trio quest. Unlike other villagers, he does not give you trading sticks if you have favour from Tai Bwo Wannai Cleanup. He can be found up the ladder in the hut north-west of Tai Bwo Wannai.
