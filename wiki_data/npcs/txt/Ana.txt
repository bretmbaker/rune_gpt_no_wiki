Item: Ana
Summary: Ana is a girl from Misthalin who likes exploring. Unfortunately, she likes it a little too much and has recently disappeared into the Kharidian Desert. The player meets her mother, Irena, at the beginning of the quest, 'The Tourist Trap', in which they must rescue Ana. After searching the desert and finding her scarf, the player will discover that she has been captured by the slavers of the Desert Mining Camp. After a series of trials and tricks, the player is able to smuggle her out of the camp by hiding her in a barrel, and thus reuniting Ana with her mother.
