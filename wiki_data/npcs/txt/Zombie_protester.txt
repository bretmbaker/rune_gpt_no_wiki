Item: Zombie protester
Summary: Zombie protesters are found on Braindeath Island protesting for rum. The factory had shut down. The protesters are part of Captain Donnie's crew. They are the reason of the brewers of Captain Braindeath cowering, scared to leave the factory as they will be torn to pieces by the zombies outside. All of the zombies have big signs with bottles of 'rum' on them. They will follow players if they get in range.
