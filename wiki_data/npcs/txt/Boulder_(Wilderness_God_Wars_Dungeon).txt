Item: Boulder (Wilderness God Wars Dungeon)
Summary: The boulder blocks one of the entrances to the Wilderness God Wars Dungeon, and when passed leads to the Armadylean encampment in the main dungeon. It requires 60 Strength to pass, which can be boosted, and yields 5 Strength experience. It is in a single-way combat area and spells cannot be cast on players on the other side.
