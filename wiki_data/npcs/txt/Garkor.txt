Item: Garkor
Summary: Sergeant Garkor is a gnome working for King Narnode Shareen as the leader of the 10th Squad, a battalion in the gnomish military. After the events of The Grand Tree, he is instructed to take his squad to the Karamja's eastern Ship Yard. Glough, the anti-human extremist, had secretly begun construction on a fleet of ships which he planned to use to invade the human nations. However, since his plot was foiled during The Grand Tree, the shipyard was now scheduled for decommission.
