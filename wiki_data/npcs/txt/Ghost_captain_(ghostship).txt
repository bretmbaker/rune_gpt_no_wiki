Item: Ghost captain (ghostship)
Summary: The ghost captain  on the northern part of the docks in Port Phasmatys is captain of the adjacent Ghostship. With a Ghostspeak amulet, Morytania legs 2, 3, or 4 equipped, the player asks the captain where the captain sails to. He explains the ship currently cannot go anywhere because the mini-Ectofuntus powering the ship is in need of major repairs.
