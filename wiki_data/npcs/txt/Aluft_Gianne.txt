Item: Aluft Gianne
Summary: Aluft Gianne was the original owner of the Gnome Restaurant business in the Tree Gnome Stronghold. He offered a small tutorial on Gnome cooking, which allowed players to make gnome food, and sold premade versions of the food in Gianne's Restaurant. Afterwards, he would hire the player to cook specific foods for him, offering Coins and Cooking experience as a reward.
