Item: Odmar
Summary: Odmar is a dwarf that lives in the dwarven capital of Keldagrim. He can be found in the west side of the Consortium building, contemplating what to buy from the many shops in Keldagrim. He asks the player for suggestions on what to buy. The options are: new clothes, a warhammer, a piece of rope, an oversized hat, or nothing. Yet he is still displeased regardless to your choice.
