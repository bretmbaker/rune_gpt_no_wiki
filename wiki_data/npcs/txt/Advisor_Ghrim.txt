Item: Advisor Ghrim
Summary: Advisor Ghrim is the advisor to King Vargas. He is involved in Throne of Miscellania and Royal Trouble. He helps players to manage the kingdom of Miscellania. He is located on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] of the castle in Miscellania. If players ask for advice about managing their kingdom, he will give them a book on the subject, titled Managing Thine Kingdom for Noobes.
