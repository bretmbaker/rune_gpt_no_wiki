Item: Monolith
Summary: Monoliths can be found within Golrie's storeroom in the eastern side of Tree Gnome Village dungeon. During The Path of Glouphrie players must push the monoliths out of the way in order to access the chests, singing bowl, and Yewnock's machine within the storeroom. After unlocking the gate to the west, the monoliths are fixed in position and cannot be moved.
