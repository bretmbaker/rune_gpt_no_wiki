Item: Irwin Feaselbaum
Summary: Irwin Feaselbaum is a young assistant necromancer who is found at the ground floor of the Necromancer Tower. When engaged in conversation he reveals that he acts as sort of a home help in keeping the master of the tower in line, and keeping the house in order while waiting for his master's mortal return. He also says that Necromancers can raise themselves from the dead.
