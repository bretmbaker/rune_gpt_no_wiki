Item: Dr Fenkenstrain
Summary: Dr Fenkenstrain is a scientist that lives in the castle Lordship of the North Coast north-east of Canifis. He is involved in the Creature of Fenkenstrain and The Great Brain Robbery quests. Players may pick his pocket to obtain a ring of charos. After completion of the Great Brain Robbery, he can be found in the basement of Harmony Island windmill.
