Item: Sabre-toothed kebbit
Summary: The sabre-toothed kebbit is a kebbit located in the Rellekka Hunter area captured with deadfall traps at 51 Hunter, yielding 200 Hunter experience, bones and kebbit teeth. Setting up a deadfall trap requires a knife, logs, and optionally baiting it with raw meat. It is recommended to bring an axe as there are trees nearby.
