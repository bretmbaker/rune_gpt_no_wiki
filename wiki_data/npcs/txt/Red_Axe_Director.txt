Item: Red Axe Director
Summary: Hreidmar, the Red Axe Director is the villainous director of The Red Axe, a company that had positioned itself as the most powerful company in the Dwarven Consortium in an attempt to achieve dictatorship over Keldagrim. He follows Zamorak and is the main antagonist of the Rise of the Red Axe quest series. Hreidmar lived in Keldagrim, the dwarven capital city, until his company left the Consortium, after which he went into hiding. The dwarf owns a pet cat, whom he allows to roam his office freely.
