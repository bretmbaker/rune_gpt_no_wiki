Item: Priest (West Ardougne)
Summary: A Priest can be found in the spacious Saradominist chapel in the south-east of West Ardougne, the plague-infested western half of Ardougne, the capital of Kandarin. He plays a small role in Song of the Elves when the player visits him disguised as a mourner to collect taxes and destroy food supplies to spark the Ardougne Revolution.
