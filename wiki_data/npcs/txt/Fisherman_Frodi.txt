Item: Fisherman Frodi
Summary: Fisherman Frodi is a citizen of Miscellania, located on the docks. During Throne of Miscellania, players need to gain his support; after the quest, players can help him by catching fish, to gain favour. Each fish caught is instantly given to him without the need for the player to stop fishing or move (no open inventory slots are needed). Fisherman Frodi is one of four residents players can assist to gain favour; the other three citizens players can assist are Gardener Gunnhild, Lumberjack Leif, and Miner Magnus.
