Item: Oathbreaker E'piws
Summary: Oathbreaker E'piws is an Oathbreaker, a former monk of Saradomin, who has defected and now worships Zamorak. He and his fellow Oathbreakers can be found in the Sisterhood Sanctuary, just south of the The Nightmare's arena. The Oathbreakers have taken a vow of silence and do not speak, instead Oath Lord D'rows speaks on their behalf.
