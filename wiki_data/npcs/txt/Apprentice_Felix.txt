Item: Apprentice Felix
Summary: Apprentice Felix is a wizard apprenticed to Wizard Traiborn. During the quest Temple of the Eye, he can be found on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] of the Wizards' Tower, where the player helps him with Traiborn's riddle. After the events in the quest, he stays together with Apprentice Cordelia and Tamara inside the Temple of the Eye to aid The Great Guardian.
