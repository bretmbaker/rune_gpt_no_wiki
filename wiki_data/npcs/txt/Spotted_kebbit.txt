Item: Spotted kebbit
Summary: The spotted kebbit is a kebbit that players can capture with the Hunter skill using falconry at level 43 or above (can be boosted), granting 104 Hunter experience. Players can expect to achieve xp rates of 60k xp/hr while hunting them. Spotted kebbits may be assigned as a hunters' rumour task by Guild Hunters Gilman, Cervus, or Ornus.
