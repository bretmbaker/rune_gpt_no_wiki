Item: Odd Mushroom
Summary: Odd Mushroom is a troll found in the troll town of Weiss, who is an inventor and also Mother's advisor. Unlike most trolls, Odd Mushroom is extremely intelligent and speaks perfect common tongue, like the Troll father, troll mother and troll child. He values skills over combat, having more affection for Mother's daughter Snowflake due to being his pupil and her quick learning capabilities.
