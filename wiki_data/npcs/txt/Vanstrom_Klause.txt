Item: Vanstrom Klause
Summary: Vanstrom Klause, formerly Ascertes Hallow, is a vampyre noble, one of the brutal henchmen of Lord Lowerniel Drakan, and the leader of the Vyrewatch. Vanstrom is a murderous fiend who strikes terror into the hearts of the Myreque, a resilient group of guerrilla fighters from Morytania dedicated to fighting their vampyric overlords. Vanstrom is Safalaan Hallow's father, the former King Ascertes Hallow, who was transformed into a vampyre by Lowerniel Drakan after his capture during the Siege of Hallowvale. He is defeated by the player and Safalaan at the end of the Sins of the Father quest.
