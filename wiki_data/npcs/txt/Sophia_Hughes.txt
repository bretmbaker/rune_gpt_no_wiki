Item: Sophia Hughes
Summary: Sophia Hughes is a councillor of the Kourend Council. During The Queen of Thieves, players discover that she has ties with the Disciples of Yama, a cult who follows the demon Yama, who resides deep below the Chasm of Fire. She is arrested, and her position as a councillor removed. After the quest, she can be found on the top floor of the Kourend Castle, inside a cell.
