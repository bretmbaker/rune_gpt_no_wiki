Item: Prince Itzla Arkan
Summary: Prince Itzla Arkan is the son of Zyanyi Arkan, the Sun Queen of Varlamore. His father is Servius, the Teokan of Ralos. Prince Itzla is first encountered during the Children of the Sun quest, where he leads the delegation visiting Varrock on behalf of the Kingdom of Varlamore. He is also involved in Twilight's Promise but disappears near the end of The Heart of Darkness where he is teleported to an unknown location during the encounter with Amoxliatl.
