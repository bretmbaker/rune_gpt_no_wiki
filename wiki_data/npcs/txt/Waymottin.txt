Item: Waymottin
Summary: Waymottin is a gnome found in the Ape Atoll Dungeon, near Zooknock during Monkey Madness I. During the earlier portions of the quest, when you are not a member of the 10th squad, he will not talk to you. He can be found looking after Bunkwicket in Gnome Stronghold's Blurberry Bar after the quest.
