Item: Sam (barmaid)
Summary: Sam is the barmaid at the entrance to the Burthorpe Games Room. She explains what games can be played and how to play the games in the game room. She stocks the same beers as Tostig from the nearby Toad and Chicken pub: 12 Asgarnian ale, 12 Wizard's mind bomb, and 12 Dwarven stout at 2 coins each.
