Item: Elfinlocks
Summary: Elfinlocks is a player spoof in RuneScape who was included in the break-in of the Draynor Village bank. Elfinlocks was the source of the Wise Old Man's partyhat. Elfinlocks, 1337sp34kr, and two of the guards were killed by the Wise Old Man. One of the female bankers survived but the other two were killed and their money stolen.
