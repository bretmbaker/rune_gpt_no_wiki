Item: Mi-Gor
Summary: Mi-Gor is a sinister zombie surgeon working for Rabid Jack. His zombie pirates have laid siege of Harmony Island and turned the monks into zombies. Mi-Gor built Barrelchest to be his bodyguard. Mi-Gor has a problem with his breathing system, so he has to wear a complicated breathing device, which is a problem when he tries to do an evil laugh.
