Item: Charlie Brown
Summary: Charlie Brown was a monk located in Hosidius, up the ladder in the small church to the south (which has since been replaced with monk camping site). When spoken to, he revealed that he had journeyed to Great Kourend in search of luck and fortune,[1] and wears monk's robes to keep him holy when praying to Arengeesus, the Goddess of Luck.[2][3]
