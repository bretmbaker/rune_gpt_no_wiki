Item: Juliet
Summary: Juliet Leptoc is a member of Varrockian nobility, and daughter to the wealthy and esteemed Lord Draul Leptoc. She resides on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] of her father's estate just outside the western gates of Varrock, north of the path. She plays a major part in the Romeo & Juliet quest in which the player attempts to reunite her with her hopeless boyfriend Romeo.
