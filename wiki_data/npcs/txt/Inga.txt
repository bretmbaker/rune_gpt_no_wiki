Item: Inga
Summary: Inga is one of the many normal citizens of Rellekka. Inga plays a small part in The Fremennik Trials as she is a council member. However, her vote is impossible to get as she doesn't like the player. She is located in a hut with Freidir in the south-east part of Rellekka. Her hut is exactly north of Lanzig's hut. At level 45 Thieving and after The Fremennik Trials, Inga can be pickpocketed for 40 coins and 65 Thieving experience.
