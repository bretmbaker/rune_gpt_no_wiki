Item: Prospector Percy
Summary: Prospector Percy is the discoverer of the Motherlode Mine and owner of Prospector Percy's Nugget Shop. Players can trade him golden nuggets for components of the prospector outfit and other items such as the coal bag and gem bag. He also sells an increase to the paydirt sack capacity, access to the upper section of the Motherlode Mine and use of the upper floor hopper. To the frustration of player, despite his proximity to a hammer and the water wheels, he is incapable of fixing them himself.
