Item: Broken Handz
Summary: Broken Handz is a player spoof who can be seen using his telescope on an island in the Poison Waste, accessible via the fairy ring code DLR. He will let you use his telescope to view a Zulrah fight that is taking place. Similar to spectating at Nightmare Zone, the spectator must put in a player's name down that is fighting Zulrah. He is accompanied by three badgers.
