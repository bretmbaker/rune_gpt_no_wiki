Item: Nelta of Ranul
Summary: Nelta of Ranul refers to several NPCs who are each nelta that serve in the Sect of Ranul in Varlamore. Several are found in the crypt below the temple of Ralos in Civitas illa Fortis. 
Their duties are mainly focused on death and funerary rites.[1] They answer to the Teoki of Ranul as well as the Teokan, Metzli.
