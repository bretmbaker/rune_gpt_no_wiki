Item: Henja
Summary: Henja is the son of Anja and Hengel, and the nephew of Eric. Players accompany him and his sister, Angel, to the crypt underneath the Rimmington church during the 2018 Halloween event in order to help Eric entertain them.
After the event he can be found on the ground floor[UK]1st floor[US] in the house of his parents, Anja and Hengel.
