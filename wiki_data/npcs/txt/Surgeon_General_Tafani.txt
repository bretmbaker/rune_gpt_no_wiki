Item: Surgeon General Tafani
Summary: Surgeon General Tafani is the lead surgeon at the Emir's Arena hospital. She, along with Jaraah and the nurses Sabreen and A'abla, can heal players if they have lost hitpoints (but cannot cure poison). They can heal players who are not playing the minigame, and can be useful to players fighting in the nearby area. The healing is free of charge.
