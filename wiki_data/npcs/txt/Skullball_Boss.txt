Item: Skullball Boss
Summary: The Skullball Boss oversees the Werewolf Agility Course. He will teach players how to use the Werewolf Skullball course properly. This course requires a ring of charos and completion of Creature of Fenkenstrain. If you want to start a game of Skullball, you'll have to talk with him and a skull, which acts as a ball, will appear.
