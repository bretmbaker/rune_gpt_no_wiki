Item: Curator Haig Halen
Summary: Haig Halen is the Curator of the Varrock Museum. He can be found on the ground floor. When asked about treasure, he'll tell the player that the museum is "full of treasures" and that anything the museum found took some time to acquire. You can trade him 250 cut rubies, 100 cosmic runes, 500 fire runes and a Digsite pendant for a curator's medallion.
