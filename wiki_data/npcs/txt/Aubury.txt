Item: Aubury
Summary: Layte Aubury [1] is the owner of the Aubury's Rune Shop in Varrock. He features heavily in the Rune Mysteries quest. He stocks many free-to-play runes, including chaos and death runes. Players who have completed Rune Mysteries can also ask him to be teleported to the rune essence mines. When he teleports players, he utters "Senventior Disthine Molenko!".
