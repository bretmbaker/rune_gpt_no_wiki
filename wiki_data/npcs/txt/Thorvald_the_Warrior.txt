Item: Thorvald the Warrior
Summary: Thorvald the Warrior is a fremennik council member featured in the quest The Fremennik Trials. During the quest, he tasks the player with finding and defeating the fierce warrior Koschei the Deathless. Players can win Thorvald's vote by defeating Koschei at least three times, even if they die to his last form. It is recommended players bring lots of food for the fight, in addition to rings of recoil.
