Item: Dog
Summary: A dog can be found on the farm south of Falador, and in the caves under The Hollows. The dog in Falador farm is kept as a pet by Sarah, the farm's owner, along with Sadie and a sheepdog that lives inside the house. He roams the chicken pen on the farm's western side. Two additional dogs are located within a small cave accessible after entering the tunnels during In Search of the Myreque. Take the middle tunnel on the western wall to see them.
