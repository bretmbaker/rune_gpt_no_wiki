Item: Hamal the Chieftain
Summary: Hamal the Chieftain, also known as Hamal, Son of Wulfgard, rules the Mountain Camp. Players must talk to him to start the Mountain Daughter quest. His daughter, Asleif, has disappeared and the player must find her. It is revealed that she was killed by a man posing as The Kendal. Hamal also gives players back their bearhead if they have lost it.
