Item: Lady Vulcana Lovakengj
Summary: Lady Vulcana Lovakengj is the leader of the Lovakengj House. She is encountered during The Forsaken Tower quest, who players speak to in order to begin the quest. She is a severe alcoholic, often calling upon Jorra, her personal assistant, to bring her sherries in the middle of conversations with the player. As a result, she often asks her assistant who the player is, and why they are in her office.
