Item: Mamma Bufetta
Summary: Mamma Bufetta is one of the villagers in Tai Bwo Wannai village. She lives with her family in the building that is facing the stone statue. Her daughter is Layleen, and her husband is Sharimika. Players can receive trading sticks from her if they have favour earned from the Tai Bwo Wannai Cleanup. She will also tell players how to make Spider on stick.
