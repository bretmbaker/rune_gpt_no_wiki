Item: Volf Olafson
Summary: Volf Olafson is the son of Olaf Hradson and Ingrid Hradson and is involved in Olaf's Quest. He is found wandering near the Helmet Shop to the north of the Longhall. When spoken to he seems to believe his father's stories about success treasure hunting. Giving him the cruder carving during Olaf's Quest will result in him rewarding you with a cooked shark.
