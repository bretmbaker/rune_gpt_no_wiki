Item: Dwarven Boatman (ore hauler)
Summary: This Dwarven Boatman hauls gold ore mined by players who have finished the quest Between a Rock... He will take a cut of the ore mined for himself as a charge for this service, but will bring the rest to the bank in Keldagrim. He can be found in the Keldagrim south-west mine to the east of Dondakan the Dwarf and the Arzinian Mine.
