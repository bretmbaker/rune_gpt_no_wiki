Item: Armod
Summary: Armod Brundtson is a Fremennik teenager trying to pass his trial of adulthood and son of Brundt the Chieftain. To do this, he and his friends Reinn, Hild, Signy, and Beigarth took part in a plot to create a war between Miscellania and Etceteria, which they could then settle, cheating their way through their trials. His diary is found during the Royal Trouble quest.
