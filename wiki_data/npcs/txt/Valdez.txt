Item: Valdez
Summary: Valdez is wandering around Glarial's tombstone and can only be seen while wearing a Ring of visibility. He is the first mysterious ghost that must be spoken to in the Curse of the Empty Lord miniquest, and he will give the player the Ghostly robe (top). Do not forget to bring your Ghostspeak amulet otherwise you will not be able to communicate with the ghosts. After completion of the miniquest, you can speak with him to recover any piece of the ghostly robes.
