Item: Icelord (2019 Christmas event)
Summary: The Icelord was an Icelord locked in the bakery below Varrock during the 2019 Christmas event. It was originally brought in by Jack Frost to help with icing the Gingerbread gnomes. During the event, the player must craft a Gingerbread shield to protect from the Icelord's attacks, in order to retrieve the Bakery storage key and free Paul and Mary.
