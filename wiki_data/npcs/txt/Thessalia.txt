Item: Thessalia
Summary: Thessalia Nitter[1] runs Thessalia's Fine Clothes a makeover shop in Varrock. Items of clothing can be bought from her, or a player can get a makeover to change their appearance underneath their worn items free of charge. She also exchanges frog tokens for a royal frog costume, a frog mask, or an experience lamp. She will also return bunny ears and scythes to a player if they have lost them.
