Item: Lady Carys Hefin
Summary: Lady Carys Hefin is the leader of the Hefin Clan. During the Song of the Elves quest, the player must navigate a series of stepping stones to reach her camp on an island in the eastern part of the Poison Waste. She is initially referred to as Elf Hermit before it is revealed that she is Lady Carys Hefin. After the quest, she can be found in north-west Prifddinas.
