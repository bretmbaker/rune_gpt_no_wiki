Item: Sawmill operator
Summary: The Sawmill operator can be found along the southern wall of the Varrock Lumber Yard on members servers and at the Woodcutting Guild in Great Kourend, their location indicated by the  icon on the minimap. They can turn four kinds of logs into planks (see sawmill), and also runs a Construction Supplies shop selling bolts of cloth, three kinds of nails, and saws. They will not accept noted logs, except indirectly through house servants.
