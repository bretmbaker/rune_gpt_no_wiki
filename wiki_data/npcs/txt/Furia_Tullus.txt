Item: Furia Tullus
Summary: Furia Tullus is a servant of the Royal Family of Varlamore, dealing with administrative work alongside her twin brother Ennius Tullus. Both were part of the delegation visiting Varrock on behalf of the Kingdom of Varlamore during Children of the Sun. She is also involved in Twilight's Promise, recruiting the player's help in rounding up six knights to help discover the traitor among them.
