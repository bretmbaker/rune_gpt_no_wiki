Item: Psykk bat
Summary: Psykk bats can be found inside the Chambers of Xeric where they can be caught using a butterfly net in order to obtain a raw psykk bat (6). They require a Hunter level of 90 to catch and grant 29 Hunter experience. Once caught, raw psykk bats can be cooked into a psykk bat (6), requiring a Cooking level of 90 and granting 28 Cooking experience.
