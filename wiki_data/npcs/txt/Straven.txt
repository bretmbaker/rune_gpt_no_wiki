Item: Straven
Summary: Straven Enroy [1] is a professional thief and the leader of the Phoenix Gang. He is found in an underground basement in southern part of Varrock, in the Phoenix Gang Hideout. For players who are starting the quest Shield of Arrav, Straven assigns the player the task of killing Johnny the beard before he will let the player become a part of the Phoenix Gang. He also provides the Weapon store key to players who are members of his gang.
