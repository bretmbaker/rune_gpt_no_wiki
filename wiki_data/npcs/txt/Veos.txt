Item: Veos
Summary: Veos is a portmaster and treasure hunter, located directly south of the Rusty Anchor Inn in Port Sarim. He disappears after the quest A Kingdom Divided and is replaced by Cabin Boy Herbert. He will take players to Port Piscarilius and Land's End in Great Kourend, a large kingdom west of the mainland. From Port Piscarilius, he will offer travel to Land's End and Port Sarim.
