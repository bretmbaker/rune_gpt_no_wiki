Item: Kathy Corkat
Summary: Kathy Corkat, together with Devin Mendelberg have a rowing boat west of the Tree Gnome Stronghold, just across the river, where they sail players up to the Piscatoris Fishing Colony for 50 coins or for free with a Ring of Charos (a). This is useful during the quest Swan Song. After Swan Song, she offers the boat ride for free.
