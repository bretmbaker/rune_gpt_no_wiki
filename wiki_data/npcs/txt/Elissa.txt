Item: Elissa
Summary: Elissa is a woman who is found in the north-eastern part of the digsite, east of Varrock. She is the wife of Varmen, who has done excavations in Uzer. She is included in The Golem quest, She is also involved Desert Treasure II where she directs the player to the Burning Man monument during their search for Vardorvis' medallion. She appears to be wearing Lucien's pendant.
