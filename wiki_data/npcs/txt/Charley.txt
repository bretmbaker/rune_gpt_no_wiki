Item: Charley
Summary: Two Feet Charley owns Two Feet Charley's Fish Shop. on Mos Le'Harmless. Despite the name, he actually has a pair of peg legs. He is called Two Feet Charley because he was found after a shipwreck with two feet jammed down a shark's throat. Players need to have a book o' piracy when trying to trade with him, otherwise he will respond with unintelligible pirate slang.
