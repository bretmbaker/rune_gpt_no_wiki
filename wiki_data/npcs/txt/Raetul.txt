Item: Raetul
Summary: Raetul is a merchant found in east Sophanem. He runs a store, which can only be accessed after several pages of dialogue, called Raetul and Co's Cloth Store. He stocks items such as desert clothing, silk, and crafting tools. In the Icthlarin's Little Helper quest, players purchase linen from him for 30 coins. He is seen in a cutscene in Contact! as part of the High Priest's delegation.
