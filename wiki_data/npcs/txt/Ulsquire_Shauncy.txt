Item: Ulsquire Shauncy
Summary: Ulsquire Shauncy is an afflicted resident of Mort'ton, and a local priest and teacher in the town which was famed for its religious ceremonies. Ulsquire is located in one of the three still-standing houses of Mort'ton, along with a sink acting as the town's only water source. He is good friends with Razmire Keelgan, the local shop owner.
