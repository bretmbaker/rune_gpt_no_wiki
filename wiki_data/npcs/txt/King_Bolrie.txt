Item: King Bolrie
Summary: Bolrie is a gnome seen in a cutscene during The Path of Glouphrie. The grandfather of Golrie, Bolrie once led the gnomes of the Tree Gnome Village late in the Fourth Age, and also waged war on the humans led by General Khazard, under the pretence that they were preparing to attack, seeing that King Healthorg would not do anything to deal with the supposed threat.
