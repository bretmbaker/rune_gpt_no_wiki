Item: Johnathon
Summary: Johnathon Fitzharmon is the brother of Caleb and Avan and one of the sons of Dimintheis. He can be found on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] of the Jolly Boar Inn. Johnathon plays a role in the Family Crest quest: he was fighting Chronozon, a powerful demon in the Edgeville Dungeon, but began losing the fight and tried to retreat. In his way was a poison spider, which bit him, poisoning him. When the player meets him, he will ask for an antipoison to cure himself.
