Item: Chancellor Hassan
Summary: Chancellor Hassan is in the southern room of the ground floor of Al Kharid palace. He is the chancellor to the desert nation of Al Kharid, a large city on the northernmost fringe of the vast Kharidian Desert. Hassan, prior to completing Prince Ali Rescue, is the acting ruler of Al Kharid in place of the Emir. He is also the start and ending points for the quest.
