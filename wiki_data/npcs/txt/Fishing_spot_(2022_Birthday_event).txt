Item: Fishing spot (2022 Birthday event)
Summary: Fishing spots were a type of fishing spot used during the 2022 Birthday Event in Al Kharid. During the event, players would join a fishing contest hosted by Larry Leprechaun, which involved trying to catch fish in the small Al Kharid ponds. The player had to obtain desert bait from Ali Morrisane to be able to fish a tiny fish, which would be given to Larry to win the fishing contest and complete the event.
