Item: Rug Merchant
Summary: Rug Merchants operate magic carpets for Ali Morrisane's flying carpet fleet. There is usually a monkey around these men. They are stationed in the Kharidian Desert at the Shantay Pass, Uzer, the Bedabin Camp, north and south Pollnivneach, Nardah, Sophanem, and Menaphos. Southern Pollnivneach and Shantay Pass have several travel options acting as hubs of transportation.
