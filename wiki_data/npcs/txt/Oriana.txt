Item: Oriana
Summary: Oriana is a thoughtful thief located just outside the western bank in Civitas illa Fortis. She is involved in the stealing valuables activity, buying valuables that have been looted from Lavinia's, Caius', or Victor's houses from the player for 55 coins each (or 65 coins each after earning Gladiator rank at the Fortis Colosseum). Before speaking to her, Oriana's name appears as Dodgy Character.
