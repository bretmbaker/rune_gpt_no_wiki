Item: Iona
Summary: Iona is an elf child and one of the many citizens of Lletya who may be found in Lletya square, playing tag with Eoin, her brother. When spoken with, she will remark, "I can never catch Eoin, he is just too fast, I am always 'It'." According to her mother, Mawrth, she is 34 years old, although that is of child age for elves, as elves live longer and age more slowly than humans.
