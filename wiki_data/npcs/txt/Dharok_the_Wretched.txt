Item: Dharok the Wretched
Summary: Dharok the Wretched is the second oldest of the six Barrows brothers. Dharok's crypt is located in the north-east of the Barrows area. He uses melee equipment, which makes him vulnerable to Magic attacks. Dharok's set effect is Wretched Strength, which increases his max hit by 1% for each Hitpoint he is missing.
