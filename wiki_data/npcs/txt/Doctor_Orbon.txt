Item: Doctor Orbon
Summary: Doctor Orbon is located in the East Ardougne chapel, and is involved in the Sheep Herder Quest. He sells the plague set, consisting of a plague jacket and a pair of plague trousers, that protects players from the poisonous sheep. He seems to be a scientist who has invented a suit that helps protect people from the infected sheep around the north of East Ardougne. The quest cannot be completed without his help.
