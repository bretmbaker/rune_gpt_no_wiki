Item: Tamayu
Summary: Tamayu is the eldest son of the chief of Tai Bwo Wannai, Timfraku, and one of the brothers in Tai Bwo Wannai Trio. His father describes him as a natural born hunter, killing his first monkey at the age of three. During the quest he can be found south-east of Tai Bwo Wannai near the mining spot. To avenge the deaths of his kin, he must hunt their killer, The Shaikahan. To be successful, he needs an iron spear, or better, with Karambwan poison and some Agility potions.
