Item: Red Axe Cat
Summary: The Red Axe Cat is the pet cat of Hreidmar, the director of The Red Axe mining company in Keldagrim. He is seen lying on the floor in the Red Axe's office and is later seen during the meeting at end of The Giant Dwarf quest. Afterwards, along with Hreidmar, the cat is no longer seen in the office. It can be observed again sleeping next to Hreidmar while in the Red Axe base during Forgettable Tale....
