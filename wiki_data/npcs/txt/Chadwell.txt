Item: Chadwell
Summary: Chadwell is a shopkeeper who runs the West Ardougne General Store. The store, which is one of the few stores still operating in the city of West Ardougne, is located in the south-western corner of the city, and Chadwell sells almost all of the supplies and apparel needed when going through the Underground Pass, which may be useful if the player forgets something they need. He also sells different kinds of food which can be used to heal any lost Hitpoints in the pass.
