Item: Dimintheis
Summary: Dimintheis Fitzharmon is a non-player character and a noble of Varrock. He is featured heavily in the quest Family Crest, and plays a crucial role in the quest Defender of Varrock. He is a descendant of an important Elder of Avarrocka, and is the father of Avan, Caleb, and Johnathon Fitzharmon. His house is located south of Varrock's east gate, north of the Fancy dress shop in the fenced off area.
