Item: Daga
Summary: Daga is a monkey trader found on Ape Atoll and owner of Daga's Scimitar Smithy scimitar shop. Players can only talk to him while they are holding a greegree and wearing the m'speak amulet (they may still trade without an m'speak amulet amulet if they use the right-click option) unless they have completed Monkey Madness II.
