Item: Mage of Zamorak (Ourania)
Summary: The Mage of Zamorak, found by the entrance of the Ourania Cave, is a member of the Zamorak Magical Institute, who has taken possession of the cave after their discovery of the Ourania Altar. He briefly tells the player about the altar's extraordinary properties as well as the dangers on the path towards it. His robes suggest he is part of the Dagon'hai.
