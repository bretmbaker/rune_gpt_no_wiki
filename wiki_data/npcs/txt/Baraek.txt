Item: Baraek
Summary: Baraek Brigson [1] is a fur trader in the Varrock Square (the centre of Varrock) who owns a Fur stall although you cannot steal from it. Baraek is also a member of the Phoenix Gang and plays a small role in the Shield of Arrav quest. If players wish to be in the Phoenix Gang, he must be bribed to reveal the location of their headquarters.
