Item: Regulus Cento
Summary: Regulus Cento is a quetzal keeper for the Varlamore royal family, located just outside and south of the east gate of Varrock and south-east of the Sunrise Palace in Varlamore's capital, Civitas illa Fortis. He will only appear once the player has completed Children of the Sun. He can take the player directly to and from Civitas illa Fortis and Varrock with his quetzal, Primio.
