Item: Councillor Unkar
Summary: Councillor Kubec Unkar was a member of the Kourend Council that governed Kourend. He is a dwarf located in the Lovakengj Assembly in Lovakengj. During A Kingdom Divided, it is revealed that alongside the other members of the Kourend Council, he conspired to kill King Kharedst IV and his daughter Crown Princess Rose with the Xerician mage Xamphur.
