Item: Lorelai
Summary: Lorelai, an apprentice of Kamfreena, is located in the basement of the Warriors' Guild, training some extra-strong cyclopes for Kamfreena.  Lorelai and the basement of the Warriors' Guild can be accessed by proceeding north-west from the Warriors' Guild bank past Lidio's food shop, out the back door west of the Warriors' Guild, and climbing down a ladder.
