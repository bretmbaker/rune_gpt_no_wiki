Item: Dakh'thoulan Aegis
Summary: Dakh'thoulan Aegis is member of the Dagon'hai order, he can be found in the Tunnel of Chaos beneath the outskirts of Varrock. The player has an option of mentioning this, saying "You're pretty nice for a Zamorakian", at which point he will tell the player that they should not believe everything they hear from others, implying that followers of Zamorak are unfairly vilified by followers of Saradomin, whose religion is more widespread and has more influence in Gielinor.
