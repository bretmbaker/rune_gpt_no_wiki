Item: Hiylik Myna
Summary: Hiylik Myna is a Saradominist priest, found near Paterdomus. He runs the Minigame called Temple Trekking.
He seeks strong adventurers to aid him and his mercenaries in taking down Vanstrom Klause along with the help of the Myreque. He makes sure that those who guide the mercenaries are rewarded with the help of the Burgh de Rott banker, Cornelius.
