Item: Woodcutting Leprechaun
Summary: The Woodcutting Leprechaun appears as part of the Woodcutting Leprechaun Forestry event. Players who were actively cutting trees near the event when he appears can use his services as a bank deposit box for all items in the inventory and log basket. If players were not eligible to partake in an event, he will only allow players to deposit Woodcutting resources instead.
