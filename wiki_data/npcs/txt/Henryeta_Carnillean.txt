Item: Henryeta Carnillean
Summary: Lady Henryeta Carnillean is the wife of Sir Ceril Carnillean, a wealthy nobleman of Ardougne, and the head of the Carnillean family. Henryeta lives in Carnillean Mansion, in the south-western end of East Ardougne, where she features briefly in the Hazeel Cult. She and Ceril have a son, Philipe Carnillean, who is very spoiled.
