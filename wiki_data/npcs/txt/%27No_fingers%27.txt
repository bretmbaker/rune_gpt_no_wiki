Item: 'No fingers'
Summary: 'No fingers' is one of the contractors building the Tower of Life. During the quest, you must pickpocket him in order to obtain a pair of Builder's boots, with a 25% chance to successfully pickpocket him.[1] Players may pickpocket him to get a Triangle sandwich and 8 thieving experience. Rogue equipment does not give double loot when pickpocketing this NPC.
