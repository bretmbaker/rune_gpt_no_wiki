Item: Savant
Summary: Savant (real name Elyssa van Lendes) is a Senior Mystical Researcher (S.M.R for short) and chief librarian for the Temple Knights of Falador. She plays a fairly major role in two quests, namely Wanted!, where she aids the player by helping slow the teleports of the evil murder mage Solus Dellagar, and The Slug Menace, in which she briefs the player and saves them from being taken over by the Sea Slug queen Mother Mallum.
