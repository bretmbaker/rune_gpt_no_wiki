Item: First mate 'Davey-boy'
Summary: First mate 'Davey-boy' is the first mate aboard the Lady Zay. Players must gather information from him during the Lunar Diplomacy quest. He is responsible for keeping the ship at the Pirates' Cove, using magical runes marked across the ship's interior. During the quest, Davey-Boy will give the player a Bullseye lantern with an emerald lens to locate and erase the runes.
