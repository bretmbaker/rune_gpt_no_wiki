Item: Lord Ieuan Amlodd
Summary: Lord Ieuan Amlodd is the leader of the Amlodd Clan. During the Song of the Elves quest, the player must use a crystal seed to travel to a cliff in the southern part of the Enchanted Valley, where a Mysterious Figure, who is shortly after revealed to be Lord Ieuan Amlodd, is waiting. Otherwise he may be found in the room north of Arianwyn. After the quest, he can be found in north-west Prifddinas.
