Item: Trader Stan
Summary: Trader Stan is the owner of the charter ships that sail players to various places in RuneScape for a price, including Catherby, Brimhaven. Land's End, Musa Point, the ship yard, Port Khazard, Port Phasmatys. Port Piscarilius, Port Sarim, and Port Tyras. Trader Stan himself can be found at the southern most dock of Port Sarim, wearing a mustard-yellow outfit.
