Item: Grimesquit
Summary: Grimesquit Grime[1] is a somewhat crazed woman found in Varrock Sewers, with her sister, Phingspet. Both she and her sister can be found near ladder to east Varrock. Grimesquit and her sister help the player to train their cat by catching eight rats during the Ratcatchers quest. After this is accomplished, they will redirect the player to Jimmy Dazzler.
