Item: Brana
Summary: Brana Talvoy is the husband of Launa Talvoy and the father of Tolna Talvoy. In the year 144 of the Fifth Age, his son ran away from home, fed up of living on meagre earnings.[1] He and his wife searched for their son for 25 years, eventually coming upon a rift.[2][3] Brana went down in the hope of finding Tolna, but when he didn't reappear after several days Launa sent an adventurer down to rescue them both.[4]
