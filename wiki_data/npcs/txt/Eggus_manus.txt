Item: Eggus manus
Summary: Eggus manus is encountered during the 2024 Easter event. During the event, he takes the player to the church of Ayaster and gives the player the book Yolklore of ayaster. After the player discovers that Eggus manus and the priests are confusing Ayaster for "Easter", the player introduces them to the Easter Bunny to prove that easter eggs are just chocolate.
