Item: Toy boatman
Summary: Toy boatman was one of the five types of possessed toys found in the 2013 Halloween event. Shooting the boatman with a ghost buster 500 loaded with a white destabiliser would turn it into a red geist, which could then be dismissed by shooting it with a red destabiliser. After busting 3 of one type of geist, Diango would reward you with one piece of the skeleton outfit.
