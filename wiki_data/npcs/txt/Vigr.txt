Item: Vigr
Summary: Vigr is a dwarf that lives in the dwarven capital city of Keldagrim. There he runs a warhammer shop, simply known as Vigr's Warhammers. The shop stocks bronze through adamant warhammers. He and his shop can be found on the ground floor[UK]1st floor[US] of the Consortium's palace, on the west side of the River Kelda.
