Item: Zygomite
Summary: Zygomites, referred to as Mutated Zygomites by the Slayer masters, are monsters that appear as Fungi until provoked (Pick Fungi) that require level 57 Slayer to damage. They resemble giant mushrooms, and are found in Zanaris near the Cosmic Altar (level 86) or the furnace (level 74). Their ancient variants are found in Fossil Island. Players must use fungicide spray on Zygomites to kill them.
