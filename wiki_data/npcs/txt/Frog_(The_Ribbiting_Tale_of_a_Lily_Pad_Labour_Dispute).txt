Item: Frog (The Ribbiting Tale of a Lily Pad Labour Dispute)
Summary: Frogs are found at the Locus Oasis before and during The Ribbiting Tale of a Lily Pad Labour Dispute. After starting the quest, Gary and Sue will have a talk-to option and will introduce themselves and explain the labour strike dispute with Marcellus. After this conversation, all five frogs will have their names displayed and can be talked to.
