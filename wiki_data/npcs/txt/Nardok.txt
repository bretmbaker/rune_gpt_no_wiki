Item: Nardok
Summary: Nardok is a shifty Dorgeshuun weapon merchant found in the Dorgeshuun Mines, near the entrance to the cave goblin capital of Dorgesh-Kaan. He runs the Nardok's Bone Weapons shop. After The Lost Tribe quest, he will sell a variety of weapons, such as bone bolts, daggers, spears, clubs and Dorgeshuun crossbows to players.
