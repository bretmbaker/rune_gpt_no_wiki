Item: Wizard Persten
Summary: Wizard Persten, of the Order of Wizards, is a wizard who is researching the Abyss. During the events of the quest, she accidentally reopens the rift in the Temple of the Eye and is sucked inside into The Scar, leaving her fate unknown. The Great Guardian is certain that she is dead while Archmage Sedridor believes that she could survive its dangers.
