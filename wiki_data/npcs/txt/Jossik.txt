Item: Jossik
Summary: Jossik is the keeper of the Lighthouse west of Rellekka. He also runs The Lighthouse Store on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] of the lighthouse, but players must finish the quest Horror from the Deep to access it. His store sells various items similar to general stores, but other notable items are several kinds of alcohol and water-filled vial packs. He also sells Greenman's Ale, Wizard's mind bomb, and Dwarven stout. After you finish the quest, he will give you an empty god book of your choice but it must be one of the main gods: Saradomin, Guthix, or Zamorak.
