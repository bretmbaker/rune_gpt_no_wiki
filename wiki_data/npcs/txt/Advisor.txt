Item: Advisor
Summary: The advisor is King Bolrie's advisor, who guided him through the war against General Khazard in the Fourth Age, and advised him on his ambition to succeed King Healthorg. However, during a confrontation with Argenthorg, Healthorg's son, he was exposed by Yewnock the Engineer as Glouphrie the Untrusted, an exiled gnome who once deceived King Healthorg.
