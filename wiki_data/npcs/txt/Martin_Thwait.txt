Item: Martin Thwait
Summary: Martin Thwait is a rogue who can be found inside the Rogues' Den, located in Burthorpe by entering a trapdoor inside The Toad and Chicken inn. He runs a general store called Martin Thwait's Lost and Found which sells a variety of Thieving-related items, like lockpicks for chests, stethoscopes for the local vaults, and an assortment of claws, and buys all tradeable items.
