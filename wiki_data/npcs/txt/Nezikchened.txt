Item: Nezikchened
Summary: Nezikchened is a level 187 black demon that has to be defeated three different times during the Legends' Quest. He is also among the strongest demons in the game. At the start of each fight he drains a player's prayer points. Like all demons, he is also vulnerable to the effects of demonbane weapons. He has a very peculiar habit of calling the player 'Vacu'. According to Gujuo 'Vacu' was the name for priests who were enslaved by the 'spirits of the underworld'.
