Item: Sigmund
Summary: Sigmund is a high-ranking member of Humans Against Monsters, a former advisor to Duke Horacio and the main antagonist in the Dorgeshuun quest series. He has been involved in three attempts to destroy the Dorgeshuun goblins. The first was during the Lost Tribe quest, the second during the Death to the Dorgeshuun quest, and again in the Another Slice of H.A.M. quest.
