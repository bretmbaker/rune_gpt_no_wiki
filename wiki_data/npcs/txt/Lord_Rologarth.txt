Item: Lord Rologarth
Summary: Lord Rologarth is the real owner of Fenkenstrain's Castle. He was killed and sold to someone by Dr Fenkenstrain. His brain was saved and put into a new body during the Creature of Fenkenstrain quest, and a lightning strike revived him. After the quest he replaces Dr Fenkenstrain in the castle. Rologarth has a brother called Rologarric as revealed in a letter.
