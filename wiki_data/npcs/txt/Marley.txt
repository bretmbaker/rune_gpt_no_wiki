Item: Marley
Summary: Marley is one of the members of Willow's gang. He is recruited in the Below Ice Mountain quest as a skilled thief. He can be found near the Edgeville ruins. He is required to complete the quest, and to progress the player needs to give him a steak sandwich. After the delivery he helps to gain access to the Ruins of Camdozaal by disarming and removing several traps at the entrance hall.
