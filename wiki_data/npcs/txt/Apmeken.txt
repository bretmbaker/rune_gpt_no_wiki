Item: Apmeken
Summary: Apmeken (Jagex pronunciation: AP-muh-ken) is the lesser goddess of companionship and one of the four minor gods of the Menaphite Pantheon, a collection of deities that rule over the Kharidian Desert. According to legend from the Book of Light and Day, she was created from the first of four dreams that the main god Tumeken once had over four days.
