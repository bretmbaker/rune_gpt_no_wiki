Item: Oath Lord D'rows
Summary: Oath Lord D'rows is the lord of the Oathbreakers, former monks of Saradomin, who have defected and now worship Zamorak. He and his fellow Oathbreakers can be found in the Sisterhood Sanctuary, just south of the The Nightmare's arena. The Oathbreakers have taken a vow of silence and do not speak, instead Oath Lord D'rows speaks on their behalf.
