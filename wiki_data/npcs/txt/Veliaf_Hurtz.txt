Item: Veliaf Hurtz
Summary: Veliaf Hurtz is the leader of the Mort Myre division of The Myreque, a group dedicated to fighting Lord Drakan's dominion of Morytania. Veliaf is a bold and resourceful leader, and he is very dedicated to his cause. In In Search of the Myreque, the player saves him and the other members of the group from Vanstrom Klause and slay Vanstrom's Skeletal Hellhound.
