Item: Smoggy
Summary: Smoggy is a Lovakengj dwarf. He resides on the southern beach of Hosidius overseeing the creation of juniper charcoal in the charcoal furnaces, for which he must be paid a fee of 600 coins per furnace. He will also provide players with a tinderbox if they do not have one. He is the son of Moggy, who is found in Lovakengj.
