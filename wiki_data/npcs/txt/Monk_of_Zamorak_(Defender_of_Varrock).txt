Item: Monk of Zamorak (Defender of Varrock)
Summary: This Monk of Zamorak is an unattackable version of the  level 17 Monk of Zamorak held in a cage under the stairs on the ground floor[UK]1st floor[US] of the Varrock Palace. The Monk of Zamorak appears during Defender of Varrock when the palace is attacked by Zemouregal's forces. The monk repeatedly exclaims his support for the invading zombies during the fighting.
