Item: Tina
Summary: Tina is a bartender, along with Emily and Kaylee at the Rising Sun Inn in Falador. She can be found on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] of the building. She sells three types of ale: dwarven stout, wizard's mind bomb, and asgarnian ale. Like her co-workers, she is a young and cheerful woman, but when players ask for a drink and they don't have the money, she gets angry with them, exclaiming "I said 3 coins! You haven't got 3 coins!"
