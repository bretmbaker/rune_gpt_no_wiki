Item: Farmer Fromund
Summary: Farmer Fromund is a farmer that lives on the Fremennik island of Etceteria. He can be found on the southern part of the island, not far from the Miscellanian docks. Fromund can be seen raking weeds. After players complete Royal Trouble, they can help him by raking, watering, or applying compost to his patches, improving their approval rating.
