Item: Larry Leprechaun (2022 Birthday event)
Summary: Larry Leprechaun was a variant of Larry the Tool Leprechaun who was involved with the 2022 Birthday Event in Al Kharid. During the event, Larry Leprechaun was tasked with running a fishing contest at the ponds by the gnome glider. The player had to obtain desert bait from Ali Morrisane to be able to catch a tiny fish, which would be given to Larry to win the fishing contest and complete the event.
