Item: Draul Leptoc
Summary: Draul Leptoc is a wealthy nobleman who can be found in his mansion in Varrock. He is Juliet's father and plays a small role in Romeo & Juliet. If the player talks to him during the quest he will be suspicious and aggressive towards the player. If the player talks to him while having the cadava potion for Juliet with them, he will demand to know what it is.
