Item: Leon
Summary: Leon can be found in Aleck's Hunter Emporium in Yanille. He sells the Hunters' crossbow for 1300 coins at his own shop, Leon's Prototype Crossbow.
He can also make regular and long kebbit bolts used by the crossbow. To do this you need either one spike and 20 coins for 6 regular bolts or one long spike and 40 coins for 6 long bolts.
