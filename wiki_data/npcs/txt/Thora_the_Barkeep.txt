Item: Thora the Barkeep
Summary: Thora the Barkeep, a female Fremennik, tends the Rellekka Longhall Bar in the capital of the Fremennik Province. She serves council members such as Manni the Reveller, and even Brundt, the Fremennik chieftain. She is involved in The Fremennik Trials quest a few times, supplying a legendary cocktail, beer by the glass, tankard and keg. She has a personal vendetta against Askeladden, the sneaky young Fremennik who keeps trying to sneak into Thora's bar.
