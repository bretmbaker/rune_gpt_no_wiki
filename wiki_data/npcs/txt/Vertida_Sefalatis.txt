Item: Vertida Sefalatis
Summary: Vertida Sefalatis is a member of the Sanguinesti division of The Myreque. He is first seen in the quest Darkness of Hallowvale, when the player arrives for the first time in the Myreque Hideout. He is the only one in the hideout at the time, and asks you who you are and what you want. When you explain your reasons, he calms down a little and tells you to talk to his leader, Safalaan Hallow.
