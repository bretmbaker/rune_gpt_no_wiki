Item: Miazrqa
Summary: Miazrqa (pronounced My-az-ra) is a princess of Asgarnia and the great niece of King Vallance.[1] During Grim Tales, she holds captive the dwarf Rupert the beard. She asks you to go fetch her pendant before she will free the dwarf, who gives you a piece of his armour as a reward for freeing him. Her second cousin twice-removed is the witch of Taverley, Nora T. Hagg.[2]
