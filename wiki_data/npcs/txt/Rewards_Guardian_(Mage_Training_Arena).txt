Item: Rewards Guardian (Mage Training Arena)
Summary: The Rewards Guardian is the owner of the Mage Training Arena reward shop and can be found on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US]. The shop exchanges pizazz points for magic-related items in the Mage Training Arena minigame. It is the only way to obtain infinity items, wands, and mage's book (aside from trading with players who have already earned them). It is also the only way to obtain the Bones to Peaches spell. Players must acquire the appropriate number of pizazz points in each of the four rooms to buy items from the Rewards Guardian.
