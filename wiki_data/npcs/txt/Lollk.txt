Item: Lollk
Summary: Lollk is a young dwarf, son of Gilob. He is in the Goblin Cave south-east of the Fishing Guild, north of East Ardougne. He is involved in the Dwarf Cannon quest. To find him, take the centre path when first entering the cave, and head to the north-western most room of the cave. He will be in one of the crates in the north-eastern part of this room.
