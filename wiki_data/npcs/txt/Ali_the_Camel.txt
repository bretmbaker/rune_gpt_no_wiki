Item: Ali the Camel
Summary: Ali the camel is the pseudonym adopted by two camels in southern Pollnivneach, prior to the events of The Feud. After completion of the quest, their true names are revealed as Jasim the camel and Ibil the camel. They can be found in a pen near the local camel trader, Sami, where more ugthanki dung can be obtained by using red hot sauce on their food trough.
