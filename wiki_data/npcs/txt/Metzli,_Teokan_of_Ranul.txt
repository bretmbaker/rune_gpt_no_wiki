Item: Metzli, Teokan of Ranul
Summary: Metzli is the Teokan, or high priest, of the Sect of Ranul. She is involved in Twilight's Promise, first encountered at the temple in central Fortis. Later, at the Teomat, she gives the player information on Ximoua as well as the Twilight Emissaries. After cultists attack during the quest, Metzli disappears for unknown reasons.
