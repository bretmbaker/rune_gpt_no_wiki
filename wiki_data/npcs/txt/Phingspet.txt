Item: Phingspet
Summary: Phingspet Grime [1] is an elderly and somewhat crazed woman found in Varrock Sewers, with her sister, Grimesquit. Both she and her sister can be found near the ladder to east Varrock. Phingspet and her sister help the player to train their cat by catching eight rats during the Ratcatchers quest. After this is accomplished, they will redirect the player to Jimmy Dazzler.
