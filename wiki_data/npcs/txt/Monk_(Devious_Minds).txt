Item: Monk (Devious Minds)
Summary: The mysterious hooded monk is encountered during the Devious Minds quest. He can be found outside of Paterdomus Temple, a church on the border between Misthalin and Morytania. In the quest, the monk asks the player to make him a bow-sword, for which they need to use Doric's whetstone, found in his hut. Players will need a mithril 2h sword, a bow string and level 65 Smithing to be able to do this.
