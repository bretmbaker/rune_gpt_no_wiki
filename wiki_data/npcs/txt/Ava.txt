Item: Ava
Summary: Ava is a scientist residing in the secret room of Draynor Manor, at the south-west part behind the hidden door next to the bookcase. She works as the assistant to Professor Oddenstein, after the incident where Ernest was accidentally polymorphed. As part of her research, she created a device which retrieves used ammunition and other metal items. However, lacking the required materials to make the device, she enlisted the help of a passing adventurer to gather its components. Afterwards, she rewards the player with an attractor. She returns lost devices for a fee of 999 coins.
