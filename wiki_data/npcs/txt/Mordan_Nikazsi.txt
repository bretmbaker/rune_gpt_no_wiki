Item: Mordan Nikazsi
Summary: Mordan Nikazsi is a vampyre found in the south-eastern area of Darkmeyer. During Sins of the Father, he must be spoken to in order to track down the slaves of Desmodus Lasiurus, who players discover were sold to Lord Alexei Jovkai. When asked as to why he disobeyed a request, he states that he would have a quick death rather than die to starvation.
