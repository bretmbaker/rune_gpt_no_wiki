Item: Knight of the Wolf
Summary: The Knight of the Wolf is a ghost who once served the Justiciar of the Wolf. He can be found while looting coffins in the Hallowed Sepulchre. There is a 1% chance of this occurring.[1] After this encounter, the Knight of the Wolf can be found in the lobby of the Hallowed Sepulchre while the ring of visibility or ring of shadows is equipped.
