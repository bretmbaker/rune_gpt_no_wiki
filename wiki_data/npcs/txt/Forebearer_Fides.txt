Item: Forebearer Fides
Summary: Forebearer Fides is a high-ranking member of the Twilight Emissaries encountered during The Heart of Darkness. She begins the initiation ceremony in the Twilight Temple and introduces recruits to the group's leader, the Augur. She then assigns the player and other recruits the task of helping to clear the entrance to the Ruins of Tapoyauik beneath the Temple.
