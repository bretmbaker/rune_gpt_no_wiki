Item: Bork Sigmundson
Summary: Bork Sigmundson can be found fighting Ice trolls in The Fremennik Isles, and when talked to, he will help the player by giving 10 cooked tunas, 3 prayer potion (3) and 3 strength potion (4). If you do not have enough inventory space when you request the items, you will not be able to obtain them again and they will not drop on the ground.
