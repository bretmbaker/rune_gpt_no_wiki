Item: Tinsay
Summary: Tinsay is the middle son of the chief of Tai Bwo Wannai, Timfraku, and one of the brothers in the Tai Bwo Wannai Trio quest. During the quest, he asks for a banana in Karamjan rum, a monkey skin sandwich with seaweed and marinated jogre bones. Before completing the quest, he is located on Cairn Isle; it is recommended to have high agility to get across the bridge to the island safely.
