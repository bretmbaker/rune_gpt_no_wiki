Item: Mandrith
Summary: Mandrith guards the Resource Area in the deep Wilderness. He allows players to enter the arena for a fee of 7,500 coins. However, players will only need to pay 6,000 coins to enter if they have completed the medium Wilderness Diary, 3,750 coins if they have completed the hard Wilderness Diary, and can enter free of charge if they have completed the elite Wilderness Diary.
