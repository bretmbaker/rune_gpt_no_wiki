Item: Gulluck
Summary: Gulluck is the weapon salesman on the 2nd floor[UK]3rd floor[US] of the Grand Tree. He owns Gulluck and Sons. He sells bronze arrows, bronze bolts, shortbows, longbows, crossbows, bronze to mithril arrowtips, iron and steel axes, iron to mithril battleaxes, and bronze to adamant two-handed swords. He may also be an easy order in the Gnome Restaurant minigame.
