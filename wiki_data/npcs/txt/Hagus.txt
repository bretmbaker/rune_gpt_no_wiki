Item: Hagus
Summary: Hagus is a member of the Zamorak Magical Institute who can be found within the Scar essence mine. Having spent many years in the Scar, Hagus has learned how to use the Ventriculus, the abyssal extractor, within the mine. He will offer to help players withdraw runic extracts from the Ventriculus' extract orifice, provided that they have deposited enough coins in his coffer.
