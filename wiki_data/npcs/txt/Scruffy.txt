Item: Scruffy
Summary: Scruffy is the dog of Sir Ceril Carnillean. During the events of Hazeel Cult, he was 20 years old.[1] Scruffy dies after eating a dish of soup given to it by Philipe Carnillean which was originally poisoned by the player if they sided with the Cult of Hazeel in Hazeel Cult. Scruffy's grave can be found in the corner of the yard.
