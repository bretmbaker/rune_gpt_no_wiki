Item: Nelta of Ralos
Summary: The Nelta of Ralos are a collection of nelta who work in service of Ralos. They are typically located near a Shrine of Ralos, which are found at various locations across Varlamore, including the temple within Civitas illa Fortis and the Teomat. They answer to the teoki of Ralos and the Teokan, Servius.
