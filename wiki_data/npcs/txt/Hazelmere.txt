Item: Hazelmere
Summary: Hazelmere is a gnome who lives on a small archipelago just east of Yanille. Hazelmere was the last surviving creator of the Grand Tree, a massive living fortress within the Gnome Stronghold. He was also an accomplished battle mage, the third advisor to King Healthorg, tree whisperer,[1] soothsayer, and telepath with a great knowledge of history and culture. Hazelmere plays a prominent role in The Grand Tree, The Eyes of Glouphrie, The Path of Glouphrie and While Guthix Sleeps quests.
