Item: Street urchin
Summary: The Street urchin is a mischievous thief found in the crime-infested town of Pollnivneach. Players will meet this child during The Feud quest to lure and knock out a villager. After the quest, the player can talk to the street urchin and he will ask the player for money in return for his help during the quest. The player can then give him some coins. This is a one-time interaction.
