King Awowogei (Jagex: /ˈɑːwuːˌwuːɡiː/ AH-woo-woo-gee) is the powerful monkey king and monarch of Ape Atoll.

His palace is located south of the Temple of Marimbo which is in east Marim, the monkey capital. King Awowogei has ape guards blocking the entrance; to get through, talk to the south ape and he will move out of your way. As with the monkeys on Ape Atoll, he is a devoted follower of Marimbo.

Awowogei was one of the party guests attending to Duke Horacio's party during the 2021 Birthday event.

Quests[edit | edit source]

In Monkey Madness I, an adventurer, disguised as a Karamjan monkey, manages to get an audience with Awowogei under the pretence as an ambassador. While he has heard that Karamjan monkeys are inferior to them (since they have no societal structure), he is aware of their resourcefulness. He asks them to rescue another of its kind from the Ardougne Zoo before considering. When they return with the monkey, he allows them to stay as long as they want while he considers the alliance.

Later on, it is shown that he is secretly working with Glough, who sent him a Jungle Demon to dispose of the 10th squad.

In Monkey Madness II, he plans another invasion alongside Glough's invasion. He is concerned with Kruk, as Kruk has been consistently late for the recent war meetings. The adventurer finds Kruk in his dungeon, kills him, and uses Zooknock's powers to masquerade as Kruk. Awowogei reveals that he has recruited the trolls of Trollheim and ogres of Gu'Tanoth to a separate invasion, a new war fleet, and Kruk's secret weapon. The adventurer defeats the champions, destroys the war fleet, and foils the "secret" weapon. When Awowogei hears of this, he dismisses Kruk for his failures and eventually decides on placing Duke as the head of the ninja monkey guards.

At the end of the quest, he negotiates peace with King Narnode, stating that Glough would have sent the "two races into their deaths". It is presumed that Awowogei found out what the "secret" weapon was, and was horrified by what Glough had planned to do with his people. He opens the gates of Ape Atoll to all visitors as a result of this new peace treaty.

Awowogei is part of the Lumbridge Secret Council. During the banquet, he is frozen by the Culinaromancer, but Aris saves everyone in time. The adventurer speaks with Awowogei in Ape Atoll in an attempt to find out what his favourite food is, but he refuses to reveal it. They eventually find out through the three monkeys he mentions that his favourite food is the stuffed snake. When given to free him from the Culinaromancer's grasp, Awowogei remarks about the tasteful flavors of the snake, and teaches the adventurer the Ape Atoll Teleport spell.

While exploring the Tombs of Amascut, the adventurer would come upon the undead baboon matriarch Ba-Ba and defeated her, who left behind a big banana in her haste. The adventurer brought the banana to Awowogei, who was interested in it and asked where they obtained it from. The adventurer recalled their encounter with Ba-Ba and her troupe. He had heard about other troops of his kind across the land, but it was the first he had heard about the baboons. He promised to uphold their memory and was given the banana.

Treasure Trails[edit | edit source]

King Awowogei is the solution to the hard anagram clue: WOO AN EGG KIWI, with a challenge scroll answer of 24. Those who have not completed Monkey Madness II will need to bring a Karamjan monkey greegree and an M'speak amulet to be allowed access by the Elder Guard.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The princely monkey resembles King Awowogei.
  • His examine text describes him as 'dapper'. Despite this, he isn't wearing clothes aside from his hat.
  • King Awowogei's option text in Ape Atoll is cyan, not yellow. This is because he is a piece of scenery, rather than an NPC.
    • Looking at the minimap also reveals that he is actually an object instead of an NPC.
  • The Elder Guard that blocks access to King Awowogei can be bypassed by an action that forcibly moves the player such as planting mithril seeds, though some form of greegree and the m'speak amulet are still required to speak to him.
