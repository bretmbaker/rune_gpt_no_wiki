Miners are dwarves that are located throughout the Motherlode Mine. Eleven are found throughout the mine, and their appearance and examine text varies on each dwarf.

They are able to mine rockfalls.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Several of the miners have examine text which are cultural references:
    • Secretly, he'd like to save princesses and eat mushrooms is a reference to the popular Nintendo game Super Mario Bros, that the miners have a similar colour scheme and outfit to the protagonist of.
    • He could have been a great ballet dancer if they'd let him try... is likely a reference to the famous British movie Billy Elliot.
    • He became a miner after his agoraphobia stopped him doing Sailing. is likely a reference to the failed 2015 proposal of the Sailing skill.
    • M C Pickaxe in da house! is most likely a reference to the popular rap artist MC Hammer.
    • Professional miners don't actually whistle while they work. is a reference to the song "Heigh Ho - Whistle While You Work" from Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs.
    • Mine mine mine mine MINE! could be a reference to "The Mine Song" from the Icelandic children's show LazyTown.
  • When a miner is struck by a rockfall he will scream Ow!, but will take no damage.
  • Despite appearing to mine ore veins in the Motherlode Mine, miners will not consume the vein like a player does.
