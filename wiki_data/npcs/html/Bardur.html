Bardur can be found deep inside the Waterbirth Island Dungeon, fighting dagannoth fledgelings. He will trade the player a cooked shark in exchange for a Fremennik blade, shield, or helm, any of which are obtainable as drops from dagannoths.

During The Fremennik Exiles quest, players can buy a Fremennik shield from him for 150,000 coins, or charm him into giving it to you for free.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • His name seems to be a play on the word barter, the act of exchanging objects for others without the use of money.
  • It is possible for players to have the Fremennik name "Bardur".
  • Players could previously attack Bardur prior to the release of The Fremennik Exiles on 26 September 2019. This was removed presumably to make it easier to talk to him if you wish to buy the Fremennik Shield he sells for 150,000. He had a combat level of 94.
