Not to be confused with Leela (Mahogany Homes).

Leela is a spy from Al Kharid and daughter of Osman, the head spymaster. She can be found just north of the jail in Draynor Village. She is involved in the Prince Ali Rescue quest, where she helps the player organising Prince Ali's escape from Lady Keli's clutches.
