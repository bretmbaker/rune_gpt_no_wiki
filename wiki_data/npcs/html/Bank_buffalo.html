Bank buffalos are buffalos equipped with a saddle and can be used as, or found next to, a bank.

The bank buffalo at the Darkfrost is not recognised as a bank by the Dr. Jekyll random event, allowing a player to retrieve herbs from the bank to receive specific potions from Dr. Jekyll in return.
