The Weird Old Man is an insane man who can be found outside the Northern entrance to the Kalphite Lair.

Transport[edit | edit source]

A quick way to reach the Weird Old Man is by using the nearby fairy ring BIQ.

Treasure Trails[edit | edit source]

Weird Old Man is the solution to the hard cipher clue: ZHLUG ROG PDQ. He will require the player to solve a challenge scroll asking the question: SIX LEGS! All of them have 6! There are 25 of them! How many legs? The answer is 150.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • It's implied that the Weird Old Man may be the famed bug hunter and kalphite researcher Iqbar Ali-Abdula. According to one of the natural historians in the Varrock Museum, Iqbar was driven insane by his research and ran off into the desert.[1]
