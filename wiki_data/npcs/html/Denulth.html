Commander Denulth is the forefront of the Burthorpe Imperial Guard in the Principality of Burthorpe, and acting leader during Prince Anlaf's sudden illness.

Talking to him is required to start the Death Plateau and Troll Stronghold quests. He can be found on the training grounds by a rectangular tent, near The Toad and Chicken inn.

Denulth also appears in the Dragon Slayer II Varrock Palace cutscene.
