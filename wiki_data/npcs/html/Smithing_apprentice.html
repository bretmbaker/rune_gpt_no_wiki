The Smithing apprentice (named Feoras) is one of several tutors stationed in Lumbridge. He provides information about Smithing to both new and advanced players. He is apprentice of the Master smithing tutor Sani in Varrock.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • He is noticably shorter than normal male adult NPCs.
  • Feoras is an Irish name that means Rock, relating to Smithing's connection to rocks.
