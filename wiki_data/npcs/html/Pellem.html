Pellem runs various hunter related shops and services in the Hunter Guild. He is the owner of Pellem's Fur Store, and can make players hunter clothing through the Custom Fur Clothing panel.

Through dialogue, players lacking the Crafting level to craft mixed hide armour themselves can also pay Pellem to create it for them. Players must have 46 Hunter to be able to use any of Pellem's Services.
