A flowering bush was a plant that appeared as part of the flowering bush event, where players needed to pollinate a specific pair of bushes with strange pollen until they became strange bushes.

Before an update on 12 July 2023, flowering bushes only had one colour, which made identifying the correct pair difficult to call out for other players. After the update, this variant was removed and replaced with coloured flowering bushes.
