The sneaky undead fowl is a minor NPC for the Animal Magnetism quest. After helping Alice and Malcolm, a cutscene plays where the husband tries to catch the fowl. Despite having a combat level of 1, players cannot fight or interact with it. Strangely, it will woo at the player, regardless if they're wearing the Ghostspeak amulet or not.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • If the player right-clicks it, they can see that the fowl has a space character at the start of its name, making its name " Sneaky undead fowl".
