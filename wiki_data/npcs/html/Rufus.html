Rufus runs the Rufus' Meat Emporium in Canifis. He is also involved in the quest The Great Brain Robbery. He is a werewolf, but is never seen outside of his humanoid form.

In The Great Brain Robbery, you have to talk to him wearing a Ring of charos, so he'll agree to let you use a crate and gives you crate parts for use in the quest. His connection with the meat store gives rise to comments about the werewolves' preference for raw meat.
