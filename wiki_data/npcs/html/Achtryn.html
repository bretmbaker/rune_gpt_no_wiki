Achtryn is a Slayer master who temporarily replaces Mazchna during the events of While Guthix Sleeps. According to him, it has been a while since he was last asked to fill in for Mazchna, who he considers to be a "mine of information".

Slayer points[edit | edit source]

Players can receive Slayer reward points for completing Slayer tasks—this is based on how many tasks players have accomplished in a row. Completion of a task assigned by Achtryn will grant you:

Task interval Points given
Every task 2
Every 10th 5
Every 50th 15
Every 100th 50
Every 250th 70
Every 1,000th 100
