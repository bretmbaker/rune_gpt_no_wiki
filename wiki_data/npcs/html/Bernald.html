Bernald grows vines outside The Toad and Chicken in Burthorpe. His vine seeds are needed during Garden of Tranquillity, but they are dying.

To cure his prized grape vines, the player must develop a special plant cure. Bernald suggests visiting Alain the gardener, who tends to the tree patch in Taverley.

The cure for the vine is one rune essence, crushed with a hammer, and ground with a pestle and mortar. The remaining rune dust is put into a plant cure and used on the vine to heal it.
