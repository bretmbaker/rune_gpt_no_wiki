Grum is a man who runs Grum's Gold Exchange in Port Sarim. He buys and sells unenchanted rings, amulets and necklaces from sapphire up to diamond.

During the Merlin's Crystal quest, players will have to bring a loaf of bread to his gold exchange and give it to a beggar in order to receive the Excalibur from The Lady of the Lake.
