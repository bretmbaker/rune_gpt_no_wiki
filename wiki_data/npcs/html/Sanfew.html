Sanfew is the leader of the druids and resides in Taverley, on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] of Jatix's Herblore Shop. He plays a part in several quests, including Druidic Ritual, Eadgar's Ruse, and One Small Favour.

Goutweed Exchange[edit | edit source]

Sanfew will trade any goutweed for a single herb, which is rolled off the standard herb drop table. The average goutweed exchange is worth 1,220.16.
