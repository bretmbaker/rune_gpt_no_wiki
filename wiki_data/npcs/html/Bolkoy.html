Bolkoy is a gnome who is found by climbing up a ladder in the Tree Gnome Village, he runs Bolkoy's Village Shop, the village's general store.

Treasure Trails[edit | edit source]

Bolkoy is the solution to the hard anagram clue: BY LOOK. The answer to his challenge scroll is 13.
