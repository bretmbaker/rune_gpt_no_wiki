Noella is a client living in East Ardougne, just north of the Ardougne Market, and is involved in the Mahogany Homes activity. She is one of the possible NPCs that the player can be tasked with helping.

Construction contracts[edit | edit source]

Noella is a possible client given to the player by Mahogany Homes, and may ask you to build the following furniture for them, depending on the type of contract that player chose:
