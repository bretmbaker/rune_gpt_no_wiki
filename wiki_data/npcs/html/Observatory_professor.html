Professor Mambo-duna-roona Gronigen (labelled as Observatory professor) is a scholar found in the Observatory reception (north of Castle Wars). He is the main NPC for the Observatory Quest. After the quest, he is found in the observatory itself. He and his assistant often bicker. In Recipe for Disaster, he is referred to as Professor Gronigen during the short quiz for the Enchanted egg and also in the update in which Observatory Quest was released.
