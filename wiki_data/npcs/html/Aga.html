Aga is a female troll, and one of the only female members of the species as of yet seen in-game, alongside the ice troll females on the Fremennik Isles, the mother of the troll child, and Snowflake from Making Friends with My Arm.

Aga is primarily involved in the Troll Romance quest, and must be convinced by the player to fall in love with Ug, instead of her current boyfriend Arrg. The player does this by giving her trollweiss and defeating Arrg in combat. After the quest, Aga and Ug are together.

Naming convention[edit | edit source]

Most trolls are named after the first thing they eat; if the first thing eaten is unknown, they're named after the first sound they make, which in this case was "aga." This is also how Ug and Arrg got their names.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • If the players uses trollweiss on Aga, they will receive the message "That's probably not a good idea..."
