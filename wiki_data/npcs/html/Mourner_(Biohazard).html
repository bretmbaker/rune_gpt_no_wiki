Mourners are characters who can be encountered throughout West Ardougne during the Biohazard quest.

Originally, most of the mourners could be fought by the player. The three types of mourners in West Ardougne had combat levels of 11, 24, and 18. The two types found in the Mourner Headquarters had combat levels of 24 and 12.
