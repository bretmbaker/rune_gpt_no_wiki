Betty is the owner of Betty's Magic Emporium in Port Sarim. She is the only character selling eyes of newt in free-to-play worlds.

Betty features in The Hand in the Sand and While Guthix Sleeps quests by assisting with the creation of pink dye.

Dye[edit | edit source]

After the completion of The Hand in the Sand quest, players can buy more pink dye from Betty for 20 coins each. The dye does not appear in her usual store, and can only be bought through dialogue with Betty.
