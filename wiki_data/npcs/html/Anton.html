Anton is a smith who runs the Warrior Guild Armoury where he sells various types of armour. He is located on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] of the Warriors' Guild, located in Burthorpe.

Anton talks to himself as he makes the rounds of his shop, extolling the virtues of his wares, but also stating "Ow my toe! That armour is heavy." for a bit of armoury humour.
