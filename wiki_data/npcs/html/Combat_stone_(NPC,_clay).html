Combat stone is an NPC used to display the state of a clay attack stone in the stone space of the games room in a player-owned house. Attack stones cannot be attacked with ranged or magic, only with melee. There are a number of different states for a combat stone, with each representing a different combination of damage from stab, slash, and crush-based attacks.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

Curiously, the stone which should represent one level of stab and one level of crush damage has a model showing one level each of slash, crush, and stab damage. Notably, the misplaced slash damage is shown at the bottom of the stone, rather at the top like all other instances of stones with one level of a given type of damage. Similarly, the stone representing one level of stab and three levels of crush damage shows one level of stab and only two levels of crush damage. Both of these modelling errors are duplicated across all three types of attack stones.
