Elise is the taskmaster for the Kourend & Kebos Diary, located south-east in the courtyard for Kourend Castle in the centre of Great Kourend. She rewards players with Rada's blessing for completing the diary.

She appears to be wearing a dragon platebody, platelegs, boots and gloves.
