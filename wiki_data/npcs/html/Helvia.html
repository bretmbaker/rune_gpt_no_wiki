Helvia can be found in the Sunrise Palace of Civitas illa Fortis. She manages appointments for people who wish to speak with Queen Zyanyi Arkan.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Helvia says, You do not have an appointment. twice to the player, which is a direct movie quote from Mirage from The Incredibles, where she says to Mr. Incredible the same line twice.
