Farmer Maggot is a rabbit in Bunbridge found during the 2020 Easter event, who keeps a farm of calves and a cabbage patch. Broken eggs can be found littered in his farmstead and some chickens are roaming around the farm. There is also a haystack just south of his hut.

If players speak to him with cabbages in their inventory, he will take them away. This is a reference to Farmer Maggot from The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring.
