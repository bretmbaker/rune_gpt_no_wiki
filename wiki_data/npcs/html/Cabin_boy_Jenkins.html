Cabin boy Jenkins is a crew member on the Lady Lumbridge, a ship that a player buys during the Dragon Slayer I quest. Despite the ship's changing hands, Jenkins continued to serve aboard.

He comes with the player and Ned when the Lady Lumbridge sets sail towards to Crandor. However, nearing the island, Elvarg swoops out of the sky and attacks the ship. He is incinerated by Elvarg's dragonfire, along with much of the ship.
