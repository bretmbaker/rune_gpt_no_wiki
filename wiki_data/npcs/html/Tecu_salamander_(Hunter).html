The tecu salamander is a creature found south-east of Ralos' Rise near the minimap mining icon that players can capture with the Hunter skill at level 79 by using a net trap.

Unlike other salamanders, which are caught and able to be used as weapons from the get go, tecu salamanders are often caught at their immature stage; on rare occasions, players may catch a fully grown tecu salamander that can be used as a weapon.

Tecu salamanders may be assigned as a hunters' rumour task by Guild Hunters Gilman, Aco, or Wolf.
