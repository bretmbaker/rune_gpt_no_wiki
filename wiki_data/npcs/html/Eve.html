Eve is an NPC located in the upper area of the Taverley Dungeon, accessed via the steps in the blue dragon area.

She is one of Nieve's many relatives. If players attempt to attack the dragons while not on a slayer task of blue dragons, she will stop the player from attacking them.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • She wears wizard boots and appears to wield an infused wand.
  • Her name and dress are a reference to the iconic protagonist of EverQuest, High-Elf Priestess Firiona Vie.
