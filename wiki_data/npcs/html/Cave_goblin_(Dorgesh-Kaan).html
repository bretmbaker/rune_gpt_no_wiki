Cave goblins are citizens of Dorgesh-Kaan. They can be found wandering the marketplace or in their houses. Sometimes they can be seen conversing with one another.

At level 36 Thieving they can be pickpocketed for 40 experience. Failing to pickpocket a cave goblin may alert a nearby guard, who will then attack the player in response.
