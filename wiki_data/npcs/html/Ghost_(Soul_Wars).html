Players are turned into a ghost when they die in battle during a Soul Wars game. After ~10 seconds, the player will revert to their usual self.

As a ghost, players are unable to run or leave the graveyard they respawned in. Should the graveyard they are in be captured by the enemy team while they are a ghost, the player will instantly revert to their usual self.
