Mary is an experienced farmer living at the farm south of the Farming Guild. She and her husband, Gordon, moved to the farm after finding the land was very fertile.

After completion of the Getting Ahead quest, the player can speak to Mary and she will thank them for their efforts. After this, she will act as a tanner for the player.
