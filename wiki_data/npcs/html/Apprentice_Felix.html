Apprentice Felix is a wizard apprenticed to Wizard Traiborn. During the quest Temple of the Eye, he can be found on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] of the Wizards' Tower, where the player helps him with Traiborn's riddle. After the events in the quest, he stays together with Apprentice Cordelia and Tamara inside the Temple of the Eye to aid The Great Guardian.

Players can purchase unique Runecraft gear from his shop in exchange for abyssal pearls earned from the Guardians of the Rift minigame, as well as purchase abyssal lanterns through dialogue. In addition, abyssal dyes can be exchanged for other colour dyes via Felix, and he will buy any extra abyssal dyes and lanterns from the player for abyssal pearls.

Date Changes
3 May 2023
(update)

Players can now purchase an abyssal lantern from Felix through dialogue for 1,500 abyssal pearls.

30 March 2022
(update)

The amount of Abyssal pearls required to purchase pieces of the Raiments of the Eye outfit from Felix's shop has been reduced:

  • Hat of the eye (reduced from 500 to 400)
  • Robe top of the eye (reduced from 400 to 350)
  • Robe bottoms of the eye (reduced from 400 to 350)
  • Boots of the eye (reduced from 300 to 250)
