Romily Weaklax is the pie baker on the ground floor of the Cooks' Guild. He operates the Pie Shop where you can buy a pie recipe book and most types of pies. Selling most pies to him is not recommended, as he generally pays less than the trade value of the pie.

Players can get tasks from Romily Weaklax to bake between 4 and 28 pies of a certain type, for which he pays. Romily will not request a type of pie if the adventurer lacks the Cooking level to bake it themselves.[1] Some of the rewards he gives for these tasks are given in the table below.

Romily Weaklax only appears to members.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • His examine option states he's a baker from the "North", and according to Mod Ash, he is a reference to a real-life baker from Norths Bakery who would sell pies outside Jagex's offices.[2]
  • The golem from Desert Treasure II - The Fallen Empire has Romily Weaklax as a potential search term, although the player is denied from accessing the data pertaining to him. It is currently unknown as to why this may be the case.
