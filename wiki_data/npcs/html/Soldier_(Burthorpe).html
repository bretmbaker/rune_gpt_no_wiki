Soldiers are members of the Burthorpe Imperial Guard. There are some Soldiers that you cannot attack who are doing military drills in the main area of Burthorpe. They appear to be wearing spiny helmets coloured black without the spikes. When talking to them they will say a random sentence in Latin. They are trained by Sergeants.
