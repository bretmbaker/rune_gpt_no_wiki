Mosol Rei is a warrior and inhabitant of Shilo Village, and involved in the Shilo Village quest. After completion of the quest he offers a way into the village past the Undead ones.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
17 July 2024
(update)

Mosol Rei’s warning messages for climbing over the cart can now be disabled.

18 April 2019
(update | poll)

A right-click/long press option was added to Mosol Rei for players who have completed the Shilo Village quest for entering Shilo village quickly.

5 July –
19 September 2005

The NPC's examine was changed from "Slightly under dressed." to "A warrior from Shilo Village."
