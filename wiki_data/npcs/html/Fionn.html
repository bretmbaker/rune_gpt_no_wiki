Fionn was a pixie featured in the 2013 Halloween event. He could be found near the entrance of Diango's Workshop beneath Draynor Village, along with Cait, Cormac, and Sorcha.

During the 2017 Christmas event, he joins the pixies on a strike, due to them not being able to take a holiday off despite Santa having gone on one.
