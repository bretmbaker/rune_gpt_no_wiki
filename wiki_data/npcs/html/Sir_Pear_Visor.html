Sir Pear Visor can be found in the centre of the Old School Museum. Speaking with him after completing the 2023 Birthday event enables additional drops from activities across the game, to help complete exhibits in the museum's global contribution event.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • As with many of the White Knights in Falador, his name is a play on words; when spoken with a non-rhotic accent, it sounds like "supervisor".
