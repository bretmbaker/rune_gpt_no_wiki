Former vampyres are the result of a player curing a vampyre juvenile or vampyre juvinate in Meiyerditch of vampyrism via the combined use of a Rod of Ivandis or Ivandis flail's special attack and a dose of Guthix balance. They reward the player's kindness by giving them a variety of items.
