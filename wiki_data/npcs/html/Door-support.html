Door-supports are NPC barricades that block players from entering the ladder room on the first floor of the Waterbirth Island Dungeon. There are three door-supports, with one blocking each of the three paths to the ladder room.

The props can be knocked down by attacking them, but they cannot be attacked from the front - attempting to do so will result in a chatbox message stating "This door does not seem to be openable from this side..."

Players wishing to progress past the door-supports have a few options:

  1. They can have another player attack their door-support from within the ladder room (with any combat style), or from one of the other two paths (requires a Magic or Ranged attack with the appropriate tile range).
  2. They can use the special attack of a rune thrownaxe to attack one of the adjacent door-supports; this will cause the thrownaxe to bounce between all three supports, and knock them all down. NOTE: If there are any Dagannoth spawns in the ladder room, the thrownaxe may bounce to them instead of bouncing to the player's door-support. Players should either bring backup thrownaxes, or hop worlds if there are any spawns in the ladder room.
  3. They can use the rocks shortcut with 85 Agility to get to the ladder and bypass the door-supports completely.

Notably, players cannot use a charged Venator bow in place of a rune thrownaxe; the arrow will bounce between the two opposing door-supports, but the one closest to the player will remain standing with the same chatbox message as if the player had tried to attack it directly.

Upon entering the ladder room, two eggs will immediately hatch dagannoth spawns, who will attack with melee. Monsters from the other rooms may also enter through the now open doors to attack with melee, ranged or magic.
