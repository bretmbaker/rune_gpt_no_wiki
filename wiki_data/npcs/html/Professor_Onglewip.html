Professor Onglewip is a gnome sent by King Narnode Shareen to study human magic at the Wizards' Tower. He is found on the ground floor[UK]1st floor[US], generally in the library, although he can sometimes be found outside the tower. If players are doing hard orders in the Gnome Restaurant minigame they may have to deliver food to Onglewip.
