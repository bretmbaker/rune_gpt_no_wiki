Jonathan is a historian specialising in the history of Hosidius, found in a house just south of the bank.

During Dragon Slayer II, the player is sent to him by Imerominia in order to inquire about Camorra Shayzien, but he will explain that a heroine like her wouldn't have associated with Hosidius, as early Hosidius settlers were pacifistic.
