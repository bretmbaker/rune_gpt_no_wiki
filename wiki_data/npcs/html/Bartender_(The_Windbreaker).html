The Bartender at The Windbreaker in Quetzacalli Gorge is a non-player character who sells a variety of food and drinks to players.

During The Heart of Darkness, players must talk to the Bartender to rent a room at The Windbreaker while awaiting a contact of Prince Itzla Arkan (who turns out to be Itzla himself).
