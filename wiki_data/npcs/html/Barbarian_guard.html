The Barbarian guard is one of five NPCs in the Barbarian Outpost. He does not let players in the Barbarian Outpost until they complete the Alfred Grimhand's Barcrawl miniquest. Unlike many other barbarians, he cannot be attacked. He stands next to the gate of the Barbarian Outpost, just east of the Barbarian Assault building.

Players who have completed Alfred Grimhand's Barcrawl will gain the ability to automatically smash vials upon drinking the last dose of a potion. This ability can be toggled on or off by speaking to him or in settings.

Completion of the Barcrawl allows access to the Barbarian agility course, one of the first agility courses added to the game. This agility course can be used at level 35 Agility and gives rates of up to 16k experience per hour.

The Barbarian guard can be reached by teleporting to Barbarian Outpost, and walking out the building and to the east. The Barbarian Teleport or Tele Group Barbarian spells on the Lunar spellbook and the Barbarian Assault minigame teleport also transports players close.
