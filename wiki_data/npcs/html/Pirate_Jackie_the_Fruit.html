Pirate Jackie the Fruit can be found at the Brimhaven Agility Arena entrance. She runs the Agility Arena Store, exchanging the tickets and vouchers obtained in the arena for various rewards, consisting of valuable herbs, amylase packs, Agility experience, and the expensive pirate's hook. The amount of Agility experience received can be increased by 10% by wearing Karamja gloves 2 or higher. Players can also use graceful outfit pieces that have been recoloured blue on her to revert them to their default colouring, although this does not refund any tickets.

She also awards players the Karamja gloves for completing easy, medium, hard and elite Karamja Diary tasks. After the completion of the elite diary she lets the player switch whether bars from the metal dragons in Brimhaven Dungeon drop noted or not.

She also runs the former Brimhaven Agility Arena Ticket Exchange for players who still hold the discontinued tickets, and can also exchange the discontinued tickets for Brimhaven vouchers.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
15 May 2024
(update)

Players can convert the discontinued Agility Arena Tickets into Brimhaven vouchers by using the tickets directly on Pirate Jackie the Fruit.

8 May 2024
(update)
  • Pirate Jackie the Fruit's shop has been revamped into the Agility Arena Store.
    • Players with old Agility arena tickets can still access the former Brimhaven Agility Arena Ticket Exchange to purchase rewards.
  • Agility arena tickets can now be redeemed for 345 Agility experience each, and experience rewarded is no longer scaled by the amount exchanged.
  • Brimhaven vouchers can be used to purchase non-experience rewards from the Agility Arena Store.
  • Amylase packs can be purchased from the Agility Arena store for 60 Brimhaven vouchers.
3 March 2021 - 14 February 2022

Pirate Jackie the Fruit's dialogue was updated to mention double TokKul rewards from TzHaar-Ket-Rak's Challenges when claiming the elite Karamja diary rewards.

1 June 2017 - 14 February 2022

Pirate Jackie the Fruit's dialogue was updated to mention double TokKul rewards from Inferno when claiming the elite Karamja diary rewards.

30 March 2017
(update)

Pirate Jackie the Fruit is now the diary master for all of tiers of the Karamja diary, rather than solely for the Easy tier.

24 March 2016
(update | poll)

It is now also possible to get multiple copies of achievement diary equipment by talking to relevant diary masters or by talking to Perdu, found upstairs in the Edgeville general store. Perdu will charge a small fee.

12 March 2015
(update)

Information on achievement diary rewards is now available from each of the taskmasters at any time rather than only when you complete a diary.

22 June 2005
(update)

The NPC's appearance was altered again with the second rework of female bodykits.

31 May 2005
(update)

The NPC's appearance was altered with the rework of female bodykits.
