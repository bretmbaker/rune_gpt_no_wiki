Lenissa is a ghost who lived during the Second Age. While she was alive, she worked as a spy for Saradomin. She features in the Curse of the Empty Lord miniquest.

While a spy for Saradomin hiding among Zaros' minions, she discovered that someone was offering to sell the staff of armadyl. She sent a message to Dhalak so he could tell Saradomin, unable to go herself as Lucien was watching her and suspicious of her movements.

Locations[edit | edit source]

Lennissa can be found in the following locations.

  • In Port Sarim, near the boat from the Dragon Slayer I quest, second-most southern dock.
  • Inside the Entrana church building.
  • Just south of the Gnomeball Court.

After speaking to her and listening to her story, you'll receive the Ghostly robe (bottom). She will ask you to speak to her superior named Dhalak the Mage.
