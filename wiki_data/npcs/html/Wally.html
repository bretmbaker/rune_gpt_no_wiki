Wally is one of Varrock's most famous heroes. When the powerful demon Delrith attempted to destroy the city, Wally narrowly managed to defeat him with the sword Silverlight in the 19th year of the Fifth Age, 150 years ago. He died sometime after, and has since been largely forgotten. His battle with Delrith is shown to the player by Aris during Demon Slayer.

Sir Prysin is his great grandson and is currently in possession of Silverlight, which he later bestows to the player after they have proved their worth in Demon Slayer.

His helmet, which he is said to have worn when he battled Delrith, is displayed at the Outpost near Ardougne after completion of the Making History quest.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Despite his helmet being present in the outpost, he is not shown to wear it when he is shown fighting Delrith during Demon Slayer.
  • Although Wally is visible in a cutscene during the Demon Slayer quest, he has no examine option, because he is technically the player character transformed into him. Therefore, his examine information of "Wally." can never actually be seen ingame.
  • Wally holds Silverlight as a two-handed weapon, unlike the player.
