Piles is a banknote exchange merchant located inside the Resource Area, and will note items obtained within the Resource Area for a fee of 50 coins per item.

According to Piles, he was inspired by Miles, Giles, and Niles who occupied the Draynor Village market, noting items, and decided to change his name and follow in their footsteps.

Treasure Trails[edit | edit source]

  • Piles is the solution to the master cryptic clue: One of several rhyming brothers, in business attire with an obsession for paper work.
    • The Resource Area entry fee is required (7,500 by default; 6,000 if the medium Wilderness Diary is completed, 3,750 if the hard diary is completed, or free if the elite diary is completed).
