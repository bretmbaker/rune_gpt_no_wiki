Larrissa is a Fremennik woman who dwells outside of the Lighthouse, north of the Barbarian Outpost. She is the starting point of the Horror from the Deep quest, where she asks the player to help her find her boyfriend Jossik, the Lighthouse keeper.

At some point Jossik gave her a spare key to the Lighthouse, which she left in the care of her cousin Gunnjorn for safekeeping. Her family lives in Rellekka, which was recently cut off from the Lighthouse by a storm that wrecked the bridge to the causeway.

Larrissa also appears to be a follower of Armadyl, based on her first interaction with the player, in which she gives thanks to Armadyl.[1] Notably, this would make her the only known living human follower of Armadyl aside from the Guardians of Armadyl.
