Nasty Nick is located directly west of Mistrock near the water. If the player asks him for a quest, he will reply that he has hidden some goodies around Varlamore, and that if the player brings them to him then he will give the player a nasty little surprise.

If the player brings him all four nasty tokens, he will exchange them for a nasty surprise.
