Father Urhney is an irritable priest who lives as a hermit in the Lumbridge Swamp. He has left Lumbridge to perform a full two-years meditation and retreat. Father Urhney never stops meditating, however, since he is constantly interrupted by people asking for amulets or claiming they have come to repossess his house. He suspects they are part of 'a bizarre conspiracy by some fiendish cult.'[1]

Father Urhney plays a role in The Restless Ghost, giving the player a Ghostspeak amulet, which is required to speak to the Restless ghost in Lumbridge. If the player loses the Ghostspeak amulet, even after the quest is done, he will provide the player with a new amulet.
