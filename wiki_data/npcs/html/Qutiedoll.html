Qutiedoll is a player spoof and one of the victims of the Wise Old Man during the Draynor Bank Robbery. He has a combat level of 16, and appears to wear a pair of bunny ears, a studded body, green d'hide chaps, leather boots, an unknown necklace, and a pair of iron claws.

He also makes an appearance in a cutscene during My Arm's Big Adventure.
