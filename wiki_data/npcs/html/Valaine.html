Valaine is the owner of Valaine's Shop of Champions on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] of the Champions' Guild.

She runs a general store, allowing players to sell anything to her. She sells blue capes, black full helms, black platelegs, and adamant platebodies. It is not recommended to sell items to her as she is far away from a bank.
