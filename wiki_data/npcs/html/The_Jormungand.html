The Jormungand is a massive basilisk and the final boss of The Fremennik Exiles. After being subdued by V many years ago, he is reawakened by Bakuna.

Mechanics[edit | edit source]

The Jormungand's basic attack styles are Melee and Magic. As with regular basilisks, a mirror shield or V's shield is required. Protect from Magic should be used during the fight. He also has two special attacks:

Gaze/Glare Attack

The player must turn away from The Jormungand or take large amounts of damage (35+).

During this attack:

  • The screen will be tinted red.
  • The Jormungand gazes upon you! will appear in the game chatbox.
  • Brundt will yell either Turn away [Fremennik name]!, Don't look into his eyes!, Don't look at him [Fremennik name]!, or [Fremennik name]! Do not face him!.

Bind/Entomb Attack

The player will be unable to move and must repeatedly click to break free. Clicking anywhere will cause the player to struggle, and after 5 attempts they will break free. This attack functions similarly to Hespori's bind attack.

During this attack:

  • The Jormungand will fire a slow-moving, stone-like projectile.
  • The Jormungand entombs you in stone! will appear in the game chatbox.
  • Brundt will yell either The stone is weak! You can break it!, Try to break free [Fremennik name]!, [Fremennik name]! You need to break loose!, or You can free yourself [Fremennik name]!.

Should the player die during this fight, they can reclaim their items from Brundt the Chieftain on the Island of Stone for 50,000.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • In Norse mythology, Jörmungandr (meaning "huge monster") also known as the Midgard Serpent is a giant sea serpent which may initiate an apocalypse known as Ragnarök.
  • Unlike other basilisks, the Jormungand does not appear to have any limbs, only a massive serpentine body.
