Ogre guards can be found all over Gu'Tanoth and Jiggig. To gain access to the ogre city, you must present the Ogre relic to a guard.

In the city, the Ogre Guards will ask you to bring them something like a gold bar or, from the market (a rock cake). If you haven't got it the second time you meet them, they punch you down the hill and you will lose a varied amount of Hitpoints.
