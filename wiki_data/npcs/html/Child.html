Children can be found wandering around West Ardougne. Like most of the citizens of West Ardougne, they are terrified of catching the mythical plague, and when spoken to, are told that they are not allowed to speak to strangers. If spoken to while wearing Mourner gear, they will say that they are not allowed to speak to mourners and back away. Children are found near civilians, men, and women of West Ardougne.
