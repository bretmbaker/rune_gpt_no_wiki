Citizens are the common people of Civitas illa Fortis, though a small subset can be found in other areas of the kingdom. Almost all of them can be pickpocketed, save for those that are carrying babies in their slings. Civitas illa Fortis is also home to wealthy citizens, who can be pickpocketed at 50 Thieving and are part of the stealing valuables Thieving activity.

A specific citizen can be pickpocketed under certain specific conditions to find a red token. When the player finds one, a game message will state You find a small token while pickpocketing.
