Ghosts are long dead spirits found throughout the Ruins of Camdozaal, visible only when wearing a ring of visibility. There are three ghosts:

  • A male spy wearing rogue equipment can be found wandering around the Camdozaal Mines.
  • A female spy wearing rogue equipment can be found wandering around the bank area.
  • A wizard wearing hooded robes can be found wandering around the area containing Chaos Golems.

The ghosts are Zarosian loyalists who lived in the beginning of the Third Age after the events of the Betrayal of Zaros. Stuck in time, speaking to them reveals that the ruins were once the Zarosian fortress of Lassar, and it was Saradomin and the Imcando dwarves who laid siege to it.
