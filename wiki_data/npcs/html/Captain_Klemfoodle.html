Captain Klemfoodle is the Gnome glider pilot stationed at south-eastern Karamja (Gandius). A player may ride on his glider to the Grand Tree.

During The Grand Tree, a player can be redirected from the original glider that first crashes on Karamja to land south of the Shipyard safely.

He is also a hard customer of the Gnome Restaurant minigame but does not give any special reward such as the Gnome Scarf or Gnome Goggles.

He also features in a Treasure trail riddle, where one has to dig by the fire near him. Additionally, he has been suspected of stealing passengers' rum.
