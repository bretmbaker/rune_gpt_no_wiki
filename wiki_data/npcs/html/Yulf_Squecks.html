Yulf Squecks is the farmer of the spirit tree farming patch in Etceteria. Players might find it useful to use this farming patch as they collect rewards from their kingdom.

Shop[edit | edit source]

Yulf Squecks also sells basic farming tools via the Farming Supplies shop, which can be accessed by talking to him and asking Can you sell me something?, or by the right-click Trade option.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
9 April 2025
(update)

The wander range for Yulf Squecks has been reduced to a 3-tile diameter.

25 September 2024
(update)

Yulf Squecks now has a standard trade interface for purchasing farming supplies, instead of a dialogue menu, alongside all farmers who protect a player's crops.

22 November 2018
(update)

Gardeners at farming patches now sell plant pots.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Mod Wilson and Mod Isobel devised the name Yulf Squecks by jointly smashing on a keyboard.[1]
