The Gnome Coach is a gnome that can be found at the Tree Gnome Stronghold. He is the NPC who shouts at players participating in the Gnome Ball minigame.

He is a potential easy customer in the Gnome Restaurant minigame and is referred to simply as "Coach".

Treasure Trails[edit | edit source]

Gnome Coach is the solution to the hard anagram clue: C ON GAME HOC He will then ask players "How many gnomes on the gnomeball field have red patches on their uniforms?" and the answer is 6.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Unlike most NPCs, he does not wander around, but walks in circles around the Gnome Ball arena in a clockwise direction, similar to Hans, who walks around Lumbridge Castle. When his path is obstructed for a duration of roughly 30 game ticks, he teleports forward several tiles in his predetermined path and continues walking. When a dialogue is initiated however, he halts indefinitely and only continues walking if the player ends the dialogue and performs some other action that disengages his focus.
