The Recruiter is a non-player character who was found south of the Edgeville bank. Players were able to speak to him to redeem a secret code provided by one of five players (B0aty, MMORPG, Sick Nerd, Torvesta, and Knightenator) who acted as the Generals in the Wilderness Wars.

If players did not have a code to redeem, they could still speak to the recruiter to find out information on the Wilderness Wars.
