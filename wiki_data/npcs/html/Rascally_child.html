Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The examine text of the rascally children is "These children are getting out of hand, now there are six of them" is a reference to a line from the film Star Wars: Episode I – The Phantom Menace, where a character says "This is getting out of hand...now there are two of them." in response to the unexpected appearance of another character.
