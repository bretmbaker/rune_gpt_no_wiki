Brother Jered is a monk at the Edgeville Monastery. Players may speak to him to have their Unblessed symbol blessed one at a time. Since he is on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US], only those with a Prayer level of 31 can access him. Players who achieve level 99 Prayer may purchase the Prayer cape from him for 99,000 coins.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • He believes that the Prayer cape is the hardest to acquire because it requires great devotion to the gods.
  • With the exception of his Prayer cape, he looks identical to Brother Brace.
  • During the 2021 Halloween event, Brother Jered would not help the player on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US], exclaiming to come see him downstairs for his paperwork (where his 2021 Halloween event variant is also located).
