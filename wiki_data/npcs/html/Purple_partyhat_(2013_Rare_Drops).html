The purple partyhat was an item that spawned as part of the 2013 Rare Drops event. Picking up the spawn would provide the player with a purple partyhat. This was the first introduction of partyhats in Old School RuneScape, though they were later made available as part of the annual Christmas holiday events. The spawns were available for 10 days, and dropped at the same time as the red, yellow, blue, green, and white partyhats.

During the event, items had approximately 100 fixed spawn locations across the entire map, and 1 would be active at a time. When the item was picked up, it would immediately spawn at one of the other locations. Only 1 player would be able to pick up a given spawn. When items were visible on the ground there would be fireworks and firework sound effects playing, and there would not be a red dot on the minimap like a normal item.

When picking up the item, a message would say You have found a tradeable rare item. If you lose it, you will not be able to get a replacement. There was no limit to the number of spawns a player could pick up.
