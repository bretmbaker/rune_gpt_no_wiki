A pile of bones is left behind after casting crumble undead on the first boneguard encountered during the Enakhra's Lament quest.

Players have to Climb-over the pile to proceed in the quest. This does not give any experience.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • If the player tries to cast a spell on the pile of bones, the chatbox will say "You can't attack this npc".
