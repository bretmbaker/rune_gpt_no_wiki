The Frog Princess is a frog that is transformed into a human, after the player kisses or pats her to break the spell, during the Kiss the frog random event.

According to the Princess, she had been transformed into a frog by a wizard, who has an obsession with frogs.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The frog princess is likely a reference to the Grimm's fairy tale: The Frog King; or, The Iron Heinrich
