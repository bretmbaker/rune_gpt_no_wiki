Mushroom is a sleeping mountain troll found near the entrance to the Troll Stronghold. He has no interactions, although players can hear a snoring sound if they stand near him.

There is a treasure trail clue which leads the player just next to him.

Most trolls are named after the first thing they eat; in this case, he must have eaten a mushroom.
