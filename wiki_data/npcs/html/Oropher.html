Oropher is one of the many citizens of Prifddinas.

The player can only access Oropher to pickpocket after completing Song of the Elves and require level 85 Thieving. A successful pickpocket yields 353.3 Thieving experience.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • His examine text is a play on the fact that he is found next to a well.
  • In J.R.R. Tolkien's The Silmarillion, Oropher was father to the elf Thranduil of Mirkwood and grandfather to Legolas.
