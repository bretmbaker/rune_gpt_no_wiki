The carpenter is a resident of Sophanem involved in the Icthlarin's Little Helper quest.

If the potion is used on the Carpenter (to relieve him of the itching), he will unfortunately get some of the potion in his eye. Futhermore, the potion has no effect and the Carpenter furiously sends the player away. He is seen in a cutscene in Contact! as part of the High Priest's delegation.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • A section of commented-out code can be found in the game's source files that would have allowed players to sell willow logs to the carpenter for 40 coins each prior to completion of the Contact! quest.[1] Regardless, this feature has never been enabled in Old School RuneScape.[2]

References[edit | edit source]

  1. ^ Jagex. Mod Ash's Twitter account. 30 September 2016. Archived from the original on 13 May 2023. Mod Ash: "[How far through Itchlarin's Little Helper do you need to get for the Carpenter in Sophanem to buy willow logs for 40gp ea?] I don't know that feature, and it looks to me like that code's commented out."
  2. ^ Jagex. Mod Ash's Twitter account. 8 December 2016. Archived from the original on 30 April 2020. Mod Ash: "I had a look at his code. He wants one during the quest, but the feature for selling more later was never released in OSRS."
