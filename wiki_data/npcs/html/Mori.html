Mori is a non-player character involved in The Ascent of Arceuus quest and A Kingdom Divided quest.

After the quest, Mori can be found mourning Thana in the Arceuus Church.

The Ascent, a story included in the Kourend Chronicles, was written by Mori.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • "Mori" is a reference to the Latin phrase memento mori ("remember that you will die"), a symbolic reminder of human mortality.[1]
