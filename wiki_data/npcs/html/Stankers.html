Stankers is a dwarf who runs the coal trucks. Players may place their coal into the trucks and collect them later at the coal truck depot. Players may also obtain a poison chalice from him for free.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Stankers is named after a personal friend of the Gowers, who had a hobby of creating different cocktails, hence the poison chalice.[1]

References[edit | edit source]

  1. ^ Jagex. Mod Ash's Twitter account. 14 December 2018. Archived from the original on 14 December 2018. Mod Ash: "I met this old uni friend at a recent party: Stankers - also a friend of the Gowers, who named the coal truck operator after him back in @RuneScape Classic. They added the Poison Chalice drink to honour Stankers' hobby of inventing "cocktails" by that name and giving them out."
