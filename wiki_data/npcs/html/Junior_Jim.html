Junior Jim is the shipwright located at The Pandemonium. Shipwrights are NPCs found at large ports. They offer several boat-related services, such as buying a new boat, customizing an owned boat and retrieving a boat that has capsized or is at another port. Additionally, he also gives out captain's logs.

During the Pandemonium quest, the player gives Junior Jim an old cup in exchange for a Kandarin raft.
