Ivan Strom is the youngest member of The Myreque, and a holy man. He joined the Myreque at a young age, after his parents were killed by vampyres. He had a strong connection with Sani Piliu, who was also an orphan working with the Myreque.

Ivan Strom is first found in the Myreque Hideout beneath Canifis, alongside other members of The Myreque. During In Aid of the Myreque the player escorts him to Paterdomus Temple, where he can be found during Sins of the Father preparing to travel to Meiyerditch to rejoin The Myreque. After completion of Sins of the Father, Ivan is located in south Meiyerditch, under the trapdoor in the northern room of Old Man Ral's home, in the old Myreque Hideout.

Quests[edit | edit source]

Ivan Strom first appears as the youngest member of The Myreque in the Myreque Hideout near the end of In Search of the Myreque. During In Aid of the Myreque, the player is tasked with escorting Ivan to Paterdomus, the Temple on the banks of the River Salve, to become a priest under Drezel.

During the events of Sins of the Father, it is revealed that the reason why the Myreque and Drezel are so protective of him is because he, along with Drezel, are the only two living descendants of the holy seven priests, who blessed the River Salve against the vampyres in the Fourth Age. By the end of the quest, Ivan is one of the few remaining members of the Myreque to survive the group's clash against Drakan and Vanstrom as part of Vanescula's plan to overthrow her brother, having been able to narrowly teleport himself, Veliaf and the player away from Castle Drakan's courtyard after the arrival of the Vyrewatch Guards, right after Vanstrom's death at the hands of the player and Safalaan's final blast. Safalaan, however, had to be left behind and was captured by the Vampyres as he was too far away to be teleported out by Ivan.

Following the quest, Ivan takes over Vertida Sefalatis's role of selling the player silver weapons. Should players need another Drakan's medallion, Ivan Strom provides an infinite amount of them after completing Sins of the Father.
