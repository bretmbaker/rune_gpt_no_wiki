Barb, the Banker tutor, is a non-player character located in Lumbridge, on the 2nd floor[UK]3rd floor[US] of the Lumbridge Castle. She teaches players about how the bank and bank PIN numbers work. In addition to that, she also serves as a banker within the castle, allowing you to have the option to store or collect items from her.
