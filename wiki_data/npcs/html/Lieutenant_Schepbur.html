Lieutenant Schepbur is a gnome found at the Khazard Battlefield and the commander of the battle tortoises of the Armoured Tortoise Regiment. Lieutenant Schepbur has a golden mace-like weapon that is shaped like a flattened tortoise, and even wears a tortoise shell as armour.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • If he is given gnome food as a delivery in Gnome Restaurant, he'll say: "Soon, gnome cuisine will take over the world."
  • According to him, the tortoises he is currently using were trained in the Tree Gnome Stronghold, and the ones currently being trained there will be sent to him shortly.
