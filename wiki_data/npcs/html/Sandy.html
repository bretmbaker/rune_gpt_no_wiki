Sandy is the owner of Sandy's Sand Corp. His office is located in a small building north-west of the bar in Brimhaven. He owns all the sandboxes in RuneScape. He is featured in The Hand in the Sand, where it is revealed that he murdered a wizard and hid his dismembered body parts all over RuneScape. Players discover this by slipping Truth serum into his drink. He disappears after The Hand in the Sand.
