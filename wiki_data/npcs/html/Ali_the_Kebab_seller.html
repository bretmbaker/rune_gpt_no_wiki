Ali the Kebab seller is a man who runs his kebab business in the town centre of Pollnivneach. He sells kebabs for three coins each, super kebabs for five coins each, and gives red hot sauce for free if the player asks for it, if the player doesn't have it in their bank or inventory. The drop trick will only work to get more than one bottle by using Telekinetic Grab on the dropped item.

After completion of The Feud quest, most citizens of Pollnivneach will have their name changed to their true name; in his case, he becomes known as Isma'il The Kebab Seller.

Treasure Trails[edit | edit source]

Ali the Kebab seller is the solution to the medium cipher clue: BMJ UIF LFCBC TFMMFS. He will present the player with a challenge scroll asking, "How many coins would you need to purchase 133 kebabs from me?", the answer to which is 399. Despite his name changing after The Feud, the cipher does not change to reflect this.
