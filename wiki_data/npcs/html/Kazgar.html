Kazgar is a cave goblin that replaces Mistag in the tunnel near Lumbridge Castle cellar after completing The Lost Tribe quest.

He can show you the way to Mistag in the Dorgeshuun Mines and to Dartog near the water mill cellar after completing Death to the Dorgeshuun.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
23 June 2016
(update)

A "Follow" option was added allowing fast travel to the Dorgeshuun Mines. After Death to the Dorgeshuun this is replaced by "Mines" and "Watermill" options.

20 March 2007
(update)

The NPC was graphically updated with the release of Dorgesh-Kaan.
