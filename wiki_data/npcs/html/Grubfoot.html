Grubfoot is a small, peace-loving goblin who currently lives in Goblin Village and serves as assistant to General Wartface and General Bentnoze. He plays roles in a number of quests, namely Goblin Diplomacy, Another Slice of H.A.M. and Land of the Goblins.
