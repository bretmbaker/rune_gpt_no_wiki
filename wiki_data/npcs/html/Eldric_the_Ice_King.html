Eldric the Ice King is a giant boss, being one of the Royal Titans, along with Branda the Fire Queen. Like Scurrius and Amoxliatl, the Royal Titans are considered an entry-level boss designed for players with 70-90+ combat level, and is meant to introduce the practice of gear switching.

Despite his high ranged defence of 700, the player's ranged accuracy is increased by 500% (x6) whenever he is out of melee distance,[1] and lasts until he moves back within melee distance. This boost also applies during the entirety of the Titans' Elemental Blast attack, which includes the charging portion. As such, players are incentivised to attack him with melee when possible.

Eldric uniquely drops the deadeye prayer scroll, which unlocks the Deadeye prayer - a direct upgrade to the Eagle Eye prayer. Furthermore, he drops the ice element staff crown, which along with the fire element staff crown (dropped by Branda the Fire Queen) and a battlestaff, are combined to create the twinflame staff.
