Sir Renitee is the chief herald of Falador, he is located on the east end of the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] of the White Knights' Castle in Falador. He can sell the player various paintings, maps, to place inside a player-owned house and tell one's heritage to paint as a crest on a steel, adamant or rune kiteshield or on steel, adamant or rune full helms.

Quest Hall items[edit | edit source]

Sir Renitee sells three types of items mountable in a player's Quest Hall:

  • Maps, ranging in size from small to large. Each map size requires a number of Quest points achieved before purchase.
  • Portraits, depicting notable NPCs. Each portrait requires relevant quest completions.
  • Landscapes, depicting areas visited. Each landscape requires relevant quest completions.

Items can be purchased by speaking to him and asking to buy a painting ( 1•2)…

1I don't know, what can you do for me?
2Can I buy a painting?

Mounting an Isafdar painting in your Quest Hall is a requirement for the hard Western Provinces Diary.

Family crest[edit | edit source]

Main article: Heraldic crest

Players with 16 Construction (boostable) can ask Sir Renitee about their family crest.

  • The current family crest can be seen by asking Sir Renitee ( 1•2)…
    1I don't know, what can you do for me?
    2Can you see if I have a family crest?
  • The family crest can be changed by asking Sir Renitee for a different one ( 1•2•3)…
    1I don't know, what can you do for me?
    2Can you see if I have a family crest?
    3I don't like that crest. Can I have a different one?

Asking Sir Renitee to report their family crest is a requirement for the easy Falador Diary.

Changing the family crest to Saradomin is a requirement for the hard Falador Diary.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • His name is a pun on the word "serenity".
  • Originally, players were able to retrieve their own painted helmets and kiteshields after dying to another player. This was deemed unfairly advantageous by the developers and promptly corrected.
