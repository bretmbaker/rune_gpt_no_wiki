Khorvak is a dwarven engineer with the on-click name shown as "Khorvak, a dwarven engineer". He is found in the tunnel under White Wolf Mountain. He's inside the bar and stumbling around with a glass in his hand. Khorvak plays a small role in both the Between a Rock... and the Forgettable Tale of a Drunken Dwarf quests.
