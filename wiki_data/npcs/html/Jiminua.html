Jiminua is a lady on the north side of Tai Bwo Wannai village. She runs Jiminua's Jungle Store. Her store is often used by people making nature runes, to un-note pure essence by selling her some, and then buying it back.

Interestingly, she sells cooked beef for more money than cooked bread, although the bread heals more.

The price of items is affected by the use of Karamja gloves 3 or higher.
