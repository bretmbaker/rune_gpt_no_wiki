Probita is a self-employed[1] young woman who runs the pet insurance bureau in East Ardougne, in the small building next to Aemad's Adventuring Supplies.

If a player loses a one-off pet (which are automatically insured when received), they may reclaim the pet from Probita for a reclaim token. Each token costs 1,000,000 coins; a maximum of 250 tokens may be owned at once. If the player receives a duplicate pet, they will receive a free reclaim token. Checking what pets you have insured is a requirement for the Easy Ardougne Diary.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
26 June 2024
(update)

Probita can now look harder for missing pets if her first search failed.

24 April 2024
(update)

Players who run out of Membership are now able to freely reclaim Quetzin and Smol heredit upon regaining Membership status.

6 July 2022
(update)

Players must now confirm their purchase if they wish to buy more than 10 reclaim tokens at once from Probita.

22 June 2022
(update | poll)

One-off pets are now automatically insured upon receiving one. Players who've paid the insurance fee of 500,000 coins prior to this update will have the fees paid added to Probita's reclaim fee coffer, which can then be used as a discount towards any further reclaims. For example, if players insured two pets for 500,000 coins each, they will be given one free reclaim token, and if five were insured, they will be given two free reclaim tokens and a 500,000 coins discount towards the next reclaim token purchase.

3 February 2021
(update)

Probita's menu now shows the price for reclaiming pets.

28 August 2014
(update)

Probita now animates correctly when walking.
