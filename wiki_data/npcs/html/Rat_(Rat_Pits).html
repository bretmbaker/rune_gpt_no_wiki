Rats can be found in the Rat Pits. During the minigame, they are fought by a player's cat. Each Rat Pit has a different strength of rat, with East Ardougne possessing the weakest and Port Sarim the strongest.

Outside of the minigame, some can be seen being fought by Claude, Topsy, Treacle, Mittens, Loki, Captain Tom, Turbogroomer, and Pusskins.

Combat stats[edit | edit source]

Each of the Rat Pits has three different types of rat of varying combat abilities, although East Ardougne's are all seemingly identical.
