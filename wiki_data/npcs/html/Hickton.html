Hickton is the master of Fletching, and resides in a two-story building near the bank in central Catherby. Hickton also owns an archery supplies shop called Hickton's Archery Emporium. Players with level 99 in Fletching may buy a Fletching cape from Hickton. To purchase the cape, players have to access Hickton's Archery Emporium by trading him to see the cape being sold for 99,000 coins.

Treasure Trails[edit | edit source]

Hickton is the solution to the medium anagram clue: THICKNO. The answer to his challenge: How many ranges are there in Catherby? is: 2.

Fletching cape[edit | edit source]

If a player has 99 Fletching, they will have access to a duplicate version of the shop, with the only difference being the addition of the Fletching cape. The stock of this shop is not shared with the regular shop.
