Spin Blades are obstacles found in the Rogues' Den minigame. The spin blades move in an anticlockwise circle and can be avoided by waiting in alcoves. The spin blades are only found in the north-east corner of the Rogues' Den, which is on the longest path to the centre of the maze and therefore rarely visited by players.

An unused variant of the spin blades exists in the game cache.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The spin blades are represented as an NPC instead of a scenery object, and therefore have a yellow name when hovered. This is likely because the spin blades move along a path, which is only possible for NPCs.
