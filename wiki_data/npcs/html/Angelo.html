Angelo is an assistant carpenter of Mahogany Homes at the Hosidius branch. Players must sign up with Amy to become part of the company and receive construction contracts from clients from Varrock, Falador, East Ardougne and Hosidius. The player can speak to Amy or one of her employees: Marlo, Ellie or Angelo to receive contracts.

Angelo offers the player rewards for their carpenter points, which they receive from completing contracts.
