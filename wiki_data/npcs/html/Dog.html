A dog can be found on the farm south of Falador, and in the caves under The Hollows. The dog in Falador farm is kept as a pet by Sarah, the farm's owner, along with Sadie and a sheepdog that lives inside the house. He roams the chicken pen on the farm's western side. Two additional dogs are located within a small cave accessible after entering the tunnels during In Search of the Myreque. Take the middle tunnel on the western wall to see them.

Players can pet the dog, resulting in the player saying Who's a good doggy! and a chatbox message stating He tries to lick your hands as you pet him.
