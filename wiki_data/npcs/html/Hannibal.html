Hannibal is the cat sleeping in the Clan Hall kitchen. Players can pet him, resulting in the message: He lets out a quiet purr before going back to resting. He can also be fed fish.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Hannibal's name and likeness come from Mod Elena's family cat, who passed away the year prior to the release of the Clan Hall.[1]
