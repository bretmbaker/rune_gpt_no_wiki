Fairy Fixit is the only known fairy builder in RuneScape and is probably very experienced in Construction. Players may find her wandering around the main fairy ring in Zanaris. Being the only fairy builder in Gielinor, she likely maintains the whole fairy ring system.

She owns a shop called Fairy Fixit's Fairy Enchantment, which can only be accessed after completion of Fairytale II - Cure a Queen.
