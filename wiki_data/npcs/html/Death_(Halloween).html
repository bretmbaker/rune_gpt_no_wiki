Death is a non-player character who appears at a player's death during Halloween events. When he appears, he proclaims a random phrase (a list of which can be seen below). According to Mod Ash, Death is an aspect of the Grim Reaper, and is merely a visual representation of the "inconceivable reality".[1]

He is removed at the end of each holiday event.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • His examine text, "Do not fear the Reaper", is a reference to the Blue Öyster Cult's song, "(Don't Fear) The Reaper".
  • A possibility is that Death is a lesser reaper, or that "Death" is a generic term for all lesser reapers (explaining why multiple Deaths can appear at once).
