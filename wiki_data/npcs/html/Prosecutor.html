The prosecutor plays a part in King's Ransom, where you must rebut his evidence that Anna Sinclair was the murderer of Lord Sinclair. He has the following evidence:

  • Anna's fingerprints on the dagger
  • Anna having bought poison and not used it
  • A thread from Anna's clothing being found at the crime scene
  • Anna being at the scene of the crime

After successfully defending her, he will accuse you of helping a cold-blooded murderer go free when you talk to him at the end of the case.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • He will explain to the player that the Seers' Village Courthouse is built over the old Party Room after the balloons fall from the celling.
