Void Knights are followers of Guthix found on the Void Knights' Outpost. While not playing Pest Control, players can talk to Void Knights to spend the commendation points they have earned.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The robe top worn by these NPCs has a different appearance to the one obtained by players.
  • As their Hitpoints levels were revealed to be 200 in Pest Control and the requirements to wear Void Knight equipment are level 42 Attack, Strength, and Defence as well as level 22 Prayer, the Void Knights must have a Combat level of at least 90.
