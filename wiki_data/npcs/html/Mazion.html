Mazion is a monk NPC who resides on the holy island of Entrana, and manages the sand pit there. He is briefly involved with the Hand in the Sand quest, and helps the player to uncover the grisly fate of Clarence, a mage killed by Sandy in order to cover up his money-making scheme of paying his worker, Bert, a pitiful amount of money for a lot of work. Mazion uncovered the head of Clarence in his sand pit.

When talked to by players not doing the Hand in the Sand quest, Mazion says one of a list of few things, such as:

  • "Please leave me alone. A parrot stole my banana."
  • "Hello <player name>, fine day today!"
  • "Nice weather we're having today!"
