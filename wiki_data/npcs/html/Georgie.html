Georgie was one of the children protesting in Varrock Square during the 2019 Christmas event. After the player completed the event, he could be found happily jumping around, waiting for the delivery of gingerbread gnomes.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Georgie is a reference to the character from Stephen King's novel, It.
  • He has the same appearance as Kennith.
