Orsaga can be found at the Hunter Guild in Varlamore. Unlike other NPCs in the guild, Orsaga is not a hunter. Rather, she gathers the hunters' reports for details on stitching small, soft versions of the creatures.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • She appears to be wearing Baron slippers and has a pet chinchompa.
  • She is a reference to WitchCrafty, an artist who makes Old School RuneScape plushies.
