GreatScott85 was an NPC encountered during the 2022 Christmas event.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • GreatScott85's name and design is a reference to Dr. Emmett Brown from the Back to the Future franchise. "Great Scott!" is a catchphrase Doc Brown uses when something surprising or shocking happens. 85, short for 1985, is the year the original Back to the Future film was released and the present year in the movie's story. His combat level, 55, is likely short for 1955, the year Marty McFly is sent back to on accident during the events of Back to the Future.
