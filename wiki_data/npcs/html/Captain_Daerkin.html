Captain Daerkin is an off-duty gnome glider pilot who enjoys watching fights at the Emir's Arena viewing area.

He is a hard Gnome Restaurant customer, and is one of the two customers that can reward the gnome scarf and gnome goggles, the other being Captain Ninto.
