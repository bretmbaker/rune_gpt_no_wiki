Bethan was a fisherwoman involved in the 2023 Pride event. She was in love with Mikey, and wished to write a poem for him to invite him to the pride march.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Bethan had the exact same examine text as Kylie Minnow, who wears a different coloured angler's outfit.
