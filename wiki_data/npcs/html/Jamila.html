Jamila is a merchant in the Sophanem market on the east side of Sophanem. After the quest Contact!, she opens up Jamila's Craft Stall which supplies various Crafting-related goods.

Players can also speak to Jamila to begin the quest Beneath Cursed Sands, after completing Contact! and attaining the quest requirements.
