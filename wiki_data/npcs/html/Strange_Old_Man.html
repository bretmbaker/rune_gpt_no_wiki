The Strange Old Man can be found digging in the Barrows mounds. As his title suggests, he is a very peculiar individual with an obsession for digging, often saying strange things. The crumbling tome is occasionally received when he is talked to. Players can also ask him to condense the warnings when entering the Barrows crypts into only one message.

He is involved in the His Faithful Servants miniquest where he asks players to loot a strange icon for him from the Barrows, after which he destroys it. It is revealed in Desert Treasure II that the destruction of the icon was part of Sliske's plan to prevent Azzanadra from contacting Zaros.

Treasure Trails[edit | edit source]

Strange Old Man is the solution to the hard anagram clue: DRAGONS LAMENT. The answer to the challenge scroll is 40.
