Wounded soldiers are found in the infirmary of Shayzien. They previously were part of the Kourend Favour system, where healing a wounded soldier with a Shayzien medpack would award the player 0.2% Shayzien favour.

Date Changes
10 January 2024
(update)

The NPC's "Talk-to" option was removed alongside the Favour System.

16 March 2022
(update)

Healing the wounded Shayzien soldier would now grant 0.2% favour instead of 0.1%.

16 June 2021
(update)
  • Minor grammatical changes were made in wounded soldier's dialogues.
