The Avatar of Destruction is one of the two avatars in the Soul Wars minigame, being the counterpart of the Avatar of Creation.

This avatar can be found on the red team's side of the battlefield. It initially has 100% damage reduction, but every Soul fragment sacrificed to the Soul obelisk will reduce this by 1%. Every set of bones buried in one of the graveyards will restore the damage reduction by 1%.

Belonging to the red team, it cannot be attacked by and in turn will not attack anyone on said team. The avatar will target whichever player on the opposing team has the highest offensive bonuses.

A pet version of the Avatar of Destruction, Lil' destructor, can be obtained as a possible reward from opening spoils of war. It is initially obtained as a Lil' creator, but the player can switch between them at will.
