The zapper is a power-up that can appear in the Nightmare Zone during Rumble mode. When activated, it damages nearby monsters every two ticks (with the amount of attacks it fires varying each time), and the attacks deal damage roughly equal to 3% of their maximum hitpoints. The zapper does not damage bosses immune to normal attacks (such as the Dagannoth Mother and Tanglefoot). It will last for 1 minute before disappearing. Unlike the Ultimate force power-up, monsters killed by the Zapper will grant Nightmare zone points.
