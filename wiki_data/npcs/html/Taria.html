Taria is the lady who looks after the bush farming patch in Rimmington. She will not look after Poison ivy bushes though, because they cannot be diseased. She may be used as one of the five gardeners that a player must speak to during the Fairytale I - Growing Pains quest.

Treasure Trails[edit | edit source]

Taria is the solution to the medium anagram clue: Ratai

The answer to the challenge scroll she gives you is 7.

Shop[edit | edit source]

Taria also sells basic farming tools via the Farming Supplies shop, which can be accessed by talking to her and asking Can you sell me something?, or by the right-click Trade option.

Changes[edit | edit source]

(update)

The wander range for Taria has been reduced to a 3-tile diameter.

25 September 2024
(update)

Taria now has a standard trade interface for purchasing farming supplies, instead of a dialogue menu, alongside all farmers who protect a player's crops.

(update)

Gardeners at farming patches now sell plant pots.
