Sergeant Damien runs the Drill Demon random event. Unlike most demons in RuneScape, he is not hostile to the player.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • When he appears to take you to the drill demon event, he asks the player, "Do you think you can be the best?" This is a reference to the British army adverts that say, "Be the best!"
