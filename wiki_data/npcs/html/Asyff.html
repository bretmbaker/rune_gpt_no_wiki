Asyff Bymajique,[1] the fancy dress shop owner, runs the Fancy Clothes Store in Varrock. Players can bring Hunter furs or hides to him to have him make Hunter clothing for a small fee. He is also involved in the Eagles' Peak quest.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Asyff is a reference to 1970's British children's cartoon series Mr Benn. In the cartoon, there is a shopkeeper who wears nearly identical clothing and also runs a fancy dress shop.
    • Additionally, Asyff's full name is a play on "as if by magic", the motto of the shopkeeper in Mr Benn.
  • Unlike most NPCs who are members only but are in free-to-play areas, the shop owner will not inform you that he can only be talked to in a member's world. Instead, a message will appear in the chatbox.
  • The shop owner is one of few humans with a non-standard height (he is taller than a dwarf, but shorter than a human).
