Monkey minders are zoo keepers who look after the monkeys at Ardougne Zoo. Players must transform into a monkey and talk to a minder during the Monkey Madness I quest to gain access into the monkey cage.

Note: You may only enter the monkey cage if you have transformed into a Karamjan monkey. Otherwise, he will say "I don't know what kind of monkey you think you are, but you aren't going anywhere near my monkeys."
