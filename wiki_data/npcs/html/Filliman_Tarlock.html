Filliman Tarlock is the central character of the Nature Spirit quest. Players must wear the ghostspeak amulet to speak to him. During the quest, the player helps him transform from a ghost into a nature spirit. After the quest, he will disappear and reemerge during the quests, Fairytale I - Growing Pains and A Night at the Theatre.

He lives in his grotto in Mort Myre Swamp where an altar of Guthix can be found after completing the quest. To reach his grotto, the most efficient way is to run all the way south of the western coast of the swamp.

During the Fairytale I - Growing Pains quest, players will need to ask Filliman to enchant regular secateurs into magic secateurs, used to defeat Tanglefoot. Random items are required to do so; this varies with each player. The complete list can be seen here.

During A Night at the Theatre quest, players must ask Filliman about Hespori and its whereabouts. Filliman will explain that Hespori is found near the island north-east of his grotto.
