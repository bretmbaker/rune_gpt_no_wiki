Wizard Cromperty, located in north-east Ardougne, can teleport you to the Rune essence mine. This is a requirement for the Easy Ardougne Diary. He is also involved with the Tribal Totem, One Small Favour and King's Ransom quests. He has a slight problem with the Postal service.

After completion of the Ardougne medium achievement diary he will give you 100 pure essence for free every day, 150 upon completion of the hard tasks, and 250 upon completion of the elite tasks.

Treasure Trails[edit | edit source]

  • Wizard Cromperty is the solution to the master cryptic clue: A chisel and hammer reside in his home, strange for one of magic. Impress him with your magical equipment. Wear equipment adding up to at least 100 magic attack bonus when talking to him. The passive magic attack multiplication effect of Tumeken's shadow does not count towards the +100 requirement, but its base magic attack bonus of +35 will.
