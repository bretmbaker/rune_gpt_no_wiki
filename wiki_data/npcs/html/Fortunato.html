Fortunato is the owner of Wine Shop in Draynor Village. Free players can purchase jugs and jugs of wine from him, while members can additionally purchase bottles of wine and jugs of vinegar from him once they have started the Rag and Bone Man I quest. He will also stop players from stealing from his wine stall if he is near a player attempting to do so (requiring 22 Thieving and granting 27 experience per successful attempt).

Fortunato buys back all empty wine bottles for 2 coins a piece. This option, however, does not come up in any dialogue; a player must use the empty wine bottle on him.

If you steal from the wine stall before buying the vinegar from him for the quest, you will have to wait 30 minutes before you can talk to him without him calling the guards on you.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Fortunato's name and examine text are references to the Edgar Allan Poe story "The Cask of Amontillado". The character, Fortunato, was a self-proclaimed wine expert and a member of the masonic lodge. Later, his enemy, Montresor, gets him drunk and seals him into a wall using his skills as a mason.
  • Fortunato is Italian for "the fortunate one".
