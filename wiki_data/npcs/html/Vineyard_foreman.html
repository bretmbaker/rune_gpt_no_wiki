The Vineyard foreman is the supervisor of the vineyards located on Aldarin.

When asked if they need any help in the vineyards the Foreman gives the player a grape barrel to fill with ripe grapes and bring back to him. To fill the grape barrel the player has to pick from four ripe grapevines from any of the three vineyards, located north and south of the main building and south-east near the quetzal landing site.

A grapevine can be determined to be ripe if it sparkles/shines, and only one set of grapes can be obtained per ripe grapevine. There will only be four ripe grapevines available for each batch collected, refreshing only after turning in a full grape barrel. Returning with a full grape barrel rewards the player with 5 noted grapes. The task can be repeated as a slow but free and reliable way to obtain grapes with no requirements other than completion of Children of the Sun.
