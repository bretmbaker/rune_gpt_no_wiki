Fishing spots can be found on Tempoross Cove where the player can fight Tempoross. These spots are harpoon only and are used to catch raw harpoonfish and crystallised harpoonfish. The active version of this fishing spot (can be viewed as harpoonfish jumping out of the water) has a chance to catch two fish at once.

Fishing chance[edit | edit source]

The chance to catch a harpoonfish scales from 78.13% at level 35 up to 100% at level 99. When fishing at the spots with leaping harpoonfish, there is a 50% chance to catch a bonus fish on a successful roll.[1]
