Sleepwalkers are non-interactive Morytanians wandering towards the Sisterhood Sanctuary in their sleep. They were added as a teaser prior to the release of The Nightmare.

They spawn in one of 4 locations and walk to the entrance of the Sisterhood Sanctuary where they despawn:

  • In front of the Taxidermist's house in Canifis, walking towards Andras's boat north of Port Phasmatys and riding it to Slepe.
  • Outside the Crombwick Manor.
  • South-west of the entrance to Ver Sinhaza.
  • Outside the Canifis clothing shop walking to Andras's boat.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The sleepwalkers' examine text is a reference to Bring Me the Horizon's song "Sleepwalking".[1]
