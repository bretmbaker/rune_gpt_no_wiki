Banisoch is the sister of Saniboch. She is found west of Tai Bwo Wannai, by a hole that leads down to the bronze dragons within the Brimhaven Dungeon.

In order to access the entrance, players must pay her a one-time fee of 5,000 trading sticks, as she requires it to assuage her feelings of guilt for allowing the player to enter without paying her brother. After paying her, she wields the trading sticks, and her examine text changes.
