Aluft ("Lufty") Gianne Jr is the son of Aluft Gianne snr., and the proprietor of the Gnome Restaurant minigame. Speak to him to start the minigame. Aluft Gianne jnr. is found in the north-west area of the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] of the Grand Tree, just a little west of the cafe. Among his restaurant staff includes the gnomes Timble, Tamble and Spang.

He is involved in The Path of Glouphrie quest, telling the player that Longramble has a long-standing order from the restaurant.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • In front of Aluft is his office table with rotary dial telephones devices on top of it. One is vibrating, as if it is ringing; the handset of the other telephone is picked up and answered by Gianne jnr. When Gianne requests you deliver to Garkor, he mentions hearing monkeys when he called in for the delivery, revealing the device is indeed a telephone.
  • Aluft Gianne snr. and Gianne jnr. replaced an earlier character simply called Aluft Gianne.
