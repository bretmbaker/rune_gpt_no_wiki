Fighters are found in the Emir's Arena. Each of the four arenas will have a pair of these non-interactable fighters in them. They will fight each other until one of them dies, they'll then be replaced by another fighter. Unlike most other NPCs who fight each other, the fighters are capable of healing themselves, when their HP falls low, performing the eating animation after which they restore some of their health.

The melee fighters appear to wear either Maoma's med helm, Maoma's full helm or Maoma's great helm in addition to an elite calamity chest and breeches, dragon warhammer (or), Toktz-ket-xil, fire cape, barrows gloves, spiked manacles and an onyx amulet.

The ranging fighters appear to wear either Koriff's headband, Koriff's cowl or Koriff's coif in addition to an elite calamity chest and breeches, heavy ballista (or), Ava's accumulator, barrows gloves, spiked manacles and an onyx amulet.

The magic fighters appear to wear either Saika's hood with a calamity chest and breeches, Saika's veil with an elite calamity chest and breeches or Saika's shroud with a superior calamity chest and breeches in addition to a smoke battlestaff, barrows gloves, spiked manacles and an onyx amulet.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Players cannot throw rotten tomatoes at the fighters.
  • Most of the fighters do not wear a full set of armour of the same tier. All melee and ranging fighters wear tier-3 chest and breaches although some of them use tier-1 or tier-2 headgear. The magic fighters wearing the tier-2 and tier-3 chests and breaches are wearing the wrong headgear for their tier (tier-3 and tier-2 respectively).
