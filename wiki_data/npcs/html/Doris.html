Doris is Evil Dave's mother. She can be found in her house in Edgeville, just west of the bank. She claims to have lived in Lumbridge in the past but moved due to the lack of guards and the large numbers of pickpockets and people killing the citizens there.

Doris can tell the player a bit of Dave's past and can instruct the player how to restore a Hellcat into a normal cat. Alternatively, a player can talk to Doris with their hellcat out and ask for it to be turned into a normal cat.

Basement[edit | edit source]

Main article: Evil Dave's Basement of Doom

Below Doris's house is her basement which her son has taken as his own. It houses both the basement-dweller Dave and a large swarm of Hell-Rats.

Treasure Trails[edit | edit source]

  • Doris is the solution to the easy cryptic clue: Talk to the mother of a basement dwelling son.
