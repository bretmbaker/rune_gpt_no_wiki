Tiles is a banknote exchange merchant located next to the Bandit Bargains store in the Bandit Camp. Players can show him bank notes and he will offer to exchange them for their equivalent items for a fee of five coins per item.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Tiles, along with the Banknote Exchange Merchant in Pollnivneach, Aisles in Jiminua's Jungle Store, and Phials in Rimmington, was added to the game soon after the introduction of the Ultimate Ironman gamemode in order to give those players a method of un-noting items easily.
  • The banknote exchange merchants' naming convention follows that of the Certer random event NPCs Niles, Miles, Giles, and Piles. This is a reference to the original "Certer" NPCs in RuneScape Classic, who converted items to and from certificates, a precursor to bank notes.
