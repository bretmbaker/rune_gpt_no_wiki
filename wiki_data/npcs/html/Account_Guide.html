The Account Guide is a man found in the Tutorial Island's bank who teaches players about their account.

He will provide information about worlds, membership, bonds and the various options within the Account Management interface.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
27 March 2024
(update)

The Account Guide will no longer advise players with a Jagex Account to link emails to it, as it is no longer necessary.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The Account Guide replaced the Financial Advisor on release, as his predecessor was deemed not as useful. Despite this, they share the same appearance.
