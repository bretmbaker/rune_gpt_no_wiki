Arnold Lydspor runs the Piscatoris Fishing Colony general store and bank, and is somewhat involved with Swan Song. The store and the bank are only available after finishing Swan Song. According to George Laxmeister, Arnold's past is a rather mysterious one. He is suspected to be from a barbarian tribe, but he never speaks of it.

His store, Arnold's Eclectic Supplies, provides rather unusual supplies for a general store. If you sell to Arnold, he will pay 55% of the item value.

Treasure Trails[edit | edit source]

Arnold Lydspor is the solution to the hard cipher clue: BSOPME MZETQPS. He will challenge the player to solve a puzzle box. You must have completed Swan Song to prompt the dialogue.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Arnold's surname (Lydspor), is Danish and Norwegian for "Soundtrack".
  • Should players pick the "Nothing, just came to chat." option while speaking to him after completing Swan Song, he will give players a cabbage.
