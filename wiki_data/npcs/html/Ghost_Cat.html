The ghost cat is the form taken by Marika's loved one during the 2024 Halloween event. They appear in a cutscene after the player plants the correct pumpkins in the former cabbage patch in the South Falador Farm.

The ghost cat has one line of dialogue in the cutscene, and can be understood if players wear both the catspeak amulet and Morytania legs 2 before the cutscene starts. Alternatively, players need only wear the ghostspeak amulet if they have completed Dragon Slayer II up to the point where the Sphinx bestows the ability to understand cats without a catspeak amulet.
