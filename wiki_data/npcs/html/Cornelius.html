Cornelius is the banker of Burgh de Rott. During In Aid of the Myreque, he explains to the player what needs repairing in the bank to make it usable as such. After fixing the bank booth and patching up the wall, he mentions that the player needs to find someone to operate it, to which the player answers by offering him the job, and he gladly accepts after "getting dressed appropriately for the job", when he just puts on a hat with poker cards fit in it. After this, the bank in Burgh de Rott remains unlocked for the rest of the game.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
29 December 2019-June 2020

Cornelius can now be talked to again, using the 'Talk-to' option while standing across from the bank booth. Previously, this would give the message I can't reach that!.

21 March 2013
(update | poll)

With the change to Bank booths' options from 'Use' and 'Use-quickly' to 'Bank' and 'Collect', it became impossible to talk to Cornelius. Previously, this was only possible through the 'Use' option on the bank booth.
