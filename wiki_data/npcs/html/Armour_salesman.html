Aaron the Armour salesman is the owner of Aaron's Archery Appendages. in the Ranging Guild. His shop sells low level Ranged armour. Players who have achieved 99 Ranged can purchase the Ranged Cape of Accomplishment from him for 99,000 coins.

Ranging cape[edit | edit source]

If a player has 99 Ranged, they will have access to a duplicate version of the shop, with the only difference being the addition of the Ranging cape. The stock of this shop is not shared with the regular shop.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • He is the only skillcape salesman who wears the matching hood of his skillcape.
  • His name is not given in-game or in chat, but can be found out from the name of his shop, Aaron's Archery Appendages.
  • He wields a two-handed weapon and a shield, in this case a longbow and a steel kiteshield, at the same time. This is similar to how Vannaka wields both a two-handed weapon and a shield at the same time.
