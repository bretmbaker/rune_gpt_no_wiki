Yrsa is a fashion designer and runs Yrsa's Accoutrements and a makeover shop. She plays a role in Sigmund the Merchant's task in The Fremennik Trials quest.

As with most Fremenniks merchants, Yrsa will not change your shoes or sell you clothes if you have not completed The Fremennik Trials quest.

Shoe makeover[edit | edit source]

Yrsa offers a very wide variety of shoes (and shoe colours) that the player can choose from. There are no costs attached to picking a different pair of default shoes. Since Yrsa effectively gives access to the makeover interface, players can change any other aspect of their appearance free of charge.

Yrsa's Accoutrements[edit | edit source]

Main article: Yrsa's Accoutrements

Yrsa sells a variety of fremennik clothing including hat, robe, shirt, skirt, cloak, gloves and boots. The shirts and cloaks are sold in different colours.
