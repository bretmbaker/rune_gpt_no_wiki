Sigurd is a barbarian who mans the canoe station east of the Barbarian Village. When a player new to canoe making talks to him, he tells a story about when he invented the log canoe, the lowest level of canoe, requiring level 12 Woodcutting to make.

When spoken to, Sigurd will tell players of the best canoe they can make at their level, sometimes accompanied by a comment about an NPC at another canoe station.
