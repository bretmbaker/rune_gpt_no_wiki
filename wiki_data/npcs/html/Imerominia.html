Imerominia is a staff member at the Library Historical Archive in Arceuus. She is a horologist who can tell the player more about the Royal Accord of Twill, and the date convention Great Kourend uses.

She is also spoken to during Dragon Slayer II, where players ask her for information about Camorra.
