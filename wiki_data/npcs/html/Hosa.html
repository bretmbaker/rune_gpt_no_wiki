Hosa is the Hosidius architect in Great Kourend. He is located by the statue of King Rada I at Kourend Castle.

Prior to the removal of the Favour system, players could speak to him to commence the Architectural Alliance miniquest. Players who had spoken to him would discover that he wished to rebuild the statue of King Rada I in the centre of Great Kourend after it was heavily damaged in a storm. However, he was unable to receive the assistance of the other architects in their respective Houses, and requested that you do so for him as part of the miniquest.

Players had to speak to him again after gaining 100% Hosidius favour as part of the Architectural Alliance miniquest. He built the head of the statue.
