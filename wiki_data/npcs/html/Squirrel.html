Squirrels are small rodents who inhabit the north-western part of the Kourend Woodland and the path between Barbarian Village and Falador. They cannot be interacted with and serve mainly as scenery.

While regular squirrels can have grey, dark or brown fur, there are also white squirrels that can be found at Weiss, and black squirrels that can be found on Fossil Island.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • All variants of squirrels, with the exception of Twiggy O'Korn's squirrel in Draynor Village, had a festive override wearing a santa hat during the 2017, 2018, 2019, 2022, 2023, and 2024 Christmas events.
