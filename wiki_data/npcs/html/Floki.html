Floki is a sheepdog that can be found north-west of Land's End. Players can pet the dog, as well as give the dog bones or pieces of meat.

Offerings[edit | edit source]

Floki enjoys most bones and some meats. When presented with an item, Floki will either gnaw/eat it happily, reject it for being "disgusting", or ignore it.

Liked items[edit | edit source]

Floki will take these items from the player:

  • All bones, including exotic bones such as Ourg bones, with some exceptions;
  • Cooked meat and all of the raw meats that can be made into cooked meat, with some exceptions;
  • Cooked chicken and raw chicken

Disliked items[edit | edit source]

Floki will reject these items, with the explanation that they are "disgusting":

  • Shaikahan bones

Ignored items[edit | edit source]

The following items are ignored by Floki:

  • Raw yak meat; this is notable as cooking raw yak meat results in cooked meat, yet Floki will not eat it like the other raw meats;
  • Raw chompy and cooked chompy
  • Raw jubbly and cooked jubbly
  • Raw bird meat and roast bird meat
  • Cheese
  • Fish

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • An unnamed sheepdog was once found where Floki is. It was removed and replaced with Floki after player complaints that the sheepdog did not have a "Pet" option.
  • Fayrg bones, Raurg bones, and Ourg bones retain their capitalisation when shown in the dialogue after giving them to Floki.
  • Curved bones and other items that are only a single item will still be singular in the dialogue after giving them to Floki, but the surrounding dialogue will not be adjusted to match.
