Juan was an Iron Man Tutor located on Tutorial Island and outside Lumbridge Castle. Juan, along with Adam and Paul, could set a player's account to Ironman mode while on Tutorial Island, and gave Ironman armour, hardcore ironman armour, and ultimate ironman armour to Ironman players in Lumbridge.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Juan's name is a play on how "Juan" is pronounced similarly to "one", referencing the one life hardcore ironman players have.
