Mama La'Fiette runs The Other Inn. on Mos Le'Harmless. She sells stews and various beverages after the Cabin Fever quest.

The last cutscene in the Cabin Fever quest is a chat between Mama and Bill Teach.

She will be only available to sell if holding the book o' piracy. Without it, she will babble in pirate language and your character will avoid further conversation.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Mama La'Fiette's last name could be a reference to the real-life 19th century pirate Jean Lafitte.
  • Mama's bar is next door to a bar owned by Joe. Together their names form "Joe Mama", or "yo mama."
