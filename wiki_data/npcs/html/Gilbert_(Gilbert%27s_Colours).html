Gilbert was a leprechaun who sent the player to collect colour strands from rainbows found in various places throughout Gielinor in the event Gilbert's Colours. When completed, he rewarded them with a rainbow scarf.

He returned to Gielinor as a permanent addition exactly 5 years after his removal as part of the 2022 Pride event.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Gilbert is named after Gilbert Baker, an American artist, gay rights activist, and designer of the rainbow flag, which is associated with LGBT rights causes and a symbol of gay pride.
