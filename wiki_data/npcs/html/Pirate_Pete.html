Pirate Pete is the cousin of Party, Postie, and Parroty Pete. Players must talk to him to start the Rum Deal quest. He is on a dock near the Ectofuntus. Zogre Flesh Eaters is required to do the quest.

Players must rescue him in the Recipe for Disaster quest by preparing a fishcake.

Treasure Trails[edit | edit source]

Pirate Pete is the solution to the hard cipher clue: ZSBKDO ZODO. He will challenge the player to solve a puzzle box in order to continue.
