Apprentice Tamara is a wizard apprenticed to Wizard Traiborn. During the quest Temple of the Eye, she can be found on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] of the Wizards' Tower, where the player helps her with Traiborn's riddle. After the events in the quest, she stays together with Apprentice Cordelia and Felix inside the Temple of the Eye to aid the Great Guardian.

During a game of Guardians of the Rift, she can lock the barrier to prevent players from entering once the rift becomes active. This cannot be done on themed worlds.
