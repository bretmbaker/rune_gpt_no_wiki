H.A.M. members can be found in the H.A.M. Hideout north-west of Lumbridge. They are part of the Humans Against Monsters group, as such, they believe humans to be superior over what they view as inferior races. They are low ranking members that follow the orders of high-ranked members such as the H.A.M. Deacon, Sigmund, and Johanhus Ulsbrecht.

Players need 15 Thieving to pickpocket the H.A.M. members, receiving 22.2 experience. If you are caught pickpocketing multiple times there is a chance you may be beaten unconscious and are taken outside of the main area. When players awaken they may find themselves inside a jail cell with Jimmy the Chisel or may find themselves thrown outside of the H.A.M. Hideout.

They are a popular source of easy clue scrolls for low-level and Ironman accounts due to their low requirements. Ironmen also benefit from buttons which are used in Animal Magnetism and rusty swords used for the Easy Ardougne Diary. During the Death to the Dorgeshuun quest the chance to receive H.A.M. robes is considerably higher; you get either of the 7 pieces if you don't get a clue scroll.[1][2]

Getting thrown out[edit | edit source]

When failing to pickpocket a H.A.M. member, players will either receive the message You feel slightly concussed from the blow. or Your agility helps you partially avoid the blow. Upon receiving three slight concussions, the chat box will read You're beaten unconscious and bundled out of the HAM camp. and players get removed from the H.A.M. Hideout (see map below), or locked up in the prison inside the hideout. However, a high Agility level increases the chances of partially avoiding the blow, which will not count toward the three concussions before being kicked out -- at level 99 Agility, players can stay in the hideout nearly indefinitely, regardless of Thieving level. Voluntarily leaving and re-entering the hideout resets the concussion count back to zero.

In addition to Agility level, a player can reduce their chances of being put in jail or thrown outside by wearing H.A.M. robes. Each individual piece of the H.A.M. equipment reduces the player's odds of getting booted from the hideout by about 4%; a full set of all seven pieces thus lowers the odds of being kicked out by roughly 28%.[3] However, neither wearing H.A.M. robes nor having a high Agility level increases the chances of successful pickpockets.[4]

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
9 November 2022
(update)

Both male and female H.A.M. members can now be pickpocketed at level 15 Thieving, granting 22.2 XP per successful pickpocket. Previously male H.A.M. members required level 20 Thieving and dealt 3 damage on failure, and female H.A.M. members granted 18.5 XP.

26 March 2020
(update | poll)

Players can no longer talk to pickpocketable H.A.M. Members, making their left-click option 'Pickpocket'.

26 April –
13 June 2005
  • The male H.A.M. Member's chathead was graphically updated.
  • The skill guide icon was graphically updated.
