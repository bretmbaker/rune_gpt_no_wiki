Dung is a troll chef found on Death Plateau. He stands next to Ash at the centre of the plateau, just in front of the cooking pot they both maintain.

When the player searches the cooking pot in My Arm's Big Adventure, the troll cooks will attack with weak ranged attacks, hoping to put you in the stew for dinner.

Naming convention[edit | edit source]

Most trolls are named after the first thing they eat; in this case, Dung most likely ate goat dung from one of one of the mountain goats found wandering around Trollheim.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Dung and Ash often argue about how best to cook humans, with Dung citing that they taste better when fried, as opposed to boiled.
  • Dung shares a very similar name origin with Goat poo.
