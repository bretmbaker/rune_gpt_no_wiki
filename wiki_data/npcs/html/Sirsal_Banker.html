The Sirsal Bankers are two bankers who run the bank on Lunar Isle. They are two of the only three men on Lunar Isle (the third is the pirate-banker 'Bird's-Eye' Jack). Like the women of the Moon Clan, the two bankers float behind the bank's counter.

Before completing Dream Mentor, players are unable to use their bank without a seal of passage, being sent to Rellekka if they attempt to use the bank or begin dialogue. A seal is still required to bank with either of them after the quest, but 'Bird's-Eye' Jack and his booth do allow banking without one post-quest.

This is the closest bank to the Astral Altar and is used by players when crafting Astral runes.
