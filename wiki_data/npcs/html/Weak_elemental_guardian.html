Weak elemental guardians are rune guardians that assist the Great Guardian as part of the Guardians of the Rift minigame.

They are assembled via the elemental essence pile near the entrance whilst having a chisel and weak cell in the inventory. Doing so grants 30 Runecraft experience and 80 Crafting experience, as well as 5 elemental energy.
