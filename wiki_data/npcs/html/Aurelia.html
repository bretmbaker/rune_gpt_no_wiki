Aurelia is a street urchin found south of the bazaar in Civitas illa Fortis. She will periodically distract the nearby wealthy citizens, allowing players with 50 Thieving to freely pickpocket them without becoming stunned for a short time.

Whenever Aurelia distracts a wealthy citizen while close to the player, a game message will appear: You notice an urchin distract a wealthy citizen nearby. A sound that commonly plays in bank areas will also play whenever this happens, alerting players to this event.
