Clerk is Bravek's secretary and can be found in the Civic Office of West Ardougne. He plays a minor role in the day-to-day running of West Ardougne, mostly administering paperwork. In the Plague City quest, the player has to convince him to let the player see Bravek.

Another clerk can be found in the eastern room of the building.
