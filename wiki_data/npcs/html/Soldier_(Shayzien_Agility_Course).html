The soldier at the Shayzien Agility Course tracks the players individual stats for the basic and advanced Agility courses as well as the global stats for both courses. This includes number of laps and fastest completion time.

As of 22 March 2025, for the basic course the total laps are 10,990,677 and the global best time is 0:49.20, and for the advanced course the total laps are 8,816,123 and the global best time is 0:42.00.
