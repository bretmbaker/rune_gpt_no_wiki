Alry the Angler is a hunter and fisherman found on the island in the middle of Lake Molch. He teaches the player Aerial fishing and sells fishing-related items in exchange for Molch pearls, a currency obtained through aerial fishing. Alry the Angler's Angling Accessories include the Angler's outfit, equipable fishing rods and the fish sack.

A golden tench can be given to Alry in exchange for 100 molch pearls.
