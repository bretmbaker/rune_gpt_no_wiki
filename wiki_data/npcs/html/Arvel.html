Arvel is one of the many Elven citizens of Lletya. Arvel is distrustful of adventurers, and will talk to the player about how they often cause as much trouble as they put right. She offers the parting advice "always do the right thing" when the conversation ends.

The player can only access Arvel to pickpocket after starting Mourning's End Part I and require level 85 Thieving. A successful pickpocket yields coins or other items and 353.3 Thieving experience.
