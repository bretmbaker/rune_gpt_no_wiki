Immenizz can be found in the land of Puro-Puro. Players can talk to him if they want to leave the land of Puro-Puro, in order to go back to Zanaris or the crop circle from which they came.

He seems to be puzzled as to why players wish to leave, and provides some basic information on the crop circle network. He is the only character to refer to aetheric threads, a physical phenomenon involved in inter-dimensional teleportation. He, along with Wandering impling, are the only implings to be able to chat. He resembles an essence impling, but is wearing red robes.

Treasure Trails[edit | edit source]

Immenizz is the solution to the master anagram clue: IM N ZEZIM. He will ask you to solve a puzzle box or light box.
