Sir Spishyus is a White Knight helping the Temple Knights with recruitment. He tests players with a puzzle during the Recruitment Drive quest.

Players have to carry the chicken, bag of grain, and fox across a rickety bridge. Players are only allowed to be holding any one at a time and place all of them on the other side without the chicken eating the grain, or the fox eating the chicken.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • His name is a play on the word "suspicious".
  • His name is an anagram on Sisyphus, the Greek mythological king.
  • He is one of the few NPCs who wields a two-handed sword in one hand and has his helmet equipped in the shield slot.
