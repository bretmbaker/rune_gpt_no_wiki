The Black Knight Captain is a prominent member of the Kinshra order in north-eastern Asgarnia. The captain can be seen in an inaccessible part of the Black Knights' Fortress on Ice Mountain, alongside Greldo, a witch and her black cat.

When players listen through the grills, the above persons can be heard plotting to a make a potion to assist in the destruction of the White Knights. Even in the conversation he has with the witch, the captain does not speak much.

Because the room in which the captain is found cannot be entered, players are unable to interact with him, despite him having a talk option. If somehow one were to find a way to talk to him, he will tell the player to go away and find something to guard if the player is disguised as a fortress guard, or scold the player for being out of uniform if not.[1]
