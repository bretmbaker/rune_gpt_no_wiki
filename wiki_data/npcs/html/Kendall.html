Kendall is a travelling student from the College of Bards in Varlamore. She can be found in the Kingstown park, along with her fellow students Reshi and Thomdril. If the player attempts to talk to her, Thomdril will interject and tell them that Kendall does not talk much, ever since an incident involving demons.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Kendall is a reference to the character of the same name in The Demon Cycle, a series of novels by author Peter V. Brett.
