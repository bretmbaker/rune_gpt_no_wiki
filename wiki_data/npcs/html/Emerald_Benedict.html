Emerald Benedict is a non-player character in the south-west corner of the Rogues' Den, below The Toad and Chicken bar. He runs a bank. Players can interact with him as they can with any other banker, albeit with more humorous dialogue.

He stands near a fire that never goes out. He will never walk away from his spot, making him one of the closest bank to a Cooking-training location. The other being the bank chest on the 1st floor[UK]2nd floor[US] of the Myths' guild.

Players can now purchase bank slots from Emerald Benedict.

(update)

The "Collect" right-click option was added, allowing players to access their offers on the Grand Exchange.

23 January 2014
(update)

Emerald Benedict will no longer disappear under certain conditions.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Prior to an update, Emerald Benedict was able to move away from his spot, this was changed to make cooking simpler.
