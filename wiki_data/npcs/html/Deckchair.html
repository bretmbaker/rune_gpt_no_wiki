The Deckchair is the chair that Leprechaun Larry, Troll Stronghold's herb patch Tool Leprechaun, is laying on. During a cutscene in the quest My Arm's Big Adventure, when the Giant Roc flies in, the chair is tipped on its side and the leprechaun disappears.

As with Tool Leprechauns, players can use harvested crops on the deckchair to exchange items into banknotes. However, the deckchair does not have access to the Leprechaun's tool storage.
