Mistag is a cave goblin who plays a significant part in The Lost Tribe quest and starts Death to the Dorgeshuun. The rag on his left shoulder covers the arm he lost in a mining accident a few years ago.

He can lead the player to Kazgar near the Lumbridge Castle cellar. After completing Death to the Dorgeshuun, he can also lead the player to Dartog near the water mill cellar.

He buys iron ore for 13.6 coins and silver ore for 60 coins, which are equivalent to 80% of their store value. He does not buy noted ore.

After completion of The Lost Tribe, players can give him the Brooch in exchange for a Mining Helmet. This can only be done once.
