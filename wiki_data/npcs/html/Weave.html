Weave is a researcher found within the Morytania Spider Cave. She is the aunt of the Slayer masters Nieve and Steve.

She informs the player that the araxytes found within the cave are very venomous. She also prevents players from killing Araxxor and the araxytes within her cave chamber without a Slayer task.
