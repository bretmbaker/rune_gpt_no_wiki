Hopleez is a player spoof who can be found all along the South Eastern coast of Hosidius. He wields red chinchompa, and wears a white unicorn mask, amulet of fury (or), rangers' tunic, infinity gloves, black d'hide chaps, and flippers.

His name is a play on the phrase "hop please", which means to tell a player to change worlds so that their spot in a certain training area is not taken.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
16 June 2021
(update)

Hopleez now roams around the various sand crab areas, and can no longer be consistently found on any single world.[1] In addition, he can no longer be spoken to.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Hopleez's appearance is based on Mitch Vanvaerenbergh's cosplay[2] at RuneFest 2016.
