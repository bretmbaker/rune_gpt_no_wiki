Zeke is the owner of Zeke's Superior Scimitars in Al Kharid. He sells scimitars up to mithril.

If asked about dragon scimitars, Zeke will say "A dragon scimitar? A DRAGON scimitar? No way, man! The banana-brained nitwits who make them would never dream of selling any to me. Seriously, you'll be a monkey's uncle before you'll ever hold a dragon scimitar." This is a reference to the Monkey Madness I quest which must be completed to wield a dragon scimitar, and in which the player pretends to be the uncle of a Monkey Child. If the player has completed the quest, they will respond with "Hmmm, funny you should say that...".

Zeke plays an important role in the 2021 Birthday event. Duke Horacio had heard Zeke's trap designs could safely trap monkeys and sent the player to talk to Zeke, where Zeke helps the player by supplying premium bananas and bamboo for the player at his shop to create monkey cages. Later he was also invited by Duke Horacio to join the party as one of the party guests.
