Twiggy O'Korn is the master of Achievement Diaries located south of Draynor Village bank. If a player has completed all of the Achievement Diaries, they can purchase an Achievement diary cape and its corresponding hood from her for 99,000 coins.

Twiggy appears to be wearing fancy boots, Karamja gloves 1 (in their original design prior to a graphical update), and bunny ears.

Stock[edit | edit source]

Note: If a player has completed every quest, they will receive the trimmed variant of the cape instead.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • She is one of only four female NPCs that sells a cape of accomplishment, the others being Surgeon General Tafani, the hunting expert, and Kuradal.
  • Her overall appearance is similar to Cecilia.
  • She will sometimes give the player a chocolate bar.
  • Her name may be a reference to the metal band Korn, and Marilyn Manson bassist Twiggy Ramirez.
