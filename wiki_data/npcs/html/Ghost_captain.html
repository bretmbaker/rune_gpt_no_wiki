The ghost captain is a ghost on the docks of Port Phasmatys. He takes players to Dragontooth Island in a small rowboat located in the south-eastern area of the dock, just east of the furnace. As with all ghosts, players must be wearing a ghostspeak amulet obtained from The Restless Ghost quest in order to understand and converse with this NPC.

He will take players to Dragontooth Island for 25 ecto-tokens, or 10 with the ring of charos (a) after completing the Ghost Ahoy quest. Alternatively, players can ride for free after paying him a one-off fee of 500 ecto-tokens.
