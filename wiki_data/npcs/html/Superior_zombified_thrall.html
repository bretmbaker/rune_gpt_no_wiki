Superior zombified thralls are undead that have been resurrected via the Resurrect Superior Zombie spell. Besides the required runes, summoning superior thralls requires four prayer points and having the book of the dead equipped or in the player's inventory.

Superior zombified thralls have an attack speed of 4 (attacking every 2.4 seconds) and attack using Melee (with a max hit of 2), ignoring any accuracy checks in the process. They will attack the same target that the player is attacking, and cannot be damaged. However, they cannot attack other players.

Thralls will remain for a set amount of time (0.6*CurrentMagicLevel seconds), but players can cast resurrection spells again after 10 seconds, albeit the existing thrall will disappear if players do so.
