Sir Vyvin is a ranked White Knight officer that is involved in The Knight's Sword quest, his being the sword that was lost. He lives at the White Knights' Castle in Falador, in the eastern part of the 2nd floor[UK]3rd floor[US]. He sells white equipment to anyone who has completed Wanted!.

Buying a white 2h sword from his shop is a requirement for the elite Falador Diary.

Treasure Trails[edit | edit source]

  • Sir Vyvin is the solution to the hard cryptic clue: Surviving. Players will need to solve a puzzle box.
  • Sir Vyvin is the solution to the master cryptic clue: Hopefully this set of armour will help you to keep surviving. Wear a white platebody and white platelegs.

Knight rankings[edit | edit source]

After completing the Wanted! quest, players can increase their White Knight rank by killing black knights which increase the player's number by 1 per knight. Killing Elite Black Knights will increase the player's number by 12 per knight. Killing white knights decreases the player's number by 1 per knight.

Rank Kills Equipment available
Novice 100 Chainbody, Platelegs
Peon 200 Dagger, Shortsword
Page 300 Med helm, Plateskirt, Square shield
Noble 500 Mace, Staff, Longsword
Adept 800 Full helm, Platebody, Kiteshield, Boots, Gloves
Master 1,300 Two-handed sword, Halberd, Scimitar, Warhammer, Battleaxe, Claws

Players can check their rank by reading the Wanted! quest journal, with wordings of "I am currently a (Rank) White Knight with a kill score of XXX".

Note: If a player gets demoted to "no rank" after killing enough white knights, Sir Vyvin will refuse to sell anything to them.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • His name sounds similar to the word 'surviving'; this word is used as a clue to lead to Sir Vyvin.
  • Sir Vyvin was involved in the 2021 Christmas event, in which the Squire accidentally stained his armour pink. He also had two graphical overrides during the event: a variant without armour, and a festive variant that wore a Santa hat.
