Seven fishing spots can be found in the Ruins of Camdozaal: four next to the preparation table and offering altar, and three near the mine.

These spots give access to raw guppy, raw cavefish, and raw tetra, and members can fish raw catfish. Of these, the three lower levelled fish can be caught with a small fishing net and the two higher levelled fish can be caught with a big fishing net. Additionally, a frogspawn fishing spot can be found to the north of these fishing spots.
