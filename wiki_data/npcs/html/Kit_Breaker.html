Kit Breaker is an aspiring White Knight involved in the 2024 Pride event. During the event, he is knighted by Gilbert after agreeing to be the Protector of the Glade, becoming Sir Kit Breaker. This heroic feat allows them to join the White Knights without having to be a knight's squire.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Unlike most NPCs, who have a Talk-to option, Kit has a Talk-To option where both T's are capitalised.
  • Kit uses he/them pronouns.[1]
  • Sir Kit Breaker is a play on "circuit breaker".
