Funbo is a dwarf found in The Lost Pickaxe pub in Cam Torum. Players can chat with him to have a variety of dialogue options, mostly regarding how much he's had to drink.

Treasure Trails[edit | edit source]

  • Funbo is the solution to the elite cryptic clue: With drinks and dwarves I make my way, upon my head a glass will stay.
