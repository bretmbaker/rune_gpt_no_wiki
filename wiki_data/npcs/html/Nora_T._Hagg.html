Nora T. Hagg is a witch who lives in a house located in Taverley. She is the central figure in the Witch's House quest, during which the player must retrieve the boy's lost ball from her garden.

She has a pet experiment that she hides in her shed, along with other experiments of hers that live in the sewers beneath her house.

During the Grim Tales quest, it is revealed that she is the second-cousin, twice-removed of Miazrqa[1], which implies she's a distant relative of King Vallance.
