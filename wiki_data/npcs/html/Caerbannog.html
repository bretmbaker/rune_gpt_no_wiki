Caerbannog is a rabbit found in the chocolate mine south of Falador during the 2018 Easter event.

Although it cannot be attacked, it has an apparent combat level of 666.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Caerbannog is a reference to the Rabbit of Caerbannog from the film Monty Python and the Holy Grail.
