Sergeant Garkor is a gnome working for King Narnode Shareen as the leader of the 10th Squad, a battalion in the gnomish military. After the events of The Grand Tree, he is instructed to take his squad to the Karamja's eastern Ship Yard. Glough, the anti-human extremist, had secretly begun construction on a fleet of ships which he planned to use to invade the human nations. However, since his plot was foiled during The Grand Tree, the shipyard was now scheduled for decommission.

When travelling to Karamja via military glider, however, they were blown off course, stranding them on Crash Island, next to Ape Atoll. While on Ape Atoll, Garkor covertly investigates King Awowogei, the leader of the intelligent monkeys on the island. After the player investigates Ape Atoll during Monkey Madness I, he gives the player a 10th squad sigil to equip to teleport to the final fight against the Jungle demon, which he and his squad help with. After completing the quest, Garkor remains on Ape Atoll near King Awowogei to continue observing the monkeys to learn more about them.

During Monkey Madness II, after Glough attacks the Tree Gnome Stronghold, Garkor is at the entrance to the crash site, north-west of the Grand Tree. After killing the tortured gorillas, Nieve and the player must enter the Crash Site Cavern. Garkor will help the players enter the area.

After Monkey Madness II, Garkor is in Glough's house, north of the hot air balloon.

Garkor is a potential customer in the Gnome Restaurant minigame.
