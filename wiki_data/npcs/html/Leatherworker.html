The Leatherworker is a Tanner and a member of the Ranging Guild. Found on the north-eastern side of the guild, she can cure and tan hides into leather. Her shop is marked with the tanning icon ().

Unlike other tanners, she does not have a specific Option to access the tanning interface. It is instead accessed through a dialogue option when talking to her.
