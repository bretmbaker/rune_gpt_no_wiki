Lacey is a teenager found in the back of the mansion during Misthalin Mystery. She is tied up by the Killer and dies.

The Killer asks her who the vampyre found in southern Misthalin is. If players stay quiet, she answers Count Draynor. She is killed because Count Draynor is found in western Misthalin, and the correct answer was Count Check. If players attempt to answer in her place, she is killed as well.
