The Nylocas Queen is a massive nylocas created by Verzik Vitur through her extensive breeding program in order to create "the nylocas to end all nylocas". The Nylocas Queen had been growing inside Verzik Vitur after she was bitten by a young prototype nylocas, with the Nylocas Queen presumably ending up killing Verzik, but assuming both her form and her nature.

Background[edit | edit source]

The Nylocas Queen came into existence through Verzik's attempt to breed forth new and more powerful variants of nylocas. By cross-breeding a nylocas from her homeworld Vampyrium with a Gielinorian araxyte, Verzik created this new variant. The young prototype spider bit Verzik and perished instantly after; Verzik thought her experiment was a failure, not knowing of the consequences of the bite. The nylocas made Verzik Vitur change, as she began to put on excessive amounts of weight and began to become ill. She sent for the vampyre doctor and surgeon Vulcan Orvoros, who tried to find the source of her illness.

At first, Orvoros noted Verzik's weight gain as well as her losing her invulnerability to non-silver weapons. He started to do some tests, eventually discovering some eggs within Verzik's abdomen, which he removed, only for them to reappear shortly after. Orvoros noted that more than just the strange eggs were found inside Verzik's body, but before he could leave, the Nylocas Queen burst forth from Verzik's body, presumably killing her in the process. The Nylocas Queen declared that the Nylocas would overthrow the vampyres and rule in their place, before abruptly killing Orvoros.

The Nylocas Queen has remained in Ver Sinhaza since, having adopted Verzik's behaviour and appearing in her form. Only when a group of brave adventurers went up against her – after completing all the challenges of the Theatre of Blood – did the Nylocas Queen reveal her true form as a vampyre/nylocas/araxyte tribrid. However, the queen made her escape by transforming into a bat and flying away.
