Phileas the Lumbridge Guide gives information to new players about where certain places are and what map icons mean. He tells other players not to tell people their password, and gives general advice such as what to kill, how to make money, and more. He does not specialise tutoring in any non-combat or combat skill.

He also plays a role in the Recipe for Disaster quest, where the player must save him from the Culinaromancer. To do this, they must visit Wizard Traiborn, and have him assist them in making a cake of guidance.

In his younger days, Phileas was a part of the Order of Wizards until his beloved, Thenia, was murdered during a robbery. Players can exchange the tarnished locket for the amulet of the eye.

Treasure Trails[edit | edit source]

Lumbridge Guide is the solution to the hard anagram clue: BLUE GRIM GUIDED. He will give you a puzzle box.
