My Arm is a troll farmer at the Troll Stronghold, and Burntmeat's assistant. He is the main focus of the My Arm's Big Adventure and Making Friends with My Arm quests, and is also a regular character in holiday events.

History[edit | edit source]

My Arm loves goutweed, as do most trolls. After finding out it was possible to grow it in Trollheim, Burntmeat directs the player to teach My Arm how to farm, and defend against the baby and giant rocs who attempt to eat it. After the quest, My Arm grows goutweed on the stronghold roof.

When Wolfbone arrives to Trollheim, Burntmeat decides to send My Arm to Weiss to teach the trolls there the art of Farming. To ensure the trolls could still grow goutweed, My Arm teaches Drunken Dwarf's Leg how to farm before departing.

The adventurer helps My Arm reach the city, where he shows Mother how to farm, but the troll leader is not interested in it. My Arm soon falls in love with Mother's daughter, Snowflake, but Mother disapproves. To get on his side, My Arm, Snowflake, Odd Mushroom and the adventurer pretend to kill the Wise Old Man, but Mother sees through their ruse and mortally wounds Odd Mushroom.

My Arm and the player later break out of the prison and confront Mother. After the adventurer slays Mother in battle, they perform a wedding ceremony for My Arm and Snowflake, and the couple start a new life in Weiss.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Trolls are named after the first thing they eat; since he first tried to eat his father's arm, his father decided to name him My Arm.
  • My Arm has a dead relative called My Other Arm.
  • A variant of My Arm hosted a cookout contest during the 2022 Birthday event.
