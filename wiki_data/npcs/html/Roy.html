Roy is the new barkeep for the Rat & Bat pub in the town of Slepe. After the player discovers Carl's and Damien Leucurte's conspiracy to drug the population of Slepe with Elixir of Everlasting-infused bloody bracers, Roy takes over from Carl after he flees.
