The monk is an NPC who will expel anyone carrying weapons or armour from Entrana. If a player somehow manages to acquire any prohibited items while on the island, he will appear and say Hey, get that stuff off our island!. The player is then knocked unconscious and wakes up back at Port Sarim, exclaiming Urrrrrgh!

If the player logs in after creating a prohibited item prior to logging out, they will also be expelled by the monk upon logging in. Black dragonhide armour appears to be excluded from this, as it is used in a master clue step
