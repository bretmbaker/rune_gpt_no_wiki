Mazchna (/ˈmæʃnə/ mash-ner[1]) is the second-to-lowest level Slayer Master, giving tasks to players with a Combat level of 20 and above. He is located in north-eastern Canifis, and therefore completion of the Priest in Peril quest is required to access him. Mazchna is notable for giving Slayer tasks close to his area. His tasks are relatively easy and ideal for players with lower combat levels.

He has a unique sword and is wearing Dharok's platebody as well as a pair of Dharok's platelegs. During While Guthix Sleeps, he is replaced by Achtryn.

Diaries[edit | edit source]

Getting a Slayer task from Mazchna is a task in the Morytania Easy Diary.

Slayer points[edit | edit source]

Players can receive Slayer reward points for completing Slayer tasks—this is based on how many tasks players have accomplished in a row. Completion of a task assigned by Mazchna will grant you:

Task interval Points given
Every task 2
Every 10th 5
Every 50th 15
Every 100th 50
Every 250th 70
Every 1,000th 100
