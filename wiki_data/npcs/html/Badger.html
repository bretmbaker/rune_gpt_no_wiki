Badgers are non-interactive, non-player characters that accompany Broken Handz on an island in the Poison Waste, accessible using the fairy ring code DLR.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The badgers (and their proximity to the fairy ring and Zulrah) are a reference to the famous Weebl animation.
  • Their appearance is near-identical to RuneScape 3's honey badger Summoning familiar.
    • As such, they are also based on honey badgers.
  • Using the fairy ring while a badger is underneath you will result in the badger momentarily appearing in Zanaris.
