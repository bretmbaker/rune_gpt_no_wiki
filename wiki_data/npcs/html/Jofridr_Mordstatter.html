Jofridr Mordstatter is the owner of Neitiznot supplies, a Crafting shop on Neitiznot. She can be found inside the store.

If you talk to her, you will find that she gets her wool from Ned in Draynor Village, and gives him rope in exchange. After the player finds this out, they can talk to Ned and he will give them one free rope.
