Hadley is a tourist guide who resides by the Lake Baxtorian. He shares information about Baxtorian Falls and the legendary elven king Baxtorian during the Waterfall Quest and helps locate the Shadow Dungeon during Desert Treasure I.

When riding out of the Ancient Cavern on a log, players end up near his house.
