Lensig, referred to in-game simply as Witch, is a witch serving the Kinshra order of Black Knights based in the Black Knights' Fortress.

She can be seen in an inaccessible room in the fortress, alongside Greldo, the Black Knight Captain and her black cat. Lensig's name is revealed in the Temple Knight archives concerning Lord Daquarius, accessed through the commorb.

Helda, the witch involved with the Animal Magnetism quest, is her cousin.
