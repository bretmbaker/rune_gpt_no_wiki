Bailey is the keeper of the Fishing Platform, and plays a significant role in the quests Sea Slug and The Slug Menace. Throughout the first two quests of the Sea Slug series, he tries his best to get rid of the sea slugs and manages to evade being mind-controlled.

Roles[edit | edit source]

During Sea Slug, he is the only one on the Fishing Platform who is not possessed by the sea slugs. He discovers the slugs' weakness is heat so you can continue your search for Kennith.

During the The Slug Menace quest, he makes you sea slug glue to glue together fragments, making Page 3 of Brother Maledict's holy book.
