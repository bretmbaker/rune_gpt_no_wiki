Curator Herminius is the head of the Grand Museum in the north-eastern portion of Civitas illa Fortis. The Curator instructs visitors on the history and religion of Varlamore. He is also involved in the Ethically Acquired Antiquities quest, in which he enlists the player's help to recover a stolen item: Xerna's Diadem.
