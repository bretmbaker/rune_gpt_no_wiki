Murcaily can be found in Tai Bwo Wannai, right next to the gate to the hardwood grove. He is not visible until you have completed the Jungle Potion quest, although he will speak to you if you try to enter the grove without paying.

Players must speak to Murcaily to start the Tai Bwo Wannai Cleanup minigame. Entering the grove costs 100 trading sticks.

Murcaily plays a significant part in My Arm's Big Adventure in learning how to grow goutweed. The troll chef Burntmeat discovered this when interrogating one of the ingredients in his stew.

The hardwood grove area has 9 teaks and 4 mahogany trees. The grove contains three level 5 snakes which can make Woodcutting for pures awkward. However, this does not affect the majority of players. There are four axes lying on the floor, two bronze and two iron, which are generally not useful for the players who use the grove for experience, as cutting teak or mahogany is well above the requirement for using a bronze or iron axe for Woodcutting.
