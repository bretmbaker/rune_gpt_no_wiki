Marlo is a carpenter for Mahogany Homes, located in the Varrock Estate Agent's office. He offers Construction contracts for the player. He also plays a role in the Daddy's Home miniquest, where he requests the player's assistance in building some new furniture for his father Old Man Yarlo.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
4 September 2024
(update)

Marlo now has a "Last-tier contract" right-click option that allows players to receive a new Mahogany Homes contract of the same tier as their previous selection.
