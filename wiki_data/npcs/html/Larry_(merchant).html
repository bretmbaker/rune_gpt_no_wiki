Larry is a cape merchant who sells team capes for 50 coins each. His shop Larry's Wilderness Cape Shop sells the 3 variety of team capes which have a cross symbol. He can be found roaming in the western portion of the Wilderness ranging from levels 14-35 (note that he has also briefly walked to level 9 and up to level 36) and though moving constantly, he does stay in certain areas for a minute or two.
