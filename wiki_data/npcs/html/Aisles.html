Aisles is a banknote exchange merchant located in Jiminua's Jungle Store in Tai Bwo Wannai. Players can show her bank notes and she will offer to exchange them for their equivalent items for a fee of five coins per item.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Aisles, along with the Banknote Exchange Merchant in Pollnivneach, Tiles in the desert Bandit Camp, and Phials in Rimmington, was added to the game soon after the introduction of the Ultimate Ironman gamemode in order to give those players a method of un-noting items easily.
  • The banknote exchange merchants' naming convention follows that of the Certer random event NPCs Niles, Miles, Giles, and Piles. This is a reference to the original "Certer" NPCs in RuneScape Classic, who converted items to and from certificates, a precursor to bank notes.
  • She is shorter than most human NPCs.
