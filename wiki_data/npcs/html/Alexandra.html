Alexandra is a gardener found in the north wing of the Farming Guild, near the Redwood tree patch. She can be paid six Dragonfruits to look after the redwood tree once it has been planted, and must be paid 2,000 coins in order to remove it.

She will comment "these take ages!" when quoting the price for cutting it down, referencing the Redwood tree's enormous size.

Shop[edit | edit source]

Alexandra also sells basic farming tools via the Farming Supplies shop, which can be accessed by talking to her and asking Can you sell me something?, or by the right-click Trade option.
