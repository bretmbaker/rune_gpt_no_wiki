Sarah is a woman who lives at the farm between Falador and Port Sarim. She owns two dogs, one being a sheepdog. She runs Sarah's Farming shop, which sells farming supplies.

During A Porcine of Interest, she places a bounty on a monster that attacked her, which was discovered to be a sourhog.
