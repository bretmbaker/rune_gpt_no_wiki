An Elder Chaos druid is located outside the Chaos Temple in level 38 Wilderness. He will offer to unnote any type of bones for a fee of 50 coins per bone. This is useful for prayer training on the Zamorakian altar inside the temple.

While he is visible on free-to-play worlds and provides his bone-unnoting service to free players, he is most useful on members' worlds as the Prayer-training feature of the temple is for members only.

He is also visible on Deadman Mode worlds, but does not offer his bone-unnoting service to Deadmen on seasonal Deadman Mode worlds.[1]
