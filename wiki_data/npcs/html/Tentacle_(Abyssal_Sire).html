Tentacles are monsters controlled by the Abyssal Sire who resides in the Abyssal Nexus. There are six in the boss area, and they protect the Respiratory systems from players. If the player stands next to them, they will attack with melee, which can be partially reduced with prayer.

During phase 1, their presence will severely reduce damage dealt to the systems, with hits dealing 0-3 damage irrespective of the attack's actual damage. This immunity is lifted for 30 seconds when the Sire is stunned with a shadow spell. In phase 2, they will be temporarily disabled, but will be active again from phase 3 until the end of the encounter.
