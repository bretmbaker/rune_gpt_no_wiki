Port master was part of the March 2025 Sailing Alpha.
It was only available on temporary alpha worlds, and can't be found in the main version of Old School RuneScape.

Port masters can help players claim rewards or cancel their current task. In addition, port masters can bottle/unbottle a boat and recover a lost bottled boat.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Each of the port masters' examine texts involve a pun or play on words.
    • The Port Khazard port master's examine is a play on "trip hazards".
