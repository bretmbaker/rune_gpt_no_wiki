Gujuo is a native of the Kharazi Jungle and a proud member of a Kharazi tribe. He can be summoned anywhere in the Kharazi Jungle using a bullroarer during and after the Legends' Quest with the exception of introducing yourself - this must happen near one of the totems.

He plays an important part in this quest, frequently communicating with the player about his tribe and how they may be of help to them. The player must help exorcise an evil spirit called Nezikchened from one of their spiritual totems. In return Gujuo gives them a gilded totem which will buy their membership of the Legends' Guild.

He also plays a minor role in Dragon Slayer II, where players can ask him for the location of the Karamjan Temple.
