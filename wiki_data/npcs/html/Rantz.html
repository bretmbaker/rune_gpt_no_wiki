Rantz is an ogre who lives in a cave in the eastern part of Feldip Hills with his children, Fycie and Bugs. He is located at the only quest start icon in that area, and is first encountered during the Big Chompy Bird Hunting quest. After the quest, players can kill chompies to claim Rantz's feather hats.

Quests[edit | edit source]

In addition to Big Chompy Bird Hunting, where Rantz teachers players how to kill chompies, he is also encountered during Recipe for Disaster, where players must run an errand for him in order to learn how to catch jubblies. Players will also have to do a "small favour" for Rantz during the One Small Favour quest.

Chompy hunting[edit | edit source]

Main article: Chompy bird hunting

Rantz will give players the ogre bow during the Big Chompy Bird Hunting quest, and sells replacements for somewhere between 500 and 550 coins (the exact amount changes each time). Players must use a variation of the ogre bow to kill chompies.

Players who kill a set number of chompies (the minimum being 30, and the maximum being 4,000) will be able to obtain feather hats. You must have an ogre bow or comp ogre bow equipped or in your inventory to claim feather hats from Rantz.

Diary tasks and rewards[edit | edit source]

Players are required to claim hats from him for all stages of the Western Provinces diary:

Diary tier Hat required Kills required
Easy Ogre bowman hat 30
Medium Marksman hat 125
Hard Ogre forester hat 300
Elite Ogre expert hat 1000

Players who have completed different tiers of the Western Provinces Diary can also talk to Rantz for a set amount of free ogre arrows per day, depending on the tier completed:

Diary tier Daily arrows given
Easy 25
Medium 50
Hard 100
Elite 150

Treasure Trails[edit | edit source]

Rantz is also used in various clues for Treasure Trails; the fastest way to reach him by using fairy ring code AKS.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Rantz appears on one of the seven Postbag from the Hedge stamps.
  • His name is a play on the term "Rants", which is a way to describe criticism. This would pertain to the fact that Rantz is always criticising the player during Big Chompy Bird Hunting.
