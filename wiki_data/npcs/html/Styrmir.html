Styrmir is a Fremennik reveller currently visiting the Rellekkan longhall. He can be found seated with his fellow revellers - Fridgeir, Torbrund, and Ospak - waiting for a bard to perform.

During The Fremennik Trials, Styrmir is amongst those that insults the player whilst they try to play a lyre in the longhall.
