Treznor Withings[1] is a gardener who looks after the tree patch outside of Varrock Castle. He is a member of the Group of Advanced Gardeners. Treznor will chop down a planted tree for a fee of 200 coins.

Since he is member of G.A.G., he can be one of the 5 gardeners players must speak to in the Fairytale I - Growing Pains quest.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • His name is a reference to Trent Reznor, the frontman of Nine Inch Nails.[2]
  • In the 11th issue of the Postbag from the Hedge, his moniker is revealed to be "Treznor the Grubby".
  • In the Varrock Census, the profession of Treznor is revealed to be "Kitchen Boy".
