Ambassador Spanfipple is a gnome emissary currently stationed at the Falador White Knights' Castle, presumably discussing diplomatic resolves with acting leader Sir Amik Varze. Speaking to him will reveal how much he dislikes the white colour scheme that the castle possesses.

Spanfipple is a hard level customer during the Gnome Restaurant minigame.
