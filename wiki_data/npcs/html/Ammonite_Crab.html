Training Combat[edit | edit source]

Ammonite crabs are one of the creatures that are recommended for training of all levels. They are similar to rock and sand crabs as they have virtually no combat stats but have the highest health of all the crabs. Low level players should bring some food.

Like all other crabs, ammonite crabs start off aggressive but will become tolerant of the player after ten minutes. As they must be active in order to be fought, players must reset their aggression timer once they become tolerant.

A ring of wealth can be worn to automatically collect the frequently dropped numulite from ammonite crabs, for a small amount of profit.
