Brother Maledict is a priest who worships Saradomin in Witchaven and plays a role in the quest The Slug Menace. Like all generic villagers in Witchaven, he falls under the mind-controlling properties of the sea slugs during The Slug Menace.

Roles[edit | edit source]

During the Slug Menace quest he is given a welcome to the 'family' by a unknown character, possibly Mayor Eustace Hobb and becomes possessed by the sea slugs. He disappears after the quest along with Col. Jake O'Niall and Mayor Eustace Hobb.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • "Maledict" is an adjective meaning cursed.
  • The scarf around his neck gets a gold trim when he is possessed.
