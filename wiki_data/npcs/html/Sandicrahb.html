Sandicrahb is a tribesman from Karamja. He can ferry players to Crabclaw Isle for 10,000 coins per trip. This is halved if players complete the easy Kourend & Kebos Diary, and the trip is free if the medium diary is complete.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Sandicrahb says that his boat is made of the Fyburglars tree. This is a reference to the actual material fibreglass, which is a synthetic material commonly used to make boats.
