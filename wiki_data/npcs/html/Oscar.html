Oscar is involved in the 2022 Pride event. He will either ask, along with his boyfriend Alan, the player to try and spur their friends Sylvia and Marsha into getting together as a couple, or Sylvia and Marsha will request the same for Alan and Oscar, in exchange for a quality violet tulip seed.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Oscar is a reference to Oscar Wilde, a famous Irish poet and playwright who was imprisoned for engaging in homosexual acts in 1895.
