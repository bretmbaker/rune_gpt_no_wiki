Justine runs the rewards shop for the minigame, Justine's stuff for the Last Shopper Standing in the Last Man Standing lobby in the Ferox Enclave.

She appears to be wearing a victor's cape (50), a slightly dull amulet of defence (t) and wielding two swift blades.
