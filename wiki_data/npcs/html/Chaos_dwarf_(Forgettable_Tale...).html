An army of Chaos dwarves can be seen in a cutscene during Forgettable Tale... The Red Axe Director reveals he has created them as part of his plan to conquer Keldagrim. Before the player can report this information to Commander Veldaban, they are knocked out by Grunsh.

Unlike the regular Chaos dwarf, these cannot be attacked or reached by the player.
