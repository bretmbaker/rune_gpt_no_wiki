Fadli is the banker at the Emir's Arena. He is also the owner of the Shop of Distaste, where you can buy rotten tomatoes for 1 coin each, these can be thrown at other players. Fadli also serves as a collection point for your Grand Exchange items.

According to Fadli, he is the best warrior in Al Kharid. He wears leather gloves, leather boots, and a hardleather body.
