Captain Ninto is a drunk, off-duty Gnome glider pilot that can be found inside the dwarven passage under the White Wolf Mountain (Sindarpos). He can only be reached after the player has completed the Fishing Contest quest.

He is a hard customer of the Gnome Restaurant minigame and is one of the two customers that can reward the Gnome scarf and Gnome goggles, the other being Captain Daerkin.
