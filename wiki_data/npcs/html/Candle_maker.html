The candle maker can be found in his Candle Shop in Catherby. He sells candles for three coins each. During Merlin's Crystal, the player must give a bucket of wax to the candle-maker, and he will make a black candle for the player. Although he considers black candles unlucky, he will continue selling them for three coins each.

Diaries[edit | edit source]

  • The candle maker is involved in the Easy Kandarin Diary, where players are given the task of "buy[ing] a candle from the Candle maker."

Treasure trails[edit | edit source]

  • The Candle Maker is the solution to the elite cryptic clue: I burn between heroes and legends.
