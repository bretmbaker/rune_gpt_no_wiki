Abigaila is a refugee from Morytania who lives upstairs in the Lumbridge General Store. She can also be found by the spirit tree north-east of the Grand Exchange. She managed to escape from the vampyres across the River Salve, and is paid by the local gentry for her scrying skills.

Players can speak to her to spectate participants within the Theatre of Blood, but they are not allowed to bring any items with them (excluding the oculus orb), and they must not be poisoned or diseased.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • A tatty toy can be found in the Lumbridge general store that bears the name of her dead son.
  • It is possible to unlock music tracks by spectating groups completing the Theatre of Blood.
  • When spectating a group the player is placed within the various Theatre of Blood arenas.
    • In the Maiden of Sugadinti, Pestilent Bloat and Soteseg rooms the player is placed on one of the outer ledges of the arena, and is free to move.
    • In the Nylocas room the player is trapped in one of the ceiling webs, possibly beside a web holding a Twisted Bow.
    • In the Predator Xarpus room the player is placed on one of the higher ledges of the arena, restricted by two iron bars beside them.
    • In the Verzik Vitur room the player is trapped in one of the cages beside Verzik's throne.
    • In Verzik's treasure vault the player is placed in the center area besides the War table, where spectators can learn various details about the performance of the participants.
