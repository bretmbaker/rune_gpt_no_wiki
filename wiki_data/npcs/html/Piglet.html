Piglets are young pigs kept as livestock by humans. They can be found inside pens at the farm south of Falador as well as in Draynor Village.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
11 July 2005
(update)

The NPCs' examines were changed from "Porker."

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The Mini Pig examine is a reference to a line in the 1999 comedy film Austin Powers: The Spy Who Shagged Me.
