The Leagues Assistant was found in the former location of the Leagues Tutor in the Kourend Castle courtyard. She was there to inform players that the tutor has relocated to just outside of Lumbridge Castle.

She wears the Twisted Relichunter (T1) outfit, but with a pair of twisted boots (t3).
