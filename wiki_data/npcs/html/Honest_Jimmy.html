Honest Jimmy runs Honest Jimmy's House of Stuff outside of the Trouble Brewing minigame. He trades items for Pieces of eight that are rewarded from Trouble Brewing.

He can be contacted with the NPC Contact Lunar spell. He can tell you how many players are currently waiting in the lobby for that world as well as how much time until the next game of Trouble Brewing starts. If you cast the spell while standing right next to him, he will tell you he doesn't have time to deal with you.

Trivia[edit | edit source]
