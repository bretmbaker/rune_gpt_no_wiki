Prince Itzla Arkan is the son of Zyanyi Arkan, the Sun Queen of Varlamore. His father is Servius, the Teokan of Ralos. Prince Itzla is first encountered during the Children of the Sun quest, where he leads the delegation visiting Varrock on behalf of the Kingdom of Varlamore. He is also involved in Twilight's Promise but disappears near the end of The Heart of Darkness where he is teleported to an unknown location during the encounter with Amoxliatl.

Itzla is the current heir of the Arkan dynasty and a descendant of Mezpah Arkan.

In combat[edit | edit source]

BUG: When killing him with a Scythe of Vitur, he will not die, you must unequip the Scythe and punch him for the final hit.

When fighting Itzla during the fourth trial, he will use strong melee attacks and will hit up to 16 hitpoints with normal attacks, so Protect from Melee is advised. He will use protection prayers whenever you perform a special attack, and uses two in turn, both of which he will warn you about in advance. For instance, he will pray Protect from Magic if a Voidwaker special attack is used.

Special attacks[edit | edit source]

The first special attack is his sword swipe which will disable the player's Prayer and will deal some damage. Once his animation is complete, immediately reactivate Protect from Melee and continue fighting him.

The second special attack is his area-of-effect (AoE) damage, which he will indicate with a message saying, You might wanna dodge this one! two ticks before launching this special attack. Simply run toward the back of him as he will launch a trapezoidal-shaped AoE attack in front of him.
