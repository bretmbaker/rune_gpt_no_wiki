Elnock Inquisitor is a gnome whom players need to speak to in order to start the Impetuous Impulses minigame. He will also supply players with a butterfly net and 7 empty impling jars to start them off. If players lose any of the original equipment he gave them, they may NOT reclaim it from him.

Players can store butterfly nets, an imp repellent, and up to 1,000 impling jars via the right-click "Exchange" option. Players can also use the right-click "Quick-withdraw" option to withdraw their equipment.

Players may also trade Elnock certain jarred implings in return for objects such as imp repellent, a magic butterfly net, a jar generator, or empty impling jars. In addition, Elnock sells 10 impling jars per day for 2,000 coins per jar.

Lastly, players are automatically opted out from gaining Strength experience while pushing through magical wheat, but can choose to receive Strength experience when doing so by asking the Elnock Inquisitor about wheat.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
20 March 2024
(update)

Elnock's dialogue has been streamlined so that it no longer prompts players to acquire an impling scroll every time he is spoken to.

1 March 2023
(update)

Players are now opted-out of gaining Strength experience while pushing through magical wheat by default, and can opt back in by talking to the Elnock Inquisitor.

20 January 2021
(update)

Elnock can now perform a number of services:

  • Toggle whether or not players get Strength XP from pushing magical wheat.
  • Store a butterfly net, imp repellent and up to 1,000 impling jars.
  • Trade for up to ten jars a day for 2,000 coins each.
