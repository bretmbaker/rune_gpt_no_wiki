Bugs is a rabbit found in the chocolate mine south of Falador during the 2018 Easter event.

Although he cannot be attacked, he has an apparent combat level of 80. He can however, be reported, which results in the player simply talking to themselves.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Bugs and his examine text is a reference to Bugs Bunny.
  • His reporting option is a pun on game glitches, often referred to as bugs.
