Nurse Emma Gentsy is a nurse who works at the Battlefront, next to General Vir's quarters.

Her name is a play on the word "emergency".

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
12 February 2025
(update)

The NPC's gown has been recoloured to its original intended colour, as part of a fix involving the implementation of detecting malformed recolours in the game's configs.

16 February 2022
(update)

The red cross symbol has been changed to a red asterisk to comply with Article 53 of the 1949 Geneva Convention I as well as Section 6 of the United Kingdom's Geneva Conventions Act 1957.

16 June 2021
(update)

Nurse Emma Gentsy has been graphically updated.
