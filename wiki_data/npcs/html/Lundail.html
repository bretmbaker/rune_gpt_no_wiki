Lundail sells runes in his store Lundail's Arena-side Rune Shop in the Mage Arena bank, which is located just west of the Mage Arena, and is not located in the Wilderness. He sells air, water, fire, earth, body, mind, nature, chaos, law, cosmic, and death runes.

After completing the easy Wilderness Diary, players can claim-runes from him to receive one type of catalytic rune, selected randomly from the seven kinds he sells. The number of runes increases according to the tier of Wilderness Diary completed:

Diary Amount Average GE price Runes
Easy 40 3,680

Medium 80 7,360
Hard 120 11,040
Elite 200 18,400

Visiting Lundail requires the player to traverse deep Wilderness; the closest teleport is to start in Edgeville, then pull the Wilderness lever to the Deserted Keep. A slash weapon such as the Wilderness sword, a knife, or Aranea boots are required to cut the webs along the way to the Mage Arena.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
30 August 2023
(update)

The random, free runes given by Lundail once per day for the completion of Wilderness Diaries have been increased:

  • The easy diary has increased from 10 to 40 runes.
  • The medium diary has increased from 20 to 80 runes.
  • The hard diary has increased from 30 to 120 runes.
  • The elite diary has increased from 50 to 200 runes.

In addition, he no longer gives air, earth, fire, or water runes, and now gives death runes.

23 March 2022
(update)

The NPC's examine text was changed; previously, it was "He sells rune stones."

5 March 2015
(update | poll)

Lundail now gives free random runes once per day for the completion of Wilderness Diaries:

  • 10 runes for the Wilderness easy diary.
  • 20 runes for the Wilderness medium diary.
  • 30 runes for the Wilderness hard diary.
  • 50 runes for the Wilderness elite diary.
