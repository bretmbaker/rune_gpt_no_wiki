Wizard Jalarast is a wizard who wears full splitbark armour in the Wizards' Tower. She tells you how to gather the materials required to craft it, and will also make it for you for a fee.

To obtain some splitbark armour from her, the player needs to bring her some fine cloth and bark, with some coins. In total, a player is required to have 11 bark, 11 fine cloth, and 77,000 coins in order to make a full set.
