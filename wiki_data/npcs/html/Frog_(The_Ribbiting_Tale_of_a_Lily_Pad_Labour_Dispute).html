Frogs are found at the Locus Oasis before and during The Ribbiting Tale of a Lily Pad Labour Dispute. After starting the quest, Gary and Sue will have a talk-to option and will introduce themselves and explain the labour strike dispute with Marcellus. After this conversation, all five frogs will have their names displayed and can be talked to.

The frog variants for Dave, Jane, and Cuthbert with a talk-to option are never encountered during the quest and appear to be unused.
