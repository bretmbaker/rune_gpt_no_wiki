Cute creatures are six-legged creatures initially found in the Tree Gnome Stronghold and Tree Gnome Village. There are six cute creatures in the Stronghold and one in the village. Later, during The Eyes of Glouphrie, they are revealed to be disguised evil creatures. The Village cute creature is exposed during The Path of Glouphrie.
