Eblis is a Zarosian mystic who plays a large role in the Desert Treasure I quest. During the quest, the player helps Eblis free the Mahjarrat Azzanadra from his prison in the Ancient Pyramid; to do this, the four Diamonds of Azzanadra must be collected, and to this end Eblis (with the player's help) enchants a number of mirrors to discern the diamonds' locations.

Eblis can be found just south-east of the Bandit Camp at the mystical mirrors after Desert Treasure I is completed. If Desert Treasure I is not completed, he can be found in the eastern building in the Bandit Camp.

If you bring Eblis an ancient icon along with an ancient staff, he will combine the two together, upgrading the staff to the ancient sceptre. If you do not already have the ancient staff he sells, use the ancient icon on him with an ancient staff in your inventory.

Similarly to the bandits of the bandit camp, Eblis will not talk to players wearing any Zamorak or Saradomin God equipment.

Stock[edit | edit source]

He will sell a player one Ancient staff upon completion of the Desert Treasure I quest for 80,000 coins. In addition, he will give players ancient signets, a component in building Ancient altars and occult altars.
