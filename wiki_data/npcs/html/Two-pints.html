Two-pints is located in the Flying Horse Inn at East Ardougne. She is known for being the taskmaster for the Ardougne Diaries. Her name is most likely a reference to the fact that she had one too many pints, as her drunken gait suggests. She supplies the player with free Ardougne cloaks once they complete the corresponding achievement diary.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
4 January 2018
(update)

Two-Pints now gives more accurate information about the diary cape reward teleports.

24 March 2016
(update | poll)

It is now also possible to get multiple copies of achievement diary equipment by talking to relevant diary masters or by talking to Perdu, found upstairs in the Edgeville general store. Perdu will charge a small fee.

12 March 2015
(update)

Information on achievement diary rewards is now available from each of the taskmasters at any time rather than only when you complete a diary.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Two-pints is a reference to a nickname given to Mod Lottie by Mod John C.[1][2]
  • Two-pints wears the Ardougne Cloak 3 rather than the Ardougne Cloak 4, unlike other taskmasters that wear the fourth tier of their respective Diary reward.
