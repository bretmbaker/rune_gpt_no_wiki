Starflowers grow in the cosmic entity's plane.

Picking the flower requires 49 Farming. The flowers can be used during and after the Fairytale II - Cure a Queen quest to create a magic essence potion.

When first arriving on this plane no star flowers will be available, but they will start to bloom within a minute of arriving. The flowers grow faster the more players are present.
