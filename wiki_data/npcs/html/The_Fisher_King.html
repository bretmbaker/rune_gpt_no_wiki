The Fisher King ruled the Fisher Realm and protected the Holy Grail. During the quest he and his land have a connected sickness. This is cured only when Sir Percival, his son, arrives to take the crown from his father's sick head. At the end of the quest, it is speculated he dies.

During the quest, the Fisher King appears to be wearing a white chainbody, though it has a shoulder pad and a sleeve on the left arm. He is also wearing white platelegs, leather gloves, and wielding a staff.
