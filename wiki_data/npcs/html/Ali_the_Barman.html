Ali the Barman owns The Asp & Snake Bar in Pollnivneach. Other than serving alcoholic beverages to his thirsty customers, he also assists players with The Feud quest.

After completion of The Feud quest, most citizens of Pollnivneach will have their name changed to their true name; in his case, he becomes known as Faisal the Barman.

If the player tells Ali that someone has poisoned Traitorous Ali's drink, he will offer the player a beer as thanks. This can only be done once.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
9 November 2022
(update)

As part of the Diversity & Inclusion updates, a new variant of the NPC with their true name appears in place of the original after completing The Feud quest.

13 April 2022
(update)

The NPC's name has been changed from "Ali The barman" to "Ali the Barman".

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • During The Feud, he informs the players that Traitorous Ali has gone to the bathroom, but that "he might be gone a while" because the nearest outhouse is on Fossil Island.
    • Prior to the release of Fossil Island, Ali the Barman instead said "The only problem is that no one has ever actually seen a toilet in RuneScape so he may take a little while."
