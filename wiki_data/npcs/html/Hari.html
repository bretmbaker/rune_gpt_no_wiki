Hari stands at the canoe station in Edgeville. If spoken to, he tells you about a underground passage to a lake in the deep Wilderness and how to make canoes.

Hari will tell players what the best canoe they can currently make at their Woodcutting level is.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Hari resembles the Māori people of New Zealand, evident by the face tattoo and the waka canoe being his people's invention.
