The High Priest of Entrana is one of the most powerful holy men in the Saradominist religion. He lives on the holy island of Entrana, the most influential Saradomin-aligned settlement in all of Gielinor. The priest, who plays some role in Desert Treasure I, a large role in Devious Minds, and minor roles in the Heroes' Quest and Holy Grail quests, can be found in Entrana's chapel.
