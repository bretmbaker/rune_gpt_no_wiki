Financial Seers act as Financial Wizards in Neitiznot, located in the bank. Players can give them ten items that they wish to keep safe; when the 10 items are given to the Financial Seer, they are securely stored in the Safe Deposit Box and cannot be used.

Players cannot store untradeable items. Stackable items such as bank notes, runes, arrows, bolts, etc. will not count as one item. For example, 10 air runes will count as 10 items.
