Rod Fishing spots can be found throughout Gielinor along rivers. They can be fished for raw pike, raw trout, raw salmon or raw rainbow fish depending on the bait and fishing rod you use.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Lure/Bait fishing spots south of Prifddinas are almost always immobile due to a quirk that exists with that specific area:
    • After 2-5 minutes, fishing spots are programmed to move to a designated tile, but will not do so if it is currently occupied by a fishing spot.
    • Whilst in Prifddinas, parts of Tirannwn around the city are also generated, which contains spawns of identical Lure/Bait fishing spots.
    • However, the Lure/Bait spots in the Prifddinas area are programmed to move to the designated tiles in the main world, eventually causing all designated tiles to be occupied shortly after a server restart. This results in the fishing spots there appearing permanently immobile, while in reality the fishing spots cannot move due to all designated tiles being occupied.
