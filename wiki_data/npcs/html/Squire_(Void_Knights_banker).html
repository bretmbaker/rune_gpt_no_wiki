Squires of the Void Knights order can be found at the Void Knights' Outpost. They allow the player to access their banks from the island.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
26 February 2015
(update)

The "Collect" right-click option was added, allowing players to access their offers on the Grand Exchange.
