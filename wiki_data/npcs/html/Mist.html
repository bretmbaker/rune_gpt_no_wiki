League region Morytania✓
ExamineA billowing cloud of fine mist...it looks creepy.
Advanced data
NPC ID4435
LinkMOID

Mist forms while fighting vampyre juvinates, when their health approaches 0. Without the use of the special attack of either the Rod of Ivandis, Ivandis flail or Blisterwood flail and a dose of Guthix balance to finish them, vampyres turn into mist and escape to rejuvenate.
