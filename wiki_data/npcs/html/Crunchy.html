Crunchy is a skeleton found in the Old School Museum. Jagex often used him for promotional material on the RuneScape website. Crunchy is depicted as a skeletal warrior with a metallic helmet, and was accompanied by a human named Tim. The small dragon seen with the two is Tim's pet, Eski.

His most famous role was holding the "High Detail" button before RuneScape HD's release. The two also featured on various other pieces of artwork released by Jagex, as well as on the cover of RuneScape - The Official Handbook.
