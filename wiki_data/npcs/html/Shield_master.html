William, the Shield master, is an archer and craftsman found in the western corner of the Ranging Guild. He introduces players to leather shields, a class of ranged shield.

If asked, he can give players a leather shields flyer, which details the materials needed to create each shield, although speaking to him is not required to craft or equip leather shields.
