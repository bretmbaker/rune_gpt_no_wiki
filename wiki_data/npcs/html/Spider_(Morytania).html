Spiders are found throughout Morytania, notably within the Haunted Woods and inside the Arboretum in Darkmeyer. The ones in the Haunted Woods attempt to follow players, and will summon Feral Vampyres to attack players who get too close.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • This variant of spider was added to the Arboretum to replace the attackable spiders located there on 2 September 2020. However, the developer did not realise this variant of spider summoned Feral Vampyres. It was quickly hotfixed and a non-vampyre summoning variant was added the week after.
