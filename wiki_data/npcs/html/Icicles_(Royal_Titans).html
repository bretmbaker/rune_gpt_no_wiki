Icicles are obstacles conjured by Eldric the Ice King during the fight against the Royal Titans. The wall of icicles will fully form four ticks after first manifesting, blocking the row they appear in. Standing on a targeted tile will deal up to 12 damage to the player, but prevents icicles from forming.

Though the icicles dissipate on their own in time, they can potentially block the player from reaching melee distance with the Titans, or the safespot for their Elemental Blast attack. As such, they must be melted with Fire Blast or greater, which will always melt them regardless of the player's magic attack bonus, and without incurring any attack delays.
