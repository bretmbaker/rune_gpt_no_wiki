Ramarno is an Imcando dwarf found in the Ruins of Camdozaal, accessible after completion of the quest Below Ice Mountain. Ramarno believes that he is the last remaining Imcando dwarf. He requests the player's assistance in gathering Barronite for the Sacred Forge as he supposedly is blind. Players can collect Barronite shards throughout Camdozaal which can be given to Ramarno in exchange for buffs. All five of the permanent buffs can be activated at the same time.

Barronite shards can be stored in the Sacred Forge by the player directly or given to Ramarno himself by using the shards on him. Shards stored in the forge can be used in Ramarno's Shard Exchange or used to access the Camdozaal Vault.

If the player has completed The Knight's Sword quest, they will tell him about Thurgo. Doing so will give Ramarno hope of the future existence of the Imcando race.

If you bring him a redberry pie, he states that he's never tried one before, but enjoys it.

Ramarno will not appear at the Sacred Forge until you speak with him at the entrance of Camdozaal dungeon after completion of Below Ice Mountain.

Exchanging items[edit | edit source]

Players can exchange excess items for Barronite shards:

  • Barronite head - 100 shards
  • Barronite handle - 100 shards
  • Barronite guard - 100 shards
  • Barronite mace - 500 shards
  • Imcando hammer (Broken) - 350 shards
  • Imcando hammer - 500 shards
    • The Imcando hammer (off-hand) is not exchangeable.
