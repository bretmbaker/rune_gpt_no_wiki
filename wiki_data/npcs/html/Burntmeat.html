Burntmeat is a troll featured in Eadgar's Ruse and My Arm's Big Adventure. He can be found in the Troll Stronghold kitchen.

Burntmeat is criticised by other trolls for only being able to cook burnt meat; however, he believes that he is a master chef. Should players help him during a quest, he rewards them with burnt meat, which he claims is very valuable.

Naming convention[edit | edit source]

Most trolls are named after the first thing they eat; Burntmeat, however, was named after the first thing he tried to cook.
