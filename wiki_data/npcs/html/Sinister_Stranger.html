The Sinister Stranger, also known as Vlad, is one of the competitors during the Fishing Contest quest. He claims to not be a vampyre, despite very obviously acting like one.

During the quest, he is using the best fishing spot, which the player must use to win, so the player has to hide a garlic next to him, which causes him to swap places allowing them to win.
