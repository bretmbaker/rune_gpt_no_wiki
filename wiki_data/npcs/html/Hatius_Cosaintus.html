Hatius Cosaintus is located just outside of the Lumbridge Castle. He awards players the Explorer's ring for completing easy, medium, hard, and elite tasks of the Lumbridge Diary.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • His name is a play on the phrase "Hate us cause they ain't us", due to the fact he is wearing very rare and highly coveted equipment.
  • He appears to be wearing a rune cane, briefcase, 3rd age platebody, 3rd age platelegs, 3rd age cloak, ranger boots, top hat & monocle and amulet of fury (or), which have an approximate value of 1,187,279,718.
    • According to Hatius, all of these items were obtained from only three clue scrolls.[1]
  • His ostentatious appearance has been criticised by players as unfitting for his location, which led to the proposal to have him removed in content polls #50 and #78. However, it failed both polls with 73.4% and 54.3% support respectively.
