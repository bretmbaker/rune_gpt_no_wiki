Benjamin is a former Vampyre Juvinate that has been brought back to human form by use of a dose of Guthix balance. He rewards the player's kindness by giving them a variety of noted items. Benjamin's grey skin colour may be due to the lack of sunlight he received during his days as a vampyre.
