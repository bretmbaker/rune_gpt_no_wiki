Bighead is the high priest of the goblins in the Goblin Temple, who follow the Big High War God. During Land of the Goblins, he tests the player's knowledge of the goblin religion and also expands on it afterwards.

Out of respect to the Big High War God, only the High Priest is allowed to use his real name, Bandos. In addition, the name Bandos was only to be used by High Priests on holy days. The rest of the time, he calls him the Big High War God, as the rest of the goblins.
