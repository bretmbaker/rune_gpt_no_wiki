Jorral is a historian located at his Outpost, which is surrounded by aggressive level 64 wolves and level 42 hobgoblins, north of West Ardougne. He starts the Making History quest. During Making History, Jorral enlists the help of the player to save the outpost from King Lathas by researching its past. After the player saves the outpost, Jorral turns it into a small museum.

He also features in The Slug Menace quest, where the player takes a transcript of the runes to take him for translating which leads him to describing Mother Mallum's history.

The quickest way to get to Jorral is by using the Necklace of passage's outpost option.

During a master clue: "Buried beneath the ground, who knows where it's found. Lucky for you, A man called Jorral may have a clue." He will give you a strange device.
