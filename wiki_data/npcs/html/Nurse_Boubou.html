Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Her name is a pun on "boo-boo", a minor injury, and her examine text is a reference to the tradition in which parents kiss abrasions or other boo-boos sustained by their children as a placebic method of lessening pain - "kissing a boo-boo makes everything better."
