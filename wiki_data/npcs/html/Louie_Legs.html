Louie Legs is a shopkeeper who owns Louie's Armoured Legs Bazaar located in the south-eastern corner of Al Kharid. Louie sells platelegs from bronze to adamant in his shop. If you bring him one acorn and one set of bones, he will give you the corrupted platelegs and skirt.
