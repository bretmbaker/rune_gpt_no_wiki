Sol Heredit is the final boss in the Fortis Colosseum, and is currently the strongest monster in Old School RuneScape (barring monsters in seasonal events such as Leagues) with a combat level of 1,563.

Upon reaching wave 12 of the Colosseum, he will address the player and begin the fight after jumping off of his throne. During this wave, the Colosseum arena is reduced to the area between the four pillars, bordered by NPCs wielding shields.

History[edit | edit source]

According to Mod Ed, Sol Heredit intentionally does not have any backstory in-game. Instead, it has been opted that players would think up the lore surrounding the final boss of the Colosseum.[1]

Heredit would oversee the Fortis Colosseum, watching challengers battle in the stadium for glory and fame, though he appeared to have considered them insignificant. When a particular adventurer defeated all of the combatants of the Colosseum, Heredit became intrigued, claiming that there was finally a worthy challenger and decided to challenge them himself. Despite the numerous abilities at his disposal, the challenger also defeated him. Heredit's son would consider his defeat to them a humiliation.[2]

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The name "Sol Heredit" comes from the Latin words sōl ("sun") and hērēditō ("to inherit"), loosely translating as "Heir of the Sun".
