Prissy Scilla is a gardener who looks after the tree patch at the Tree Gnome Stronghold. He is a member of the Group of Advanced Gardeners. Prissy will chop down a planted tree for a fee of 200 coins.

Since he is member of G.A.G., he can be one of the 5 gardeners players must speak to in the Fairytale I - Growing Pains quest.

Paying him 25 coconuts to protect a magic tree is a requirement for the elite Western Provinces Diary.
