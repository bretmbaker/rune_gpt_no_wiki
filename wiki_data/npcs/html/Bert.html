Bert is a worker found in Yanille. He's a main figure during The Hand in the Sand quest.

After the quest, and once per day, the player is able to ask him (talk or right-click option) for a free delivery of 84 buckets of sand directly to their bank. After completion of the elite Ardougne Diary, Bert automatically sends 84 buckets of sand to the player's bank every day they log in, including if membership ends. This can be toggled off by speaking to him.

Claiming the first delivery will complete a medium Ardougne Diary task. (Ultimate ironmen are instead tasked to fill a bucket at Bert's sand pit).

Ultimate ironmen are excluded from having any sand handed to them, due to the nature of the game mode.

He can be contacted using the NPC Contact spell in the Lunar spellbook.
