The Costumer is an NPC the player encounters during the Death on the Isle. She assists the player with the murder investigation by answering a few questions.

She is located in the wine cellar under Villa Lucens on Aldarin.

After completing the quest, players can speak to her to obtain the costume needle, which acts as both a needle and an unlimited source of thread.
