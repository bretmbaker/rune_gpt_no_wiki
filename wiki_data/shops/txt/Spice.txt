Spiceis an item used inCooking. It can be bought from theCulinaromancer's Chestafter completing the beginning portion ofRecipe for Disasteror fromspice stallsinArdougne,Civitas illa Fortis, andPrifddinas. It can also be stolen from those stalls with level 65Thievingor looted from aGourmet implingorBaby impling. Though it comes in a shaker, it is a single-use item.

You need spice in the goblin sub-quest ofRecipe for Disaster, as well as for theDesert Treasure Iquest.

Spice can be used to makeCurryin place of 3Curry leaves.
