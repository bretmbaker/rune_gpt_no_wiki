Theblack kiteshieldis aBlack Knightkiteshieldthat requires 10Defenceto wield. The black kiteshield has the same defensive bonuses as awhite kiteshield, but without the +1prayerbonus.

The black kiteshield can be found in trimmed and heraldic forms fromTreasure Trails.

As with allblack items, it can not be made with theSmithingskill.
