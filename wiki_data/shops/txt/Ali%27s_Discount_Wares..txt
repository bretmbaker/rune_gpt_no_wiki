Ali's Discount Waresis ashopowned by the swindlerAli Morrisane. It sells similaritemsas ageneral store, as well as desert survival gear. After completion ofRogue Trader, other items can be bought from the store.

It is worth noting that the rune shop prices do not fluctuate. ManyIronmenwill find themselves with an excessive amount ofbloodandsoul runeswhich sell for200and150respectively (50% of the item value).
