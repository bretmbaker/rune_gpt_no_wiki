TheAdamant plateskirtis a plateskirt made out ofadamant. At least 30Defenceis required to wear it. The plateskirt has the same stats as theAdamant platelegs, but are 1kg lighter.

They can be made at level 86Smithingusing 3adamantite bars, which grants 187.5 experience.
