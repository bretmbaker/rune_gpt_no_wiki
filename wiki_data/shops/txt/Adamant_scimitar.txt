Theadamant scimitaris the fourth strongestscimitarin the game, behindrune,gildedanddragon. Like mostadamant weapons, it requires30Attackto wield.

Players can make it with75Smithingusing twoadamantite bars, earning 125experience. The scimitar can also be obtained from killingmonsters, and is a reward from the questThe Feud.

Smithing an adamant scimitar in theResource Areais a task for theWilderness Hard Diary.
