TheHeroes' Guildis located just south ofBurthorpe. The guild is only accessible tomemberswho have completed theHeroes' Quest.Dragon macesanddragon battleaxescan be purchased here, andamulets of glorycan be recharged at thefountain.

Getting there[edit|edit source]

ThePortal of Heroeson the2ndfloor[UK]3rdfloor[US]of theMyths' Guildcan be used to teleport outside the front door of the Heroes' Guild.TheTeleport to Housespell (if theplayer-owned houseis in Taverley), or a redirectedTaverley teleporttablet.Agames necklaceor thegrouping teleportto theBurthorpe Games Room.Acombat braceletcan be used to teleport to theWarriors' Guild, north of the Heroes' Guild.

Basement[edit|edit source]

The Heroes' Guild basement is accessed via a ladder on theground floor[UK]1stfloor[US]and is a miniature dungeon. It contains 2 monsters - 5giant bats, as well as a cagedblue dragonthat is good for trainingRanged,Magic, or evenMeleewith ahalberd.
TheHeroes' Guild mineis in the basement, too, with several rocks for higher levelminers. There are:

11coal rocks2mithril rocks2adamantite rocks2runite rocks

These are all arranged along the cavernous walls to the east side of the dungeon.

TheFountain of Heroesis in a small side passage off the dungeon. If anamulet of gloryis dipped in the fountain, it will be recharged to four teleports.

The fountain used to be located on the ground floor, but when theBurthorpe Games Roomwas introduced along with its associated teleport via thegames necklace, it was moved downstairs to the basement. A shortcut is available to those with 67 Agility and who have completed the hardFalador Diary, which leads directly from the basement entrance to the Fountain of Heroes.
