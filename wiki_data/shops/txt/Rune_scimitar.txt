Therune scimitaris ascimitarrequiring level 40Attackto wield. It can be created with 2runite barsat level 90Smithing, or dropped by various monsters such asZamorak warriorsorFire giants.

It is the second strongest scimitar available behind theDragon scimitar, and the strongest scimitar infree-to-play. Furthermore, it is considered the strongestmelee weaponin most situations in free-to-play due to its unmatched attack and strength bonuses for its attack speed.

Ironmenmay prefer to create abarronite maceor purchase arune swordfrom theChampions' Guild(requiring 32Quest points).Members-only alternatives to the rune scimitar include therapier(afterCabin Fever),brine sabre, andancient staff(afterDesert Treasure I).

Players can attach aGuthix,SaradominorZamorak ornament kit, obtained frombeginnerTreasure Trails, to create aGuthix,SaradominandZamorak rune scimitar, respectively. This only alters its appearance, and gives no stat enhancements, though the Saradomin and Zamorak variants will provide protection from the respective factions in theGod Wars Dungeon. It can be dismantled at any time, returning the scimitar and ornament kit.
