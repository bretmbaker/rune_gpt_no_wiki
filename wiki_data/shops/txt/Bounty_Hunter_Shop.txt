Location



TheBounty Hunter Storeis a store run by theEmblem Trader, located inDaimon's Crater. Here, players can purchase items with their accumulated points obtained from playingBounty Hunter.

Historically, it was a store run by the Emblem Trader inEdgeville, near thebank. Onmembers' worlds, the Emblem Trader now claims he hurt his back liftingbounty cratesand can't do it anymore, thus retiring his shop.

Stock (Daimon's Crater)[edit|edit source]

All purchases are made in Bounty Hunter Points. The majority of the rewards are untradeable, with the exception ofBlightedsupplies. Many of the weapons and equipment can ONLY be used within the minigame. Note that Ancient Warrior Armour can only be sold back to the BH shop if uncharged, in the as-bought form (not corrupted).

Combat experience[edit|edit source]

Players can also purchase combat experience from the shop, giving a varying amount of XP based on the player's level, which is granted upon closing the shop interface.

Purchasing XP in bulk will grant bonus XP; the player will gain 1% additional XP for a purchase of 10-99, and 10% additional XP for a purchase of 100 or more (ex. from choosing the "Buy-50" option twice).

If a player's target kills are greater than their rogue kills, theHunter's honourwill be available. Players can switch the hat's appearance by having a greater amount of kills in either rogue or target kills.
