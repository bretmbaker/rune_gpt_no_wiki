[Shop: Bow]

Bowsareranged weapons. Most bows can be crafted using theFletchingskill. Most bows requirearrowsto shoot. There are three different kinds of bows:shortbows,longbows, andcomposite bows. Shortbows attack faster but have a shorterattack rangethan longbows. Longbows have longer range but have a slowerattack speed. Composite bows have the same attack range as longbows, but fire slightly faster and have slightly higher accuracy.
