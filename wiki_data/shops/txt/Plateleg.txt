Platelegs







Slot | Legs slot

Type | Melee



Platelegsare a type ofarmourworn mainly bywarriorsin the leg slot. Amongmeleeattack types, platelegs provides good defence against the slash attack type but is more vulnerable to the crush attack type. Platelegs gives a player penalties towards ranged attacks and penalties towards magic defence. However, platelegs have very good defence against ranged attacks.

Platelegs are made with theSmithingskill or purchased at the plate legs shop identified by theicon on themap. One such location isLouie's Armoured Legs Bazaarin Al Kharid.Horvik's Armour Shopoffers iron platelegs.

They are more popular and consequently more expensive than the plateskirt, despite having identical stats and weighing 1 kg more, and they are not gender-locked, (i.e. male characters can wear plateskirts and female characters can wear platelegs).
