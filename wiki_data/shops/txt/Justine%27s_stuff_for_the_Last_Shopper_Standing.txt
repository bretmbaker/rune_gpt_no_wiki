Unlockable rewards[edit|edit source]

The only unlockable rewards areanimation overridesfor theGodswordspecial attack, changing the colour to gold, each override costs 75 Last Man Standing points.

Once unlocked, the gold trim can be toggled at the shop whenever the player desires.
