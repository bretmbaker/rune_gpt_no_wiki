TheRellekka Longhall Baris a bar found in theRellekkalonghall run byThora the Barkeep. It sellsbeerby the glass,tankard, orkeg. Some NPCs located in the bar are the focus of thequestThe Fremennik Trials, in which the player must enlist the support ofManni the Revellerand begin the quest by talking toBrundt the Chieftain.

The stage of this bar is used by players during The Fremennik Trials, where they play an instrument for progression of the quest.
