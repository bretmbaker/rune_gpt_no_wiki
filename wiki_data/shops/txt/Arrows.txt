Arrowsare standard rangedammunitionused bybows, weapons used inrangedcombat. They can be created bymembersthroughFletching, by fletchingarrow shaftsout ofnormal logs, and attachingfeathersand metalarrowheadsto them. Arrows can only be fired from bows that are strong enough to use them.

Arrows can be bought fromarchery shopsowned by NPCs inVarrock(Lowe's Archery Emporium),Rimmington(Brian's Archery Supplies),Catherby(Hickton's Archery Emporium), and theRanging Guild(level 40 Ranged).

There is a 20% chance arrows will be lost when shot. If anAva's deviceis worn, most arrows will not be consumed and they will remain in theammunition slot, instead of being dropped on the ground. Arrows that fail to be retrieved will eventually de-spawn, or may be picked up by other players.
