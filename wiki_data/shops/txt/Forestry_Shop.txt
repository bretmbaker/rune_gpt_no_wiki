TheForestry Shopis the shop forForestryrewards, run by theFriendly ForesterinDraynor Village,Seers' VillageandPrifddinas, where players can purchase several unique rewards with theiranima-infused barkand logs. Players (excluding Ultimate Ironmen) can sell thecape pouch,funky-shaped log, andlog basketback to the shop for 80% of their cost; this includes a refund of both anima bark and logs. Players may also sell duplicate pheasant feathers and sturdy beehive parts.

Ultimate ironmencan make use of a log storage option at theFriendly Foresterthat will permanently store logs exclusively for use in the Forestry Shop, i.e. they may not be withdrawn once deposited. Logs deposited in this manner can be used to substitute noted logs in the Forestry Shop.
