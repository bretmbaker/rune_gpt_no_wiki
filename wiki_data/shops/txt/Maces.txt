Macesare meleeweapons, and one of the few weapons which haveCrushfor a primaryattack type. Maces also have a controlled attack style, giving equal experience toAttack,Strength, andDefence.

Maces are special in that they provide aPrayerbonus, a trait not found among any of the common weapons.

Trivia[edit|edit source]

The mace prayer bonus may be an allusion tosine effusione sanguinis,a possibly apocryphal notion that Medieval Christians and the clergy wielded maces and other blunt weapons to avoid shedding blood, in conflicts such as the Crusades.
