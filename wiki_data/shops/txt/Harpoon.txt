Aharpoonis anitemused in theFishingskill. It can first be used at level 35 Fishing to catchtuna. Then, at level 50, you will begin to catch an array of tuna andswordfish. Eventually at level 76, you can harpoonsharks. Swordfish and tuna are caught in Cage/Harpoonfishing spotswhile sharks are caught in Net/Harpoon spots. It can be stored in atackle box.

Forpay-to-playplayers, wieldable alternatives, specifically thebarb-tail harpoonanddragon harpoon, can be used. Additionally, players can learnBarbarian fishingto do barehanded Fishing (48 Fishing, 15Agility, 15Strengthto start/55 Fishing, 35 Strength to complete).
