Outer Fortisis a part ofCivitas illa Fortisfound outside the main walls. The local population, mostly being farmers, use the area to grow crops to support the city's population.

Features[edit|edit source]

Farming[edit|edit source]

Outer Fortis has a lot of farms and as such players can gather several crops here. There are fields withonions,wheat,cabbages, and lastlysweetcornwhich is unique to Outer Fortis.

It's also possible to picknettlesin several of these fields. One of the farms has achickencoop, and a windmill for making wheat into flour is located on the west side of Outer Fortis.
