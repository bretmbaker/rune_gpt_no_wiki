Platebody







Slot | Body slot

Type | Melee



Aplatebodyis a type ofarmourworn mainly bywarriors. Amongmeleeattack types, platebody provides good defence against the slash attack type but is more vulnerable to the crush attack type.

Most platebodies give players penalties towardsmagicandrangedattacks and penalties towardsmagicdefence. However, platebodies have very good defence against ranged as well as melee attacks.

Platebodies are made with theSmithingskill or purchased atplatebody shops, which is identified by theicon on the map. Most platebodies will interfere withAva's accumulatorandAva's attractor.
