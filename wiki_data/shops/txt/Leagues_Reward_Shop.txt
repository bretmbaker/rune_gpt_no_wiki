TheLeagues Reward Shopis a store run by theLeagues TutorinLumbridge Castlecourtyard. They sell rewards fromOld School RuneScape Leaguesin exchange for points earned within the Leagues onmembers' worlds. The shop owner and location changes depending on the most recent League held.
