Blood runesare one of therunesused to cast spells in theMagicskill. They are used in some of the highest level spells, the lowest of which isRuby Crossbow Bolt Enchant, and the highest isHeal Group. Blood runes are also used in charging theScythe of Vitur,Sanguinesti staff, andsoul bearer.

Runecraft info[edit|edit source]

Crafting blood runes at one of the two availablerunic altarsrequires 77Runecraft. ABlood essencecan be activated to provide a 50% chance per essence or fragment crafted to produce an extra blood rune. It is consumed and will disappear after 1,000 extra blood runes are crafted.

Thefalse Blood Altarnorth ofArceuushas no quest requirements to use and does not require any items to access it, unlike the true blood altar. Eachdark essence fragmentyields 23.8 Runecraft experience, not including the 0.625 Runecraft experience from binding the dense essence. Eachdark essence blockyields 95.2 Runecraft experience and an additional 2.5 experience for binding it at thedark altar. It is possible to reach approximately 38,000 experience per hour crafting blood runes in Arceuus. Using the fullgraceful outfitand having 78+Agilityshould be enough to have enough run energy to last the whole trip, depending on how often thedense runestonedepletes. With anachievement diary cape, theAbysscan be used by teleporting toLesser Fanatic, to achieve slightly higher experience rates of approximately 45,000 per hour at peak efficiency, although it involves a slight risk of being attacked by other players.

Alternatively, thetrue Blood AltarinMeiyerditch Laboratoriesrequires completion ofSins of the Father, where eachpure essenceused yields 10.5 Runecraftexperience. The quest is also required to use theGuardian of Bloodin theGuardians of the Riftminigamefor crafting blood runes withguardian essence. If not entering through Guardians of the Rift, ablood tiara,blood talisman,catalytic talisman,catalytic tiara, orRunecraft capeis required to enter the altar. Using the abyss to craft blood runes in Meiyerditch Laboratories provides approximately double the amount of runes crafted per hour compared to crafting at Arceuus, with only slightly more experience due to the difference in experience per essence, though Arceuus requires less actions and focus, and is less dangerous due to not needing to enter the Wilderness, while also providing a small amount of Mining and Crafting experience.

Blood runes can also be crafted at theOurania altarbelow level 77 Runecraft, however they are received relatively rarely.
