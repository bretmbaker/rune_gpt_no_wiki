Raw potatoesare afooditem that can be picked from fields aroundGielinoror grown through theFarmingskillat level 1 withpotato seeds. They heal 1hitpointwhen eaten raw. When picking potatoes from fields,membersmay randomly receive 1 potato seed.

They are used in the following recipes:

Baked potato(must be cooked on arange, not afire)Veg ball(Gnome cooking)StewFish pie (admiralandregular, whichtemporarily boostFishinglevel by 5 and 3, respectively)

Raw potatoes are purchasable from various stores for1coineach.Hudoat theGrand Treehas a stock of 10;Cobado's Groceriesa stock of 10;FrenitainYanillea stock of 5; andWydininPort Sarima stock of 1, to name a few.

Members can store up to 10 potatoes in anempty sack, creating asack of potatoes. 1 sack of potatoes is needed to protect growingonions. Adding potatoes to acompost binmakes regularcompost.

Obtaining in bulk[edit|edit source]

Memberscan obtain a lot of potatoes in a relatively short time, without waiting for farming patches, by taking plenty ofempty sacksto any potato field and filling them to create asack of potatoes. Another method for members is to pick potatoes at the potato field north of theLumbridge cow fieldand note them at thetool leprechaunby thehops patchto the west.Ironmenlooking to stock up on large numbers very quickly to maketuna potatoeswill findSigmund the MerchantinRellekkato their liking, depositing withPeer the Seera short distance away.
