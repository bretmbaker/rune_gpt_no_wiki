Two-handed swordsor2h swordsare powerful yet incredibly slow weapons. Their slow attack speed is made up for by the high damage they can deal in a single attack. The use of arune 2h swordinplayer killingonfree-to-playworlds as a finishing weapon is common.

Two-handed swords offerslashandcrushattack stylesin which they provide high bonuses in compensation for their slow speed. Players should choose the primary slash against most opponents, and the secondary crush against opponents wearing plate armour, such asplatebodyandplatelegs.

Godswordshave an attack speed of 6, compared to an attack speed of 7 on other two-handed swords. TheSaradomin swordhas an attack speed of 4, matching that of aswordorscimitar.
