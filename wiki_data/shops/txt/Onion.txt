Onionsare afooditem that can be picked from fields aroundGielinoror grown through theFarmingskillat level 5 withonion seeds. They heal 1hitpointwhen eaten. When eaten, the messageYou eat the onion.is displayed. If eaten at full health, a message will appear saying,It's always sad to see a grown [man/woman/person] cry.. If eaten below full health, the messageIt makes your eyes water, but it does heal some health.is displayed instead. When picking onions from fields,membersmay randomly receive 1 onion seed.

They are used in the following recipes:

Mushroom potatoUgthanki kebabVegetable batta,veg ball,worm hole(Gnome cooking)Garden pie(temporarily boostsFarming level by 3)

Onions are purchasable from various stores for3coinseach.Hudoat theGrand Treehas a stock of 10;Cobado's Groceriesa stock of 10; and theGreengrocersofMiscellaniaa stock of 10, to name a few.

Unlimited onions can be taken fromteak lardersinside aplayer-owned house. They can also be gathered from theTheatre of Bloodchestif the player died during certain fights. An onion spawns on the1stfloor[UK]2ndfloor[US]of theLumbridge General Store.

Members can store up to 10 onions in anempty sack, creating asack of onions. 1 sack of onions is needed to protect growingcabbagesandAsgarnian hops. Adding onions to acompost binmakes regularcompost.

Two onions are used in creatingyellow dyeby taking them to eitherAggieorAli the dyer.
