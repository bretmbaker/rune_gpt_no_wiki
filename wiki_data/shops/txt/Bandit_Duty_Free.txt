Location



Bandit Duty Free, owned byNoterazzo, is ageneral storelocated in theWilderness Bandit Camp. It is one of four to buy items at a higher cost than the normal general store cost, the other three beingMartin Thwait's Lost and Found,West Ardougne General Store, andPollnivneach General Store; on empty stock, both the West Ardougne General Store and the Pollnivneach General Store will buy at 55% ofitem valuewhile the other two buy at 60%, the same asHigh Level Alchemyvalue.

If players askNoterazzoabout his store buying at high prices, he will explain that he can do so because the tax collector does not come to his store due to its dangerous location.

Warning:As the lowest price this store buys items for is identical to every other shop, it is not recommended to sell items in bulk at this location due to the risk of getting attacked.
