The black kiteshield is a Black Knight kiteshield that requires 10 Defence to wield. The black kiteshield has the same defensive bonuses as a white kiteshield , but without the +1 prayer bonus.

The black kiteshield can be found in trimmed and heraldic forms from Treasure Trails .

As with all black items , it can not be made with the Smithing skill.
