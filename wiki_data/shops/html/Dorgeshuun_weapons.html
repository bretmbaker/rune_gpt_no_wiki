Bone weapons
This article is about the bone weapons used by the Dorgeshuun. For the ratbane weapons, see Rat bone weapons .

Dorgeshuun weapons are weapons made of bone, used by Dorgeshuun tribe. Most of them may be purchased from Nardok in Dorgeshuun Mines Nardok's Bone Weapons shop after completing The Lost Tribe quest .

Weapons [ edit | edit source ]

  • Bone dagger
  • Bone spear
  • Bone club
  • Dorgeshuun crossbow (uses Bone bolts as ammo)
