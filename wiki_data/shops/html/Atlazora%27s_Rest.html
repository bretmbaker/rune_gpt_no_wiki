Atlazora's Rest is a bar found in Outer Fortis . During Twilight's Promise , players must visit Atlazora's Rest to find two off-duty knights , Velam and Azali.

Despite the upper floor of the pub being inaccessible, a bronze dagger and broken glass spawn can be seen.
