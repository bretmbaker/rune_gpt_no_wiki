The Fancy Clothes Store is a shop in south-eastern Varrock . It is owned and run by Asyff . The shop sells needles and thread and different kinds of clothes such as gloves , boots , capes and hats . The store is also one of the places where players can obtain hunter clothing , through the Custom Fur Clothing panel, some of which increases one's Thieving abilities or provides weight reduction.

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • When the Fancy Clothes Store was first released, it sold Red partyhats , although players could not buy them since the stock was always 0. This was removed 3 weeks later on 13 November 2002 .
