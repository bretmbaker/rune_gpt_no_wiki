Axes are a weapon and a tool necessary for Woodcutting . It is classified as a melee weapon, available in all varieties of metal, although its power and speed are less favourable than most others. Alternatively, they can be used to slash webs.

Higher-level axes have a greater chance of obtaining logs from a tree and thus are generally faster to woodcut with. However, certain low-level trees become attainable at maximum speed at higher woodcutting levels, even with lower-level axes.

Uses [ edit | edit source ]

Achievement Diary [ edit | edit source ]

  • Varrock Diary (Easy) : Chop down a dying tree in the Lumber Yard .

Forestry [ edit | edit source ]

  • Felling axes used with Forester's rations in their inventory or inside their Forestry kit offer 10% more Woodcutting experience than regular axes while also having a 20% chance to not obtain a log.

Quests [ edit | edit source ]

  • Grim Tales : 1 × any axe to chop down the beanstalk
  • Animal Magnetism : 1 × mithril axe needed to obtain the blessed axe
  • Lost City : 1 × any axe to chop a tree where Shamus is hiding
  • Legends' Quest : 1 × any axe to chop trees whilst navigating through the Kharazi Jungle and cutting/shaping the adult yommi tree into a yommi totem
  • Olaf's Quest : 1 × any axe to chop down the windswept tree to obtain dry wood
  • A Kingdom Divided : 1 × any axe to chop down vines whilst inside the Forthos Ruins
  • Lunar Diplomacy : 1 × any axe to chop down dream trees whilst in the Dream World
  • Sins of the Father : 1 × any axe to chop down trees at Canifis to fix the broken bridge and to cut down the Blisterwood tree
  • Song of the Elves : 1 × any axe to chop down the dead tree to free Lord Piquan Crwys

Relative Chance for Standard Axes [ edit | edit source ]

The table below compares the standard axes' chance to obtain a log, relatively speaking, and be read by going across first and then down.

For example: the chance to obtain a log using an adamant axe (across) is 150% that of a steel axe (down), or 50% higher.

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • Broken axes , before being removed after an update, were able to be wielded. But once unequipped, they could no longer be equipped by the player. They possessed the same bonuses as their unbroken counterparts.
