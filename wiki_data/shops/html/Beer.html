Beer is a type of ale that restores 1 Hitpoint and temporarily boosts Strength by 2% + 1, while draining Attack by 6% + 1. Unlike other ales, it cannot be created through brewing .

Barrels [ edit | edit source ]

Main article: Barrel (beer)

Throughout the game, there are barrels of beer from which the player can fill an empty beer glass . To fill an empty glass, the player must use it on the barrel, after which it will be replaced with beer. This interaction is only possible with a subset of barrels in the game. These barrels are visually distinguished by the presence of a tap that the player presumably draws the beer from. Note that while all such barrels have this distinguishing tap, not all barrels with a tap yield beer. Below is a list of known locations for these barrels.

  • Behind the bar at The Toad and Chicken
  • Edgeville Dungeon near the hobgoblins
  • Both boats in Corsair Cove
  • The boat to Corsair Cove from Rimmington
  • Two houses in Draynor Village
  • The kitchen of a Player-owned house if the owner has constructed a Beer barrel
  • The castle wall room where you find Perdu in Lumbridge
