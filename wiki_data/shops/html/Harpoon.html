A harpoon is an item used in the Fishing skill . It can first be used at level 35 Fishing to catch tuna . Then, at level 50, you will begin to catch an array of tuna and swordfish . Eventually at level 76, you can harpoon sharks . Swordfish and tuna are caught in Cage/Harpoon fishing spots while sharks are caught in Net/Harpoon spots. It can be stored in a tackle box .

For pay-to-play players, wieldable alternatives, specifically the barb-tail harpoon and dragon harpoon , can be used. Additionally, players can learn Barbarian fishing to do barehanded Fishing (48 Fishing, 15 Agility , 15 Strength to start/55 Fishing, 35 Strength to complete).
