Silk can be bought from various silk traders around Gielinor .

Silk can be stolen from silk stalls with a Thieving level of 20, yielding 24 experience . The Silk merchant who runs the silk stall in Ardougne will buy silk from the player for as much as 60 coins through his dialogue, if the player first offers to sell them for 120 coins each.

Achievement Diary [ edit | edit source ]

  • Ardougne Diary (Easy) : Sell silk to the Silk merchant in East Ardougne for 60 coins each.

Quests [ edit | edit source ]

  • Dragon Slayer I : 1 × silk to open magic door
  • Ghosts Ahoy : 1 × silk to make model ship with flag
  • Mourning's End Part I : 2 × silk to repair mourner trousers
  • Enlightened Journey : 10 × silk to make the Hot Air Balloon
  • Cold War : 1 × silk to make the clockwork suit
  • Song of the Elves : 1 × silk to make Ardougne knight tabard
