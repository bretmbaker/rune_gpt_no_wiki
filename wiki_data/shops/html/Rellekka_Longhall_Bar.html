The Rellekka Longhall Bar is a bar found in the Rellekka longhall run by Thora the Barkeep . It sells beer by the glass, tankard , or keg . Some NPCs located in the bar are the focus of the quest The Fremennik Trials , in which the player must enlist the support of Manni the Reveller and begin the quest by talking to Brundt the Chieftain .

The stage of this bar is used by players during The Fremennik Trials, where they play an instrument for progression of the quest.
