Crossbow limbs

Crossbow limbs are members -only items used to make crossbows . They can be smithed, but most can also be purchased at the crossbow shops in Keldagrim , White Wolf Mountain , and the Dwarven Mines . The experience and Smithing level needed to make them are as follows:

Smithing table [ edit | edit source ]

Level Item Materials Experience
6 Bronze 12.5
13 Blurite 17
23 Iron 25
36 Steel 37.5
56 Mithril 50
76 Adamantite 62.5
91 Runite 75
