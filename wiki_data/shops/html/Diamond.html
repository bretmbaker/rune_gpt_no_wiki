Diamonds are precious gems used mainly in Crafting . They can be cut from uncut diamonds with a chisel at level 43 Crafting, yielding 107.5 experience .

A diamond is required to enter the Zanaris marketplace.

Magic [ edit | edit source ]

Diamond jewellery can be enchanted using Lvl-4 Enchant , requiring level 57 Magic . The spell requires 1 cosmic rune , 10 earth runes and the piece of diamond jewellery that is to be enchanted.

Changes [ edit | edit source ]

Date Changes
Date unknown

Gems available on free-to-play worlds can no longer be crushed with a hammer to receive a members object ( crushed gem ).

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • Diamond was used as one of the tiers of post count badges on the official Runescape Forums . Players needed to have made 250,000 posts to be given the diamond badge.
