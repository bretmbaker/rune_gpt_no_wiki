The Forestry Shop is the shop for Forestry rewards, run by the Friendly Forester in Draynor Village , Seers' Village and Prifddinas , where players can purchase several unique rewards with their anima-infused bark and logs. Players (excluding Ultimate Ironmen) can sell the cape pouch , funky-shaped log , and log basket back to the shop for 80% of their cost; this includes a refund of both anima bark and logs. Players may also sell duplicate pheasant feathers and sturdy beehive parts.

Ultimate ironmen can make use of a log storage option at the Friendly Forester that will permanently store logs exclusively for use in the Forestry Shop, i.e. they may not be withdrawn once deposited. Logs deposited in this manner can be used to substitute noted logs in the Forestry Shop.

Changes [ edit | edit source ]

Date Changes
5 February 2025
( update )

The golden pheasant egg and fox whistle have been added to the shop.

14 February 2024
( update )

Twitcher's gloves are no longer a stackable, consumable single-use item, and are now a permanent item. To compensate, the price has been increased twenty-fold.

1 November 2023
( update )

The description of twitcher's gloves in the shop have been updated to state that they are a stackable, single-use item.

12 July 2023
( update )
  • Ultimate Ironmen may now deposit logs via the Friendly Forester for purposes of spending it in the Forestry Shop; they cannot be withdrawn.
  • Selling back items has been disabled for Ultimate Ironmen.
  • The description for the funky-shaped log was changed for better readability.
5 July 2023
( update )
  • The forestry kit can now be obtained for free from the Forestry Shop.
  • The only items that can now be sold back to the Forestry Shop are the funky-shaped log and the log basket .
29 June 2023
Hotfix

Players can no longer sell items back to the Forestry Shop.
