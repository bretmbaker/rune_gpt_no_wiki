Frankie's Fishing Emporium is a food shop run by Frankie in the south-east corner of Port Piscarilius which sells raw fish .

The shop sells a wide variety of fish in large quantities, making the shop useful for Ironmen wanting to train Cooking or to quickly stock up on food. It will also buy raw fish for a higher price than other shops, making it also useful for getting rid of unwanted fish. However, non-Iron accounts will get a lot more money by selling raw fish on the Grand Exchange.
