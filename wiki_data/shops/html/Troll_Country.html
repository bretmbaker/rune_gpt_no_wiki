The Troll Country is a region in north Gielinor . It is a mountainous kingdom controlled mainly by trolls .

Locations [ edit | edit source ]

  • Death Plateau – A dangerous plateau in the south part of the kingdom.
  • God Wars Dungeon – Where the armies of various gods fight.
  • Ice Path – A cold and deadly path up a mountain.
  • Trollheim – The largest mountain in Gielinor.
  • Trollweiss Mountain – Another large mountain in the north-west part of the kingdom.
  • Troll Stronghold – A large mountain troll settlement.
  • Weiss – An ice troll settlement.
  • Ghorrock - An abandoned Zarosian fortress.
  • Zemouregal's Fort - A fort belonging to Zemouregal .

Hunting [ edit | edit source ]

The Trollweiss hunting area is to the west of the kingdom and is the only hunting area entitled with "ice" or "snow". It is home to various huntable creatures.
