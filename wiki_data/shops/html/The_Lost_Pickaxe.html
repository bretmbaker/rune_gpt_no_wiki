The Lost Pickaxe is a bar found on the north west part of Cam Torum . The bar sells steamforge brews and sun-shine not found in bars on the mainland.

The bar has several Pickaxes of the Lost mounted on the walls, presumably belonging to regular patrons of the bar who have deceased.

All of the items sold are of infinite stock.
