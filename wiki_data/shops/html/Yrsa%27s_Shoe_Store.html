Yrsa's Shoe Store was a shop in Rellekka . As well as selling clothes , Yrsa could change your shoe colour and style free of charge. Both access to the store's stock and the ability to change one's shoes required the completion of The Fremennik Trials quest . Visiting the store was a part of the easy Fremennik Diary .

The shop has since been superseded by the makeover interface, also offered by Yrsa.

Changes [ edit | edit source ]

Date Changes
14 August 2024
( update )

The interface is no longer available.

9 November 2022
( update )

Changing the player's default boots at Yrsa's Shoe Store was made free of charge as part of the Diversity & Inclusion updates .

9 March 2022
( update )

Yrsa's Shoe Store was updated to allow players to select their foot shape. The option was previously only available upon initial account creation.

19 September 2013
( update )

Thessalia, the hairdresser, the Makeover Mage and Yrsa now have right-click options for accessing their makeover services.

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • Before the shop's removal and the introduction of the makeover interface, boots were the only piece of standard clothing that could not be changed on a free-to-play world, due to the store's location.
