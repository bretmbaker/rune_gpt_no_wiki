The adamant scimitar is the fourth strongest scimitar in the game, behind rune , gilded and dragon . Like most adamant weapons , it requires 30 Attack to wield.

Players can make it with 75 Smithing using two adamantite bars , earning 125 experience . The scimitar can also be obtained from killing monsters , and is a reward from the quest The Feud .

Smithing an adamant scimitar in the Resource Area is a task for the Wilderness Hard Diary .
