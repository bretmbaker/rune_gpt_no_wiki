Bows are ranged weapons . Most bows can be crafted using the Fletching skill. Most bows require arrows to shoot. There are three different kinds of bows: shortbows , longbows , and composite bows . Shortbows attack faster but have a shorter attack range than longbows. Longbows have longer range but have a slower attack speed . Composite bows have the same attack range as longbows, but fire slightly faster and have slightly higher accuracy.
