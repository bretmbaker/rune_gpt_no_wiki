Construction supplies is a Construction supplies store found at the Sawmill north-east of Varrock , the Woodcutting Guild , and Prifddinas . It is run by the Sawmill operator at the Varrock sawmill and the Woodcutting guild, and by Sawmill Operator in Prifddinas. The shop stocks several items that can be used for creating furniture, and is the only place to get bolts of cloth without buying them from other players.
