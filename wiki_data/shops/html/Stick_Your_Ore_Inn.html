The Stick Your Ore Inn is a bar found in Mistrock , on the southern side of the island of Aldarin . The bar is run by a bartender . The bar sells steamforge brews and sun-shine not found in bars on the mainland.

All of the items sold are of infinite stock.

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • The name of the bar is a reference to the phrase "Stick your oar in" which means soliciting unwanted advice or opinions.
