Arrows Arrows

Arrows are standard ranged ammunition used by bows , weapons used in ranged combat . They can be created by members through Fletching , by fletching arrow shafts out of normal logs , and attaching feathers and metal arrowheads to them. Arrows can only be fired from bows that are strong enough to use them.

Arrows can be bought from archery shops owned by NPCs in Varrock ( Lowe's Archery Emporium ), Rimmington ( Brian's Archery Supplies ), Catherby ( Hickton's Archery Emporium ), and the Ranging Guild (level 40 Ranged).

There is a 20% chance arrows will be lost when shot. If an Ava's device is worn, most arrows will not be consumed and they will remain in the ammunition slot , instead of being dropped on the ground. Arrows that fail to be retrieved will eventually de-spawn, or may be picked up by other players.
