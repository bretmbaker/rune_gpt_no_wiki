Ali's Discount Wares is a shop owned by the swindler Ali Morrisane . It sells similar items as a general store , as well as desert survival gear. After completion of Rogue Trader , other items can be bought from the store.

It is worth noting that the rune shop prices do not fluctuate. Many Ironmen will find themselves with an excessive amount of blood and soul runes which sell for 200 and 150 respectively (50% of the item value).

Blackjacks [ edit | edit source ]

Requires completion of The Feud and blackjack section of Rogue Trader . At any given point in time, only one of these two shops will be available. The player can alternate between the two shops by doing the blackjack section of Rogue Trader again.
