A holy symbol is an amulet donning the symbol of Saradomin . They are made by having an unblessed symbol blessed by Brother Jered in the Edgeville Monastery , or by blessing it via using a holy book or book of balance on an unblessed symbol at 50 Prayer , requiring a few prayer points. As Brother Jered is located on the upper floor of the monastery, blessing the symbol effectively requires a minimum of level 31 Prayer due to the entry requirement.

It has the third highest prayer bonus for an amulet in game (after god stoles and the dragonbone necklace ). It is cheap, easy to obtain, and is commonly used in conjunction with Proselyte armour and other high prayer granting armour when using protect prayers as a form of defence while training.

Wearing a holy symbol will decrease the chance of vampyres healing when attacked (this healing is similar to Banshees ).

Wearing a holy symbol allows players to use a prayer book to cure poison.
