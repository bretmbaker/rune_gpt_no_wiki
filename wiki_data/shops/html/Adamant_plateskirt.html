The Adamant plateskirt is a plateskirt made out of adamant . At least 30 Defence is required to wear it. The plateskirt has the same stats as the Adamant platelegs , but are 1kg lighter.

They can be made at level 86 Smithing using 3 adamantite bars , which grants 187.5 experience.
