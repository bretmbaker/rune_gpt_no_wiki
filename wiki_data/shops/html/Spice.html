Spice is an item used in Cooking . It can be bought from the Culinaromancer's Chest after completing the beginning portion of Recipe for Disaster or from spice stalls in Ardougne , Civitas illa Fortis , and Prifddinas . It can also be stolen from those stalls with level 65 Thieving or looted from a Gourmet impling or Baby impling . Though it comes in a shaker, it is a single-use item.

You need spice in the goblin sub-quest of Recipe for Disaster , as well as for the Desert Treasure I quest.

Spice can be used to make Curry in place of 3 Curry leaves .
