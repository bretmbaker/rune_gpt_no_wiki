Outer Fortis is a part of Civitas illa Fortis found outside the main walls. The local population, mostly being farmers, use the area to grow crops to support the city's population.

Features [ edit | edit source ]

Farming [ edit | edit source ]

Outer Fortis has a lot of farms and as such players can gather several crops here. There are fields with onions , wheat , cabbages , and lastly sweetcorn which is unique to Outer Fortis.

It's also possible to pick nettles in several of these fields. One of the farms has a chicken coop, and a windmill for making wheat into flour is located on the west side of Outer Fortis.

Shops [ edit | edit source ]

Plenty of goods are already traded inside the city of Civitas illa Fortis but the village still houses a few shops and a pub:

  • Atlazora's Rest
  • Outer Fortis General Store
  • Spike's Spikes
  • Emelio's Kebab Shop

Personalities [ edit | edit source ]

  • Atza is part of the At First Light quest and specialises in fur but doesn't provide any services.
  • Bartender (Atlazora's Rest) is the bartender at the Atlazora's Rest pub.
  • Shopkeeper (Outer Fortis) is the owner of the General store.
  • Spike is the owner of Spike's Spikes that sells maces.
  • Xolo , Lola , and Molossus are all dogs found in the village.
  • Emelio , who sells Varlamorian kebabs after completing the Meat and Greet quest.
