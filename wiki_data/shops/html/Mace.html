Mace
Mace
Primary type Crush
Secondary type Stab
Attack speed
4 ticks (2.4 seconds)

Maces are melee weapons , and one of the few weapons which have Crush for a primary attack type . Maces also have a controlled attack style, giving equal experience to Attack , Strength , and Defence .

Maces are special in that they provide a Prayer bonus, a trait not found among any of the common weapons.

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • The mace prayer bonus may be an allusion to sine effusione sanguinis , a possibly apocryphal notion that Medieval Christians and the clergy wielded maces and other blunt weapons to avoid shedding blood, in conflicts such as the Crusades.
