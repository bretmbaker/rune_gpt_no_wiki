Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point | Talk to Islwyn inside the elven woods of Isafdar . Show on map

Official difficulty | Experienced

Official length | Short

Description | Deep in the elven forests of Isafdar, live a duo of roving elves: Islwyn a grumpy, old elf who dislikes humans and his companion, Eluned , who is a beautiful, friendly, young female elf with a kind heart. In this quest, you are challenged to overcome Islwyn's prejudice against humans and alleviate the burdens of the past.

Requirements | Completion of the following quests: Regicide Underground Pass Biohazard Plague City Waterfall Quest The ability to defeat a level 84 enemy unarmed, with heavy restrictions 56 Agility ( boostable ) ( not required to start ) , unless travelling with a charter ship to the elven woods

Items required | Glarial's pebble (can be obtained during the quest) Key (can be obtained during the quest) Spade Rope

Recommended | 60 Combat level The requirements to start Mourning's End Part I directly after this quest, to obtain the teleport crystal 60 Ranged ( not boostable ) 50 Thieving ( not boostable ) Completion of Sheep Herder and Big Chompy Bird Hunting Antipoison and food Ring of recoil , goblin paint cannon , prayer potions (for players with 43 Prayer to sustain Protect from Melee ) and a super combat potion in the fight against the Moss Guardian . Fast travel to the Waterfall Dungeon and Glarial's Tomb ( Games necklace or minigame teleport to the Barbarian Outpost, or skills necklace to the Fishing Guild) Fast travel to Islwyn and Eluned ( Iorwerth camp teleport or charter ship to Port Tyras) Ring of Charos (a) to reduce charter ship costs, if travelling with them

Enemies to defeat | Moss Guardian (level 84) (has to be killed with restricted items , can be flinched )

Ironman concerns | You must briefly visit Glarial's Tomb which has significant item restrictions . Items dropped outside the tomb may be picked back up within three minutes.

League notes | Unlocking Tirannwn ✓ will auto-complete the quest
