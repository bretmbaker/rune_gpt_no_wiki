In Search of Knowledge/Quick guide





Released | 4 July 2019 ( Update )

Members | Yes

Quest series | None

Developer(s) | Mod Husky



In Search of Knowledge is a miniquest which involves the player filling three tomes found in the Forthos Dungeon 's Temple Library.

Start point | Brother Aimeri , towards the south-eastern end of the Forthos Dungeon . Show on map

Requirements | None - but a high combat level is recommended.

Items required | Combat equipment 5 pieces of food (must be meat, fish or vegetables) A slash weapon or knife to cut through webs

Recommended | 45 Combat level

Enemies to defeat | Varies
