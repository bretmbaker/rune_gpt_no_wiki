The Fisher Realm [ edit | edit source ]

To fight the Black Knight Titan , bring armour, Excalibur , food, and one magic whistle . Note he is extremely resistant to Ranged and Magic , so melee is recommended. Bring antipoison if your combat level is lower than 41. You can use another weapon for a majority of the fight with the Black Knight Titan, but you must use Excalibur for the last hits. Note: He will heal to full health if your last attack is with something other than the Excalibur.
