Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

One of the three imbued god capes , by bringing the god cape you want to imbue to Kolodion .

After the quest, players can obtain more imbued god capes by bringing the respective component of the god cape that they wish to imbue to Kolodion by killing the respective follower:

Justiciar's hand for imbued saradomin cape Ent's roots for imbued guthix cape Demon's heart for imbued zamorak cape
