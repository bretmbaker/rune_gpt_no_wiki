Details

Start point | Inspect the frozen dwarf in the Lumbridge Castle dining room to start the quest.

Official difficulty | Special

Official length | Short

Description | To save the dwarf, you will need a special dwarven rock cake. Only his father in the tunnel under White Wolf Mountain knows how to make one.

Requirements | Completion of the following quests: Another Cook's Quest Cook's Assistant Fishing Contest The ability to defeat a level 13 icefiend if you don't have the ice gloves .

Items required | Bucket of milk (can be bought from the Culinaromancer's Chest ) Pot of flour (can be bought from the Culinaromancer's chest) Egg (can be bought from the Culinaromancer's chest) Bowl of water (a bowl and sink can be found in the kitchen of Lumbridge Castle ) 4 Asgarnian ales (can be bought during the quest at the Rising Sun Inn in Falador ) Recommended to bring at least 2 additional ales in case Rohak sobers up 306 coins (300 + 1 coin per ale, 100 instead of 300 if you choose to bring an activated ring of charos ) Bring additional coins if buying the other ingredients or bringing additional ales Gloves , If not using gloves make sure to bring Telekinetic Grab spell runes ( Ice gloves are the best, but other gloves will work fine too )

Recommended | Fast travel to several locations Falador ( Falador teleport ) Lumbridge ( Lumbridge teleport ) White Wolf Mountain ( Taverley teleport , Teleport to House if your house is in Taverley, Camelot teleport or a Gnome Glider ) Ice Mountain, if you have no access to ice gloves ( Combat bracelet to Monastery or Skull Sceptre Additional ingredients (bucket of milk, pot of flour and egg—in case you drop or lose the cake)

Enemies to defeat | Icefiend (level 13) (only if you lack ice gloves )

League notes | & — location requirement

Walkthrough

Items required: 316 coins , a bucket of milk , flour , an egg , a bowl of water , 4 Asgarnian Ales , gloves ( ice gloves recommended), runes for telegrab (unless you have gloves), and 2 Falador teleports (1 if you have ice gloves).

Rewards

1 Quest point 1,000 Slayer experience 1,000 Cooking experience Further access to the Culinaromancer's Chest Ability to obtain additional dwarven rock cakes from Rohak for either 100 coins or the raw ingredients
