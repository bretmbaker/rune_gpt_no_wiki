Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point | Speak to Mori in Arceuus . Show on map

Official difficulty | Intermediate

Official length | Short

Description | A long time ago, the citizens of Arceuus house gave up their human bodies and claimed immortality. This event was known as the Ascent of Arceuus and it marked the end of death within Arceuus house. Or so they believed. For the first time in a thousand years, a member of Arceuus house has fallen. You will need to solve the mystery of this death. However, time is of the essence and the fate of Arceuus house lies in your hands.

Requirements | Completion of the following quests: Client of Kourend X Marks the Spot 12 Hunter ( not boostable ) ( required to start )

Items required | None

Recommended | 15 Combat level Runes to cast Crumble Undead ( 2 2 1 ) on Tormented Souls and Trapped Soul Access to fairy rings ( Dramen / Lunar staff and started Fairytale II - Cure a Queen ) Two Battlefront teleports , or use the fairy ring C I R A Stamina potion Xeric's Talisman or runes for Kourend Castle Teleport ( 1 1 2 ) Skills necklace for teleportation to the Farming Guild , just south-west of Mount Karuulm , or via fairy ring C I R Arceuus library teleport tablets A pickaxe to complete an easy Kourend & Kebos Diary step A weapon
