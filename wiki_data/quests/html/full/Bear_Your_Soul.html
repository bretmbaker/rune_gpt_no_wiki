Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

The Arceuus Library is found in the north-west section of the city.

Journey of Souls [ edit | edit source ]

Begin the miniquest by making the trip to the Arceuus Library . The library can be reached by walking west from the ship you arrive in from in Port Piscarilius , and then north-west once you reach Arceuus . Alternatively, you can use the fairy ring code C I S and walk southward.

Once in the library, search for a book called The Journey of Souls (called "Soul journey" in the inventory). If you are asked to find this book by other NPCs ( Sam , Professor Gracklebone , and Villia ), you can ask Biblia on the top floor to narrow-down its location.

Once the book is obtained, read through it, and you will receive the message, The book referred to an artefact being buried in Kourend, but the author did not specify where it was buried. Maybe you could persuade her to change her mind. . If you do not get this message, read through the book again.

Upon examining the book, you will find that it was written by Aretha , who can be found by the Soul Altar in the northern part of Arceuus . Walk north from the library, then slight north-east past the Dark Altar , and follow the green-bluish road to reach her.

At the Soul Altar, speak to Aretha and select the option "I've been reading your book..." , and she will eventually mention the Key Master , who may have the knowledge to repair the artefact. She will further state that the artefact, known as the Soul Bearer, is buried in the crypt in the Arceuus church.

The church is located east of the library, in the area with blue around the edges and green in the middle on the minimap. Once you have reached the church, climb up the stairs to enter the building, and then climb down the stairs that follow to enter the centre area. Dig anywhere with a spade to obtain a damaged soul bearer .

The Key Master [ edit | edit source ]

Head to the Key Master , who is found in Cerberus' Lair within the Taverley Dungeon (refer to the map below for his location). Speak to him, and he will repair the damaged artefact into a usable soul bearer . If the player loses it, they can receive another from digging in the crypt in the Arceuus church.


Congratulations, miniquest complete!
