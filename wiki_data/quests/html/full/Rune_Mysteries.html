Rune Mysteries (#51)
Released 29 March 2004 ( Update )
Members No
Quest series Order of Wizards , #1
Lead developer(s) James B

Rune Mysteries is a novice level Free-to-play quest , and a requirement for mining rune essence within the Rune Essence mine or using lamps and books on runecraft .

Note that multiple air talismans can be obtained by using the drop trick at Duke Horacio . Grabbing 50-200 talismans can be useful for early runecraft training methods such as crafting air tiaras or mist runes . For details, see this guide . The Duke is unaware of the contents of group storage for group ironmen.

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

Talk to Duke Horacio on the 1 st floor [ UK ] 2 nd floor [ US ] of Lumbridge Castle , and ask him if he has any quests to give out. He will explain that although he himself has no tasks for the player, he did find a strange talisman. Curious about its origin, he will give you an air talisman and ask you to bring it to the arch mage of the Wizards' Tower . Note that at this point, it is possible to use the drop trick to collect any amount of spare air talismans , either for banking some Runecraft experience on ironman accounts, or for making a small amount of money. See the money making guide for more info.

Go to the Wizards' Tower, which is south of Draynor Village . Once there, descend the ladder into the basement and converse with Archmage Sedridor . He will take your air talisman and give you a research package . He will then instruct you to deliver it to Aubury in Varrock .

Aubury is located south of Varrock's east bank , inside of his rune shop . Talk to Aubury, and he will give you some notes and instruct you to deliver them back to Wizard Sedridor.

Make your way back to the Wizards' Tower , and talk to Archmage Sedridor once more. He will give you back your air talisman and will explain to you the basics of Runecraft .

Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 1 Quest point
  • The ability to mine rune essence
  • An air talisman
  • The ability to use lamps and books of knowledge from random events on Runecraft
  • Completing this quest awards the player with 5 Kudos which can be claimed by talking to Historian Minas in the Varrock Museum .

Required for completing [ edit | edit source ]

Completion of Rune Mysteries is required for the following:

  • Enter the Abyss
  • Lunar Diplomacy
  • One Small Favour
  • The Lost Tribe
  • What Lies Below
  • Easy Ardougne Diary - Have Wizard Cromperty teleport you to the Rune essence mine.
  • Easy Lumbridge & Draynor Diary - Have Archmage Sedridor teleport you to the Rune essence mine.
  • Easy Varrock Diary - Have Aubury teleport you to the Essence mine.

Changes [ edit | edit source ]

Date Changes
23 March 2022
( update )

Dialogue pertaining to the quest was rewritten to flow better and be consistent with established lore. The quest now focuses on rediscovering the essence mine rather than runecrafting as a whole.

11 November 2020
( update )

The wording in the quest reward scroll was changed from "Runecrafting skill" to "Rune Essence Mine Access" to more accurately reflect the existing quest rewards.

16 January 2007
( update )

The quest was renamed from "Rune Mysteries Quest" to "Rune Mysteries".

29 March 2004
( update )

The quest became permanently available with the launch of RuneScape 2 .

1 December 2003
( update )

The quest was added to the RuneScape 2 Beta.
