Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Speak to Ennius Tullus or Furia Tullus in front of the Sunrise Palace . Show on map
Official difficulty Intermediate
Official length Short
Description Take your first steps into Varlamore and unlock the Quetzal Transport System .
Requirements
  • Completion of the following quests:
    • Children of the Sun
Items required
  • Items for two different combat classes or a poisoned weapon (be warned that the poison method is much slower). Also note that autocasting magic on the Knight of Varlamore with a staff is not possible due to a bug, though manually casting does work.
Recommended
  • 40 Combat level
  • Some stamina potions
Enemies to defeat
  • Knight of Varlamore (level 81)
  • 8 cultists (level 34)
League notes

Unlocking Varlamore ✓ will auto-complete the quest

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

With friends like these... [ edit | edit source ]

  • Speak to Ennius or Furia Tullus outside the Sunrise Palace . ( 1 ) …
    1 Yes.
  • Head south to the temple inside city walls and speak to Metzli, Teokan of Ranul . ( 1 ) …
    1 I'm meant to be meeting Prince Itzla here.
  • Head down the stairs into the temple crypt.
  • Head north through the gate and talk to Prince Itzla Arkan and Servius, Teokan of Ralos .
  • Return to Ennius and Furia inside the Sunrise Palace, in the green area by the entrance, to receive a Varlamore crest .

Oh Brother! [ edit | edit source ]

  • Six Knight of Varlamore must now be found around the city.
    • Arrun and Claudia:
      • Head south to the bazaar and talk to the first two Knight of Varlamore .
      • Pickpocket the citizen in the sky blue toga in the bazaar to retrieve the stolen amulet .
      • Return to knights to give back the amulet.
    • Nel:
      • Head to the Fortis Cothon (harbour) to the north-east of the city.
      • Speak to the knight in the south-west corner of the cothon to find the correct crate (Make sure to finish the dialogue or you cannot successfully search the crate).
      • Search the crate on the north-east side between two fishing barrels and opposite a bench.
      • Speak to the knight again.
    • Mezan:
      • Head inside the Fortis Colosseum and head north to the training area near the Gladiators. (Bring two different combat styles) (For non-combat accounts, poison dynamite and recoil can be used)
      • Talk to the knight and fight him. (Swap combat styles when he prays)
    • Velam and Azali:
      • Head south of the city walls to the pub, north of the Outer Fortis General Store .
      • Speak to both knights there and lead one of them to the fountain to the east. Make sure to keep talking to her to keep her from getting distracted.
      • After the short cutscene, speak to the knight again to progress the quest to the next step.
  • Head back to the Sunrise Palace and speak to Ennius and Furia. They will want you to search the Kualti Headquarters to the west for evidence.
  • Head to the headquarters (large building west of combat training) and go to the top floor. Search the southern chest in the south-eastern room to find an incriminating letter .
  • Read the letter.
  • Return to Ennius and Furia, and watch the cutscene.
  • Speak to Ennius again.

The Twilight Emissaries [ edit | edit source ]

  • Speak to Regulus Cento south-east of the palace. ( 2 ) …
    2 I was told to ask you about getting to the Teomat.
  • Feed Renu .
  • Travel with Renu to Ralos' Rise (The Teomat).
  • Speak with Prince Itzla inside the Teomat temple.
  • Speak to Metzli in the northern building.
  • Defeat all 8 cultists.
  • Finish the cutscene and ensuing dialogue.
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 1 Quest point
  • Ability to use the Civitas illa Fortis Teleport spell
  • Ability to use the Quetzal Transport System
  • 3,000 Thieving experience
