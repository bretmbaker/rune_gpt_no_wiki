Making History/Quick guide
This quest has an in-depth guide .
It contains a more detailed description of dialogue, cutscenes, and storyline.

Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Talk to Jorral in the outpost north-west of West Ardougne . Show on map
Official difficulty Intermediate
Official length Short
Description A secluded outpost has served the City of Ardougne for generations, playing stage to many exceptional events. Its current occupant wants to employ your skills to construct its true history before time runs out and history itself is wiped away!
Requirements
  • Completion of the following quests:
    • Priest in Peril
    • The Restless Ghost
Items required
  • Spade
  • Sapphire amulet
  • Ghostspeak amulet or Morytania legs 2 or higher
  • A way to enter Port Phasmatys
    • Completion of Ghosts Ahoy or
    • 2 ecto-token or
    • 1 bone , 1 pot and 1 bucket to earn 5 ecto-tokens (you still only need two) or
    • 5,200 gold for a Charter ship to Port Phasmatys (cheaper depending upon where you travel from and if you use ring of charos ).
Recommended
  • Teleport runes for Ardougne Teleport or Ardougne teleport tablets
  • Ectophial
  • Ring of dueling
  • Enchanted lyre , Rellekka teleport tablets or Fremennik Sea Boots 1 or higher
  • Necklace of passage (to teleport to Jorral instead of walking from Ardougne )

or access to:

  • Fairy rings (or Teleport to House if player's house is in Rellekka ),
  • Stamina or Energy potions
Enemies to defeat None
Ironman concerns
  • 24 Crafting (if making own sapphire amulet (u) )
League notes

Fremennik ✓ — location requirement
Kandarin ✓ — location requirement
Morytania ✓ — location requirement
Unlocking Tirannwn ✓ will auto-complete the quest

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

Starting out [ edit | edit source ]

  • Walk to or use the Necklace of Passage to teleport to the outpost north-west of Ardougne.
  • Talk to Jorral . ( 1 • 1 ) …
    1 Tell me more.
    1 Ok, I'll make a stand for history!
  • Complete the next 3 segments in any order.

Erin the silver merchant [ edit | edit source ]

The location to dig for the chest.
  • Talk to the Silver merchant in the East Ardougne market. ( 2 ) …
    2 Ask about the outpost.
  • Dig with your spade to find the chest buried north of Castle Wars .
  • Open the chest with the enchanted key to get a journal.

Droalak the Ghost [ edit | edit source ]

  • Equip your Ghostspeak amulet and talk to Droalak outside the Port Phasmatys general store.
  • In the building east of the general store, directly south of the pub, give Melina a strung sapphire amulet.
  • Talk to Droalak to receive a scroll.

Reporting back [ edit | edit source ]

  • Talk to Jorral, back at the outpost, with the scroll and journal.
  • Talk to King Lathas , located on the 1 st floor [ UK ] 2 nd floor [ US ] of the Ardougne Castle . ( 1 ) …
    1 F
  • Talk to Jorral, back at the outpost.
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 3 Quest points
  • 1,000 Crafting experience
  • 1,000 Prayer experience
  • 750 coins
  • Enchanted key , allowing you to go on your own treasure digs until it disappears
  • Access to the museum in the outpost
  • Ability to complete master clue hot/cold Treasure Trails .
  • Ability to claim 5 Kudos and an antique lamp for 1,000 experience (in any skill over level 20) by talking to Historian Minas in the Varrock Museum .
