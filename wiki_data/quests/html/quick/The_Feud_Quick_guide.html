The Feud/Quick guide
This quest has an in-depth guide .
It contains a more detailed description of dialogue, cutscenes, and storyline.

Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Talk to Ali Morrisane east of the Gem Trader in northern Al Kharid . Show on map
Official difficulty Intermediate
Official length Medium
Description Ali Morrisane - RuneScape's self proclaimed 'greatest merchant' - is having difficulties. He is selling things at such a rate that he cannot keep his market stall in stock. Word has it that he is on the lookout for someone; someone who can recruit some help from his home town Pollnivneach - a somewhat anarchic place, south of the Shantay Pass.
Requirements
  • 30 Thieving ( not boostable ) ( required to start )
  • The ability to kill a level 70 bandit and a level 75 Menaphite (both can be safespotted).
Items required
  • 501+ coins
  • Gloves - ( Leather gloves , graceful gloves , zombie gloves and mime gloves do work, Barrows gloves , ice gloves , vambraces , and Slayer gloves do not work!)
  • Kharidian headpiece (can be bought from Ali Morrisane )
  • Fake beard (can be bought from Ali Morrisane )
  • 3 beers (can be obtained during quest, bought from Ali the Barman for 2 coins each)
  • Bucket or Ugthanki dung (can be obtained during quest, spawns at the location it is needed – bring three extra buckets, four total, if you plan on doing My Arm's Big Adventure as three Ugthanki dung are needed for that quest). One more might be needed for Forgettable Tale... (1/25 chance for each player).
  • if you have the snake charm in your bank already you will need to retrieve it
Recommended
  • 40 Combat level
  • Stamina or energy potions
  • Armour and some food
  • Waterskin (one is enough)
  • Fast travel to Al Kharid ( Ring of Dueling or an Amulet of Glory )
  • Fast travel to Pollnivneach ( Magic carpet or Pollnivneach teleport , or House teleport if your house is in that city)
    • Ring of Charos (a) to save money on magic carpet rides
Enemies to defeat
  • Bandit champion (level 70)
  • Tough Guy (level 75)
League notes

Unlocking Asgarnia ✓ will auto-complete the quest
Unlocking Desert ✓ will auto-complete the quest
Unlocking Fremennik ✓ will auto-complete the quest

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

Getting Started [ edit | edit source ]

  • Talk to Ali Morrisane next to the Silk Stall in Al Kharid . ( 1 • 2 • 1 • 2 ) …
    1 If you are, then why are you still selling goods from a stall?
    2 I'd like to help you but.....
    1 Yes.
    2 Okay.
  • Buy a kharidian headpiece and a fake beard and combine them.

Pollnivneach [ edit | edit source ]

  • Head to Pollnivneach .
  • Use the 3 beers on Drunken Ali at the bar. (Make sure to use one beer on him, then talk to him in full, then use the second beer, talk in full, then finally the third beer, and talk in full.)
    • Note: Don't skip dialogue after giving a beer. You can buy them from Ali the Barman .
  • Talk to the bandits in Pollnivneach by the general store.
  • Go west of the bar and question the Menaphite Thugs .

Camels [ edit | edit source ]

  • Head to the camel store just north-west of the bar.
  • Talk to Ali the Camel Man to buy the camels for 500 coins. You will need two free spaces in your inventory. ( 4 • 2 • 3 ) …
    4 Are those camels around the side for sale?
    2 What price do you want for both of them?
    3 Would 500 gold coins for the pair of them do?
  • Talk to a Menaphite and a bandit to give them each a receipt .

The Menaphites [ edit | edit source ]

Pickpocketing [ edit | edit source ]

  • Go to Ali the Operator and ask to join his gang (big purple tent, south-west) ( 1 • 2 ) …
    1 Yes, of course, those bandits should be taught a lesson.
    2 No thanks.
  • Attempt to pickpocket a villager once then return to Ali the Operator. ( 1 • 2 ) …
    1 Yeah, I could do with a bit of advice.
    2 No thanks.
  • Talk to the street urchin near the centre town well.
    • Pay him ten coins
  • Pickpocket the villager with an arrow over their head.
  • Talk to Ali the Operator ( 1 ) …
    1 Yeah, I could do with a bit of advice.
  • Equip the oak blackjack and then lure a villager into a secluded area.
    • Knock out the villager with the blackjack equipped
    • Pickpocket the villager

Jewels [ edit | edit source ]

  • Talk to Ali the Operator.
  • Put on the desert disguise and gloves.
  • Hide behind the large cactus near the mayor's house (the large house to the East by the fountain).
  • Use the keys on the door.
  • Go up the stairs in the north-eastern corner.
  • Search the landscape picture near the bed.
  • Hit the button on the dial to reset it.
    • Click on 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, in that order, clockwise.
  • Give the jewels to Ali the Operator.

Traitor [ edit | edit source ]

  • Talk to Ali the Operator.
  • Talk to a Menaphite Thug.
  • Talk to Ali the Operator. ( 2 ) …
    2 No thanks.
  • Talk to Ali the barman. ( 3 • 1 ) …
    3 I'm looking for Traitorous Ali.
    1 No thanks I'm ok.
  • Buy special sauce from the kebab seller. He can be found north-west of the well in the middle of the town. ( 3 • 2 ) …
    3 Would you sell me that bottle of special kebab sauce?
    2 No thanks, I'm good.
  • Use it on a trough behind the camel shop.
  • Use a bucket on the dung if it is brown. Obtain three more buckets of dung if you plan to complete My Arm's Big Adventure .
  • Use a coin on the snake charmer's money pot, sitting south of the bar ( 1 ) …
    1 Wow, a snake charmer. Can I have a go? Can I have a go? Please?
    with 2 free inventory spaces to get a snake charm and a snake basket .
  • Use the snake charm on a desert snake north of the Menaphite tent until it goes into the snake basket .
  • Go to Ali the Hag north-west of the kebab seller. Talk to her twice to give her the snake basket and Ugthanki dung to receive the hag's poison in exchange.
  • Use the hag's poison on the table with a beer in the bar.
  • Talk to Ali the Operator.

Dealing with the thugs [ edit | edit source ]

  • Prepare for battle and talk to the Menaphite Leader (just outside the tent).
  • Kill the Tough Guy (level 75) ( safespot behind bush). He drops a willow blackjack .
  • Talk to a villager. ( 2 ) …
    2 Ok I'll get to it.
  • Talk to the Bandit Leader wandering around the northern magic carpet station.
  • Kill the bandit champion (level 70) (safespot behind chair in house to the east). He drops an adamant scimitar (if you have a willow blackjack in your inventory).
  • Talk to a villager.
  • Talk to Ali the Mayor by the well in village centre.
  • Return to Ali Morrisane (with whom you started the quest). ( 1 ) …
    1 No, I'm really too busy.
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 1 Quest point
  • 15,000 Thieving experience
  • 500 coins
  • An oak blackjack
  • A desert disguise (created during the quest)
  • A willow blackjack (as a drop from the tough guy )
  • An adamant scimitar (as a drop from the bandit champion )
  • Access to the Rogue Trader miniquest – Completing this will allow Ali Morrisane to sell blackjacks , various runes , and different clothing from around the Kharidian Desert
  • The ability to pickpocket villagers , bandits , and Menaphite thugs in Pollnivneach

Required for completing [ edit | edit source ]

  • My Arm's Big Adventure
