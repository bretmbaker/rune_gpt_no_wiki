Bear Your Soul/Quick guide
Released 19 May 2016 ( Update )
Members Yes
Quest series None
Developer(s) Ashleigh B

Bear Your Soul is a miniquest that involves the player searching for a damaged ancient artefact that is mentioned in a book.

During the miniquest, players will require a spade and a method to reach Cerberus' Lair within the Taverley Dungeon . Players thus need a dusty key if they do not have at least level 70 in Agility (can be boosted with a Summer pie from 65). Alternatively, players who have Key master teleports can teleport directly to Cerberus' Lair.

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

The Arceuus Library is found in the north-west section of the city.
  • Find and read the book Soul journey in the Arceuus Library . fairy code CIS.
  • Talk to Aretha at the Soul Altar . ( 3 • 1 ) …
    3 I've been reading your book...
    1 Yes please.
  • Go to the Arceuus church east of the library and climb up a staircase to get inside.
  • Climb down the inner staircase to the courtyard in the centre.
  • Dig anywhere to get the Damaged soul bearer .
  • Speak to the Key Master , who is found in Cerberus' Lair within the Taverley Dungeon .
  • Receive the Soul bearer .
  • Congratulations, quest complete!
