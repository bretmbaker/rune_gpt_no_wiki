Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Speak to the barbarian guard at the entrance of the Barbarian Agility Training Course. Show on map
Official difficulty Novice
Official length Medium
Description Go to bars and buy alcohol.
Requirements None
Items required
  • 208 coins to buy all the required alcohol
Recommended
  • Restore potions to restore skills that are lowered
  • Food to recover any lost hitpoints
  • Stamina or energy potions
  • Fast travel to several locations
    • Barbarian Outpost ( Games Necklace )
    • Varrock ( Varrock teleport , Chronicle or Combat Bracelet to the Champions' Guild)
    • Lumberyard ( Lumberyard teleport , Digsite pendant or Digsite teleport )
    • Tree Gnome Stronghold ( Balloon Transport System , Gnome Glider , Spirit Tree or Slayer Ring to the Slayer Cave)
    • Brimhaven ( Brimhaven teleport , Teleport to House if your house is in Brimhaven, or ship from Ardougne )
    • Musa Point ( Amulet of Glory to Karamja or ship from Port Sarim )
    • Port Sarim ( Explorer's Ring 2 , Amulet of Glory to Draynor Village or Rat Pits minigame teleport)
    • Yanille ( Watchtower teleport or Nightmare Zone minigame teleport)
    • Ardougne ( Ardougne teleport or Ardougne cloak )
    • Falador ( Falador teleport )
    • Seers' Village ( Camelot teleport or Combat Bracelet to the Ranging Guild)
Enemies to defeat None
League notes

Kandarin ✓ - location requirement
Unlocking Kandarin ✓ will auto-complete the bar visits in Asgarnia ✓
Unlocking Fremennik ✓ will auto-complete the miniquest

  • Talk to a Barbarian Guard outside of the Barbarian Outpost . ( 1 • 2 ) …
    1 I want to come through this gate
    2 Looks can be deceiving, I am in face a barbarian
  • Talk to the Bartender at Blue Moon Inn in Varrock . ( 4 ) …
    4 I'm doing Alfred Grimhand's Barcrawl.
  • Talk to the Bartender at Jolly Boar Inn in Varrock , near the Lumber Yard . ( 4 ) …
    4 I'm doing Alfred Grimhand's Barcrawl.
  • Talk to Kaylee or Emily at Rising Sun Inn in Falador . ( 2 ) …
    2 I'm doing Alfred Grimhand's Barcrawl.
  • Talk to the Bartender at Rusty Anchor Inn in Port Sarim . ( 3 ) …
    3 I'm doing Alfred Grimhand's Barcrawl.
  • Talk to Zembo at Karamja Spirits Bar in Musa Point . ( 3 ) …
    3 I'm doing Alfred Grimhand's Barcrawl.
  • Talk to the Bartender at Dead Man's Chest in Brimhaven . ( 4 ) …
    4 I'm doing Alfred Grimhand's Barcrawl.
  • Talk to the Bartender at Flying Horse Inn in Ardougne . ( 3 ) …
    3 I'm doing Alfred Grimhand's Barcrawl.
  • Talk to the Bartender at Forester's Arms in Seers' Village . ( 3 ) …
    3 I'm doing Alfred Grimhand's Barcrawl.
  • Talk to Blurberry at Blurberry Bar in Tree Gnome Stronghold (Grand Tree). ( 1 ) …
    1 I'm doing Alfred Grimhand's Barcrawl.
  • Talk to the Bartender at Dragon Inn in Yanille . ( 4 ) …
    4 I'm doing Alfred Grimhand's Barcrawl.
  • Talk to a Barbarian Guard outside of the Barbarian Outpost . ( 1 • 2 ) …
    1 I want to come through this gate
    2 Looks can be deceiving, I am in fact a barbarian.
  • Congratulations, miniquest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

Upon completion of the miniquest, players will now be able to access the Barbarian Outpost Agility course , which is required to enter during Scorpion Catcher and Horror from the Deep .

In addition, players can speak to the barbarian guard to learn how to automatically smash empty vials upon drinking the last dose of a potion.
