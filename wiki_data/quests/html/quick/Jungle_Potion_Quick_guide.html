Jungle Potion/Quick guide
This quest has an in-depth guide .
It contains a more detailed description of dialogue, cutscenes, and storyline.

Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Speak to Trufitus at Tai Bwo Wannai . Show on map
Official difficulty Novice
Official length Short
Description Trufitus Shakaya of the Tai Bwo Wannai Village requires that you collect five special jungle herbs for a potion so he can commune with his Gods.
Requirements
  • Completion of Druidic Ritual
  • 3 Herblore ( boostable ) ( not required to start )
  • The ability to defend against level 53 Jogres and level 46 Harpie Bug Swarms .
Items required None
Recommended
  • 20 Combat level
  • 1-2 antidote++ or other antipoison potions
  • Food
  • Stamina potions
  • Fast travel to the Tai Bwo Wannai Village ( Brimhaven teleport , Teleport to House if your house is in Brimhaven, charter ship or ship from Ardougne to Brimhaven, Fairy Rings to C K R or Amulet of Glory to Karamja)
  • Spade to dig up the Clue hunter trousers
Enemies to defeat None
League notes

Unlocking Karamja ✓ will auto-complete the quest

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

Getting started [ edit | edit source ]

  • Talk to Trufitus in his house located north-east of Tai Bwo Wannai . ( 3 • 1 • 1 ) …
    3 It's a nice village, where is everyone?
    1 Me? How can I help?
    1 It sounds like just the challenge for me.
    Everytime you gather a new herb you need to go show it to Trufitus before being able to get a new kind of a herb.

Gathering herbs [ edit | edit source ]

Locations of all materials.

Snake weed [ edit | edit source ]

  • Go south-west of Tai Bwo Wannai until green and yellow vines labelled ' marshy jungle vine ' are seen.
  • Search the vines and clean the herb. Your character may spend several minutes finding a herb.
    • Note: Take three if you intend to do Legends' Quest and Zogre Flesh Eaters .
  • Talk to Trufitus. Make sure you cleaned the herb. ( 1 ) …
    1 Of course!

Ardrigal [ edit | edit source ]

  • Head north-east from Trufitus. There will be Harpie Bug Swarms and palm trees.
  • Search the palm trees and clean the herb.
    • Note: Take two if you intend to do Legends' Quest .
  • Talk to Trufitus. ( 1 ) …
    1 Of course!

Sito foil [ edit | edit source ]

  • To the direct south of Tai Bwo Wannai Village is a patch of scorched earth .
  • Search the scorched earth (the squares immediately next to the fire) for the herb and clean it.
  • Talk to Trufitus. ( 1 ) …
    1 Of course!

Volencia moss [ edit | edit source ]

  • Go south-east from Trufitus until you reach the Karamja Jungle mine , marked with a pickaxe on minimap.
  • Search the rocks for the herb and clean it.
    • Note: Take two if you intend to do Fairytale I - Growing Pains (possible required item).
  • Talk to Trufitus. ( 1 ) …
    1 Of course!

Rogue's purse [ edit | edit source ]

Note: The Rogue's purse must be obtained from the following method. Herbs gained anywhere else cannot be used.

  • Go back by the Harpie Bug Swarms and head north-west until a cave is seen over the cavern. Search the rocks and enter the cave.
  • In the cave, head south, look for green spots on the wall labelled ' Fungus covered Cavern wall ', then search them and clean the herb. Do not try to use a Rogue's purse dropped by a Jogre. It must be obtained via searching the wall.
    • Note: Take two if you intend to do Zogre Flesh Eaters .
    • Killing a Jogre inside this cave completes an easy Karamja Diary task, and is worth doing along the way.
  • Talk to Trufitus. ( 1 ) …
    1 Of course!
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 1 Quest point
  • 775 Herblore experience

Required for completing [ edit | edit source ]

Completion of Jungle Potion is required for the following:

  • My Arm's Big Adventure - To work in the Tai Bwo Wannai Cleanup minigame during the quest
  • Shilo Village
  • Tai Bwo Wannai Trio
  • Zogre Flesh Eaters
  • Several tasks in the Karamja Diary
