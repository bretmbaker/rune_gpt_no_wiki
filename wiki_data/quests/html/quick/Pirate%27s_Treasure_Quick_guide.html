Pirate's Treasure/Quick guide
This quest has an in-depth guide .
It contains a more detailed description of dialogue, cutscenes, and storyline.

Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Speak to Redbeard Frank at the northernmost dock at Port Sarim , just outside The Rusty Anchor . Show on map
Official difficulty Novice
Official length Very Short
Description Redbeard Frank knows the location of pirate treasure, but he'll only part with the knowledge for a bottle of Karamjan rum .
Requirements Able to defeat or run away from a Combat level 4 gardener .
Items required
  • Karamjan rum (obtainable during the quest for 30 coins)
  • White apron (obtainable during the quest in Port Sarim at Gerrant's Fishy Business. )
  • 60 coins (only 30 is needed if using a Ring of charos(a) )
  • 10 bananas (obtainable during the quest)
  • Spade (obtainable during the quest)
Recommended
  • Varrock Teleport
  • Falador Teleport
Enemies to defeat Gardener (level 4, optional)
League notes

Asgarnia ✓ — location requirement
Unlocking Morytania ✓ will auto-complete the quest

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

Captain Redbeard [ edit | edit source ]

  • Talk to Redbeard Frank by the pub to the north Port Sarim dock. ( 1 • 1 ) …
    1 I'm in search of treasure.
    1 Ok, I will bring you some rum
  • Travel to Karamja for 30 coins (talk to Seaman Lorris the south-west docks).

The island of Karamja [ edit | edit source ]

  • Head west to the Karamja Wines, Spirits, and Beers bar and buy one Karamjan rum for 30 coins. (Do not try to leave the island with the rum.)
  • Grab 10 Bananas from the nearby trees.
  • Talk to Luthas the banana farmer about the customs workers, ( 2 ) …
    2 That customs officer is annoying isn't she?
    then talk to him again to ask for a job. ( 1 ) …
    1 Could you offer me employment on your plantation?
  • Place your rum in the crate outside, then right-click Fill to add the 10 bananas (the crate outside, not in the bedroom. Careful not to eat the bananas).
  • Talk to Luthas and get your payment from him.
  • Take the boat back to Port Sarim. ( 1 • 2 • 1 ) …
    1 Can I journey on this ship?
    2 Search away, I have nothing to hide.
    1 0k.

Back at Port Sarim [ edit | edit source ]

  • Take a white apron from the fishing store Gerrant's Fishy Business. .
  • Talk to Wydin in the food store South of Gerrant's and get a job while wearing your white apron. ( 4 ) …
    4 Well, can I get a job here?
  • Search the crate with the banana on it to get the rum.
  • Give the rum to Redbeard Frank , he will give you a key . ( 1 ) …
    1 Ok thanks, I'll go and get it.
  • Go upstairs in the Blue Moon Inn in Varrock and use your key on the chest in the room directly west. Read the pirate message .

Buried treasure [ edit | edit source ]

  • Head to Falador Park .
    • If you didn't have a spade already, you can get one from the Estate agent 's house on the south-west side of the park, right next to the study desk.
  • Dig in Falador park, kill the Gardener (or run away to despawn him).
  • Dig again to get a casket.
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 2 Quest points
  • Ability to use the pay-fare option to and from Karamja .
  • Access to One-Eyed Hector's Treasure Chest (contains a gold ring , an emerald , and 450 coins ).
