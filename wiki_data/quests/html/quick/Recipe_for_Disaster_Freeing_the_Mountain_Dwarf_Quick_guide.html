Recipe for Disaster: Freeing the Mountain Dwarf/Quick guide
This quest has an in-depth guide .
It contains a more detailed description of dialogue, cutscenes, and storyline.


Details

Start point Inspect the frozen dwarf in the Lumbridge Castle dining room to start the quest.
Official difficulty Special
Official length Short
Description To save the dwarf, you will need a special dwarven rock cake. Only his father in the tunnel under White Wolf Mountain knows how to make one.
Requirements
  • Completion of the following quests:
    • Another Cook's Quest
      • Cook's Assistant
    • Fishing Contest
  • The ability to defeat a level 13 icefiend if you don't have the ice gloves .
Items required
  • Bucket of milk (can be bought from the Culinaromancer's Chest )
  • Pot of flour (can be bought from the Culinaromancer's chest)
  • Egg (can be bought from the Culinaromancer's chest)
  • Bowl of water (a bowl and sink can be found in the kitchen of Lumbridge Castle )
  • 4 Asgarnian ales (can be bought during the quest at the Rising Sun Inn in Falador )
    • Recommended to bring at least 2 additional ales in case Rohak sobers up
  • 306 coins (300 + 1 coin per ale, 100 instead of 300 if you choose to bring an activated ring of charos )
    • Bring additional coins if buying the other ingredients or bringing additional ales
  • Gloves , If not using gloves make sure to bring Telekinetic Grab spell runes ( Ice gloves are the best, but other gloves will work fine too )
Recommended
  • Fast travel to several locations
    • Falador ( Falador teleport )
    • Lumbridge ( Lumbridge teleport )
    • White Wolf Mountain ( Taverley teleport , Teleport to House if your house is in Taverley, Camelot teleport or a Gnome Glider )
    • Ice Mountain, if you have no access to ice gloves ( Combat bracelet to Monastery or Skull Sceptre
  • Additional ingredients (bucket of milk, pot of flour and egg—in case you drop or lose the cake)
Enemies to defeat Icefiend (level 13) (only if you lack ice gloves )
League notes

& — location requirement

Walkthrough

Items required: 316 coins , a bucket of milk , flour , an egg , a bowl of water , 4 Asgarnian Ales , gloves ( ice gloves recommended), runes for telegrab (unless you have gloves), and 2 Falador teleports (1 if you have ice gloves).

  • Inspect the dwarf in Lumbridge Castle's dining room.
  • Go to the Rising Sun Inn in Falador and talk to Emily or Kaylee . Pay her to learn how to make Asgoldian ale . ( 2 • 2 ) …
    2 What can you tell me about dwarves and ale?
    2 I could offer you some in return, how about 200 gold?
  • Use coins on your Asgarnian ales to get Asgoldian ales.
  • Go to the tunnel under White Wolf Mountain.
  • Talk to " An old Dwarf " located in the bar and give him Asgoldian ales until he agrees to make a dwarven rock cake.
  • Talk to him 3 times and he will place a dwarven rock cake on the table. (His name will change to Rohak)
  • If you have ice gloves , equip them and pick up the cake. Otherwise either telegrab the cake or equip other gloves and pick it up, then kill an icefiend (at Ice Mountain ) to cool the cake.
  • Give the dwarven rock cake to the dwarf in Lumbridge.
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards

  • 1 Quest point
  • 1,000 Slayer experience
  • 1,000 Cooking experience
  • Further access to the Culinaromancer's Chest
  • Ability to obtain additional dwarven rock cakes from Rohak for either 100 coins or the raw ingredients
