This quest has an in-depth guide .
It contains a more detailed description of dialogue, cutscenes, and storyline.

Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Speak to Father Reen , south of Al Kharid bank. Show on map
Official difficulty Intermediate
Official length Medium
Description A sinister group of dark wizards is attempting to summon Agrith-Naar, a demon of exceptional mystical power. Rather than try to prevent them, a Saradominist priest asks the player to help summon the demon and then kill it once and for all. But all is not as it seems...
Requirements
  • 30 Crafting ( boostable )
  • Completion of the following quests:
    • The Golem
    • Demon Slayer
  • The ability to defeat a level 100 demon
Items required
  • Silverlight (obtainable at start of quest)
  • Strange implement (obtainable during quest)
  • Any black outfit (minimum of 3 pieces, see below for the allowed gear — the dyed Silverlight does not count as a piece of black clothing)
    • Note: You can make a black desert robe and shirt during the quest if you brought a desert robe and shirt
  • Black dye (from grinding a black mushroom using a pestle and mortar alongside a vial)
  • Silver bar
  • Means to enter the desert (such as the Shantay pass , Desert amulet 2 , Teleport tablets or scrolls , Fairy rings or the Pharaoh's sceptre )
Recommended
  • 50 Combat level
  • Fast travel to Al Kharid to start the quest ( Amulet of Glory or Ring of Dueling )
  • Fast travel to Uzer ( Necklace of Passage to Eagles' Eyrie, Fairy Rings to D L Q or using the magic carpet transport)
  • Food and armour to defeat a level 100 demon
  • 2 waterskins
  • Chisel and hammer (optional to obtain several gems from the throne, unless already taken during The Golem )
Enemies to defeat Agrith-Naar (level 100)
League notes

Desert ✓ — location requirement

Tip: To save some time, you can buy a black wizard hat from Betty's Magic Emporium in Port Sarim and Priest robes from Varrock , or dye desert robes with black dye (requires pestle and mortar and vial to make), a black cape can be used for the third item. Note that dyed desert robes no longer protect you from desert heat . Void gear does not count.

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

Starting out [ edit | edit source ]

  • Talk to Father Reen outside Al Kharid bank. ( 1 ) …
    1 That's me!
  • Travel to Uzer via the magic carpet south of Al Kharid (or use the necklace of passage teleport to Eagle's Eyrie and run south-east). You can also use a fairy ring ( D L Q ) and go north-east.
  • Talk to Father Badden near the ruin entrance. ( 1 • 3 • 1 ) …
    1 Reen sent me.
    3 So what do you want me to do?
    1 How can I do that?
    Be sure to have Silverlight in your inventory.
  • Pick and use a black mushroom (or black dye) on Silverlight to dye it black (you can find some beside the staircase).
  • Go down the stairs and grab a copy of the Strange implement in the north-west corner of the ruin.
  • Talk to Evil Dave in the dungeon wearing your 3 black items and your dyed Silverlight. ( 2 • 1 ) …
    2 I want to join your group.
    1 I'm evil!

Ritual [ edit | edit source ]

  • Talk to Denath on the other side of the portal. ( 4 ) …
    4 What do I have to do?
  • Talk to Jennifer to get a demonic sigil mould . ( 2 ) …
    2 Do you have the demonic sigil mould?
  • Talk to Matthew . ( 3 ) …
    3 Do you know what happened to Josef?
  • Go to any furnace and smelt a demonic sigil (use silver bar on furnace and select 'demonic sigil').
  • Your black outfit can now be discarded and replaced with combat gear (keep Silverlight with you).
  • Head back to Uzer and talk to the clay golem outside about last night. ( 3 ) …
    3 Did you see anything happen last night?
  • Search the nearby kilns for a book (there are four kilns – the last two are on the east side of the ruin, and by the magic carpet).
  • Read the book, and take it to Matthew on the other side of the portal
  • Go north and talk to Denath. ( 4 ) …
    4 I forgot the incantation.
    (or ( 2 ) …
    2 I forgot the incantation.
    during the ritual). Write down the incantation he tells you as it is used twice during the quest.
  • Complete the circle and click the sigil in your inventory to chant the incantation (unequip the Silverlight if nothing happens).
  • TAKE THE DEMONIC SIGIL DENATH DROPPED ON THE FLOOR. (If you do not, you will have to create another Demonic sigil)
  • Enter the portal to exit the room and take Tanya's sigil.
  • Tell Evil Dave to return to the lair ( 2 ) …
    2 You've got to get back to the throne room!
    – you should have 4 sigils now.

Rallying the troops [ edit | edit source ]

  • Talk to Father Badden outside.
  • Convince Father Reen to join the ritual. ( 3 ) …
    3 Oh, don't be so simple-minded!
  • Talk to the golem, he will refuse to help. ( 3 ) …
    3 I could just reprogram you again.
  • Use the Strange implement on the golem to reprogram him.
  • Talk to the golem again to make him enter the dungeon.

The final fight [ edit | edit source ]

This fight takes place in an instance .
On death , all unprotected items will be moved to a grave outside the instance. Any items left on the ground will be lost.

NOTE: this boss can be flinched by standing behind the north-western torch.

UIM concerns: If you die during the fight, all your items will appear on the ground outside the portal in the temple.

  • Talk to Matthew to start the incantation, say it backwards . ( 1 ) …
    1 Yes
  • Kill Agrith Naar with any weapon, but the final blow must be with Silverlight. The demon will heal if you run away as the final blow is being dealt.
  • Unequip Silverlight.
  • Choose your reward to finish the quest.
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 1 Quest point
  • 10,000 experience in any combat skill other than Prayer .
  • Silverlight turns into the Darklight sword - it has a special attack and is even more powerful against demons . It costs slightly more to replace.
  • Six cut gems: two sapphires , two rubies and two emeralds , if you use a hammer and chisel on the demon's throne and had not yet removed them during The Golem .

Unlocks:

  • Agrith-Naar accessible in the Nightmare Zone .
