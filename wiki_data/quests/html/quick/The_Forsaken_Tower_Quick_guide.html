Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Speak to Lady Vulcana Lovakengj in Lovakengj . Show on map
Official difficulty Intermediate
Official length Short
Description For thousands of years, the Wintertodt has remained safely sealed behind the great Doors of Dinh. Alas, despite the best attempts of the Lovakengj smiths and the Arceuus mages, the doors are failing. Only Dinh himself knew the secrets behind the creation of these doors. Without his knowledge, there may be nothing to stop the Wintertodt escaping and bringing ruin upon Kourend. It is up to you to recover this knowledge.
Requirements
  • Completion of the following quests:
    • Client of Kourend
      • X Marks the Spot
Items required
  • Tinderbox (obtainable during quest)
Recommended
  • Antipoison
  • Food
  • Stamina potion
  • Teleports to the quest start, Lovakengj Assembly :
    • Xeric's talisman
    • Fairy ring code D J R , may require a dramen or lunar staff
    • Battlefront teleport
    • Kharedst's memoirs with Shayzien teleport
  • Wintertodt Teleport:
    • Games necklace
  • Bring 6 Coal and 1 Adamantite ore to complete a Hard Kourend & Kebos Diary task (requires 70 Smithing ( boostable ) )
Enemies to defeat None
League notes

Unlocking Kourend ✓ will auto-complete the quest

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

Start [ edit | edit source ]

  • Talk to Lady Vulcana Lovakengj in the Lovakengj Assembly building to the west of the southern bank. ( Xeric's Talisman , Xeric's Inferno) ( 1 • 3 ) …
    1 Yes.
    3 I'll get going.
  • Go to the Wintertodt camp ( Games necklace teleport).
    • Talk to Ignisia in the north-east of the camp if you haven't previously done so.
  • Talk to the dwarf Undor inside the camp. ( 4 ) …
    4 I've been sent to help you.
    • If he tells you to talk to the "Fiery lass" Talk to Ignisia just to the north east, before returning to him.

The Hammer [ edit | edit source ]

Powergrid [ edit | edit source ]

  • Go to The Forsaken Tower to the east of Mount Karuulm , west of Lovakengj (north-east of fairy ring C I R , Battlefront Teleport or Skills necklace to Farming Guild).
  • Inspect the Display Case.
  • Go down the ladder behind the Display Case and open the north-eastern door.
  • Search the north-north-east crate for a generator crank .
  • Inspect the steam generator in the south. ( 1 ) …
    1 Start the generator.
    • Start the generator.
  • Inspect the Power Grid. ( 1 ) …
    1 Yes.
    • Align the Power Grid as shown below.

Furnace [ edit | edit source ]

  • Go up the ladder and then into the northern room. Search the cupboards for a tinderbox , 5-gallon jug , and 8-gallon jug .
    • Fill the 5-gallon jug with coolant through the coolant dispenser in the west.
    • Use the 5-gallon jug on the empty 8 gallon jug.
    • Fill the 5-gallon jug with coolant.
    • Use the 5-gallon jug on the 8 gallon jug.
    • Empty the 8 gallon jug (right-click check).
    • Use the 5-gallon jug on the empty 8 gallon jug (there should be 2 gallons in the jug).
    • Fill the 5-gallon jug with coolant.
    • Use the 5-gallon jug on the 8 gallon jug.
    • Fill the 5-gallon jug with coolant.
    • Use the 5-gallon jug on the 8 gallon jug.
    • You should now have 4 gallons of coolant in the 5-gallon jug. Use it on the furnace coolant west of the furnace, then light the furnace with the tinderbox.
  • If doing the diary task, smelt the adamantite bar now.

Potion [ edit | edit source ]

  • Go upstairs.
  • Open the door, then take the notes from the eastern cupboard.
  • Take the cleansing fluid from the northern table. The position will be random for everyone. Look at the notes to see the riddle: the fluids are listed in order of 1-2-3-4-5 (e.g. if cleansing fluid is the second potion mentioned in the fourth sentence, it is number 5).
    • Warning : If you choose the wrong potion, you will take high damage (25+) and become poisoned. This can be avoided by logging out before advancing the dialogue.
  • Use the cleansing fluid on the refinery to the south, then activate it ( 1 • 1 ) …
    1 Yes.
    1 Yes.

Altar [ edit | edit source ]

  • Go up the ladder.
  • There are four discs. In the following order, remove a disc from a pylon and place it on another pylon:
    • From West (W) to East (E)
    • From W to Centre (C)
    • From E to C
    • From W to E
    • From C to W
    • From C to E
    • From W to E
    • From W to C
    • From E to C
    • From E to W
    • From C to W
    • From E to C
    • From W to E
    • From W to C
    • From E to C
  • Go downstairs and get Dinh's hammer from the display case.

Finishing up [ edit | edit source ]

  • Return to Wintertodt Camp .
  • Use the hammer on Undor .
  • Return to Lady Vulcana Lovakengj .
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 1 Quest point
  • 500 Mining experience
  • 500 Smithing experience
  • 6,000 coins
  • Free use of the Lovakengj Minecart Network
  • A page for Kharedst's memoirs , ' Jewellery of jubilation .' It teleports players north-east of the bank in Lovakengj.
  • Ability to recolour the graceful outfit to the colours of Lovakengj .
