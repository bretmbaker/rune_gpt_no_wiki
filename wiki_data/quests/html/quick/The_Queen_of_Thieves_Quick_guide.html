Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Speak to Tomas Lawry outside the Port Piscarilius bank. Show on map
Official difficulty Intermediate
Official length Very Short
Description The gangs of Kourend have recently been uniting under a mysterious figure called the Queen of Thieves, somewhere in Port Piscarilius. This novice quest tasks the player with infiltrating the gangs of Kourend in order to find the Queen of Thieves.
Requirements
  • Completion of the following quests:
    • Client of Kourend
      • X Marks the Spot
  • 20 Thieving ( not boostable ) ( required to start )
Items required
  • Stew (Cooking ingredients can be obtained during quest for 6 coins . Requires 25 cooking )
Recommended
  • Stamina potion
  • Coins
  • Kourend Castle Teleport
Enemies to defeat None
League notes

Unlocking Kourend ✓ will auto-complete the quest

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

The locations of various NPCs during the quest.
The location of Councillor Hughes ' house.
  • Speak to Tomas Lawry outside the Port Piscarilius bank. ( 2 • 1 • 1 ) …
    2 I'm looking for a quest.
    1 Absolutely.
    1 Yes.
  • Head south and speak to Robert O'Reilly in the house directly west of the bridge. He will ask for a stew . ( 1 ) …
    1 Okay.
    A stew can be made with raw beef and a potato purchased from Kenelme directly west along with a bowl from the general store south-east.
  • Enter the manhole to the east marked by the icon, cross the bridge a bit to the south, continue west.
  • Speak to Devan Rutter , near the fish stall. ( 1 ) …
    1 Nope, sounds good to me.
  • Optional: Trade the General Store Shop Keeper in The Warrens for an easy Kourend & Kebos Diary task.
  • Return to the surface from the way you entered and murder Conrad King (non-combat) on the dock on the southern side of the Piscarilius Foodhall (the largest building). Pick either option.
  • Go back down the manhole and enter the tent.
  • Speak to the Queen of Thieves.
  • Exit the sewers and travel south-west to Kingstown and enter the house immediately north from the entrance (south of the icon), and climb the stairs to the 1 st floor [ UK ] 2 nd floor [ US ] .
  • Picklock the chest in the east side of the room to find a letter . (You may have to click the lock multiple times)
  • Return to Tomas Lawry . ( 1 ) …
    1 Let's talk about my quest.
  • Return to The Queen of Thieves (now called Lady Shauna Piscarilius ).
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 1 Quest point
  • 2,000 Thieving experience
  • 2,000 coins
  • A page for Kharedst's memoirs , ' The fisher's flute .' It teleports players to the centre of Port Piscarilius.
  • Ability to recolour the graceful outfit to the colours of Port Piscarilius .
