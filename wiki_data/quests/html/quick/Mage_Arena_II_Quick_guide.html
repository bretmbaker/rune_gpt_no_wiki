Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Speak to Kolodion at the Mage Arena bank located in level 53 Wilderness . Show on map
Official difficulty Master
Official length Medium
Description Can you defeat the followers of the gods?
Requirements
  • Completion of Mage Arena I
  • 75 Magic ( not boostable ) ( required to start )
  • Cast Claws of Guthix , Flames of Zamorak , and Saradomin Strike each 100 times inside the Mage Arena.
Items required
  • God staves
  • Runes to cast god spells ( air , fire , and blood )
  • Item to cut webs ( knife , slash weapon or any Wilderness sword )
Recommended
  • 80 Combat level
Enemies to defeat Porazdir (level 235), Justiciar Zachariah (level 348), and Derwen (level 235)
Ironman concerns
Warning: This quest requires travel to the deep, level 56 Wilderness, and can require travel through various multi-combat Wilderness areas as well. Beware of Player-killers at all times.
League notes Wilderness ✓ - location requirement

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

Beware: Caution must be taken in the Wilderness getting to the arena and during large parts of the miniquest where player killers can attack you.

Starting out [ edit | edit source ]

Items needed: Item to cut web outside of Mage Arena bank, such as knife , slash weapon or any Wilderness sword .
  • Talk to Kolodion at the Mage Arena bank located in level 53 Wilderness to receive an enchanted symbol . ( 3 • 1 ) …
    3 Are there any more challenges available?
    1 Great, I've been waiting for an improvement!

Locating the followers [ edit | edit source ]

A map displaying the general locations in which the followers will spawn.
Items needed: Enchanted symbol , energy potions such as stamina , food, item to cut web
Items recommended: Teleport item such as amulet of glory , an antipoison .
  • Use the enchanted symbol in various areas in the Wilderness by process of " hot & cold ", to find the location a follower spawns in. The location is random and different for each player, and changes occasionally.

Killing the followers [ edit | edit source ]

Items needed: Enchanted symbol , god staff , runes for 50+ casts of a god spell ( blood runes , fire runes , air runes ), food, restore potions , energy potions such as stamina , item to cut web
Items recommended: Teleport item such as amulet of glory , an antipoison potion if near poison spiders .
  • Bring the appropriate god staff for the first follower you choose to kill and enough runes for at least 50 casts of the respective god spell .
  • Go to the location of the follower you choose to kill and use the enchanted symbol to spawn the follower.
  • Kill the follower that spawns and pick up the remains it drops.
  • Go to Kolodion and give him the remains. This can be done one at a time or all at once.
  • At the bank, refill your runes and supplies and switch to the god staff that matches your next follower. Repeat the above for the remaining two followers, going to Kolodion after each kill to give the dropped remains.
  • Congratulations, miniquest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

One of the three imbued god capes , by bringing the god cape you want to imbue to Kolodion .

After the quest, players can obtain more imbued god capes by bringing the respective component of the god cape that they wish to imbue to Kolodion by killing the respective follower:

  • Justiciar's hand for imbued saradomin cape
  • Ent's roots for imbued guthix cape
  • Demon's heart for imbued zamorak cape
