Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Speak to Doric in the small house north of Falador , just outside the east gate of Taverley . Show on map
Official difficulty Novice
Official length Very Short
Description Doric the dwarf is happy to let you use his anvils but first he would like you to run an errand for him.
Requirements None
Items required
  • 6 clay (not soft clay )
  • 4 copper ores
  • 2 iron ores
Recommended
  • Pickaxe and 15 Mining ( boostable ) if you wish to mine the ores yourself ( bronze pickaxe obtained during the quest)
Enemies to defeat None
Ironman concerns
  • 15 Mining ( boostable ) , OR
  • The ability to kill monsters such as Flesh Crawlers , Mind / Body Golems , or other monsters for their iron ore drops.
League notes

Asgarnia ✓ — location requirement
Unlocking Desert ✓ will auto-complete the quest
Unlocking Fremennik ✓ will auto-complete the quest

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

The quest's start location.
  • Obtain 2 iron ores , 4 copper ores , and 6 clay un-noted from either of the following methods:
    • Buy from the Grand Exchange for: 1,002
    • Head to the Dwarven Mine on the south side of the Ice mountain (east from the quest start) with a pickaxe (Doric will give you a bronze pickaxe at the start of the quest):
      • 2 iron ores and 4 copper ores can be mined at a spot just south-east of the ladder down into the mine.
      • 6 clay can be mined south of the iron and copper, near the Agility shortcut .
      • Players with very low combat levels should be wary of the aggressive Scorpions and King Scorpions . Bring food if required.
  • Talk to Doric with the un-noted items in your inventory. ( 1 • 1 ) …
    1 I wanted to use your anvils.
    1 Yes, I will get you the materials.
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 1 Quest point
  • 1,300 Mining experience
  • 180 coins
  • The ability to use Doric 's anvils
  • Bronze pickaxe given at the start of the quest
