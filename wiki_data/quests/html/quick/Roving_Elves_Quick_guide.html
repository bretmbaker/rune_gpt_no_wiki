Roving Elves/Quick guide
This quest has an in-depth guide .
It contains a more detailed description of dialogue, cutscenes, and storyline.

Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Talk to Islwyn inside the elven woods of Isafdar . Show on map
Official difficulty Experienced
Official length Short
Description Deep in the elven forests of Isafdar, live a duo of roving elves: Islwyn a grumpy, old elf who dislikes humans and his companion, Eluned , who is a beautiful, friendly, young female elf with a kind heart. In this quest, you are challenged to overcome Islwyn's prejudice against humans and alleviate the burdens of the past.
Requirements
  • Completion of the following quests:
    • Regicide
      • Underground Pass
        • Biohazard
          • Plague City
    • Waterfall Quest
  • The ability to defeat a level 84 enemy unarmed, with heavy restrictions
  • 56 Agility ( boostable ) ( not required to start ) , unless travelling with a charter ship to the elven woods
Items required
  • Glarial's pebble (can be obtained during the quest)
  • Key (can be obtained during the quest)
  • Spade
  • Rope
Recommended
  • 60 Combat level
  • The requirements to start Mourning's End Part I directly after this quest, to obtain the teleport crystal
    • 60 Ranged ( not boostable )
    • 50 Thieving ( not boostable )
    • Completion of Sheep Herder and Big Chompy Bird Hunting
  • Antipoison and food
  • Ring of recoil , goblin paint cannon , prayer potions (for players with 43 Prayer to sustain Protect from Melee ) and a super combat potion in the fight against the Moss Guardian .
  • Fast travel to the Waterfall Dungeon and Glarial's Tomb ( Games necklace or minigame teleport to the Barbarian Outpost, or skills necklace to the Fishing Guild)
  • Fast travel to Islwyn and Eluned ( Iorwerth camp teleport or charter ship to Port Tyras)
  • Ring of Charos (a) to reduce charter ship costs, if travelling with them
Enemies to defeat
  • Moss Guardian (level 84) (has to be killed with restricted items , can be flinched )
Ironman concerns
You must briefly visit Glarial's Tomb which has significant item restrictions . Items dropped outside the tomb may be picked back up within three minutes.
League notes

Unlocking Tirannwn ✓ will auto-complete the quest

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

Tracking Islwyn [ edit | edit source ]

Items recommended: teleport to Ardougne and either: ring of charos(a) & 1600gp or 3200gp for each trip.
  • Head to Tirannwn ( Catherby teleport (tablet) , use charter to Port Tyras )
  • Track down Islwyn , who will be either west of Lletya or north-east of the Tyras Camp . Use this map to assist you. (If the elves are not there, switch worlds until they appear)
  • Offer your help to Islwyn. ( 1 • 1 ) …
    1 Tell the truth?
    1 Maybe I could help.
  • Talk to Eluned .

Obtaining the Seed [ edit | edit source ]

Items needed: Glarial's pebble
Items recommended: skills necklace , food, prayer potions , ring of recoil
  • Bank all your combat equipment, runes included.
    • Go to Tree Gnome Village dungeon and retrieve Glarial's pebble if you didn't save one from Waterfall Quest.
  • Travel north-west of the Fishing Guild and use Glarial's pebble on Glarial's tombstone .
  • Kill a Moss Guardian ( level 84 ). It will drop a consecration seed .
  • Take the seed to Eluned.

Planting The Seed [ edit | edit source ]

Items needed: rope, spade, consecration seed
Items recommended: games necklace (to Barbarian Outpost ), Ardougne teleport.
  • Enter the Waterfall Dungeon .
    • Board the log raft north of Baxtorian Falls .
    • Use a rope on the rock.
    • Use a rope on the dead tree.
    • Open the door to enter.
  • (Skip this step if you already have the key .) Search the most northern crates in the east room for a key.
  • Take the west path all the way north.
  • Left click the seed in the final room to plant it.
  • Return to Islwyn to choose your reward.
  • Congratulations, quest complete!
    • Optional: Speak to Eluned to start Mourning's End Part I, which will allow you to receive the teleport crystal to Lletya.

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 1 Quest point
  • 10,000 Strength experience
  • Crystal shield or bow with 500 charges

Unlocks:

  • Moss Guardian accessible in the Nightmare Zone .

Required for completing [ edit | edit source ]

  • Mourning's End Part I
