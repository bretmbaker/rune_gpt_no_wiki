Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Talk to Emelio at his home in Outer Fortis . Show on map
Official difficulty Experienced
Official length Short
Description Bring the joy of kebabs to the land of Varlamore.
Requirements
  • Completion of the following quests:
    • Children of the Sun
Items required None
Recommended
  • 60 Combat level
  • 43+ Prayer to use Protect from Melee and Protect from Magic
  • Stamina potions or energy potions
  • Combat equipment
  • Food
Enemies to defeat
  • Dire Wolf Alpha (level 113)
  • Minotaur (level 193)
League notes

Varlamore ✓ — location requirement

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

  • Talk to Emelio in south-east Outer Fortis ( 1 ) …
    1 Yes.
  • Talk to the Spice Merchant in the Bazaar about missing delivery ( 2 ) …
    2 I'm here about a missing delivery.
  • Open the box using the code 2546 .
  • Talk to Alba at the north of Ortus Farm just outside the western entryway of the city. In the building north of the Buffalo pen.
  • Go west of the farm to the wolf den and inside the cave and kill the Dire Wolf Alpha . Bring food, as this fight takes place in a multicombat area with both melee and magic damage being dealt simultaneously.
  • Return to Alba.
  • Return to Emelio
  • Talk to Emelio again and give him these numbers:
    • 4 portions of meat
    • 2 portions of salad
    • 1 portion of spice
    • 3 portions of sauce
  • Give the test kebab to Vincens , Renata or Lucas outside.
  • Talk to Emelio again.
  • Talk to Lelia inside the Fortis Colosseum . She can be found in the southern room.
  • Fight and defeat the Minotaur .
    • Protect from Melee. When he says "Moo!" switch to Protect from Magic.
    • When he says "Moooo!" , he will attack 1 tick faster.
    • If you die or escape, talk to Lelia to re-enter the fight.
    • Occasionally, the Minotaur will yell "moo" and attack with Melee.
  • Talk to Lelia after defeating the minotaur.
  • Talk to Emelio to finish the quest.

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 1 Quest point
  • 8,000 Cooking experience
  • Access to Emelio's Kebab Shop
