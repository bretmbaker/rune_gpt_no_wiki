Priest in Peril/Quick guide
This quest has an in-depth guide .
It contains a more detailed description of dialogue, cutscenes, and storyline.

Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Speak to King Roald in the Varrock Palace . Show on map
Official difficulty Novice
Official length Short
Description Recently contact has been lost with the temple to Saradomin on Misthalins' Eastern border. King Roald would be very interested to know what has caused this lack of communication, and seeks an adventurer willing to report back to him on the situation.
Requirements None
Items required
  • 50 unnoted rune essence or pure essence (can be mined by talking to Aubury in Varrock , after completing Rune Mysteries . All 50 essence does not have to be of the same type.)
  • Bucket (spawns above the Varrock Castle's kitchen in the north-easternmost room (go to the kitchen, and climb up the stairs))
Recommended
  • 15 Combat level
  • Essence pouches - optional, but helps carry more essence.
  • Food (recommended for low levels)
  • 3 Varrock teleports
  • Stamina potions or (super) energy potions
Enemies to defeat
  • Temple guardian dog (level 30) You cannot use Magic . A Ring of recoil will not register that you killed the Temple guardian without inflicting at least 1 damage yourself.
  • Monk of Zamorak (level 30). A Ring of recoil will not allow key drops without inflicting at least 1 damage yourself.
League notes

Unlocking Misthalin ✓ will auto-complete the quest

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

Starting out [ edit | edit source ]

  • Speak to King Roald . ( 1 • 1 • 3 ) …
    1 I'm looking for a quest!
    1 Yes.
    3 I'll get going.
    (#1 on map)
  • Bring a ranged or melee weapon for the next part, to kill the Temple Guardian .
    • Magic attacks and the Dwarf Multicannon will not work.
  • Head to the temple east of Varrock by the river. (#2 on map)
  • Click on the large door. ( 1 • 1 ) …
    1 Roald sent me to check on Drezel.
    1 Sure. I'm a helpful person!
  • Climb down the trapdoor just to the north. ( 1 ) …
    1 Yes.
    (#3 on map)
  • Kill the Temple Guardian . Players may safespot it by getting it stuck in the middle section of the room against the east wall and standing on the east wall one section north.

The vampyre [ edit | edit source ]

  • Go back and speak to King Roald . (#1 on map)
  • Take 25 unnoted pure or rune essence , a bucket , a weapon and 1 free inventory slot.
    • As a low level, you may need to make more than two trips if you will be taking food, although the Monk can be safespotted over the pews using ranged or magic.
  • Enter the temple again and proceed up the stairs, then the ladder until you reach the 2 nd floor [ UK ] 3 rd floor [ US ] . Talk to Drezel . ( 2 • 1 ) …
    2 So, what now?
    1 Yes, of course.
  • Kill the level 30 Monk of Zamorak on the 1 st floor [ UK ] 2 nd floor [ US ] to obtain a golden key . (#2 on map)
  • Go back to the trapdoor, climb down, and go through the first door. (#3 on map)
  • Study the monuments until you find one with a key.
  • Use the golden key on the monument.
  • Use the bucket on the well in the middle of the monuments.
  • Go back to the top floor of the temple where Drezel is located. (#2 on map)
  • Use the Iron key on the cell door and open it.
  • Talk to Drezel .
  • Use the blessed bucket of water on the coffin .

The River Salve [ edit | edit source ]

  • Talk to Drezel again. (#2 on map)
  • Go back down the trapdoor, through the monument room, and proceed through the eastern door. Talk to Drezel . (#3 on map)
  • Give him 50 unnoted essence (this will take a few trips without using pouches ).
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 1 Quest point
  • 1,406 Prayer experience
  • Wolfbane dagger , prevents the citizens of Canifis from transforming into werewolves when attacked
  • Route to Canifis and Morytania
  • Completing this quest awards the player with 5 Kudos which can be claimed by talking to Historian Minas in the Varrock Museum .

Required for completing [ edit | edit source ]

Completion of Priest in Peril is required for the following:

  • Animal Magnetism
  • Creature of Fenkenstrain
  • Desert Treasure I
  • Dragon Slayer II
  • Ghosts Ahoy
  • Haunted Mine
  • Making History
  • Nature Spirit
  • Recipe for Disaster – Freeing the Lumbridge Guide
  • Shades of Mort'ton
  • Wanted!
