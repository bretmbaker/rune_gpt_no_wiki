In Aid of the Myreque/Quick guide
This quest has an in-depth guide .
It contains a more detailed description of dialogue, cutscenes, and storyline.

Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Go to Canifis and down the ladder behind the pub there, through the secret wall and squeeze past the stalagmite on the east side of the cave. Speak to Veliaf Hurtz . Show on map
Official difficulty Intermediate
Official length Medium
Description After the attack on the Myreque by Vanstrom Klause and with the deaths of both Sani and Harold, Veliaf is counting on you to help. Now that the Myreque freedom fighters' base camp has been revealed, they'll need to move on and set up base somewhere else. Veliaf has heard of a little known village south of Mort'ton, referred to as 'Burgh de Rott'. This could be just the place to set up operations to strike back against the evil of Lowerniel Vergidiyad Drakan and his Vampyric overlordship in Morytania. And who knows what other secrets about Morytania and its dark past will be revealed?

  • 25 Agility ( boostable )
  • 25 Crafting ( not boostable )
  • 15 Mining ( not boostable )
  • 7 Magic ( boostable )

    • A blessed silver sickle (obtained during Nature Spirit )
    • A normal silver sickle
    • A blessed axe (obtained during Animal Magnetism )
    • A wolfbane dagger (obtained during Priest in Peril , which is a prerequisite for this quest)
    • Silver- , Dark- , Arclight or Emberlight
    • Any weapon whilst wearing an Efaritay's aid
  • Food
  • A spade
  • A bucket (up to 5 recommended)
  • Any pickaxe
  • A hammer
  • 11 planks (Only regular planks will work.)
  • 44 nails (42 are obtainable during the quest)
  • Swamp paste
  • 10 raw mackerel or 10 Snail meats (depending on what is asked)
  • 10 bronze axes
  • 4 tinderboxes
  • 2 steel bars
  • Coal
  • Soft clay
  • Rope
  • A silver bar
  • A mithril bar
  • A sapphire
  • On the standard spellbook for Lvl-1 Enchant :
    • A cosmic rune
    • A water rune
  • Optional to give to Ivan Strom ( Strongly recommended if you don't have a high combat level 85+):
    • A steel med helm
    • A steel chainbody
    • Steel platelegs
    • A silver sickle
    • 15 cooked salmon , snails , slimy eels , or stew
Recommended
  • 45 Combat level
  • Completion of Shades of Mort'ton for access to the Shades of Mort'ton minigame teleport.
  • Partial completion of Fairytale II - Cure a Queen for quick access to Canifis via Fairy rings (code C K S ).
  • 50 Agility ( boostable ) to use the B I P fairy ring, which skips the swamp and most ghasts.
  • A dramen staff
  • A slayer ring
  • Several Mort'ton teleport scrolls/ Barrows teleport tablets
  • Efaritay's aid (allow you to harm vampyres with any weapon )
  • A big fishing net and 16 Fishing (in case you are asked to supply mackerel )
  • Amulet of glory to teleport to Edgeville furnace
Enemies to defeat
  • Gadderanks (level 35)
  • 2 Vampyre Juvinates (level 54)
  • 2 Vampyre Juvinates (level 75) or 4 level 50s depending on route taken
Ironman concerns
  • 50 Smithing ( boostable ) or obtaining Mithril bar as a drop.

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

Starting out [ edit | edit source ]

  • Climb down the trapdoor behind the Canifis pub .
  • Search the wall and enter the cave entrance just south-east.
  • Talk to Veliaf Hurtz . ( 1 ) …
    1 I want to join your organisation.

Patching up Burgh De Rott [ edit | edit source ]

Items needed: At least 1 piece of food you don't mind losing (5 pieces is recommended if running past ghasts without a druid pouch ), a pickaxe , a spade , 1-5 buckets , a hammer , 44 nails , 11 planks , 1 swamp paste , 3 tinderboxes, 10 bronze axes, 10 raw mackerels or 10 snails (depending on what is asked).
If Shades of Mort'ton has been completed, a hammer, planks, and swamp paste can be purchased from Razmire Builders Merchants in Mort'ton and the tinderboxes, axes, and mackerels/snails can be brought noted then sold to Razmire General Store to unnote them. If using this trick make sure you bring extra gold for the buy back process and to do it fast as the quantities deteriorate if you do not. You cannot use the general store trick as an ironman.
Items recommended: Mort'ton teleport or fairy ring B I P
  • Talk to Florin through the gate to Burgh de Rott .
  • Put a piece of food into the open chest , then open the gate.
  • Talk to Razvan near the pub. ( 4 ) …
    4 Are there any 'out of the way' places in here?
  • Enter the pub, climb over the broken wall , then use your pickaxe on the rubble blocking the trapdoor .
  • Climb down and mine the rest of the rubble . Anything you drop in the basement will be lost upon leaving.
  • Use your spade on the remnants to fill the buckets. There is enough here to fill five buckets.
    • Upon mining the rubble, you will find a plaster fragment and a dusty scroll .
    • The items dug up can be safely dropped, unless you need the nails .
  • When finished, use the buckets of rubble on the rubble pile outside the pub.
  • Talk to Razvan again. ( 3 ) …
    3 I'd like to help fix up the town.
  • Talk to Aurel in the general store to the north-east. ( 3 ) …
    3 I'd like to help fix up the town.
  • Climb up the ladder right outside.
  • Use 3 planks and 12 nails on the broken roof of the store.
  • Climb back down and Use 3 planks and 12 nails on the damaged wall of the general store.
  • Talk to Aurel again to get a crate . ( 3 ) …
    3 What should I do now?
    • Tip: If you are asked for items you aren't satisfied with before receiving the crate, you can disrupt the dialogue by walking away to re-roll the requested items.
    • If you weren't able to bring all of the items, head to a bank now to get the remaining items for this section.
  • Add 10 bronze axes , 10 raw mackerels or 10 snails (depending on what is asked), and 3 tinderboxes to the crate.
  • Talk to Aurel.
  • Talk to Cornelius in the bank. ( 4 ) …
    4 What should I do now?
  • Use a hammer with 2 planks, 1 swamp paste, and 8 nails on the broken bank booth
  • Use a hammer with 3 planks and 12 nails on the damaged wall outside.
  • Talk to Cornelius to recruit him. ( 4 ) …
    4

You now have access to the Burgh de Rott bank.

Defending the village [ edit | edit source ]

Items needed: Hammer , tinderbox , 2 steel bars , 1 coal , Efaritay's aid or a silver weaponry such as a silver sickle (b) , and some food for lower-levelled players.
  • Talk to Razvan . ( 4 ) …
    4 What should I do now?
  • Repair the furnace to the south-east. Use your steel bars, then coal, then tinderbox on the furnace.
  • Talk to Gadderanks in the general store.
  • Talk to the Vampyre Juvinate in the store.
  • Talk to Wiskit .
  • Kill Gadderanks and the 2 juvinates (you'll need a silver weapon or Wolfbane dagger or Efaritay's aid to damage them).
  • Talk to Gadderanks.
  • Talk to Veliaf Hurtz.

Transporting Ivan [ edit | edit source ]

Items needed: Combat equipment, including silver weaponry or Efaritay's aid to defeat 2 level 75 or 4 level 50 Vampyre Juvinates. Deposit any followers you have into your bank as Ivan Strom will refuse to leave if you have one, even in your inventory.
Items recommended: To give to Ivan Strom: Steel med helm , Steel chainbody , Steel platelegs , Silver sickle , 15 pieces of cooked food ( snail , salmon , slimy eel , or stew )
  • Talk to Veliaf Hurtz in the hideout where the quest started. (The room under the Canifis pub)
  • Talk to Polmafi Ferdygris .
    • Highly Recommended : Give Ivan Strom some food (he only takes snail, salmon, slimy eel, or stew) and steel armour (he only takes a steel med helm, steel chainbody, and steel platelegs) before leaving.
This fight takes place in an instance .
On death , all unprotected items will be moved to a grave outside the instance. Any items left on the ground will be lost.
  • Talk to Ivan Strom to go on a simplified version of Temple Trekking and take either the short or long route. (you can't bring a pet - it must be banked!)
    • Route 1 features two level 75 Vampyre Juvinates , whereas route 2 features 4 level 50 Juvinates.
  • Kill the Vampyre Juvinates before they can kill Ivan Strom.
    • Tip: Alternate your attacks between them so they stay focused on you. Also, it may be possible to talk to Ivan to prevent the vampyre from attacking him
  • After you're done escorting Ivan, talk to Drezel in the underground temple for a key. ( 1 • 1 • 2 • 3 • 4 • 3 ) …
    1 Veliaf told me about Ivandis.
    1 So, you're sure that Ivandis is buried here then?
    2 What proof do you have that Ivandis is buried here?
    3 What do you know about the history of this temple?
    4 Is there somewhere that I might get more information about Ivandis?
    3 The lives of those pitiful few left in Morytania could rest on this!
  • Use the key on the keyhole on the eastern wall and climb down the trapdoor .
  • Search the bookcases in the hidden room until you find The sleeping seven (The title of the book at the top when reading it is "The Sleeping Seven"). Read it.

The rod of Ivandis [ edit | edit source ]

Items needed: Hammer , soft clay , mithril bar , silver bar , a water rune or staff, a cosmic rune , a cut sapphire , and a rope .
Items recommended: Teleports to a furnace and back to Burgh de Rott
  • Return to the room under the Canifis pub, then go to the blockaded cave entrance just south-east of the wall.
  • Use a hammer on the wooden boards and enter the cave.
  • Use your soft clay on the coffin to make a rod mould.
  • Go to any furnace with your mould, a silver bar, a mithril bar and a sapphire, and craft a silvthrill rod . (Fairy ring D K R for Edgeville Furnace .)
  • Cast Lvl-1 Enchant on the rod.
  • Use the rod on the well underneath the Paterdomus Temple (where Drezel was) with a rope in your inventory. (Nearest fairy ring C K S )
  • Return to the Burgh de Rott pub basement with the Rod of Ivandis (it must be in your inventory, not equipped), then speak to Veliaf. ( 1 • 1 ) …
    1 I have brought you the Rod of Ivandis!
    1 Yes, I've come to give the Rod of Ivandis to you!
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 2 Quest points
  • 2,000 Attack experience
  • 2,000 Strength experience
  • 2,000 Crafting experience
  • 2,000 Defence experience
  • Access to Burgh de Rott
  • Access to the Temple Trekking minigame
  • A Gadderhammer from defeating Gadderanks
  • The ability to make the Rod of Ivandis
  • Completing this quest awards the player with 5 Kudos which can be claimed by talking to Historian Minas in the Varrock Museum .

Required for completing [ edit | edit source ]

Completion of In Aid of the Myreque is required for the following:

  • Darkness of Hallowvale
  • Medium tier of the Morytania Diary
