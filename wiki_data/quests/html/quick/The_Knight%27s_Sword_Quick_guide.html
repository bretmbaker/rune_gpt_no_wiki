The Knight's Sword/Quick guide
This quest has an in-depth guide .
It contains a more detailed description of dialogue, cutscenes, and storyline.

Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Speak to the squire in the courtyard of the White Knights' Castle in Falador . Show on map
Official difficulty Intermediate
Official length Short
Description Sir Vyvin's squire is in trouble. He has accidentally lost Sir Vyvin's ceremonial sword. Help him find a replacement without Sir Vyvin finding out.
Requirements
  • 10 Mining ( boostable ) ( not required to start )
  • The ability to run from level 57 ice warriors and level 53 ice giants could help.
Items required
  • Redberry pie (not half a redberry pie ), (Members can purchase one from Romily Weaklax in the Cooks' Guild )
  • 2 iron bars (4 if you want your own blurite sword ) (one spawns west of the Graveyard of Shadows in the Wilderness )
  • Blurite ore (2 if you want your own blurite sword; it is obtained/obtainable during the quest) (If you plan on doing Falador easy diary AND making a second sword, mine a third blurite ore [members only])
    • Any pickaxe (one spawns in south Falador)
Recommended
  • 20 Combat level
  • Fast travel to Varrock ( Varrock teleport )
  • Fast travel to Falador ( Falador teleport )
  • (Members only) Fast travel to Rimmington ( Pest Control minigame teleport, Fairy Rings to A I Q , Rimmington Teleport or Teleport to House if the player's house is in the village)
  • Armour and some food for aggressive enemies near the blurite rocks
Enemies to defeat None
Ironman concerns
  • 15 Smithing (not needed if obtaining iron bar from drops)
  • 15 Mining (not needed if obtaining iron bar from drops)
  • 10 Cooking (Pie dish can be found easily in the kitchen of Varrock Palace, and can be cooked there as well. Best to come here after speaking to Reldo ) or partial completion of Perilous Moons to purchase a Redberry pie from Yarnio
For ironmen to obtain the iron bars, head to the trees located in level 18 Wilderness next to the bears west of the Graveyard of Shadows. A safer alternative is to kill dwarves in the mines under Falador. For Members accounts that wish to avoid combat entirely, there is an iron bar that spawns inside Thurid's Brain Buckets . This requires completion of the Children of the Sun quest in order to gain access to Varlamore .

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

  • Talk to the Squire in the White Knights' Castle . ( 1 • 2 • 1 • 1 ) …
    1 And how is life as a squire?
    2 I can make a new sword if you like...
    1 So would these dwarves make another one?
    1 Ok, I'll give it a go.
  • Talk to Reldo in the library of Varrock Palace . ( 2 ) …
    2 . What do you know about the Imcando dwarfs?
    ( ( 4 ) …
    4 . What do you know about the Imcando dwarfs?
    if Shield of Arrav not started.) Skip this step if you already met Thurgo during The Giant Dwarf .
  • Talk to Thurgo south of Port Sarim (by the anvil, north of the fairy ring A I Q ) and give him a redberry pie . ( 2 ) …
    2 Would you like a redberry pie?
    ( ( 3 ) …
    3 Would you like a redberry pie?
    if The Giant Dwarf quest is in progress.)
  • Talk to him again. ( 1 ) …
    1 Something else.
  • Go back and talk to the Squire.
  • Climb-up the ladder on the eastern side of the castle to the 1 st floor [ UK ] 2 nd floor [ US ] . Go west and climb-up the staircase. Search the cupboard in Sir Vyvin 's bedroom for a portrait . He must not be looking at you.
  • Proceed back to Thurgo and give him the portrait . ( 1 ) …
    1 About that sword...
    Bring iron bars with you.
  • Go to the Asgarnian Ice Dungeon using the trapdoor to the east of Thurgo .
  • Once inside, follow the path west, north, and then east. Keep along the southern wall in the icy area and mine a blurite ore . Mine another if planning on doing the Falador Easy Diary .
  • Talk to Thurgo with your iron bars and blurite ore . ( 1 ) …
    1 Can you make that replacement sword now?
  • Bring the sword to the Squire.
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 1 Quest point
  • 12,725 Smithing experience
  • A blurite sword (if you got Thurgo to make you an extra one)
  • The ability to smelt blurite ore into blurite bars (members only)
