Troll Stronghold/Quick guide
This quest has an in-depth guide .
It contains a more detailed description of dialogue, cutscenes, and storyline.

Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Talk to Denulth in Burthorpe . Show on map
Official difficulty Intermediate
Official length Short
Description The Imperial Guard raid was a failure, and Dunstan's son has been captured by the trolls! Journey through Trollheim to the Troll Stronghold, and rescue him!
Requirements
  • Completion of Death Plateau
  • 15 Agility ( boostable ) ( not required to start ) (47 is recommended)
  • The ability to defeat a level 113 Troll General (can be safespotted )
Items required
  • Climbing boots or 12 coins to buy them
Recommended
  • 50 Combat level
  • Weapon capable of safespotting ( Ranged , Magic , or a Halberd )
  • Armour and some food
  • 43 Prayer for Protect from Melee
  • At least 30 Thieving ( boostable ) to prevent a fight with two level 71 guards (higher levels are recommended for higher chance of success)
  • Games necklace for Burthorpe teleports
  • Stamina or energy potions
Enemies to defeat
  • Dad (level 101) (can be safespotted)
  • Troll General (level 113) (can be safespotted)
  • Berry and Twig (both level 71) (can be safespotted) Only if you have less than 30 Thieving or fail to pickpocket them (can be safespotted)
League notes

Unlocking Asgarnia ✓ will auto-complete the quest
Unlocking Desert ✓ will auto-complete the quest
Unlocking Fremennik ✓ will auto-complete the quest
Unlocking Kandarin ✓ will auto-complete the quest

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

Starting out [ edit | edit source ]

  • Speak to Denulth directly South of the Burthorpe Teleport. ( 1 • 1 • 1 ) …
    1 How goes your fight with the trolls?
    1 Is there anything I can do to help?
    1 I'll get Godric back!
  • Follow the path to the stronghold. You can buy the climbing boots along the way from Tenzing . (Note: If you have completed The Easy Combat Achievements you can skip this section, including the fight with Dad, by teleporting with Ghommal's hilt 1 )
  • Enter the arena and attempt to kill Dad . The fight will end before Dad is killed (can be safespotted from the gate; magic or a halberd are good choices due to his high range defence).
  • After nearly killing Dad exit the arena through the north gate.
  • Go through the cave and continue the path to the stronghold; Thrower trolls can be dealt with by:
    • Sprinting past them.
    • Protecting from Missiles against them.
    • Taking agility shortcuts around them.
  • Enter the stronghold through the cave entrance.

Stronghold Dungeon [ edit | edit source ]

  • Follow the red path to Troll General . Kill one and loot the prison key . (Can be safespotted with a halberd, magic, or a ranged weapon behind the counter) (Hardcore Ironmen beware! The general is aggressive and his max hit is 38)
  • Follow the green path to jail.
  • Pickpocket (30+ ) or kill the sleeping Twig and Berry (this can be safespotted through the open cell doors either side of them), free both of the prisoners.
  • Teleport to Burthorpe with the games necklace OR follow them out the secret exit and once outside climb over the south-east rocks.
  • Talk to Dunstan .
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 1 Quest point
  • Law talisman (if you lose it, you can get another from Dunstan for 1,000 coins )
  • Law talismans can be obtained via Guardians of the Rift
  • Ability to make law runes
  • Access to Trollheim , the Troll Stronghold and the Law Altar
  • Ability to access the God Wars Dungeon (though it can be accessed without the quest via the Ghommal's Hilt teleport)

Unlocks:

  • Dad accessible in the Nightmare Zone

Required for completing [ edit | edit source ]

  • Requirement for Desert Treasure I
  • Requirement for Devious Minds
  • Requirement for Eadgar's Ruse
  • Requirement for Monkey Madness II
  • Requirement for Troll Romance
  • Level 15 Agility and defeating Dad are prerequisites for entry to the God Wars Dungeon
