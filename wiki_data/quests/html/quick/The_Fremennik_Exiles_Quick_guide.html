Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Speak to Brundt the Chieftain in Rellekka . Show on map
Official difficulty Master
Official length Medium
Description The Fremennik bards often sing tales of the past. Of great battles, deadly foes and heroes long gone. However, in recent years, things have been much quieter in the Province, as there's increasingly less to sing about. All that is about to change though, as what starts off as a simple investigation soon leads to one of the greatest threats the Fremennik have ever seen.

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

A disturbance in Rellekka [ edit | edit source ]

Items required: Mirror shield , 650 + if you still need 2 kegs of beer .

Recommended: a weapon to kill a level 53 Basilisk Youngling

  • Buy 2 kegs of beer from Thora in the Longhall before you start the quest to save time later. (bank them when you have the chance to reduce weight while running around)
  • Talk to Brundt in the Longhall to start the quest. ( 2 • 1 ) …
    2 Do you have any quests?
    1 Yes.
    or ( 3 • 1 ) …
    3 Do you have any quests?
    1 Yes.
  • Go north and talk to Freygerd in the house south of the sandpit.
  • Equip your mirror shield (to avoid taking damage and stat drain), then search the sandpit (to the north) and kill the Basilisk Youngling (level 53).
  • Pick up the Unsealed letter and read it.
  • Search the rockslide west of the house to get the Fang .
  • Search the boxes south of the house to get the Venom gland .
  • Talk to Freygerd to give her the items.
  • Return to Brundt to receive a note, read it, then talk to him again for a cutscene. ( 2 ) …
    2 Ask about the investigation.
    or ( 3 ) …
    3 Ask about the investigation.
  • Go south-east of Rellekka, following the dirt path, and talk to Brundt . Go through all chat options to learn how to restore V's shield . ( 1 • 1 • 2 • 3 ) …
    1 Where can I find a Fremennik Shield?
    1 Where can I get Lunar Glass?
    2 How do I make the Polishing Rock?
    3 How do I make V's Sigil?

Redeeming the exile [ edit | edit source ]

You can do the following sections in any order as long as you use them on each other while you have a glassblowing pipe .

Fremennik shield [ edit | edit source ]

Items required: Rune thrownaxe and pet rock for option 3.

Recommended : Stamina potions , food , Ring of charos (a) .

  • Obtain a Fremennik shield (this is not the tradeable Neitiznot shield ).
    • Option 1: Go to Waterbirth Island and kill dagannoth until they drop the shield (uncommon drop).
    • Option 2: Go to Rellekka and kill Market Guards or Warriors until they drop the shield (rare drop).
    • Option 3: Travel to Bardur deep in the Waterbirth Dungeon and wear a Ring of charos (a) to get the shield for free. Without the ring it will cost 150,000 .

Lunar glass and V's sigil [ edit | edit source ]

Items required : Seal of passage , molten glass , 100 astral runes , any pickaxe , hammer , and an amulet of glory for a fast teleport to a furnace.

  • Go to Baba Yaga on Lunar Isle to turn your molten glass into molten glass (i) . ( 3 ) …
    3
    or ( 4 ) …
    4
    if using lunar spellbook and Ourania teleport is not unlocked.
  • Head north-east of the town to Lunar Isle mine and mine stalagmites for 3 lunar ore .
  • Leave the mine and travel to the Astral Altar with 100 astral runes in your inventory and craft on the altar to obtain Lunar glass . Then return to Rellekka.
    • Optional: Destroy the Seal of passage and talk to the Oneiromancer to be teleported there. This guide will remind you later to re-obtain it at a convenient moment.
  • Smelt 3 lunar bars in a furnace (Rellekka is convenient) or you can use Superheat Item .
  • Smith the V sigil on an anvil (Rellekka is convenient).
  • Talk to the Fossegrimen to enchant the sigil. (Found by summoning at the Strange altar south-west of Rellekka) ( 3 ) …
    3 Ask about V's Sigil.
    .

Polishing rock [ edit | edit source ]

Items required : Pet rock , Fishing rod , V sigil (e) , Lunar glass , Ice gloves , Glassblowing pipe , Fremennik shield .

Recommended: 2 teleports to the Mountain Camp ( Slayer ring or Fairy rings access code A J R ), a teleport back to Rellekka ( Enchanted Lyre or Fremennik Sea boots ) and stamina potion

  • Head east of Rellekka to the Mountain Camp
  • Follow the path north of the Mountain Camp and inspect the Large Geyser . Use your pet rock on the Geyser. ( 1 ) …
    1 It's for the good of the Province!
  • Go to Rellekka and talk to Peer the Seer found south-west of the Rellekka market.
  • Return to the geyser and wear the ice gloves , if you forget you may re-equip them and try again.
  • Inspect the large geyser to the west to get the polishing rock .
  • Use any of the components together while having a glassblowing pipe in the inventory to create V's shield .

Basilisks in the Market [ edit | edit source ]

Items required : V's shield , combat equipment, food, 2 kegs of beer (If you forgot to buy them at the quest start, buy them from Rasolo or on the Grand Exchange . Keg of beer from the table in the Longhall does not work.) You can go ahead and bring the recommended items for the next step as well.

This fight takes place in an instance .
On death , all unprotected items will be moved to a grave outside the instance. Any items left on the ground will be lost.
  • Talk to Brundt south-east of Rellekka.
    • If you destroyed your Seal of passage to return to Rellekka quickly, now is a good time to ask for another one ( 2 ) …
      2 Ask about a Seal of Passage.
      and then talk to Brundt again.
  • Return to the Longhall and talk to Brundt again.
  • Wield V's shield and fight the basilisks until you reach 100%. You have to hit a basilisk at least once to make its death count towards completion.
    • Monstrous Basilisk (level 135) counts for 10% completion.
    • Basilisk (level 61) counts for 3.33% completion.
  • Travel on the Fremennik Boat to the Island of Stone .

Island of Stone [ edit | edit source ]

Items required : V's shield , combat equipment to fight Typhor (level 218) and The Jormungand (level 363), a one-handed weapon (must wield V's shield )

Recommended: 1-2 super restore(4) , high-healing food . Sarachnis Cudgel, Leaf-bladed battleaxe , Zombie axe , dragon mace , or a dragon warhammer are good weapon choices as the monster is weak to crush. Note that the Zombie helmet provides the highest crush boost of all helmets.

  • Speak with Brundt to explain the cave door puzzle.
  • Solve the cave door puzzle ( Mastermind ).
    • Each red check means one rune is correct but in the wrong position.
    • Each green check means one rune is correct and in the right position.
This fight takes place in an instance with a deathbank .
On death , you can pay Brundt the Chieftain 50,000 to retrieve lost items.
Any unsafe death will delete all items left inside the deathbank. Any items left on the ground will be lost.
  • Equip V's shield and keep it equipped for the full fight.
  • Enter the cave.
  • Fight Typhor (level 218)
    • Pray against either Magic or Melee , depending on which attack style you are weaker against.
  • There is a checkpoint after Typhor is defeated. You may wish to teleport out between fights to resupply. If you do, you can return to the Island of Stone by talking to Haskell on the western-most pier in Rellekka (the same one the Waterbirth Island boat is on)
  • Fight The Jormungand (level 363). There are two special attacks to avoid:
    • When the screen turns red, click to face away from the attack (hits around 40 damage and drains your stats a little).
    • When frozen in rock, click to attack repeatedly to break free (otherwise take 30+ damage as the rock explodes).
    • Pray Protect from Magic .
    • It is weak to crush attacks.
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 2 Quest points
  • V's shield
  • Access to the Island of Stone
  • Ability to kill a Basilisk Knight as a Slayer Task
  • A Fremennik honorific, Dräpare, added to the player's Fremennik name
  • Ability to create and equip the Neitiznot faceguard
  • 50,000 Slayer experience
  • 50,000 Crafting experience
  • 30,000 Runecraft experience
