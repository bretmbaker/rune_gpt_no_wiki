Animal Magnetism/Quick guide
This quest has an in-depth guide .
It contains a more detailed description of dialogue, cutscenes, and storyline.

Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Speak to Ava in Draynor Manor . Show on map
Official difficulty Intermediate
Official length Medium
Description Draynor Manor has a new resident and, adventurers rejoice, she is a damsel in distress. Far from being Sleeping Beauty, she finds the Manor's beds not to her liking and is suffering from insomniac nights. If you fancy yourself able to aid Ava in her search for a good night's sleep, who knows what the budding scientist will produce as a reward?
Requirements
  • Completion of the following quests:
    • The Restless Ghost
    • Ernest the Chicken
    • Priest in Peril
  • 18 Slayer ( not boostable ) ( required to start )
  • 19 Crafting ( not boostable ) ( required to start )
  • 30 Ranged ( not boostable ) ( required to start )
  • 35 Woodcutting ( not boostable ) ( required to start )
Items required
  • Mithril axe (required to obtain the blessed axe ; no other type of axe will do)
  • 5 iron bars
  • Ghostspeak amulet ( Morytania legs will not work)
  • 20 ecto-tokens (or 4 bones , 4 buckets and 4 pots to gain the tokens)
  • Hammer
  • Hard leather
  • Holy symbol
  • Polished buttons
Recommended
  • 10 more ecto-tokens to purchase an undead chicken that you can keep
  • Stamina or energy potion
  • Fast travel to several locations
    • Draynor Manor ( Draynor Manor Teleport or amulet of glory to Draynor Village)
    • Port Phasmatys ( Ectophial , charter ships or fairy ring code A L Q )
    • Slayer Tower ( Slayer ring , Fenkenstrain's Castle Teleport , Kharyrll Teleport , Salve Graveyard Teleport or fairy ring code C K S )
    • Burthorpe ( Games necklace , Burthorpe Games Room grouping teleport, combat bracelet to the Warriors' Guild, Taverley teleport or Teleport to House if it is in Taverley)
    • Rimmington ( Rimmington teleport , Teleport to House if it is in Rimmington, skills necklace to the Crafting Guild or Rat Pits grouping teleport)
Enemies to defeat None
Ironman concerns
  • Mithril axe ( 51 Smithing ( boostable ) ); 28 Thieving ( boostable ) for the Isle of Souls dungeon chest ; 60 Woodcutting ( boostable ) to purchase axe from Perry's Chop-chop Shop in the Woodcutting Guild ; access to fairy rings ( B K Q ) and killing tree spirits , blue dragons, or king sand crabs)
  • Holy symbol ( 31 Prayer ( not boostable ) )
League notes

Asgarnia ✓ — location requirement
Morytania ✓ — location requirement
Unlocking Fremennik ✓ will auto-complete the quest

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

Undead chickens [ edit | edit source ]

The location of the old crone's house

Items needed: A ghostspeak amulet , Ectophial , and 20 ecto-tokens (bring 4 empty pots , 4 empty buckets and 4 bones if you wish to obtain the tokens during the quest)

  • Talk to Ava in the west wing of Draynor Manor and complete the dialogue. ( 1 ) …
    1 I would be happy to make your home into a better place.
  • Go to the undead chicken farm west of the Ectofuntus with your ghostspeak amulet and ecto-tokens (or materials for them), and talk to Alice . ( 4 ) …
    4 I'm here about a quest.
  • Keep talking between Alice and Malcolm in the cow pen 4 times until she tells you to go to talk to the old crone for an amulet.
  • Run west of the farm until you reach the old crone's house just east of the Slayer Tower .
  • Speak twice to the old crone to receive a crone-made amulet . ( 2 ) …
    2 I'm here about the farmers east of here.
  • Speak with Malcolm to give him the Crone-made amulet. ( 1 ) …
    1 Okay, you need it more than I do, I suppose.
  • Speak with Malcolm again.
  • After the cutscene, speak to Malcolm who will now sell you two undead chickens for 20 ecto-tokens . ( 1 • 2 ) …
    1 Could I buy those chickens now?
    2 Could I buy two chickens?

Magnet [ edit | edit source ]

Approximate area of where to stand facing north

Items needed: 5 iron bars , a hammer , Skills necklace for teleport to Crafting Guild , and your two undead chickens .

  • Take your 5 iron bars, hammer, and two undead chickens back to the west wing of Draynor Manor and speak to Ava .
  • Speak to the Witch in the north-west corner of Draynor Manor (inside the building).
  • Speak to the Witch again to receive a selected iron .
  • Take the selected iron to the centre of the Rimmington mine , face north, and use the hammer on it.
  • Return to Ava and give her the magnet.

Undead twigs [ edit | edit source ]

Items needed: Mithril axe , polished button , hard leather , games necklace for teleport to Burthorpe , and a holy symbol

  • Talk to Ava.
  • Use your axe on an Undead tree (not Dead tree). You can exit through the door of the east room. (An Undead tree is found in front of the manor, has a yellow examine option.)
  • Talk to Ava again. She will instruct you to go to Turael with the Mithril axe and Holy Symbol to exchange them to a Blessed axe . NOTE: If you dont do this step, Turael might not have the 4th chat option available.
  • Talk to Turael, the Slayer master in a house in south-eastern Burthorpe . ( 4 ) …
    4 I'm here about a quest.
  • Talk to Turael again to receive the Blessed axe, provided you have a Mithril axe and a Holy symbol in your inventory. ( 4 • 1 ) …
    4 Hello, I'm here about those trees again.
    1 I'd love one, thanks.
  • Return to the mansion and cut the undead tree again.
  • Bring the Undead twigs to Ava.
  • Talk to Ava again, and she will give you garbled research notes that you need to solve.
    • To translate turn off all the green orbs except for the 2nd, 5th, and 9th (left to right, see image)
  • Talk to Ava again.
  • Combine the solved notes with the polished button and the hard leather.
  • Give the container to Ava.
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 1 Quest point
  • 1,000 Crafting experience
  • 1,000 Fletching experience
  • 1,000 Slayer experience
  • 2,500 Woodcutting experience
  • Ava's attractor if your Ranged level is less than 50, or Ava's accumulator if your Ranged level is 50 or higher
  • The ability to trade with Ava and purchase her devices

Required for completing [ edit | edit source ]

Completion of Animal Magnetism is required for the following:

  • Dragon Slayer II
  • Lumbridge & Draynor Medium Diary
