Plague City/Quick guide
This quest has an in-depth guide .
It contains a more detailed description of dialogue, cutscenes, and storyline.

Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Talk to Edmond , living next to the wall surrounding West Ardougne , north of Ardougne Castle . Show on map
Official difficulty Novice
Official length Very Short
Description The western part of Ardougne has been shut off as a deadly plague sweeps through the people. However, a young woman who went in to try to help those stricken by the illness has disappeared and her parents have become worried.
Requirements None
Items required
  • Dwellberries (Spawns in McGrubor's Wood , squeeze through the loose railing, use A L S , or Telekinetic Grab them. They can be bought from Heckel Funch or Hudo in the Tree Gnome Stronghold or grown with 36 Farming .)
  • Rope (Purchasable in the East Ardougne general store , Port Khazard general store , Ned , or a spawn in Karamja general store.)
  • Spade (One spawns near Edmond.)
  • 4 buckets of water (Can be used one-by-one, there is a water source and bucket spawn near Edmond)
  • Hangover cure or the ingredients to make one:
    • Chocolate dust
      • Chocolate bar
      • Knife or pestle and mortar
    • Bucket of milk (Obtained by using a bucket on a dairy cow , one is north of Ardougne , or bought from Hudo or Heckel Funch .)
    • Snape grass (Spawns west of the Crafting Guild or the peninsula north-east of Hosidius . It can be grown at 61 Farming . Can also be obtained from killing Tribesmen in Karamja .)
  • Picture of Elena (Inside Alrena and Edmond's house)
Enemies to defeat None
Ironman concerns
  • The hangover cure can be made before the quest starts by combining milk, chocolate dust, and snape grass to conserve inventory space.
  • The hangover cure itself is an untradeable item so if preparing for Group Ironman, do not premake these as you will be unable to share them with your group through Group Storage or Trading.

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

Starting the quest [ edit | edit source ]

Bring all items with you. Make the hangover cure now by putting chocolate dust in a bucket of milk , then add snape grass , being careful not to drink or eat anything.

  • Speak to Edmond . ( 1 • 1 ) …
    1 What's happened to her?
    1 Yes.
  • Speak to Alrena inside or near the house.
  • Take the Picture of Elena off the table in the house.
  • Speak to Edmond.
  • Use 4 buckets of water on the mud patch behind the house (between the cabbages and potatoes).
  • Dig with a spade on the softened ground.
  • Go south to the farthest pipe .
  • Attempt to open the grill .
  • Use a rope on the grill.
  • Speak to Edmond.
  • Put on the gas mask and climb up the pipe.

Finding Elena [ edit | edit source ]

  • Go east and speak to Jethick ; he will give you a book . ( 1 ) …
    1 Yes, I'll return it for you.
  • Go north to the house against the wall (next to the rotten apple spawn).
  • Attempt to open the door.
  • Speak to Martha Rehnison or Ted Rehnison in the house.
  • Go upstairs and speak to Milli Rehnison .
  • Go south of the town square to the large building along the southern wall with an "X" on the doors and a staircase inside going down. Attempt to open the door. ( 2 ) …
    2 I fear not a mere plague.

Getting inside [ edit | edit source ]

  • Go to the large main building on the north side of the town square, north of the manhole .
  • Talk to the Clerk in the main room. ( 1 • 3 ) …
    1 Who is through that door?
    3 This is urgent though! Someone's been kidnapped!
  • Speak to Bravek . ( 1 • 3 ) …
    1 This is really important though!
    3 Do you know what's in the cure?
    He will give you a scruffy note for a hangover cure .
  • Use the hangover cure on Bravek; he will give you a warrant . ( 3 ) …
    3 They won't listen to me!

Finishing up [ edit | edit source ]

  • Return to the Mourner house, the one with "X" on the doors and the stairwell.
  • Search the barrel west of the staircase for a small key .
  • Go downstairs, enter the cell, and speak to Elena .
  • Return to the town square, go down the manhole, through the sewer, climb the mud pile , and speak to Edmond at his house.
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 1 Quest point
  • 2,425 Mining experience
  • Ardougne teleport scroll , reading it unlocks the Ardougne Teleport with 51 Magic and use of the Ardougne teletab .

Required for completing [ edit | edit source ]

Completion of Plague City is required for the following:

  • Biohazard
  • Ardougne Diary
