Druidic Ritual/Quick guide
This quest has an in-depth guide .
It contains a more detailed description of dialogue, cutscenes, and storyline.

Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Speak to Kaqemeex in the stone circle north of Taverley . Show on map
Official difficulty Novice
Official length Very Short
Description The Druids north of Taverley need help making a potion to reclaim the stone circle south of Varrock.
Requirements None
Items required
  • Raw bear meat
  • Raw rat meat
  • Raw beef
  • Raw chicken
Recommended
  • 10 Combat level
Enemies to defeat None
League notes

Unlocking Misthalin ✓ will auto-complete the quest

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

  • Talk to Kaqemeex in the stone circle north of Taverley . ( 2 • 1 ) …
    2 I'm in search of a quest.
    1 Okay, I will try and help.
  • Talk to Sanfew , who is upstairs in the Herb shop in Taverley. ( 1 • 2 ) …
    1 I've been sent to help purify the Varrock stone circle.
    2 Ok, I'll do that then.
  • Bring the four meats to the Cauldron in Taverley Dungeon , and use them on the Cauldron to enchant them.
  • Talk to Sanfew .
  • Talk to Kaqemeex .
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Reward [ edit | edit source ]

  • 4 Quest points
  • 250 Herblore experience (Herblore Level 3. 26 Herblore XP away from Herblore Level 4)
  • The ability to use the Herblore skill
