Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Speak to Councillor Halgrive outside of the East Ardougne church. Show on map
Official difficulty Novice
Official length Short
Description Dispose of plagued sheep and save Ardougne!
Requirements None
Items required
  • 100 coins
Recommended
  • Energy restoring items
  • Ardougne Teleport for access to beginning of quest.
  • Skills Necklace or other teleport near fishing guild
Enemies to defeat None
League notes

Kandarin ✓ — location requirement
Unlocking Tirannwn ✓ will auto-complete the quest

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

Location of the sheep.
  • Talk to Councillor Halgrive outside the East Ardougne church. Ensure that you have at least 1 empty inventory slot. ( 1 • 1 ) …
    1 What's wrong?
    1 I can do that for you.
  • Talk to Doctor Orbon in the church. ( 1 ) …
    1 Okay, I'll take it.
  • Put on the plague jacket and trousers and go to the enclosure north-west of the Ardougne north bank.
  • Pick up & wield the cattleprod next to the incinerator.
  • There are sheep to the north-west (blue), north (yellow), east (green), and south (red) of the incinerator.
  • Prod one sheep of each colour through the gate.
    • Blue sheep
    • Yellow sheep
    • Green sheep
    • Red sheep
  • Feed your sheep sheep feed until they're all dead.
  • Pick up the bones and use them on the incinerator.
  • Return to Councillor Halgrive to complete the quest.
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

  • 3,100 coins
