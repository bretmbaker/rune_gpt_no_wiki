Recipe for Disaster: Freeing the Goblin Generals/Quick guide
This quest has an in-depth guide .
It contains a more detailed description of dialogue, cutscenes, and storyline.
For the main quest guide, see Recipe for Disaster/Quick guide .


Details

Start point Inspect one of the goblin generals in the Lumbridge Castle dining room.
Official difficulty Special
Official length Short
Description The goblin generals need a food that they'll both eat. Find a chef in Goblin Village to learn how to make it.
Requirements

Completion of the following quests:

  • Recipe for Disaster/Another Cook's Quest
    • Cook's Assistant
  • Goblin Diplomacy
Items required
  • Bread
  • Orange slices
    • Use a knife on an orange
  • Either blue , black , green or purple dye (purple dye spawns directly east of the spirit tree at the Khazard Battlefield , south of the Ardougne Castle )
  • Spice or Gnome spice (can be bought or stolen from the Ardougne market , bought from the Culinaromancer's Chest or bought in the Tree Gnome Stronghold )
  • Fishing bait
  • Bucket of water
  • Charcoal or Ground charcoal (buy in Shilo Village , Jiminua's Jungle Store or Gunslik's Assorted Items .)
Recommended
  • Falador teleport
  • Lumbridge teleport
  • Goblin village sphere (Requires Another Slice of H.A.M. quest)
Enemies to defeat None
League notes

Asgarnia ✓ — location requirement
Kandarin ✓ OR Morytania ✓ OR Fremennik ✓ OR Varlamore ✓ — location requirement

Walkthrough

  • Inspect one of the goblin generals in Lumbridge Castle dining room.
  • Go to the Goblin Village and climb down the ladder in the north-east building.
  • Talk to the Goblin Cook . ( 2 • 1 ) …
    2 I need your help...
    1 What do you need? Maybe I cam get it for you.
    , then talk to him again and give him the charcoal. ( 1 ) …
    1 I've got the charcoal you were after.
  • Talk to the Goblin Cook again. ( 3 ) …
    3 I need your help...
    • Slice an orange and add blue, green, or purple dye. (Doesn't matter which)
    • Use spice on a fishing bait.
    • Use a bucket of water on bread.
  • Talk to the Goblin Cook again. ( 3 ) …
    3 I've got the ingredients we need...
  • Give the slop of compromise to the generals in Lumbridge.
  • Congratulations, section complete!

Rewards

  • 1 Quest point
  • 1,000 Cooking experience
  • 1,000 Farming experience
  • 1,000 Crafting experience
  • Further access to the Culinaromancer's Chest .
