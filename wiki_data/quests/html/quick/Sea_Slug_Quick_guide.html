Sea Slug/Quick guide
This quest has an in-depth guide .
It contains a more detailed description of dialogue, cutscenes, and storyline.

Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Speak to Caroline just north of Witchaven . Show on map
Official difficulty Intermediate
Official length Short
Description Something strange is happening on the Fishing Platform .

Missing fishermen and the presence of dozens of strange sea creatures gives cause for concern.

Investigate the platform, discover the truth before it's too late.
Requirements
  • 30 Firemaking ( boostable ) ( not required to start )
Items required
  • Swamp paste (made by mixing flour with swamp tar then cooking the mixture on a fire; can also be bought from a Trader crewmember for 75 coins, from Rasolo for 60 coins west of the Fishing Guild , or from the Khazard General Store for 42 coins at full stock)
Recommended
  • Small fishing net for Ardougne Medium Diary (can be bought near quest start in Witchaven)
  • Unlit torch (a small glitch might occur where you do not receive the torch during the quest)
Enemies to defeat None
League notes

Kandarin ✓ — location requirement

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

The platform [ edit | edit source ]

  • Speak with Caroline in Witchaven . ( 1 ) …
    1 I suppose so, how do I get there?
  • Talk to Holgart (right next to Caroline) and give him the Swamp paste .
  • Right-click Holgart and select the Travel option to travel to the fishing platform.
  • Pick up the Broken glass on the floor (hut to the west).
  • Go to the north-east corner of the platform and pick up some Damp sticks .
  • Climb the ladder right next to the sticks.

Upper platform [ edit | edit source ]

  • Go inside the cabin on the west side of the platform and talk to Kennith .
  • Go back down the ladder to the sea level.

Remote island [ edit | edit source ]

  • Travel or speak with Holgart for a trip to a remote island.
  • Talk to Kent .
  • Talk to Holgart again to go back to the Fishing Platform .

Saving Kennith [ edit | edit source ]

  • Speak with Bailey in the southwest cabin for an Unlit torch .
  • Use the Broken glass on the Damp sticks to dry them.
  • Rub the Dry sticks together to light the torch.
  • Go back up the ladder.
  • Go talk to Kennith again.
  • From outside of the cabin, kick the badly repaired wall to make an opening.
  • Talk to Kennith again.
  • Just outside of Kennith's cabin, activate the crane controls to rotate it (a cutscene will play if you've followed the steps correctly - make sure you've talked to Kennith after you've kicked in the wall ).
  • Go back down the ladder to the sea level.
    • Tip: Before leaving, you can catch a fish with a small fishing net on the downstairs platform to complete a task for the Ardougne Medium Diary .

Finishing up [ edit | edit source ]

  • Speak with Holgart again to return to the shore.
  • Report to Caroline .
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 1 Quest point
  • 7,175 Fishing experience
  • Access to the Fishing Platform
  • Oyster pearls

Required for completing [ edit | edit source ]
