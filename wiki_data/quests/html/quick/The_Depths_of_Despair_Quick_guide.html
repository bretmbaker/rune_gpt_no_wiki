Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Speak to Lord Kandur Hosidius at his home in Hosidius , north-west of the Vinery . Show on map
Official difficulty Intermediate
Official length Short
Description Artur Hosidius has gone missing, and Lord Kandur Hosidius needs your help to find his son and bring him home.
Requirements
  • Completion of the following quests:
    • Client of Kourend
      • X Marks the Spot
  • 18 Agility ( not boostable ) ( required to start )
  • Ability to kill a level 36 Sand Snake
Items required None
Recommended
  • 15 Combat level
  • Skills necklace to teleport to Woodcutting Guild
  • Xeric's talisman
  • Super energy / Stamina potions
  • A weapon
  • Food (if low level)
  • An Arceuus Library Teleport
  • Dramen / Lunar staff for fairy ring teleport to Arceuus , Ardougne cloak 1 's monastery teleport brings you close to a fairy ring as well as the amulet of glory to Edgeville then running east to the grand exchange fairy ring. Alternatively, if you haven't completed the Ardougne easy diary, a Games necklace to teleport to Wintertodt.
Enemies to defeat Sand Snake (level 36)
League notes

Unlocking Kourend ✓ will auto-complete the quest

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

The start point of the quest.
  • Speak to Lord Kandur in the house west of the Vinery (Xeric's Glade teleport on talisman). ( 2 • 1 ) …
    2 Anything I can help you with?
    1 Yes.
  • Speak to Chef Olivia in Lord Kandur's kitchen.
  • Head to the Arceuus Library ( fairy ring code C I S , or games necklace to Wintertodt Camp ) and speak to Galana . (north-east wing, ground floor [ UK ] 1 st floor [ US ] )
  • Speak to Galana again. She will tell you where to find the Varlamore envoy book. This is random for everyone.
  • Read the Varlamore envoy book.
  • Enter the Crabclaw Caves south of the Woodcutting Guild marked by the icon. Kill a sandcrab for easy diary while here. (Rada's blessing, Skills necklace teleport, Xeric's Talisman to Xeric's Look-Out, or Veos ship/charter ship to Land's End )
This fight takes place in an instance .
On death , all unprotected items will be moved to a grave outside the instance. Any items left on the ground will be lost.
  • Navigate to the end of the caves and climb down the tunnel at the end.
  • Speak to Artur Hosidius .
  • Kill the Sand Snake (level 36).
  • Search the chest.
  • Return to Lord Kandur Hosidius and talk to him.
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 1 Quest point
  • 1,500 Agility experience
  • 4,000 coins
  • A page for Kharedst's memoirs , ' Lunch by the lancalliums '. It teleports players to just east of the Hosidius Kitchen .
  • Ability to recolour the graceful outfit to the colours of Hosidius .
  • Ability to fight the Sand Snake within the Nightmare Zone .
