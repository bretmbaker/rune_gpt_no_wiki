Rune Mysteries/Quick guide
This quest has an in-depth guide .
It contains a more detailed description of dialogue, cutscenes, and storyline.

Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Speak to Duke Horacio on the 1 st floor [ UK ] 2 nd floor [ US ] of Lumbridge Castle . Show on map
Official difficulty Novice
Official length Short
Description Recent research at the Wizards' Tower has found a way to create Runes for the first time in centuries. Assist the head wizard Sedridor in his research and he too may teach you these secrets!
Requirements None
Items required None
Recommended
  • Fast travel to Varrock ( Varrock teleport , Chronicle or ( members only) Ring of Wealth to the Grand Exchange or Combat Bracelet to the Champions' Guild )
  • (Members only) Fast travel to the Wizards' Tower ( Necklace of Passage , Amulet of Glory to Draynor Village or Fairy Rings to D I S )
Enemies to defeat None
League notes

Unlocking Misthalin ✓ will auto-complete the quest
Unlocking Desert ✓ will auto-complete the quest
Unlocking Fremennik ✓ will auto-complete the quest
Unlocking Kandarin ✓ will auto-complete the quest
Unlocking Wilderness ✓ will auto-complete the quest

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

  • Ask Duke Horacio in Lumbridge Castle for a quest and he'll give you an air talisman . ( 1 • 1 ) …
    1 Have you any quests for me?
    1 Yes.
  • Talk to Archmage Sedridor in the Wizards' Tower basement and give him the air talisman . ( 1 • 2 • 1 ) …
    1 Okay, here you are.
    2 Actually, I'm not interested.
    1 Yes, certainly.
  • Talk to Aubury south of Varrock east bank in the Varrock rune shop and give him the research package . ( 2 ) …
    2 I've been sent here with a package for you.
  • Take the research notes back to Sedridor.
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 1 Quest point
  • The ability to mine rune essence
  • An air talisman
  • The ability to use lamps and books of knowledge from random events on Runecraft
  • Completing this quest awards the player with 5 Kudos which can be claimed by talking to Historian Minas in the Varrock Museum .
