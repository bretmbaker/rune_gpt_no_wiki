Creature of Fenkenstrain/Quick guide
This quest has an in-depth guide .
It contains a more detailed description of dialogue, cutscenes, and storyline.

Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Read the signpost in the middle of Canifis . Show on map
Official difficulty Intermediate
Official length Medium
Description Dr Fenkenstrain , master and sole occupant of the castle to the north-east of Canifis , needs a new servant to go on a dark errand for him. Do you have the stomach to help Fenkenstrain complete his twisted purpose?
Requirements
  • Completion of Priest in Peril
  • Completion of The Restless Ghost
  • 20 Crafting ( boostable ) ( not required to start )
  • 25 Thieving ( boostable ) ( not required to start )
  • Able to defeat a level 51 monster and run past level 72 monsters
Items required
  • Hammer
  • Ghostspeak amulet
  • Silver bar
  • 3 bronze wire (unnoted)
  • Needle (Can NOT use costume needle )
  • 5 thread (Can NOT use costume needle )
  • Spade
  • 1 law & 1 air rune for Telekinetic Grab OR 50 coins
Recommended
  • 30 Combat level
  • Holy symbol (for safety against Feral Vampyres )
  • 2 ecto-tokens (for access to furnace, only if Ghosts Ahoy has not been completed) or coins for a charter ship
  • Ghosts Ahoy for Ectophial
  • 6 empty places in the inventory
  • Weapon
  • Armour
  • Stamina potion
  • Teleport to Canifis - Kharyrll Teleport or Salve Graveyard Teleport if you have not completed Desert Treasure I
  • 2 Fenkenstrain's Castle Teleport
  • Food for low combat levels
  • Access to Arceuus spellbook , level 48 Magic and runes for Fenkenstrain's Castle Teleport
  • Dramen staff
  • Amulet of glory for Edgeville teleport for quick access to a Furnace
Enemies to defeat
  • Experiment (level 51)
Ironman concerns
  • 4 Smithing ( boostable ) (to make a bronze wire )
League notes

Unlocking Desert ✓ will auto-complete the quest
Unlocking Fremennik ✓ will auto-complete the quest
Unlocking Kandarin ✓ will auto-complete the quest
Unlocking Morytania ✓ will auto-complete the quest

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

  • Start the quest by reading the signpost in the centre of Canifis or by talking to Dr Fenkenstrain .
  • Take a pickled brain off the table in the tavern in Canifis for 50gp, or by using Telekinetic Grab .
  • Talk to Dr Fenkenstrain inside of Fenkenstrain's Castle . ( 1 • 4 • 4 ) …
    1 Yes.
    4 Braindead
    4 Grave-digging
  • Go upstairs by the eastern or western staircase to the bookcases and read:
    • "The Joy Of Grave Digging" in the west bookcase. ( 4 ) …
      4 The Joy of Gravedigging
    • "Handy Maggot Avoidance Techniques" in the east bookcase. ( 2 ) …
      2 Handy Maggot Avoidance Techniques
    • Combine the marble amulet and obsidian amulet to form the star amulet .
  • Talk to the Gardener Ghost on the ground floor in the back of the castle with the ghostspeak amulet equipped. ( 2 ) …
    2 What happened to your head?
  • Right-click dig at the cross-shaped grave marker in the Haunted Woods , south-east of Fenkenstrain's castle and immediately east of fairy ring ( fairy ring A L Q )
    • The gardener does NOT have to be following you, this step will work even if he disappears. But you cannot teleport away from him.
    • Be quick, the area is infested with vampyres . Take a holy symbol and food for low levels.
  • Combine the decapicated head and the pickled brain
  • Head north out of the Haunted Woods and use your star amulet on the easternmost memorial (south-east of Fenkenstrain's castle)
  • Push the memorial to enter the Experiment Cave .
  • Inside, kill a level 51 Experiment for the cavern key . If you have started Rag and Bone Man II , kill additional Experiments (level 25) for the Experiment bone .
  • Follow the north-western path to the end.
  • Search the chest and climb the ladder.
  • Right-click Dig at the three graves to get the torso , arms and legs .
  • Return to Dr Fenkenstrain ( Fenkenstrain's castle teleport ) and give him the body parts. ( 1 ) …
    1 I have some body parts for you.
  • Talk to him again to give him the needle and thread.
  • Talk to the Gardener Ghost for the shed key (ensure ghostspeak amulet is equipped). ( 2 ) …
    2 Do you know where the key to the shed is?
  • Search the cupboard inside the shed to get a garden brush .
  • Take 3 canes from the pile of canes next to the shed
  • Use all the canes (3) on the garden brush to get an extended brush (using a total of 3 bronze wires).
  • Go upstairs to the western fireplace and use the extended brush on the fireplace for a conductor mould.
  • Go to a furnace (closest is in Port Phasmatys, teleport to Falador, fairy ring to Zanaris , amulet of glory to Al Kharid, or Home Teleport to Lumbridge) and smelt a silver bar into a conductor (lightning rod).
  • Return to the castle, go upstairs and then climb the southern ladder to reach the lightning conductor .
  • Repair the lightning conductor.
  • Return to Dr Fenkenstrain and talk to him.
  • Go upstairs, then through the metal door, then climb the northern ladder.
  • Talk to Fenkenstrain's monster.
  • Go downstairs, then pickpocket the doctor for the ring of charos .
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 2 Quest points
  • 1,000 Thieving experience
  • The Ring of charos , which allows access to the Werewolf Agility Course , and, when activated during Garden of Tranquillity , reduced cost to many transport links.

Required for completing [ edit | edit source ]

Completion of Creature of Fenkenstrain is required for the following:

  • Garden of Tranquillity
  • The Great Brain Robbery
