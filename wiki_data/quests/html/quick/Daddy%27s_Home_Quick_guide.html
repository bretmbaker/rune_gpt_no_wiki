Daddy's Home
Released 26 August 2020 ( Update )
Members Yes
Quest series None
Developer(s) Ashleigh B

Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Speak to Marlo in the Estate Agent's house in north-east Varrock. Show on map
Official difficulty Novice
Official length Short
Description Marlo's father requires someone to refurnish his disastrous house.
Requirements None
Items required
  • 10 planks – (oak/teak/mahogany will not work)
  • 5 bolts of cloth (can be bought from the Sawmill for 650 coins per bolt)
  • At least 16 nails of any type, e.g. steel nails (more are recommended as nails bend when building furniture. 100 is recommended at 1 Construction , obtainable from the Sawmill during the quest)
  • Hammer (Obtainable during the quest from Yarlo)
  • Saw (Obtainable during the quest)
Recommended
  • Varrock Teleport
  • Stamina potion
  • Lumberyard Teleport
Enemies to defeat None
League notes Misthalin ✓ - location requirement

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

  • Talk to Marlo in the house of the Varrock Estate Agent in north-east Varrock. ( 2 • 3 • 3 ) …
    2 What kind of favour do you want me to do?
    3 Tell me more about the job.
    3 Tell me where he lives, and I'll do the job.
    • Pick up a saw here if you didn't bring one.
  • Talk to Old Man Yarlo in his house south-west of Aubury's Rune Shop .
  • Remove all of the old furniture in the house, for a total of 7 hotspots: 6 pieces of furniture and a rug.
    • one broken chair
    • one rotten carpet
    • two broken stools
    • two broken tables
    • one campbed
  • Talk to Old Man Yarlo again. ( 2 ) …
    2 Skip Yarlo's lecture. He'll offer it later if you like.
  • Search the crates with the black symbol on them west of the range for 3 waxwood logs .
  • Go to the Varrock sawmill and talk to the Sawmill operator . ( 1 ) …
    1 I need some waxwood planks for Old Man Yarlo.
    • Make 3 waxwood planks using the waxwood logs.
    • Make 10 planks now if you don't already have them.
    • Purchase your steel nails and five bolts of cloth now if you need them.
  • Go to Old Man Yarlo's home and build the new furniture (all white translucent objects).
    • Talk to Old Man Yarlo for a hammer and a saw if you didn't bring them. ( 3 • 1 ) …
      3 I need some tools.
      1 Yes please.
  • Talk to Old Man Yarlo.
  • Talk to Marlo to claim your reward. ( 1 ) …
    1 Yeah, what have you got for me?
  • Congratulations, miniquest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 400 Construction XP
    • An additional 544 Construction XP is gained when building the furniture, for a total of 944 XP
  • A player-owned house in Rimmington or 1000 coins if you already own one.
  • Marlo's crate , which contains 25 planks , 50 mithril nails , 5 steel bars , 10 oak planks , 8 bolts of cloth , 5 house teleports , and 1 Falador teleport , for a total value of 29,191
