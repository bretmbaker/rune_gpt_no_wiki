Start point Inspect the empty display on the ground floor of the Grand Museum in Civitas illa Fortis . Show on map
Official difficulty Novice
Official length Short
Description Investigate a mysterious theft from the Fortis Grand Museum.
Requirements
  • 25 Thieving ( unknown )
  • Completion of the following quests:
    • Children of the Sun
    • Shield of Arrav
Items required None
Recommended
  • 3,000 coins for the charter ship to Port Sarim , Ring of charos for reduced charter cost, or Explorer's ring 2 or better for teleport.
  • Varrock Teleport
  • Civitas illa Fortis Teleport
Enemies to defeat None
League notes

Varlamore ✓ — location requirement

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

  • Inspect the empty display case in the north-east corner of the Grand Museum in Civitas illa Fortis to start the quest ( 1 ) …
    1 Yes.
  • Talk to Curator Herminius in the middle of the museum. Talk about the empty display case ( 4 ) …
    4 Could you tell me more about that empty display?
  • Investigate the tools behind the display case.
  • Inspect the display case again.
  • Talk to the citizens, academics, and tourists around the room about their suspicions until one mentions a mysterious individual.
  • Talk to Regulus Cento by the quetzals south-west of the museum ( 2 ) …
    2 Have you seen anybody suspicious around?
  • Go east and talk to a Trader Crewmember (charter ship crewmember) about the suspicious man (1 inventory space required) ( 3 • 1 ) …
    3 Have you seen a man with a case?
    1 As long as it is just the one favour...
  • Go to the crafting shop south-east and get the sail repaired by Artima ( 2 • 1 ) …
    2 I was hoping for some help.
    1 Go on then.
  • Go back to the Trader Crewmember and talk about the suspicious man again ( 3 ) …
    3 So, about that man with the case
  • Head to Port Sarim talk to Trader Stan or any of the Trader Crewmembers who are located at the most south dock ( 3 ) …
    3 Have you seen a grey-haired man with a case?
  • Talk to Betty in the runeshop in north-west Port Sarim ( 2 ) …
    2 Have you seen a grey-haired man with a case?
  • Read the note you receive
  • Talk to Curator Haig Halen in Varrock Museum about Xerna's Diadem ( 1 ) …
    1 I'm looking for Xerna's Diadem.
    - Option 2 if Bone Voyage is complete
  • Pickpocket him for the storeroom key .
  • Open the storeroom in the north-east part of the museum and search the crates.
  • Go back and talk to Haig Halen selecting option 1 to begin the scene ( 1 ) …
    1 I found Xerna's Diadem...
    - Option 2 if Bone Voyage is complete
  • Select options that will shame Haig Halen
  • Return to the Grand Museum in Civitas illa Fortis and talk to Curator Herminius to complete the quest ( 4 ) …
    4 About that empty display...

  • 1 Quest point
  • 6,000 Thieving experience
  • 5,000 coins
