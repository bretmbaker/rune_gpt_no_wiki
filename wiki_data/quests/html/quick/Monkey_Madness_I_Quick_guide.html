Monkey Madness/Quick guide
This quest has an in-depth guide .
It contains a more detailed description of dialogue, cutscenes, and storyline.

Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Talk to King Narnode Shareen in the Grand Tree . Show on map
Official difficulty Master
Official length Long
Description The King of the northern Gnomes, Narnode Shareen, is once again in need of your help. He recently decided to send an envoy of his Royal Guard, the 10th squad, to oversee the decommissioning of the Gnome owned ship-building facilities on the eastern coast of Karamja .

It has been quite some time since the 10th squad were dispatched and they have been deemed missing in action. It will be up to you, should you decide to help, to find out what fate befell the 10th squad and if possible, track them down.

If only it were so simple. Sinister forces have begun to spread through the Gnome hierarchy and threaten to unleash an unknown terror upon the world. Far across the land, the fires of vengeance are being stoked once again. Can you unravel the mystery behind the deception? Can you separate the truth from the lies? Can you decide for yourself what is real and what is not?
Requirements
  • Completion of the following quests:
    • The Grand Tree
    • Tree Gnome Village
  • The ability to defeat a level 195 Jungle Demon
Items required
  • A gold bar
  • Five empty inventory slots
  • A ball of wool
  • Monkey bones or corpse from Karamja (obtainable during quest)
  • Five bananas (obtainable during quest))
Recommended
  • 65 Combat level
  • 43+ Prayer highly recommended , as the player will be attacked by numerous hard-hitting melee enemies in multicombat
  • Poison cures such as antipoison or antidote++
  • Energy-restoring items such as energy and stamina potions
  • Prayer potions
  • A knife for free healing, as there are pineapple plants around Ape Atoll
  • High healing food
  • A lockpick
  • Weight-reducing clothing
  • Emergency teleport (a ring of life works as well)
  • Ardougne cloak 1 or Ardougne teleports
  • A ring of dueling to bank quickly (the Ardougne cloak could be used instead, although it's somewhat further from a bank; the Battlefield of Khazard has a spirit tree, and the Gnome Stronghold has a bank and glider service)
  • 200,000 coins (if skipping the puzzle)
Enemies to defeat
  • Jungle Demon (level 195)
Ironman concerns
  • 40 Smithing ( boostable ) (to make the gold bar if not dropped by a monster) or 33 Magic ( boostable ) (to telegrab a gold bar from Varrock west bank basement.) A gold bar is also a reward from The Digsite , Goblin Diplomacy , and Waterfall Quest
League notes

Unlocking Kandarin ✓ will auto-complete the quest

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

Starting out [ edit | edit source ]

Items needed: Gold bar , Ball of wool and 2 empty inventory spaces.
  • Speak to King Narnode Shareen at the Grand Tree . ( 1 ) …
    1 Yes
    • Without an open inventory space, the gnome royal seal will be dropped on the ground.
  • Climb up the tree and take the glider to Gandius.
  • If you don't have the monkey bones yet, you can kill a monkey nearby for some. (If you have started Tai Bwo Wannai Trio, only a monkey corpse will drop. That will work as well)
  • Enter the Shipyard gate to the north. Make sure you have the Royal Seal with you.
  • Speak with G.L.O. Caranock in the building south of the gate.
  • Return to King Narnode and speak to him. (Make sure you have 1 inventory space free as he will drop Narnode's orders to the floor otherwise).
  • Talk to Daero in the bar on the 1 st floor [ UK ] 2 nd floor [ US ] (east side) of the Grand Tree. ( 1 • 4 • 2 • 4 • 3 • 4 • 4 • 1 ) …
    1 Talk about the journey...
    4 Return to previous menu
    2 Talk about the 1-th squad...
    4 Return to previous menu
    3 Talk about Caranock...
    4 Return to previous menu
    4 Leave...
    1 Who is it?
    Make sure to go through all of the Chat Options to be teleported to a new location.
  • Talk (not "travel") to Daero again inside the hangar.
  • Click the panel in the south-eastern region of the hangar and solve the sliding puzzle. (guide here )
    • Alternatively, pay Glough (back in the Stronghold) 200,000 to solve it for you.
  • Watch the cutscene. Talk to Daero, then Waydar to fly to Crash Island. ( 1 ) …
    1 Yes
  • Talk to Lumdo .
  • Talk (not "travel") to Waydar twice, who will convince Lumdo to follow orders. ( 4 ) …
    4 I cannot convince Lumdo to take us to the island.
  • Talk to Lumdo again to travel west to Ape Atoll.

Ape Atoll [ edit | edit source ]

Note: You will need to teleport away and return to the island several times.
To get back to the Grand Tree, use a Ring of dueling to teleport to the Al Kharid PvP Arena, then take the Al Kharid Gnome Glider .

  • You may wish to keep Protect from Melee prayer on at all times, switching to Protect from Missiles prayer when you get near the gate (where indicated) .
  • If at any point in the island you have the guards called on you, it is recommended to log out or hop worlds to avoid going back to jail.
  • On the island, follow the red path on the map into the valley. You will be shot and arrested.
  • Watch the guards' pattern, pick the lock on your cell door and escape the prison.
    • Stay at least 2 squares away from the guards even while in the cell .
  • Follow the green path and talk to Garkor (Complete dialogue or Zooknock will not give you the m'speak amulet).

Obtaining amulet parts [ edit | edit source ]

  • Follow the blue path to the U-shaped building and enter through the southern door.
    • Stay on the dark brown floor or you will alert the guards.
  • Search the stacked crates on the light brown floor for Monkey dentures . (Complete the dialogue)
  • Search the south-eastern crate to fall into a cave. ( 1 ) …
    1 Yes, I'm sure.
    • Heal up before you do, you can take fall damage.
  • Search the crates in the north-west for a M'amulet mould .
  • You may now teleport away.

Meeting Zooknock [ edit | edit source ]

Items needed: Monkey dentures , M'amulet mould , Ball of Wool , and Gold bar
Items recommended: Good food , prayer potions , antipoison , and stamina potion or energy potions
  • Now is an ideal time to resupply at a bank before heading into the Ape Atoll Dungeon .
  • Return to Ape Atoll by talking to Daero , Waydar , and Lumdo .
  • Follow the yellow path west and climb down the ladder into the Ape Atoll Dungeon .
  • Run to the end of the dungeon.
    • Use Protect from Melee prayer and ignore all the hazards, eating food and drinking potions as needed.
  • Talk to Zooknock .
  • Use your Monkey dentures , M'amulet mould , and Gold bar on Zooknock .
    • Talk to Zooknock to get an Enchanted bar and your M'amulet mould back.
    • Do not use the m'amulet mould on Zooknock when you already have the enchanted bar, otherwise he will keep it and you will have to obtain another.
  • Teleport away, then return by talking to Daero , Waydar , and Lumdo .
  • Follow the red path, get arrested, escape, then follow the pink path to the temple.
  • (Note: bearded guards cannot be walked through!) Enter the trapdoor. You may need to climb up the ladder to the south then down in the east to avoid the gorillas.
  • With a M'amulet mould in your inventory, use the Enchanted bar with a wall of flames, then string it with your Ball of wool to create the M'speak amulet .
  • Optional : If you plan on doing the Freeing King Awowogei section of Recipe for Disaster :
    • Kill one of the Monkey Zombies and pick up the zombie monkey bones .
    • Climb back up the rope, kill one of the Monkey Guards and pick up the gorilla bones .
      • If you're having trouble with the guard, stay out of melee range so it doesn't heal.

Well I'll be a monkey's uncle [ edit | edit source ]

  • Follow the pink path back to the jail, then the cyan path to the Monkey child 's house.
    • Keep an eye on The Monkey's Aunt , it's only safe to be in the garden while she isn't near it.
  • Equip your M'speak amulet and talk to the monkey child.
  • Talk to the monkey child twice more. ( 4 • 2 ) …
    4 Well I'll be a monkey's uncle!
    2 How many bananas did Aunty want?
  • Pick 5 Bananas from the trees in the garden.
  • Talk to the monkey child to give him the bananas.
  • Leave the garden and wait for the aunt to pass through.
  • Talk to the monkey child to get a Monkey talisman .
  • Optional : If you plan on doing the Freeing King Awowogei section of Recipe for Disaster :
    • Talk to the monkey child again. ( 2 ) …
      2 I've lost that toy you gave me...
    • Leave the garden, hop worlds or wait for the monkey child to stop crying.
    • Talk to the monkey child again. ( 2 • 1 ) …
      2 Wow - can I borrow it?
      1 Ok, I promise!
    • Repeat the above steps until you have four Monkey talisman in total.
    • If doing Freeing King Awowogei section, climb the ladder in the east building from Child, cross bridge east and there will be Monkey Archers you can kill and pick up one set of ninja monkey bones

The greegree [ edit | edit source ]

Items needed: Monkey bones or corpse , monkey talisman . (Bring extra monkey bones and monkey talismans if planning to do Freeing King Awowogei section of Recipe for Disaster )
  • Teleport away, then return by talking to Daero , Waydar , and Lumdo .
    • Now is an ideal time to resupply at a bank for the Ape Atoll dungeon.
    • Bring good Food , as well as Prayer , Antipoison , and Stamina / Energy potions.
  • Follow the yellow path west and climb down the ladder.
  • Run to the end of the dungeon.
    • Use Protect from Melee prayer and ignore all the hazards, eating food and drinking potions as needed.
  • Give Zooknock your Monkey talisman and Monkey bones (make sure these are bones from the regular monkeys; examine Text: These are small monkey bones. ). After a moment he will give you a Greegree .
    • Do not accidentally bury the bones! Right click them and carefully select "use".
    • Do not leave the dialogue with Zooknock prematurely or you will lose your items. Should the player exit the cutscene before it has finished, talking to Zooknock again will restart the cutscene, and no items will be lost.
  • Optional : If you plan on doing the Freeing King Awowogei section of Recipe for Disaster :
    • Talk to Zooknock again. ( 2 • 1 ) …
      2
      1
    • Use another talisman and bone on him. The type of bones you give him will determine the Greegree you receive. After a moment he will give you another Greegree .
    • Repeat the above steps until you have four Greegrees in total. ( Karamjan , Ninja , Zombie , and Gorilla )
  • Note: Teleporting while wielding the Greegree will force it into your inventory. If your inventory is full then it will be dropped. If unnoticed, the Greegree can be lost.

An audience with King Awowogei [ edit | edit source ]

Items needed: Karamjan monkey greegree , M'speak amulet , at least one empty inventory slot
Items recommended: Mithril Seeds or log and tinderbox to skip finding Kruk or Garkor.
  • Head to the Ardougne Zoo and equip your Karamjan monkey greegree , then talk to the Monkey minder . If you haven't talked to him before, click through his dialogue (ask any question), then equip the Karamjan monkey greegree .
  • Equip your M'speak amulet and talk to a monkey, it will jump in your bag .
  • Remove your greegree and talk to the minder again.
  • Travel by foot (walk or run) back to the Grand Tree, then return to Ape Atoll by talking to Daero , Waydar , and Lumdo . Do not use any means of teleportation.
  • Equip your M'speak amulet and Karamjan monkey greegree . Ape Atoll is no longer your enemy when wearing a greegree!
  • If you want to skip going to talk to Kruk and you brought mithril seeds or tinderbox and logs you can stand on the east side of the Elder Guard and plant the mithril seeds or light a fire to be force moved one tile west and thus bypass needing to talk to Kruk or to Garkor .
  • Follow the red path, go through the gates, then walk east to the coast and talk to Garkor .
  • Talk to the nearby Elder Guard .
  • Head to the west coast of the island (orange path), then climb up the hill to the south.
  • Climb the ladder, walk across the bridge, climb down, and talk to Kruk .
  • Talk to Awowogei .
  • Talk to Awowogei again. Then talk to the Elder Guard again to be let out.
  • Talk to Garkor and watch the cutscene.
  • Talk to Garkor again to get the 10th squad sigil .

The final battle [ edit | edit source ]

Items needed: Combat equipment, 10th squad sigil
Items recommended: Teleportation, prayer potions and high healing food are highly recommended
  • Prepare for final battle.
  • Equip the 10th squad sigil when you are ready to fight the Jungle Demon .
    • Tip: Use Protect from Magic prayer and stay out of melee range to avoid taking damage.
  • Kill the Jungle Demon.
  • Return to the Grand Tree and talk to King Narnode Shareen .
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 3 Quest points
  • 10,000 Coins
  • 3 Diamonds
  • Speaking to Daero after the quest gives the choice of either, 35,000 experience in Attack and Defence and 20,000 experience in Strength and Hitpoints OR 35,000 experience in Strength and Hitpoints and 20,000 experience in Attack and Defence . The skills you tell Daero you want to focus on will receive 35,000 XP each, and the others 20,000 each.
  • The ability to buy and wield the Dragon scimitar .
  • Full access to Ape Atoll, with the exception of the bank and javelin store, which are unlocked after Monkey Madness II .

Required for completing [ edit | edit source ]

Completion of Monkey Madness I is required for the following:

  • Completion of Recipe for Disaster - Freeing Awowogei
  • Monkey Madness II
