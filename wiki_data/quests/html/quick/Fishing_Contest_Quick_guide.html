Fishing Contest/Quick guide
This quest has an in-depth guide .
It contains a more detailed description of dialogue, cutscenes, and storyline.

Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Mountain Dwarves at either of the entrances to the tunnel that goes under White Wolf Mountain . Show on map
Official difficulty Novice
Official length Very Short
Description The mountain Dwarves' home would be an ideal way to get across White Wolf Mountain safely. However, the Dwarves aren't too fond of strangers. They will let you through if you can bring them a trophy. The trophy is the prize for the annual Hemenster fishing competition.
Requirements
  • 10 Fishing ( not boostable ) ( required to start )
Items required
  • Spade
  • 5 coins (10 if you buy a fishing rod from Jack)
  • Garlic (obtainable during quest)
  • Fishing rod (obtainable during quest), oily fishing rod does not work
  • Red vine worm (obtainable during quest)
Recommended
  • Fast travel to White Wolf Mountain ( Camelot teleport , Taverley teleport , games necklace to Burthorpe, Catherby Teleport , or Teleport to House if your house is in Taverley)
  • Fast travel to McGrubor's Wood ( Camelot teleport , combat bracelet to the Ranging Guild or fairy rings to A L S )
  • Food and armour for low levels
Enemies to defeat None
League notes

Kandarin ✓ — location requirement

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

This quest can be started from either side of the White Wolf Mountain.
  • Speak to either dwarves on either side of the White Wolf Mountain . ( 1 • 2 • 3 • 1 • 1 ) …
    1 I was wondering what was down those stairs?
    2 Why not?
    3 If you were my friend I wouldn't mind it.
    1 Well, let's be friends!
    1 And how am I meant to do that?
    Note: Starting on the west side makes travelling by foot easier.
  • If you need garlic , go to Seers' Village and take it from the house south-west of the bank (it spawns on the table).
  • Bring a spade and make your way to McGrubor's Wood (either enter through the back or teleport to fairy ring A L S if you completed at least half of Fairytale II - Cure a Queen ) to gather 3 red vine worm . You can collect them by checking the vine just west of the fairy ring A L S .
  • Either walk south to Hemenster or teleport to the Ranging Guild via your combat bracelet and go north-west.
  • If you don't have a Fishing Rod, purchase one from Grandpa Jack for 5 coins in the house outside Hemenster entrance.
  • Enter Hemenster with the Fishing pass in your inventory.
  • Put a Garlic in the pipes to the north. Make sure to finish the dialogue.
  • Pay Bonzo 5 coins. Wait for the Sinister Stranger to move away from the pipes. Start fishing and wait for the contest to end.
  • After winning the contest, make your way back to the dwarf and show him the trophy.
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 1 Quest point
  • 2,437 Fishing experience
  • Access to the underground White Wolf Mountain passage
  • Ability to catch minnows in the Fishing Guild with 82 Fishing and a full Angler's outfit

Required for completing [ edit | edit source ]

Completion of Fishing Contest is required for the following:

  • Recipe for Disaster
  • Forgettable Tale...
  • Between a Rock...
