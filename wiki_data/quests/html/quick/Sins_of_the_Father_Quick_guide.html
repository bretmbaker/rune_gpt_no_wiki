Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Speak to Veliaf Hurtz outside the church in Slepe . Show on map
Official difficulty Master
Official length Long
Description A darkness has fallen over Morytania. The Myreque freedom fighters have achieved their first major victory over the vampyres with the death of Ranis Drakan . However, their newly found hope is unlikely to last. After many years of absence, Lord Lowerniel Drakan , the vampyre overlord of Morytania, has finally emerged from his castle. He has but one goal. To avenge his brother and eradicate the Myreque once and for all.

  • 60 Fletching ( not boostable ) ( required to start )
  • 56 Crafting ( not boostable ) ( required to start )
  • 52 Agility ( not boostable )
  • 50 Attack ( not boostable )
  • 50 Slayer ( not boostable )
  • 49 Magic ( not boostable )

  • Any axe
  • Vyrewatch outfit ( top , legs , and shoes ) or 1,950 coins to purchase it from Trader Sven's Black-market Goods (can be obtained during quest)
  • Ivandis flail
  • Ruby
  • Knife (can be obtained during quest)
  • Chisel
  • Runes for Lvl-3 Enchant (1 cosmic rune , 5 fire runes ) or an enchant ruby or topaz tablet
Recommended
  • 95 Combat level
  • Teleport to Haunted Woods (fairy ring A L Q , Fenkenstrain's Castle Teleport, Kharyrll Teleport, or ectophial will get you close).
  • You may need an additional 10,000 coins to pay Andras for a trip to Slepe
  • Any pickaxe (bronze pickaxe obtainable during quest)

  • Stamina potions or energy potions

League notes

Morytania ✓ — location requirement

Players attempting to start the quest may find that Veliaf Hurtz is not present in Slepe . If this happens, he must first be interacted with in the Burgh de Rott basement, he will then appear in Slepe.

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

Slepe's Sleeper Plague [ edit | edit source ]

Items needed: Combat equipment (preferably ranged)
Items recommended: Food, prayer potions , stamina potion for following Carl, Drakan's medallion or an Ectophial alongside 10,000 coins , teleport to Drezel (Fairy ring C K S , Salve Graveyard Teleport tablet , Slayer ring ), teleport to Fenkenstrain's Castle ( Fenkenstrain's Castle Teleport tablet ).
  • Talk to Veliaf Hurtz , outside of the church in Slepe . ( 1 ) …
    1 Yes.
  • Talk to Hameln the Jester inside the church. ( 1 ) …
    1 Do you know how he fell ill?
This fight takes place in an instance .
On death , all unprotected items will be moved to a grave outside the instance. Any items left on the ground will be lost.
  • Talk to Carl in the pub south-east of the church. ( 2 ) …
    2 Where do you get your Bloody Bracers from?
  • Inspect the barrel south of the pub to follow Carl to his supplier.
    • Go through the church and hide at the north doors, wait there until he passes the doors.
    • Proceed to the hollow tree just north of the graves, and hide on the eastern side.
    • When he reaches the centre square of the town, hide at the northern side of the hollow tree directly next to the gallows.
    • Once he approaches the graveyard, hide at the western side of the hollow tree to the west of the graveyard.
  • After the cutscene, kill Kroy . Kroy does not have to be killed with the Ivandis flail, and can instead be killed with normal weapons.
    • Use Protect from Magic , and dodge his potion-throw attack.
    • Stand behind a lab table and attack him with ranged to negate all his damage.
  • Destroy both lab tables.
  • Leave the lab by clicking the southern gate.
  • Talk to Veliaf in the Slepe church graveyard. ( 2 ) …
    2 Fair enough, so what now?
  • Talk to Veliaf Hurtz next to Drezel at Paterdomus . ( 2 ) …
    2 I see. So that's why you want to keep him safe, Veliaf?
    (Fairy ring C K S , Salve Graveyard Teleport tablet , Slayer ring )
  • Talk to Ivan Strom south of Fenkenstrain's Castle . ( Fenkenstrain's Castle Teleport tablet )

An Unwelcome Surprise [ edit | edit source ]

Items needed: Knife , hammer and an axe (or 3 logs which is slightly faster), combat equipment and a weapon to harm tier 2 vampyres (such as the Ivandis flail )
Items recommended: Three empty spaces and food to heal yourself and Ivan Strom. Bring druid pouches in case you run into the Ghast event which will turn your food rotten .
  • Talk to Ivan Strom east of Paterdomus (north of fairy ring C K S ) to begin the trek. ( 1 ) …
    1 I'm ready.
    • Cut three vines from the swamp tree to the north-east.
    • Use the vines on each other to make a long vine.
    • Use the long vine on the swamp tree branch.
    • Swing across the water and continue the trek by clicking the last tile.
    • Kill the Nail beasts, use food on Ivan if his health becomes too low, and continue the trek.
    • Use your logs on the bridge (chop 3 logs if you didn't bring any. If you forgot your axe, kill the zombie for one.)
    • Cross the bridge and continue the trek.
    • Before the dialogue ends, attack the easternmost Vampyre (the one in-front of Ivan) so that it doesn't attack Ivan.
    • Use your Ivandis Flail to kill these Vampyres.
    • Continue the trek to end up in Burgh de Rott .
    • Talk to Ivan and complete the dialogue to send him to the boathouse.
    • After completing the trek, bank and prepare for the next step.

A Nice and Simple Plan [ edit | edit source ]

Items needed: Pickaxe , combat equipment to kill a Mutated Bloodveld (level 123) and some free inventory space.
Items recommended: Drakan's medallion and either Morytania legs 3 or Mort'ton teleport
  • Head south-east of Burgh de Rott to the boat house and talk to Ivan Strom.
  • Board the boat and head to the Icyene graveyard .
  • Talk to Veliaf Hurtz.
  • After the cutscene, talk to Vanescula Drakan
  • Unlock the mausoleum door north of you.
  • Solve the Mausoleum Door's puzzle .
  • Talk to everyone in this order:
    • Vertida Sefalatis .
    • Kael Forshaw .
    • Radigad Ponfit .
    • Polmafi Ferdygris .
    • Ivan Strom .
    • Veliaf Hurtz .
  • Finish the conversation with Vanescula Drakan.
  • Use Drakan's medallion to teleport to Ver Sinhaza and ask the Vyrewatch next to the bank to send you to the mines. ( 1 ) …
    1 Send me to the mines.
  • Mine 15 Daeyalt ore and place them in the cart. (If you forgot a pickaxe, talk to any miner for a bronze one.)
  • Talk to the Vampyre Juvinate .
  • Head south-east to the house with the slashed tapestry and the statue.
  • Walk through the tapestry.
  • Go down the staircase.
  • Talk to Safalaan. ( 1 ) …
    1 Shall we get going then?
  • Kill the Mutated Bloodveld .
  • Talk to Safalaan again.
  • Search the bookshelf on the western wall inside the room filled with mutated bloodvelds.
  • Talk to Safalaan. ( 1 ) …
    1 Sure. Here you go.
  • Head back to Burgh de Rott. ( Morytania legs 3 , Mort'ton teleport or Shades of Mort'ton minigame teleport and run south-east to the boat or Drakan's Medallion and run south-west to the boat.)

A Vyre-ry Noble Disguise [ edit | edit source ]

Items needed: 1950 coins (or Vyrewatch outfit ), melee combat equipment, Ivandis flail
Items recommended: Drakan's medallion , some food , an antipoison , 1-2 prayer potions , and 1 super combat potion , 3 empty inventory slots for the outfit.
  • Take the boat back to the Icyene Graveyard.
  • Talk to Veliaf at the eastern part of the graveyard.
  • Use your Drakan's medallion to teleport to Ver Sinhaza.
  • Exit to the west towards Meiyerditch.
    • If you don't have the Vyrewatch outfit head to Trader Sven 's house south of Old Man Ral's house, near the shore. Trade him and buy a Vyrewatch outfit. (Do not equip the outfit)
  • Go down the trapdoor in Old Man Ral's house to the west.
  • Talk to Polmafi Ferdygris . ( 1 ) …
    1 Here you go.
    (Make sure to upgrade the outfit to Noble)
  • Make your way back to Icyene Graveyard by either:
    • Climbing back up, going south-west to the wall, climb the wall and navigate to the boat
    • Or go back to Burgh de Rott to get to the boat
  • Talk to Vanescula Drakan .
  • Take the boat to Slepe.
This fight takes place in an instance .
On death , all unprotected items will be moved to a grave outside the instance. Any items left on the ground will be lost.
  • Talk to Veliaf at the graveyard. ( 1 ) …
    1 Let's do this.
  • Kill Damien Leucurte .
    • Wield your Ivandis Flail.
    • Use Protect from Magic.
    • Drink your antipoison when poisoned.
    • Stomp out the magical fires Damien periodically spawns.
  • Talk to Veliaf.
  • Use your Drakan's Medallion to teleport to Ver Sinhaza and prepare for the next step.

The Branches of Darkmeyer [ edit | edit source ]

Items needed: Vyre noble clothing , an axe , eight free inventory spaces
Items recommended: Drakan's medallion , weight reducing gear, stamina potion, ways to restore run energy, at least 10k coins for the boat from the ectofuntus
  • Go back to the Icyene Graveyard (Use Morytania legs 3 or Drakan's Medallion and take the boat to the north or Mort'ton teleport and take the boat south-east of Burgh de Rott).
  • Talk to Vanescula Drakan.
  • Equip the Vyre noble clothing.
  • Use your Drakan's medallion to teleport to Ver Sinhaza.
  • Talk to the Vyrewatch right next to the bank and ask to be sent to the mines. ( 1 ) …
    1 Send me to the mines.
  • Talk to a Vampyre Juvinate to exit the mine. You do not have to mine Daeyalt ore this time.
  • Go through the crack north of the mining hole.
  • Head to the Arboretum. (The tree icon east of the bank in Darkmeyer.)
  • Finish the dialogue with the guard Desmodus Lasiurus.
  • Head to the house with a water symbol north-east of the furnace, along the east wall near the crack.
  • Talk to Mordan Nikazsi .
  • Head to the prison in the northern part of Darkmeyer, north of the Arboretum.
  • Talk to Maria Gadderanks .
  • Head to the Arboretum and enter it.
  • Search the toppled shelves for an old note.
  • Read it, and take note of how many gallons the tree requires to be cut.
  • Look up the required gallons in the solution table and set the north and south valve to the given values.

The Final Preparations [ edit | edit source ]

Items needed: 8 blisterwood logs , knife , chisel , ruby , Ivandis flail , or 20,000 coins if you don't have one, and either 5 fire runes and a cosmic rune or an enchant ruby tablet .
  • Head back to the underground lair under Old Man Ral's house in Meiyerditch.
  • Talk to Vertida Sefalatis and give him 7 Blisterwood logs. ( 1 ) …
    1 Here you go.
  • Search the nearby crate for a silver sickle (b) .
  • Use the ruby on the sickle.
  • Enchant the ruby sickle (b) .
  • Use the blisterwood log on the enchanted ruby sickle (b) .
  • Use your Ivandis flail on the blisterwood sickle.
  • Prepare for the fight with Vanstrom Klause .

A Bloody Show [ edit | edit source ]

Items needed: Blisterwood flail , combat equipment.
Items recommended: High magic defence gear, equipment with crush attack bonuses, a fast ranged weapon, Efaritay's aid , 43 prayer to use protection prayers (70+ recommended for Piety ), high healing food, prayer potions, super combat potions and an emergency teleport.
This fight takes place in an instance with a deathbank .
On death , you can pay Veliaf Hurtz 50,000 to retrieve lost items.
Any unsafe death will delete all items left inside the deathbank. Any items left on the ground will be lost.
  • Go back to the Icyene Graveyard.
  • Talk to Vanescula Drakan.
  • Run north-east of the graveyard.
  • Talk to Safalaan Hallow. ( 1 ) …
    1 I'm ready.
  • Kill Vanstrom Klause , use Protect from Magic.
    • Vanstrom uses three special attacks. He will use a special attack every 8-10 attacks, and will never use the same one twice in a row. He can also use an attack that lowers your Attack level similar to Shadow Spells.
      • "My pets will feast on your corpse!" - Acidic Bloodveld is spawned and will explode on the player for high damage. Kill with ranged.
      • "Blood will be my strength!" - A blood orb spawns that periodically heals Vanstrom. Walking on top of it (3x3 area) damages the player, but Vanstrom can be lured on top to damage him.
      • "Stare into darkness!" - Heavy damage is taken if the player is facing Vanstrom. The attack gives very little warning and is near impossible to avoid on reaction. It is recommended that the player keep a count of how many attacks Vanstrom has done and turn around preemptively.
    • After you deplete his hitpoints, he will heal himself 200 hitpoints and stop using his special attacks.
      • Turn off protection prayers to save on prayer points as they no longer have any effect, but continue using Piety or other buffing prayers
      • Vanstrom summons lightning to strike tiles with a max hit of 46. Tiles that are about to be struck will have a dark circle on them and should be avoided.
      • Consider turning off runelites 117HD plug in, as it makes it much harder to see where the lightning will strike.
    • Note: You can speak to Veliaf if you wish to re-attempt the fight.
  • Watch the cutscene.
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 2 Quest points
  • Access to Darkmeyer
    • Access to the Daeyalt Essence Mine
    • Access to the Hallowed Sepulchre
    • Ability to shop at stores in Darkmeyer while wearing Vyre noble clothing
    • Ability to use the wall shortcut on the eastern side of Darkmeyer with 63 Agility, after purchasing two long ropes from the Darkmeyer General Store and using them on it.
  • A tome of experience , granting 15,000 experience to any skill. (level 60 or higher). This reward can be utilised six times and may be used for the same skill.
  • The Blisterwood flail
  • Darkmeyer teleport via Drakan's medallion
  • Boat travel between Burgh de Rott , Slepe and the Icyene Graveyard
  • Access to the mutated bloodvelds section of Meiyerditch Laboratories
  • Access to the True Blood Altar (with a Blood talisman )
    • Access to the Guardian of Blood in the Guardians of the Rift minigame
  • Promotion to the rank of Lieutenant within The Myreque and its associated title
