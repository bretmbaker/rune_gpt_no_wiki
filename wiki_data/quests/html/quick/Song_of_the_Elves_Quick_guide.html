Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Talk to Edmond , who can be found at his home in East Ardougne, north of the Flying Horse Inn . Show on map
Official difficulty Grandmaster
Official length Very Long
Description Our story begins with you, the hero, answering the call to adventure when Edmond, a resident of Ardougne, asks for your aid. By rescuing his daughter Elena from Plague City, the quarantined West Ardougne, you begin to unravel the mystery surrounding the disease. With her help, you discover the plague is a hoax - a plot by King Lathas to section off the West Ardougne. By confronting him, you find out this was done to protect the rest of Gielinor from the threat in the elven lands.

The king sends you on a mission to travel through the Underground Pass and help his ally, Lord Iorwerth, in eradicating his brother King Tyras who threatens to revive the mysterious Dark Lord. After an arduous journey through the pass, you meet with Iorwerth and his forces to end Tyras' evil once and for all.

Upon your return to Lathas you are intercepted by Arianwyn, a rebel elf who opposes Lord Iorwerth. He informs you of Lathas and Iorwerth's deception. They manipulated you into removing their final opposition and were now free to bring the Dark Lord back to Gielinor for their own intentions. You work with the elven rebellion to discover the purpose of the plague was to disguise the digsite of the long lost Temple of Light. An ancient place that is key to bringing back the Dark Lord.

To make amends for your past mistakes and put a halt in King Lathas' and Lord Iorwerth's plans, you solve the intricate light puzzle designed to protect the Death Altar within the temple. After sealing off the temple and preventing the return of the Dark Lord, it's now time to turn your attention to King Lathas and Lord Iorwerth to end their schemes once and for all in Song of the Elves.

The attack on Lletya [ edit | edit source ]

  • Enter the house to the south and search the east table to find Eoin .
  • Go up the ladder and search the Cooking Pots by the South Cooking Range to find Iona .
  • You will now have to fight Arianwyn.
    • Range or melee are suggested, as magic is inaccurate against him
    • Protect from Range will completely nullify his normal attacks
    • Purple arrows will turn off any prayers, so make sure to turn them back on.
    • Orange arrows are 1x1 bombs, and will hit high amounts of damage even through prayer. Move to a different tile after the arrow starts flying to avoid them.

Note: If you die during this fight, you can collect your items from the Rebel Scout outside of Lletya for 80,000 coins. Using a teleport crystal to teleport back doesn't restart the fight, but places you next to the Rebel Scout.

Warning: Dying again before collecting your items from the Rebel Scout will delete your items.

Near death Arianwyn will animate an arrow which will lead to a cutscene.

Finding Lady Ithell and Lady Meilyr [ edit | edit source ]

First step of the clue scroll
Items needed: Spade , 8 limestone bricks , saw , hammer
Items recommended: Anti-poison , stamina potions , charged teleport crystal to Lletya , Iorwerth camp teleport , fairy ring access and 76 Agility (71 with summer pie ) or a Zul-andra teleport . To save banking, for the next part bring combat gear, food, super restores .
  • Talk to Baxtorian to receive a clue scroll. ( 1 • 1 ) …
    1 What's Lady Ithell's clue?
    1 What's Lady Meilyr's clue?
  • Search Lletya for the five glowing symbols on the walls:
    • First symbol is inside the bank on west wall, glowing white.
    • Second symbol is inside the building north of Elena in the south-west section.
    • Third symbol is on the south wall of the general store to the north.
    • Fourth symbol is in the upper floor of the general store, on the north wall.
    • Fifth and final symbol is on the west wall of the upper floor prayer altar room.
  • Take the limestone bricks, hammer, and saw, and build the statue in the centre of the village. (Make sure to finish the dialogue or you won't be able to continue the next part)
  • Speak with Lady Ithell at her statue on Lletya.
  • Bank the hammer/saw and get the rest of your supplies now. (You will still need your spade!)
  • Go to the south of Isafdar , near the swamp, and dig there. (see picture)
  • Dig two steps north of the STASH unit near Zul-Andra ( B J S and run east, 76 Agility OR Zul-andra teleport OR charter a ship to Port Tyras and run South East)
  • Dig outside the south gates of Prifddinas ( Iorwerth camp teleport )
  • Spin outside the western entry to the Underground Pass with nothing equipped ( Teleport Crystal to Lletya ).
  • Bank now if you did not bring combat gear

Sealing the Underground Pass [ edit | edit source ]

Required items: Combat gear, food, Super restores or Restore potions . A teleport to a bank to gear up for the final battle, You also need 1 inventory space to hold the 'Explosive Potions' .

  • Enter the Underground Pass from the Isafdar side, and enter the Well of voyage. Proceed south along the winding path (see dungeon map for path from Iban chamber to stairs, eventually travelling through a cave to dwarf camp at the ground level.
  • Speak with Baxtorian.
  • Speak with Elena. ( 2 ) …
    2 Baxtorian said you had an idea to help our defence.
  • Fill the seven holes outside of the camp with the Explosive potions .
  • Speak with Baxtorian. ( 1 ) …
    1 I'm ready.
  • Defend the Dwarf camp for four minutes.
    • You do not need to kill every attacker, simply keep them from destroying the barricade. A new archer/melee spawns randomly after killing one.
    • Attack any elf that is attacking the barricade, to draw their attention towards you instead. Alternatively you can kill off all the rangers until there's only melee attackers left and block the path to the gate.
    • Protect from Melee is recommended, to protect from the higher levelled Elf warriors, switch to Protect from Missiles once the warriors pile up and stop attacking you.
  • Defeat Essyllt .
    • Protect from Melee will nullify most of his damage. Can quickly change to Protect from Range after his stun attack to avoid all damage.
    • After his potion attack, drink a restore to counteract the stat drain.

Fighting the Dark Lord [ edit | edit source ]

Items needed: Combat gear
Items recommended: Magic gear, Saradomin brews , super restores , fast ranged weapons such knives , darts , or chinchompas .
  • After a cutscene, you will automatically enter the Temple of Light . If you are not equipped for this fight immediately teleport out, you can re-enter the Temple of Light through the Underground Pass, through the same crack that you entered the Dwarven camp with.
  • Defeat the Fragment of Seren .
    • Magic is the only form of combat that is effective against the Fragment.
    • Protect from Range reduces most of its damage from normal attacks.
    • Equipment lost on death while in the instance can be reclaimed from an elf NPC standing outside of the cave entrance for the cost of 80,000 coins. If the player dies elsewhere before reclaiming their items, their unclaimed items will be permanently lost .
    • Magic attack and damage bonus should be prioritised. High-level magic equipment such as Ancestral robes , Ahrim's robes , or void knight equipment along with the trident of the swamp and Ancient Magicks blood spells are an effective combination.
    • The Fragment has four special attacks that always occur in the following order, repeating until The Fragment is defeated:
      • 1. An orb that teleports you to The Fragment. Simply run away after being teleported to maintain a safe distance from a melee attack.
      • 2. The Fragment will split into multiple parts, and you must attack the correct one. The real Fragment will have crystals underneath it, and a lighter shade of aura.
      • 3. The Fragment will summon healing spells. Simply attack the spells once with any attack to defeat them. Fast-hitting weapons such as throwing knives or darts are excellent here. Chinchompas are a great choice as their long range makes it easier to stay out of melee range while only being 1 tick slower than knives/darts.
      • 4. The Fragment will teleport you to one place and freeze you there while she winds up an attack. Then, she will unleash a heavy attack that hits for 99% of your max hp. If your hitpoints are at or below 99% of your max hp, you will be killed in 1 shot. It is highly recommended to bring Saradomin brews or anglerfish to heal above 100% health prior to the attack landing to avoid being killed.
  • After defeating the Fragment of Seren, Baxtorian will invite you into Prifddinas.
  • Congratulations, quest complete!
