Hazeel Cult/Quick guide
This quest has an in-depth guide .
It contains a more detailed description of dialogue, cutscenes, and storyline.

Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Central Ardougne , south of King Lathas ' castle . Talk to Ceril Carnillean . Show on map
Official difficulty Novice
Official length Very Short
Description Discover the truth behind the Carnillean family fortune. Decide for yourself whether to aid the Carnilleans in retrieving stolen goods, or join the Hazeel cult members in their mission to resurrect the infamous Lord Hazeel.
Requirements Able to defeat a level 13 cultist (this applies only if you're taking Ceril's side)
Items required None
Recommended
  • 10 Combat level
  • Ardougne cloak for Monastery teleports.
  • Stamina or energy potions .
Enemies to defeat Alomone (level 13) if taking Ceril's side
League notes

Kandarin ✓ — location requirement
Unlocking Desert ✓ will auto-complete the quest
Unlocking Fremennik ✓ will auto-complete the quest

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

  • Speak with Ceril Carnillean ( ground floor [ UK ] 1 st floor [ US ] of the house south-west of East Ardougne castle) ( 1 • 1 ) …
    1 What's wrong?
    1 Yes.
  • Enter the Ardougne Sewers east of the clocktower (south of Valve 3)

Use this image as a reference for the following steps.

  • Talk to Clivet ( 2 • 2 ) …
    2 Alright, I've made my decision.
    2 I won't help you.
  • Go back outside
  • Turn Valve 1 RIGHT
  • Turn Valve 2 RIGHT
  • Turn Valve 3 LEFT
  • Turn Valve 4 RIGHT
  • Turn Valve 5 RIGHT
  • Head back to cave, board the raft, and talk to Alomone .
  • Kill Alomone and loot the Carnillean armour from the chest.
  • Board the raft again and run back to the Carnilleans house.
  • Talk to Butler Jones and then Ceril Carnillean (quest incomplete!)
  • Search the cupboard in the room upstairs east of the staircase to find the evidence.

  • Talk to Clivet to receive a bottle of poison ( 2 • 1 • 1 ) …
    2 Alright, I've made my decision.
    1 I'll help you.
    1 Alright, how do I do it?
  • Turn Valve 1 RIGHT
  • Turn Valve 2 RIGHT
  • Go to the basement of the Carnilleans house (ladder)
  • Use the bottle of poison on the cooking range.
  • Talk to Ceril on the ground floor [ UK ] 1 st floor [ US ] .
  • Talk to Clivet.
  • Turn Valve 3 LEFT
  • Turn Valve 4 RIGHT
  • Turn Valve 5 RIGHT
  • Go back to the cave and board the raft.
  • Talk to Alomone .
  • Go back to the Carnilleans' house.
  • Talk to Butler Jones .
  • Go in the basement and search the crate for a Chest key.
  • Go up the ladder and stairs and knock on the wall in the east room, and go up the ladder.
  • Unlock and search the chest to find the scroll .
  • Return to Alomone.

  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 1 Quest point
  • 1500 Thieving experience
  • 2000 coins (plus 5 from the fake ending if you sided with the Carnillean family)
  • Hazeel's mark (if you sided with Hazeel )
  • Carnillean armour (if you sided with Ceril and obtained an extra during the quest)
  • 5 Kudos , which can be claimed by talking to Historian Minas in the Varrock Museum
