Dwarf Cannon/Quick guide
This quest has an in-depth guide .
It contains a more detailed description of dialogue, cutscenes, and storyline.

Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Talk to Captain Lawgof , south of the Coal Trucks , north-west of the Fishing Guild . Show on map
Official difficulty Novice
Official length Short
Description For several years now, the Dwarven Black Guard have been developing the latest in projectile warfare.

With the constant attack of goblin renegades, the dwarven troops who protect the mines need to put this secret weapon into action.

Only with your help can the true power of this cannon be harnessed!
Requirements None
Items required
  • Hammer (obtained during the quest)
Recommended
  • Food at very low combat levels.
  • Stamina or energy potions .
  • Fast travel to the entrance of the Dwarven Mine ( Amulet of glory to Edgeville or Combat bracelet to the Monastery)
  • Fast travel to the Coal Trucks ( Games necklace to Barbarian Outpost or Skills necklace to the Fishing Guild)
  • Fast travel to the Goblin Cave ( Skills Necklace to the Fishing Guild )
Enemies to defeat None
League notes

Unlocking Asgarnia ✓ will auto-complete the quest

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

Getting started [ edit | edit source ]

  • Talk to Captain Lawgof by the Coal truck mining site west of Seers' Village . ( 1 ) …
    1 Yes.

( Fairy ring code A L S , run east and squeeze-through the loose railing and continue west. Alternatively, use a skills necklace teleport to the Fishing Guild, run west and then north.)

Repair and retrieval [ edit | edit source ]

  • Pick up the hammer in the house to the east if you do not have one.
  • Fix the six bent railings of the west and south sides of the fence.
  • Talk to Captain Lawgof .
  • Go to the tower south of the gate.
  • Climb up two ladders and take the Dwarf remains .
  • Talk to Captain Lawgof.
  • Run south (or teleport to the Fishing Guild) and enter the cave at the south-east corner of the Fishing Guild .
  • In the cave, go as far north-west as possible. In the northwesternmost room, search the northernmost crate by the east wall to find Lollk . You don't need to finish the conversation.
  • Talk to Captain Lawgof. ( 1 ) …
    1 Okay, I'll see what I can do.
  • Use the toolkit you got from Captain Lawgof on the broken multicannon next to him. ( In this order: )
    • Use the hook tool (right) on the spring.
    • Use the pliers (middle) on the safety switch at the bottom.
    • Use the tooth tool (left) on the gear located at the bottom of the hammer.
  • Talk to Captain Lawgof. ( 2 ) …
    2 Okay then, just for you!
    • Note: This conversation must be completely finished.

Finishing up [ edit | edit source ]

  • Go to the Dwarven Black Guard camp north-east of Falador / south of Ice Mountain / west of Barbarian village.
  • In the outpost, talk to Nulodion . He is in the house on the west side of the mining camp.
  • Return to Captain Lawgof , bring Nulodion's notes and your cannonball mould with you.
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Reward [ edit | edit source ]

  • 1 Quest point
  • 750 Crafting experience
  • The ability to buy and use a dwarf multicannon
  • The ability to make cannonballs
  • Membership of the Black Guard
