Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Inspect Pirate Pete .
Official difficulty Special
Official length Medium
Description Pirate Pete needs a fishcake to be protected. Talk to the Lumbridge Cook to learn how to make one.
Requirements
  • 31 Cooking ( boostable )
  • Completion of the following quests:
    • Recipe for Disaster/Another Cook's Quest
      • Cook's Assistant
  • The ability to defeat 5 level 30 monsters.
Items required
  • A raw cod (It's highly recommended to get at least 3 of all the ingredients, as the Fishcake can burn)
  • A pestle and mortar
  • Bread (at least 3 recommended)
  • A knife
  • A fishbowl (empty)
  • A needle
  • 3 bronze wire
Enemies to defeat
  • 5 Mudskippers (level 30/31)
  • Crab (level 21/23)
Ironman concerns

This quest has a weight limit.

  • 42 Crafting ( boostable ) (If crafting a fishbowl yourself or by completing Rum Deal )
  • 4 Smithing ( boostable ) (To make the bronze wire.)
League notes

Kandarin ✓ — location requirement
Unlocking Morytania ✓ will auto-complete the quest

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

Starting off [ edit | edit source ]

  • Inspect Pirate Pete.
  • Talk to the cook in the next room about protecting the pirate. ( 3 ) …
    3 Protecting the Pirate
  • Go through all 5 chat options. ( 1 • 1 • 2 • 3 • 4 • 5 ) …
    1 Where do I get Ground Cod?
    1 Where do I get Ground Kelp?
    2 Where do I get Ground Giant Crab Meat?3
    3 Where do I get Breadcrumbs?
    4 What do I do with all of it?
    5 Thanks!
  • Use your pestle and mortar on the raw cod .
  • Use your knife on the bread to make breadcrumbs .
  • Talk to Murphy in Port Khazard . ( 1 ) …
    1 Talk about Recipe for Disaster.
    ( Fishing Trawler minigame teleport is the easiest way to get there - requires 15 Fishing )
  • Talk to Murphy again. ( 1 ) …
    1 Yes, Let's go diving.

Under the Sea [ edit | edit source ]

Note: Before diving your weight must be under 27 kg and you must have at least 7 free inventory spaces (10 recommended). Paired with pieces of the Graceful outfit , you can easily match this weight while still bringing a weapon and armour such as the Varrock armour from the easy Varrock Diary , which weighs 1 kg. You also cannot go underwater with a pet following you or in your inventory. You may also wish to acquire multiple ingredients in case the fishcake is burnt.

  • Equip your fishbowl helmet and diving apparatus , Bring the needle and three bronze wire . Agree to go diving.
  • Pick some kelp . (It's highly recommended to pick at least 3 kelp as the fishcake can burn).
    • If you don't have at least 5 inventory spaces, skip this until leaving as you must gather 5 hide.
  • Go north and talk to Nung .
  • Go west and pick up 5 rocks , then enter the cave.
  • Kill 5 mudskippers (level 30/31) for 5 hides.
    • You can give the hide in more than one trip
  • Talk to Nung twice.
  • Enter the crab pen, kill a crab and take its meat. (It's highly recommended to get at least 3 Crab meat as the fishcake can burn).
  • Grind the kelp and crab meat with a pestle and mortar .
  • Teleport away, or go south and climb the anchor
  • Talk to the Cook in Lumbridge about freeing the pirate again. ( 3 ) …
    3 Protecting the Pirate
  • Use the ingredients on each other then cook the fishcake .
  • Give the fishcake to Pirate Pete in Lumbridge.
  • Congratulations, section complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 1 Quest point
  • 1,000 Cooking experience
  • 1,000 Crafting experience
  • 1,000 Fishing experience
  • 1,000 Smithing experience
  • The ability to go diving and fight crabs (you need your diving apparatus )
  • The ability to cook crab meat (heals 10 hitpoints in 5 bites)
  • The ability to kill crabs to get their shells and claws, which can be made into a helmet and gloves with a chisel
  • Further access to the Culinaromancer's Chest
