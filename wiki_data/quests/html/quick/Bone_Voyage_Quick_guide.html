Details [ edit | edit source ]

Start point Talk to Curator Haig Halen in the Varrock Museum . Show on map
Official difficulty Intermediate
Official length Short
Description Curator Haig Halen needs your help to finish the construction of a barge fit to take the Varrock museum crew to an unexplored landmass to the east. There have been many attempts to reach the island, but none were successful. This quest will have you venturing across Gielinor, aiding the Varrock museum and making the success of the voyage a possibility.
Requirements
  • 100 Kudos
  • Completion of the following quest:
    • The Dig Site
Items required
  • 2 vodka
  • Marrentill potion (unf)
Recommended
  • Fast travel to the Digsite ( Digsite teleport or Digsite pendant ; 3-4 teleports recommended)
  • Fast travel to the Lumber Yard (short walk from the Digsite or use a Lumberyard teleport -- alternatively, use the Balloon Transport System , costing a willow log per flight; 2 teleports recommended)
  • Fast travel to the Woodcutting Guild ( Skills necklace or Xeric's talisman to Xeric's Lookout)
  • Fast travel to Varrock ( Varrock teleport )
  • Fast travel to reach Port Sarim ( Amulet of glory or Explorer's ring 2 )

Post-quest recommended:

  • An iron bar , 2 oak planks , 5 nails and a hammer if you want to build the bank chest on Fossil Island ( Note: This requires 21 Construction )
Enemies to defeat None
League notes

Unlocking Misthalin ✓ will auto-complete the quest

Walkthrough [ edit | edit source ]

Starting out [ edit | edit source ]

  • Speak to Curator Haig Halen in the Varrock Museum . ( 1 • 1 ) …
    1 Have you any interesting news?
    1 Sign me up!

The finishing touches [ edit | edit source ]

  • Go to the canal barge north of The Digsite and speak to the barge foreman .
  • Speak to the sawmill operator in the Lumber Yard . ( 4 ) …
    4 I'm here on behalf of the museum archaeological team.
  • Bring the sawmill proposal to the sawmill operator in the Woodcutting Guild . ( 4 ) …
    4 I'm here on behalf of the museum archaeological team.
    • If you don't have the requirements to enter, just try and open the eastern gate of the guild to complete this step.
  • Bring the sawmill agreement back to the Lumber Yard's sawmill operator. ( 4 ) …
    4 I'm here on behalf of the museum archaeological team.

The cursed voyage [ edit | edit source ]

  • Speak to the barge foreman .
  • Right click the Board option on the barge guard .
  • Speak to the Lead Navigator . ( 1 • 2 ) …
    1 Yep, that would be me.
    2 I'm aware, no need to tell me about them.
  • Talk to Jack Seagull in the Rusty Anchor Inn in Port Sarim . ( 2 ) …
    2 Ever made any cursed voyages?
    or ( 3 ) …
    3 Ever made any cursed voyages?
  • Return to the northern part of the digsite and board the boat through the barge guard.
  • Talk to the Lead Navigator .
  • Speak to the Odd Old Man at the limestone mine to the north. You can get there by deboarding the boat and walking west, north, then east. ( 1 • 2 ) …
    1 Talk about lucky charms.
    2 I'm making a cursed voyage.
    He will give you the Bone charm .
  • Speak to the Apothecary in Varrock. Bring your two bottles of vodka and your Marrentill potion (unf)
    • Chat options depend on quests completed. Pick Talk about something else. , then pick Talk about Bone Voyage .
  • Speak to him again, selecting the same chat options as before.
  • Return to the Lead Navigator to give him the potion of sealegs .
  • Speak to the Junior Navigator to give him the bone charm . ( 1 ) …
    1 I'm ready, let's go.

The "cursed" voyage [ edit | edit source ]

  • You will have to steer left or right using the controls on the interface. The progress bar will indicate how far you have to go to reach the island.

When the top arrow tilts to the left, click on the right arrow.

When the top arrow tilts to the right, click on the left arrow.

Changing the height of the sails will alter how drastically the boat shifts from side to side. Having the boat's sails as large as possible when going in a straight line will allow for a more consistent travel route.

  • Once the progress bar is filled, you will arrive at Fossil Island.
  • Congratulations, quest complete!

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

  • 1 Quest point
  • Access to Fossil Island - to disembark the boat upon arrival at Fossil Island, speak to the Junior Navigator .
