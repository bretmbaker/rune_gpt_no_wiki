[Weapon: Jade Dragon Bolts (E)]


Jade dragon bolts (e)arejade dragon boltsthat are enchanted via theEnchant Crossbow Bolt (Jade)spell by a player with level 14Magic. The spell enchants 10 bolts per cast, and uses 1cosmicand 2earth runes. These bolts can only be used with adragon crossbowor better.
Enchanted jade dragon bolts have a 6% chance of triggering theEarth's Furyeffect, which results in the bolts knocking their target to the ground for 8 ticks (4.8 seconds), although agile opponents may resist this.
