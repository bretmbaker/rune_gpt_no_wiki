Ghommal's lucky pennyis an item equipped in the ammunition slot, obtained fromGhommalorLaidee Gnonockafter claiming the master tier rewards from theCombat Achievementssystem.
When equipped, it offers a 5% chance to not use a charge when using items that consume charges (such as jewellery and weapons). When equipped in conjunction with anelvenorcelestial signet, both items will roll independently at a chance to not use a charge while using a piece ofcrystal equipment.[1]
