[Weapon: Rada%27S Blessing 4]


Rada's blessing 4is anAchievement diaryreward for completing all eliteKourend & Kebos Diarytasks. It is given byElisein theKourend Castlecourtyard, and can be retrieved for free if lost. Players can also trade withPerduwho sells blessings for 200 coins each, this is especially handy if buying bulk asPerduallows the player to buy a full inventory at once. It can be stored in atackle box.
Rada's blessing 4 gives the best-in-slotprayer bonusfor the ammunition slot at +2, surpassinggod blessings.
