[Weapon: Dragonstone Bolts]


Dragonstone boltsare runite bolts tipped with dragonstone. Dragonstone bolts can be created at level 71Fletchingskill by attaching dragonstone bolt tips to runite bolts. For everydragonstone bolt tipattached to arunite bolt, a player is granted 8.2 Fletching experience.
Players can enchant dragonstone bolts by using the spellEnchant Crossbow Bolt (Dragonstone), producingdragonstone bolts (e). The spell itself requires asoul rune,cosmic rune, and 15earth runes. The spell requires 68 Magic and enchants 10 dragonstone bolts at a time.
