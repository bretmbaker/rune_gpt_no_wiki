[Weapon: Ruby Bolts (E)]


Ruby bolts (e)areadamant boltsthat have been enchanted after having been tipped withruby bolt tips.Ruby boltscan be enchanted with the spellEnchant Crossbow Bolt (Ruby)by a player with level 49Magic, requiring 46Rangedto wield. The spell enchants 10 ruby bolts at a time and uses 1blood rune, 1cosmic rune, and 5fire runes.
Ruby bolts (e) have a chance of activating theBlood Forfeiteffect. When activated, damage dealt to the target will be 20% of the opponent'scurrentHitpointswith a cap of 100 damage[1]and at the cost of 10% of the player'scurrentHitpoints. The effect does not activate if the player cannot spare at least 1 hitpoint. A long, shrieking noise can be heard when this activates.
