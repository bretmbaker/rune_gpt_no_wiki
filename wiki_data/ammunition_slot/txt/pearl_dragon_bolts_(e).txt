[Weapon: Pearl Dragon Bolts (E)]


Pearl dragon bolts (e)arepearl dragon boltsthat are enchanted via theEnchant Crossbow Bolt (Pearl)spell by a player with level 24Magic. The spell enchants 10 bolts per cast, and uses 1cosmicand 2water runes. These bolts can only be used with aDragon crossbowor better.
Enchanted pearl dragon bolts have a chance (6%) of triggering theSea Curseeffect. This will cause a mighty bolt of water to hit the opponent. This ability is negated by opponents wielding water staves, but increased by opponents wearing fire staves or fiery monsters.
