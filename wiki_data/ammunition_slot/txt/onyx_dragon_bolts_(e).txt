[Weapon: Onyx Dragon Bolts (E)]


Onyx dragon bolts (e)areonyx dragon boltsthat are enchanted via theEnchant Crossbow Bolt (Onyx)spell by a player with level 87Magic. The spell enchants 10 bolts per cast, and uses 1cosmic, 1death, and 20fire runes. These bolts can only be used with aDragon crossbowor better.
Enchanted onyx dragon bolts have a chance of triggering theLife Leecheffect. This will deal 20% extra damage  (32% when under the effect of theZaryte crossbow)[1]and heal the attacker'sHitpointsby 25% of the total damage dealt. This does not work on the undead, as they have no "life" to leech.
