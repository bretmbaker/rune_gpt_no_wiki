[Weapon: Bronze Arrow#(P)]


Thebronze arrowis anarrowmade ofbronze. These arrows are the weakest type of arrows inOld School RuneScape. Anybowis capable of shooting bronze arrows. As with mostarrows, they can bepoisoned.
In order to create this item players must first smeltbronze bars. Then using ahammerandanvil, the player must turn the bar intobronze arrowtips. Creating the bronze bar has no Smithing requirement, while a player must have level 5Smithingto create the arrowheads. Then players should cut logs, and using aknife, turn them intoarrow shafts. After attachingfeathersto the arrow shafts (creatingheadless arrows) players can then use the arrowheads on the headless arrows to create bronze arrows. This entire process requires level 1Fletching.
