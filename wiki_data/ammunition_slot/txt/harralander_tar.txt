[Weapon: Harralander Tar]


Harralander taris an item used as food, or "ammunition", forblack salamanderswhen used as a weapon. It is also optionally used as bait for huntingblack salamandersto increase the catch rate by 3%.
Harralander tar can be made at level 44Herbloreby using 15swamp taron aharralander, whilst having apestle and mortarin the inventory. This process yields 15 harralander tar, and grants 72.5 Herblore experience.
