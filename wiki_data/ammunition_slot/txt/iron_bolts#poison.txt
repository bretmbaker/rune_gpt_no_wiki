[Weapon: Iron Bolts#Poison]


Iron boltsare the third-weakest bolts, stronger thanblurite bolts. Iron bolts may be shot fromironor bettercrossbows. As with mostbolts, they can bepoisoned, however this will prevent the addition ofbolt tips.
Iron bolts may be created by players. First, a player must smithunfinished iron bolts. This requires aSmithinglevel of 18, and for eachiron bara player smiths they will get 10 unfinished iron bolts and 25 Smithingexperience. Then a player must attach flights to the bolts. Attaching flights to each bolt requires aFletchinglevel of 39 and 1feather, granting 1.5 Fletching experience per bolt.
