[Weapon: Diamond Bolts]


Diamond boltsareadamant boltstipped withdiamond bolt tips. They are the fourth strongestgem-tipped bolt in the game. These bolts can be created using theFletchingskill. 65 Fletching is required to make these bolts, and 7 Fletchingexperienceis rewarded per bolt created.
Diamond bolts can be enchanted with theEnchant Crossbow Boltspell, producingDiamond bolts (e). This requires 57Magic. The spell enchants 10 bolts at a time.
