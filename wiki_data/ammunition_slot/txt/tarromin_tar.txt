[Weapon: Tarromin Tar]


Tarromin taris an item used as food, or "ammunition", forred salamanderswhen used as a weapon. It is also optionally used as bait for huntingred salamandersto increase the catch rate by 3%.
Tarromin tar can be made at level 39Herbloreby using 15swamp taron atarromin, whilst having apestle and mortarin the inventory. This process yields 15 tarromin tar, and grants 55 Herblore experience.
