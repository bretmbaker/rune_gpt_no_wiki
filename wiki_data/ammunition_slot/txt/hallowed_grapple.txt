[Weapon: Hallowed Grapple]


Hallowed grappleis an item that has a 100% chance of succeeding a grapple shortcut inside theHallowed Sepulchre. It can be purchased from theMysterious Hallowed Goodsshop for 100hallowed marks. The grapple doesnotwork outside of theHallowed Sepulchre.
The grapple also counts as a Saradomin item for the purposes of recharging your runenergyat the Magical Obelisks at the end of each floor, as well as inGod Wars Dungeon.
