[Weapon: Moonlight Antler Bolts]


Moonlight antler boltsare ranged ammunition for thehunters' sunlight crossbow. They can be created at level 72Fletchingby using achiselon amoonlight antelope antler; each antler produces 12 bolts and 12 Fletching experience.
Unlike other arrows and bolts,Weapon poisonis not able to be applied to them.
