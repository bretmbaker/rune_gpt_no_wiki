[Weapon: Marrentill Tar]


Marrentill taris an item used as food, or "ammunition", fororange salamanderswhen used as a weapon. It is also optionally used as bait for huntingorange salamandersto increase the catch rate by 3%.
Marrentill tar can be made at level 31Herbloreby using 15swamp taron amarrentill, whilst having apestle and mortarin the inventory. This process yields 15 marrentill tar, and grants 42.5 Herblore experience.
