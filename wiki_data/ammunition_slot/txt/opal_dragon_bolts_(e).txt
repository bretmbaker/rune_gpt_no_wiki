[Weapon: Opal Dragon Bolts (E)]


Opal dragon bolts (e)areopal dragon boltsthat are enchanted via theEnchant Crossbow Bolt (Opal)spell by a player with level 4Magic. The spell enchants 10 bolts per cast, and uses 1cosmicand 2air runes. These bolts can only be used with aDragon crossbowor better.
Enchanted opal dragon bolts have a chance (5%) of triggering theLucky Lightningeffect, which results in a lightning bolt striking your opponent and causing extra damage equal to 10% of your visible ranged level. For example, at 112 ranged level, the extra damage is 11.
