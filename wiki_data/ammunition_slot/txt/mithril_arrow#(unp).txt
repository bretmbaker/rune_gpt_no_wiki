[Weapon: Mithril Arrow#(Unp)]


Mithril arrowsarearrowsmade ofmithril. Awillow bowor better is required to shoot mithril arrows. Stronger thansteel arrows, mithril arrows can be made at level 45Fletchingby usingmithril arrowtipsonheadless arrows. Doing so grants 112.5experiencefor every batch of 15 arrows made. They can be bought fromLowe's Archery EmporiuminVarrock. As with mostarrows, they can bepoisoned.
