[Weapon: Sunlight Antler Bolts]


Sunlight antler boltsare ranged ammunition for thehunters' sunlight crossbow. They can be created at level 62Fletchingby using achiselon asunlight antelope antler; each antler produces 12 bolts and 10 Fletching experience.
Unlike other arrows and bolts,Weapon poisonis not able to be applied to them.
