[Weapon: Blurite Bolts#Unpoisoned]


Blurite boltsare the second weakest type of bolts. They are stronger thanbronze boltsbut weaker thaniron bolts. They can only be fired from at least ablurite crossbowor better. As with mostbolts, they can bepoisoned, however this will prevent the addition ofbolt tips.
Blurite bolts cannot be bought, and can only be created by players. First, in order to make blurite bolts, a player must smithunfinished blurite bolts. This requires aSmithinglevel of 8, and for eachblurite barthat player smiths they will get 10 unfinished blurite bolts and 17 Smithingexperience. Then a player must attach flights to the bolts. Attaching flights to each bolt requires aFletchinglevel of 24 and 1feather, granting 1 Fletching experience per bolt.
