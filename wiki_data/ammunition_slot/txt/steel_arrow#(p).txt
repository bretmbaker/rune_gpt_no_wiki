[Weapon: Steel Arrow#(P)]


Thesteel arrowis anarrowmade out ofsteel arrowtips,arrow shaftsandfeathers. AFletchinglevel of 30 is required to make steel arrows. You also have to have aSmithinglevel of 35 to make your own steel arrowtips. Steel arrows can be fired fromoak bowsor better. As with mostarrows, they can bepoisoned.
Ava's accumulator(from theAnimal Magnetismquest) generates steel arrows in theammo slot, and also randomly attracts items into your inventory.
