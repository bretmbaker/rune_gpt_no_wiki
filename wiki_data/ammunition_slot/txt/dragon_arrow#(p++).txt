[Weapon: Dragon Arrow#(P++)]


Dragon arrowsare the strongest type ofarrowin the game. They are only usable with thedark bow,twisted bow,venator bow,scorching bow, and the3rd age bow. As with mostarrows, they can bepoisoned.
They can be created through theFletchingskillat level 90 by usingdragon arrowtipsonheadless arrows, granting 15 Fletchingexperienceper arrow made. 15 arrows can be made at a time, giving 225 Fletching experience.
