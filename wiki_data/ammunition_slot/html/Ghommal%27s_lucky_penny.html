Ghommal's lucky penny is an item equipped in the ammunition slot, obtained from Ghommal or Laidee Gnonock after claiming the master tier rewards from the Combat Achievements system.

When equipped, it offers a 5% chance to not use a charge when using items that consume charges (such as jewellery and weapons). When equipped in conjunction with an elven or celestial signet, both items will roll independently at a chance to not use a charge while using a piece of crystal equipment.[1]
