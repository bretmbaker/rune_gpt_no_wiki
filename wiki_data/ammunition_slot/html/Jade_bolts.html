Jade bolts are blurite bolts tipped with jade. Jade bolts can be created through the Fletching skill. A player must first have a Fletching level of 26 and take a cut jade and use a chisel with it to make 12 Jade bolt tips, granting 2 Fletching experience. Attaching jade bolt tips also requires a Fletching level of 26, and for each jade bolt tip attached to a blurite bolt, a player is granted 2.4 Fletching experience. Once the jade bolt tips have been attached, the blurite bolts become jade bolts.

Jade bolts can be enchanted through the spell Enchant Crossbow Bolt (Jade) by a player with level 14 Magic. The spell enchants 10 jade bolts at a time, and once they are enchanted they become enchanted jade bolts and gain a special ability.
