Mithril arrows are arrows made of mithril. A willow bow or better is required to shoot mithril arrows. Stronger than steel arrows, mithril arrows can be made at level 45 Fletching by using mithril arrowtips on headless arrows. Doing so grants 112.5 experience for every batch of 15 arrows made. They can be bought from Lowe's Archery Emporium in Varrock. As with most arrows, they can be poisoned.
