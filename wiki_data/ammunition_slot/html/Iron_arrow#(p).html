The Iron arrow is an arrow made by combining arrow shafts with feathers, then with iron arrowtips. These can be bought from Lowe's Archery Emporium in Varrock or Hickton's Archery Emporium in Catherby. They can also be obtained from completing the Maze random event. As with most arrows, they can be poisoned.
