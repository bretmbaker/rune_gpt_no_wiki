Pearl dragon bolts (e) are pearl dragon bolts that are enchanted via the Enchant Crossbow Bolt (Pearl) spell by a player with level 24 Magic. The spell enchants 10 bolts per cast, and uses 1 cosmic and 2 water runes. These bolts can only be used with a Dragon crossbow or better.

Special effect[edit | edit source]

Enchanted pearl dragon bolts have a chance (6%) of triggering the Sea Curse effect. This will cause a mighty bolt of water to hit the opponent. This ability is negated by opponents wielding water staves, but increased by opponents wearing fire staves or fiery monsters.

The Sea Curse effect increases damage by 1/15 of the player's visible Ranged level to fiery units, and 1/20 against other targets. The fiery units affected are:

  • All adult chromatic dragons, including lava dragons and the King Black Dragon
  • All metal dragons
  • Fire giants
  • Pyrefiends
  • Fire elemental
  • Players equipped with the fire cape or fire staves

If the player has completed the Hard Kandarin Diary, there is a 10% increase of the likelihood that the special effect of an enchanted bolt will occur (to 6.6%). This effect is always active regardless if the headgear is equipped.

This effect rolls its chance of activation regardless of whether or not it passes an accuracy roll.
