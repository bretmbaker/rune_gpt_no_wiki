Harralander tar is an item used as food, or "ammunition", for black salamanders when used as a weapon. It is also optionally used as bait for hunting black salamanders to increase the catch rate by 3%.

Harralander tar can be made at level 44 Herblore by using 15 swamp tar on a harralander, whilst having a pestle and mortar in the inventory. This process yields 15 harralander tar, and grants 72.5 Herblore experience.
