Amethyst arrows can be used with any bow made with magic logs or stronger, such as the magic shortbow(i). They can also be used by the bone shortbow. As with most arrows, they can be poisoned.

They can be crafted with a Fletching level of 82 or higher by using headless arrows on amethyst arrowtips in sets of 15, granting 202.5 Fletching experience.

Amethyst arrows have a +6 higher Ranged strength bonus compared to rune arrows. The strongest arrow type, dragon arrows, have a +5 higher Ranged strength bonus advantage over amethyst arrows. However, dragon arrows are much more costly to use.

Despite not being made of metal, amethyst arrows do function with Ava's devices obtained from the completion of Animal Magnetism.
