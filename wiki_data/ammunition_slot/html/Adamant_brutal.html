Adamant brutal arrows are used to kill Zogres south of the Castle Wars arena or Chompy birds. They are made with flighted ogre arrows and Adamantite nails (a hammer is also required). Level 62 Fletching is required to make these and they will yield 10.2 experience per arrow. They can only be used with a Composite ogre bow.
