Amethyst javelins are a type of Ranged ammunition used by the light ballista and the heavy ballista. They are the second-strongest type of javelin, surpassed only by the dragon javelin. As with most javelins, they can be poisoned.

They can be fletched by attaching amethyst javelin heads to javelin shafts at level 84 Fletching, granting 202.5 experience per 15 javelins made.
