Amethyst fire arrows are amethyst arrows wrapped in an oily cloth, which are then lit by using them on a tinderbox. They, along with any other fire arrow, are primarily used to pierce the rope in the Underground Pass to lower the bridge so that a player may walk across.

They have the same Ranged Strength as regular amethyst arrows.
