Kree'arra is one of Armadyl's most trusted generals. He is the most powerful of the surviving aviansie, and leader to the forces of Armadyl's Eyrie in the God Wars Dungeon.

In order to reach the encampment, players must first wield any crossbow along with a mithril grapple. A Ranged level of at least 70 is then required to navigate the chasm. To enter the boss room, players must use an ecumenical key, or have slain 40[1] followers of Armadyl, with aviansie and their spiritual variants being his only followers within the dungeon.

Kree'arra can be seen yelling several battle cries in his chamber. However, unlike the other generals, his taunts are non-verbal.

Fight overview[edit | edit source]

Main article: Kree'arra/Strategies

Like other God Wars Dungeon bosses, Kree'arra roams around a rectangular room with his bodyguards until a player enters, attacking them on sight. He attacks primarily with Ranged, and his bodyguards use a different combat style each. However, if no one is targeting Kree'arra, he will fly up to players to use Melee attacks as well. If all players leave, his health and stats will be reset.

Like other Armadylean followers in the God Wars Dungeon, he and his bodyguards cannot be attacked with Melee. This includes Magic-based special attacks from melee weapons, such as the Voidwaker.
