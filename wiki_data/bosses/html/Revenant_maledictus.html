The Revenant maledictus is the demi-boss variant of the ethereal revenants, an undead spectral being shrouded in Zarosian power. It has a rare chance to appear whenever any revenant is slain in the Revenant Caves. It guarantees an ancient emblem or ancient totem drop for the top-damaging player, in addition to giving the player the Forinthry Surge buff if they had an amulet of avarice worn.

Mechanics[edit | edit source]

Spawning[edit | edit source]

There is a small chance of the maledictus appearing whenever any revenant is slain in the Revenant Caves. This chance is calculated each kill with the total accumulated combat levels of revenants slain by all players on that world, divided by a flat spawn rate. Once the maledictus appears, this chance is reset and the boss is prevented from appearing for 45 minutes, after which the spawn chance begins accumulating once again.

While the maledictus is active, all players in the cavern will be notified of its presence and given a hint as to where it appeared: northern, middle, or southern areas of the caves. Players in range will also see a flashing arrow on their mini-map pointing to the boss's location, making an encounter with other players all but guaranteed. The maledictus will remain active for 10 minutes[confirmation needed] before disappearing, if not killed.

The maledictus can spawn in the following areas:

  • Revenant cyclops/Revenant demon chamber (north)
  • Revenant dragon's chamber (north)
  • Northern revenant imp's area (north)
  • Revenant hellhound's/old revenants' ruins (middle)
  • Eastern revenant pyrefiend's area (middle)
  • Western revenant pyrefiend's chamber (middle)
  • Revenant dark beast's chamber (middle)
  • Northern revenant ork's chamber (middle)
  • Southern revenant ork's chamber (south)
  • Southern revenant demon's chamber (south)
  • Outside the southern revenant demon's area (south)

Attacks[edit | edit source]

Despite the caves being singles-plus combat, the maledictus itself is functionally multi-combat; it can be attacked by any number of players, and will attack all players in range simultaneously. The maledictus will cycle through its three special attacks after every few standard attacks, telegraphing each attack with the Zarosian symbol on its back. Protection prayers will reduce damage taken by half. Its attacks are as follows:

  • Standard attack: a white, spike-shaped ranged attack.
  • Ice spell: When the symbol glows blue, it will target players' current tiles, then freeze anyone who doesn't move, dealing damage and preventing them from moving or attacking.
  • Blood spell: When the symbol glows red, it will target players' current tiles, then deal damage and heal itself for half of the total damage dealt.
  • Air surge: When the symbol glows white, it will launch a typeless area-of-effect at one player. The spell explodes outward from where it lands, and deals up to 25 damage to anyone in a 5x5 area, but less near the edges.

Forinthry Surge[edit | edit source]

Upon death, top-damaging players wearing an amulet of avarice will receive the Forinthry Surge () skull, which provides an additional 15% accuracy and damage buff against all revenants for 30 minutes. The Forinthry Surge skull can also be acquired by killing another player who has it, as long as both players are wearing an avarice. Taking the amulet off will remove the buff, while changing worlds will reduce its duration by 1 minute. The equipped amulet can be checked to show the Surge's time remaining.

Bonus drop[edit | edit source]

The revenant maledictus rolls three drops for the top-damaging player: one for either an ancient totem or emblem, and the remaining two are rolls taken from the revenant dragon's drop table and rates. All players that dealt damage to the boss receive one blighted super restore and two pieces of blighted food, dropped under them (even after leaving the area). Ironmen as normal can only obtain drops when killing the boss by themselves, but can still receive the Forinthry Surge. The drop table shown below has been copied from the revenant dragon, and only represents one of the two non-guaranteed loot rolls.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • "Maledictus" is a Latin word meaning "accursed [one]".
  • Its examine text is a reference to The Lord of the Rings.
  • The design of the revenant maledictus references the visual language of a typical Magic-based player killer: its horns evoke the elongated shoulder pads of a mystic robe top, while the large Zarosian icon carried on its back alludes to the ancient staff.[1]
