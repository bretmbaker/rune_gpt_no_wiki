Spindel is a weaker variant of Venenatis who is fought in the Web Chasm, a singles-plus combat area in level 29 Wilderness. To access Spindel, players must have completed the medium Wilderness diary.

Compared to Venenatis, the fight is easier and safer in return for lower drop quantities and rates.

Killing Spindel counts as a Venenatis alternative for a slayer Boss task, the elite Wilderness diary task, and the combat achievements.

Upon any player entering the chasm, all players inside will be unable to teleport for 3 ticks. This delay no longer occurs after completion of the hard Wilderness diary.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
17 April 2024
(update)

Spindel will now wait a short period of time after respawning before attacking players.

10 April 2024
(update | poll)

Spindel's respawn time was decreased from 25 ticks (15 seconds) to 9 ticks (5.4 seconds).

20 September 2023
(update)

Access to Spindel is now locked behind the medium Wilderness diary.

14 June 2023
(update)

Spindel now drops strange fruits at a rate of 1 in 5.

12 April 2023
(update)

Spindel has been added to the HiScores.

8 February 2023
(update)

Fixed an issue where Spindel's hitpoints bar was moving during attack animations.

2 February 2023
(update)
  • Spindel is limited to a maximum of 10 spiderlings, no matter how many players are in the fight.
  • Increased the minimum loot reward from 0% to 15%.
  • Reduced maximum number of players able to receive loot from 15 to 10.
  • Increased quantity of base rewards.
  • Spindel is now immune to Ring of recoil and similar effects.

Trivia[edit | edit source]
