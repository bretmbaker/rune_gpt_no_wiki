General Danknuck was a high ranking goblin general who lead the final attempt on the invasion on Falador during the 2014 Goblin Invasion event. He could only be harmed with an event rpg, and had similar drops to Painted Goblins, albeit in higher quantities. He was removed from the game at the end of the event.
