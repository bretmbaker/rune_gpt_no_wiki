Calvar'ion is a weaker variant of Vet'ion who is fought in the Skeletal Tomb, a singles-plus combat area in level 21 Wilderness. To access Calvar'ion, players must have completed the medium Wilderness diary.

Compared to Vet'ion, the fight is quicker and safer at the cost of an overall lower loot value.

Killing Calvar'ion counts as a Vet'ion alternative for a slayer Boss task, the elite Wilderness diary task, and the combat achievements.

Upon any player entering the tomb, all players inside will be unable to teleport for 3 ticks. This delay no longer occurs after completion of the hard Wilderness diary.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
17 April 2024
(update)

Calvar'ion will now wait a short period of time after respawning before attacking players.

10 April 2024
(update | poll)

Calvar'ion's respawn time was decreased from 30 ticks (18 seconds) to 14 ticks (8.4 seconds).

6 March 2024
(update)

Calvar'ion's lightning circle is now brighter and more visible.

20 September 2023
(update)

Access to Calvar'ion is now locked behind the medium Wilderness diary.

12 April 2023
(update)

Calvar'ion has been added to the HiScores.

2 February 2023
(update)
  • Calvarion now only summons two hounds no matter how many players are in the fight.
  • Calvarion will now only spawn up to seven lightning strikes at one time, regardless of the number of players in the arena.
  • Increased the minimum loot reward from 0% to 15%.
  • Reduced maximum number of players able to receive loot from 15 to 10.
  • Increased quantity of base rewards.
  • Calvar'ion is now immune to Ring of recoil and similar effects.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Its name comes from "calvria", meaning "skull" in Latin.
  • Its examine text may reference the "King of the Dead" from the Lord of the Rings.
