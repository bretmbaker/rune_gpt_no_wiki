Lumbridge & Draynor Diary
Lumbridge & Draynor Diary Released 5 March 2015 ( Update ) Area(s) Lumbridge , Draynor , Al Kharid , Dorgesh-Kaan and Zanaris Members Yes Item reward Ring Lamp XP rewards 2,500 7,500 15,000 50,000 Taskmaster(s) Hatius Cosaintus League region Misthalin âœ“
The Lumbridge & Draynor Diary is a set of achievement diaries whose tasks revolve around areas in and around Lumbridge , such as Draynor Village , Al Kharid , Dorgesh-Kaan , and Zanaris .
Several skill, quest and item requirements are needed to complete all tasks. Unless stated otherwise, temporary skill boosts can be used to meet the skill requirements.
Rewards can be claimed from Hatius Cosaintus east of Lumbridge Castle 's courtyard in Lumbridge . Note that the previous tiers of a diary must be completed to claim rewards of the next tier.
