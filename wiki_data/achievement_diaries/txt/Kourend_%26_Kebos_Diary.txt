Kourend & Kebos Diary
Kourend & Kebos Diary Released 10 January 2019 ( Update ) Area(s) Great Kourend , Kebos Lowlands Members Yes Item reward Blessing Lamp XP rewards 2,500 7,500 15,000 50,000 Taskmaster(s) Elise League region Kourend ✓
The Kourend & Kebos Diary , also known as the Zeah Diary , is a set of achievement diaries whose tasks revolve around areas in Great Kourend as well as the Kebos Lowlands .
Several skill, quest and item requirements are needed to complete all tasks. Unless stated otherwise, temporary skill boosts can be used to meet the skill requirements.
Rewards can be claimed from Elise , south-east of the statue of King Rada I in the courtyard of the Kourend Castle . Note that the previous tiers of a diary must be completed to claim rewards of the next tier.
Certain tasks that can be tracked, such as finishing a raid in Chambers of Xeric , will be automatically marked as done if the player had completed it before the diary was released.
