Ardougne Diary
Ardougne Diary Released 5 March 2015 ( Update ) Area(s) Ardougne , Port Khazard and Yanille Members Yes Item reward Cloak Lamp XP rewards 2,500 7,500 15,000 50,000 Taskmaster(s) Two-pints League region Kandarin âœ“
The Ardougne Diary is a set of achievement diaries whose tasks revolve around areas in and near Ardougne , such as Yanille and Port Khazard .
Several skill, quest and item requirements are needed to complete all tasks. Unless stated otherwise, temporary skill boosts can be used to meet the skill requirements.
Rewards can be claimed from Two-pints in the Flying Horse Inn , in East Ardougne . Note that the previous tiers of a diary must be completed to claim rewards of the next tier.
