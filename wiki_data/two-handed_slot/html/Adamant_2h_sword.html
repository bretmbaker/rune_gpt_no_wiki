The adamant 2h sword is a two-handed sword that requires 30 Attack to wield. As with other two-handed weapons, a shield may not be equipped while wielding one.

Players may smith one at level 84 Smithing with 3 adamantite bars for 187.5 Smithing experience.

Its strength bonus is identical to that of a rune scimitar.
