The iron felling axe is a type of felling axe requiring an Attack level of 1 to be equipped, and a Woodcutting level of 1 to be used. Players can create the axe by using a felling axe handle on an iron axe while having a hammer in their inventory and standing next to an anvil; this process is irreversible, but the felling axe itself remains tradeable.

Iron felling axes will chop at the same speed as a normal iron axe. If the player is carrying forester's rations however (either in the inventory or inside a forestry kit), they will receive 10% more Woodcutting experience each time they successfully chop a tree and gain a 20% chance not to receive logs when chopping trees (or tree roots in the Rising Roots forestry event), at the cost of consuming one ration. This also results in a 20% chance to not receive anima-infused bark from tree roots.

If the player has no rations on their person, the felling axe will function like a regular iron axe.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
1 November 2023
(update)
  • A bug in which the XP bonus from felling axes did not correctly stack with the lumberjack outfit has been fixed.
  • The item's name in the skill guide interface has been corrected from "Iron feeling axe".
25 October 2023
(update)
  • The item was made obtainable and members-only.
  • The item was renamed from "Iron 2h axe" to "Iron felling axe".
  • The item's weight was increased from 1.36 kg to 1.814 kg.
  • The item's value was decreased from 161 to 56.
  • The item's examine was changed; previously, it was "A woodcutter's axe."
28 June 2023
(update)

The item was added to the game cache, but was unobtainable.
