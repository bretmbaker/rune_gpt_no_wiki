The Amlodd bow of Faerdhinen (c) is a cosmetic variant of the bow of Faerdhinen (c) made by using the crystal of Amlodd on any variant of the bow.

The bow's colours, based on that of the Amlodd Clan, may be changed to other colours of the Elven Clans by buying crystals from Lliann's Wares in Prifddinas, which cost 500,000 each.
