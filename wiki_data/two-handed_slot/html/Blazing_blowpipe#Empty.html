The blazing blowpipe is a toxic blowpipe with a Trailblazer reloaded blowpipe ornament kit added onto it. The kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the blowpipe. To add the kit, players must empty the blowpipe of Zulrah's scales and darts. Players can configure the charges while the blowpipe is in their bank by right-clicking on Configure-Charges, as long as players have the required materials.

Whilst in its ornamented state, the item can be reverted anytime, returning the blowpipe and kit.
