The Bandos godsword (or) is a Bandos godsword with a Bandos godsword ornament kit attached to it. The attached ornament kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the godsword. It costs 16,802,298 for both the parts to make the Bandos godsword (or).

While the godsword is untradeable in its ornamented state, it can be dismantled anytime, returning the tradeable godsword and ornament kit.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
9 December 2021
(update)

The Bandos godsword (or) now shows its ornamentation correctly when wielded by female characters. Previously, it would appear as if a normal Bandos godsword was being wielded.

7 July 2021
(update)

The weapon's position on female players has been adjusted so that they appear to be equipped properly.

14 July 2016
(update)

The item's inventory sprite was slightly adjusted.
