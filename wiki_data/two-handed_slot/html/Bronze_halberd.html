The bronze halberd is a halberd made out of bronze. It can be purchased from the Quartermaster in the Tyras Camp or received as drops from Orks. Like all halberds, this weapon has an attack range of two squares, making it the only variety of melee weapon that can be used to safespot monsters. Halberds cannot be made with the Smithing skill.
