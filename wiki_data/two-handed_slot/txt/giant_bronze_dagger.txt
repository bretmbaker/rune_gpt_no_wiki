[Weapon: Giant Bronze Dagger]


Thegiant bronze daggeris a reward obtained from the2023 Easter event, as well as subsequent Easter events. Despite its size, its stats are equivalent to the normal-sizedbronze dagger, except it weighs much more. When wielded, players will be given a unique animation akin to that of thecolossal blade.
If destroyed, a replacement can be obtained fromDiangoinDraynor Villageor from thetoy boxof acostume room.
