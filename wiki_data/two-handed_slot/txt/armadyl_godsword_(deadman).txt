[Weapon: Armadyl Godsword (Deadman)]


TheDeadman Armadyl godswordis an ornamented variant of thearmadyl godsword, made by giving it toNigelafter having used anArmageddon weapon scrollon him. The attached ornament kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the Armadyl godsword.
While the godsword is untradeable in its ornamented state, it can be reverted to the original tradeable form by speaking to Nigel.
