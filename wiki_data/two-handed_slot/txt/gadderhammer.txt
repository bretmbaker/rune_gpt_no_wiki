[Weapon: Gadderhammer]


Thegadderhammeris a two-handedmembers-onlymaul. There are no combat requirements for wielding the gadderhammer. To obtain the gadderhammer the player must defeatGadderanksduringIn Aid of the Myreque. Even though it can be purchased from another player or theGrand Exchange, it cannot be wielded until Gadderanks is defeated.
This weapon cannot be made using theSmithingskill. It has a very similar appearance and combat animation to thegranite maul, and stats similar to anAdamant warhammer.
