[Weapon: Yew Longbow]


Theyew longbowis alongbowstronger than themaple longbowthat can use arrows up torune. The yew longbow requires aRangedlevel of 40 or higher to wield.
Players can make a yew longbow through theFletchingskill at level 70. First, a player must cut ayew longbow (u)fromyew logs, giving 75 Fletchingexperience. The unstrung bow is then strung with abow string, for an additional 75 experience for a total of 150 experience a log. It can also be obtained as a very rare drop from ayoung impling.
