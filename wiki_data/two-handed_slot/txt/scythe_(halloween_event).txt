[Weapon: Scythe (Halloween Event)]


Thescytheis an untradeableevent reward. It can be obtained by completing one of theHalloweenholiday eventsthat occur once per year.
The scythe is not particularly powerful, as it is intended to be a decorativeweapon. It has very low bonuses and a slow attack speed. Notably, it has several unique attack animations.
