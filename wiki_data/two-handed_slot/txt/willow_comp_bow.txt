[Weapon: Willow Comp Bow]


Willow composite bowsare a reward from EasyTreasure Trails. It requires aRangedlevel of 20 to use and can fire up tomithril arrows. The attack range of the willow composite bow is ten squares.
The willow composite bow cannot be made using theFletchingskill. It can, however, be obtained through trading with another player.
