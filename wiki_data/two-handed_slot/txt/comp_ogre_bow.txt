[Weapon: Comp Ogre Bow]


Thecomp ogre bowwas released with the questZogre Flesh Eatersand is made by using a knife on anachey tree logswithwolf bonesin a player's inventory to create anunstrung comp bow, which can then be strung with abow stringto create the finished bow. This is the only composite bow that can be made through theFletchingskill at level 30.
When compared to the normalogre bow, the comp bow has the same attack bonus and a faster attack speed, but a shorter range. It requires 30Rangedto wield.
