[Weapon: Blazing Blowpipe#Empty]


Theblazing blowpipeis atoxic blowpipewith aTrailblazer reloaded blowpipe ornament kitadded onto it. The kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the blowpipe. To add the kit, players must empty the blowpipe ofZulrah's scalesanddarts. Players can configure the charges while the blowpipe is in their bank by right-clicking on Configure-Charges, as long as players have the required materials.
Whilst in its ornamented state, the item can be reverted anytime, returning the blowpipe and kit.
