[Weapon: Steel Felling Axe]


Thesteel felling axeis a type offelling axerequiring anAttacklevel of 5 to be equipped, and aWoodcuttinglevel of 6 to be used. Players can create the axe by using afelling axe handleon asteel axewhile having ahammerin their inventory and standing next to ananvil; this process isirreversible, but the felling axe itself remains tradeable.
Steel felling axes will chop at the same speed as a normal steel axe. If the player is carryingforester's rationshowever (either in the inventory or inside aforestry kit), they will receive 10% more Woodcutting experience each time they successfully chop a tree and gain a 20% chance not to receive logs when chopping trees (ortree rootsin theRising Rootsforestry event), at the cost of consuming one ration. This also results in a 20% chance to not receiveanima-infused barkfrom tree roots.
