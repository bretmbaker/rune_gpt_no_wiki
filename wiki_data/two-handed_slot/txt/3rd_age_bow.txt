[Weapon: 3Rd Age Bow]


The3rd age bowis a part of the3rd age range set. It is a possible reward fromeliteandmasterTreasure Trailsand cannot be made via theFletchingskill. Requiring 65Rangedto wield, the 3rd age bow can fire arrows up todragon arrowsat the same speed as that of ashortbowwhilst having a longer attack range.
It is considered to be one of the more useful 3rd age equipment pieces; it can be utilised to shoot faraway monsters duringFight CavesandInfernospeed runs and is also valued in theBarbarian Assaultattacker role.
