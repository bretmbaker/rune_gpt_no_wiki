[Weapon: Adamant Spear#(P)]


Theadamant spearis a two-handedmembers'-onlymelee weapon. It can be bought from theMount Karuulm Weapon ShoporFortis Blacksmith, or obtained as a drop from monsters. Using theSmithingskill, players can create adamant spears with level 75SmithingThis requires 1adamantite barand 1yew log. However, players must have begunBarbarian Smithingtraining to be able to smith these spears. Like most spears, the adamant spear can bepoisoned. Smithing an adamant spear at the anvil atOtto's Grottois a requirement for theKandarin Hard Diary.
