[Weapon: Steel Spear#(P++)]


Thesteel spearis a two-handedmeleeweaponthat requires 5Attackto wield. It can be bought from theMount Karuulm Weapon ShoporFortis Blacksmith, or obtained as a drop from monsters. Players who have started learningBarbarian Smithingand have level 35Smithingmay produce a spear at aBarbarian anvilfrom onesteel barand onewillow log, receiving 75 Smithingexperience. Smithing spears at the Barbarian anvil requires completion ofTai Bwo Wannai Trio. Like mostspears, it can bepoisoned.
