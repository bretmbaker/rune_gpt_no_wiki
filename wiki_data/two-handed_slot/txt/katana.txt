[Weapon: Katana]


Thekatanais a two-handed weapon that can be obtained as a very rare reward fromeliteTreasure Trails. It requires 40Attackto wield. Compared to arune scimitar, while the Katana has better defensive stats, it has 4 less strength bonus and is two-handed, making the use of adefenderor ashieldin conjunction with it impossible.
