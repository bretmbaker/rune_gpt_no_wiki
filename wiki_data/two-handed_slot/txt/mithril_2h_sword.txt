[Weapon: Mithril 2H Sword]


Themithril 2h swordis atwo-handed swordmade of mithril. As with allmithril weaponry, it requires 20Attackto wield.
At level 64Smithing, players can make one from threemithril bars, granting 150 Smithingexperience. Players can also purchase one from a few shops, includingGaius' Two Handed ShopinTaverleyandGulluck and Sonsin theGrand Tree.
