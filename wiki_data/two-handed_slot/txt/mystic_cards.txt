[Weapon: Mystic Cards]


Themystic cardsare an equipable set of cards obtained by redeeming the codev1r354cqu1r173und0fromDiangoinDraynor Village, via the right-click "Redeem-code" option.
The cards are awarded upon completing the final step of theRunestone Basalt ARGclue-hunting event, which was released alongside the launch of theShattered Relics League. The appearance of the mystic cards is styled after the tarot cards that could be found perched on the three runestone basalt pillars during that league, which would instantly fade away once interacted with.
