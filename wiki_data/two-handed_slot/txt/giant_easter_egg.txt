[Weapon: Giant Easter Egg]


TheGiant easter eggis a cosmetic reward obtained from the2019 Easter event, as well as subsequent Easter events. When wielded, players will be given a unique animation of them struggling to carry the egg. The egg has a unique Easter dance emote that can be used by right clicking on the item.
After the event, ahammercan be used on the giant easter egg to find thebunnyman mask. Holding the giant easter egg causes theBunny Manto have different dialogue, which hints at the use of a hammer.
