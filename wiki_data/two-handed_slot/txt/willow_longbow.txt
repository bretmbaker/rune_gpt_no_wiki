[Weapon: Willow Longbow]


Thewillow longbowis alongbowmade fromwillow logs. It requires 20Rangedto equip and can fire arrows up tomithril. It has a longerattack rangethan itsshortbowcounterpart, but has a slowerattack speed. It can be purchased fromLowe's Archery EmporiuminVarrock.
Willow longbows can be created at level 40Fletchingby using aknifeon willow logs to create awillow longbow (u), granting 41.5 experience, and then using abowstringon the unstrung bow, granting another 41.5 experience.
