[Weapon: Black Felling Axe]


Theblack felling axeis a type offelling axerequiring anAttacklevel of 10 to be equipped, and aWoodcuttinglevel of 11 to be used. Players can create the axe by using afelling axe handleon ablack axewhile having ahammerin their inventory and standing next to ananvil; this process isirreversible, but the felling axe itself remains tradeable.
Black felling axes will chop at the same speed as a normal black axe. If the player is carryingforester's rationshowever (either in the inventory or inside aforestry kit), they will receive 10% more Woodcutting experience each time they successfully chop a tree and gain a 20% chance not to receive logs when chopping trees (ortree rootsin theRising Rootsforestry event), at the cost of consuming one ration. This also results in a 20% chance to not receiveanima-infused barkfrom tree roots.
