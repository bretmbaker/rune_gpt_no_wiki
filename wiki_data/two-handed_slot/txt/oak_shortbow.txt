[Weapon: Oak Shortbow]


Theoak shortbowis a shortbow stronger than thestandard shortbowthat can use arrows up tosteel. It requires aRangedlevel of 5 or higher to wield.
Oak shortbows can be made at level 20Fletchingby using aknifeonoak logsto cut them into anoak shortbow (u), granting 16.5 Fletching experience. Then, the player must string theunstrung oak shortbowwith abow string, granting another 16.5 experience.
