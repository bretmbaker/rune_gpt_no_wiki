[Weapon: Saradomin%27S Blessed Sword#Partially Charged]


Saradomin's blessed swordis atwo-handed swordwhich requires 75Attackto wield. It is created by combiningSaradomin's tearwith theSaradomin sword. The blessed sword has +18 Slash and +6 Strength more than the normal variant.
If the sword isnotprotected upon death, it will fully degrade intoSaradomin's tear. However, if it is protected on death, it will remain in its regular state as Saradomin's blessed sword.

[Special Attack]
A player performing the Saradomin's blessed sword's special attack,Saradomin's Lightning.
Saradomin's blessed sword has aspecial attack,Saradomin's Lightning, which deals aMagic-based attack (Magical melee) that increases the player's max hit by 25%, consuming 65% of the player's special attack energy.
The special attack rolls against the opponent's Magic defence.[1]Under perfect conditions, the special attack can hit up to 72. Despite being Magic-based, it will still inflict damage onCyclopsin theWarriors' Guildand soldiers inside theShayziencombat ring.
