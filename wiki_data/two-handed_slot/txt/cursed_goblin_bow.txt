[Weapon: Cursed Goblin Bow]


Thecursed goblin bowis one of the threeweaponsthat were released during theGoblin Invasionevent. It was obtainable as a drop from thegoblin lieutenantsfound in the cave that theVarrockscout led players to. It was exceptionally powerful againstgoblinsfought during the event. It can be bought fromDiangoinDraynor Villagefor 45coins. It can shoot arrows up toiron, although it is slower than a regularshortbowand offers no accuracy bonuses. Since it has no requirements to wield, its exceptionally bright colour makes it a popular choice forskillersthat want to avoid accidentally attacking NPCs.
