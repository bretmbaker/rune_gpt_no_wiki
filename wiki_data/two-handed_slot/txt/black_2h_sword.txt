[Weapon: Black 2H Sword]


Theblack 2h swordis atwo-handed swordstronger than thesteel 2h sword, requiring level 10Attackto wield. Players can purchase this two-handed sword fromGaius' Two Handed ShopinTaverley. It can also be a reward from theObservatory Quest, from theShades of Mort'ton minigame, completing aTreasure Trailor as a drop from various monsters.
As with allblackandwhite equipment, the black 2h sword cannot be made from raw materials using theSmithingskill.
