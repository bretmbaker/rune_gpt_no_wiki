[Weapon: Maple Shortbow]


Amaple shortbowis a two-handedRanged weaponthat can be created with theFletchingskill at level 50 by using abow stringon amaple shortbow (u), granting 50experience. ARangedlevel of 30 is required to wield it. The bow can use arrows up toadamant. However, this bow isn't powerful enough for the use of broad arrows.
It is notable for being the best bow thatfree-to-playplayers can use.
