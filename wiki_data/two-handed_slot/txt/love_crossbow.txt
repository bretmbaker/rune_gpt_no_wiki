[Weapon: Love Crossbow]


Thelove crossbowis afun weaponunlocked by speaking toGilbertin the flower field south-east ofBarbarian Village, after which it can be obtained fromDiangoor retrieved from atoy boxbuilt in thecostume room. It was originally received as a reward for completing the2023 Pride event.
Despite resembling a crossbow, it is in fact a two-handed melee weapon, and as such cannot be used to fireboltsormith grapples. Like thegoblin paint cannon,ham joint,swift blade, andmystic cards, the love crossbow is one of the fewmeleeweapons to possess anattack speedof 3. However, due to hefty offensive penalties similar to those of the mystic cards, it is not advised to train with it.
