[Weapon: Elder Maul (Or)]


Theelder maul (or)is a cosmetic variant of theelder maulwith anelder maul ornament kitadded to it. It can be dismantled at any time, returning both parts back to the player. It is a two-handedcrush weaponthat requiresAttackandStrengthlevels of 75 to wield.
Along with the non-cosmetic variant, it is the highest tier crush weapon available inOld School RuneScape. It overtakes thegodswordsas the weapon with the higheststrength bonus. Despite the strength bonus, theabyssal bludgeonoften has higher overall damage output against low-defence monsters because of its quickerattack speed, making it only useful in places such asTekton. Because of its high strength bonus, it is often used as a finisher inplayer killing.
