[Weapon: Steel Claws]


Steel clawsare a fast two-handedmeleeweapon. To wield them the player must have at least level 5Attack. They are given as a reward for completing theDeath Plateauquest.
After completing the Death Plateau quest, they can be created at 43Smithing. They require 2Steel bars, and yield 75 Smithing experience. They can also be bought fromMartin Thwaitin theRogues' Den.
