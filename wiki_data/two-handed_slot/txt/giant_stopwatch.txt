[Weapon: Giant Stopwatch]


TheGiant stopwatchis a cosmetic item which can be purchased for 1,200Quest Speedrunningreward points from theSpeedrunning Reward Shop.
When wielded, players will see a "Bonk" option on all other players. Picking that option will cause the player to hit the other player.
