[Weapon: Barrelchest Anchor (Bh)]


TheBarrelchest anchor (bh)is a two-handed weapon that requires level 50Attackand 40Strengthto wield. It is created by using aBarrelchest anchor imbue scrollon theBarrelchest anchor, which is purchased as a reward fromBounty Hunter.
In comparison to the regular anchor, the imbued anchor has a +10 increased crush and strength bonus, and a +15% increase to the special attack's damage.

[Special Attack]
A player using the barrelchest anchor (bh)'s special attack,Sunder.
The barrelchest anchor (bh) has a special attack,Sunder, which doubles the player's accuracy, increases max damage by 25%, and drains the opponent's combat stats equivalent to 10% of the damage dealt in the following order:Defence,Attack,Ranged, thenMagic.[1]This consumes 50% of the player's special attack energy.
On a successful hit, the player's next attack will land 2 ticks faster (4 ticks instead of 6 ticks).
