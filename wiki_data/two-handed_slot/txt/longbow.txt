[Weapon: Longbow]


The basiclongbowis a bow made from regularlogs. It can be equipped at anyRangedlevel and can firearrowsup toiron. It has a longer range than itsshortbowcounterpart, but fires at a slowerspeed.
A longbow can be made at level 10Fletchingby using a knife on some logs to create alongbow (u), granting 10 experience, and then using abowstringon the unstrung bow, granting another 10 experience.
