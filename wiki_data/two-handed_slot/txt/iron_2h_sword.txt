[Weapon: Iron 2H Sword]


Theiron 2h swordis atwo-handed swordthat is stronger than thebronze 2h sword. As with otheriron weaponry, it can be wielded at level 1Attack.
Players can make an iron 2h sword with theSmithingskill at level 29 using 3iron bars, giving the player 75 Smithingexperience.
