[Weapon: Dark Bow (Deadman)]


TheDeadman dark bowis an ornamented variant of thedark bow, made by giving it toNigelafter having used anArmageddon weapon scrollon him. The attached ornament kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the dark bow.
While the dark bow is untradeable in its ornamented state, it can be reverted to the original tradeable form by speaking to Nigel.
