[Weapon: Mithril Claws]


Mithril clawsare a two-handedmeleeweaponthat aremember's only. To wield them the player must have at least level 20Attack.
Mithril claws can be created by using 2mithril barson ananvilwith ahammerin the inventory after completing theDeath Plateauquest. This requires level 63 inSmithing, and grants the player 100 Smithingexperience.
