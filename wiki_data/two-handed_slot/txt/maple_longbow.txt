[Weapon: Maple Longbow]


Amaple longbowis created by attaching abow stringto amaple longbow (u)with at least level 55Fletching. Fletching from amaple logyields a total of 116.5 experience (58.3 for fletching and 58.2 for stringing). The bow can use arrows up toadamant.
The maple longbow differs from theshortbowonly in terms of speed andrange, they share the sameRangedbonus. However, the shortbow is usually preferred over the longbow due to its fasterattack speed. It can also be a reward for theObservatory Quest.
