[Weapon: Boxing Gloves#Red]


Boxing glovesare items that can only be obtained from aglove rackin aplayer-owned house. They come in two different colours: red and blue. They count as a two-handed slashingweapon, and deal a max hit of 12 to other players in the boxing ring. Other gloves cannot be worn while wielding the boxing gloves, and anything worn in the glove slot will be automatically unequipped when putting them on.
When walking with these equipped your character holds them up to their face in a typical boxing stance. They are the only weapon that can be used in aboxing ringin a player'sCombat Room, and they are destroyed if a player tries to leave a house with them. Fighting with them in a player-owned house is their only use.
