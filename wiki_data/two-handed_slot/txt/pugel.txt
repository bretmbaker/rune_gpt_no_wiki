[Weapon: Pugel]


Apugelis the weapon you use on thebalance beam, a form of thecombat ringinplayer-owned houses.
It has no wield option, but when stepping on a balance beam, a second pugel will be wielded while the original remains in the player's inventory. The pugel provides no bonuses, and its attack options are the same as when the player is unarmed.
