[Weapon: Rat Pole#Two Rats]


Therat poleis anitemthat players obtain during theRatcatchersquest. Players who lose their rat pole can obtain a new empty one by talking toPhingspetandGrimesquit.
Players can attach up to six deadratsto the pole. To attach a rat, the player must first be wielding the pole, and not just have it in theirinventory. The player must use theircatto catch rats. When the cat catches a rat, the player has the option to put the rat on the pole. An adult cat or wily cat is recommended for this, as they have a much better success rate thankittens. The rat pole is ineffective as a weapon, serving mostly as a cosmetic item.
