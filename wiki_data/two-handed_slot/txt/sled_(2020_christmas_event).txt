[Weapon: Sled (2020 Christmas Event)]


Thesledis an item originally obtained during the2020 Christmas event, inScrubfoot's cavebeneathGoblin Village. It is used to slide down the slope to the area wheregreenandred firefliescan be found.
The sled is only useable in this instance; if a player attempts to ride the sled anywhere else, the chatbox will state"You cannot use that here!"
