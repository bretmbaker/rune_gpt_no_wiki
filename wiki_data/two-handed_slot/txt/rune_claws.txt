[Weapon: Rune Claws]


Rune clawsare a two-handed melee weapon, requiring 40Attackto wield. They are the third strongest pair ofclaws.
Rune claws can be obtained either throughsmithingor lootingninja implings. They can be made at level 98 Smithing with tworunite barsafter completion of theDeath Plateauquest, granting 150 Smithing experience.

[Special Attack]
A player using the rune claws' special attack,Impale.
Rune claws have aspecial attack,Impale, which consumes 25% of the player's special attack energy and deals 10% increased damage. The special attack increases the time until the next attack by one tick (0.6 seconds). This effectively lowers your damage output by taking 25% more time to deal only 10% more damage.
