[Weapon: Merfolk Trident]


Amerfolk tridentcan be bought fromMairin's Marketin theunderwaterarea ofFossil Islandfor 400mermaid tears.
It is one of the few weapons that allow the player to swim while wielding it. Its main purpose is aiding withdrift net fishing, where it gives an increased chance to scare offfish shoalsinto the net. It shares this effect with thetrident of the seas.[1]The trident has no attack level requirements and is a 2-handed weapon.
