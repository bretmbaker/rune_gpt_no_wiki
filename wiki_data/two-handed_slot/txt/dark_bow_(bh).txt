[Weapon: Dark Bow (Bh)]


Thedark bow (bh)is a two-handedranged weaponthat requires level 60 inRangedto wield. It is created by using adark bow imbue scrollon adark bow, which is purchased as a reward fromBounty Hunter.
The imbued bow has +105 Ranged attack, instead of the regular bow's +95. In addition, the minimum hit from the special attack is increased from 5 to 7 for non-dragon arrows, and from 8 to 10 fordragon arrows. The special attack's damage also rolls between the minimum and maximum damage, rather than zero to max.

[Special Attack]
A player performing one of the dark bow (bh)'s special attacks,Descent of Dragons.
The dark bow (bh) has a uniquespecial attackcomprised of two different attacks, both of which consume 55% of the player's special attack energy.
The 1st special attack,Descent of Darkness, deals up to 30% more damage with a minimum of 7 damage per arrow. The animation shows two arrows fired at the target, surrounded in a black shroud.
If the player hasdragon arrowsequipped, the 2nd special attack,Descent of Dragons, is used instead. This attack deals up to 50% more damage with a minimum of 10 damage per arrow, while capping the max hit at 48 damage per arrow.[1]
Lastly, the special attack damage also rolls between the minimum (7 or 10 depending on ammo) and maximum damage, rather than zero to max. This results in a higher average hit with the special attack when compared to the regulardark bow.
