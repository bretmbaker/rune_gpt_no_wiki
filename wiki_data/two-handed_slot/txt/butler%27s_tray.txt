[Weapon: Butler%27S Tray]


TheButler's traycan be obtained by speaking to theHead Butlerat theVilla LucensinAldarinafter completing theDeath on the Islequest.  Multiple trays may be obtained by speaking to Head Butler again.
The butler's tray is equipped in both hands when worn and gives the player a unique pose and walking animation, in which the tray is held in the player's left hand, and the player's right hand is held behind their back.
