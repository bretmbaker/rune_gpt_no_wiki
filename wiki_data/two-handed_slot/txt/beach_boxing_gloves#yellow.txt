[Weapon: Beach Boxing Gloves#Yellow]


Beach boxing glovesareholiday itemsreleased on 2nd July, 2013 to celebrate summer. They were found at the2013 Midsummer eventbyCatherbybeach,Mudskipper Point, and south of theAl Kharidbank. The gloves come in two colours: yellow and pink. The player can now purchase both sets of gloves fromDiangoinDraynor Villagefor 75gp each.
The gloves are similar to regularboxing glovesthat can be found in aplayer-owned houseonce aboxing ringis constructed, albeit with having the additional capability of being able to be carried anywhere.
