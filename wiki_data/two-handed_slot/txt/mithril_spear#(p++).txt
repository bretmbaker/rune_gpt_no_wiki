[Weapon: Mithril Spear#(P++)]


Themithril spearis atwo-handedmeleeweaponmade frommithril. Mithril spears are amembers-only weapon. It can be bought from theMount Karuulm Weapon ShoporFortis Blacksmith, or obtained as a drop from monsters. Using theSmithingskill, players can create mithril spears on one of thebarbarian anvilsin eitherOtto's GrottoorBarbarian Village. However, players must have begunBarbarian Smithingtraining to be able to smith these spears. Like most spears, the mithril spear can bepoisoned.
