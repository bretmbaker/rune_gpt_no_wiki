[Weapon: Zamorak Godsword (Or)]


TheZamorak godsword (or)is aZamorak godswordwith aZamorak godsword ornament kitattached to it. The attached ornament kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the godsword. It costs16,414,779for both the parts to make the Zamorak godsword (or).
While the godsword is untradeable in its ornamented state, it can be dismantled anytime, returning the tradeable godsword and ornament kit.
