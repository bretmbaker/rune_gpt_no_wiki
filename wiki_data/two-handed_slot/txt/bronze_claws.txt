[Weapon: Bronze Claws]


Bronze clawsare a two-handedmeleeweapon, and are the weakest set ofclawsavailable. They can be wielded at level 1Attack.
Bronze claws can be obtained either by using theSmithingskill to make a pair, or buying them fromMartin Thwait's Lost and Foundshop in theRogues' Den. The requirements for making them are completing theDeath Plateauquestand level 13 Smithing. To make them, use twobronze barson ananvilwith ahammerin the inventory. This grants 25 experience.
