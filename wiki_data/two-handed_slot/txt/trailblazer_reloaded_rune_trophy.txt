[Weapon: Trailblazer Reloaded Rune Trophy]


TheTrailblazer reloaded rune trophyis a cosmetic reward that can be obtained by having at least 42,000 League points by the end of theTrailblazer Reloaded League. When equipped, the trophy can be right-clicked to perform the Trophy Emote.
The trophy can be displayed by using it on furniture built in theLeague hall'spedestal space.
