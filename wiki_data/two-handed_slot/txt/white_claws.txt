[Weapon: White Claws]


Thewhite clawsare aWhite Knightweaponthat requires 10Attackand the completion of theWanted!questto wield. They have the same bonuses as theblack claws, with an added +1prayer bonus.
Players can purchase them fromSir Vyvinafter achieving therankof Master (1,300black knightkills). They can also be bought on the Grand Exchange or obtained through trading other players.
