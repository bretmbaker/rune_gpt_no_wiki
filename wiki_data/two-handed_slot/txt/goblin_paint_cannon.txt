[Weapon: Goblin Paint Cannon]


Thegoblin paint cannonis a two-handed weapon that can be bought fromDiango's Toy StoreinDraynor Villagefor 225 coins. It was first introduced during the2014 Goblin Invasionevent, when it could be obtained by speaking to either theGoblin Recruiteror theGuard Recruiter, depending on whose side you joined. Opposing goblins or guards could be covered in paint using the cannon, which would make them attackable.
The goblin paint cannon is unusual, being one of only fourmeleeweapons to possess anattack speedof 3 (along with theswift blade, theham joint, and themystic cards), equal toshortbowson rapid attack style. Because of this, it is commonly used with lower levelPvPbuilds especially among players with 1Attackwhere its attack speed out-performs other weapons of a similar tier like theiron scimitar.
