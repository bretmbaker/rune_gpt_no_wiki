[Weapon: 3Rd Age Felling Axe]


The3rd age felling axeis a type offelling axerequiring anAttacklevel of 65 to be equipped, and aWoodcuttinglevel of 61 to be used. Players can create the axe by using afelling axe handleon a3rd age axewhile having ahammerin their inventory and standing next to ananvil; this process isirreversible, but the felling axe itself remains tradeable.
3rd age felling axes will chop at the same speed as a normal 3rd age axe. If the player is carryingforester's rationshowever (either in the inventory or inside aforestry kit), they will receive 10% more Woodcutting experience each time they successfully chop a tree and gain a 20% chance not to receive logs when chopping trees (ortree rootsin theRising Rootsforestry event), at the cost of consuming one ration. This also results in a 20% chance to not receiveanima-infused barkfrom tree roots.

[Special Attack]
The 3rd age felling axe has aspecial attack,Lumber Up, that it shares with thedragon,infernal,3rd age, andcrystal axesand their variants. Ittemporarily booststhe player'sWoodcuttinglevel by 3, consuming 100% of the player's special attack energy. The boost degrades every 60 seconds, with the player's Woodcutting level returning to normal after 3 minutes. UsingPreservecan increase the duration by 50%.
The special attack can be used to allow players to chop with the axe itself starting at level 58 Woodcutting, although it cannot be used without the level 65Attackrequired to wield the axe.
Note that any levels boosted above 99 with the special attack will be treated as 99. Therefore, you will start to receive diminishing returns with the special attack at level 97 and 98[1], with visible boosts past level 99 only influencing thepetandclue nestchances.[2]
