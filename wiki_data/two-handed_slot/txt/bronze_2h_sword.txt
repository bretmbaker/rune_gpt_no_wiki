[Weapon: Bronze 2H Sword]


Thebronze 2h swordis the weakesttwo-handed swordinOld School RuneScape. Like allbronze weaponry, it can be wielded at level 1Attack.
It can be created at level 14Smithingby using 3bronze barson an anvil with ahammerin the inventory, granting 37.5 Smithing experience.
