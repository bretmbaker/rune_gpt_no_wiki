[Weapon: White 2H Sword]


Thewhite 2h swordis aWhite Knightweaponthat requires 10Attackand the completion of theWanted!questto wield. It has the same bonuses as ablack 2h sword, with an added +1prayer bonus.
Players can purchase it fromSir Vyvinafter achieving therankof Master (1,300black knightkills). It can also be bought on the Grand Exchange or obtained through trading other players.
