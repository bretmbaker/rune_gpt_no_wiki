The tangleroot is a skilling pet that can be obtained when checking the health (for patches that have this option, like fruit trees ) or when harvesting the final crop (for patches without a "Check-health" option) of a farming patch . It may also be obtained while playing the Tithe Farm minigame or from killing the Hespori . The chances of getting it are dependent on the player's Farming level and the crop the player is harvesting.

When a player receives the pet, it will automatically try to appear as their follower. At the same time, a message in the chatbox will state You have a funny feeling like you're being followed. However, if a player receives a pet while having a follower out (for example, a cat ), it will be placed into their inventory . When this occurs, the message in the chatbox will instead state You feel something weird sneaking into your backpack.

Unlike pets such as the rock golem , an incoming farming resource such as a herb or hespori seed will take precedence over the farming pet. If the player already has a follower and the remaining inventory slots are then filled by an incoming farming resource, the player will not receive the pet [1] and must instead claim it from Probita in East Ardougne for a reclaim token, costing 1,000,000 coins . Should the player obtain the pet while harvesting giant seaweed , the pet will not be lost upon drowning .

On death , if a player loses their pet (as a follower or in your inventory) it will wander around the area for 30 seconds before despawning. Even if the player returns in time, the pet cannot be picked up, and can be reclaimed from Probita for a reclaim token.

Variants [ edit | edit source ]

Players can change the tangleroot's appearance by using various seeds on it. This will consume the seed. To revert the tangleroot to its original form, players must use an acorn on it. The different appearances will remain permanently toggleable.

Seed Seed cost Image Chat head Seed Seed cost Image Chat head
Acorn −118 Crystal acorn 0
Dragonfruit −218,266 Guam −29
Redwood −26,081 White lily −4,173

These seeds may be obtained by:

  • Checking seed nests for acorns, dragonfruit, and redwood seeds.
  • Defeating certain monsters with the seed drop table for guam seeds, or by pickpocketing a master farmer .
  • Defeating and farming Hespori for white lily seeds.
  • Catching crystal implings or opening the elven crystal chest for crystal acorns. Completion of the Song of the Elves quest is also required to obtain them. (Crystal acorns cannot be traded with other players.)

Drop rates [ edit | edit source ]

The pet chance is rolled primarily during a patch's check-health stage; if the patch does not have an option to check health, it instead rolls during the final harvest. [2]

A player's actual chances of receiving it is 1 in B - ( Lvl * 25) , where B is the base chance and Lvl is the player's Farming level. [2]

The fastest method on average to receive Tangleroot is by frequently farming cactus , belladonna , hespori , giant seaweed , and mushrooms , as well as doing regular tree runs. Although using only trees takes the least amount of time spent on Farming (thus being more efficient), it is extremely slow in real time, taking upwards of two years on average with daily tree runs.

Even though Tangleroot is obtainable from the Tithe Farm , it is extremely unlikely: in terms of probability, it would take nearly 3 billion Farming experience on average, equivalent to 25,000 hours of playing.

To find the pet chance for a method at a given level, refer to the skill pet chance calculator .

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • The pet is likely a reference to the Tanglefoot , fought during Fairytale I - Growing Pains , and Groot , a character from Marvel Comics . Groot is a talking tree in Marvel's Guardians of the Galaxy film, who is only capable of saying "I am Groot", "We are Groot", or "I love you guys".
  • The examine text is a reference to the line said by the ent Treebeard in The Lord of the Rings .
  • Even though it is possible to get the pet underwater at seaweeds, it is not possible to re-enter the underwater area with a pet.
