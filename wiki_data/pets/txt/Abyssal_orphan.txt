[Pet: Abyssal_orphan]

TheAbyssal orphanis a bosspetthat resembles thescionsthat are encountered during theAbyssal Sireboss fight.

Players have a 5/128 (1/25.6) chance of obtaining the abyssal orphan when they place anunsiredon theFont of ConsumptionbehindThe Overseer. As an unsired is a 1/100 drop, this effectively makes the chance of an abyssal orphan 1/2,560. When the unsired is placed into the font, if the pet chance is rolled, a message will appear in the chatbox statingThe Font appears to have revitalised the Unsired!.

On death, if a player loses their pet (as a follower or in your inventory) it will wander around for 30 seconds before disappearing completely. It can then be reclaimed fromProbitainEast Ardougnefor a reclaim token, which costs 1,000,000coins.
