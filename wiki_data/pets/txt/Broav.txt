[Pet: Broav]

Thebroavis an animal obtained duringWhile Guthix Sleepsand kept afterward. Thehunting experttrains theunconscious broavthat the player catches into a broav. It is used to track the location ofMovario's basevia the scent from hisdirty shirt.

Though itsRuneScapecounterpart is a truepet, dropping the broav inOld School RuneScapewill result in the broav moving to a random square adjacent to the player and turning to look at them before standing still; it will not follow them like a true pet. Despite this, players cannot drop it while having a true pet following them. However, itcanbe stored in themenagerieof aplayer-owned housewhere it wanders around like a pet.

Similar to true pets, other players in the game can see the broav but have no right-click options on it. It will appear as a yellow dot on the owner's minimap.

Moving too far away from the broav (beyond roughly 16 tiles) will cause it to return to your inventory with the message"Your broav gets nervous being left alone and quickly returns to you."If your inventory is full, the broav will go feral and disappear. Logging out or hopping worlds also causes the broav to return to you.

The broav will mysteriously disappear if left for 1 hour and 40 minutes. Logging out and back in will cause it to reappear at the player's side (not in the inventory like usual).

If the broav is lost, the original process can be repeated both during and after the quest to obtain another one, using apitfall trapand aMort Myre fungusas bait.
