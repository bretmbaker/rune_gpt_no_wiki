The dragon sq shield (g) is a dragon sq shield with a dragon sq shield ornament kit attached to it. The attached ornament kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the square shield. It costs 307,593 for both the parts to make the dragon sq shield (g).

While the square shield is untradeable in its ornamented state, it can be dismantled anytime, returning the tradeable square shield and ornament kit.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room, as part of the dragon gold-trimmed armour. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
