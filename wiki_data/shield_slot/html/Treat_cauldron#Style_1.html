The treat cauldron is a holiday reward received from completing the 2022 and 2023 Halloween event. It can be changed into various styles, each appearing more and more filled than the last. In addition, it has an emote that can be performed whilst equipped.

Players may also use the cauldron's "Check" option to display the total amount of treats deposited by all players during the event, as well as the personal amount deposited.

During the 2022 and 2023 Halloween event, it was used to trick-or-treat with various NPCs, store collected treats, and deposit treats into the Great Cauldron. The cauldron held a maximum of 8,000 treats. When attempting to put more treats in when the cauldron is full gives the message, Your Treat cauldron is full and can't hold any more treats.

If destroyed, a replacement can be obtained from Diango in Draynor Village or from the toy box of a costume room.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • During the event, attempting to add a purple sweet into the treat cauldron, resulted in a game message stating Treats should be earned by Trick-or-Treating, you can't sneak your own in!
  • During the 2022 Halloween event, only the first three styles were unlocked as part of the community goals, only reaching 151.7 million treats deposited out of the 300 million required by the end of the event.
    • During the 2023 Halloween event, all styles were made unavailable again until the new community goals were reached, which was reduced to a total of 200 million, which was achieved on 31 October.
