The Falador shield 3 is a reward from completing the hard Falador Diary tasks given to you by Sir Rebral in the White Knights' Castle courtyard and can be retrieved from him for free if lost. Its defensive bonuses are equivalent to a Mithril kiteshield, except it has an added Prayer bonus, a higher Ranged attack bonus, and weighs more.

Benefits[edit | edit source]

When worn or in the player's inventory
  • 100% prayer restore once daily
  • An indicator will point to the location of the Giant Mole while inside its lair

A number of other benefits are always active upon claiming rewards from the hard tier of the Falador diary.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
16 November 2017
(update)

A "Check" option was added to view the number of remaining charges.

9 March 2017
(update)

The shield will now show the Giant Mole locator hint-arrow automatically while you're in its lair, without having to click options on the items.

24 March 2016
(update | poll)
  • It is now also possible to get multiple copies of achievement diary equipment by talking to relevant diary masters or by talking to Perdu, found upstairs in the Edgeville general store. Perdu will charge a small fee.
  • The "Destroy" option was replaced with a "Drop" option.
30 April 2015
(update)

Attempting to recharge your prayer with a Falador shield when you're already full prayer points no longer consumes one of your daily charges.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The animation that plays when using the shield's prayer restore is identical to that of the elysian spirit shield when its passive effect occurs.
