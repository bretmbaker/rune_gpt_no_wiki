The training shield is a beginner's item. The shield has an appearance similar to a white decorative shield and has the same bonuses as a Wooden shield, though it weighs significantly more. It can be obtained for free from the melee combat tutor east of Lumbridge Castle. Players are only allowed one shield at any time.
