Adamant kiteshield (g) is a part of adamant gold-trimmed armour and a reward from medium Treasure Trails. It has the same stats as a normal Adamant kiteshield, except for the ranged attack bonus (-2 rather than -3), and requires level 30 Defence to wear. This item cannot be made via the Smithing skill.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room, as part of the adamant gold-trimmed armour set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
