The Gilded kiteshield is a gold-plated rune kiteshield and is part of the Gilded armour set. It can either be received as a possible reward from hard, elite and master tier Treasure Trails.

It is usually worn as a showcase of wealth. Armour cannot be trimmed or created by players.
