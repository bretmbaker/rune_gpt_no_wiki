A wooden shield can be found in the weapons rack of a player-owned house. It provides no bonuses.

This shield is obtainable through the Construction skill. Players require at least 35 Construction to build a fencing ring, and subsequently need to be able to construct a 'Level 2 Weapons rack' (44 Construction) to obtain this shield from their own house. However, it may be retrieved from another player's weapons rack.

Upon dropping or leaving the player's house, it is destroyed.
