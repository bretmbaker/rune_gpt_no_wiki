Amy's saw (off-hand) is a variant of Amy's saw that can be equipped in the shield slot. The saw loses its combat bonuses, but otherwise functions identically to the normal variant.

The saw can be swapped back into its normal variant by using the "Swap" option.

This item can be stored in the magic wardrobe of a costume room.
