[Weapon: Malediction Ward (Or)]


Themalediction ward (or)is amalediction wardwith award upgrade kitattached to it. The kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the ward. It costs2,447,787for both the parts to make the malediction ward (or).
While the ward is untradeable in its ornamented state, it can be reverted anytime, returning the malediction ward. However, the kitwill notbe returned.
