[Weapon: Twisted Buckler]


Thetwisted buckleris a ranged-classshieldthat is obtained as a possible reward from theChambers of Xeric, requiring level 75RangedandDefenceto equip.
The buckler currently offers the best Ranged Attack and Ranged Strength bonus for theshield slotin the game. It also has some of the bestMagicdefence in its slot, beaten only by thedragonfire wardandspectral spirit shield. Its melee defence bonuses are comparable to anadamant sq shield.
