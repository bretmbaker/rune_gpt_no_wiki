[Weapon: Runefest Shield]


TheRunefest shieldis a cosmetic kiteshield adorned with theRuneFestdragon insignia, promoting and celebrating the festivities ofRuneFest 2017, which took place on the 23rd of September, 2017 at Battersea Evolution Events Centre inBattersea Park,Wandsworth,United Kingdom.[1]
Players can claim one fromDiangoinDraynor Villagevia the right-click "Holiday-items" option.
