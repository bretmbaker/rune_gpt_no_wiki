[Weapon: Fremennik Shield]


TheFremennik shieldis ashieldcommonly worn byFremennikwarriors. To wield one, the player must have completedThe Fremennik Trialsquest. They can be obtained as a monster drop from Fremennikmarket guards,dagannoths(only in theWaterbirth Island Dungeon) or Fremennikwarriors. ADefencelevel of 30 is required to wield the shield.
The stats of the shield are identical to those of anadamant kiteshieldexcept that the Fremennik shield is lighter.
