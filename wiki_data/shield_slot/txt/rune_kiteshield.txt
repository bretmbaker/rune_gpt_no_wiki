[Weapon: Rune Kiteshield]


TheRune kiteshieldis the second best smithablekiteshield, surpassed only by theDragon kiteshield. The rune kiteshield requires 40Defenceto wield. A player with 97Smithingcan make the shield by using threerunite barsand ahammeron ananvil, granting 225 Smithingexperience.
The rune kiteshield is often used as a tank shield by low-risk andvoidrangers; despite the negativerangedattack bonus, it is much cheaper, and provides a substantialmeleedefence bonus, which theOdium wardlacks.
