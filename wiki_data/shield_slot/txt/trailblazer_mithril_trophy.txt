[Weapon: Trailblazer Mithril Trophy]


TheTrailblazer mithril trophyis a cosmetic reward that can be obtained by being in the top 40% of players in the Leagues Hiscores by the end of theTrailblazer League, which required having at least 6,480 League points to do so. When equipped, the trophy can be right-clicked to perform the Trophy Emote.
The trophy can be displayed by using it on furniture built in theLeague hall'spedestal space.
