[Weapon: Dragonfire Shield#Charged]


Adragonfire shieldis an upgradedanti-dragon shield, and one of the best shields in the game behind theelysian spirit shieldandDinh's bulwark. Equipping it requires 75Defenceand having startedDragon Slayer I. It retains the samedragonfireprotection as the anti-dragon shield and it also provides partial protection against theicy breathofwyverns, like thedragonfire ward,elemental shield, andmind shield.
When uncharged, it is tradeable and offers slightly more defensive bonuses than amithril sq shield, however, each charge it gains increases melee defensive bonuses and the range defensive bonus by 1 for a total of +50 at maximum for those four defensive stats. To charge the shield,dragonfireneeds to be absorbed from dragonfire or icy breath attacks while it is equipped.Bottled dragonbreathcan also be used to charge the shield.
