[Weapon: V%27S Shield#The Fremennik Exiles]


V's shieldis a shield created duringThe Fremennik Exilesquest. It is a recreation of the shield owned by the legendary warriorV. It requires 40 Defence and 60 Slayer to wield.
It acts as amirror shieldwhen fightingbasilisks,basilisk knights, andcockatrice. It has stronger defensive stats than the mirror shield, on par with anAdamant kiteshieldbut positive magic defence and without negative stats.
