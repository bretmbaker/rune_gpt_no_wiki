Theiron defenderis a defender made ofiron. It is dropped by any of theCyclopson the top floor of theWarriors' Guild, but only once players have obtained abronze defender. It is one of the two defenders that does not provide astrength bonus, along with the bronze defender.
If the player dies below level 20Wilderness, it will remain in the player's inventory. However, the item will be unusable and must be repaired with 2,000 coins by using the item onPerdu.
