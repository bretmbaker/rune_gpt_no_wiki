[Weapon: Green Satchel]


Agreen satchelis a drop fromunicows, which can be created in thebasementof theTower of Lifeafter completing theTower of Lifequest. When equipped, it will also enhance thebeckonemote.
It can hold abanana, acakeandtriangle sandwich, which heal a total of 20 hitpoints. It takes multiple bites to consume the food, but it is much cheaper than most food that heals a similar amount.
