[Weapon: Saradomin Kiteshield]


TheSaradomin kiteshieldis a decoratedrune kiteshieldthat is affiliated with the godSaradomin. Sharing the same stats as the rune kiteshield except for the ranged attack bonus andprayer bonus, the Saradomin kiteshield also looks visually similar. Its appearance differs in that the shield features an ornamental white trim.
The shield requires 40Defenceto wear andcannotbe made using theSmithingskill.
