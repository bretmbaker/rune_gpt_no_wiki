[Weapon: Shattered Relics Iron Trophy]


TheShattered Relics iron trophyis a cosmetic reward that could be obtained by being in the top 80% of players in the Leagues Hiscores by the end of theShattered Relics League, which required having at least 480 League points to do so.
A total of 144,745 players met this threshold by the end of the League.
