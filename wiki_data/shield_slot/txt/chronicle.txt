[Weapon: Chronicle]


TheChronicleis an equipable book that can be used toteleportthe player to just outside theChampions' Guild, making it useful for players who plant seeds inBushandHopspatches as well as free-to-play ironmen. The teleport is also a short distance fromCharlie the Tramp, making it useful for those doingbeginner clues.
During the2016 Easter event, players were able to acquire it for free by defeating theChocolate Chickenduring the event. It can now be purchased fromDiango's Toy Storefor300coins. If the player has a copy of the Chronicle in the inventory orbank, trying to buy another one fromDiangowill fail, and the user will receive the following message:"You've already got one of those."However, itispossible to own more than one Chronicle if buying them fromPerdu.
