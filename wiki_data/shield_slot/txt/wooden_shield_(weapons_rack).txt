[Weapon: Wooden Shield (Weapons Rack)]


Awooden shieldcan be found in theweapons rackof aplayer-owned house. It provides no bonuses.
This shield is obtainable through theConstructionskill. Players require at least 35 Construction to build afencing ring, and subsequently need to be able to construct a 'Level 2 Weapons rack' (44 Construction) to obtain this shield from their own house. However, it may be retrieved from another player's weapons rack.
