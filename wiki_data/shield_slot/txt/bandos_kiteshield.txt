[Weapon: Bandos Kiteshield]


TheBandos kiteshieldis a decoratedrune kiteshieldthat is affiliated with the godBandos, requiring 40Defenceto equip. Sharing the same stats as the rune kiteshield except for the ranged attack bonus andPrayer bonus, the Bandos kiteshield also looks visually similar. Its appearance differs in that the shield features an ornamental brown trim.
Players can obtain a Bandos kiteshield as a reward from completinghardTreasure Trails. The shieldcannotbe made using theSmithingskill.
