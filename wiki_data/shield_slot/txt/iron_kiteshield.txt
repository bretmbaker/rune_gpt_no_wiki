[Weapon: Iron Kiteshield]


TheIron kiteshieldis the second-weakest smithablekiteshield, requiring 1Defenceto wield. A player with 27Smithingcan make the shield by using threeiron barson ananvilwhile carrying ahammer, granting 75 Smithingexperience. It cannot be bought fromCassie's Shield Shopforironmen, as there is no stock available.
The iron kiteshield yields the highest melee defence bonuses for any shield slot item at 1Defence.
