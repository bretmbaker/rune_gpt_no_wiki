[Weapon: 3Rd Age Kiteshield]


The3rd age kiteshieldis a part of the3rd age melee set. Level 65Defenceis required to wield the kiteshield. The 3rd age kiteshield is a possible reward fromhard,eliteandmastertierTreasure Trailsand cannot be made through theSmithingskill.
Although it is one of the more powerfulshieldsavailable in-game, it is rare to see it used incombatas there are several similarly powerful shields which are cheaper or significantly easier to obtain.
