[Weapon: Hallowed Focus]


TheHallowed focusis an item that will give a 100% chance to succeed at conjuringPortal Framesinside theHallowed Sepulchreand will slightly speed up enchanting the portal when equipped. It also causes the player to use the lowest level enchantment spell available instead of the highest. It can be purchased from theMysterious Hallowed Goodsshop for 100hallowed marks.
The focus also counts as a Saradomin item for the purposes of recharging your runenergyat the Magical Obelisks at the end of each floor, as well as inGod Wars Dungeon.
