[Weapon: Rune Sq Shield]


Therune square shieldis the second strongestsquare shieldinOld School RuneScape, requiring aDefencelevel of 40 to wield. It is obtainable via therare drop table. A player with level 93Smithingcan make the shield by using tworune barson ananvilwhile carrying ahammer, granting 150experience. Rune square shields can also be obtained fromMagpie impling jarsas well as a common drop from aGreater Nechryael.
Any monster that has access to therare drop tablecan also drop the rune square shield.
