[Weapon: Elemental Shield]


Theelemental shieldmay be made during and after theElemental Workshop Iquest in the Elemental Workshop dungeon.
To make the shield, players must have aSmithinglevel of 20, anelemental metal, ahammer, and either abattered bookorslashed bookin their inventory. Use the elemental metal on aworkbenchin the elemental workshop to make the shield. Smithing the shield grants the player 20 Smithingexperience. To equip the shield, players must have completed theElemental Workshop Iquest.
