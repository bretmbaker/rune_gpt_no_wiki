[Weapon: Falador Shield 1]


TheFalador shield 1is a reward from completing theeasy Falador Diarytasks given to you bySir Rebralin theWhite Knights' Castlecourtyard and can be retrieved from him for free if lost. Its defensive bonuses are equivalent to anIron kiteshield, except it has an addedPrayerbonus and higherRangedattack bonus.
A number of other benefitsare always active upon claiming rewards from the easy tier of the Falador diary.
