[Weapon: Mirror Shield]


Amirror shieldis a piece ofslayer equipmentused for fighting theCockatriceandBasiliskSlayer monsters, requiring 25Slayerand 20Defenceto wield. When fighting these monsters, the shield must be equipped, or else the player's stats will be drastically reduced. It can be bought from anySlayer Master, or by trading with another player.
The mirror shield possessesiron-tierstats, but with an emphasis onSlashandMagicdefence and no negative bonuses. Its +5 bonus inMagic defencemakes the shield useful not only against Cockatrices and Basilisks, but as a magic-defence alternative toelemental shields(except againstwyverns) if players haven't finished the questElemental Workshop I.
