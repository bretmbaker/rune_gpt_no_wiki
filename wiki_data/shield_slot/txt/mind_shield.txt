[Weapon: Mind Shield]


Amind shieldis an upgraded version of theelemental shield. It is made during and after theElemental Workshop IIquest in the elemental workshop dungeon.
Making the shield requires 30Smithing, aprimed mind bar, ahammer, and aslashed bookin the inventory. Right-click the "Smith" option on aworkbenchin the elemental workshop to smith the shield, which grants the player 30 Smithingexperience. Equipping a mind shield requires completion ofElemental Workshop II.
