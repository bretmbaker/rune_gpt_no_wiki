[Weapon: Book Of The Dead]


Thebook of the deadis an equipable book obtained as a reward from completingA Kingdom Divided. Having it equipped or in the player's inventory is required when castingresurrection spells.
As it is an improved version ofKharedst's memoirs, the book is able to teleport to the same locations as the memoirs, and holds up to 250 teleport charges (150 + 20 per page added) compared to the memoirs' 100 (20 per page added).
