[Weapon: Willow Shield]


Awillow shieldis a shield that requires at least level 30Defenceto be worn. It is created by using aknifeon twowillow logs. Creating the shield requires level 42 inFletching, and grants the player 83 experience.
It is used as the base for creating asnakeskin shield.
