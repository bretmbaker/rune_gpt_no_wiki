[Weapon: Bug Lantern#Lit]


Thelit bug lanternis an item used in theSlayerskill. It requires level 33 Slayer to wield and must be equipped in order to fightHarpie Bug Swarms.
Level 33Firemakingand atinderboxare required to light the unlit lantern. Bug lanterns are worn in theshield slot, but only lit lanterns are effective against Harpie Bug Swarms for completing slayer tasks.
