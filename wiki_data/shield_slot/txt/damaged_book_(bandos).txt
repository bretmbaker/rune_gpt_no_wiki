[Weapon: Damaged Book (Bandos)]


Thedamaged bookis a book held in the shield slot that can be obtained for 5,000coinsfromJossikafter completingHorror from the Deep. The fourBandos pagescan be added to upgrade it to thebook of war, which gives a damage bonus. Both the damaged and upgraded book count as agod itemforBandosin theGod Wars Dungeon.
After completing Horror from the Deep, you are rewarded with a damaged book of your choice: either Saradomin, Guthix, or Zamorak. Youdo notneed to complete your current damaged book before obtaining a new one of a different type from Jossik. You can buy every damaged book in the same day. Dropping or destroying a damaged book and talking to Jossik results in him returning it to you.
