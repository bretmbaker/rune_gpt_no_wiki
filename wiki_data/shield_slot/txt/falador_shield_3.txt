[Weapon: Falador Shield 3]


TheFalador shield 3is a reward from completing thehard Falador Diarytasks given to you bySir Rebralin theWhite Knights' Castlecourtyard and can be retrieved from him for free if lost. Its defensive bonuses are equivalent to aMithril kiteshield, except it has an addedPrayerbonus, a higherRangedattack bonus, and weighs more.
A number of other benefitsare always active upon claiming rewards from the hard tier of the Falador diary.
