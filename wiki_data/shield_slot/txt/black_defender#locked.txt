[Weapon: Black Defender#Locked]


Theblack defenderis adefendermade of black metal. It is similar to a dagger, but held in the off-hand in place of a shield. It requires 10Attackand 10Defenceto wield. The black defender is dropped byCyclopeson the top floor of theWarriors' Guild, but only once you have obtained asteel defender.
If the player dies below level 20Wilderness, it will remain in the player's inventory. However, the item will be unusable and must be repaired with 5,000 coins by using the item onPerdu.
