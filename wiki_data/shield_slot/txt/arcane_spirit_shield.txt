[Weapon: Arcane Spirit Shield]


Thearcane spirit shieldrequires 75Defence, 65Magicand 70Prayerto wield.
It is made by attaching anarcane sigil, a rare drop from theCorporeal Beast, to ablessed spirit shieldusing ahammerat ananvil; this process requires 90Prayerand 85Smithing. Players without the required skill levels to create the shield may askAbbot Langleyin theEdgeville Monasteryto combine the sigil and the blessed spirit shield, for a fee of 1,500,000 coins.
