[Weapon: Broodoo Shield (Disease)#(1)]


The diseasebroodoo shieldis a specialmembers onlyshield. The shield requires 25Defenceand 25Magicto wear. When created, the shield comes with 10 charges.
Broodoo shields have a passive effect when used in combat, similar to theblack mask; when damage is taken it has a 10% chance of draining the opponent'sstrengthstat by 1, and then 5% of their remaining total. The chance for the ability to occur happens whenever an enemy attacks the player adjacently. The enemy must still have over half of itsHitpointsand the affected skill must be at its base level for it to be affected. The shield works even if you are not fighting back and at any distance from the enemy.
