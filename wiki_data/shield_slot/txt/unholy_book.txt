[Weapon: Unholy Book]


Theunholy bookis a book held in place of ashield, and is thegod bookaligned withZamorak. It is obtained from theHorror from the Deepquest. It can be filled with the 4pagesobtained fromTreasure Trails, granting attack bonuses and the ability to preach lines from the book. With 50Prayerand a completed book, players can enchantunpowered symbolsintounholy symbols.
The Zamorak unholy book provides the best all-around attack bonuses of the original three.
