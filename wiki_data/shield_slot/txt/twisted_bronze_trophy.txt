[Weapon: Twisted Bronze Trophy]


Thetwisted bronze trophyis a cosmetic reward that could be obtained by having at least 10 League points by the end of theTwisted League. When equipped, the trophy can be right-clicked to perform the Trophy Emote.
The trophy can be displayed by using it on furniture built in theLeague hall'spedestal space.
