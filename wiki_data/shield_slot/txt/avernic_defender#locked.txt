TheAvernic defenderis adefenderwielded in theshield slot, requiring 70AttackandDefenceto wield. It is obtained by combining adragon defenderwith anAvernic defender hilt. This process can be reversed to return the dragon defender, but the hilt will bedestroyedin the process. This defender boasts the higheststrength bonusof any shield slot equipment, being 1 higher than thedragonfire shield. It also has the highestmeleeattack bonusesof any shield slot equipment. The defensive bonuses are comparable to that of anadamant kiteshield.
Ghommal's hilt 5or6can be attached to it, creatingGhommal's avernic defender 5or6respectively, giving the bonuses of the Avernic defender and the additional benefits of the hilt. This process is reversible, returning both the hilt and the defender.
