[Weapon: Spectral Spirit Shield]


Thespectral spirit shieldis a shield which requires 75Defence, 70Prayerand 65Magicto wield.
It is made by attaching aspectral sigil, a rare drop from theCorporeal Beast, to ablessed spirit shieldusing ahammerat ananvil; this process requires 90Prayerand 85Smithing. Players without the required skill levels to create the shield may askAbbot Langleyin theEdgeville Monasteryto combine the sigil and the blessed spirit shield, for a fee of 1,500,000 coins.
