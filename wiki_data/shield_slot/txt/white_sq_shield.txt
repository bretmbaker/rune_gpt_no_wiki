[Weapon: White Sq Shield]


Thewhite sq shieldis aWhite Knightsquare shieldthat requires 10Defenceand the completion of theWanted!questto wield. It has the same bonuses as ablack sq shield, with an added +1prayer bonus.
Players can purchase it fromSir Vyvinafter achieving therankof Page (300black knightkills). It can also be bought on the Grand Exchange or obtained through trading other players.
