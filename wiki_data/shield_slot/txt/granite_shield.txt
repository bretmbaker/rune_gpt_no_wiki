[Weapon: Granite Shield]


Thegranite shieldis a shield made ofgranite. It requires 50Defenceand 50Strengthto equip. It has defensive stats equal to theToktz-ket-xil, minus the +5strength bonus, and isn't commonly used formeleecombat as a result.
It can be used in place of therune kiteshielddue to its superiorrangeddefence bonus. However, some players choose not to use it due to its weight and slightly lower melee defence bonuses.
