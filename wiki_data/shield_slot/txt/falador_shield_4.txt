[Weapon: Falador Shield 4]


TheFalador shield 4is a reward from completing theelite Falador Diarytasks given to you bySir Rebralin theWhite Knights' Castlecourtyard and can be retrieved from him for free if lost. Its defensive bonuses are equivalent to anAdamant kiteshield, except it has an addedPrayerbonus, a higherRangedattack bonus, and weighs less.
A number of other benefitsare always active upon claiming rewards from the elite tier of the Falador diary.
