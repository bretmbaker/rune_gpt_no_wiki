[Weapon: Elidinis%27 Ward (F)]


Elidinis' ward (f)is a magical shield requiring 80Magic,Defence, andPrayerto equip. The fortified ward provides +5 more Magic Attack bonus and +1 Prayer bonus more than thearcane spirit shield, alongside a 5%magic damagebonus. Its defence bonuses are identical to the arcane spirit shield.
The ward is fortified by attaching anarcane sigiltoElidinis' ward, requiring level 90Prayer, 90Smithing, and 10,000soul runes, granting 260 experience in both skills in the process. Boosts can be used for the Prayer and Smithing level requirements to fortify the ward; players cannot payAbbot Langleyto fortify it for them, though he can remove the sigil from an arcane spirit shield. Upon fortifying Elidinis' ward, the chatbox states "You fuse your Arcane sigil to Elidinis' ward, returning it to its former glory."
