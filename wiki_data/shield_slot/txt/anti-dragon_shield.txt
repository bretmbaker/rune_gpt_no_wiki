[Weapon: Anti-Dragon Shield]


Theanti-dragon shieldis an item given byDuke HoracioinLumbridge Castleduring theDragon Slayer Iquest. After the quest, the shield can be purchased fromOziach, or obtained fromDuke Horaciofor free. It is tradeable, but can only be equipped if the player has started Dragon Slayer I.
The anti-dragon shield mitigates the effects ofdragonfire, reducing the maximum hit of most dragon-breath attacks by roughly ~80%. It also preventsElvargfromseverelydraining the player's combat stats. The effects ofantifire potionstack with the anti-dragon shield; using both provides full protection from standard dragonfire attacks.
