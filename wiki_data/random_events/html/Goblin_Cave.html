The Goblin Cave , known to the goblins as the plain of mud , is a dungeon filled with goblins located east of the Fishing Guild and south of Hemenster . Some of its inhabitants are aggressive no matter what level you are.

The cave contains the entrance to the Goblin Temple , built by goblin tribes for Bandos during the Fourth Age after the Battle of Plain of Mud .

Quests [ edit | edit source ]

  • Dwarf Cannon , when finding the missing dwarf child, Lollk .
  • One Small Favour , as Slagilith is encountered here.
  • Land of the Goblins , as the entrance to the Goblin Temple is here.
  • The General's Shadow miniquest , in which players need to kill Bouncer .

Monsters [ edit | edit source ]

  • Goblin (level 2)
  • Dungeon rat (level 12)
  • Giant bat (level 27)
  • Slagilith (level 92, One Small Favour only)
  • Bouncer (ghost) (level 160, fought during The General's Shadow )
  • Rat (level 1)
  • Chicken (level 1, can be found by searching the Crate (Goblin Cave) and Boxes (Goblin Cave) throughout the west side of the caves. Note : This causes surrounding goblins to become aggressive)
  • Spider (level 1, can be found by searching the Crate (Goblin Cave) and Boxes (Goblin Cave) throughout the west side of the caves. Note : This causes surrounding goblins to become aggressive)
