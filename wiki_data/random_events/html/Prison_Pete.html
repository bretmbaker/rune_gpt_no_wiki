Prison Pete is the main NPC in the Prison random event in ScapeRune . To escape, players must locate balloon animals that resemble the one shown to them when they pull the lever on the wall. The player receives a prison key in their inventory after popping a balloon animal. The player must give him three correct keys in a row to leave the cell and escape to freedom. Logging out resets the random event's progress.

Strategy [ edit | edit source ]

The balloon animals are made of three parts, each part having two different possible appearances. Remembering the three parts separately is much easier than trying to keep the whole image in your head.

  • Head: Antlers or no antlers.
  • Body: Thick or thin.
  • Tail: Long or short.

Rewards [ edit | edit source ]

The event can be left before completion by talking with Prison Pete, but this grants no rewards.

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • ScapeRune is a facsimile of the Falador Party Room and the surrounding area of eastern Falador .
  • When the Party Room was located in Seers' Village , ScapeRune was a facsimile of the building and its surrounding area.
  • Dying in this random event, by dealing damage to yourself, will cause you to respawn inside the event with the text The power of this special place protects your possessions. appearing in the chatbox.
  • It is possible to make Pete angry by littering in the exit hallway. To do this, players must first drop an item on the floor while completing the event. Then, when leaving the event, click to stay when prompted to pick up items. After that, players can attempt to drop an item in the exit hallway, prompting Pete to get angry at the player.
