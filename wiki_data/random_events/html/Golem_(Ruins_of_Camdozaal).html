Golem (Ruins of Camdozaal)

From Old School RuneScape Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

A golem in the Ruins of Camdozaal is any of the following four types of golems:

  • Flawed Golem , a level 13 golem.
  • Mind Golem , a level 30 golem associated to mind runes and the Mind Altar
  • Body Golem , a level 50 golem associated to body runes and the Body Altar
  • Chaos Golem , a level 70 golem associated to chaos runes and the Chaos Altar (members only)

They can be found by awakening a pile of rubble located in the east and south-east section of the ruins. Each type of golem resides in a specific part; see the image to the right.

These golems are notable for dropping Runecrafting cores and the Barronite guard . This guard is a part of the Barronite mace , a weapon to which these golems are susceptible .

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • The protagonist's script to awaken a golem is "Oi, Ge'rup!". This is completely 'out of character' compared to any other interaction of the protagonist and NPC/monster in the game. In no other transcript does the protagonist use vernacular language. It is unclear why the game designers chose to change how the protagonist speaks in this one instance.
