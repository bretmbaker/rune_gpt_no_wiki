Lake Molch is a large lake found just west of Great Kourend . Here, players can speak to Alry the Angler to engage in aerial fishing , a combined Fishing and Hunter activity. It requires a minimum level of 43 Fishing and 35 Hunter. Players can hunt for various fish and salamanders with a cormorant , much like falconry where players hunt kebbits with a gyr falcon .

Getting there [ edit | edit source ]

To reach the island in the centre of Lake Molch, the player must take a boat at one of the three docks located on the northern, western, and eastern shores of the lake.

  • To reach the northern or eastern dock the player can use fairy ring D J R to the chasm of fire and either run south-west for the eastern dock or west for the northern dock.
  • To reach the eastern dock, the player can teleport to Shayzien using Kharedst's memoirs or the book of the dead and run west.
  • To reach the western dock, the player can use a skills necklace to teleport to the Farming Guild and run south-east through Molch .
  • To reach the eastern dock, the player can use any Rada's blessing to teleport to the Kourend Woodland , then travel to C: Shayzien West using the nearby Lovakengj Minecart Network .

The following items may also be rarely obtained:

Changes [ edit | edit source ]

Date Changes
7 February 2019
( update | poll )

The golden tench can now be exchanged for 100 Molch pearls by speaking to Alry the Angler .

31 January 2019
( update | poll )

The rate of Molch pearls now scale based on Fishing and Hunter level. Previously they were obtained at a flat rate of 1/100. The angler's outfit was also added to the store.

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • The examine text on the boat that transports you to Molch Island is a reference to the viral internet meme " Boaty McBoatface "
  • The music track for this area's name is a reference to the Quentin Tarantino film series " Kill Bill "
