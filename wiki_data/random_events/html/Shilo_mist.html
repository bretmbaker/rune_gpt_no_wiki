Shilo mist

Shilo mist can be found killing the zombie variant of undead ones on Karamja . All players within a square of the mist will receive 2-3 damage per hit. To avoid this, players need to be at least two squares away from where the mist spawns.

As the mist does not inflict recurring poison , anti-poison potions are not required.

Historical [ edit | edit source ]

Shilo mist was also part of a former random event that occurred while Thieving from chests. Occasionally the chest would become stuck and mist would come out and damage nearby players. Despite the fact it had a green hitsplat , however, the mist did not inflict recurring poison.
