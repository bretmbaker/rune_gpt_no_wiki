[Page: Count Check (random event)]

Count Checkis avampyrewho appears as arandom event. The Count appears near players and asks them about their account's security settings. If players are using aJagex Accountand have aBank PINset, he will give player alampwhich grants 10 timesexperienceof the current level in a skill of choice. Count Check is one of three events which will follow players into banks and will not despawn at banks, alongsideDr Jekylland theGenie.

If the player is not using aJagex Accountor has noBank PINset, no items are rewarded. The Jagex Account requirement is waived on worlds for temporary game modes and the permanentDeadman modeworld, and the Bank PIN requirement is waived forUltimate Ironmen.
