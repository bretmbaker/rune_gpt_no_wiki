TheGoblin Cave, known to thegoblinsas theplain of mud, is adungeonfilled withgoblinslocated east of theFishing Guildand south ofHemenster. Some of its inhabitants areaggressiveno matter what level you are.
The cave contains the entrance to theGoblin Temple, built by goblin tribes forBandosduring theFourth Ageafter theBattle of Plain of Mud.
