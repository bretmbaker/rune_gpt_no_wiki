Ectophial teleport
A player empties an ectophial to activate its teleportation.

The ectophial is a reward for completing the Ghosts Ahoy quest. Players can empty it, causing them to be teleported to the Ectofuntus outside Port Phasmatys . Once the player teleports, they will automatically refill it. The player cannot interrupt the refilling animation themselves, and is immune to damage for the duration; on PvP worlds , an attacker will still gain experience on a successful hit, but no damage will be dealt. This makes the ectophial basically an unlimited teleport to the Ectofuntus.

If the player loses the ectophial, they can get a replacement from Velorina in Port Phasmatys (must wear a ghostspeak amulet or Morytania legs 2 or better) or purchase it from Perdu for 4,600 coins. Claiming a replacement from Velorina will give the player a full ectophial. Claiming an ectophial from Velorina if the player already has one will give an empty one instead, which the player first has to fill at the Ectofuntus before they can use it.

It can be used to quickly escape from fights because it is easy to replace, costs nothing, and is a one click teleport from your inventory.

Like most teleport items, the ectophial does not work past level 20 Wilderness.

Changes [ edit | edit source ]

Date Changes
28 November 2019
( update )

Players can no longer interrupt refilling the ectophial after a teleport.

24 September 2015
( update | poll )

A custom animation has been added for the ectophial. Choosing to teleport with the ectophial will now have the player pour out the liquid and get whisked away by a mysterious undead character.

4 September 2014
( update | poll )

It is now possible to get multiple ectophials.

23 May 2013
( update | poll )

When you have teleported with the ectophial, you will now automatically try to refill it from the Ectofuntus.
