Land's End is a small village in Great Kourend that is located in the south-western corner of the kingdom, within its Woodland .

Players can reach Land's End via Port Sarim or Port Piscarilius by speaking to Veos (or Cabin Boy Herbert after A Kingdom Divided ), and can leave the village via the small port where Captain Magoro is.

Taking a boat here is a task in the easy Kourend & Kebos Diary .

Features [ edit | edit source ]

Bank chest [ edit | edit source ]

A bank chest can be found within Hugor 's house, and a deposit box can be found just outside his house as well. In addition, there is a water pump just outside Lan the Butcher 's house.

Skilling [ edit | edit source ]

There are also fishing spots for small net, big net, cage and harpoon fishing to catch most common fish up to sharks , as well as two redberry bushes and five flax plants.

Spawns [ edit | edit source ]

Lastly, a lit candle , crossbow and a pile of bones spawn in the village. Players can also find various hunting equipment in crates throughout the village:

  • Bird snare - the south-western crate in Hugor's house
  • Box trap - the crate adjacent to the bookcases in the north-western house
  • Butterfly net - the crate in the north-east corner of Lan the Butcher's house

Charter ship [ edit | edit source ]

The charter ships also have a dock in the harbour in Land's End. It provides rides to several other locations. After completing Cabin Fever , the charter ship fare is reduced by 50%. Wearing the ring of charos(a) also reduces the fare by 50%. These two bonuses can be stacked to reduce the cost to 25%.
