Catherby Teleport
This article is about the spell. For the teleport tablet, see Catherby teleport (tablet) .
Catherby Teleport
Released 24 July 2006 ( Update )
Members Yes
Level 87
Spellbook Lunar
Type Teleport
Experience 92
Runes 10 3 3
Casting speed 4 ticks
Description Teleports you to Catherby
Animation
Sound effect

Catherby Teleport teleports the caster near the bank in Catherby . As with all Lunar spells , the quest Lunar Diplomacy must be completed to cast this spell.

Casting this spell is a requirement for the elite Kandarin diary .
