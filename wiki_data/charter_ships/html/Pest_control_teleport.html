Pest control teleport scrolls are consumable teleport scrolls that teleport the player to the Void Knights' Outpost. They can be obtained from all levels of Treasure Trails except beginner.

There are no requirements to use this teleport.

Although players can teleport directly to Pest Control every 20 minutes via the minigame grouping tab , these direct teleport scrolls have a few beneficial uses.

Pest Control teleport scrolls provide the closest teleport to an anvil for those doing clues and arguably the most important aspect is they provide players with the closest bank teleport that is not diary locked. By having access to such a close bank, these scrolls are ideal for using when doing Corporeal Beast resupplying or skill related activities.

For Ultimate ironmen , an added major benefit is that it is one of the closest teleports to a rune shop and a general store that sells axes.
