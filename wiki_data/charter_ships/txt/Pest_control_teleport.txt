Pest control teleportscrolls are consumableteleport scrollsthat teleport the player to theVoid Knights' Outpost.They can be obtained from all levels ofTreasure Trailsexcept beginner.

There are no requirements to use this teleport.

Although players can teleport directly to Pest Control every 20 minutes via theminigame grouping tab, these direct teleport scrolls have a few beneficial uses.

Pest Control teleport scrolls provide the closest teleport to ananvilfor those doing clues and arguably the most important aspect is they provide players with the closest bank teleport that is not diary locked. By having access to such a close bank, these scrolls are ideal for using when doingCorporeal Beastresupplying or skill related activities.

ForUltimate ironmen, an added major benefit is that it is one of the closest teleports to arune shopand ageneral storethat sells axes.
