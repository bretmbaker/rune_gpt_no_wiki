Trader Stan's Trading Postis a unique series of shops, operated by theTrader crewmembersandTrader Stan. There is a separate shop instance at each of thecharter shiplocations.

Each shop has an identical inventory which includes the standardgeneral store, however the prices are twice as expensive compared to regular general stores. The Trading Post also carries exotic items in addition to the general store stock.

The shop closest to a bank is the one inLand's End.
