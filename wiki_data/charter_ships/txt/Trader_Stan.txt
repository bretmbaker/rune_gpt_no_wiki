Trader Stan himself can be found at the southern most dock of Port Sarim, wearing a mustard-yellow outfit.

He also sells a number of items inTrader Stan's Trading Post, including common items likepots,jugs,bowlsandtinderboxes, somefooditems includingraw rabbitandbananas, and somefishingitems such asfishing rodsandlobster pots. He also sells more unusual items such asswamp paste,eye patches,glassblowing pipes,bucket of sand,seaweed,soda ash, andTyras helms.
