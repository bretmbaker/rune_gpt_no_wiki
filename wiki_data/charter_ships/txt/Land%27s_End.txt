Land's Endis a small village inGreat Kourendthat is located in the south-western corner of the kingdom, within itsWoodland.

Players can reach Land's End viaPort SarimorPort Piscariliusby speaking toVeos(orCabin Boy HerbertafterA Kingdom Divided), and can leave the village via the small port whereCaptain Magorois.

Taking a boat here is a task in theeasy Kourend & Kebos Diary.
