Gloves of darkness are obtained as a reward from master Treasure Trails. They are part of the robes of darkness set and require 40 Magic and 20 Defence to wear.

The gloves are aligned with Zaros, allowing the wearer protection while in the Ancient Prison located in the God Wars Dungeon.

This item can be stored in the treasure chest of a costume room, as part of the robes of darkness set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
