The adventurer's graceful gloves are one of many variants of graceful gloves. They are recoloured from graceful gloves by using it on adventurer's vambraces, which is purchased from the Speedrunning Reward Shop, alongside the rest of the tier 3 adventurer's outfit, for 1,800 Quest Speedrunning reward points. After purchasing the set once, you can claim unlimited sets from the Speedrunning Reward Shop. You can speak to Osten in Shayzien to revert your Adventurer's Graceful back to the default appearance for free.

This item can be stored in the magic wardrobe of a costume room.
