Warm gloves are obtained as a reward from the Reward Cart by contributing in the subduing of the Wintertodt.

Although they match the appearance of the Pyromancer outfit, they do not count towards the set bonus. They only provide warmth while subduing the Wintertodt, which reduces the damage taken. This item can be stored in the magic wardrobe of a costume room.

Players can also trade in warm gloves for an extra supply crate or 50 burnt pages via Ignisia.

If the player has three pairs of warm gloves in their bank and/or inventory, the reward cart will instead give a magic seed when it would have given gloves.[1] Currently the Grand Exchange price of 50 burnt pages is 80,950 and the price of a magic seed is 99,999.


The gloves do not work as protection when picking Belladonna; it is uncertain whether this was intended or not.
