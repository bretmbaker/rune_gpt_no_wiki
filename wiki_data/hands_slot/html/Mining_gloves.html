Mining gloves are a pair of gloves purchasable from Belona's shop, Mining Guild Mineral Exchange, for 40 unidentified minerals. Despite technically only requiring level 20 Mining to equip, level 60 is required to enter the guild to purchase them.

By speaking to Belona with these gloves and the superior mining gloves, players can pay her 40 unidentified minerals to combine them into a pair of expert mining gloves.

This item can be stored in the armour case of a costume room.

Effects[edit | edit source]

When mining certain rocks with the gloves equipped, the rock will not be depleted one time, and the player will continue mining the rock until it depletes. This effect stacks with other mining enhancers.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
12 June 2024
(update)

Mining gloves now work correctly in Miscellania.

8 May 2024
(update)
  • The price was reduced from 60 unidentified minerals to 40.
  • Mining gloves now work on iron rocks, and now provide a flat 1 rock save on iron, coal, silver, and gold rocks, instead of a certain percent chance to do so.
  • The examine text was updated. Previously, it was "These will help me mine Silver, Coal and Gold."
16 November 2022
(update | poll)

All variants of mining gloves can now be stored in the armour case of a player's costume room.

2 July 2020
(update)

Mining gloves can now be used to harvest cave nightshade.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The Mining skill guide states that the gloves require level 20 Mining to wear, even though it is impossible to obtain them at that level, as they are purchased within the Mining Guild, which requires level 60 Mining to enter.
  • Although they match the appearance of the prospector kit, they do not count towards the set bonus.
