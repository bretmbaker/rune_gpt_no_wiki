The ancient ceremonial gloves are an item dropped by all NPCs in the Ancient Prison, excluding Nex. Part of the ancient ceremonial robes set, the gloves have no requirements to wear. In addition, as a Zarosian item, it will also grant god protection towards Zarosian NPCs in the God Wars Dungeon.

Unlike the version of the robes set in RuneScape, wearing them will not allow players to bypass the Zarosian kill count to reach Nex's chamber, as the ecumenical key already exists to do so.

This item can be stored in the magic wardrobe of a costume room, as part of the ancient ceremonial robes set. Ultimate Ironmen will be unable to retrieve individual pieces until the full set is stored.
