Mourner gloves are received by killing a level 11 Mourner during Mourning's End Part I. The mourner will throw a potion at the player that lowers all combat stats to 20.

They are part of the mourner outfit. Like most of the Mourner equipment, the gloves do not need repair.

If you've completed Song of the Elves, you can find this cloak in a closed chest in the basement of the Mourner headquarters.

This item can be stored in the armour case of a costume room.
