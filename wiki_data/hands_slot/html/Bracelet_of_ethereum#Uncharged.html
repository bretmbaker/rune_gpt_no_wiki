The bracelet of ethereum is a unique drop from revenants in the Revenant Caves. Players can charge the bracelet with revenant ethers, and can hold up to 16,000 ether. Players can configure the charges while the bracelet is in their bank by right-clicking on Configure-Charges, as long as players have the required materials. When charged, the bracelet becomes untradeable and damage from revenants is reduced by 75%, with 1 charge being consumed per attack. Revenants are also made tolerant when a charged bracelet is worn.

It is always lost on death, and any ether stored within it will be dropped as well. Thus, it is recommended not to place too many charges in the bracelet, as player killers will always receive the remaining ether within it as loot upon death.

Players can dismantle an uncharged bracelet to receive 250 revenant ethers. This will result in a profit of 25 coins.

Players can right-click the Toggle-absorption option on the bracelet to automatically absorb revenant ether from defeated revenants.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
5 March 2025
(update)

The Bracelet of ethereum supports a bank item option for changing charges.

6 August 2020
(update)

Wearing the bracelet of ethereum no longer gives complete immunity of revenant attacks, now reducing incoming damage from them by 75%.

8 February 2018
(update)

The item was graphically updated.

11 January 2018
(update)

The "Toggle-absorption" option was added to the Bracelet of ethereum that will when toggled on; absorb the ether dropped from revenants when killed.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The bracelet of ethereum may be a reference to the cryptocurrency Ether, as the term 'ethereum' is not found in the English language outside of its association with the cryptocurrency.
