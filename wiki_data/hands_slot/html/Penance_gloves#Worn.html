Penance gloves are gloves obtained from Barbarian Assault, requiring 40 Defence to wear. Players with 150 honour points in each of the four roles can purchase the gloves by speaking to Commander Connad, located on the main floor of the Barbarian Outpost. Players do not have to kill the Penance Queen to obtain the gloves.

Penance gloves provide 4.535 kg in weight reduction, along with modest defensive bonuses.

These gloves can be taken to Entrana.

This item can be stored in the armour case of a costume room.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • Penance gloves are one of eleven weight-reducing items for the gloves slot, the other ten being the many cosmetic variants of graceful gloves.
  • The penance gloves provide a higher weight reduction (-4.535 kg) than the graceful gloves (-3 kg), but do not provide increased run energy restoration rates (graceful gloves provide +3% by themselves, or +5% when worn with the full outfit).
