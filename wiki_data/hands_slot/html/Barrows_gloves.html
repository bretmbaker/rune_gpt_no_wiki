Barrows gloves are gloves that can be purchased for 130,000 coins (104,000 after completing the Elite Lumbridge & Draynor Diary) from the Culinaromancer's Chest in the Lumbridge Castle cellar after completing the entire Recipe for Disaster quest.

Of all gloves in Old School RuneScape, barrows gloves provide the second highest offensive bonuses for Ranged behind the Zaryte vambraces, the second highest Magic offensive bonus behind the tormented bracelet, and the second highest Strength bonus behind the ferocious gloves. For these reasons, many players recommend completing Recipe for Disaster and acquiring these gloves as a long-term goal that every beginner should work toward.

While these gloves lack a Defence requirement, they cannot be acquired at 1 Defence without using glitches due to the levels required to complete and experience gained from completing the necessary quests to complete Recipe for Disaster. The minimum Defence level a player can have to acquire and equip these gloves is 35 when all quest requirements and experience rewards are calculated, assuming the player did not accept Daero's training at the end of Monkey Madness I.

As with all Culinaromancer's gloves, this piece of armour cannot be made using the Smithing skill.

Purchasing a pair of Barrows gloves is a requirement for the Hard Lumbridge & Draynor Diary.

When lost in a PvP death, they turn into 78,000 coins.

Trivia[edit | edit source]
