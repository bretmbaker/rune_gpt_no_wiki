The Shayzien gloves (4) are gloves that require 20 Defence to equip, and are part of the Shayzien armour (tier 4) set.

It is dropped by the tier 4 soldier at the Combat Ring in the Shayzien Encampment. It can also be created at an anvil with 75 Smithing and 1 lovakite bar.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
10 January 2024
(update | poll)
  • The "Destroy" option has been replaced with "Drop". Previously, the destroy text stated "You fought a Shayzien soldier for this. You'll have to repeat the fight for a replacement."
  • The item can now be created through the Smithing skill with the removal of Kourend Favour.
10 January 2019
(update)

The examine text was modified, the old examine text was "Dress like a tier 4 soldier of the Shayzien House."
