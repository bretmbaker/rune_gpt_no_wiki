The zenyte bracelet is made by using a gold bar, a zenyte, and a bracelet mould on a furnace with a Crafting level of 95. Doing so gives the player 180 experience in Crafting.

Players can cast Lvl-7 Enchant on a zenyte bracelet to enchant it into a tormented bracelet. This requires level 93 Magic and grants 110 Magic experience.
