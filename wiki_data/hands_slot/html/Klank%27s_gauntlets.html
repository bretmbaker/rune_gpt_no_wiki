Klank's gauntlets are a pair of gauntlets obtained during the Underground Pass quest to prevent the half-soulless from biting the player when searching their cages for Iban's dove. If lost, they can be recovered during and after the quest from Klank for 5,000 coins. Klank's gauntlets have the same stats as steel gauntlets.
