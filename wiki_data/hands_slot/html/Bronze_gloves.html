Bronze gloves are special gloves that can be bought from the Culinaromancer's Chest after completion of one Recipe for Disaster subquest.

Bronze gloves, like most other untradeable items, are kept on death in the Wilderness if they are among your protected items.

As with all Culinaromancer's gloves, this piece of armour cannot be made using the Smithing skill.
