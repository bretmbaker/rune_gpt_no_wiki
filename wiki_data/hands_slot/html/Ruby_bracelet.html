The ruby bracelet is a jewellery item that players can make by using a gold bar on a furnace while they have a ruby and a bracelet mould in their inventory. It requires a Crafting level of 42, and gives 80 experience when made. It offers no combat bonuses when worn.

Members can enchant the ruby bracelet into an inoculation bracelet by casting Lvl-3 Enchant on it. Doing so requires level 49 Magic and grants 59 Magic experience.
