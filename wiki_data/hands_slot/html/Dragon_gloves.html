Dragon gloves are a pair of Culinaromancer's gloves that can be purchased from the Culinaromancer's Chest after completion of all eight Recipe for Disaster subquests. As with all of the Culinaromancer's gloves, they cannot be made using the Smithing skill.

Dragon gloves cost the same as Barrows gloves, but have significantly lower stats. However, obtaining dragon gloves only requires 107 quest points, compared to the 175 needed to complete the quest and unlock the Barrows variant.
