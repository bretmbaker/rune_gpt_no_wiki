[Weapon: Regen Bracelet]


Theregen braceletis an enchanted bracelet that is worn in thehand slot. It is created by castingLvl-6 Enchanton anonyx bracelet, requiring 87Magicand granting 97 Magicexperience.
When worn, it doubles the player's naturalHitpointsregeneration amount, healing 2 Hitpoints per minute instead of the standard 1 Hitpoint per minute. The effect of the regen bracelet stacks with either theRapid Healprayeror with theHitpoints cape(which do not stack with each other); using either of these in conjunction with a regen bracelet effectively quadruples the natural rate of Hitpoint regeneration, increasing it to 2 Hitpoints per 30 seconds. This is a very helpful perk during activities that deal low amounts of continuous damage, reducing the need to bank frequently and rely on outside sources of healing. Examples of activities where it is helpful are fighting weak but accurateNPCs, subduing theWintertodt, fightingZalcano, doingPyramid plunder, orpickpocketing(though most players would prefer the added chance of double loot from wearingrogue gloveswhile pickpocketing).
