[Weapon: Mystic Gloves (Dark)]


Mystic gloves (dark)are magic armour that is part of themystic robe setworn in the gloves slot. Mystic gloves come in blue, white and black, but all offer the same bonuses. All mystic gloves require 40Magicand 20Defenceto wear. Dark mystic gloves are rarely dropped by Banshees, which require 15 slayer to kill.  It can be stored in amagic wardrobein a player-owned house.
