[Weapon: Fremennik Gloves]


Fremennik glovescan be bought fromYrsa's Clothing ShopinRellekkaafter completingThe Fremennik Trials. These gloves can also be obtained as a random reward fromKingdom ManagementonMiscellania. They give the same stats as normalleather glovesandCanifis gloves. These gloves can also be a drop fromDagannothsin theWaterbirth Island Dungeon.
