[Weapon: Void Knight Gloves#Locked]


Void Knight glovesare a piece ofVoid Knight equipment. They can be bought from aVoid Knightfor 150commendations, although a player must have at least 42Attack,Strength,Defence,Hitpoints,Ranged, andMagic, along with 22Prayerto buy and wear them.
If the player dies below level 20Wilderness, it will remain in the player's inventory. However, the item will be unusable and must be repaired with120,000 coinsby using the item onPerdu.
