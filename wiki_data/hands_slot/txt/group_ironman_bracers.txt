[Weapon: Group Ironman Bracers]


Group ironman bracersare obtainable only by players who chose to be a standard group ironman inGroup Ironmangame mode. They can be obtained by using theArmourright-click option on theGroup Ironman tutor.
Along with theGroup ironman bracers (unranked)andHardcore group ironman bracers, these are the only F2P hands slot equipment that provide a Ranged Defence bonus.
