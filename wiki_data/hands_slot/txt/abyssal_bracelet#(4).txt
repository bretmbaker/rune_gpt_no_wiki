[Weapon: Abyssal Bracelet#(4)]


Anabyssal braceletis made by casting theLvl-4 Enchantspell on adiamond bracelet, requiring aMagiclevel of 57. Diamond bracelets can be made at level 58Crafting.
Wearing the bracelet will prevent the player from beingskulledwhen entering theAbyss. Each bracelet holds 5 charges, and 1 charge is used each time the wearer enters the Abyss. When all charges are used, the bracelet crumbles to dust.
