[Weapon: Barrows Gloves]


Barrows glovesareglovesthat can be purchased for 130,000coins(104,000 after completing theElite Lumbridge & Draynor Diary) from theCulinaromancer's Chestin theLumbridge Castlecellar after completing the entireRecipe for Disasterquest.
Of all gloves inOld School RuneScape, barrows gloves provide the second highest offensive bonuses forRangedbehind theZaryte vambraces, the second highestMagicoffensive bonus behind thetormented bracelet, and the second highestStrengthbonus behind theferocious gloves. For these reasons, many players recommend completing Recipe for Disaster and acquiring these gloves as a long-term goal that every beginner should work toward.
