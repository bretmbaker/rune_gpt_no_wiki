[Weapon: Emerald Bracelet]


Anemerald braceletis made by using agold bar, anemerald, and abracelet mouldon afurnace. It requires aCraftinglevel of 30 and provides 65 Craftingexperiencewhen made. It offers no combat bonuses when worn.
An emerald bracelet can be enchanted into aCastle wars braceletusing theLvl-2 Enchantspell by players with aMagiclevel of 27, granting 37 experience.
