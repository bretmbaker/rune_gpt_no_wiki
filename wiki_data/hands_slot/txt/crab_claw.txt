[Weapon: Crab Claw]


Acrab clawis a gauntlet worn in the gloves slot. There are no level requirements to wear the crab claw. Crafting this claw has a chance to fail, producing abroken crab claw.
Players cannot wear crab claws whilst havingclawsequipped; attempting to do so results in the chatbox stating "You need to remove your claws first."
