[Weapon: Black Spiky Vambraces]


Black spiky vambracesareblack dragonhide vambraceswithkebbit clawsattached. The difference in stats between spiked and non-spiked vambraces is a bonus to strength of +2 and a slight increase in melee defence for the spiked ones.
Like the black dragonhide vambraces they are created from, this variant also requires 70 Ranged to be worn.
