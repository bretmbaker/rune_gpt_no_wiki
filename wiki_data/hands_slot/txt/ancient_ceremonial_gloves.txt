[Weapon: Ancient Ceremonial Gloves]


Theancient ceremonial glovesare an item dropped by all NPCs in theAncient Prison, excludingNex. Part of theancient ceremonial robesset, the gloves have no requirements to wear. In addition, as aZarosian item, it will also grantgod protectiontowards Zarosian NPCs in theGod Wars Dungeon.
Unlike the version of the robes set inRuneScape, wearing themwill notallow players to bypass the Zarosiankill countto reach Nex's chamber, as theecumenical keyalready exists to do so.
