[Weapon: Smiths Gloves (I)]


Thesmiths gloves (i)is a piece of theSmiths' Uniformset that is made from usingsmiths glovesandice gloveson each other, combining the effects of both.
The gloves give a 20% chance of speeding up a smithing action performed at ananvilby 1 tick; it also gives a 20% chance to give increased progress onpreformsin theGiants' Foundry. Wearing the full set will increase this chance to 100%. They also allow players to pick up hot metal bars at theBlast Furnaceand preforms in theGiants' Foundrywithout the need for abucket of waterto cool them down.
