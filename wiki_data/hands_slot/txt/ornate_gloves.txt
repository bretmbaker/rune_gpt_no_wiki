[Weapon: Ornate Gloves]


Ornate glovesare obtained from completing the first clue during theCrack the Clue IIevent. They are part of theornate armourset, and are obtainable only once if you already have them in your inventory or bank. If you happen to accidentally destroy them, you will need to discover them again.
They are obtained alongside theornate boots.
