[Weapon: Bracelet Of Clay]


Abracelet of clayallows players tominesoft clayinstead of hardclay. It is made by enchanting asapphire braceletwith theLvl-1 Enchantspell.
A single bracelet can mine 28soft clay, after which it disintegrates. The player can check how many more soft clay it can mine by equipping it and then right-clicking "check." The number of charges is stored per player, not per bracelet, so equipping another bracelet will not reset the number of charges a player has.
