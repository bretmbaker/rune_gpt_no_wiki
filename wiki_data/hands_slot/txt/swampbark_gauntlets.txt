[Weapon: Swampbark Gauntlets]


Swampbark gauntletsare a piece ofswampbark armourworn in thehands slot, requiring level 50Magicand 50Defenceto equip. The gauntlets can be made with level 42Runecraftby bringingsplitbark gauntletsand 100nature runesto theNature Altar. Doing so requires players to have first learnt the secret to infuse the armour via reading therunescroll of swampbark.
They do not contribute to the swampbark armour set's effect of increasing the duration of bind spells.
