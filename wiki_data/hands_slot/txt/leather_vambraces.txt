[Weapon: Leather Vambraces]


Leather vambracesareleather armourworn on the hands. With no requirements to equip, they are most commonly used by low-level rangers, and often mages (as there is no magic penalty). Overall they are generally a superior alternative toleather gloveswith a notable exception of thecrushdefence, and could be considered the best "gloves" for free-to-play users who lack 40Rangedto equipgreen dragonhide vambraces, as well as the best-in-slot mage gloves due to their lack of negative penalties.
Kebbit clawscan be used on these vambraces to makespiky vambraces, requiring aCraftinglevel of 32.
