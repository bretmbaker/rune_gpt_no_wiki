[Weapon: Green Spiky Vambraces]


Green spiky vambracesis a piece ofRangedarmour worn in the gloves slot. They are created by usingkebbit clawswithgreen dragonhide vambraceswhich requires acraftinglevel of 32. The difference in stats between spiked and non-spiked vambraces is a bonus to strength of +2 and a slight increase in physical defence for the spiked ones.
Like the green dragonhide vambraces they are created from, this variant also requires 40 Ranged to be worn.
