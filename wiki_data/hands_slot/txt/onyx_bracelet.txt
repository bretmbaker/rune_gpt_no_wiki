[Weapon: Onyx Bracelet]


Theonyx braceletis made by using agold bar, anonyx, and abracelet mouldon afurnacea withCraftinglevel of 84, earning 125experience. It is the bracelet of the second highest gem quality in the game, behindzenyte.
The bracelet has no bonuses of any kind until enchanted. If it is enchanted using the level 87 spellLvl-6 Enchant(earning 97Magicexperience), it becomes aregen bracelet, which adds combat bonuses and doubles the natural restoration ofHitpoints.
