[Weapon: Twitcher%27S Gloves]


Twitcher's glovesare an item purchased from theForestry Shopfor 5,000anima-infused barkand 500 notedwillow logs. These logs can be bought on theGrand Exchangefor39,000.
Whilst equipped, the player will have a 20% increased chance to receive a specific type ofbird nestwhenever they receive one fromWoodcutting; the player can right-click "Settings" on the gloves to specify which type:Ring,Egg,Seed, orClue. This effect stacks with the effects of astrung rabbit foot.[1]Additionally, althoughredwood treesonly yield clue nests when cut, it appears the chance of getting it is increased nonetheless.[2]
