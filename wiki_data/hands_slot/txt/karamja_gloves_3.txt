[Weapon: Karamja Gloves 3]


Karamja gloves 3are the reward for completing all the Easy, Medium and Hard tasks in theKaramja Diary. The gloves can be claimed by talking toPirate Jackie the FruitinBrimhaven. Alternatively, players can purchase one fromPerdufor 200 coins should they lose it.
The teleport provided by these gloves is very close to adeposit chest.
