[Weapon: Gold Bracelet]


TheGold braceletis made by using agold baron afurnace. It requires aCraftinglevel of 7 and gives 25experiencewhen made. It offers no combat bonuses when worn.
It is commonly used as aHigh Alchemyitem because it is very cheap and very easy to make. Due to the high price ofgold ore, players who want to buy materials to make these are advised to buygold barsdirectly rather than purchasing gold ore and smelting their own bars.
