[Weapon: Dragon Gloves]


Dragon glovesare a pair ofCulinaromancer's glovesthat can be purchased from theCulinaromancer's Chestafter completion of all eightRecipe for Disastersubquests. As with all of the Culinaromancer's gloves, theycannotbe made using theSmithingskill.
Dragon gloves cost the same asBarrows gloves, but have significantly lower stats. However, obtaining dragon gloves only requires 107quest points, compared to the 175 needed to complete the quest and unlock the Barrows variant.
