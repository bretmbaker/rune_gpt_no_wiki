[Weapon: Pirate%27S Hook]


ThePirate's hookis a reward that can be obtained from theBrimhaven Agility Arena. It is purchased fromPirate Jackie the Fruitfor 800Brimhaven vouchers.
Without the completion of theelite Karamja diary, it takes a minimum of 800 minutes (or 13 hours 20 minutes) to earn enough tickets for this item, assuming no dispensers are missed. Players with the diary completed have a 10% chance to gain double tickets which lowers the time to 728 minutes (12 hours 7 minutes).
