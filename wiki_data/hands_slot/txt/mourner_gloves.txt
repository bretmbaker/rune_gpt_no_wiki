[Weapon: Mourner Gloves]


Mourner glovesare received by killing a level 11MournerduringMourning's End Part I. The mourner will throw a potion at theplayerthat lowers all combat stats to 20.
They are part of themourner outfit. Like most of the Mourner equipment, the gloves do not need repair.
