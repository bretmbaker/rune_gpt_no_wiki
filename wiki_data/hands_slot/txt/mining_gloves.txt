[Weapon: Mining Gloves]


Mining glovesare a pair of gloves purchasable fromBelona's shop,Mining Guild Mineral Exchange, for 40unidentified minerals. Despite technically only requiring level 20 Mining to equip, level 60 is required to enter the guild to purchase them.
By speaking to Belona with these gloves and thesuperior mining gloves, players can pay her 40 unidentified minerals to combine them into a pair ofexpert mining gloves.
