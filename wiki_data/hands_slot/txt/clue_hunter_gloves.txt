[Weapon: Clue Hunter Gloves]


Clue hunter glovesare obtained from completing the first clue during theCrack the Clue!event. It is part of theclue hunter outfit. Obtainable only once if you already have it in your inventory or bank. If you happen to accidentally destroy it, you will need to discover it again.
These items are obtained alongside theclue hunter boots.
