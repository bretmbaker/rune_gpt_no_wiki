[Weapon: Yellow Gloves]


Yellow glovescan be purchased inCanifisfromBarkerat his store,Barker's Haberdashery, and are also dropped byCrawling Hands. The gloves come in five different colours: grey, red, yellow, teal, and purple. These gloves are often worn to match certain armour sets, such asgold-trimmedandgod armoursets.
Their defence bonus is equal to that of leather gloves.
