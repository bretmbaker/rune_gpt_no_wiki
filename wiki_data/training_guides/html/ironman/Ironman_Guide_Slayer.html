Ironman Guide

Training Slayer on an ironman account is the sole way to upgrade some of the best-in-slot items. It is also an excellent method to make significant money and train combat skills. Many of the key worthwhile items are locked behind Slayer levels for Ironmen, such as the abyssal whip , dragon boots , trident of the seas , occult necklace and dragon hunter lance .

Duradel is generally the best Slayer master to use. Konar quo Maten should be used for every milestone task for the extra Slayer reward points .

Making cannonballs is not worthwhile for efficient Slayer training (except for smoke devils in some cases), because it is simply faster to complete a task without using a dwarf multicannon than making cannonballs and using one. Because of this, certain tasks which are decent on a main account, such as suqahs and kalphites , are not worth doing on an Ironman account, as they have no notable drops and offer mediocre experience rates without a cannon. Blocking suqahs and kalphites should be one of your first priorities as an ironman player.

Once you have completed hard tasks in the Morytania Diary , it is worthwhile to use the bonecrusher for tasks where you normally would not pick up the bones. Using the bonecrusher provides supplemental Prayer experience in the long run and also restores Prayer points when doing certain tasks in the Catacombs of Kourend. Bonecrusher charges can be easily sustained by the daily bonemeal reward from Robin .

For tasks you want to finish quickly (usually when prioritising Slayer points), it is recommended to wear the Expeditious bracelet , since it has a 25% chance to treat each kill as 2 kills towards the Slayer task. If you instead want to extend the task, a Bracelet of slaughter will have a 25% chance for a kill to not count towards the Slayer task

When using Slayer reward points, prioritise blocking and skipping poor tasks over unlocking perks and extensions. Unlock superior Slayer monsters, the Slayer helmet and the ability to craft Slayer rings first. If you are using Ranged often, consider unlocking Broader Fletching perk early on for the ability to fletch broad bolts .

Ring of wealth is a good choice for the ring slot before you have access to Fremennik rings, as it automatically collects coin drops from monsters.

Unless you want to get quicker Magic levels, use defensive casting when bursting tasks (such as dust devils ), since it gives more experience overall. If you are low on herb seeds , kill normal nechryael with melee instead of bursting Greater nechryael in the Catacombs of Kourend .

Before you can kill abyssal demons for the abyssal whip , train only Strength after you have 75 Attack (for Arclight ) and 70 Defence (for Barrows equipment ). Unless you want to hit combat level 100 sooner, for Duradel. Once you have obtained the abyssal whip, train on controlled until you have 99 Strength (if you haven't already), and after that train Attack and Defence. If you have a kraken tentacle , use the excess abyssal whips for an abyssal tentacle .

Starting off [ edit | edit source ]

The natural history quiz has no requirements and rewards 1,000 Slayer experience, making for an easy way to reach level 9 Slayer.

To train Slayer efficiently, it is recommended that you begin after reaching 80â€“90 combat, with reasonable gear.

Recommended skills:

  • 70+ , and
  • 48 ( Kourend Castle Teleport )
  • 43 ( Protect from Melee )
  • 45 ( super attack potions ) or 55 ( super strength potions )
  • 70 ( Taverley Dungeon shortcut to blue dragons )
  • 27 for the ability to make emerald rings

Using Ranged for Slayer tasks in general is not recommended due to the poorer damage output of the available weapons and ammunition compared to dragon scimitar. However, some tasks can prove to be difficult or tedious to do without Prayer flicking or use of Prayer potions, so safespotting can be a justified option in some cases.

Point boosting [ edit | edit source ]

Turael [ edit | edit source ]

When starting Slayer training, the first priority is to block the most common tasks that are bad while having some Slayer points in reserve for skipping less common, but equally bad tasks. It is therefore optimal to start by boosting Slayer points by using Turael and doing every 10th task at Konar quo Maten or the highest level Slayer master you are able to use. While Turael tasks do not give good experience, they are very easy and fast to complete unlike some of the tasks given by higher-level Slayer masters.

Turael can be quickly accessed via the games necklace 's teleport to Burthorpe . Wearing heavy armour is not recommended, as you will be running a lot and the monsters you are killing do not deal much damage. Whenever you need to bank, teleport to the Ferox Enclave via a ring of dueling or use the minigame teleport and use the free-for-all portal to reset stats and run energy after banking.

Recommended inventory setup for Turael tasks:

  • Games necklace
  • Ring of dueling for resetting stats at the Ferox Enclave
  • 2 super attacks (and super strengths if not using a dragon battleaxe)
  • Runes for teleportation
  • Ardougne cloak 1 (or higher) and a dramen / lunar staff for access to fairy rings
  • Ectophial
  • Chronicle
  • A light source
  • Dragon battleaxe or dragon dagger for special attacks
  • Energy potions, antipoison (for cave crawlers and slimes) and some food (optional)

Once you have attained enough Slayer points, block these tasks as soon as you can:

  • Black demon
  • Hellhound
  • Suqah (Konar quo Maten does not assign these)
  • Kalphite (block last, Kalphite Workers are reasonably fast to kill)

Once you have these tasks blocked and some spare points for skipping, move on to the highest-level Slayer master you are able to use.

Krystilia (Wilderness Slayer) [ edit | edit source ]

Alternatively, doing Wilderness Slayer tasks from Krystilia is another option for obtaining a good amount of Slayer points due to rewarding significantly more points per completed task than any other master. Krystilia has no requirements, but players should be sufficient enough to survive in the Wilderness and are strongly recommended to have at least 43 prayer for access to overhead prayers . When doing Wilderness tasks, only risk easily replaceable equipment you are willing to lose, such as monk robes or dragonhide armour. It's important to note that Krystilia can assign tasks of any combat level (Assuming skill and quest requirements are met), making this a potentially less preferable option at lower combat levels due to the chance of being given slow and/or difficult tasks early on.

Monsters killed for wilderness tasks have the chance to drop larran's keys which can be used to open larran's chests for plenty of cash and useful resources for ironmen like seeds, gems, ores, food and alchables. Slayer monsters within the Wilderness Slayer Cave have the added bonus of generally increased drop rates compared to their normal counterparts which, along with access to looting bags for an extra 28 inventory slots, make this a high risk but extremely high reward option of training slayer. Lastly, monsters within the Wilderness can drop blighted food , blighted super restores and blighted rune sacks all of which can only be used within the wilderness, but are particularly beneficial for ironmen as they add some level of self-sustainability and reduce the need to obtain these resources yourself.

Equipment [ edit | edit source ]

Melee [ edit | edit source ]

The aim for melee equipment is to maximise strength bonus.

Typical low/mid-level gear includes:

  • Slayer helmet (i) : Can be assembled after purchasing the Malevolent Masquerade ability from a Slayer master, costing 400 Slayer reward points. Black mask can be obtained from cave horrors .
  • Any God blessing : Can be obtained from Treasure Trails . Alternatively, Rada's blessing 2 or higher can be used.
  • Fighter torso : Can be obtained from the Barbarian Assault minigame.
  • Obsidian platelegs : Can be purchased in TzHaar-Hur-Zal's Equipment Store .
  • Climbing boots : Can be bought from Tenzing .
  • Barrows gloves : Can be purchased from Culinaromancer's Chest after completion of Recipe for Disaster .
  • Amulet of glory : Dragonstone amulet can be made at 80 Crafting and enchanted into an amulet of glory with Lvl-5 Enchant , or obtained from dragon implings .
  • Fire cape : Can be obtained as a reward for defeating TzTok-Jad in the Fight Caves .
  • Ring of wealth : Dragonstone ring can be made at 55 Crafting, which can be turned into a ring of wealth by Lvl-5 Enchant. Hardcore Ironmen should wear a ring of life on tasks which they can easily die if they disconnect.
  • Dragon scimitar : Can be purchased from Daga on Ape Atoll after completion of Monkey Madness I .
  • Dragon defender : Can be obtained as a rare drop in the basement of the Warriors' Guild from the cyclopes that reside there.
  • Zombie axe : Can be repaired with 70 smithing, after obtaining it in broken form as a rare drop from an Armoured zom bie after starting Defender of Varrock .

Turael skipping [ edit | edit source ]

Higher levelled players may find that they wish to spend Slayer points en masse in an attempt to get one (or a few) specific tasks, such as cave kraken, smoke devil, hydra, greater demons, etc.

Players should avoid spending all of their points on skips to try and get specific tasks in this manner .

Instead, if players receive an undesired task from their Slayer master, they can return to Turael to sacrifice their Slayer streak and replace their task with a much faster one. Players can then complete Turael's task and return to their preferred Slayer master to try to get the task they want, rinse and repeat. Players that are about to begin Turael skipping should make sure that they are not too close to a major point streak, as they could be missing out on some free points in that case. Even regardless of that, the amount of points that players will save by not spending them on skips will greatly outweigh the loss of their streak. As far as for levelling Slayer, focusing on Turael skipping may not necessarily grant high Slayer experience rates, and may only be useful for getting specific tasks.

The inventory setup is similar to that of Point boosting with Turael, but higher level players can add things to the list such as: a rune pouch with NPC contact (if they have a POH with various portals), full graceful, a Royal seed pod, Salve amulet (e), and maybe even a Cannon and Cannonballs.

Killing bosses and demi-bosses on task [ edit | edit source ]

Abyssal Sire (Abyssal demons) [ edit | edit source ]

Abyssal Sire drops the unsired , which can be placed at the Font of Consumption and gives the player a reward in the process. The most notable rewards are the abyssal bludgeon pieces and the abyssal dagger .

Alchemical Hydra (Hydras) [ edit | edit source ]

The Alchemical Hydra notably drops the hydra's claw , which can be combined with a Zamorakian hasta to create a dragon hunter lance , one of the best items to have on an Ironman account. Additionally, the Alchemical Hydra also drops hydra leather, which can be made into ferocious gloves , and brimstone ring pieces. The Alchemical Hydra is also a generous source of money, Prayer and Herblore experience. Hydras can only be assigned by Konar quo Maten.

The twisted bow is the most effective weapon to kill the Alchemical Hydra, followed by the dragon hunter crossbow with ruby bolts (e) until it reaches 40% remaining Hitpoints , upon which players should switch to using a toxic blowpipe . Toxic blowpipe is also viable throughout the whole kill should players not have a twisted bow or dragon hunter crossbow, although its rapid consumption of Zulrah's scales should be noted, especially if planning to kill the boss enough to obtain all the unique drops.

Cerberus (Hellhounds) [ edit | edit source ]

Cerberus drops the primordial , pegasian and eternal crystals , which are used to create best-in-slot boots in the game. Typically Cerberus is killed with an arclight, but you can still use a Zamorakian hasta/abyssal bludgeon/toxic blowpipe if you run out of charges. Hellhounds are very commonly assigned by Duradel.

Ironmen are strongly advised to use a different strategy which lets players often skip the Prayer-draining ghost phases entirely, letting players easily use less than a quarter of the Prayer potions that they would normally. The strategy revolves around keeping Cerberus above 400 health until 56 seconds passed from when she first attacked (or more precisely, after her second set of combo attacks, wait for 3 more regular attacks), before switching to your best DPS gear/spec weapons to finish her off. Players must be able to maintain more than ~7.5 DPS after the 56 seconds have passed to skip ghosts entirely. Using a full set of Guthan's equipment for the first 56 seconds is very important for extending trips with this strategy.

But if killing Cerberus normally (without attempting to skip ghost phases), it is recommended to be able to effectively pickpocket Master Farmers for ranarr seeds, as players can easily run out of Prayer potions if they go dry on the uniques.

Dagannoth Kings (Dagannoths) [ edit | edit source ]

Dagannoth Kings are killed for their unique drops (notably the rings and the dragon axe). After completion of the elite Fremennik Diary , killing the boss trio also offers good Prayer experience from the noted dagannoth bones .

Demonic gorillas (Black demons) [ edit | edit source ]

Demonic gorillas drop zenyte shards , which can be used to make the best-in-slot jewellery. Demonic gorillas also drop good alchables and decent amount of high-level tree seeds.

Lizardman shamans (Lizardmen) [ edit | edit source ]

Lizardman shamans drop the dragon warhammer , which has one of the most useful special attacks in the game. On task, the player can kill lizardman shamans in the Lizardman Caves which is often way less crowded than the Lizardman Canyon .

Kalphite Queen (Kalphites) [ edit | edit source ]

Kalphite Queen is killed for Herblore and Farming supplies, most notable being the wines of Zamorak and potato cacti .

Kree'arra (Aviansie) [ edit | edit source ]

Kree'arra is killed for Armadyl armour pieces.

K'ril Tsutsaroth (Greater demons) [ edit | edit source ]

K'ril Tsutsaroth is mainly killed for the Zamorakian spear , which can be made into a Zamorakian hasta. Other notable uniques include the staff of the dead and Zamorak hilt . Typical setup uses Arclight and armour with high Magic defence. If you do not have Karil's leatherskirt, Void Knight robe gives slightly more Magic defence than black d'hide chaps. Players with a bow of Faerdhinen and crystal armour or a twisted bow will be able to get much faster kills using these weapons. Greater demons are commonly assigned by Duradel.

Vorkath (Blue dragons) [ edit | edit source ]

Vorkath is killed for money, Prayer and Crafting experience.

  • Extension: No
  • Bracelet: Expeditious

  • Weight: 9 (Duradel), 7 (Konar quo Maten)
  • Combat style: Magic
  • Location: Catacombs of Kourend
  • Extension: Yes
  • Bracelet: Slaughter

Lure the monsters with darts, run around a corner until they stack up and kill with Ice Barrage. If low on health from the death spawns, cast Blood Barrage to heal up. Nechryael (melee) [ edit | edit source ]

  • Weight: 9 (Duradel), 7 (Konar quo Maten)
  • Combat style: Melee
  • Location: Slayer Tower
  • Extension: Yes
  • Bracelet: None
  • Experience per hour: ~31,000

Kill normal nechryael with melee in the Slayer Tower, use Arclight if you have charges. Bring runes for High Level Alchemy and a seed box if you have one. Saradomin godsword is very helpful for this task for its special attack, and allows the player to do the task in one trip.

When the superior spawns chaotic death spawns, run away until they become unaggressive. If you are doing the task upstairs, you can also climb down and up the chain to reset aggressiveness.

  • Weight: 9 (Duradel), 7 (Konar quo Maten)
  • Combat style: Magic
  • Location: Smoke Devil Dungeon
  • Extension: N/A
  • Bracelet: Slaughter
  • Experience per hour: 75,000â€“80,000 (no cannon), 120,000â€“175,000 (cannon)

Lure Smoke Devils around the western bone pile and manually cast Ice Barrage on the eastern stack every 5 ticks. Run around bone pile to stack the monsters.

Making cannonballs for luring is only worthwhile if you are able to get good experience rates (160,000+ Slayer experience per hour). Otherwise it is faster overall to lure them with a shortbow, you can switch between rapid/longranged to lure more. You can now also use a Goading potion to maintain aggression.

Aviansie 1 8 69-148 Should only be unlocked if killing Kree'arra for Armadyl armour . Killing Kree'arra effectively typically requires use of chinchompas or gear such as crystal armour and a Bow of faerdhinen or a Tumeken's shadow and appropriate mage gear. Unlock only if killing Kree'arra .
Basilisks 40 7 61, 204 Killing the basilisk knight variant drops the basilisk jaw at a rate of 1/1,000 while on task. Slow kills for lower leveled players, poor drops before completion of The Fremennik Exiles Unlock only if killing basilisk knights .
Black demons 1 8 172 Decent Prayer experience from ensouled demon heads . Should only be done if killing demonic gorillas or Skotizo . Poor experience rates and very long tasks. Poor drops if not killing demonic gorillas . Skip/Block (unless killing demonic gorillas )
Black dragons 1 9 227 If killing baby black dragons , a very fast task when unextended. N/A Do
Bloodvelds 50 8 76, 81, 123 Decent experience rates, mutated bloodvelds frequently drop a generous amount of blood runes . Decent Prayer experience from ensouled bloodveld heads . Can be crowded. Poor drops if not killing mutated bloodvelds . Not worth doing at higher levels. Do/Skip 30,000
Blue dragons 1 4 111 Good Prayer experience. Blue dragons on task are more efficient overall than green dragons off task. Vorkath may be killed instead if the player has a dragon hunter crossbow or a dragon hunter lance / Osmumten's fang . Requires frequent banking. Very long task if killing Vorkath . Do 20,000
Bosses Varies (1 for all except a few) 12 Varies Can be a relatively quick task depending on what boss you get, in addition to 5,000 Slayer experience upon completion of the task. Some bosses can be rather annoying to kill, and it requires 200 Slayer points to unlock boss tasks. Do/Skip/Should not be unlocked
Cave horror 58 4 80 Decent Herblore and Prayer experience, relatively fast task if blowpiping. Slow experience rates if using melee due to the monsters being spread apart. Not worth doing unless Slayer points are an issue. Do/Skip 22,000 (using the toxic blowpipe)
Cave kraken 87 9 127, 291 Killing the boss variant yields a decent amount of various supplies. Slow experience rates, long task if killing the boss. Do until you get the trident of the seas and kraken tentacle , block afterwards 15,000
Dagannoths 1 9 74, 92 Good Herblore experience, decent Prayer experience from ensouled dagannoth heads . Good experience rates if maging. Dagannoth Kings may be killed instead for Prayer experience. Maging them requires a lot of practice and money for the runes. Do 30,000 (melee), 55,000â€“80,000 (Magic)
Dark beasts 90 11 182 Fast task when unextended. Somewhat poor drops for their level. Do 30,000
Drakes 84 8 192 Decent Prayer experience. A very long task, requires frequent banking if banking the bones. Somewhat poor drops for their level. Requires protection from the mountain heat if elite Kourend & Kebos Diary hasn't been completed. Skip/Block
Dust devils 65 5 93 Good experience rates if maging in the Catacombs of Kourend . Alchables cover most of the money spent into runes. Often crowded in the Catacombs of Kourend . Do 68,000 (Magic)
Elves 1 4 90, 108 Decent Herblore and Prayer experience. Not worth doing unless Slayer points are an issue. Skip
Fire giants 1 7 86, 104, 109 None. Rather poor drops. Skip
Fossil Island wyverns 66, 82 7 139, 147, 152, 210 Decent Prayer experience, decent drops including seaweed spores . Relatively fast task with high combat stats. Not worth doing at higher levels. Stop the Wyvern should be purchased once the player has enough Slayer points. Block ( Stop the Wyvern ), do otherwise
Gargoyles 75 8 111 Good money and supplies. Gargoyles have no defence and are fairly inaccurate, making it an easy task when using decent food. Grotesque Guardians can be substituted. Can be crowded in the Slayer Tower . Do 31,000
Greater demons 1 9 92 Good experience rates when killing tormented demons , which additionally drop the tormented synapse and burning claws . Decent Prayer experience from ensouled demon heads . Poor drops (unless killing tormented demons or K'ril Tsutsaroth). Block (unless killing K'ril Tsutsaroth or tormented demons )
Hellhounds 1 10 122, 127 Frequently drops hard clue scrolls . No drops unless killing Cerberus . Killing Cerberus uses a lot of Prayer potions. Block (unless killing Cerberus )
Iron dragons 1 5 189 Relatively fast task if the player has a dragon hunter lance . Slow experience rates, poor drops for their level. Do/Skip 25,000
Kalphites 1 9 28, 85, 141, 333 Relatively fast task if killing the workers. The Kalphite Queen can be killed for Herblore supplies. No notable drops unless killing the Kalphite Queen . Block (unless killing the Kalphite Queen )
Kurasks 70 4 106 Decent money and Herblore supplies. Slow to kill if leaf-bladed battleaxe isn't available. Somewhat far from a bank and going to them can take a while without 61 Agility . Not worth doing at higher levels. Do/Skip
Lizardmen 1 10 53, 62, 73, 150 Should only be unlocked if killing lizardman shamans for the dragon warhammer . Blowpiping lizardman shamans requires a lot of Zulrah's scales and Prayer potions. Should not be unlocked (unless killing lizardman shamans ) 34,000 (using the toxic blowpipe )
Mithril dragons 1 9 304 A very fast task with high combat stats and a dragon hunter lance . Deal a lot of damage due to being able to attack with all three combat styles. Unlock/Do (after obtaining a dragon hunter lance )
Mutated Zygomites 57 2 74, 86 Fairly fast task with high combat stats and the appropriate gear. No notable drops, requires use of fungicide spray to finish the kill (similar to lizards and ice coolers ) Do/Skip
Nechryael 80 9 115 Good Slayer experience rates if maging in the Catacombs of Kourend . Greater nechryael drop a lot of alchables which covers the cost of runes. Alternatively, killing nechryael in the Slayer Tower offers good money and Herblore experience. Greater nechryael are often crowded and killing them requires frequent banking. Do 31,000 (melee),

80,000â€“90,000 (Magic)

Red dragons 1 8 48, 152, 289 Decent Prayer and Crafting experience if the player has a dragon hunter lance . Fairly fast task. N/A Unlock/Do (after obtaining a dragon hunter lance )
Rune dragons 1 2 380 Fast task with high combat stats, very good money. A stab weapon is required to kill them effectively. Deal a lot of damage due to being able to attack with all three combat styles. Do
Skeletal Wyverns 72 7 198 Decent supplies and Prayer experience. Slow experience rates, especially if ranging. Far from a bank which makes banking the bones inconvenient. Not worth doing unless Slayer points are an issue. Do/Skip 10,000
Smoke devils 93 9 160 High experience rates if maging. Alchables cover some of the money spent into runes. Maging them efficiently requires practice. Slow to lure without a dwarf multicannon . Do 70,000â€“120,000 (Magic)
Spiritual creatures 63, 68, 83 7 115-134 Decent drops if killing the mages. Not worth doing after getting Dragon boots or Slayer points are an issue. Skip 25,000
Steel dragons 1 7 246 Fairly fast task with high combat stats. Slow to kill at lower levels. If dragon hunter lance is unavailable, using a trident is a costly alternative. Do/Skip 25,000
Suqahs 1 8 111 None. Not worth doing unless Slayer points are an issue. Skip/Block
Trolls 1 6 Varies heavily Decent drops if killing ice trolls on Jatizso . Hard rumble in Nightmare Zone with the three trolls enabled can also be a reasonable AFK option. Travelling to Jatizso can take a while. Not worth doing unless Slayer points are an issue. Skip
TzHaar 1 10 74, 103, 133, 149, 702 High experience rates if the player is experienced in the Fight Caves . No drops other than the Tokkul reward from completing the Fight Caves , which makes this task not worth doing even for the high experience rates. Do not unlock Varies
Vampyres 1 8 61, 64, 70, 72, 77, 100, 130 Chance at a blood shard (1/1,500) if killing the vyrewatch sentinel variant, with spawns close to both a bank and an altar. Requires Actual Vampyre Slayer to be unlocked, requires Sins of the Father for the best drops, and blood shards can alternatively be obtained through Thieving . Do not unlock
Warped Creatures 56 8 98, 121, 138 Good money and supplies. Warped creatures never lose aggression. N/A Unlock/Do 30,000
Waterfiends 1 2 115 Decent supplies. A good crush weapon (such as abyssal bludgeon or Zamorakian hasta ) is required to kill them effectively. Not worth doing unless Slayer points are an issue. Skip 35,000
Wyrms 62 8 99 Decent Prayer experience from the wyrm bones , decent drops. Requires protection from the mountain heat if elite Kourend & Kebos Diary hasn't been completed. Not worth doing unless Slayer points are an issue. They are however one of two sources to get the Dragon harpoon , the other being Tempoross . Skip/Block
