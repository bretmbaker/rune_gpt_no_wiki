[Weapon: Yin Yang Amulet]


Theyin yang amuletis a cosmetic amulet that can be purchased from theMakeover Magefor 100 coins. The amulet is a replica of the amulet the make-over mage uses to change the gender of players.
According to theMakeover Mage, they found the amulet after killing a nest ofgazulibirds,[1]and askedDoricto make non-magical copies of the amulet as a result of player requests to obtain the amulet as an item.[2]
