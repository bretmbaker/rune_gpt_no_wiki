[Weapon: Occult Necklace]


Theoccult necklaceis a magical amulet that requires 70Magicto wear. It is dropped by thesmoke deviland itsboss variant. Wearing it increases the player'sMagic damageby 5%, and is the only amulet to give such a bonus. This 5% bonus will also stack with existing boosts, such as thestaff of the deadand the imbuedslayer helmet. For this reason, as well as a significant magic accuracy bonus, it is commonly considered the best neck slot item for magic combat in terms of damage output.
A combination of the occult necklace andstaff of the deadwhile usingIce Barragewould increase a player's max hit to 36, from an original maximum of 30. The max hit increases to 40 if using ablack mask (i)orslayer helmet (i)on aSlayer task.
