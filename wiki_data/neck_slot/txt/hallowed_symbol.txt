[Weapon: Hallowed Symbol]


Hallowed symbolis an item that reduces the number of sacrificedvampyre dustand speeds up the rate at which the player can pass through the Prayer barrier when passing theSaradomin Brazierskilling challenge inside theHallowed Sepulchre. It can be purchased from theMysterious Hallowed Goodsshop for 100hallowed marks.
With the necklace equipped, sacrificing at the Saradomin Braziers will only take 1vampyre dustinstead of two, and you will move through the barrier at a rate of 4 ticks instead of 5 ticks.
