[Weapon: Necklace Of Passage#(2)]


Thenecklace of passageis ajade necklaceenchanted via theLvl-2 Enchantspell requiring aMagiclevel of 27.
Right-clicking the necklace in your inventory and selecting "rub", or right-clicking it while equipped will allow the player to teleport to one of three possible locations. Using the necklace to teleport will consume a charge. After all five charges are used, the necklace will disintegrate into dust.
