[Weapon: Silver Necklace#Normal]


Thesilver necklaceis aquestitem used in theMurder Mysteryquest. It is obtained from a barrel inAnna's bedroom in theSinclair Mansionduring the quest. It can be dusted withflourand used withflypaperto obtainAnna's print, which can be compared with theunknown printto see if Anna is the killer.
This itemwill disappearif a player has it in their bank or inventory upon finishing the quest. It is a unique cosmetic item, so if a player wishes to keep it, they must drop it before finishing the quest and pick it up again afterwards. Itcannotbe obtained again after the quest is finished.
