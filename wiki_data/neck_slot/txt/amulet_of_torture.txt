[Weapon: Amulet Of Torture]


Theamulet of tortureis an enchantedzenyte amuletrequiring level 75Hitpointsto equip. It is made by enchanting azenyte amuletwith theLvl-7 Enchantspell, which requires level 93Magicand grants 110 Magic experience.
