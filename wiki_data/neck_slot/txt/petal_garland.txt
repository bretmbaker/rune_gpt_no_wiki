[Weapon: Petal Garland]


ThePetal garlandis a purely cosmeticneck slotitem that can be obtained as a possible reward from theEnchantment RitualForestry event. Note that the petal garlandcannotbe stored in aplayer-owned house.
There is a fixed chance of 1/30 to receive the petal garland upon completing at least one step of the ritual,[1]provided the player was eligible for the full rewards of the event.[2]When receiving one, a game message in the chatbox will stateThe Dryad also hands you a Petal garland.
