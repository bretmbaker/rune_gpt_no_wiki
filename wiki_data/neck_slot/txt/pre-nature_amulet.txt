[Weapon: Pre-Nature Amulet]


Thepre-nature amuletis the precursor to theamulet of nature. It is anunstrung emerald amuletwhich is strung withmagic string, granting 4Craftingexperience.
Like the enchanted amulet of nature, it cannot be bought or sold on theGrand Exchange. However, likeburnt food, it can betradedbetween players. It can be enchanted with theLvl-2 Enchantspell. Note that you can only have oneenchantednature amulet at a time.
