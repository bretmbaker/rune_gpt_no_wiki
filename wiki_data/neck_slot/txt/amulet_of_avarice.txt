[Weapon: Amulet Of Avarice]


Theamulet of avariceis anamuletthat can be received as a drop fromrevenantsfound within theRevenant Caves. The amulet has the same combat bonuses as theamulet of glory, but has an additional 20% increase in accuracy and damage when fighting revenants, similar to aSalve amulet. The effects of the amuletdo notstack with theSlayer helmet (i).
When worn, all drops received fromNPCswithin theRevenant CavesandWilderness Slayer Cave, will be in the form ofbank notes, except for secondary supply rolls and uniques. However, equipping the amulet will cause the player to becomeskulled, similar to thecape of skulls.
