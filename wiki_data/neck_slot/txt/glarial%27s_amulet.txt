[Weapon: Glarial%27S Amulet]


Glarial's amuletis used during theWaterfall Questto grant access to theWaterfall Dungeon.  Attempting to enter the dungeon during the quest without the amulet results in the player taking damage, being swept away to downstream from the door. After the quest, the amulet is not needed to enter the dungeon.
The amulet can be recovered in the westernchestinsideGlarial's Tombif lost. Players may enter the tomb by havingGlarial's pebbleand having noarmour,weapons, orrunesequipped or in their inventory. Beware of themoss guardiansin the tomb. Players may obtain more than one amulet through thedrop trick.
