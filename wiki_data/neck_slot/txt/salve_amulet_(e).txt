[Weapon: Salve Amulet (E)]


Thesalve amulet (e)is the enchanted form of thesalve amulet, granting a 20% increase (from 16.67%) in the wearer'sAttackandStrengthwhen fightingundead monsters.
The salve amulet is enchanted by usingTarn's diaryon it, acquired as part of theLair of Tarn Razorlorminiquest. An easy way to obtain the diary is by teleporting directly toTarn's Lairusing aSlayer ring. After the miniquest, players can quickly reclaim the diary from abookcaseinplayer-owned houseunder the nameThe Diary of Tarn Razorlor.
