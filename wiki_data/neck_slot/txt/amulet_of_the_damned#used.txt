[Weapon: Amulet Of The Damned#Used]


Theamulet of the damnedis an amulet that provides extra buffs toBarrows equipment. It can be obtained from silver and gold shade chests found in theShade Catacombs. The amulet has the same bonuses as anamulet of glory.
LikeBarrows equipment, it can only be traded when it hasn't been used. It will degrade at the same rate as any other barrows armour, but once it fully degrades, it will turn to dust. It becomes untradeable once it degrades. Players with multiple amulets can combine charges.
