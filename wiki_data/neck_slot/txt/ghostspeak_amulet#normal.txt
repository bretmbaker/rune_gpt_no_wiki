[Weapon: Ghostspeak Amulet#Normal]


Aghostspeak amuletis an item worn in theneck slotthat enables the wearer to talk to ghosts. Partial completion ofThe Restless Ghostis required to obtain the item, as it is given to players byFather Urhneyin order to speak to theghosthaunting theLumbridgegraveyard during the quest. Normally players can only get one ghostspeak amulet as Father Urhney will not give an amulet to players who have one in their possession, although thedrop trickcan be used to obtain more.
An enchanted ghostspeak amulet is given to players by theold cronein order to controlNecrovarusduring theGhosts Ahoyquest.
