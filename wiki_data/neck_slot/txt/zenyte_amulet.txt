[Weapon: Zenyte Amulet]


TheZenyte amuletis made by using agold bar, azenyte, and anamulet mouldon afurnacewith aCraftinglevel of 98. Doing so gives the player azenyte amulet (u)and 200 experience in Crafting. The amulet must then be strung before wearing by using aball of woolor theString Jewelleryspell.
Players can castLvl-7 Enchanton a zenyte amulet to enchant it into anamulet of torture. This requires level 93Magicand grants 110 Magic experience.
