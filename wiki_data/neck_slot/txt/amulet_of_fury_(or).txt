[Weapon: Amulet Of Fury (Or)]


Theamulet of fury (or)is anamulet of furywith afury ornament kitattached to it. The attached ornament kit gives no additional bonuses, and is only used to add aesthetics to the amulet. It costs2,868,947for both the parts to make the amulet of fury (or).
While the amulet is untradeable in its ornamented state, it can be dismantled anytime, returning the tradeable amulet and ornament kit.
