[Weapon: Ruby Necklace]


Aruby necklaceis made by using agold bar, arubyand anecklace mouldon a furnace. It requires aCraftinglevel of 40 and gives 75 experience when made.
The ruby necklace may be enchanted into aDigsite pendantafter learning the enchantment spell from one of the archaeologists in the find-cleaning area of theVarrock Museum. The spell is learned by participating in cleaning samples until aclean necklaceis extracted from a sample. When brought to an expert, they will teach the player how to enchant ruby necklaces. After this, the player can enchant them with theLvl-3 Enchantwhich requires level 49Magic.
