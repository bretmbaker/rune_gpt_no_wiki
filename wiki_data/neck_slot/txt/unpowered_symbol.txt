[Weapon: Unpowered Symbol]


Anunpowered symbolis a symbol ofZamorakmade by stringing anunstrung emblemwith aball of wool, requring 1Crafting, yielding 4 Craftingexperience. It is made into anunholy symbolby using a completedUnholy bookorBook of balance, requiring 50 Prayer, or talking to theSpirit of Scorpiusafter completion of theObservatory Questwhich also requires claiming an unholy mould from him. He will not give the player an unholy mould if there is one already in their inventory.
