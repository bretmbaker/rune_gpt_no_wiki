[Weapon: Amulet Of Nature]


Anamulet of natureis used to monitor the state of aFarmingpatch. It can only be bound to one patch at a time, and will remain bound until the amulet is used on a different patch. To bind the amulet to a particular Farming patch, use the amulet on the desired patch. While the amulet is worn or in the inventory, if the crop becomes diseased, dies, or becomes fully grown, it will vibrate and display a message in the chat interface onlyoncewhile the player is online or upon logging in:
A low hum resonates from the Amulet of Nature - it seeks audience with you.
