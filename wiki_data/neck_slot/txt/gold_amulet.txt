[Weapon: Gold Amulet]


Gold amuletis a piece ofjewellerythat is worn in the neck slot. It is made by using agold baron a furnace with anamulet mould, followed by using aball of woolon the resultingGold amulet (u). This requires level 8Craftingand the player receives 30 experience for making the amulet and an additional 4 experience for stringing it.
The gold amuletcannotbe enchanted, making it purely cosmetic. However, it can be sold toGrum's Gold ExchangeinPort Sarimfor 245 coins or at theGrand Exchange.
