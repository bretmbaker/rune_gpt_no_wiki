[Weapon: Amulet Of The Eye#Red]


TheAmulet of the Eyeis an item that allows unlimited teleportation to theTemple of the Eye, just south of theportal. It is obtained by inspecting thetarnished locketand then showing it toPhileas the Lumbridge Guide.
The amulet can be recoloured usingAbyssal dye(received from theRewards Guardian) to a red, blue, or green colour scheme.
