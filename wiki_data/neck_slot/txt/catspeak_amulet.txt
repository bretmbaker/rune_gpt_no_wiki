[Weapon: Catspeak Amulet]


Thecatspeak amuletis a special amulet that allows the wearer to speak withcats. It is received after the completion ofIcthlarin's Little Helper, and is used in several subsequent quests. If players lose the amulet, theSphinxwill give them a replacement amulet, butonly if they have a cat following them.DuringDragon Slayer II, players gain the ability to speak to cats without needing the amulet.
