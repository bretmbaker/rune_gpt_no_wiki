[Weapon: Amulet Of Power]


Anamulet of poweroffers a good balance of attack and defence bonuses for all classes ofcombat. It is created by castingLvl-4 Enchanton adiamond amulet, and has no requirements to be worn.
This item is a less expensive option for players who do not wish to buy anamulet of glory. It lacks the glory's teleportation capabilities and has weakerattackandPrayer bonuses, but has the samestrengthbonus as well as higherdefence bonuses.
