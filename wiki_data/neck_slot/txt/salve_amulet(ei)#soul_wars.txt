[Weapon: Salve Amulet(Ei)#Soul Wars]


TheSalve amulet(ei)is the enchanted and imbued form of thesalve amulet, granting a 20% increase in the wearer's accuracy and damage formelee,ranged, andmagicwhen fightingundead monsters.
The salve amulet's bonuses against the undeaddo notstack with theblack maskandslayer helmet'son-task bonuses; wearing both will only apply the bonuses of the salve amulet (ei). The salve amulet alsodoes nothave an effect on thedwarf multicannon's accuracy.[1]However, the bonuses ofVoid Knight equipmentdoesstack. Additionally, the amulet's effects apply to all targets when using multi-hitting melee weapons in amulticombat area, such as theScythe of Viturand thespecial attackof thedragon 2h sword, but not for multi-hitting magic and ranged attacks (ex.chinchompasandburst spells).
