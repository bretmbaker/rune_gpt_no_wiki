[Weapon: Crystal Pendant]


Crystal pendantis an item used inRegicide. It is given to players byLord Iorwerthso they can prove they're working for him. The crystal pendant has no uses after finishing the quest, aside from being aesthetically unique.
Warning: Pendants in the player's bank and equipped to their character will be removed when speaking toLord IorwerthandKing Lathasduring Regicide. The pendant is silently taken from the player when talking toLord Iorwerthto receive theBig book of bangs, as well as any future dialogue withLord Iorwerthduring the quest, and finally when speaking withKing Lathasto complete the quest. As such, using thedrop trickis necessary when talking to them for players who want to keep this pendant after the quest.
