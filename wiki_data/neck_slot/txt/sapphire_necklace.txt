[Weapon: Sapphire Necklace]


Thesapphire necklaceis made by using agold bar, asapphireand anecklace mouldon a furnace. This requires aCraftinglevel of 22 and gives 55 experience when made.
Grum's Gold ExchangeinPort Sarimbuys at a higher price thanHigh Alchemy, if he has no sapphire necklaces for sale. He will buy the first for 735 coins, compared with the High Alchemy value of 630 coins. However, his price decreases in relation to the amount he has in stock, going below High Alchemy value when his stock goes above five.
