[Weapon: Amulet Of Rancour]


Theamulet of rancouris the upgraded variant of theamulet of torturerequiring level 90Hitpointsto wear. It is made with level 86Craftingby attaching the amulet of torture to anaraxyte fang, which is a rare untradeable drop fromAraxxor.
Compared to theamulet of torture, the amulet of rancour gains +10 melee attack bonuses and +2 strength bonus, but with negative bonuses for magic and ranged attacks.
