[Weapon: Amulet Of Eternal Glory]


Theamulet of eternal gloryis a specialdragonstone amuletpossessing identical stats and features of a regularamulet of glory, except it has unlimitedteleportcharges instead of the regular four or six maximum charges. However, it cannot be mounted in aplayer-owned house.
It is made (only after completion of Heroes Quest) while chargingamulets of glory(including thetrimmed variant) with fewer than six charges at theFountain of Runein theWilderness. Each amulet recharged has a 1 in 25,000 chance to be turned into an amulet of eternal glory.[1]When this occurs, a message in the chatbox will appear, stating: "The power of the fountain is transferred into an amulet of eternal glory. It will now have unlimited charges."
