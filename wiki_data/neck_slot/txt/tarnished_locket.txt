[Weapon: Tarnished Locket]


Thetarnished locketis an item that can be obtained from anintricate pouch, which can be received from theRewards Guardianafter completing a round ofGuardians of the Rift. After inspecting it, players can give the locket to theLumbridge Guidein exchange for theAmulet of the Eye. If the locket is lost before turning it in, it can be reclaimed fromApprentice Felix.
