[Weapon: Amulet Of Accuracy]


Anamulet of accuracyis a reward from theImp Catcherquest. It is easily obtainable for new players because the quest has no requirements apart from killingimps; however, it is only recommended if the player is anIronmanlackingMagiclevels to enchant better amulets.
To completeImp Catcher,Wizard Mizgogtrades an amulet of accuracy for a set ofblack,red,white, andyellow beads. After the quest it is possible to give him one of each bead again for another amulet.
