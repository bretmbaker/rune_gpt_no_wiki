Therainbow scarfis aneck slotitem originally introduced as a reward fromGilbertfor completing theGilbert's Coloursevent in 2017. It can either be purchased fromDiango's Toy Storefor 120coins, or obtained freely fromGilbertin the flower field south-east ofBarbarian Village.
There are nine unique versions of the scarf representing different variants ofsexual orientationorgender identity, whose colours can be changed at any time by right-clicking the "Change" option on the scarf. When switched to another style other than the pride (rainbow) style, the item is renamed to acolourful scarf.
