[Weapon: Salve Amulet]


Thesalve amuletis an offensive amulet that is made upon completion of theHaunted Minequest, granting a 16.67%[1]increase in the wearer'sAttackandStrengthwhen fightingundead monsters.
The amulet is made by using achiselon thecrystal outcropat the bottom level of theAbandoned Mine, then using the acquiredsalve shardon aball of wool; theString Jewelleryspell will also work.
