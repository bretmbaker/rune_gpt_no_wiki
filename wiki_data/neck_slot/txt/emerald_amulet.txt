[Weapon: Emerald Amulet]


Theemerald amuletis created by using agold baron afurnacewhile also carrying a cutemeraldand anamulet mould. This requires 31Craftingand grants 70 Crafting experience. The resultingunstrung amuletis then used with aball of wool, granting an additional 4 experience.
It has no stat bonuses, but can be enchanted with theLvl-2 Enchantspell to become anAmulet of defencewhich gives some defensive bonuses.
