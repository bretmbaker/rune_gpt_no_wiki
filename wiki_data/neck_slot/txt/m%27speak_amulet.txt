[Weapon: M%27Speak Amulet]


Am'speak amuletis an item crafted duringMonkey Madness Ithat allows the player to communicate withmonkeys. It is later used in theRecipe for DisasterAwowogeisubquest as well asMonkey Madness II. After completion of Monkey Madness II, the amulet is no longer needed to speak with monkeys.
To make one, the player must takemonkey dentures, am'amulet mould, and agold bartoZooknock, who will give them anenchanted bar(and the mould back). They must then use the bar on thewall of flamein theTemple of Marimbo Dungeonwith the mould in their inventory to create anunstrung m'speak amulet. The amulet can be completed using aball of wool.
