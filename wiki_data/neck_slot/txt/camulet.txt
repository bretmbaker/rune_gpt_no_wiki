[Weapon: Camulet]


Camuletis a reward fromEnakhra's Lament. When equipped, its wearer can speak to certaincamelsfound inAl Kharidand throughout the Kharidian desert.
The Camulet has four charges to teleport to the desert, insideEnakhra's TemplenearLazim. With the hardDesert Diarycompleted, the teleport destination can be switched to the temple entrance near thequarry.
