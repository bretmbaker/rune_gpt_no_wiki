[Weapon: Giantsoul Amulet#Uncharged]


Thegiantsoul amuletis an amulet that can be obtained as an untradeable drop from looting eitherBranda the Fire QueenorEldric the Ice King. When charged, it provides teleports to the entrances of the lairs ofBryophyta,Obor, and theRoyal Titans.
The amulet is charged by using one set ofbig bonesandlaw runeon the amulet and can contain a maximum of 16,000 charges (costing10,240,000to fully charge, or640per teleport). Unlike similar items such asXeric's talismanandPendant of ates, the giantsoul amuletcannotbe uncharged to return the bones and runes.
