An amulet of accuracy is a reward from the Imp Catcher quest. It is easily obtainable for new players because the quest has no requirements apart from killing imps; however, it is only recommended if the player is an Ironman lacking Magic levels to enchant better amulets.

To complete Imp Catcher, Wizard Mizgog trades an amulet of accuracy for a set of black, red, white, and yellow beads. After the quest it is possible to give him one of each bead again for another amulet.
