The tarnished locket is an item that can be obtained from an intricate pouch, which can be received from the Rewards Guardian after completing a round of Guardians of the Rift. After inspecting it, players can give the locket to the Lumbridge Guide in exchange for the Amulet of the Eye. If the locket is lost before turning it in, it can be reclaimed from Apprentice Felix.

  • The locket opens, revealing a note inside.

Locket note

To my beloved,

While I can't be with you in person right now, I hope that this locket will remind you of me in the years to come. I think of you daily, and look forward to our inevitable reunion.

Your dearest Philly.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The note inside of the locket is written by Phileas the Lumbridge Guide, who reveals its backstory when given the locket. Phileas explains that it was a gift to his beloved Thenia, that he had sent her while being part of the Order of Wizards, which frequently led him to travel outside of Lumbridge in order to pursue his research.[1] During one such trip, robbers broke into their house and killed Thenia after she tried to repel them.[2] The locket was taken during this break in, until the player finds it in an abyssal rift and returns it to Phileas.

References[edit | edit source]

  1. ^ Lumbridge Guide. Old School RuneScape. "It all started in my youth when I was part of the Order of Wizards. I frequently spent time travelling across Gielinor, which kept me away from my beloved who worked here in Lumbridge. Due to being apart, I would often send her gifts. This locket was one of them."
  2. ^ Lumbridge Guide. Old School RuneScape. "As I said, my research often kept me away from home. During one of my trips away, our house was robbed. My dear Thenia tried to stop them, but they took her life in return. The locket was taken in the robbery."
