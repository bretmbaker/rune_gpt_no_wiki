The amulet of rancour is the upgraded variant of the amulet of torture requiring level 90 Hitpoints to wear. It is made with level 86 Crafting by attaching the amulet of torture to an araxyte fang, which is a rare untradeable drop from Araxxor.

Compared to the amulet of torture, the amulet of rancour gains +10 melee attack bonuses and +2 strength bonus, but with negative bonuses for magic and ranged attacks.

The amulet of rancour can be recoloured to an amulet of rancour (s) by speaking to a man at the end of a series of tunnels within the Morytania Spider Cave, each of which have various requirements to pass through as alluded to in the venom-riddled note.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The word rancour, first appearing in Middle English, is defined as ire, wrath, and/or bitterness. It can be traced back to Latin rancere, meaning "to stink". The word rancid shares this root.
