The giantsoul amulet is an amulet that can be obtained as an untradeable drop from looting either Branda the Fire Queen or Eldric the Ice King. When charged, it provides teleports to the entrances of the lairs of Bryophyta, Obor, and the Royal Titans.

The amulet is charged by using one set of big bones and law rune on the amulet and can contain a maximum of 16,000 charges (costing 10,240,000 to fully charge, or 640 per teleport). Unlike similar items such as Xeric's talisman and Pendant of ates, the giantsoul amulet cannot be uncharged to return the bones and runes.

Bryophyta's teleport is a short distance from an agility shortcut next to Vannaka, making the Giantsoul amulet useful for mid-level Slayer as well as Treasure Trails steps that require talking to Vannaka.

Should you die while fighting the titans, the amulet will always be retained as an additional inventory item, providing easy access to your gravestone just outside the tunnel entrance.

Players can get duplicate amulets, which can be exchanged with the Odd Old Man for 30 noted big bones each.

Teleport options[edit | edit source]

Players can access the teleports by either right-clicking the amulet in their inventory and selecting "rub", or right-clicking it while equipped and selecting one of their destinations.
