A brass necklace is a near-worthless item worn purely for aesthetic purposes, as it offers no combat bonuses. It cannot be made using the Crafting skill, and is dropped by goblins. It spawns in two locations: Edgeville Dungeon near the hobgoblins, and the downstairs area of Varrock west bank (requires Telekinetic grab or completion of Crack the Clue III to obtain).
