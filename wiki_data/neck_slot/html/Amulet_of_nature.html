An amulet of nature is used to monitor the state of a Farming patch. It can only be bound to one patch at a time, and will remain bound until the amulet is used on a different patch. To bind the amulet to a particular Farming patch, use the amulet on the desired patch. While the amulet is worn or in the inventory, if the crop becomes diseased, dies, or becomes fully grown, it will vibrate and display a message in the chat interface only once while the player is online or upon logging in:

A low hum resonates from the Amulet of Nature - it seeks audience with you.

The player can then rub the amulet to get more information on the patch as it displays which farming patch it is bound to, one of the following messages below and its growth stage:

I am afraid that your patch has succumbed to disease and died, my friend. I am truly sorry.

I am afraid that your patch has succumbed to disease, my friend. You must attend to it immediately, before it dies.

Your patch is growing nicely, free from disease.

Your patch has now grown to fruition, my friend.

If the bound patch is empty, the amulet will display a message respective to that patch. The following message for example:

I am bound to your patch in Hosidius. There is nothing growing in your patch.

This should allow the wearer to detect when the patch is diseased or fully grown, and hence can be useful when growing important crops as the player can track the crop status and immediately react when the status changes. There is no limit to how many times you can rub the amulet.

Despite the 'drop' option, it is actually destroyed upon being dropped.

You can only have 1 amulet of nature at a time and, once enchanted, it is untradeable.

An amulet of nature can be created by stringing an emerald amulet (u) with magic string to make a pre-nature amulet. Casting Lvl-2 Enchant on it will result in an amulet of nature.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
1 June 2023
(update)

The amulet of nature can now notify you about fully grown or diseased patches from your inventory, rather than having to be worn.

16 May 2019
(update | poll)

The amulet shows the growth state upon checking the patch. Additionally, a bug where the amulet did not bind to one of the twelve grape vine patches has been fixed.

17 January 2019
(update)

The amulet of nature gives its information in fewer chat boxes.

4 January 2018
(update)

Corrected grammar when checking a Nature Amulet that is tied to a Fossil Island hardwood patch.
