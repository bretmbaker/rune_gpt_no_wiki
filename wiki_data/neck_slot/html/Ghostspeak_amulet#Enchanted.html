A ghostspeak amulet is an item worn in the neck slot that enables the wearer to talk to ghosts. Partial completion of The Restless Ghost is required to obtain the item, as it is given to players by Father Urhney in order to speak to the ghost haunting the Lumbridge graveyard during the quest. Normally players can only get one ghostspeak amulet as Father Urhney will not give an amulet to players who have one in their possession, although the drop trick can be used to obtain more.

An enchanted ghostspeak amulet is given to players by the old crone in order to control Necrovarus during the Ghosts Ahoy quest.

Players who have completed the Morytania Medium Diary may use Morytania legs in place of a Ghostspeak Amulet anywhere it is required, except during the miniquest The General's Shadow as the ghostly robes must be worn, and the miniquest Curse of the Empty Lord as armour is not allowed on Entrana.

Players who have lost their amulet may obtain another one from Father Urhney.

NPC interactions[edit | edit source]

The ghostspeak amulet is also necessary for talking to any ghost NPC in Port Phasmatys, although players who have completed Ghosts Ahoy can pass through the entry barrier without it. Players who have not finished the quest will need to wear the amulet when passing through (the toll is 2 ecto-tokens).

In addition, the amulet allows players to speak to the ghosts in Melzar's Maze on the second floor. The conversation is about their experience of Elvarg's destruction; they were stationed at Rimmington.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The ghosts found by the Grave of Scorpius can be spoken to without a ghostspeak amulet.
  • Upon completion of the Ghosts Ahoy quest, any duplicates of the enchanted ghostspeak amulet, whether in one's inventory or bank, will NOT be lost.
