The desert amulet 4 is a reward from completing the elite Desert Diary. The amulet is received from Jarr at Shantay Pass, and can be retrieved from him for free if lost. It provides unlimited Nardah teleports which take the player within close proximity of the Elidinis Statuette, which completely heals the player from damage and poison and offers restoration to run energy, prayer points, and special attack when worshipped.

All of the Desert amulets provide no stat bonuses. They are the only achievement diary reward to have no stat bonuses, despite being considered one of the hardest to obtain.

Additionally, the desert amulet 4 provides complete immunity to desert heat when worn, and can be seen as a free alternative to the circlet of water.

Benefits[edit | edit source]

Desert amulet 4 only[edit | edit source]

When worn
  • Protection from the desert heat effect
When worn or in the player's inventory
  • Unlimited teleports to Nardah and the Kalphite Cave
  • Nardah teleport now takes players inside the shrine to Elidinis for easier access to the Elidinis Statuette

A number of other benefits are always active upon claiming rewards from the elite tier of the Desert diary.

The Nardah teleport on the desert amulet now teleports the player inside the shrine to Elidinis for easier access to the Elidinis Statuette.

The "Teleport" option was replaced with "Nardah" and "Kalphite Cave" options.

(update | poll)
