Fire Waveis afire-basedwave spellin thestandard spellbook, requiring level 75Magicto cast. As anoffensive combat spellit can only be cast onattackable NPCs, or other players in aPvP situation.

It is the second-strongest fire spell in the game, with the strongest beingFire Surge.

Damage[edit|edit source]

Fire Wave has a base maximum hit of 20. As with all combat spells, this maximum hit is increased based on the player'smagic damage bonus.

As a fire-based spell, Fire Wave's maximum hit is also increased against targets that areweak to fire.
