Charge Air Orbis a spell which can be cast on theAir ObeliskaboveEdgeville Dungeon, turning anunpowered orbinto anair orb. It requires 66 Magic to cast, at which point it willautocastevery 3.6 seconds. Players can speed up this time by casting it manually for each orb; it is possible to gain up to 40,000Magicexperience per hour using the spell in this way.

Using an air orb on abattlestaffmakes anair battlestaff, requiring 66Craftingand granting 137.5 Crafting experience. Charging anair orbis a hard task for theWilderness Diary.

Wilderness location[edit|edit source]

The Air Obelisk is located above the Edgeville Dungeon, and requires going into level 7Wildernessto access. Players may be attacked by other players within 7 combat levels of them; as such, some players create pures to attack low-level accounts casting the spell.
