Watchtower Teleport







Released | 7 May2003(Update)

Members | Yes

Level | 58

Spellbook | Standard

Type | Teleport

Experience | 68

Runes | 22

Casting speed | 4 ticks

Quest | Watchtower

Description | Teleports you to the Watchtower



Animation



Sound effect



Watchtower Teleportteleportsthe player to the top floor of theWatchtower, just north ofYanille. Unlocking the spell requires completion of theWatchtowerquestto read thespell scrollgiven as a reward by theWatchtower Wizard.

Casting this spell is a requirement for completing thehard Ardougne Diary. Completing the hard Ardougne Diary grants the ability to teleport to the centre of Yanille by right-clicking the Watchtower teleport icon in thestandard spellbook.
