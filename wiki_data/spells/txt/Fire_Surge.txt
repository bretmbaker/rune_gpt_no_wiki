Fire Surgeis afire-basedsurge spellin thestandard spellbook, requiring level 95Magicto cast. As anoffensive combat spellit can only be cast onattackable NPCs, or other players in aPvP situation.

It is the strongest fire spell in the game, and the highest-level combat spell in the standard spellbook.

Damage[edit|edit source]

Fire Surge has a base maximum hit of 24. As with all combat spells, this is increased based on the player'smagic damage bonus.

As a fire-based spell, Fire Surge's maximum hit is also increased against targets that areweak to fire.
