Released | 15 March2006(Update)

Spellbook | Standard

Type | Teleport

Experience | 74

Runes | 2221

Casting speed | 4 ticks

Quest | Recipe for Disaster(Awowogei)

Description | Teleports you to Ape Atoll



Animation



Sound effect



Ape Atoll Teleportteleports the caster toApe Atoll, next toHafubain the safe upstairs section of theTemple of Marimbo. Players must have completed theAwowogei sectionof theRecipe for Disasterquestto use this spell; therefore the full requirements of this spell are the completion ofCook's AssistantandMonkey Madness I, 70Cooking(boostable), 48Agility, and 64Magic.

Note that the banana required for the spell must be unnoted.

This spell cannot be transferred into ateleport tablet, but it can be added to aportalorportal nexusin theplayer owned house.

Casting this spell is a requirement for theHard Western Provincesdiary.
