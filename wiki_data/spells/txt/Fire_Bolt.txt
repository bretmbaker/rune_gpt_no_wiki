Fire Boltis afire-basedbolt spellin thestandard spellbook, requiring level 35Magicto cast. As anoffensive combat spellit can only be cast onattackable NPCs, or other players in aPvP situation.

Damage[edit|edit source]

Fire Bolt has a base maximum hit of 12. Players who have completed theFamily Crestquest can wearChaos gauntletsto increase the maximum hit by an additional +3, up to 15.

As with all combat spells, Fire Bolt's maximum hit is also increased based on the player'smagic damage bonus.

As a fire-based spell, Fire Bolt's maximum hit is also increased against targets that areweak to fire.

It should be noted that even with a fire rune-providing staff,tome of firecharges will still be consumed.
