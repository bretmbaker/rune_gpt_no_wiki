Enfeebleis a level 73Magicspell. When cast on an opponent, theirStrengthlevel is reduced by 10%. Casting Enfeeble grants 83 Magic experience and requires 1Soul, 8Earth, and 8Water runesto be cast. The spell can only be cast if the opponent's stats haven't already been lowered, meaning you cannot stack the 10% Strength reduction with multiple spells in a row.
