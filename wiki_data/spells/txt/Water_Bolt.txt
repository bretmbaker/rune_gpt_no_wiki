Water Boltis awater-basedbolt spellin thestandard spellbook, requiring level 23Magicto cast. As anoffensive combat spellit can only be cast onattackable NPCs, or other players in aPvP situation.

Damage[edit|edit source]

Water Bolt's base maximum hit starts at 10 and increases as the player unlocks additional bolt spells, up to 12. Players who have completed theFamily Crestquest can wearChaos gauntletsto increase the maximum hit by an additional +3, up to 15 at level 35 Magic.

As with all combat spells, Water Bolt's maximum hit is also increased based on the player'smagic damage bonus.

As a water-based spell, Water Bolt's maximum hit is also increased against targets that areweak to water.
