Water Waveis awater-basedwave spellin thestandard spellbook, requiring level 65Magicto cast. As anoffensive combat spellit can only be cast onattackable NPCs, or other players in aPvP situation.

It is the second-strongest water spell in the game, with the strongest beingWater Surge.

Damage[edit|edit source]

Water Wave's base maximum hit starts at 18 and increases as the player unlocks additional wave spells, up to 20 at level 75 Magic.

Magiclevel | Highest-level wave spell available | Water Wavebase maximum hit

65- 69 | Water Wave | 18

70- 74 | Earth Wave | 19

75+ | Fire Wave | 20

As with all combat spells, Water Wave's maximum hit is also increased based on the player'smagic damage bonus.

As a water-based spell, Water Wave's maximum hit is also increased against targets that areweak to water.
