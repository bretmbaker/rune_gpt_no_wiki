Earth Surgeis anearth-basedsurge spellin thestandard spellbook, requiring level 90Magicto cast. As anoffensive combat spellit can only be cast onattackable NPCs, or other players in aPvP situation.

It is the strongest earth spell in the game.

Damage[edit|edit source]

Earth Surge has a base maximum hit of 23 which increases to 24 when the player unlocksFire Surgeat level 95 Magic.
