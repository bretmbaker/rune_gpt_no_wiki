Earth Boltis anearth-basedbolt spellin thestandard spellbook, requiring level 29Magicto cast. As anoffensive combat spellit can only be cast onattackable NPCs, or other players in aPvP situation.

Damage[edit|edit source]

Earth Bolt's base maximum hit starts at 11 and increases to 12 when the player unlocksFire Boltat level 35 Magic. Players who have completed theFamily Crestquest can wearChaos gauntletsto increase the maximum hit by an additional +3, up to 15.
