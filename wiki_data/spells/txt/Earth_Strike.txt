Earth Strikeis anearth-basedstrike spellin thestandard spellbook, requiring level 9Magicto cast. As anoffensive combat spellit can only be cast onattackable NPCs, or other players in aPvP situation.

Damage[edit|edit source]

Earth Strike starts out with a base maximum hit of 6. Once the player has the Magic level required to castFire Strike, Earth Strike's base maximum hit is increased to match.

As with all combat spells, Earth Strike's maximum hit is also increased based on the player'smagic damage bonus.

As an earth-based spell, Earth Strike's maximum hit is also increased against targets that areweak to earth.
