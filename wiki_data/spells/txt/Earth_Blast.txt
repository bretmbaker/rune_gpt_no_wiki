Earth Blastis anearth-basedblast spellin thestandard spellbook, requiring level 53Magicto cast. As anoffensive combat spellit can only be cast onattackable NPCs, or other players in aPvP situation.

It is the strongest earth spell available tofree-to-playplayers.

Damage[edit|edit source]

Earth Blast's base maximum hit starts at 15 and increases to 16 when the player unlocksFire Blastat level 59 Magic.

Magiclevel | Highest-level blast spell available | Earth Blastbase maximum hit

53- 58 | Earth Blast | 15

59+ | Fire Blast | 16

As with all combat spells, Earth Blast's maximum hit is also increased based on the player'smagic damage bonus.

As an earth-based spell, Earth Blast's maximum hit is also increased against targets that areweak to earth.
