Lvl-3 Enchantis autility spellin thestandard spellbook, requiring level 49Magicto cast. The spell enchantsrubyandred topazitems, primarilyjewellery, granting 59 Magic experience when doing so.

This spell can be transferred to amagic tabletby bringing the required runes and a piece ofsoft clayto alecternin thestudyof aplayer-owned house, granting 44.3 Magic experience.
