Water Strikeis awater-basedstrike spellin thestandard spellbook, available starting at level 5Magic. As anoffensive combat spellit can only be cast onattackable NPCs, or other players in aPvP situation.

Damage[edit|edit source]

Water Strike's base maximum hit starts at 4 and increases as the player unlocks additional strike spells, up to 8 at level 13 Magic.

Magiclevel | Highest-levelstrike spellavailable | Water Strikebase maximum hit

5- 8 | Water Strike | 4

9- 12 | Earth Strike | 6

13+ | Fire Strike | 8

As with all combat spells, Water Strike's maximum hit is also increased based on the player'smagic damage bonus.

As a water-based spell, Water Strike's maximum hit is also increased against targets that areweak to water.
