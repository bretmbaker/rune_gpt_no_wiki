Lumbridge Teleportteleports the caster toLumbridge. Unlike the freeLumbridge Home Teleportspell, this is fast and cannot be interrupted by combat, though like most other teleports, it is unusable above level 20Wildernessor when the player isteleblocked. Players returning to Lumbridge may saverunesby using the free teleport instead, if they are not in danger.

Casting this spell is a requirement for themedium Lumbridge & Draynordiary.
