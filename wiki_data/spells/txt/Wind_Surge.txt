Wind Surgeis anair-basedsurge spellin thestandard spellbook, requiring level 81Magicto cast. As anoffensive combat spellit can only be cast onattackable NPCs, or other players in aPvP situation.

It is the strongest wind spell in the game, as well as the most cost-effective surge spell to cast.

Damage[edit|edit source]

Wind Surge has a base maximum hit of 21 which increases as the player unlocks additional surge spells, up to 24 at level 95 Magic.

Magiclevel | Highest-level surge spell available | Wind Surgebase maximum hit

81- 84 | Wind Surge | 21

85- 89 | Water Surge | 22

90- 94 | Earth Surge | 23

95+ | Fire Surge | 24

As with all combat spells, Wind Surge's maximum hit is also increased based on the player'smagic damage bonus.

As an air-based spell, Wind Surge's maximum hit is also increased against targets that areweak to air.
