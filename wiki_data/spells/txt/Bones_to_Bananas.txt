TheBones to Bananasspell can be used to turn allnormal bones,monkey bones, andbig bonesin one's inventory tobananas. It requires twowater runes, twoearth runes, and oneNature runeto be cast, with aMagiclevel of 15. This spell is very useful when fighting monsters which drop bones as a 100% drop, as thebananasproduced heal 2 Hitpoints each.

If you are casting the spell only for magic experience, add only one bone to your inventory at a time as it turns ALL bones in inventory into bananas for only 25 exp per spell regardless of number of bones. At least one bone or big bone must be in your inventory to be able to cast the spell.

Players can unlock the next tier of the Bones to Bananas spell,Bones to Peaches, usingPizazz pointsat theMage Training Arena. Players can then use the spell with aMagiclevel of 60, and the resultingpeachesheal 8 Hitpoints.
