Theblue moon spearis a non-degradable two-handedbladed staffthat requires level 70Attackand 75Magicto equip, which is obtained as a possible reward from theLunar ChestinNeypotzli. It canautocastelemental spells from thestandard spellbook, as well as combat spells from theArceuus spellbookandAncient Magicks. Despite the name, the blue moon spear is abladed staffand isnotconsidered acorpbane weapon.

Special attack[edit|edit source]

The blue moon spear has a special attack,Break Shackles, costing 50% special attack energy and requiring the full blue moon armour set to use.

Break Shacklesremoves all active binding effects on a target and has a 1% increase in accuracy and damage for everytickof binding removed.

When the special attack is used while casting movement impairing attack spells, the next activation of theFrostweavereffect will also trigger the special allowing for a 3-hit combo that starts with the spell cast and ends with the melee hit from theFrostweavereffect.

While the special attack uses the Fend animation it actually grantsStrengthexperience. When automatically performed, this attack can hit targets across diagonal spaces and over small fences or walls.

Frostweaver[edit|edit source]

When worn with all of the other pieces of theblue moon armourset, the player gains theFrostweaverset effect. This gives theBlue moon speara chance to attack instantly, without any action delays, after the player casts a spell that would bind, freeze or root a target.

20% chance to occur after successfully casting aBind spellfrom theStandard spellbookor anIce spellfrom theAncient spellbook, even if it does not bind a target.50% chance to occur after successfully casting aGrasp spellfrom theArceuus spellbook, even if it does not bind a target.

This attack can be performed automatically by enabling it in the combat options menu.

An effective strategy is to autocastGrasp spellsfrom theArceuus spellbookwithMark of Darknessactive. This results in a unique dual-combat style where a player gets one instant and free melee attack for roughly every 2 spells that land. This can be particularly effective in PvP encounters with lots of chasing or on monsters with low melee and magic defences.
