Lvl-4 Enchantis autility spellin thestandard spellbook, requiring level 57Magicto cast. The spell enchantsdiamondjewellery, granting 67 Magic experience when doing so.

This spell can be transferred to amagic tabletby bringing the required runes and a piece ofsoft clayto alecternin thestudyof aplayer-owned house, granting the same experience as casting the spell.

Like other jewellery enchantment spells, Lvl-4 Enchant will automatically re-cast every 7 ticks (4.2 seconds). While less click-intensive, it is much slower than enchanting each piece of jewellery individually. An inventory of 27 pieces of jewellery would take about one minute and fifty seconds to enchant at this rate.

When cast actively, a player could theoretically achieve 2,000 casts per hour for a total of 134,000 experience. When cast passively only 857 casts can be achieved, worth 57,419 experience. In practice, both of these numbers will be slightly lower due to time spent banking.
