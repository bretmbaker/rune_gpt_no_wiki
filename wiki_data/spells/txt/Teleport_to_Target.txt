Teleport to Targetis a spell that requires level 85Magicto cast, and must be unlocked by reading atarget teleport scroll. While participating in theBounty Hunterminigame, casting Teleport to Target will bring the player to within 12 squares of their assigned target inside of theDaimon's Crater.

The spell can only be cast from the lobby area at the centre of the crater, and only if player has already been assigned a target. In addition, it can only be used five times per target. This spell is available on allspellbooks.

Changes[edit|edit source]

Date | Changes

26 June2024(update) | The spell has been added to theArceuus spellbook.

24 May2023(update) | Teleport to Target can now be cast inside the lobby area of the newest Bounty Hunter iteration to be teleported near one's assigned target in the Daimon's Crater.

23 March2017(update) | The amount of teleports to the same Bounty Hunter target has been increased from 1 to 5 following feedback from the community.

16 March2017(update) | The spell can now only be used once per target.

Trivia[edit|edit source]

Prior to the closure of target worlds, Teleport to Target could be used to enter theWilderness Agility Coursewithout having the required 52Agility, if the player's target was inside the gate. However, the required Agility level of 49 was still required to complete the course.
