Claws of Guthixis acombat spellin thestandard spellbook, requiring 60Magicand the completion ofMage Arena Ito cast. In addition, players must also be wielding aGuthix staff,void knight mace, orstaff of balance; all but the first are able toautocastthe spell.

Initially, the spell can only be cast within theMage Arenaagainst thebattle mages. Upon casting the spell 100 times (regardless of whether it was asuccessful hitor not), the spell becomes usable outside the arena.

Claws of Guthix has a maximum hit of 20. Upon a successful hit, Claws of Guthix lowers the target'sDefenceby 1 + 5%.[1]This decrease does not stack nor will it reapply itself until the opponent returns to their base Defence level.

Like the othergod spells, the maximum hit is temporarily increased to 30 while theChargespell is active and the respectivegod capeis worn.
