Wind Boltis anair-basedbolt spellin thestandard spellbook, requiring level 17Magicto cast. As anoffensive combat spellit can only be cast onattackable NPCs, or other players in aPvP situation.

Damage[edit|edit source]

Wind Bolt's base maximum hit starts at 9 and increases as the player unlocks additional bolt spells, up to 12. Players who have completed theFamily Crestquest can wearChaos gauntletsto increase the maximum hit by an additional +3, up to 15 at level 35 Magic.

As with all combat spells, Wind Bolt's maximum hit is also increased based on the player'smagic damage bonus.

As an air-based spell, Wind Bolt's maximum hit is also increased against targets that areweak to air.
