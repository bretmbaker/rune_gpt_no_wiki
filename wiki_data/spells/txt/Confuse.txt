Confuse

Type | Combat

Experience | 13

Casting speed | 5 ticks

Description | Reduces your opponent's attack by 5%



Animation



Sound effect



Confuseis a low-levelMagicspell. When cast on an opponent, theirAttacklevel is reduced by 5%. The spell givesexperiencewhether it hits or misses, making it a popular way to gain experience cheaply. The spell can only be cast if an opponent's stats haven't already been lowered, meaning you can not stack stat-reduction with multiple spells in a row.

The spell grants 13 Magic experience and requires threewater, twoearth, and onebody runein order to be cast.
