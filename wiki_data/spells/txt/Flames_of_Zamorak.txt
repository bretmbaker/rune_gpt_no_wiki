Flames of Zamorakis acombat spellin thestandard spellbook, requiring 60Magicand the completion ofMage Arena Ito cast. In addition, players must also be wielding aZamorak staff,staff of the dead,Thammaron's sceptre (a),accursed sceptre (a), ortoxic staff of the dead; all but the first are able toautocastthe spell.

Initially, the spell can only be cast within theMage Arenaagainst thebattle mages. Upon casting the spell 100 times (regardless of whether it was asuccessful hitor not), the spell becomes usable outside the arena.

Flames of Zamorak has a maximum hit of 20. Upon a successful hit, the spell reduces the target'sMagiclevel by 1 + 5%.[1]This decrease does not stack nor will it reapply itself until the opponent returns to their base Magic level. Since Magic level affectsmagic defence, this causes subsequent attacks to be more accurate; however, the spell's max hit will not be increased.

Like the othergod spells, the maximum hit is temporarily increased to 30 while theChargespell is active and the respectivegod capeis worn.

Note that since Flames of Zamorak requires a Zamorak staff, staff of the dead, Thammaron's sceptre (a), accursed sceptre (a), or toxic staff of the dead, rune-providing staves cannot be used. However, a tome of fire can be worn to provide infinite fire runes. This does not grant the damage boost from the tome and will not consume its charges. Also, the staff of the dead and the toxic staff of the dead provide a (1/7) chance to negate rune cost.
