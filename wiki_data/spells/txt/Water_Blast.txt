Water Blastis awater-basedblast spellin thestandard spellbook, requiring level 47Magicto cast. As anoffensive combat spellit can only be cast onattackable NPCs, or other players in aPvP situation.

It is the strongest water spell available tofree-to-playplayers.

Damage[edit|edit source]

Water Blast's base maximum hit starts at 14 and increases as the player unlocks additional blast spells, up to 16 at level 59 Magic.

Magiclevel | Highest-level blast spell available | Water Blastbase maximum hit

47- 52 | Water Blast | 14

53- 58 | Earth Blast | 15

59+ | Fire Blast | 16

As with all combat spells, Water Blast's maximum hit is also increased based on the player'smagic damage bonus.

As a water-based spell, Water Blast's maximum hit is also increased against targets that areweak to water.
