Earth Waveis anearth-basedwave spellin thestandard spellbook, requiring level 70Magicto cast. As anoffensive combat spellit can only be cast onattackable NPCs, or other players in aPvP situation.

It is the second-strongest earth spell in the game, with the strongest beingEarth Surge.

Damage[edit|edit source]

Earth Wave's base maximum hit starts at 19 and increases to 20 once the player unlocksFire Waveat level 75 Magic.

As with all combat spells, Earth Wave's maximum hit is also increased based on the player'smagic damage bonus.

As an earth-based spell, Earth Wave's maximum hit is also increased against targets that areweak to earth.
