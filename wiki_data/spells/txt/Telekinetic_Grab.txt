Telekinetic Grab(also known astelegrab) is autility spellavailable in thestandard spellbook. It requires level 33Magicto cast, and grants 43 Magicexperienceper cast.

The spell can be used to pick up an item (or a pile of a stackable item) up to 10 tiles away, including over or through obstacles such as fences, gates, and rivers. It is useful when picking up item spawns that are guarded by dangerous monsters, such as thewine of Zamorakin theChaos Temple, and is also sometimes required for quests in order to pick up items that are guarded byNPCsor otherwise unreachable. For repeated casting, it is recommended to have astaff of airequipped to negate the need forair runes.

There are only a few items, such asAhab's beer,raw pheasant,Trouble Brewing flowers,dragon tokensand equipable reward items fromachievement diaries, which cannot be picked up with Telekinetic Grab.

Trivia[edit|edit source]

If you move to a different level while you are casting Telekinetic Grab, you will use the runes but not receive the item. You will receive the messageYour telegrab fizzles as you move too far away.
