Wind Waveis anair-basedwave spellin thestandard spellbook, requiring level 62Magicto cast. As anoffensive combat spellit can only be cast onattackable NPCs, or other players in aPvP situation.

It is the second-strongest wind spell in the game, with the strongest beingWind Surge. It is also the most cost-effective wave spell to cast.

Damage[edit|edit source]

Wind Wave's base maximum hit starts at 17 and increases as the player unlocks additional wave spells, up to 20 at level 75 Magic.

Magiclevel | Highest-level wave spell available | Wind Wavebase maximum hit

62- 64 | Wind Wave | 17

65- 69 | Water Wave | 18

70- 74 | Earth Wave | 19

75+ | Fire Wave | 20

As with all combat spells, Wind Wave's maximum hit is also increased based on the player'smagic damage bonus.

As an air-based spell, Wind Wave's maximum hit is also increased against targets that areweak to air.
