Fire Blastis afire-basedblast spellin thestandard spellbook, requiring level 59Magicto cast. As anoffensive combat spellit can only be cast onattackable NPCs, or other players in aPvP situation.

It is the strongest fire spell available tofree-to-playplayers.

Damage[edit|edit source]

Fire Blast has a base maximum hit of 16. As with all combat spells, Fire Blast's maximum hit is increased based on the player'smagic damage bonus.

As a fire-based spell, Fire Blast's maximum hit is also increased against targets that areweak to fire.

Maximum hit[edit|edit source]

While wearing gear with high magic damage bonus, Fire Blast's maximum hit can be increased up to 24. Wearing aslayer helm (i)while on a Slayer task can further increase the maximum hit to 26.
