The master wand requires 60 Magic to wield. It can be obtained by either exchanging pizazz points at the Mage Training Arena or buying one from another player. It is one of ten weapons able to autocast Ancient Magicks , and one of only four weapons able to autocast both Ancient Magicks and Arceuus combat spells . This means the wand is able to autocast the Ancient, Arceuus and Standard spellbooks.

A master wand can be upgraded to a Kodai wand if the player has a Kodai insignia , obtained from the Chambers of Xeric .

Obtaining [ edit | edit source ]

Purchasing the master wand from the Mage Training Arena requires the teacher wand equipped or in your inventory and the following points:

Amount Task
240 Telekinetic pizazz points
240 Alchemist pizazz points
2400 Enchantment pizazz points
240 Graveyard pizazz points

From scratch, buying the master wand and all of its three prerequisite wands requires:

Amount Task
480 Telekinetic pizazz points
530 Alchemist pizazz points
4800 Enchantment pizazz points
480 Graveyard pizazz points
