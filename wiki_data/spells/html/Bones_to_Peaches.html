Bones to Peaches is a spell that turns all bones up to and including big bones in the inventory into peaches , which can be eaten to heal 8 Hitpoints . Before it can be cast it must be unlocked from the Mage Training Arena , which costs all of the following:

Amount Task
200 Telekinetic pizazz points
300 Alchemist pizazz points
2,000 Enchantment pizazz points
200 Graveyard pizazz points

Unlocking this spell requires level 60 Magic, this requirement is boostable .

Its tablet form is usable without unlocking the spell, albeit granting no experience. However, crafting the tablet requires the player to have unlocked the spell.

Bones to Peaches is commonly used to extend trips when food is scarce but bones are plentiful, such as in the God Wars Dungeon or the Smoke dungeon . Players may also consider the healing effect of Guthan's equipment as an alternative (or in addition) to the spell. Tablets can be used to save inventory space instead of bringing the runes required to cast the spell.

Demonic ashes cannot be converted as they are not considered bones. Attempting to convert bones of a higher tier than big bones gives the message The bones you're holding aren't suitable for peachifying. Attempting to cast the spell without any bones gives the message You aren't holding any bones!

Casting the spell in the Al Kharid Palace is a hard task in the Lumbridge & Draynor Diary (using the tablet form of the spell does not work).

Date Changes
8 May 2024
( update | poll )

Casting Bones to Peaches will no longer stall the player.

27 March 2024
( update )

The spell is now filtered out if the player chooses to filter spells they cannot cast.

( update )

Bones to Peaches will now appear locked on the Spellbook menu if it has not been unlocked from the Mage Training Arena .

The 'Bones to Bananas' and 'Bones to Peaches' spells now work on more types of bone, including big bones.
