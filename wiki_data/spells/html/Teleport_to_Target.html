Teleport to Target is a spell that requires level 85 Magic to cast, and must be unlocked by reading a target teleport scroll . While participating in the Bounty Hunter minigame, casting Teleport to Target will bring the player to within 12 squares of their assigned target inside of the Daimon's Crater .

The spell can only be cast from the lobby area at the centre of the crater, and only if player has already been assigned a target. In addition, it can only be used five times per target. This spell is available on all spellbooks .

Changes [ edit | edit source ]

Date Changes
26 June 2024
( update )

The spell has been added to the Arceuus spellbook .

24 May 2023
( update )

Teleport to Target can now be cast inside the lobby area of the newest Bounty Hunter iteration to be teleported near one's assigned target in the Daimon's Crater.

6 August 2020
( update )

The spell's name was changed from "Teleport to Bounty Target" to "Teleport to Target".

23 March 2017
( update )

The amount of teleports to the same Bounty Hunter target has been increased from 1 to 5 following feedback from the community.

16 March 2017
( update )

The spell can now only be used once per target.

Trivia [ edit | edit source ]

  • Prior to the closure of target worlds, Teleport to Target could be used to enter the Wilderness Agility Course without having the required 52 Agility , if the player's target was inside the gate. However, the required Agility level of 49 was still required to complete the course.
