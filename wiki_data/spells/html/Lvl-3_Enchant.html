Lvl-3 Enchant is a utility spell in the standard spellbook , requiring level 49 Magic to cast. The spell enchants ruby and red topaz items, primarily jewellery , granting 59 Magic experience when doing so.

This spell can be transferred to a magic tablet by bringing the required runes and a piece of soft clay to a lectern in the study of a player-owned house , granting 44.3 Magic experience.

List of items [ edit | edit source ]

Free-to-play players can only use the spell to enchant the ruby amulet , though the Ring of Forging can be also enchanted by bringing a Ruby Ring along with 250 coins to Murky Matt .
