Bind is a level 20 Magic spell located in the Standard spellbook . Casting bind on an opponent will prevent it from moving for 8 ticks (4.8 seconds). When used on a player and the bind is about to wear off, the bound player will receive this message in their chatbox: The holding effect is beginning to fade. Players can increase the binding duration by equipping swampbark armour ; the helm , body , and legs will increase the duration by 2 ticks (1.2 seconds) each, up to a maximum of 14 ticks (8.4 seconds).

Bind gives 31 Magic experience if it successfully hits the target, or 30 experience if it splashes . It requires two nature , three earth , and three water runes to be cast.

Changes [ edit | edit source ]

Date Changes
13 September 2023
( update )

Bind now yields 31 Magic experience upon a successful cast.

21 June 2023
( update | poll )
  • The maximum Swampbark Armour set bonus for the Bind spell can now be achieved with just the helm , body and legs as opposed to the full set.
  • Each outfit piece now increases the duration of the Bind spell by an additional tick , so the complete bonus increases the duration to 6 ticks.
11 January 2023
( update )

An audio cue and message have been added to the Bind spell to tell the victims when the freeze is about to expire.

5 September 2019
( update )

Protect from Magic no longer halves the duration of Bind.
