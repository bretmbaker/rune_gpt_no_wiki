Trollheim Teleport
This article is about the teleport spell. For the teleport tablet, see Trollheim teleport (tablet) .
Trollheim Teleport
Released 5 October 2004 ( Update )
Members Yes
Level 61
Spellbook Standard
Type Teleport
Experience 68
Runes 2 2
Casting speed 4 ticks
Quest Eadgar's Ruse
Description Teleports you to Trollheim
Animation
Sound effect

Trollheim Teleport teleports the caster to the top of Trollheim , just outside of Eadgar 's cave. Players must have completed the Eadgar's Ruse quest to use this spell.

Teleporting here allows players to reach the God Wars Dungeon and Troll Stronghold easily where there is a disease-free herb patch (usable only after completing My Arm's Big Adventure ).

Players can also redirect their house tablet by using a scroll of redirection on it and directing it to Trollheim.

This is one of the few teleports that cannot be put in a player-owned house portal chamber or portal nexus .

Casting this spell is a requirement for the Hard Fremennik diary .
