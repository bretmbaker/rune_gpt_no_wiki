[Weapon: Slayer Ring (Eternal)]


TheSlayer ring (eternal)is a specialslayer ringthat is made with aneternal geminstead of anenchanted gem. It provides unlimited teleport charges, compared to the eight of its normal counterpart. It is only obtainable throughCrafting, and is otherwise crafted the same way as the normal slayer ring and has the same requirements (namely 75 Crafting and having purchased theRing blingunlock from anySlayer masterfor 300Slayer reward points).
