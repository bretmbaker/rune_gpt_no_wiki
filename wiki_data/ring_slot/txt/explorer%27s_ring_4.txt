[Weapon: Explorer%27S Ring 4]


TheExplorer's ring 4is a reward from completing the eliteLumbridge & Draynor Diary. It is received fromHatius CosaintusinLumbridge, and can be retrieved from him for free if lost.
The Check option will send a chatbox message with the amount of energy restores, teleports, and alchemy casts remaining for that day.
