Thering of sufferingis an enchantedzenyte ring. It is the best-in-slot defensive ring, exceptits imbued variant. This ring can be imbued into aring of suffering (i)using 725,000Nightmare Zonereward points or 362,500 with thehard tierofCombat Achievements, 300Zeal TokensfromSoul Wars, or aScroll of Imbuing(which can be purchased for 200Emir's Arenareward points). Aring of suffering (i)has double the bonuses of a non-imbued ring.
Players can make one by enchanting azenyte ringwith theLvl-7 Enchantspell. This requires level 93Magic, 20soul runes, 20blood runes, and 1cosmic runeand grants 110 Magic experience. As with all enchantedzenytejewellery, this ring requires aHitpointslevel of 75 to be equipped.
