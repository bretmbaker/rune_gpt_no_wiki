[Weapon: Ring Of The Gods (I)#Soul Wars]


Thering of the gods (i)is an upgraded version of the regularring of the gods. It isimbuedas a reward from theNightmare Zone, costing 650,000  reward points or 325,000 with thehard tierofCombat Achievements, atSoul Warsfor 260Zeal Tokens, or by using ascroll of imbuingpurchased from theEmir's Arena. Imbuing the ring also requires having aholy wrenchin the inventory, though it does not consume it.
The imbued version incorporates the holy wrench's benefit ofincreasing prayer points restored from some prayer potionswhen it is equipped, and also provides an extra +4 prayer bonus compared to the regular version. The increased prayer restoration effect of the ring of the gods,Prayer capeandholy wrenchdo not stack. Unlike the prayer cape and holy wrench, ring of the gods (i) must be worn to activate the prayer restoration effect anddoes notwork when in the players inventory.
