It can beimbuedusing 650,000Nightmare Zonereward points or 325,000 with thehard tierofCombat Achievements, 260Soul WarsZeal Tokens, or aScroll of Imbuing, purchased for 200Emir's Arenareward points, which doubles the magic attack and defence bonuses, and increases the magic damage bonus from 0.2% to 0.5%.
