[Weapon: Granite Ring (I)#Soul Wars]


Thegranite ring (i)is an imbued version of the regulargranite ringfrom theNightmare ZoneandSoul Warsminigames. It requires 50Defenceand 50Strengthto equip. The imbue upgrade costs either 500,000Nightmare Zonepoints or 250,000 with thehard tierofCombat Achievements, 200Zeal Tokens, or by using ascroll of imbuingpurchased from theEmir's Arena.
The upgrade doubles all defensive bonuses, including the negativeMagic defencebonus.
