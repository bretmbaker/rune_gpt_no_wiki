[Weapon: Ring Of Suffering (I)#Recoil]


Thering of suffering (i)is an upgraded version of the regularring of suffering. It can be imbued as a reward from theNightmare Zone, costing 725,000 reward points or 362,500 with thehard tierofCombat Achievements, atSoul Warsfor 300Zeal Tokens, or by using ascroll of imbuingpurchased from theEmir's Arena. Its stats are double those of the ring of suffering.
The ring can also be charged withrings of recoilto give it the recoil effect, renaming the item asring of suffering (ri). Each ring of recoil provides 40 charges, assuming the ring of recoil has not been drained prior. A maximum of 100,000 charges can be stored, equivalent to 2,500 rings and costing2,202,500. Players can configure the charges while the ring is in their bank by right-clicking on Configure-Charges, as long as players have the required materials. If a player uncharges the ring, the recoil rings stored willnotbe refunded.
