The granite ring is a defensive ring dropped by the Grotesque Guardians that can be found on the Slayer Tower's rooftop. Level 50 Defence and Strength are required to wear it.

It can be imbued using 500,000 Nightmare Zone reward points or 250,000 with the hard tier of Combat Achievements, 200 Soul Wars Zeal Tokens, or a Scroll of Imbuing, purchased for 200 Emir's Arena reward points, doubling its stats.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The granite ring is the only ring in the game that requires a Defence level to wear.
  • The granite ring is the heaviest ring in the game and one of four rings that weighs at least 1 kg, the others being the granite ring (i), the tyrannical ring and the tyrannical ring (i).
  • The granite ring is the only item in the game that has a stat decrease after imbuing it (-2 magic defence to -4 magic defence)
