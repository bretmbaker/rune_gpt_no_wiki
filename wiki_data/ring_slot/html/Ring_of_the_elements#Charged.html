The ring of the elements is an item purchased from the Guardians of the Rift reward shop for 400 abyssal pearls. It teleports players near the elemental runic altars.

The ring is charged by using one of each elemental rune (Air, Earth, Fire, Water) and one law rune together on the ring (costing 152 per teleport), up to a maximum of 10,000 charges (costing 1,520,000 to fully charge). Players can receive the runes back through the ring's right-click "Uncharge" option while at a bank. Regardless of charge, the item counts as a piece of warm clothing at Wintertodt, and is the only ring slot item to do so. The number of charges left is tracked for each player, not each ring; thus, a second ring will have the same number of charges remaining as any other ring the player owns. When charged, the ring is untradeable.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
31 March 2022
(update) 		Hotfix

The ring of elements can no longer be uncharged away from a bank.

30 March 2022
(update)

The "Check" option on the charged ring has been changed to "Uncharge", allowing players to receive the runes used upon doing so.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The ring's setting is in the same shape as the head of the elemental talisman.
  • When selecting the "Last Destination" option on the ring without having ever used it before, it will prompt the player with the normal "Rub" options menu.
