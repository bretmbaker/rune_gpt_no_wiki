A beacon ring is a ring used in the What Lies Below Quest. It is obtained from Zaff and is used to summon him during the quest. After the quest, the ring can no longer summon Zaff and the Operate option becomes unuseable; attempting the option results in the message Zaff has removed the summon charges from the ring. It will not summon him again.

Players can regain this ring for free from Zaff if they lose it, making it a free alternative to the Seers' ring, albeit with a much lower magic attack stat. The ring is similar in stats to the Lunar ring, but has 1 less Magic defence. It can also be re-purchased from Perdu for 6,000 coins.

Changes[edit | edit source]

Date Changes
29 June 2022
(update)

Beacon rings can now be bought from Perdu after defeating Surok, without requiring completion of What Lies Below.

12 August 2020
(update)

The Beacon Ring can now be purchased from Perdu for 6,000GP after completion of What Lies Below.

11 June 2015
(update)

You can no longer spam the beacon ring to constantly spawn Zaff.

Trivia[edit | edit source]

  • The text engraved on the side of the ring, which says "beacon",[1] is written in the same coded font as the letter to Surok.
  • When buying a beacon ring from Perdu with a full inventory, the ring will drop to the ground appearing larger than life, encircling the player.
