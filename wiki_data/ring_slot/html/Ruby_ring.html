A ruby ring is a jewellery item that players can make by using a gold bar, a cut ruby, and a ring mould at a furnace. Making it requires a Crafting level of 34, and grants 70 Crafting experience.

A ruby ring can be enchanted into a ring of forging using the spell Lvl-3 Enchant, which requires one cosmic rune, five fire runes, and level 49 Magic, or by paying 250 coins to Murky Matt at the Grand Exchange.

Ruby rings can be sold to Grum's Gold Exchange in Port Sarim, with a starting price of 1,417 coins if none are in stock. This is more coins than are produced by casting High Level Alchemy on a ruby ring.

A ruby ring spawn is located on a table in the cellar downstairs of the Varrock west bank. However, it is too far away to be retrieved with the Telekinetic Grab spell. In order to obtain the ruby ring from the bank vault, you'll need to enter it through Crack the Clue III.
