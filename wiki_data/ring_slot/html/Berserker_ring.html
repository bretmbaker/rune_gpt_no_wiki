The berserker ring is the strength bonus variant of the Fremennik rings and is dropped by Dagannoth Rex in the Waterbirth Island Dungeon. The Fremennik rings are some of the very few rings to give stat bonuses. Group Ironmen who do not have individual prestige require at least 1 Dagannoth Rex kill before they are able to wear this item.

It can be imbued into a berserker ring (i), doubling its bonuses, by using 650,000 Nightmare Zone reward points or 325,000 with the hard tier of Combat Achievements, 260 Soul Wars Zeal Tokens, or a scroll of imbuing purchased for 200 Emir's Arena reward points.

The berserker ring is one of a handful of rings that provide a strength bonus. Thus, it is commonly used in melee setups because strength bonus raises the player's maximum hit, which is usually more beneficial than accuracy for lowering kill times.

The ring makes the biggest difference when facing monsters with low Defence, but in general even against higher defence targets it will provide better DPS than rings which only provide accuracy bonuses. In specific situations where long-term DPS is not a concern (for example special attacks or boss mechanics), if crush accuracy is needed the Tyrannical ring should be used; for slash accuracy a warrior or bellator ring should be used.

Players can use a chisel on the berserker ring to create a berserker icon after learning how to combine ring icons from Peer the Seer. This process requires 80 Crafting (boostable), and is part of the process for creating the ultor ring.
